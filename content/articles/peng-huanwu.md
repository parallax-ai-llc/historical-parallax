## Summary

Peng Huanwu was a leading Chinese theoretical physicist who made important contributions to quantum mechanics and nuclear physics. He played a crucial role in China's nuclear weapons program, contributing theoretical calculations for the atomic and hydrogen bombs. He studied under Max Born in Edinburgh and was one of China's most respected physicists.
