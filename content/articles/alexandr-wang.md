## Summary

Alexandr Wang is the co-founder and CEO of Scale AI, a data labeling and AI infrastructure company valued at over $13 billion. He dropped out of MIT at 19 to found Scale AI, which provides training data for AI companies including OpenAI, Meta, and the U.S. Department of Defense. He became the world's youngest self-made billionaire at 25. Scale AI's data labeling services are critical to training large language models and computer vision systems.
