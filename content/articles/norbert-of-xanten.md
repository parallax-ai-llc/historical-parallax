## Summary

Norbert of Xanten was a German founder of the Premonstratensian Order (Norbertines) and Archbishop of Magdeburg. After a dramatic conversion experience, he became an itinerant preacher advocating reform. He founded the Premonstratensian Order at Prémontré in 1120, combining monastic contemplation with active pastoral ministry. The order eventually spread to over 600 houses across Europe.
