## Summary

James Brown was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer known as the 'Godfather of Soul' and the 'Hardest Working Man in Show Business.' His innovations in rhythm and groove, particularly in songs like Papa's Got a Brand New Bag and Get Up (I Feel Like Being a) Sex Machine, laid the groundwork for funk, disco, and hip-hop.
