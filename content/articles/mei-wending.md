## Summary

Mei Wending was a Qing Dynasty mathematician and astronomer who worked to integrate Western mathematical methods with traditional Chinese mathematics. He wrote over 80 works on mathematics and astronomy, demonstrating that Western mathematics had Chinese historical parallels. He helped preserve Chinese mathematical heritage during a period of Western scientific dominance.
