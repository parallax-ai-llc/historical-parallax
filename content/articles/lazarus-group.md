## Summary
The Lazarus Group is a North Korean state-sponsored hacking organization responsible for major cyberattacks including the 2014 Sony Pictures hack, the 2016 Bangladesh Bank heist ($81 million stolen), and the WannaCry ransomware attack. They are believed to fund North Korea's nuclear program.
