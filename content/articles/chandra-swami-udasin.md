## Summary

Chandra Swami was a controversial Indian spiritual figure who cultivated relationships with powerful political leaders around the world. He was close to several Indian Prime Ministers and international figures including Adnan Khashoggi and the Sultan of Brunei. He was investigated for his involvement in the Rajiv Gandhi assassination and financial scandals. He represented the complex intersection of spiritual authority and political power in modern India.
