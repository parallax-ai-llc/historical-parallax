## Summary

Matthew Fontaine Maury was an American astronomer, oceanographer, and meteorologist nicknamed the 'Pathfinder of the Seas.' He published the first comprehensive wind and current charts of the oceans, revolutionizing maritime navigation. His work on ocean currents and physical geography of the sea established oceanography as a science.
