## Summary

Koo In-hwoi founded LG Group (originally Lucky Chemical), building it into one of South Korea's largest conglomerates. He started with a cosmetics cream called 'Lucky Cream' and expanded into plastics, electronics, and chemicals. He founded GoldStar (later LG Electronics) in 1958, producing South Korea's first radio. His vision of combining chemicals and electronics under one corporate umbrella created the Lucky-GoldStar (LG) conglomerate.
