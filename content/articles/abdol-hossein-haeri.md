## Summary

Abdol Hossein Haeri was an Iranian physicist who made contributions to nuclear physics and helped establish modern physics research in Iran. He was a key figure in developing Iran's scientific infrastructure and training the next generation of Iranian physicists.

## Overview

Ayatollah Abdol-Hossein Haeri (also transliterated as al-Haeri) is an Iranian Shia cleric recognized in some contexts as a senior religious authority (marjaʿ). He has been associated with the seminary environment of Qom and is known for issuing religious opinions (fatwas) and guidance to followers within Shia jurisprudential traditions.

## Religious role

In Twelver Shia Islam, a **marjaʿ al-taqlid** is a jurist whom lay followers may emulate in matters of religious practice. Public attention to senior clerics can fluctuate based on their published rulings, teaching activities, and positions on social or political questions.

## Public presence

Haeri’s public profile has at times been discussed in connection with broader debates about the relationship between religious authority and political power in the Islamic Republic of Iran.
