## Summary

Tenzin Palmo (born Diane Perry) is a British-born Tibetan Buddhist nun who spent 12 years meditating in a remote cave in the Himalayas at an altitude of 13,000 feet. Her story, told in the book Cave in the Snow, inspired millions. She founded Dongyu Gatsal Ling Nunnery in India to provide education and training for women in the Drukpa Kagyu tradition. She is one of the most prominent advocates for the full ordination of Buddhist women.
