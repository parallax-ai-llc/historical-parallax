## Summary

Hwang Woo-suk was a South Korean veterinary scientist who gained fame for claimed breakthroughs in stem cell research, later found to be fabricated. However, his team did achieve genuine advances in animal cloning, including the first cloned dog (Snuppy) in 2005. His scandal became one of the most significant cases of scientific fraud in modern history.
