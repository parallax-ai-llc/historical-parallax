## Summary

Pir Vilayat Inayat Khan was the son of Hazrat Inayat Khan who served as the head of the Sufi Order International for over 50 years. He was educated in Paris and at Oxford, and served in the Royal Navy during World War II, participating in the D-Day landings. He developed a contemplative practice that integrated Sufi meditation with modern psychology and quantum physics. He established Sufi retreat centers worldwide.
