## Summary

Stefan Quandt is one of the wealthiest people in Germany through his family's controlling stake in BMW. The Quandt family acquired their BMW stake in the 1960s, saving the company from a takeover by Mercedes-Benz. Stefan holds approximately 25% of BMW shares and serves on the company's supervisory board. He also controls DELTON AG and SolarWorld Industries.
