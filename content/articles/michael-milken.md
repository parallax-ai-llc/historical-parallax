## Summary
Michael Robert Milken, the 'Junk Bond King,' was an American financier who was convicted of securities fraud in 1990. He revolutionized the use of high-yield bonds but engaged in illegal activities. He served 22 months in prison and paid $600 million in fines. He was pardoned by President Trump in 2020.
