## Summary

Dhammika Perera is the richest person in Sri Lanka and one of the most powerful industrialists in the country. He controls Vallibel One, a diversified conglomerate spanning ceramics, glass, textiles, power generation, and plantations. He also holds significant stakes in major Sri Lankan banks and insurance companies. He served as a member of parliament and was a key figure in Sri Lankan business for decades.
