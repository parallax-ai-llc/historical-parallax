## Summary

Canadian cult leader known as 'Moses' who led a small commune in Ontario and Quebec. He tortured, mutilated, and murdered followers. He amputated a woman's arm with a meat cleaver. Stabbed to death in prison.

## Context

Thériault is typically covered in writing about **high-control groups** and abusive communes. Summaries emphasize both the leadership dynamics that kept followers compliant and the criminal acts committed against members of the group.
