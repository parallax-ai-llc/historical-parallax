## Summary

Sarah Gilbert led the development of the Oxford-AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine, one of the most widely used vaccines in the world with over 2.5 billion doses administered. She and her team designed the vaccine in just one weekend after the SARS-CoV-2 sequence was published. The vaccine was made available at cost to developing countries, saving millions of lives.
