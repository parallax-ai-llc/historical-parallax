## Summary

Enzo Ferrari founded Ferrari S.p.A. and Scuderia Ferrari, creating one of the most iconic luxury sports car and racing brands in history. A former racing driver, he established Scuderia Ferrari as a racing team in 1929 and began manufacturing cars under his own name in 1947. Ferrari became synonymous with Formula One racing, winning more Constructors' Championships than any other team. The prancing horse logo is one of the most recognized symbols in the world.
