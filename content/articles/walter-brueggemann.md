## Summary

Walter Brueggemann is an American Old Testament scholar and theologian whose work on the prophetic imagination has profoundly influenced preaching and theology. His book The Prophetic Imagination reinterpreted the prophetic tradition as a critique of dominant culture and an energizing of alternative community. He has written over 100 books, making complex biblical scholarship accessible to pastors and laypeople. He is one of the most influential biblical scholars of the modern era.
