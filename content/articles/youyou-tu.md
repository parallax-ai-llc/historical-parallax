## High-Level Significance

Tu Youyou is widely recognized for research that led to the discovery and development of artemisinin-based antimalarial therapies. These therapies became a major tool in reducing malaria mortality globally.

Key themes associated with her work include:

- Screening traditional medicine texts and remedies for candidate compounds.
- Adapting extraction methods to preserve active ingredients.
- Translating laboratory findings into clinically useful treatments.

## Artemisinin (Overview)

Artemisinin is derived from *Artemisia annua* (sweet wormwood). Modern malaria treatment commonly uses artemisinin derivatives in combination therapies to reduce parasite load and slow the development of resistance.
