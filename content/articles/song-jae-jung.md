## Summary

Song Jae-jung is a Korean radio astronomer who contributed to the establishment of Korean radio astronomy capabilities. His work with the Korean VLBI Network (KVN) has helped Korea participate in international radio astronomy projects, including contributions to the Event Horizon Telescope that captured the first image of a black hole.
