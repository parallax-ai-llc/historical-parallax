## Summary
Mohamed Mohamed el-Amir Awad el-Sayed Atta was an Egyptian hijacker who served as the ringleader of the 19 hijackers in the September 11 attacks. He piloted American Airlines Flight 11 into the North Tower of the World Trade Center. He was one of the most educated of the hijackers, holding degrees in architecture.
