## Summary

Sakshi Malik won Olympic wrestling bronze at 2016 Rio, the first Indian woman to win an Olympic wrestling medal.

## Olympic Achievement (2016)

- **Event:** Women's freestyle wrestling, 58 kg
- **Games:** 2016 Summer Olympics (Rio de Janeiro)
- **Result:** Bronze medal
- **Significance:** Widely noted as the **first Indian woman** to win an Olympic medal in wrestling.

## Style and Impact

- Competed in **women's freestyle** wrestling.
- Became a prominent figure in the growth and visibility of women's wrestling in India after her Rio 2016 podium finish.
- Often referenced alongside other Indian wrestlers as part of a broader rise in international results during the 2010s.
