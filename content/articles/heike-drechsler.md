## Summary

Heike Drechsler won Olympic long jump gold at both 1992 Barcelona and 2000 Sydney, 8 years apart.

## Additional Context

- Drechsler competed for East Germany early in her career and later represented unified Germany after reunification.
- She is primarily associated with the long jump, but she also competed at an elite level in the sprint and horizontal jump disciplines that often complement long-jump training.
- Winning Olympic gold medals eight years apart is rare in track and field and highlights long-term consistency across different Olympic cycles.
