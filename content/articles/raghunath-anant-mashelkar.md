## Summary

Raghunath Anant Mashelkar is a renowned Indian chemical engineer who served as Director General of the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) and transformed Indian industrial research. He successfully challenged US patents on turmeric and neem that threatened India's traditional knowledge. He has been influential in shaping India's innovation policy.
