## Summary

Émile Borel was a French mathematician who made important contributions to measure theory, probability theory, and game theory. He introduced the concept of Borel sets and Borel measures, fundamental to modern analysis and probability. He also pioneered game theory before von Neumann and served in the French government as Minister of the Navy.
