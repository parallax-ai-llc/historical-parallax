## Summary

Shankar Balasubramanian is a British chemist of Indian origin who co-invented Solexa sequencing (now Illumina next-generation sequencing), which has been used to generate over 90% of all DNA sequencing data worldwide. His technology made genome sequencing thousands of times cheaper and faster, enabling personalized medicine and genomic research.
