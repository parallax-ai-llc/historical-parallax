## Summary

Cuban exile and CIA operative who masterminded the 1976 bombing of Cubana Flight 455, killing 73 people. He also organized hotel bombings in Havana and plotted to assassinate Fidel Castro. Despite being wanted, he lived freely in the US until his death.
