## Summary

Wu Wenjun (Wu Wen-Tsun) made fundamental contributions to algebraic topology, particularly his Wu classes and Wu formula. He later pioneered the field of mathematics mechanization, developing computer methods for automated theorem proving in geometry. He received the first State Supreme Science and Technology Award of China in 2000.
