## Summary
Alphonse Gabriel 'Al' Capone was an American gangster who rose to infamy as the leader of the Chicago Outfit during Prohibition. He controlled vast illegal enterprises including bootlegging, gambling, and prostitution. He was finally convicted of tax evasion in 1931 and sentenced to 11 years in federal prison.
