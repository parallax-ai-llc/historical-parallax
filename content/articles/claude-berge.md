## Summary

Claude Berge was a French mathematician who made foundational contributions to combinatorics and graph theory. He developed the theory of hypergraphs and proved the perfect graph theorem conjecture partially. His books on graph theory and combinatorics became standard references. He was also a noted sculptor and art collector.
