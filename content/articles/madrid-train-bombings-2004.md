## Summary
A series of coordinated bomb attacks on commuter trains in Madrid on March 11, 2004, killed 193 people and injured approximately 2,000. It was the deadliest terrorist attack in Spanish history. The attacks were carried out by an al-Qaeda-inspired terrorist cell.
