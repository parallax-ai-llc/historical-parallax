## Summary

Indian diamantaire and business partner of Nirav Modi involved in the $2 billion Punjab National Bank fraud. He fled to Antigua and was later found in Dominica under mysterious circumstances.

## Background

The Punjab National Bank (PNB) case became one of India’s most widely reported banking-fraud scandals. Coverage typically focuses on alleged misuse of banking instruments and weaknesses in internal controls.

## What was alleged (high-level)

- The scandal centered on fraudulent guarantees/undertakings used to obtain financing.
- Investigations described prolonged concealment and failures of oversight.

## Legal and diplomatic dimensions

- The case involved cross-border litigation, immigration status, and extradition-related disputes.
- Competing narratives have appeared in public reporting regarding his travel, detention, and claims about coercion.

## Broader significance

- The case is frequently cited in discussions about bank compliance, audit practices, and governance reforms.
- It also illustrates how high-value financial crimes can create long-running international legal processes.
