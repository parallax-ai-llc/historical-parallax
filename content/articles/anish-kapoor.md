## Summary

Anish Kapoor is an Indian-born British sculptor known for works exploring form, color, and void. His Cloud Gate (The Bean) in Chicago is one of the world's most popular public sculptures. His works often feature deep pigments, reflective surfaces, and mysterious voids that challenge spatial perception.
