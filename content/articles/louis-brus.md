## Summary

Louis Brus is an American chemist who independently discovered colloidal quantum dots in solution and demonstrated quantum size effects in semiconductor nanocrystals. Working at Bell Labs, he developed the theoretical framework for understanding quantum confinement in nanoparticles. He shared the 2023 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for quantum dot research.
