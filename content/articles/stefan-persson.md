## Summary

Stefan Persson served as chairman and CEO of H&M, building it from a Swedish retailer into the world's second-largest fashion chain. He succeeded his father Erling Persson, who founded H&M in 1947. Under his leadership, H&M expanded globally with over 4,700 stores in 75 countries. He is the wealthiest person in Sweden and one of the largest individual landowners.
