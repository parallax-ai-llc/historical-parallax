## Summary

Andrew Beal is the founder of Beal Financial Corporation and Beal Bank, one of the largest privately held banks in the United States. He made his fortune by buying distressed debt and loans at deep discounts during financial crises. His countercyclical investment strategy — buying when others panic — has made him one of the wealthiest people in Texas. He is also known as a serious poker player who challenged the best professionals.
