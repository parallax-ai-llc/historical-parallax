## Summary

Nicol David was the world number one in squash for a record nine consecutive years and won eight World Championship titles. She is the greatest female squash player in history.

## Additional Context

- David is widely recognized for sustained dominance in women’s squash during the 2000s and 2010s.
- Her career is often cited as an example of long-term consistency at the elite level, not only peak performance.

## Playing Style (General)

- Known for speed, endurance, and court coverage.
- Emphasized rally construction and retrieving, forcing opponents into long exchanges.

## Legacy

- Served as a prominent sports figure in Malaysia and an international ambassador for squash.
- Records associated with her world ranking and world titles are frequently used as benchmarks in the sport.
