## Summary

Sau Lan Wu is a particle physicist who played key roles in three major discoveries: the J/psi particle (1974), the gluon (1979), and the Higgs boson (2012). She has been one of the leading experimental physicists at CERN and was instrumental in confirming the Standard Model. She overcame significant gender and cultural barriers in physics.
