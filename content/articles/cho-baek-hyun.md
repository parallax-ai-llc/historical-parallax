## Summary

Cho Baek-hyun was one of Korea's first modern chemists and a pioneer of chemistry education in Korea. He studied chemistry in Japan and Germany before returning to Korea to establish chemistry departments at Korean universities. He published the first Korean-language chemistry textbooks and trained the first generation of Korean chemists.
