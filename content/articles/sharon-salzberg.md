## Summary

Sharon Salzberg is an American author and teacher of Buddhist meditation who is one of the founders of the Insight Meditation Society. She has been instrumental in bringing the practice of metta (lovingkindness) meditation to the West. Her books, including Lovingkindness and Real Happiness, have introduced millions to meditation practice. She studied in India under S. N. Goenka and Dipa Ma among others.
