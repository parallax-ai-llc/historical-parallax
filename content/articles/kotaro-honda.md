## Summary

Kotaro Honda was a pioneer of Japanese metallurgy who developed KS steel (the strongest permanent magnet steel of its era) and New KS steel. His research on magnetic materials advanced both scientific understanding and industrial applications of steel alloys. He established Japan as a leader in metallurgical research.
