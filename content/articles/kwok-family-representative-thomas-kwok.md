## Summary

The Kwok family controls Sun Hung Kai Properties, Hong Kong's largest property developer by market capitalization. Thomas Kwok served as co-chairman until his conviction for bribery in 2014. Sun Hung Kai Properties developed the International Commerce Centre, Hong Kong's tallest building. The family has been one of the wealthiest in Hong Kong for decades through their massive real estate holdings.
