## Summary

Nee Win Maung was a Myanmar physician and public health scientist who established HIV/AIDS treatment programs in Myanmar during a period of political isolation. He founded the Myanmar Medical Association's HIV treatment program and trained hundreds of health workers to provide antiretroviral therapy across the country.
