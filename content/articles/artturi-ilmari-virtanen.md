## Summary

Artturi Ilmari Virtanen received the 1945 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his research and inventions in agricultural and nutrition chemistry. He developed the AIV system for preserving green fodder through acidification, which improved livestock nutrition. His work on nitrogen fixation by leguminous plants advanced both chemistry and agriculture.
