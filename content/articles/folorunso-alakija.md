## Summary

Folorunso Alakija is a Nigerian billionaire businesswoman, fashion designer, and one of the richest women in Africa. She owns a 60% stake in Famfa Oil, which has an oil prospecting license for a deep-water block containing over 1 billion barrels of oil. She started as a fashion designer before entering the oil industry. She was temporarily the richest Black woman in the world.
