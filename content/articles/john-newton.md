## Summary
John Newton was a British slave trader who later became an Anglican clergyman and abolitionist. He captained slave ships before a religious conversion led him to renounce the trade. He wrote the hymn 'Amazing Grace.' His testimony was influential in the passage of the Slave Trade Act of 1807.
