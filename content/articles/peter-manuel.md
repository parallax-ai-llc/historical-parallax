## Summary
Peter Thomas Anthony Manuel was a Scottish-American serial killer who murdered at least eight people in Lanarkshire and possibly more in England between 1956 and 1958. Known as 'The Beast of Birkenshaw,' he was the third-to-last person executed in Scotland.
