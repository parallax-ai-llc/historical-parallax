## Summary

Frank Lloyd Wright was the most influential American architect of the 20th century, known for organic architecture that harmonizes with nature. His masterpiece Fallingwater, cantilevered over a waterfall, is considered the greatest American building. The Guggenheim Museum in New York was his final major work.
