## Summary

Andrew Tan is a Filipino billionaire and founder of Megaworld Corporation, one of the Philippines' largest real estate companies known for building integrated urban townships. He also owns Emperador Inc., the world's largest brandy producer. His Alliance Global Group is one of the Philippines' largest conglomerates. His township developments, including Eastwood City, pioneered the live-work-play concept in the Philippines.
