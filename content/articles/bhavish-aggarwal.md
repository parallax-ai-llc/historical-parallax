## Summary

Bhavish Aggarwal co-founded Ola, India's largest ride-hailing platform, and later founded Ola Electric, which became India's largest electric two-wheeler manufacturer. He also launched Krutrim, India's first AI unicorn. Ola disrupted Indian transportation and competed fiercely with Uber in the Indian market. His ambitious expansion into electric vehicles and AI represents India's growing tech entrepreneurship.
