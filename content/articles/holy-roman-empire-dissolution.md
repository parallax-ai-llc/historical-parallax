## Summary

The Holy Roman Empire was dissolved on August 6, 1806, when Emperor Francis II abdicated under pressure from Napoleon after the Battle of Austerlitz. The empire, which Voltaire quipped was 'neither holy, nor Roman, nor an empire,' had existed for over 800 years.
