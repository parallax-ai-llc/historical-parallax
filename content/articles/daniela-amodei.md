## Summary

Daniela Amodei is the co-founder and president of Anthropic. She previously served as VP of Operations at OpenAI and VP of Safety & Policy at Stripe. At Anthropic, she oversees business operations, go-to-market strategy, finance, and security. She and her brother Dario represent a new generation of AI entrepreneurs focused on responsible AI development while building commercially successful products.
