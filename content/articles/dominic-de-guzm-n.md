## Summary

Dominic de Guzmán founded the Dominican Order (Order of Preachers) dedicated to preaching and combating heresy through education. He pioneered a new form of religious life combining contemplation with active ministry. The Dominican Order became one of the most important intellectual forces in medieval Christianity. Dominic emphasized poverty, study, and preaching as the cornerstones of religious life.
