## High-level profile (verified, non-exhaustive)

- **Ma Huateng (Pony Ma)** is a Chinese entrepreneur and business executive best known as a **co-founder of Tencent** (1998), a major technology company headquartered in Shenzhen.[^2][^3]
- He has served in top executive roles at Tencent, including **chairman and CEO** (as widely reported in public profiles).[^2]
- Tencent is widely known for large-scale consumer internet products and platforms, including **QQ** and **WeChat/Weixin**, and for a major presence in **video games** and digital services.[^2][^3]

## Public milestones (high-level)

- **1998**: Tencent founded (co-founded by Ma Huateng and partners).[^2]
- **Late 1990s–2000s**: QQ grows into a dominant instant messaging platform in China (often cited as a foundational Tencent product).[^2]
- **2011**: WeChat (Weixin) launched, later becoming one of the world’s most widely used messaging apps.[^2]
