## Summary

Oscar Niemeyer was a Brazilian architect who was a pioneer of modernist architecture in Brazil and the principal designer of Brasilia. His sensuous, curving concrete buildings broke with the rigid geometry of International Style modernism. His works include the Cathedral of Brasilia, the National Congress, and the Niteroi Contemporary Art Museum.
