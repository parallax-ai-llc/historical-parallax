## Summary

Grote Reber built the first parabolic radio telescope in his backyard in 1937 and was the first person to systematically survey the radio sky. For several years he was the only radio astronomer in the world. His pioneering radio observations opened an entirely new window on the universe and founded the field of radio astronomy.
