## Summary

Barton W. Stone was an American frontier minister who co-led the Cane Ridge Revival of 1801, one of the most important events in American religious history. He broke from Presbyterianism to found the Christian movement seeking to restore New Testament Christianity. His movement merged with Alexander Campbell's to form the Stone-Campbell Restoration Movement. He emphasized Christian unity and the authority of Scripture alone.
