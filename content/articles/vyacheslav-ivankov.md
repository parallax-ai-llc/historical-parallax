## Summary
Vyacheslav Kirillovich Ivankov, known as 'Yaponchik' (The Little Japanese), was a Russian crime boss who expanded Russian organized crime into the United States. He was arrested in Brooklyn in 1995 and convicted of extortion. After deportation, he was shot in Moscow in 2009 and died.
