## Summary

Tyeb Mehta was an Indian painter known for his powerful, minimalist figurative paintings featuring diagonal lines and isolated human forms. A member of the Progressive Artists' Group, his work addresses violence, displacement, and the human condition. His painting Celebration sold for a record price in Indian art.
