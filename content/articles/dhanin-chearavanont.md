## Summary

Dhanin Chearavanont is the senior chairman of Charoen Pokphand Group (CP Group), the largest conglomerate in Thailand. CP Group operates in agribusiness, retail (7-Eleven Thailand), telecommunications (True Corporation), and property. He expanded the company globally with major investments in China, where CP Group was one of the first foreign investors. He is one of the most influential business figures in Southeast Asia.
