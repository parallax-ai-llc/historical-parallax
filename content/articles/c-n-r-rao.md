## Summary

C. N. R. Rao is an Indian chemist and materials scientist who has made significant contributions to solid-state chemistry, materials science, and nanomaterials. He is one of the most prolific chemistry researchers in the world, having published over 1,700 papers. He received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor, and has been a key figure in Indian scientific development.
