## Summary

Hyun-Chul Kim is a Korean roboticist at KAIST who has contributed to the development of humanoid robots and robotic systems for disaster response. His work on the HUBO humanoid robot helped Korea win the DARPA Robotics Challenge in 2015, demonstrating Korea's capabilities in advanced robotics.
