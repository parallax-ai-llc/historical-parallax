## Summary
K.D. Kempamma, known as 'Cyanide Mallika,' was an Indian serial killer who poisoned at least three women in Karnataka and Kerala between 1999 and 2007. She befriended women at temples, gained their trust, and poisoned them with cyanide to steal their jewelry. She was sentenced to death.
