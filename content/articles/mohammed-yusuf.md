## Summary
Mohammed Yusuf was the founder of Boko Haram, a jihadist militant organization in northeastern Nigeria. He advocated for strict Islamic law and opposed Western education. He was captured and killed extrajudicially by Nigerian police in 2009. His death radicalized Boko Haram under Abubakar Shekau.
