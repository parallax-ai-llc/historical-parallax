## Summary

Mira Schendel was a Swiss-born Brazilian artist whose delicate works on rice paper and transparent materials explore language, space, and the void. Her Monotipias (monotypes) of single letters and marks float between drawing, writing, and poetry. She is considered one of the most important Brazilian artists of the 20th century.
