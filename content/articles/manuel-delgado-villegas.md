## Summary
Manuel Delgado Villegas, known as 'El Arropiero,' was Spain's most prolific serial killer. He confessed to 48 murders across Spain, France, and Italy between 1964 and 1971. Diagnosed as a psychopath, he was confined to a psychiatric institution and died there in 1998.
