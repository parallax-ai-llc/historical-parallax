## Summary

Wu Guanzhong was a Chinese painter who pioneered the fusion of Western and Chinese painting techniques. His abstract landscapes combine the spirit of traditional Chinese ink painting with Western modernist composition and color. He advocated that formal beauty and abstraction are not Western monopolies but universal artistic values.
