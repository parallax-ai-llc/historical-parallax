## Summary

Kawamoto Komin was a Japanese physician and scholar of Western sciences (rangaku) who wrote the Kikai Kanran Kogi (Outline of Physical Science), one of the first Japanese texts on Western physics and chemistry. He translated and adapted Western scientific works and experimented with photography, telegraphy, and chemistry.
