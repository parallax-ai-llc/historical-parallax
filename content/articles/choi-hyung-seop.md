## Summary

Choi Hyung-seop was a Korean materials engineer who contributed to the development of POSCO's steelmaking technology. His metallurgical research on steel alloys and production processes helped POSCO become the world's most efficient steel producer, establishing Korea as a global leader in steel technology.
