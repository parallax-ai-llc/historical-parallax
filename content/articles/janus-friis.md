## Summary

Janus Friis co-founded Skype and Kazaa with Niklas Zennström, revolutionizing internet communications. He also co-founded Joost, an internet TV service, and Vdio, a video streaming service. His work on peer-to-peer technology and VoIP fundamentally changed how the world communicates. Skype's eventual $8.5 billion acquisition by Microsoft was one of the largest tech deals of its era.
