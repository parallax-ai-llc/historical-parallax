## Summary

British trader who was the first person convicted for manipulating LIBOR, the global interest rate benchmark. His manipulation affected trillions of dollars in financial products worldwide. Initially sentenced to 14 years, reduced to 11.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2006 | Began manipulating LIBOR rates at UBS and Citigroup |
| 2012 | Arrested |
| 2015 | Convicted; sentenced to 14 years, reduced to 11 |
| 2021 | Released from prison |
