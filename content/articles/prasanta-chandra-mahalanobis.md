## Summary

Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis developed the Mahalanobis distance, a statistical measure widely used in cluster analysis and classification. He founded the Indian Statistical Institute and the journal Sankhya. He was also the architect of India's second five-year plan and pioneered the use of large-scale sample surveys.
