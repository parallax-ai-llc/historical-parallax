## Summary

Ian Wilmut led the team that created Dolly the sheep in 1996, the first mammal cloned from an adult somatic cell. This breakthrough demonstrated that cell specialization was reversible and opened new possibilities in regenerative medicine. His work raised profound ethical questions about cloning and reproductive technology.
