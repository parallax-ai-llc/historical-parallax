## Summary

T. Boone Pickens was an American business magnate and financier who founded Mesa Petroleum and became one of the most prominent corporate raiders of the 1980s. He pioneered shareholder activism by launching hostile takeover bids for major oil companies. Later, he became an advocate for alternative energy and the 'Pickens Plan' for reducing U.S. dependence on foreign oil. He was also a major philanthropist, donating over $1 billion primarily to Oklahoma State University.
