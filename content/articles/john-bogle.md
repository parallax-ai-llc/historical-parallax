## Summary

John Bogle founded The Vanguard Group in 1975 and created the first index mutual fund available to individual investors. His philosophy of low-cost, passive investing transformed the asset management industry and saved investors hundreds of billions in fees. The Vanguard Group grew to manage over $7 trillion in assets. He was often called 'the father of index investing' and advocated tirelessly for individual investor interests.
