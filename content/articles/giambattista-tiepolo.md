## Summary

Giambattista Tiepolo was the greatest Italian painter of the 18th century and last great master of the Venetian decorative tradition. His luminous, soaring ceiling frescoes represent the pinnacle of Rococo illusionism. The Wurzburg Residence staircase ceiling is the largest fresco in the world.
