## Summary

Wumen Huikai was a Chinese Chan master who compiled the Wumenguan (Gateless Gate or Mumonkan), one of the most important koan collections in Zen Buddhism. The collection contains 48 koans with Wumen's commentaries and verses. He spent six years meditating on the koan 'Mu' before achieving enlightenment. The Mumonkan remains the primary koan collection used in Rinzai Zen training.
