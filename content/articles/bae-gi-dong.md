## Summary

Bae Gi-dong is a Korean archaeologist who discovered and excavated numerous prehistoric and ancient sites across the Korean Peninsula. His archaeological research has expanded understanding of Korea's Bronze Age and Iron Age civilizations and their technological achievements, including metalworking and pottery.
