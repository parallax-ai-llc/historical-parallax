## Summary

Homi Sethna was an Indian chemical engineer who served as chairman of the Atomic Energy Commission of India. He led the development of plutonium reprocessing technology and played a key role in India's first nuclear test (Smiling Buddha) in 1974. His chemical engineering expertise was crucial for India's nuclear fuel cycle.
