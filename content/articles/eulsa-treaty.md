## Summary

The Eulsa Treaty (Japan-Korea Protectorate Treaty) of November 17, 1905, stripped Korea of its diplomatic sovereignty, making it a Japanese protectorate. The treaty was signed under military coercion, and five Korean ministers who signed became known as the 'Eulsa Five Traitors.'
