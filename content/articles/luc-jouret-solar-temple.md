## Summary

Congolese-born Belgian cult leader who co-founded the Order of the Solar Temple. Mass murder-suicides linked to the cult killed 74 members across Switzerland, France, and Canada between 1994 and 1997.

## Background

- Luc Jouret is commonly described as a charismatic lecturer associated with New Age and esoteric currents in Europe in the late 20th century.
- He co-founded the **Order of the Solar Temple (OTS)** with Joseph Di Mambro; accounts emphasize a mix of ritual, hierarchy, and promises of spiritual transformation.

## The Order of the Solar Temple (High-Level)

- Public summaries often describe OTS beliefs as drawing on esoteric Christianity, Templar imagery, and apocalyptic narratives.
- Groups of this type can be difficult to characterize from the outside; reliable descriptions typically come from: court proceedings, investigative journalism, and scholarly study of new religious movements.

## Deaths Associated With OTS

- Between 1994 and 1997, a series of deaths linked to the OTS occurred in **Switzerland, France, and Canada**.
- Many reports describe these events as involving both **homicide and suicide**, with complex staging and disputed consent in some cases.
