## Summary

Rahmi Koç is the honorary chairman of Koç Holding, Turkey's largest industrial conglomerate. The Koç family built an empire spanning automotive (Ford Otosan, Tofas), banking (Yapı Kredi), energy (Tüpraş), and consumer goods (Arçelik). Founded by his father Vehbi Koç, the group represents Turkey's most successful business dynasty. Rahmi is also known for founding industrial museums in Istanbul.
