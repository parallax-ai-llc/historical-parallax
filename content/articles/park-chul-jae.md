## Summary

Park Chul-jae was a pioneering Korean nuclear physicist who contributed to the development of South Korea's nuclear energy program. He helped establish the Korea Atomic Energy Research Institute (KAERI) and trained a generation of Korean nuclear scientists. His work was instrumental in Korea becoming a major nuclear power technology exporter.
