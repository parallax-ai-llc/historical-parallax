## Summary

Wilder Penfield created the famous cortical homunculus map of the brain by electrically stimulating the brains of conscious patients during epilepsy surgery. His mapping revealed which brain areas control specific body parts, emotions, and memories. He founded the Montreal Neurological Institute and pioneered surgical treatment of epilepsy.
