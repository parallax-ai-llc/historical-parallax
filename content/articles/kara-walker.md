## Summary

Kara Walker is an American artist known for her large-scale silhouette installations exploring race, gender, sexuality, and violence in American history. Her room-sized black paper cutouts depict antebellum plantation scenes with unflinching, provocative imagery. Her sugar sphinx installation A Subtlety was one of the most talked-about artworks of 2014.
