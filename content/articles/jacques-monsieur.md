## Summary

Belgian arms dealer who illegally sold weapons to multiple conflict zones including Iran, Libya, and Pakistan. He was one of Europe's most prolific illegal arms brokers for over two decades.

## Activities and Network

Monsieur is described in reporting as a broker who facilitated weapons transfers across borders, using intermediaries, front companies, and shifting jurisdictions to move goods and payments. Such networks often exploit gaps between national export-control regimes and the pace of enforcement.

## Arrest, Trial, and Sentencing

After years of investigation, he was arrested in France in 2007. The legal proceedings that followed focused on unauthorized arms dealing and related offenses. A conviction in 2009 reflected the scale and duration of the alleged trafficking activity.
