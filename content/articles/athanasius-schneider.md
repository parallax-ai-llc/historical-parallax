## Summary

Athanasius Schneider is a Catholic bishop who serves as Auxiliary Bishop of the Archdiocese of Saint Mary in Astana, Kazakhstan. He is one of the most prominent traditionalist voices in the contemporary Catholic Church. He has been vocal in defending traditional Catholic liturgy and doctrine, particularly regarding the reception of Holy Communion. His book Dominus Est advocated for the reception of Communion on the tongue while kneeling.
