## Summary

Clemens von Pirquet coined the terms 'allergy' and 'serum sickness' and developed the tuberculin skin test (Pirquet test) for tuberculosis diagnosis. He recognized that the immune system could cause disease as well as protection. His concepts laid the foundation for modern allergy and immunology research.
