## Summary

Adyashanti (Steven Gray) is an American spiritual teacher in the Zen tradition who teaches from his own experience of awakening. He studied Zen for 14 years before experiencing a series of realizations. His books, including The End of Your World and Emptiness Dancing, present non-dual awakening in accessible language. He founded Open Gate Sangha and teaches a blend of Zen, Advaita, and Christian mysticism.
