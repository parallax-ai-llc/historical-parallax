## Summary

Tony Fadell is known as the 'father of the iPod' for leading its development at Apple. He later co-founded Nest Labs, which Google acquired for $3.2 billion in 2014. At Apple, he oversaw the first three generations of the iPhone as well. He is now an active investor and advisor in climate technology. His book 'Build' became a guide for product designers and entrepreneurs.
