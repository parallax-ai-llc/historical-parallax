## Summary

Mukomuko Sahli was an Indonesian agricultural scientist who contributed to the development of improved rice varieties for tropical Asia. His work on pest-resistant rice cultivars helped improve food security in Southeast Asia. He collaborated with the International Rice Research Institute on multiple research projects.
