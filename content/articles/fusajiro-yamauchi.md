## Summary

Fusajiro Yamauchi founded Nintendo in 1889 as a handmade playing card company in Kyoto. He began producing hanafuda (flower cards) when Western playing cards were less popular in Japan. His company grew into the largest playing card company in Japan. He laid the foundation for what would become one of the world's most important entertainment companies.
