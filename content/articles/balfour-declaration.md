## Summary

The Balfour Declaration of November 2, 1917, was a British government statement supporting 'a national home for the Jewish people' in Palestine. The 67-word letter profoundly shaped the Middle East, contributing to both the creation of Israel and the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
