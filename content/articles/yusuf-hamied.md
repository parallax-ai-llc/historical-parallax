## Summary

Yusuf Hamied is the chairman of Cipla, one of India's largest pharmaceutical companies known for making affordable generic medicines. He dramatically reduced the cost of AIDS treatment from $12,000 to $350 per year per patient, saving millions of lives in developing countries. His decision to produce generic antiretroviral drugs challenged major pharmaceutical companies and global patent regimes. He is regarded as one of the most important figures in global public health.
