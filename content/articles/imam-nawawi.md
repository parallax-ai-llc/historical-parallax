## Summary

Imam Nawawi was a Syrian Islamic scholar of the Shafi'i school who compiled some of the most widely read Islamic texts. His Forty Hadith collection and Riyad al-Salihin (Gardens of the Righteous) are among the most popular hadith compilations in the Muslim world. His commentary on Sahih Muslim remains the standard reference. Despite dying at only 44, his scholarly output was extraordinary.
