## Summary

Athanasius was the 20th Bishop of Alexandria and a central figure in the Arian controversy. He was the chief defender of Trinitarian theology against Arianism at the Council of Nicaea. He was exiled five times by various Roman emperors for his theological stance. His work 'On the Incarnation' remains a classic of Christian theology.
