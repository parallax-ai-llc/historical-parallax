## Summary

Hyun Sung-won is the chairman of Hyosung Group, one of South Korea's leading industrial conglomerates specializing in chemicals, heavy industries, and advanced materials. Hyosung is the world's largest producer of spandex and a leader in transformer manufacturing. The company has expanded into hydrogen fuel cell components and green energy technology. Hyosung operates in over 40 countries worldwide.
