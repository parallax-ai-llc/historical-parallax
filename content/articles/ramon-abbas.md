## Summary
Ramon Olorunwa Abbas, known as 'Hushpuppi,' was a Nigerian social media personality and fraudster who orchestrated international fraud schemes worth hundreds of millions of dollars. He was arrested in Dubai in 2020 and extradited to the US. He was sentenced to 11 years in 2022.
