## Summary

Gong Yu is the founder and CEO of iQIYI, one of China's largest video streaming platforms often called 'China's Netflix.' He founded the company as a joint venture with Baidu in 2010. iQIYI went public on NASDAQ in 2018 and has produced numerous original series and films. He has been a pioneer in bringing premium content to Chinese online audiences and developing China's entertainment streaming industry.
