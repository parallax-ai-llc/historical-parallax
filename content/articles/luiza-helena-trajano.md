## Summary

Luiza Helena Trajano built Magazine Luiza from a small retail chain in Brazil's interior into one of the country's largest and most innovative retailers. She pioneered e-commerce and digital transformation in Brazilian retail. Magazine Luiza's marketplace became one of Brazil's largest e-commerce platforms. She is considered one of the most influential businesswomen in Latin America.
