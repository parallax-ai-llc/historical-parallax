## Summary

Yitang Zhang is a Chinese-American mathematician who proved the first finite bound on gaps between prime numbers in 2013, a major breakthrough in number theory. Working as a relatively unknown lecturer at the University of New Hampshire, his proof that there are infinitely many pairs of primes within 70 million of each other shocked the mathematical world.
