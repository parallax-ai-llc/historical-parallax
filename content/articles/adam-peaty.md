## Additional Context

### Athletic Profile

Adam Peaty is a British competitive swimmer best known for sprint breaststroke events. He rose to prominence in the 2010s, setting multiple world records and becoming one of the dominant performers in the 100m breaststroke.

### Olympic and World Championship Success

He won Olympic gold in the men’s 100m breaststroke at the 2016 Rio Games and earned additional medals across major championships. Public records emphasize his consistency in international finals and record-setting performances.

### Why He’s Notable

Peaty is often cited in discussions of modern swimming for combining elite speed with efficient breaststroke technique, and for sustaining top performance across multiple seasons.
