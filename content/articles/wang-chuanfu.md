## Summary

Wang Chuanfu is the founder and chairman of BYD Company, which has become the world's largest electric vehicle manufacturer by sales, surpassing Tesla. He started BYD as a battery manufacturer in 1995 and expanded into automobiles in 2003. Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway invested in BYD in 2008. BYD produces EVs, batteries, and electronic components, and has become a symbol of China's dominance in the global EV market.
