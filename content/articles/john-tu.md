## Summary

John Tu co-founded Kingston Technology, the world's largest independent manufacturer of memory products. He and co-founder David Sun started the company in 1987, and it grew into a $14 billion revenue company. Kingston produces DRAM modules, USB drives, and SSDs used in computers worldwide. In 1996, SoftBank acquired 80% of Kingston for $1.5 billion, making Tu and Sun among the wealthiest tech entrepreneurs. They later repurchased the company.
