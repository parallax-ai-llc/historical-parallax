## Summary

Ted Turner founded CNN, the first 24-hour cable news channel, and built Turner Broadcasting System into a media empire. He also founded TBS, TNT, and the Cartoon Network. He owned the Atlanta Braves and the Atlanta Hawks, and won the America's Cup yacht race. In 1997, he pledged $1 billion to the United Nations, one of the largest charitable gifts in history.
