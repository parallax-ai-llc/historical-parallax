## Summary

Geoffrey Hinton is a British-Canadian cognitive psychologist and computer scientist known as one of the 'godfathers of deep learning.' His work on artificial neural networks, particularly backpropagation and deep learning techniques, revolutionized artificial intelligence. He shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Physics for foundational discoveries enabling machine learning with artificial neural networks. Hinton left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risks.
