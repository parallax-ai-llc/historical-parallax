## Summary

John Cockcroft and Ernest Walton first split the atom on April 14, 1932, at Cambridge University. They accelerated protons to break apart lithium nuclei, confirming Einstein's mass-energy equivalence. The achievement opened the door to nuclear energy and weapons.
