## Summary

Javad Nurbakhsh was an Iranian Sufi master who served as the head (Qutb) of the Nimatullahi Sufi order from 1953 until his death. He was also a trained psychiatrist and professor of psychiatry at Tehran University. After the Iranian Revolution, he moved to London and established Nimatullahi khanaqahs (Sufi centers) across Europe and America. He wrote extensively on Sufi psychology, symbolism, and practice.
