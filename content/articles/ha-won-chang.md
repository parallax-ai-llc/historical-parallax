## Summary

Ha-Won Chang is a Korean-American chemist who made significant contributions to polymer chemistry and materials science. His work on conducting polymers and electrochemical systems has applications in batteries, fuel cells, and electronic devices. He is considered one of the pioneering Korean scientists in materials chemistry.
