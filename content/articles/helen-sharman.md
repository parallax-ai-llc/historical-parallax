## Summary

Helen Sharman became the first British astronaut and the first Western European woman to visit a space station when she flew to the Mir space station in 1991 aboard Soyuz TM-12. A chemist by training, she was selected through a public competition from over 13,000 applicants. She spent 8 days conducting scientific experiments aboard Mir.
