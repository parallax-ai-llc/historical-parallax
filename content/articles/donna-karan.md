## Summary

Donna Karan founded Donna Karan International and the DKNY brand, becoming one of the most influential American fashion designers. Her 'Seven Easy Pieces' collection for working women revolutionized women's professional wardrobes. She became chief designer at Anne Klein at 26 and later built her own global brand. LVMH acquired her company in 2001, and she continues to work in fashion and wellness.
