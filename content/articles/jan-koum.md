## Summary

Jan Koum co-founded WhatsApp, the messaging platform that became the world's most widely used messaging app. Born in Kyiv, Ukraine, he immigrated to the United States at age 16. WhatsApp was acquired by Facebook for $19 billion in 2014, the largest acquisition of a venture-backed company in history. He departed Facebook's board in 2018 over disagreements about data privacy and encryption.
