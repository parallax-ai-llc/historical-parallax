## Summary

Kim Yeon-su was a Korean industrialist who founded what became the Samyang Group, Korea's first modern industrial conglomerate. He established Korea's first sugar refinery and textile factory using modern technology. His application of industrial technology to Korean manufacturing helped lay the groundwork for Korea's later industrialization.
