## Summary

Jessica Alba co-founded The Honest Company in 2011, a consumer goods company focused on non-toxic household and baby products. The company went public in 2021 at a $1.4 billion valuation. Despite facing lawsuits over marketing claims and stock price declines, The Honest Company became a significant player in the natural products market. She demonstrated that celebrity-founded consumer brands could achieve substantial scale.
