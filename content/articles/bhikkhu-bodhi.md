## Summary

Bhikkhu Bodhi is an American Theravada Buddhist monk and scholar who is considered the foremost English-language translator of the Pali Canon. His translations of the Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, and Anguttara Nikaya are the standard English editions. He served as president of the Buddhist Publication Society in Sri Lanka. He also founded Buddhist Global Relief, addressing global hunger and poverty through a Buddhist lens.
