## Summary

Lee Ufan is a Korean-Japanese artist and philosopher who is a leading figure of the Mono-ha (School of Things) movement. His art systematically explores the relationship between materials, space, and human perception, applying philosophical analysis that parallels scientific inquiry into the nature of matter and consciousness.
