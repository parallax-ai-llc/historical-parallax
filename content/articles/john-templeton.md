## Summary

John Templeton was a pioneering global investor who founded the Templeton Growth Fund and is considered the creator of the modern globally diversified mutual fund. He famously invested in Japan and other markets before they became popular. He was knighted in 1987 for his philanthropy. The Templeton Prize, awarded for progress in religion and spirituality, is the world's largest annual award ($1.5 million).
