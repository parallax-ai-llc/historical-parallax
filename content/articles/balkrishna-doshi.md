## Summary

Balkrishna Doshi was an Indian architect who worked with Le Corbusier and Louis Kahn before developing a distinctive architecture rooted in Indian culture and climate. His Aranya low-cost housing and the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore are celebrated works. He received the Pritzker Prize in 2018 at age 90.
