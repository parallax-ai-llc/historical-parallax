## Summary

The Great Kant≈ç Earthquake on September 1, 1923, devastated Tokyo and Yokohama with a magnitude 7.9 tremor. Fires destroyed most of Tokyo. Over 105,000 people died. The disaster led to major urban planning reforms and tragically sparked violence against Korean residents.
