## Summary
On October 1, 2013, the FBI shut down Silk Road, the world's first major dark web marketplace, and arrested its founder Ross Ulbricht. The site had facilitated $1.2 billion in transactions, mostly for illegal drugs. Ulbricht was sentenced to life without parole.
