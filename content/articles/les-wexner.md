Leslie "Les" Wexner is an American businessman who founded L Brands (formerly Limited Brands), the parent company of Victoria's Secret and Bath & Body Works. He had an extensive financial and personal relationship with Jeffrey Epstein.

## Background

Wexner built a retail empire and became one of America's wealthiest individuals. For decades, Epstein was Wexner's financial manager and confidant.

## Relationship with Epstein

Wexner's relationship with Epstein was unique and extensive:

**Financial Management**: Epstein managed Wexner's personal finances and had power of attorney
**Property Transfers**: Wexner transferred his Manhattan townhouse to Epstein for nominal sums
**Close Association**: The relationship lasted from the 1980s until approximately 2007

## Questions and Controversy

The relationship has raised numerous questions:
- How Epstein gained such intimate access to Wexner's finances
- Why Wexner gave Epstein such unprecedented control
- What Wexner knew about Epstein's activities

## Wexner's Response

In 2019, after Epstein's arrest, Wexner stated:
- He was "sick" over Epstein's crimes
- He was unaware of Epstein's illegal activities
- Epstein had "misappropriated" funds
- The relationship ended years earlier

## Impact

The scandal contributed to:
- Wexner stepping down as L Brands CEO in 2020
- Reputational damage to Victoria's Secret
- Questions about oversight and due diligence
