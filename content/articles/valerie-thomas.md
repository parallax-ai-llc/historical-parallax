## Summary

Valerie Thomas invented the illusion transmitter in 1980, which uses concave mirrors to create three-dimensional illusion images that can be transmitted to remote locations. Her NASA patent was ahead of its time and anticipated modern 3D display technology. She also managed NASA's Landsat image processing system.
