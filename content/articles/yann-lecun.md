## Summary

Yann LeCun is a French-American computer scientist known as one of the pioneers of convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and deep learning. His work on handwriting recognition and image classification laid the foundation for modern computer vision. He is VP and Chief AI Scientist at Meta (Facebook) and a professor at NYU. He shared the 2018 Turing Award with Hinton and Bengio for their work on deep learning.
