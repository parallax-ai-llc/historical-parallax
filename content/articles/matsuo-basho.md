## Summary

Matsuo Basho was the greatest master of haiku and the most famous poet in Japanese literature. His travel diary The Narrow Road to the Deep North is a masterpiece of Japanese prose and poetry. He elevated the haiku from a witty amusement to a profound art form capturing the essence of a moment.
