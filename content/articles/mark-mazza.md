## Summary

Capitol rioter who brought a loaded gun inside the U.S. Capitol on January 6, 2021, and later reported that the weapon was lost during the chaos.

## Key Facts

- **Event**: January 6, 2021 attack on the U.S. Capitol
- **Allegation**: Entered the Capitol while armed with a loaded firearm
- **Outcome**: Convicted and sentenced in federal court (reported in 2023)

## Background

Public reporting on Mazza primarily centers on his involvement in the Capitol attack and subsequent federal prosecution.

## January 6, 2021 (High-Level Account)

- Participated in the crowd actions that led to a breach of the U.S. Capitol.
- According to court reporting summarized in secondary sources, a loaded gun was brought into the building and was later no longer in his possession.

## Legal Case (High-Level)

- Prosecuted in U.S. federal court as part of the broader set of January 6 cases.
- The case is commonly cited as an example of the risks associated with weapons being present in a dense, violent crowd scenario.
