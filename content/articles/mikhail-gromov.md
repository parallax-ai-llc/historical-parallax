## Summary

Mikhail Gromov is a Russian-French mathematician who has made groundbreaking contributions to geometry. He revolutionized multiple areas of mathematics including Riemannian geometry, symplectic geometry, and group theory. His work on geometric group theory created an entirely new field. He received the Abel Prize in 2009 and is considered one of the most creative mathematicians alive.
