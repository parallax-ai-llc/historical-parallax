## Summary

While Zheng Xiaoying was primarily a renowned conductor, she was notable as China's first female symphony orchestra conductor. Her systematic approach to musical interpretation and her role in founding music education institutions contributed to cultural and intellectual development in China. She demonstrated that women could lead in traditionally male-dominated technical fields.
