## Summary
Ross William Ulbricht, known as 'Dread Pirate Roberts,' was the founder of Silk Road, the first major dark web marketplace for illegal drugs and other contraband. The site generated over $1 billion in revenue. He was convicted of money laundering, computer hacking, and drug trafficking. He was sentenced to life without parole.
