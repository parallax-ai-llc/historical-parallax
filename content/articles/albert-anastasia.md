## Summary
Umberto Anastasio, known as Albert Anastasia, was the head of Murder, Inc., the enforcement arm of the National Crime Syndicate. He became boss of the Gambino crime family. Known as the 'Lord High Executioner,' he was himself assassinated in a barbershop in 1957.
