## Summary

Frank Sinatra was an American singer and actor whose phrasing, style, and emotional depth made him the most popular entertainer of the 20th century. His recordings of songs like My Way, Fly Me to the Moon, and New York New York remain definitive. He won an Academy Award for From Here to Eternity.
