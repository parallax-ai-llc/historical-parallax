## Summary

Kenichi Ohmae is a Japanese management consultant and former head of McKinsey's Japan office. Known as 'Mr. Strategy,' he authored influential business books including 'The Mind of the Strategist' and 'The Borderless World.' He popularized the concept of the 'Triad' (Japan, U.S., Europe) as the key economic regions. He later founded Business Breakthrough University, an online university, demonstrating his entrepreneurial side.
