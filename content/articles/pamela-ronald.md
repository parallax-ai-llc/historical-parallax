## Summary

Pamela Ronald is an American plant pathologist who discovered the rice Xa21 gene conferring broad-spectrum disease resistance. Her research has been central to developing disease-resistant rice varieties using genetic engineering. She advocates for sustainable agriculture combining genetic engineering with organic farming practices.
