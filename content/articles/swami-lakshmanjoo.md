## Summary

Swami Lakshmanjoo was a Kashmiri mystic and scholar who was the last in an unbroken line of teachers of Kashmir Shaivism. He was the greatest modern authority on the tradition of Abhinavagupta and the Pratyabhijna philosophy. He lived his entire life in Kashmir, even during periods of political turmoil. His oral teachings, recorded by his students, preserved the living tradition of Kashmir Shaivism for future generations.
