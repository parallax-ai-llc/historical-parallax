## Summary

Morita Akio was a Japanese physicist and businessman who co-founded Sony Corporation with Ibuka Masaru in 1946. He was instrumental in bringing the transistor radio, Walkman, compact disc, and PlayStation to the world market. His talent for marketing Japanese technology globally helped transform Japan's image from a copycat to an innovator.
