## Summary

French terrorist of Algerian origin who was one of three attackers at the Bataclan theater in Paris on November 13, 2015. He detonated a suicide vest during the attack.

## Path to Radicalization (high-level)

- Reported by French authorities and media to have had a history of petty crime and instability prior to 2015.
- Was flagged in French security databases and had a record of contacts that drew law-enforcement attention.

## Role in the 13 November 2015 Paris Attacks

- Participated in the coordinated attacks carried out in Paris and Saint-Denis on **13 November 2015**.
- Was one of the attackers at the **Bataclan** concert venue.
- Died during the attack after detonating an explosive suicide vest.
