## Summary

Melanie Perkins co-founded Canva in 2013, building it into a $40 billion design platform used by over 170 million people worldwide. She started with the idea of making design accessible to everyone while selling scarves from her university dorm room. Canva's freemium model disrupted the graphic design industry previously dominated by Adobe. She became one of the youngest female CEOs of a multibillion-dollar tech company and pledged to give away the majority of her wealth.
