## Summary

Louis Leakey, with his wife Mary Leakey, made groundbreaking fossil discoveries at Olduvai Gorge in Tanzania that established East Africa as the cradle of humanity. Their discovery of Homo habilis pushed human origins back millions of years. He also mentored Jane Goodall, Dian Fossey, and Birut√© Galdikas in primate research.
