## Summary

South Korean serial killer who murdered at least 9 women in the 1990s in Seoul and Gyeonggi Province. He strangled his victims and evaded detection for years.

## Case Overview (High Level)

Hong Wan-pyo was linked to a series of murders over multiple years, with cases spanning different jurisdictions in and around Seoul. The prolonged period before arrest is often discussed in terms of investigative coordination and the challenges of connecting dispersed crimes.

## Investigation and Prosecution

Following arrest, authorities reviewed prior unsolved cases and gathered corroborating evidence to support multiple charges. Court proceedings resulted in a death sentence, reflecting the severity attributed to serial homicide under South Korean criminal law.
