## Summary

Jiao Nianzu was a Chinese physicist and historian of science who documented the history of physics in China and the contributions of Chinese physicists. His scholarly works preserved the record of Chinese scientific achievements and the challenges faced by Chinese scientists during periods of political turmoil.
