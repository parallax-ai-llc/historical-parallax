## Summary

Hannah Whitall Smith was an American lay speaker and author whose book The Christian's Secret of a Happy Life became one of the most popular devotional works of the 19th century. She was a key figure in the Holiness movement and the Keswick Convention in England. She also became active in the temperance and women's suffrage movements. Her influence on the 'Higher Life' movement was immense.
