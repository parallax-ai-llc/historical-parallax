## Summary

Charles Wesley was an English clergyman and hymn writer who, with his brother John, co-founded the Methodist movement. He wrote over 6,500 hymns, including 'Hark! the Herald Angels Sing' and 'Love Divine, All Loves Excelling.' His hymns became central to Methodist worship and influenced hymnody across all Christian traditions. He is considered one of the greatest hymn writers in Christian history.
