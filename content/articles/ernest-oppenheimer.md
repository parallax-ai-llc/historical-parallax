## Summary

Ernest Oppenheimer gained control of De Beers in 1929 and created a near-monopoly on the global diamond trade that lasted for decades. He also founded the Anglo American Corporation, which became one of the world's largest mining conglomerates. His control of diamond supply through the Central Selling Organisation fixed global diamond prices. He transformed South Africa's mining industry and built one of the country's most powerful dynasties.
