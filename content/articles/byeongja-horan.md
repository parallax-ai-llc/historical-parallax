## Summary

The Manchu invasion of Korea (Byeongja Horan) in 1636-37 forced Joseon to submit to the Qing dynasty. King Injo surrendered at Namhansanseong fortress, and Korea became a Qing tributary. The humiliation deeply scarred Korean national consciousness.
