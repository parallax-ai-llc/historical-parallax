## Summary
Angelo Anthony Buono Jr. was one of the 'Hillside Stranglers' who, with his cousin Kenneth Bianchi, raped, tortured, and murdered 10 young women in Los Angeles between October 1977 and February 1978. He was convicted of nine murders and sentenced to life without parole.
