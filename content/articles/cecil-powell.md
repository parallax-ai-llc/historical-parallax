## Summary

Cecil Powell was a British physicist who won the 1950 Nobel Prize in Physics for developing the photographic method of studying nuclear processes and for discovering the pion (pi meson). His photographic emulsion technique detected cosmic ray particles and confirmed Yukawa's prediction of the meson. He was also a passionate advocate for international scientific cooperation and nuclear disarmament.
