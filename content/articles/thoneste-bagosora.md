## Summary
Théoneste Bagosora was a Rwandan military official considered by the ICTR to have been the chief organizer of the 1994 Rwandan genocide. He was convicted of genocide and crimes against humanity and sentenced to life, later reduced to 35 years. He died in prison in Mali.
