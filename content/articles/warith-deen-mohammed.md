## Summary

Warith Deen Mohammed (W.D. Mohammed) was an American Muslim leader who succeeded his father Elijah Muhammad as head of the Nation of Islam and led the majority of its members toward orthodox Sunni Islam. He dismantled the Nation of Islam's racial theology and renamed the organization several times, ultimately becoming the American Society of Muslims. He was the most significant African American Muslim leader in bringing Black American Muslims into mainstream Islam.
