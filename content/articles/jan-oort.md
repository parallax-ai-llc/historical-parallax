## Summary

Jan Oort was a Dutch astronomer who made major contributions to understanding the structure and dynamics of the Milky Way galaxy. He hypothesized the existence of the Oort cloud, a spherical shell of icy objects surrounding the solar system. He demonstrated that the Milky Way rotates and that the Sun is located in one of its spiral arms. Oort also made pioneering contributions to radio astronomy.
