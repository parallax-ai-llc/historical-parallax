## Summary
The Srebrenica massacre was the genocide of more than 8,000 Bosniak Muslim men and boys in July 1995 by Bosnian Serb forces under General Ratko Mladić. It was the worst mass murder in Europe since World War II. The International Court of Justice ruled it a genocide.
