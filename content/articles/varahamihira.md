## Summary

Varahamihira was an ancient Indian astronomer and mathematician who authored the Pancha Siddhantika, summarizing five earlier astronomical texts, and the Brihat Samhita, an encyclopedia of natural sciences. He accurately described the concept of gravity, recognized the existence of comets, and made contributions to combinatorics and trigonometry.
