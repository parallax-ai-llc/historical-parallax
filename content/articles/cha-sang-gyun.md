## Summary

Cha Sang-gyun is a Korean display engineer who contributed to the development of OLED display technology at Samsung Display. His research on organic light-emitting diode materials and manufacturing processes helped Samsung dominate the global OLED market, which is now the standard for premium smartphone displays.
