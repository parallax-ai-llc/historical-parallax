## Summary

Palestinian commander of Hamas's military wing, the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades. He is the architect of Hamas's tunnel network and rocket arsenal. Survived multiple Israeli assassination attempts that left him severely wounded.

## Position and Organizational Role

Mohammed Deif is widely described as a senior military leader associated with Hamas, and is often linked to the group's armed wing, the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades. Public reporting commonly notes a long-running effort by Israeli security services to locate and target him.
