## Summary

Mamoru Mohri was the first Japanese astronaut to fly on the Space Shuttle, aboard Endeavour in 1992. He conducted numerous materials and life science experiments in microgravity. He later became director of the National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation (Miraikan) in Tokyo, promoting science education.
