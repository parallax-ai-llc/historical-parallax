## Summary

Peter Diamandis is an entrepreneur and futurist who founded the XPRIZE Foundation, the International Space University, and Singularity University. The $10 million Ansari XPRIZE for private spaceflight was won by SpaceShipOne in 2004, launching the private space industry. He co-founded multiple companies and has authored bestselling books including 'Abundance' and 'Bold.' He has been called one of the world's greatest tech visionaries.
