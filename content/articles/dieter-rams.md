## Summary

Dieter Rams is a German industrial designer closely associated with Braun and Vitsoe. His ten principles of good design have profoundly influenced modern product design, including Apple's design philosophy under Jony Ive. His minimalist, functional designs for consumer electronics and furniture remain benchmarks of industrial design.
