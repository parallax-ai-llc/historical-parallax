## Summary

Gang Shin-hang is a Korean mechanical engineer who has contributed to tribology (friction, wear, and lubrication) research. His work on advanced lubricants and surface engineering has been important for Korean manufacturing industries, particularly in reducing wear in automotive engines and industrial machinery.
