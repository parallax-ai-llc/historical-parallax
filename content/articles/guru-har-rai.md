## Summary

Guru Har Rai was the seventh Sikh Guru who maintained the military and political authority of the Sikhs while emphasizing humanitarian concerns. He established a herbal medicine hospital and was known for his love of nature and wildlife. He supported the Mughal prince Dara Shikoh against Aurangzeb. He maintained a cavalry of 2,200 soldiers while also preserving the spiritual traditions of Sikhism.
