## Summary

Rick Warren is an American evangelical pastor and author who founded Saddleback Church in Lake Forest, California. His book The Purpose Driven Life became one of the best-selling non-fiction books in history, selling over 30 million copies. He gave the invocation at President Obama's inauguration in 2009. He has been influential in expanding evangelical engagement with social justice issues including HIV/AIDS.
