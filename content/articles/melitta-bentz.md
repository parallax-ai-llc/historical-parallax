## Summary

Melitta Bentz invented the paper coffee filter in 1908, using a piece of blotting paper from her son's school notebook. She received a patent and founded the Melitta company, which became a global brand. Her simple invention transformed coffee preparation worldwide, producing cleaner, better-tasting coffee without grounds.
