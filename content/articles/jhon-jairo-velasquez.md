## Summary
Jhon Jairo Velásquez Vásquez, known as 'Popeye,' was a Colombian hitman for the Medellín Cartel who confessed to killing over 250 people and orchestrating the deaths of approximately 3,000 on behalf of Pablo Escobar. After serving 23 years, he became a YouTuber before dying of cancer in 2020.
