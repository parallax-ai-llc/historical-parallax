## Summary
On July 14, 2016, Mohamed Lahouaiej-Bouhlel drove a cargo truck into Bastille Day crowds on the Promenade des Anglais in Nice, France, killing 86 and injuring 458. It was one of the deadliest terrorist attacks in French history.
