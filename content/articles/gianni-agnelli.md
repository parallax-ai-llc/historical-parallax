## Summary

Gianni Agnelli was the head of Fiat and the most powerful industrialist in Italian history. Known as 'L'Avvocato' (The Lawyer), he controlled a business empire encompassing automobiles, insurance, publishing, and sports. He owned Juventus F.C. and was a global symbol of Italian style and elegance. Under his leadership, Fiat dominated Italy's economy, leading some to call it a company-state.
