## Summary

Nikolai Pirogov is considered the founder of field surgery and one of the first surgeons to use ether anesthesia in a battlefield setting during the Crimean War. He created the first anatomical atlas based on cross-sectional anatomy using frozen cadavers. His triage system and surgical techniques saved thousands of soldiers' lives.
