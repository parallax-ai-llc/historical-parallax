## Summary

Congolese militia leader and first person convicted by the International Criminal Court. He was found guilty of conscripting and enlisting child soldiers in the Ituri conflict in the DRC. Sentenced to 14 years.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2002 | Led UPC militia in Ituri |
| 2006 | Transferred to ICC |
| 2012 | Convicted of war crimes; sentenced to 14 years |
| 2020 | Released |
