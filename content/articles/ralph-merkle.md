## Summary

Ralph Merkle independently invented public-key cryptography and developed Merkle trees, a data structure fundamental to blockchain technology, peer-to-peer networks, and version control systems. His work on cryptographic hash functions and protocols has been essential to computer security. He also made significant contributions to nanotechnology research.
