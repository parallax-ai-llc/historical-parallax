## Summary

Lebanese Hezbollah operative wanted by the FBI for the 1985 hijacking of TWA Flight 847 during which US Navy diver Robert Stethem was murdered. He was killed in an Israeli airstrike on Beirut.

## Additional Context

### TWA Flight 847

The 1985 hijacking of TWA Flight 847 became one of the most internationally publicized hostage crises of the decade. During the incident, U.S. Navy diver Robert Stethem was murdered and his body was thrown from the aircraft, a detail that has remained central to U.S. legal filings and public memory of the case.

### U.S. charges and wanted status

Atwa was named in U.S. terrorism indictments and later listed by the FBI among its Most Wanted Terrorists. Such listings are typically supported by charging documents and investigative assessments rather than a completed trial in U.S. courts.
