## Summary

Chuck Berry was an American guitarist and songwriter who was one of the pioneers of rock and roll music. His showmanship, guitar style, and songs like Johnny B. Goode, Roll Over Beethoven, and Maybellene established the template for rock music. John Lennon said 'If you tried to give rock and roll another name, you might call it Chuck Berry.'
