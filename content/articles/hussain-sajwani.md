## Summary

Hussain Sajwani is the founder and chairman of DAMAC Properties, one of the largest real estate developers in the Middle East. He built luxury residential and commercial properties across the UAE, and partnered with Donald Trump on the Trump International Golf Club Dubai. His company has developed over 43,000 homes in Dubai. He played a major role in shaping Dubai's skyline and luxury real estate market.
