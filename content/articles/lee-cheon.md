## Summary

Lee Cheon was a Joseon Dynasty pharmacologist who compiled detailed records of Korean medicinal herbs and their therapeutic uses. Working under royal patronage, he systematized Korean traditional medicine knowledge and contributed to the pharmaceutical traditions that would later be incorporated into Heo Jun's Dongui Bogam.
