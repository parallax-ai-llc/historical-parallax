## Summary

Yang Hyun-suk co-founded YG Entertainment in 1996, building one of K-pop's 'Big Three' entertainment agencies. A former member of Seo Taiji and Boys, he produced global acts including Big Bang, 2NE1, and BLACKPINK. BLACKPINK became the highest-charting female K-pop act in history. He resigned from YG in 2019 amid allegations of facilitating prostitution and gambling.
