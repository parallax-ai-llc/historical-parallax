## Summary

Ben Horowitz co-founded Andreessen Horowitz (a16z) with Marc Andreessen, building one of Silicon Valley's most powerful venture capital firms. He previously co-founded Loudcloud/Opsware, which was sold to HP for $1.6 billion. His book 'The Hard Thing About Hard Things' became essential reading for startup founders. A16z has invested in transformative companies across enterprise software, crypto, and AI.
