## Summary

Emilio Segrè was an Italian-American physicist who shared the 1959 Nobel Prize in Physics with Owen Chamberlain for the discovery of the antiproton. He also co-discovered the elements technetium and astatine. Segrè was one of the original students of Enrico Fermi and played a role in the Manhattan Project. He later became a noted historian of physics.
