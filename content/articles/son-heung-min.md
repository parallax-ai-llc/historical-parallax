## Summary

Son Heung-min is the highest-scoring Asian in Premier League history. He won the 2022 Golden Boot and 2020 Puskas Award.

## Early Life and Development

- Son Heung-min was born in South Korea and developed through youth football before moving to Europe at a young age.
- His early professional development is commonly associated with German football academies and leagues.

## Club Career Highlights

- Son established himself in the Bundesliga before moving to the Premier League.
- At Tottenham Hotspur, he became known for consistent goal output, high-speed transitions, and combination play with teammates.
- Season-by-season roles have included winger and forward positions, often in systems that emphasize counterattacking runs.

## International Career

- Son has been a regular for the South Korea national team across major tournaments and qualifying campaigns.
- In addition to senior appearances, he represented South Korea at youth levels and in continental competitions.

## Playing Style

- Often described as a two-footed finisher with strong off-ball movement.
- Known for long sprints into space, direct dribbling in transition, and shooting from open play.
- Contributes defensively through pressing and tracking back, depending on tactical instructions.

## Recognition

- Individual awards frequently highlighted include the Premier League Golden Boot and the FIFA Puskás Award.
- He is widely recognized as one of the most prominent Asian footballers of his generation, both for performance and visibility.
