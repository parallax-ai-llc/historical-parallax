## Summary
Gregor MacGregor was a Scottish soldier, adventurer, and confidence trickster who created the fictional country of 'Poyais' in Central America in the 1820s. He sold land and government bonds for a country that didn't exist, causing hundreds of settlers to travel there with devastating consequences.
