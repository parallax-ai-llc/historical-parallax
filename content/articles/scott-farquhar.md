## Summary

Scott Farquhar co-founded Atlassian with Mike Cannon-Brookes, building it into one of the world's most valuable enterprise software companies. Their collaboration tools — Jira for project tracking and Confluence for documentation — became essential in software development. Atlassian grew to serve over 300,000 customers worldwide. The company's success without traditional venture capital funding became a Silicon Valley legend.
