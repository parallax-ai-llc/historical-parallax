## Summary

Immanuel Jakobovits was the Chief Rabbi of the United Hebrew Congregations of the British Commonwealth from 1967 to 1991. He pioneered the field of Jewish medical ethics with his groundbreaking work Jewish Medical Ethics. He was the first Chief Rabbi to be elevated to the House of Lords. He was known for his intellectual rigor and his engagement with contemporary ethical issues.
