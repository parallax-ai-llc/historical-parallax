## Summary
Gaetano 'Tommy' Lucchese was an Italian-American mobster who became boss of the Lucchese crime family, one of New York's Five Families. Known as 'Three Finger Brown,' he was one of the most influential Mafia figures in America. He died of a brain tumor.
