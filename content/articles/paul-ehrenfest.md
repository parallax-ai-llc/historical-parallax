## Summary

Paul Ehrenfest was an Austrian-Dutch theoretical physicist who made important contributions to statistical mechanics and quantum theory. He formulated the Ehrenfest theorem connecting quantum and classical mechanics and the Ehrenfest classification of phase transitions. He maintained a famous discussion group and was close friends with both Einstein and Bohr.
