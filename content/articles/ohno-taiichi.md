## Summary

Ohno Taiichi was a Japanese industrial engineer who developed the Toyota Production System (TPS), the revolutionary manufacturing methodology also known as lean manufacturing. He invented the kanban system, just-in-time production, and continuous improvement (kaizen) principles. His methods transformed global manufacturing and have been adopted across industries worldwide.
