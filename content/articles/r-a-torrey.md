## Summary

Reuben Archer Torrey was an American evangelist, pastor, and educator who was a key figure in the development of fundamentalism. He succeeded D. L. Moody as head of the Moody Bible Institute and conducted worldwide evangelistic campaigns. He wrote influential books defending biblical inerrancy and orthodox Christianity. He also played a role in the development of The Fundamentals, a series of essays defending conservative Christianity.
