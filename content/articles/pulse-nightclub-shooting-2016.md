## Summary
On June 12, 2016, Omar Mateen killed 49 people and wounded 53 at Pulse, a gay nightclub in Orlando, Florida. It was the deadliest mass shooting in US history at the time and the deadliest attack on the LGBTQ community in the US. Mateen pledged allegiance to ISIS.
