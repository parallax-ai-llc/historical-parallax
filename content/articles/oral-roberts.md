## Summary

Oral Roberts was an American Pentecostal evangelist and televangelist who built one of the largest ministry organizations in the world. He pioneered televangelism and faith healing on television. He founded Oral Roberts University in Tulsa, Oklahoma. His emphasis on 'seed faith' theology influenced the prosperity gospel movement.
