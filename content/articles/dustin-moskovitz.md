## Summary

Dustin Moskovitz is the co-founder of Facebook and Asana. He was Mark Zuckerberg's roommate at Harvard and became Facebook's first CTO. He left Facebook in 2008 to co-found Asana, a work management platform. Through Open Philanthropy and Good Ventures, he and his wife Cari Tuna have donated billions to effective altruism causes. He is one of the youngest self-made billionaires in history.
