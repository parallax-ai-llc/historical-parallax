## Summary
Omar Mir Seddique Mateen was an American mass shooter who killed 49 people and wounded 53 at Pulse nightclub in Orlando, Florida, on June 12, 2016. It was the deadliest mass shooting in US history at the time and the deadliest terrorist attack since 9/11. He pledged allegiance to ISIS during the attack.
