## Summary

Nat Rothschild is a British financier and member of the Rothschild banking dynasty. He co-founded Volex Capital and JNR Limited, and was an early investor in Atticus Capital hedge fund. He was involved in various mining and commodities investments, including a controversial SPAC merger with Indonesian coal company Bumi Resources. He represents the modern generation of the Rothschild family in finance.
