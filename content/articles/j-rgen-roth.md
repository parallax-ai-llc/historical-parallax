## Summary

German investigative journalist who exposed organized crime networks and corruption across Europe in dozens of books. His work on Russian and Italian mafia operations in Germany made him a target.

## Career Timeline

## Additional Context

- **Investigative focus**: Roth wrote extensively on corruption, money laundering, and the operations of organized-crime groups across European borders.
- **Public role**: He was a frequent commentator in German-language media on criminal networks and governance failures.
- **Risks and pushback**: Summaries of his career often note threats and legal pressure connected to his reporting and publications.

## Themes in His Work (High-Level)

- The intersection of organized crime with legitimate business.
- Political protection and institutional corruption.
- Transnational networks (including Russian and Italian groups) operating inside the EU.
