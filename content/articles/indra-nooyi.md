## Summary

Indra Nooyi served as CEO of PepsiCo from 2006 to 2018, leading one of the world's largest food and beverage companies. She oversaw the company's strategic transformation toward healthier products through her 'Performance with Purpose' initiative. Under her leadership, PepsiCo's net revenue grew by over 80%. Born in Chennai, India, she was consistently ranked among the world's most powerful women in business.
