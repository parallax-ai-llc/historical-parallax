## Summary

Cai Wenji, while primarily known as a poet and musician, was one of the most learned women in Chinese history. She could recite from memory thousands of books and had mastery of mathematics and astronomy. She represents the tradition of female scholarship in ancient China, though her scientific contributions were not formally recorded.
