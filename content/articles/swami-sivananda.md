## Summary

Swami Sivananda was an Indian spiritual teacher and yoga master who founded the Divine Life Society in Rishikesh. He trained as a physician before renouncing the world, and his approach combined traditional yoga with modern practical methods. He wrote over 200 books on yoga, Vedanta, and spiritual practice. His disciples, including Swami Vishnudevananda and Swami Satchidananda, spread yoga globally.
