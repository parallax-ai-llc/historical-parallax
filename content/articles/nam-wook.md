## Background

- **Name**: Nam Wook (남욱)
- **Nationality**: South Korean
- **Public notability**: Primarily known in connection with the **Daejang-dong (대장동) land development** controversy in Seongnam, South Korea.
- **Profession**: Often described in reporting as a lawyer/attorney and as an insider connected to private-sector participants in the development project.[^1]

## Why He Matters (High-Level)

Nam Wook is cited as a key private-sector figure because his **statements and testimony** were used to outline how profits were distributed among participants and how different actors were connected within the broader Daejang-dong network.[^1]
