## Summary

John Collins Warren was the surgeon who performed the first surgery under ether anesthesia during William Morton's famous Ether Day demonstration at Massachusetts General Hospital on October 16, 1846. He exclaimed 'Gentlemen, this is no humbug!' after the painless operation. He co-founded Massachusetts General Hospital and the New England Journal of Medicine.
