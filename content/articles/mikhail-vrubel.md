## Summary

Mikhail Vrubel was the foremost Russian Symbolist artist, known for his obsessive Demon painting series inspired by Lermontov. His distinctive crystalline style combined Byzantine mosaic influences with Art Nouveau. His tragic life of mental illness mirrors the tormented demons he depicted.
