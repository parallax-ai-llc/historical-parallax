## Summary

Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal is a Saudi billionaire investor and member of the Saudi royal family. Known as the 'Warren Buffett of Arabia,' he made bold investments in Citigroup, Four Seasons Hotels, and Twitter. He controls Kingdom Holding Company, which has invested in companies across technology, banking, and hospitality. He was detained during the 2017-2018 Saudi anti-corruption purge at the Ritz-Carlton Riyadh.
