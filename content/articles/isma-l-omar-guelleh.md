## Strategic Context

- Djibouti is positioned near the Bab-el-Mandeb Strait, a chokepoint connecting the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden.
- This geography has made Djibouti a hub for foreign military facilities and logistics, which is frequently cited in discussions of state revenue and international leverage.
