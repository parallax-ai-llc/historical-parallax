## Summary

Chilean-American blogger arrested in Ukraine for spreading Russian propaganda. Died in Ukrainian detention.

## Background

Gonzalo Lira was a Chilean-born commentator who produced online content in English and lived in Ukraine for part of the Russia–Ukraine war period.
He became widely known for political commentary that was polarizing and frequently criticized as pro-Russian.

## Arrest and Detention in Ukraine

Ukrainian authorities detained Lira in 2022 and later pursued legal proceedings connected to his public statements.
Public reporting commonly frames the allegations as related to propaganda or justification of Russia's actions, though precise legal characterizations vary by source.
The case attracted attention because it involved a foreign national, wartime information controls, and a high-profile online audience.

## Death

Lira died in custody in January 2024.
Different public narratives about the circumstances circulated after his death, including statements from family and commentary from political figures.
Because most details were reported through secondary sources, accounts in the public record should be treated cautiously unless supported by official documentation.
