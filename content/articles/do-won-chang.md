## Summary

Do Won Chang is the co-founder of Forever 21, the fast-fashion retail chain that became a cultural phenomenon among young shoppers. He immigrated from South Korea to Los Angeles in 1981 and opened the first Fashion 21 store in 1984. The chain grew to over 800 stores worldwide at its peak. Forever 21 filed for bankruptcy in 2019 amid the shift to online shopping, but the brand was acquired and continues to operate.
