## Summary

Marina Abramovic is a Serbian performance artist known as the grandmother of performance art. Her works including The Artist Is Present at MoMA test the limits of physical and mental endurance. She has explored the relationship between performer and audience the boundaries of the body and the possibilities of the mind over six decades.
