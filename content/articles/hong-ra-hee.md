## Summary

Hong Ra-hee is the wife of the late Samsung Chairman Lee Kun-hee and one of the most influential art collectors in Asia. She served as director of the Leeum Samsung Museum of Art, building one of the finest private art collections in the world. After her husband's death, she donated 23,000 artworks and artifacts worth over $2 billion to Korean museums, including national treasures. The donation was one of the largest art gifts in world history.
