## Summary

Fred Swaniker is a Ghanaian entrepreneur and founder of the African Leadership Group, which includes African Leadership Academy, African Leadership University, and Sand Technologies. His mission is to develop 3 million ethical leaders for Africa by 2035. He was named a Young Global Leader by the World Economic Forum. He represents a new generation of African entrepreneurs focused on human capital development and technology.
