## Overview of the Allegations and Legal Process

The corruption case(s) involving Benjamin Netanyahu are typically discussed as a set of investigations and indictments concerning alleged conflicts of interest and improper exchanges of benefits.

In broad, non-technical terms:

- **Bribery** allegations generally center on an exchange of official action for personal benefit.
- **Fraud and breach of trust** allegations generally center on misuse of public office and deception or concealment.

Netanyahu has repeatedly denied wrongdoing, and the proceedings have unfolded over multiple years, including periods when he continued to serve as prime minister.
