## Summary

Guru Arjan was the fifth Sikh Guru who compiled the Adi Granth, the first edition of the Sikh holy scripture. He completed the construction of the Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) in Amritsar. He was the first Sikh Guru to be martyred, tortured and killed by the Mughal emperor Jahangir for refusing to convert to Islam. His martyrdom transformed Sikhism into a more militant faith.
