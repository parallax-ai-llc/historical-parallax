## Summary

Janaki Ammal was a pioneering Indian botanist who made significant contributions to plant cytogenetics and phytogeography. She created hybrid sugarcane varieties that increased India's sugar production and studied the chromosomes of many plant species. She was the first Indian woman to receive a PhD in botany in the United States.
