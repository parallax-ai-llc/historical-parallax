## Summary

Takamine Jokichi isolated adrenaline (epinephrine) in 1901, the first hormone to be isolated in pure form. He also developed Takadiastase, a starch-digesting enzyme used as a digestive aid. He was a key figure in Japanese-American scientific relations and helped bring Japanese cherry trees to Washington, D.C.
