## Summary

The Cuban Missile Crisis of October 1962 was the closest the Cold War came to nuclear war. The US discovered Soviet nuclear missiles in Cuba, and President Kennedy imposed a naval blockade. After 13 tense days, the Soviets agreed to remove the missiles.
