## Summary

Henry Ward Beecher was an American Congregationalist clergyman and one of the most famous preachers of the 19th century. He championed abolition, women's suffrage, and Darwin's theory of evolution from his pulpit at Plymouth Church in Brooklyn. He was involved in a famous adultery scandal with Theodore Tilton's wife. His progressive theology and social activism represented a major shift in American Protestantism.
