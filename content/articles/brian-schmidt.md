## Summary

Brian Schmidt is an American-born Australian astrophysicist who shared the 2011 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of the accelerating expansion of the universe. He led the High-z Supernova Search Team, which independently confirmed that the universe's expansion is accelerating. He later became vice-chancellor of the Australian National University.
