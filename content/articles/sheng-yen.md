## Summary

Sheng Yen was a Taiwanese Chan (Zen) Buddhist monk, author, and educator who founded Dharma Drum Mountain, one of the major Buddhist organizations in Taiwan. He held dharma transmission in both the Linji (Rinzai) and Caodong (Soto) lineages. He earned doctorates from Rissho University in Japan and was known for making Chan accessible to modern practitioners. He established Chan meditation centers around the world and wrote over 100 books.
