## Summary

Sultan bin Salman Al Saud became the first Arab and first Muslim in space when he flew aboard the Space Shuttle Discovery in 1985 as a payload specialist. He later became chairman of the Saudi Space Commission. His spaceflight brought attention to space science across the Arab world.
