## Summary

Cumrun Vafa is an Iranian-American theoretical physicist who has made important contributions to string theory. He proposed the Swampland program, which identifies constraints that string theory places on effective field theories. He is known for F-theory and topological string theory. Vafa has been a professor at Harvard since 1990.
