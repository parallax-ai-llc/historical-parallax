## Summary

Soichi Noguchi is a Japanese astronaut who has flown in space three times: on the Space Shuttle Discovery (2005), Soyuz (2009-2010), and SpaceX Crew Dragon (2020). He became the third person to fly on three different types of spacecraft. He performed spacewalks during the first shuttle mission after the Columbia disaster.
