## Summary

Charles Bennett is a pioneer of quantum information science. He co-invented quantum teleportation, quantum cryptography (BB84 protocol), and made fundamental contributions to the thermodynamics of computation. His work on the relationship between physics and information laid the groundwork for quantum computing and quantum communication.
