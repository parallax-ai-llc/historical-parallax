## Summary

Dana White is the CEO of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), which he transformed from a near-bankrupt niche sport into a global entertainment empire. He and the Fertitta brothers purchased the UFC for $2 million in 2001 and sold it to Endeavor (now TKO Group) for $4 billion in 2016. The UFC became the fastest-growing sports organization in the world, with events in over 175 countries.
