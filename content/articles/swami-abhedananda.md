## Summary

Swami Abhedananda was a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna who headed the Vedanta Society of New York from 1897 to 1921. He lectured extensively across the United States and Europe, becoming one of the earliest Indian teachers to systematically present Vedanta in the West. He traveled to Tibet in 1922 and reportedly viewed a manuscript about Jesus's years in India. He founded the Ramakrishna Vedanta Math in Calcutta.
