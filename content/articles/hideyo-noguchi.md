## Summary

Hideyo Noguchi was a Japanese bacteriologist who made important contributions to the understanding of syphilis, yellow fever, and other infectious diseases. He demonstrated that Treponema pallidum causes syphilis and worked extensively on developing a yellow fever vaccine. He died of yellow fever while researching the disease in Ghana. His portrait appears on the Japanese 1,000-yen banknote.
