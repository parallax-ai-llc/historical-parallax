## Summary

Augustus Kountze was an American banker who founded the first bank in Omaha, Nebraska, and was instrumental in financing the transcontinental railroad. He established Kountze Brothers bank in New York and helped develop the financial infrastructure of the American West. His banking house financed numerous western business ventures and railroad construction projects.
