## Summary

Ai Xuan is a Chinese realist painter known for his haunting, photorealistic paintings of solitary figures in Tibetan snow landscapes. His melancholic scenes of lone women and children against vast frozen expanses evoke themes of isolation and spiritual yearning. He is considered one of China's most important contemporary realist painters.
