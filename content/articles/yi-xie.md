## Summary

Yi Xie is a Chinese inorganic chemist who made pioneering contributions to the synthesis of two-dimensional nanomaterials and their applications in energy conversion and catalysis. She developed novel methods for creating ultrathin nanosheets and defect engineering in nanomaterials. She was the youngest female member elected to the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
