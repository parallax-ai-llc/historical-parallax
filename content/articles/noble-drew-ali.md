## Summary

Noble Drew Ali (born Timothy Drew) was the founder of the Moorish Science Temple of America, one of the first Islamic-inspired movements among African Americans. He taught that African Americans were Moors of Moroccan descent and that Islam was their original religion. His Holy Koran of the Moorish Science Temple combined Islamic, Christian, and esoteric elements. He influenced later movements including the Nation of Islam. He died under mysterious circumstances in 1929.
