## Summary

Wang Shu is a Chinese architect who co-founded Amateur Architecture Studio with his wife Lu Wenyu. He won the Pritzker Prize in 2012 for buildings that combine modernist vision with deep respect for Chinese architectural tradition. His Ningbo History Museum uses recycled materials from demolished traditional buildings.
