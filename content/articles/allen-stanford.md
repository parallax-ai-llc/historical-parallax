## Summary
Robert Allen Stanford was an American financier and convicted fraudster who ran a $7 billion Ponzi scheme through Stanford Financial Group. He sold fraudulent certificates of deposit from Stanford International Bank in Antigua. He was convicted in 2012 and sentenced to 110 years.
