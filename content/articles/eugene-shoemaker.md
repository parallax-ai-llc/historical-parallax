## Summary

Eugene Shoemaker is considered the founder of planetary science. He proved that Meteor Crater in Arizona was caused by an asteroid impact and established the field of astrogeology. He co-discovered Comet Shoemaker-Levy 9. He trained Apollo astronauts in geology and was the first person whose ashes were sent to the Moon.
