## Summary
Angelo Buono was one of the Hillside Stranglers who, with cousin Kenneth Bianchi, raped and murdered 10 women in Los Angeles in 1977-78. Sentenced to life without parole.

## Crimes (High-Level)
- Known as part of the **Hillside Stranglers** duo.
- Location: Los Angeles, California.
- Timeframe: 1977–1978.
- Victims: 10 women.

## Investigation and Prosecution
- The case involved extensive investigation and multiple charges.
- Kenneth Bianchi and Angelo Buono were prosecuted separately with different legal outcomes.

## Sentence
- Buono received a sentence of life imprisonment without parole.
