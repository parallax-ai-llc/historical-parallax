## Summary

Patrick Steptoe was the gynecologist who, working with Robert Edwards, made the birth of the first IVF baby possible. He developed the laparoscopic technique for retrieving eggs from the ovaries, which was essential to the IVF procedure. He co-founded the world's first IVF clinic, Bourn Hall Clinic. He died before the Nobel Prize was awarded to Edwards.
