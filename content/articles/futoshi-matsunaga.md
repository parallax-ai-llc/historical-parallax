## Summary
Futoshi Matsunaga was a Japanese serial killer who, with accomplice Seisaku Nakamura, tortured and murdered seven people including family members in Kitakyushu between 1996 and 1998. He used psychological manipulation and electric shocks to control victims. He was sentenced to death.
