## Summary

Joe Gebbia is a co-founder of Airbnb who originally came up with the idea of renting air mattresses during a design conference in San Francisco. As head of product and design, his design background shaped Airbnb's user experience. He later led Samara, Airbnb's internal design studio. He stepped back from daily operations to focus on design, philanthropy, and investing through his fund.
