## Summary

Eric Lefkofsky co-founded Groupon, the daily deals website that was the fastest company ever to reach $1 billion in revenue. He also co-founded InnerWorkings and Tempus, an AI-driven healthcare company. Groupon went public in 2011 at a $13 billion valuation. Tempus, which uses AI to personalize cancer treatment, went public in 2024 and represents his evolution from e-commerce to healthcare technology.
