## Summary

J. Tuzo Wilson made key contributions to plate tectonics, including the discovery of transform faults and the Wilson cycle of ocean basin opening and closing. He proposed hot spots as fixed mantle plumes that create volcanic island chains. His work helped establish plate tectonics as the unifying theory of geology.
