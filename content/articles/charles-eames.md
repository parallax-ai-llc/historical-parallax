## Summary

Charles Eames, with his wife Ray, was one of the most influential designers of the 20th century. Their innovations in molded plywood and fiberglass furniture, including the Eames Lounge Chair, revolutionized modern furniture design. The Eames Office also created pioneering films, exhibitions, and toys that embodied their philosophy of learning through play.
