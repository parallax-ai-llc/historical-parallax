## Summary

Cesaria Evora was a Cape Verdean singer known as the Barefoot Diva for her habit of performing without shoes. Her melancholic morna music brought the sounds of Cape Verde to a global audience. She won a Grammy Award and became the most internationally famous artist from the Portuguese-speaking world.
