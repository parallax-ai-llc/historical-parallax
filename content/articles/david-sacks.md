## Summary

David Oliver Sacks (born May 25, 1972) is a South African-American entrepreneur and venture capitalist, best known as the founding COO of PayPal and founder of Yammer. A core member of the "PayPal Mafia," Sacks has built multiple successful companies and become a prominent tech investor through Craft Ventures. He is also known for co-hosting the influential "All-In Podcast" and his outspoken political views.
