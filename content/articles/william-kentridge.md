## Summary

William Kentridge is a South African artist known for his charcoal drawings and animated films that address apartheid colonialism and the human condition. His technique of drawing erasing and redrawing creates hauntingly layered animations that explore memory and history. He is widely regarded as the most important South African artist of his generation.
