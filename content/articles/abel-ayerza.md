## Summary

Abel Ayerza was an Argentine physician who described the syndrome of pulmonary hypertension with cyanosis and polycythemia, known as Ayerza's disease. He was a leading figure in Argentine internal medicine and cardiology. His clinical observations contributed significantly to the understanding of cardiopulmonary diseases.
