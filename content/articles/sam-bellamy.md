## Summary
Samuel 'Black Sam' Bellamy was an English pirate who operated in the early 18th century. He captured 53 ships in his short career. He commanded the Whydah Gally, which sank in a storm off Cape Cod in 1717, killing Bellamy and most of his crew. The wreck was discovered in 1984.
