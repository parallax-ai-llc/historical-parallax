## Summary

Ram Dass (born Richard Alpert) was an American spiritual teacher whose book Be Here Now became a counterculture classic and influenced millions. A former Harvard psychology professor, he was dismissed along with Timothy Leary for experiments with psychedelics. He traveled to India in 1967 where he met his guru Neem Karoli Baba, who transformed his life. He became one of the most beloved spiritual teachers in America, combining Hindu devotion with service and love.
