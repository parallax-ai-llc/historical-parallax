## Summary

Takagi Teiji was a Japanese mathematician who founded class field theory, one of the most important achievements in algebraic number theory. His proof of the Takagi existence theorem generalized the work of Hilbert and Kronecker. He was the first mathematician to bring Japanese mathematics to the forefront of world research.
