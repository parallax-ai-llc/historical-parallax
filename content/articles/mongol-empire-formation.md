## Summary

In 1206, Temüjin was proclaimed Genghis Khan (Universal Ruler) at a great assembly, uniting the Mongol tribes. He built the largest contiguous land empire in history, stretching from Korea to Eastern Europe, through military brilliance and ruthless efficiency.
