## Summary

Dorothy Vaughan was the first African American manager at NACA (later NASA) and a pioneer in computer programming. She headed the West Area Computing unit at NASA Langley and taught herself and her team FORTRAN programming when electronic computers replaced human calculators. She was featured in the book and film Hidden Figures.
