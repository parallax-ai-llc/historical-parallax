## Summary

Abdolkarim Soroush is an Iranian philosopher and Islamic reformer who has been called the 'Luther of Islam' for his challenge to clerical authority. He developed the theory of 'contraction and expansion of religious knowledge,' arguing that religious understanding is historically situated and evolving. Initially supporting the Iranian Revolution, he later became a major critic of theocratic governance. He has been a visiting professor at Harvard, Princeton, and other universities.
