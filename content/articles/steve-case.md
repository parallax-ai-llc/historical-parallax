## Summary

Steve Case co-founded America Online (AOL), which introduced millions of Americans to the internet in the 1990s. Under his leadership, AOL became the largest internet service provider in the world. He engineered the AOL-Time Warner merger in 2000, the largest merger in history at the time. After leaving AOL, he founded Revolution LLC to invest in startups outside Silicon Valley.
