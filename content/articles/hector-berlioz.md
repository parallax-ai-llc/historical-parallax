## Summary

Hector Berlioz was a French Romantic composer who revolutionized orchestration and programmatic music. His Symphonie fantastique pioneered the concept of the idee fixe and expanded the orchestra's expressive range. His Treatise on Instrumentation remains a foundational text on orchestral writing.
