## Summary

Melanie Perkins co-founded Canva, the graphic design platform that democratized visual design for non-designers. She was rejected by over 100 investors before Canva took off. Canva reached a $40 billion valuation, making her one of the world's youngest female billionaires. The platform serves over 170 million users in 190 countries. Her idea originated from frustration with complex design software while teaching at university.
