## Summary

The Tunisian Revolution (2010-11) sparked the Arab Spring when Mohamed Bouazizi's self-immolation triggered mass protests against President Ben Ali's authoritarian rule. Ben Ali fled after 23 years in power, inspiring uprisings across the Arab world.
