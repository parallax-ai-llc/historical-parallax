## Summary

Tan Siu Lin was a Hong Kong billionaire and founder of Nan Fung Group, one of the city's largest property developers. He started as a cotton yarn trader from Shanghai and built a real estate empire spanning residential, commercial, and industrial properties across Hong Kong. Nan Fung Group also expanded into investments and the life sciences sector. His family continued to manage one of Hong Kong's most significant property portfolios.
