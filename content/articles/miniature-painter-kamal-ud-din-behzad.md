## Summary

Kamal ud-Din Behzad was the greatest painter of the Persian miniature tradition, whose work represents the pinnacle of Islamic manuscript painting. His vivid compositions, keen observation of human character, and innovative use of space transformed the art of the book. Director of the royal library at Herat and later Tabriz, he influenced miniature painting across the Islamic world.
