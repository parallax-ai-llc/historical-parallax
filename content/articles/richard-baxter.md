## Summary

Richard Baxter was an English Puritan pastor and theologian who was one of the most prolific authors in English Protestant history. His Reformed Pastor and The Saints' Everlasting Rest were enormously influential. He served as chaplain to the Parliamentary army during the English Civil War. He sought unity among Protestants and was imprisoned for nonconformity after the Restoration.
