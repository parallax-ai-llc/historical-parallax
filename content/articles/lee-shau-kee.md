## Summary

Lee Shau-kee is the founder and chairman emeritus of Henderson Land Development, one of Hong Kong's largest property developers. He was the second-richest person in Hong Kong after Li Ka-shing for many years. He co-founded Sun Hung Kai Properties and later built Henderson Land into a major developer. His property empire spans Hong Kong and mainland China, with holdings in gas, hotels, and finance.
