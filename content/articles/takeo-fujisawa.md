## Summary

Takeo Fujisawa was the co-founder and business partner of Soichiro Honda at Honda Motor Company. While Honda focused on engineering, Fujisawa handled management, finance, and marketing. Their partnership is considered one of the most successful in Japanese business history. Fujisawa's business acumen was essential to Honda's growth from a small motorcycle maker to a global automotive giant.
