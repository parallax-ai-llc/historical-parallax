## Summary

Kiyoshi Shiga discovered Shigella dysenteriae, the bacterium that causes bacillary dysentery (shigellosis), during a devastating epidemic in Japan in 1897. The genus Shigella was named in his honor. He also worked on tuberculosis treatment and contributed to the development of chemotherapy for bacterial infections.
