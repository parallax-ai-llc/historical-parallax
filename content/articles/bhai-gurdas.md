## Summary

Bhai Gurdas was a Sikh scholar, poet, and theologian who was the first scribe of the Guru Granth Sahib. He served four Sikh Gurus and is considered the foremost interpreter of Sikh scripture. His own compositions (vaars and kabitts) are called 'the key to the Guru Granth Sahib' by Guru Arjan. He was the maternal uncle of Guru Arjan and played a crucial role in the compilation and preservation of Sikh scripture.
