## Summary

Ken Griffin founded Citadel LLC in 1990, building it into one of the world's most successful hedge funds. He also founded Citadel Securities, which became the largest market maker in U.S. equities. Citadel has generated over $65 billion in net gains for investors since inception. Griffin is known for his record-breaking real estate purchases and philanthropic donations to education and civic causes.
