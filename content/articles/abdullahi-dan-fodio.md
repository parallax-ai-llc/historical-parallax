## Summary

Abdullahi dan Fodio was a Fulani Islamic scholar and the brother of Usman dan Fodio, with whom he led the Fulani jihad that established the Sokoto Caliphate in West Africa. He was a prolific writer and jurist who governed the western provinces of the caliphate. His writings on Islamic jurisprudence, Sufism, and governance helped legitimize and organize the caliphate. He authored over 100 works in Arabic, Fulfulde, and Hausa.
