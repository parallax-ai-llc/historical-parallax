## Summary

Eckhart Tolle is a German-Canadian spiritual teacher and author whose books The Power of Now and A New Earth have sold millions of copies worldwide. He experienced a profound inner transformation at age 29 that ended years of depression and anxiety. His teaching emphasizes present-moment awareness and transcendence of the ego. Oprah Winfrey's promotion of A New Earth in 2008 brought him to a global audience. He is one of the most popular spiritual teachers in the Western world.
