## Summary

Kang Jeungsan (Gang Il-sun) was the founder of Jeungsanism, a family of Korean new religions. He claimed to have performed the 'Reordering of the Universe' (cheonji gongsa) to create a new cosmic order. He is regarded by his followers as the Supreme God incarnate. Several Korean new religious movements, including Daesoon Jinrihoe and Jeung San Do, trace their origins to his teachings.
