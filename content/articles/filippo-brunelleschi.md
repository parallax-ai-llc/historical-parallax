## Summary

Filippo Brunelleschi was a Florentine architect credited as the father of Renaissance architecture. His masterwork, the dome of Florence Cathedral, remains the largest masonry dome ever constructed. He also formalized the mathematical rules of linear perspective, transforming both painting and architecture.
