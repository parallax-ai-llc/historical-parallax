## Background

- Zhlobitsky was a Russian teenager from the Arkhangelsk region.
- Public information about his personal life is limited; most commonly cited details come from contemporaneous reporting and later summaries.

## 2018 Arkhangelsk FSB bombing

- **Date:** 2018-10-31
- **Location:** Federal Security Service (FSB) office in Arkhangelsk, Russia.
- **Method:** Detonated an explosive device inside/near the building and died at the scene.
- **Casualties:** The blast injured several people (often reported as three injuries) in addition to Zhlobitsky’s death.

## Claimed motive and messaging

- Some accounts describe him as identifying with anarchist/anti-security-service views.
- Statements attributed to him were circulated online shortly after the attack; attribution is typically reported second-hand.

## Aftermath

- Russian authorities treated the incident as a terrorism-related attack and opened an investigation.
