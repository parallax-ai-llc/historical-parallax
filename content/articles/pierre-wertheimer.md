## Summary

Pierre Wertheimer became the majority owner of Chanel's perfume business in 1924, beginning the Wertheimer family's control of the Chanel empire. He partnered with Coco Chanel to launch Chanel No. 5 globally. Despite Chanel's attempts to reclaim control during WWII, the Wertheimers maintained their stake and eventually gained complete ownership. His grandsons Alain and Gerard now control the entire Chanel empire.
