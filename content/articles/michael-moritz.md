## Summary

Michael Moritz is a Welsh-American venture capitalist and former partner at Sequoia Capital, one of Silicon Valley's most successful firms. He led investments in Google, Yahoo, PayPal, YouTube, LinkedIn, and many other major tech companies. Before venture capital, he was a journalist at Time magazine and wrote a book about Apple. His investments have generated some of the highest returns in venture capital history.
