## Summary

John Singer Sargent was the most sought-after portrait painter of his generation, celebrated for virtuosic brushwork. His controversial Portrait of Madame X is a masterpiece of modern portraiture. His later watercolors and Boston Public Library murals reveal broader artistic vision.
