## Summary

Bashar Masri is a Palestinian-American real estate developer who built the planned city of Rawabi, the first planned Palestinian city in the West Bank. The $1.4 billion project was the largest private-sector investment in Palestinian history. Rawabi was designed to house 40,000 residents with modern infrastructure. The project demonstrated the possibility of large-scale development even under the constraints of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
