## Summary
Alexander Mashinsky was the founder and CEO of Celsius Network, a cryptocurrency lending platform that collapsed in 2022, owing $4.7 billion to depositors. He was charged with fraud for misleading customers about the safety of their deposits. He pleaded guilty in 2024.
