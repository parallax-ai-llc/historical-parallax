## Summary

Barney Graham was the deputy director of the NIH Vaccine Research Center who co-developed the spike protein stabilization technology crucial to both the Moderna and Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccines. His prefusion stabilized spike protein approach, developed over years for RSV and MERS, was pivotally adapted for SARS-CoV-2.
