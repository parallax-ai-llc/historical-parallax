## Summary
Nirav Deepak Modi is an Indian billionaire jeweler who was involved in a $2 billion fraud against Punjab National Bank, India's second-largest state-owned bank. He fled India in 2018 and was arrested in London in 2019. He was extradited to India in 2024.
