## Summary

The Asian Financial Crisis of 1997-98 devastated East Asian economies. Starting with Thailand's baht devaluation, the crisis spread to Indonesia, South Korea, and beyond. The IMF provided $118 billion in bailouts, reshaping Asian economic policies.
