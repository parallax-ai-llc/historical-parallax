## Political Career (High-Level)

- **Regional leadership:** Served as Governor of the Moquegua Region (Peru).
- **Diplomacy & cabinet roles:** Held senior executive posts including service as a minister in Peru's national government.
- **Presidency (2018–2020):** Became President of Peru after the resignation of President Pedro Pablo Kuczynski.
- **Congressional crisis (2019):** Dissolved Congress and called for new legislative elections during a prolonged institutional conflict.
- **Removal (2020):** Removed from office by Congress through an impeachment-style vote ("vacancy" for alleged moral incapacity).
