## Summary

K.V. Kamath served as CEO of ICICI Bank from 1996 to 2009, transforming it into India's largest private sector bank and a technology leader in banking. He was an early advocate of internet banking and technology-driven financial services in India. He later served as the first president of the New Development Bank (BRICS bank). He is credited with creating the modern Indian private banking sector.
