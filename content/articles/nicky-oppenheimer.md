## Summary

Nicky Oppenheimer was the chairman of De Beers, the world's largest diamond mining company, continuing his family's century-long dominance of the global diamond trade. His grandfather Ernest Oppenheimer took control of De Beers in 1929. Nicky sold the family's 40% stake in De Beers to Anglo American for $5.1 billion in 2012. He is the wealthiest person in South Africa and a major conservationist.
