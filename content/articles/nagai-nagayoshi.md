## Summary

Nagai Nagayoshi was a Japanese chemist who first synthesized methamphetamine in 1893 and isolated ephedrine from Ephedra sinica in 1885. He studied under August Wilhelm von Hofmann in Berlin and became one of Japan's first modern pharmaceutical chemists. He established the foundations of Japanese pharmaceutical science.
