## Summary

Jagadish Chandra Bose was an Indian physicist, biologist, and polymath who pioneered the investigation of radio and microwave optics. He demonstrated wireless communication two years before Marconi and made groundbreaking contributions to plant science by showing that plants respond to stimuli. He invented the crescograph to measure plant growth and is considered one of the fathers of radio science.
