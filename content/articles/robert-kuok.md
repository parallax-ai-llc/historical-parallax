## Summary

Robert Kuok is a Malaysian Chinese billionaire known as the 'Sugar King of Asia' for his dominance of the sugar trade. He built a vast business empire spanning sugar, palm oil, flour milling, hotels (Shangri-La), shipping, and media. He is the richest person in Malaysia and one of the wealthiest in Southeast Asia. His Kuok Group operates across Asia and globally with interests worth tens of billions of dollars.
