## Summary

Justin Martyr was an early Christian apologist and philosopher who sought to reconcile Christian faith with Greek philosophy. His two Apologies and Dialogue with Trypho are among the earliest works of Christian theology. He established a school of Christian philosophy in Rome. He was beheaded during the reign of Marcus Aurelius.
