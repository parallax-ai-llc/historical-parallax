## Summary

Subodh Gupta is an Indian contemporary artist known for his sculptures and installations using everyday Indian kitchen utensils. His monumental works of stainless steel pots, pans, and tiffin carriers transform humble domestic objects into spectacular art. Often called 'the Damien Hirst of Delhi,' he explores themes of migration, tradition, and modernity.
