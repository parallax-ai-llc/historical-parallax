## Summary

Har Gobind Khorana was an Indian-American biochemist who shared the 1968 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his work on the interpretation of the genetic code and its function in protein synthesis. He was the first to synthesize a functional gene and demonstrated how nucleotides in nucleic acids control protein synthesis. His work was fundamental to understanding the genetic code.
