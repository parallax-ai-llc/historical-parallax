## Summary

Park Se-jik was a Korean nuclear physicist who played a crucial role in training Korea's first generation of nuclear engineers. He helped establish nuclear physics programs at Seoul National University and contributed to the design of Korea's research reactors. His educational efforts were vital for Korea's nuclear energy development.
