## Summary

Julie Mehretu is an Ethiopian-American artist known for her densely layered, large-scale abstract paintings that reference architectural plans, maps, and urban landscapes. Her paintings evoke the compressed histories and energies of cities, migration, and geopolitics. She is one of the most important painters working today.
