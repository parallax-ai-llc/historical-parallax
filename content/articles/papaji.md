## Summary

H.W.L. Poonja (Papaji) was an Indian sage and disciple of Ramana Maharshi who became one of the most influential Advaita (non-dual) teachers of the late 20th century. He attracted thousands of Western seekers to his satsangs in Lucknow, India. He emphasized the possibility of instant awakening and the recognition of one's true nature as awareness itself. His students include Gangaji, Mooji, and other popular modern non-dual teachers.
