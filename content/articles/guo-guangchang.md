## Summary

Guo Guangchang co-founded Fosun International, often called 'China's Berkshire Hathaway,' building a diversified conglomerate spanning pharmaceuticals, tourism, insurance, and fashion. Fosun acquired Club Med, Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C., and significant insurance assets globally. His investment philosophy of combining Chinese growth with global resources made Fosun one of China's most international private companies.
