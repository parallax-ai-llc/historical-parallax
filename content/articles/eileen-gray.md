## Summary

Eileen Gray was an Irish-French architect and furniture designer who was a pioneer of the Modern Movement. Her E-1027 house on the French Riviera and her lacquer screens and chrome furniture are icons of 20th-century design. She was largely forgotten until rediscovered in the 1970s.
