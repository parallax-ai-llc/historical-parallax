## Summary

Cheng Dawei was a Ming Dynasty mathematician who wrote the Suanfa Tongzong (General Source of Computational Methods) in 1592, one of the most influential Chinese mathematics texts. It systematized abacus calculation methods and remained the standard mathematics reference for over 300 years. The book spread to Korea, Japan, and Southeast Asia.
