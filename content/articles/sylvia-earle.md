## Summary

Sylvia Earle is one of the world's leading marine scientists and ocean explorers. She set a depth record for solo untethered diving (381 meters), led the first all-female team of aquanauts, and served as chief scientist of NOAA. Known as 'Her Deepness,' she has spent over 7,000 hours underwater and is a tireless advocate for ocean conservation.
