## Summary

## Arrest, Plea, and Sentencing (Publicly Reported)

- Public reporting describes him as one of the most wanted fugitives in Puerto Rico and the Dominican Republic during the period he was at large.

- Some accounts report that he received a 30-year federal prison sentence (reported in 2017), reflecting negotiated dispositions rather than solely trial verdicts.
