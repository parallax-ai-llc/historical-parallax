## Summary

Mary Baker Eddy was the founder of Christian Science and the Church of Christ, Scientist. Her book Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures laid out her theology of spiritual healing and the unreality of matter. She founded The Christian Science Monitor newspaper, which became internationally respected. She was one of the most influential American religious leaders of the 19th century.
