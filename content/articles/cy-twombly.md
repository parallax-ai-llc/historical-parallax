## Summary

Cy Twombly was known for large-scale graffiti-like paintings blending drawing, painting, and writing. His works inspired by Mediterranean culture feature looping calligraphic marks and fragments of text. Based in Rome, he created a unique synthesis of American abstraction and European classical culture.
