## Summary

Ricardo Salinas Pliego is a Mexican billionaire and founder of Grupo Salinas, which controls TV Azteca (Mexico's second-largest broadcaster), Grupo Elektra (retail and banking), and Banco Azteca. His business empire provides financial services to underbanked populations across Latin America. He is one of Mexico's wealthiest individuals and a controversial figure in Mexican media and politics.
