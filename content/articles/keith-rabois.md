## Summary

Keith Rabois (born March 17, 1969) is an American technology executive and investor, known for executive roles at PayPal, LinkedIn, Square, and as a general partner at Khosla Ventures and Founders Fund. A prominent member of the PayPal Mafia, Rabois has been involved in building multiple billion-dollar companies and is known for his strong operational playbooks, mentorship of founders, and outspoken views on tech and politics.
