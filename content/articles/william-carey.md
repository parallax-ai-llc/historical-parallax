## Summary

William Carey was an English Baptist missionary and linguist known as the 'Father of Modern Missions.' He spent over 40 years in India, translating the Bible into numerous Indian languages. He founded the Serampore Mission and Serampore College. His pamphlet 'An Enquiry' inspired the modern Protestant missionary movement.
