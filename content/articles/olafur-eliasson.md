## Summary

Olafur Eliasson is a Danish-Icelandic artist known for large-scale installations that explore perception light and nature. His Weather Project at Tate Modern attracted millions of visitors. His work addresses climate change and environmental awareness through immersive art experiences that transform architectural spaces.
