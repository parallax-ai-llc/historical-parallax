## Summary

Jerry Yang co-founded Yahoo! in 1995, creating one of the first major internet portals and search engines. Yahoo! became the most visited website in the world during the late 1990s. He served as CEO from 2007 to 2009, famously rejecting Microsoft's $44.6 billion acquisition offer. He later co-founded AME Cloud Ventures, focusing on early-stage technology investments.
