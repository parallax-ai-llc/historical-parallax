## Summary

Bernard of Clairvaux was a French abbot and the primary builder of the Cistercian monastic order. He was one of the most influential churchmen of the 12th century, preaching the Second Crusade. His mystical writings on the Song of Songs and divine love profoundly influenced Christian spirituality. He is a Doctor of the Church known as the 'Mellifluous Doctor.'
