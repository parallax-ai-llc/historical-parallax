## Summary

Kim Eun-young is a Korean stem cell biologist who has contributed to regenerative medicine research in South Korea. Her work on adult stem cell therapy applications has advanced treatments for cardiovascular and neurodegenerative diseases, helping Korea maintain its position as a leader in stem cell research.
