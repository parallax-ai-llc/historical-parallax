## Summary

Brian Armstrong co-founded Coinbase in 2012, building it into the largest cryptocurrency exchange in the United States. Coinbase went public in 2021 through a direct listing, reaching a valuation of over $85 billion on its first day of trading. A former Airbnb engineer, he navigated Coinbase through multiple crypto booms and busts. He has been a vocal advocate for cryptocurrency regulation and adoption.
