## Summary

Bob Marley was a Jamaican singer-songwriter who became an international icon through his reggae music. His songs including No Woman No Cry, Redemption Song, and One Love combine Rastafarian spirituality with social protest. He brought reggae and Jamaican culture to the world stage and remains the most widely known reggae artist.
