## Summary

Alois Alzheimer identified and described the disease that bears his name in 1906. He discovered the characteristic amyloid plaques and neurofibrillary tangles in the brain of his patient Auguste Deter. His meticulous clinicopathological observations established the field of neuropathology of dementia.
