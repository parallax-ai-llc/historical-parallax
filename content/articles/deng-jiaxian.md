## Summary

Deng Jiaxian led the development of China's first atomic bomb and hydrogen bomb, making him one of the most important figures in Chinese nuclear weapons history. He received his PhD from Purdue University at age 26 and returned to China to lead its nuclear weapons program. Known as the 'Father of China's nuclear program,' he worked in secrecy for 28 years.
