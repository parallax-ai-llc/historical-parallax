## Overview
Following weeks of civil unrest and a confused announcement by East German officials, crowds of East Germans crossed and climbed onto the Wall, joined by West Germans on the other side.

## Significance
The fall of the Wall paved the way for German reunification and symbolized the end of the Cold War.
