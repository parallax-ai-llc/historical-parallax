## Summary

Ernest Lawrence was an American nuclear physicist who invented the cyclotron, the first particle accelerator. He won the 1932 Nobel Prize in Physics for this invention which became essential for nuclear physics research. Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and element 103 (lawrencium) are named in his honor.
