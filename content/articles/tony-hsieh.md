## Summary

Tony Hsieh was an internet entrepreneur who sold LinkExchange to Microsoft and then became CEO of Zappos, the online shoe retailer famous for its customer service culture. He built Zappos into a billion-dollar company that Amazon acquired for $1.2 billion in 2009. His book 'Delivering Happiness' influenced corporate culture worldwide. He also invested $350 million in revitalizing downtown Las Vegas.
