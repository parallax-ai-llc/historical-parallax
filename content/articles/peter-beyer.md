## Summary

Peter Beyer is a German cell biologist who co-invented Golden Rice with Ingo Potrykus. He engineered the metabolic pathway to produce beta-carotene in rice endosperm and later developed improved versions with higher carotenoid content. His work aimed to address vitamin A deficiency affecting 250 million children in developing countries.
