## Summary

Liang Wengen founded SANY Group, which became China's largest construction machinery manufacturer and one of the largest in the world. SANY produces concrete pumps, excavators, and cranes used in major infrastructure projects globally. He was briefly China's richest person in 2011. He served as a delegate to the National People's Congress and symbolized the rise of Chinese private manufacturing.
