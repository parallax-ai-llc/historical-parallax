## Summary

Bill Ackman is the founder and CEO of Pershing Square Capital Management, one of the most prominent activist hedge funds. He is known for high-profile campaigns including a $1 billion short on Herbalife and investments in Chipotle, Canadian Pacific Railway, and Universal Music Group. His Twitter/X presence and willingness to take public positions on social and political issues make him one of the most visible investors on Wall Street.
