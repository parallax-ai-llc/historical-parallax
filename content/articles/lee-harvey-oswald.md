## Summary
Lee Harvey Oswald was the man identified by the Warren Commission as the assassin of President John F. Kennedy on November 22, 1963, in Dallas, Texas. He was himself murdered by Jack Ruby two days later while in police custody, making him unable to stand trial. Conspiracy theories about the assassination persist.
