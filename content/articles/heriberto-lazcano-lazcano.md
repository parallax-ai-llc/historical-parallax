## Summary
Heriberto Lazcano Lazcano, known as 'El Lazca' or 'The Executioner,' was the supreme leader of Los Zetas cartel. A former Mexican special forces soldier, he turned the Zetas into one of Mexico's most feared criminal organizations. He was killed in a shootout with Mexican marines in 2012.
