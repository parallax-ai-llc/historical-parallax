## Overview
On October 26, 1909, An Jung-geun shot and killed Ito Hirobumi, a four-time Prime Minister of Japan and key architect of the Japanese annexation of Korea, as he arrived at Harbin Station in Manchuria.

## Impact
This act of resistance drew international attention to the plight of Korea under Japanese aggression. An Jung-geun remains one of the most revered figures in Korean history.
