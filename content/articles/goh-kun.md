## Summary

Goh Kun is a Korean public administrator who served as Prime Minister and Acting President. As Seoul's mayor, he oversaw the construction of the Seoul subway system and the modernization of Seoul's infrastructure using advanced engineering technologies. His administrative expertise in deploying technology for urban development was notable.
