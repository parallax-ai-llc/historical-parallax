## Summary

Barnes Wallis designed the bouncing bomb used in the famous Dambusters raid of World War II, the geodetic airframe structure used in Vickers Wellington bombers, and the Tallboy and Grand Slam earthquake bombs. His engineering innovations solved seemingly impossible military challenges and advanced aircraft and weapons design.
