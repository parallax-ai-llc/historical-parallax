## Summary

Kim Bong-jin founded Woowa Brothers, the company behind Baedal Minjok (Baemin), South Korea's largest food delivery platform. Baemin transformed Korean food delivery with a mobile-first approach and became a cultural phenomenon with its distinctive branding. The company was acquired by Delivery Hero for $4 billion in 2019. Kim became one of South Korea's most celebrated young entrepreneurs.
