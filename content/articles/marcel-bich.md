## Summary

Marcel Bich founded Société Bic and created the iconic Bic ballpoint pen, disposable lighter, and disposable razor. He purchased the patent for the ballpoint pen and developed an inexpensive, reliable writing instrument that sold billions of units worldwide. His philosophy of creating affordable, disposable versions of everyday products transformed multiple industries. Bic products are used in virtually every country in the world.
