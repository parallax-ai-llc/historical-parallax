## Summary

Sakuma Shozan was a late Edo period Japanese scholar and technologist who experimented with Western science and military technology. He cast cannons using Western techniques, built a telegraph device, and experimented with glass-making. He advocated 'Eastern ethics, Western science' and trained many reform leaders. He was assassinated by anti-Western extremists.
