## Summary

Though primarily a musician, Tan Dun is notable for his innovative use of natural materials and technology in music. He creates instruments from water, paper, and organic materials, merging art with acoustic science. His Water Concerto and Paper Concerto explore the physics of sound in novel ways. He won the Academy Award for Crouching Tiger music.
