## Summary

Lin Hwai-min is a Taiwanese choreographer who founded the Cloud Gate Dance Theatre, one of Asia's foremost contemporary dance companies. His works fuse Chinese calligraphy, martial arts, and Western modern dance into a distinctive Asian contemporary dance language.
