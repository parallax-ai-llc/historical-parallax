## Summary

Spencer Silver invented the low-tack, reusable adhesive used in Post-it Notes while working at 3M in 1968. Though initially considered a failed experiment, his colleague Art Fry found the application of using it for bookmarks. Post-it Notes became one of the most successful office products ever, generating billions in revenue for 3M.
