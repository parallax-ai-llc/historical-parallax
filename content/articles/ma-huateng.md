## Summary

Ma Huateng (Pony Ma) co-founded Tencent Holdings in 1998, building it into China's most valuable technology company and the world's largest gaming company. Tencent's WeChat messaging app has over 1.3 billion users and dominates Chinese digital life. Tencent also controls major gaming studios, is the largest shareholder in Epic Games, and operates China's leading music and video streaming services. He has been one of Asia's richest people for over a decade.
