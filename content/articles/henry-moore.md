## Summary

Henry Moore was a British sculptor best known for his monumental semi-abstract bronze figures. His reclining figures, family groups, and organic forms became ubiquitous in public spaces worldwide. His wartime drawings of Londoners sheltering in the Underground are equally celebrated.
