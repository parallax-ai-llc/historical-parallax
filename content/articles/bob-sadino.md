## Summary

Bob Sadino was one of Indonesia's most eccentric and beloved entrepreneurs. He built a business empire from selling eggs door-to-door, eventually founding Kemchicks, Jakarta's first upscale supermarket, and Kemfood meat processing company. Known for his signature shorts and casual style, he became a folk hero for Indonesian entrepreneurs and wrote bestselling books about unconventional business philosophy.
