## Summary
Chester Dewayne Turner was an American serial killer convicted of 14 murders in Los Angeles between 1987 and 1998. He targeted sex workers and drug addicts. DNA evidence linked him to the crimes in 2002. He was sentenced to death. An innocent man, David Allen Jones, spent years in prison for one of Turner's crimes.
