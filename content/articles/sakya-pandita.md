## Summary

Sakya Pandita (Kunga Gyaltsen) was one of the greatest scholars in Tibetan Buddhist history and the founder of the Sakya school's political influence. He was renowned for his mastery of the five sciences and his works on logic and epistemology. He negotiated with the Mongol prince Godan Khan, preventing a Mongol invasion of Tibet. His Treasury of Valid Reasoning is a foundational text of Tibetan Buddhist epistemology.
