## Summary

Arnaldo Tamayo Méndez became the first person of African descent, the first Cuban, and the first Latin American to fly in space when he traveled to the Salyut 6 space station aboard Soyuz 38 in 1980. His 7-day mission included materials science and Earth observation experiments. He later became a brigadier general in the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces.
