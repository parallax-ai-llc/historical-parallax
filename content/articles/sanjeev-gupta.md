## Summary

British-Indian steel magnate under fraud investigation after Greensill Capital's collapse.

## Background

Sanjeev Gupta is known for building an industrial group focused on metals, manufacturing, and related assets, often through acquisitions of distressed or underperforming plants.
His business interests have been associated with the GFG Alliance and the Liberty Steel brand in various countries.

## Business Model (High-Level)

Public reporting has described a strategy that can include:

- Buying industrial assets (sometimes from insolvency situations).
- Rebranding and attempting operational turnaround.
- Using structured financing and supply-chain finance arrangements to fund working capital.

The group structure has been described as complex, with multiple entities and jurisdictions.

## Greensill Capital Link

After the collapse of Greensill Capital (a supply-chain finance firm), Gupta’s companies became a major focus of attention because of their reported exposure to Greensill-linked financing.
This contributed to heightened scrutiny from lenders, regulators, and law-enforcement bodies.

## Investigations and Allegations (High-Level)

Gupta has faced investigations and allegations related to financial reporting, financing arrangements, and corporate governance.
