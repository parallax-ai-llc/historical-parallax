## Summary

Renzo Piano is an Italian architect known for his high-tech approach and masterful use of light and materials. His co-design of the Centre Pompidou with Richard Rogers revolutionized museum architecture. The Shard in London and the Whitney Museum of American Art are among his most celebrated works. He received the Pritzker Prize in 1998.
