## Summary

Jesus Rafael Soto was a Venezuelan artist and a leading figure of Op Art and Kinetic Art. His Penetrables, room-sized installations of hanging colored threads that viewers walk through, create immersive experiences of movement and color. His work explores perception through viewer participation.
