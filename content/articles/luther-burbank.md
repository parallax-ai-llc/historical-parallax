## Summary

Luther Burbank was America's most famous plant breeder, developing over 800 new varieties of plants including the Russet Burbank potato, the Shasta daisy, and the Santa Rosa plum. His experimental farms in California became legendary for producing improved fruits, flowers, and vegetables. His work influenced plant genetics and agricultural productivity.
