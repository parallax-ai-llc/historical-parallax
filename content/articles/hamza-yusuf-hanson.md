## Summary

Hamza Yusuf is one of the most influential Islamic scholars in the Western world. Born Mark Hanson, he converted to Islam in 1977 and spent years studying with traditional scholars in Mauritania, the UAE, and elsewhere. He co-founded Zaytuna College, the first accredited Muslim liberal arts college in the US. He emphasizes classical Islamic learning and has been a bridge between Islamic scholarship and the wider American public.
