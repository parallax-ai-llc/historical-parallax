## Summary

Chamath Palihapitiya is a venture capitalist and founder of Social Capital who became known as the 'SPAC King' for his prolific use of special purpose acquisition companies. A former Facebook executive, he invested in companies including Slack, Box, and Virgin Galactic. His SPAC that took Virgin Galactic public in 2019 pioneered the SPAC boom. He is also known for his outspoken commentary on markets and society through his podcast 'All-In.'
