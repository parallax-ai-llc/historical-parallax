## Summary

Seyyed Hossein Nasr is an Iranian-American philosopher and scholar of Islamic studies who is one of the most prominent Muslim intellectuals in the world. He has written over 50 books on Islamic philosophy, science, art, and spirituality. He advocates for the perennial philosophy tradition and has been a vocal critic of modernism's impact on the environment and spiritual life. He was the first Muslim to deliver the Gifford Lectures.
