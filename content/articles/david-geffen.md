## Summary

David Geffen is an American business magnate who built three major entertainment empires: Asylum Records, Geffen Records, and DreamWorks SKG. He signed iconic artists including John Lennon, Nirvana, and Guns N' Roses. He co-founded DreamWorks with Steven Spielberg and Jeffrey Katzenberg. He is one of the wealthiest people in entertainment and a major philanthropist, donating to UCLA, Lincoln Center, and MoMA.
