## Summary

Elisabeth Elliot was an American Christian author and missionary who returned to serve the Auca (Waorani) people of Ecuador after they killed her husband Jim Elliot. Her book Through Gates of Splendor about the martyrdom of the five missionaries became a classic of Christian literature. She wrote over 20 books and hosted the radio program Gateway to Joy. She was one of the most influential evangelical women of the 20th century.
