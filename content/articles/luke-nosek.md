## Summary

Luke Nosek is a Polish-American entrepreneur and venture capitalist who co-founded PayPal and Founders Fund. One of the original members of the "PayPal Mafia," Nosek has been instrumental in building multiple billion-dollar companies and investing in transformative technologies including SpaceX, Tesla, and Airbnb.

## PayPal (1998-2002)

### Co-founder

**Confinity**:
- Co-founded with Max Levchin and Peter Thiel (1998)
- Vice President of Marketing and Strategy
- Helped develop Palm Pilot payment system
- Merged with X.com (1999)

**Key Contributions**:
- Business development
- Strategic partnerships
- Growth strategies
- Marketing vision

**eBay Sale** (2002):
- PayPal acquired for $1.5 billion
- Made significant wealth
- Core PayPal Mafia member
