## Summary

Luis Carlos Sarmiento Angulo is Colombia's richest person and the founder of Grupo Aval, the largest financial conglomerate in Colombia and Central America. Grupo Aval controls four of Colombia's largest banks and operates in multiple Central American countries. He also has major interests in construction, energy, and agriculture. He built his fortune from a small construction company into the dominant financial group in the Andean region.
