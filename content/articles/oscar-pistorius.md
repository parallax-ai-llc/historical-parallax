## Summary
Oscar Leonard Carl Pistorius is a South African former sprint runner who competed in the Olympics on prosthetic legs. He murdered his girlfriend Reeva Steenkamp on Valentine's Day 2013, shooting her through a bathroom door. He was convicted of murder and sentenced to 13 years. Paroled in 2024.
