## Summary

Morten Meldal is a Danish chemist who shared the 2022 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development of click chemistry and bioorthogonal chemistry. He independently discovered the copper-catalyzed azide-alkyne cycloaddition (CuAAC) reaction, a foundational click chemistry reaction used in drug discovery, materials science, and biochemistry.
