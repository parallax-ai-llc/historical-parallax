## Summary

Jimmy Iovine co-founded Interscope Records and Beats Electronics, becoming one of the most powerful figures in the music and technology industries. He discovered and produced artists including U2, Eminem, and Lady Gaga. He co-founded Beats with Dr. Dre, which Apple acquired for $3 billion in 2014. He then served as head of Apple Music. His ability to bridge music, technology, and fashion created new models for the entertainment industry.
