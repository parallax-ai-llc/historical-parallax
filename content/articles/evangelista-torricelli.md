## Summary

Evangelista Torricelli was an Italian physicist and mathematician who invented the barometer and is known for Torricelli's theorem in fluid dynamics. As a student of Galileo, he demonstrated that atmospheric pressure exists by creating the first sustained vacuum (Torricellian vacuum). The unit of pressure torr is named in his honor. He also made advances in optics and the study of cycloid curves.
