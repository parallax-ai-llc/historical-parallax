## Summary

Mata Amritanandamayi, known as Amma (Mother), is an Indian Hindu guru and humanitarian known as the 'Hugging Saint.' She is estimated to have hugged over 40 million people during her public programs worldwide. She heads the Mata Amritanandamayi Math, which runs extensive charitable programs including disaster relief, healthcare, and housing. She has been recognized by the United Nations for her humanitarian work.
