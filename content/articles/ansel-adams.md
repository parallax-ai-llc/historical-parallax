## Summary

Ansel Adams was an American photographer and environmentalist known for his iconic black-and-white landscape photographs of the American West. His images of Yosemite and other national parks combined technical mastery with deep environmental conviction. He developed the Zone System of exposure control and co-founded Group f/64.
