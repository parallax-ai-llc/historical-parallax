## Summary

Elder Amphilochios of Patmos was a Greek Orthodox monk and abbot who was a remarkable spiritual figure and early environmentalist. He served as abbot of the Monastery of Saint John the Theologian on Patmos. He was known for giving tree-planting as a penance to those who confessed to him, viewing the destruction of the natural world as a sin. He is cited as a precursor to Orthodox ecological theology.
