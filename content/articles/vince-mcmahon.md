## Summary

Vince McMahon built WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) from a regional wrestling promotion into a global entertainment empire worth billions. He transformed professional wrestling from a fragmented regional business into a nationally televised phenomenon. He created WrestleMania, one of the most successful annual entertainment events in the world. He was forced to resign in 2024 amid sexual misconduct allegations after overseeing WWE's merger with UFC.
