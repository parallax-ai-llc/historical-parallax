## Summary

Pietro Perugino was an influential Italian Renaissance painter and the primary teacher of Raphael. His serene compositions and graceful figures, exemplified in The Delivery of the Keys in the Sistine Chapel, established a visual vocabulary that shaped High Renaissance art. His sweet, harmonious style became the foundation upon which Raphael built.
