## Summary
Naw Kham was a Burmese drug lord who controlled drug trafficking along the Mekong River. He ordered the Mekong River massacre of 2011, killing 13 Chinese sailors. He was captured by Chinese police in a joint operation with Laos and executed in China in 2013.
