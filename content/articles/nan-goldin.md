## Summary

Nan Goldin is an American photographer known for her intimate, diaristic photographs documenting her life and the lives of her friends in the LGBTQ and drug subcultures. Her slideshow The Ballad of Sexual Dependency is a landmark of confessional photography. She has also become a prominent activist against the Sackler family's role in the opioid crisis.
