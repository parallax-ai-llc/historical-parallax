## Summary

Kim Chang-kyeong is a Korean physicist who pioneered photovoltaic solar energy research in South Korea. His work on silicon solar cell efficiency improvement contributed to the development of Korea's solar energy industry, which now includes major manufacturers like Hanwha Q Cells.
