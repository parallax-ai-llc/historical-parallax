## Summary

Sergius of Radonezh is the most venerated saint in Russian Orthodoxy and the founder of the Trinity Lavra of St. Sergius, the most important monastery in Russia. He revived Russian monasticism during the Mongol occupation and promoted cenobitic (communal) monastic life. He blessed Prince Dmitry Donskoy before the Battle of Kulikovo against the Mongols. He is considered the patron saint of Russia.
