## Summary
Roberto Succo was an Italian serial killer who murdered at least seven people across Italy and France. He killed both parents at 19, escaped a psychiatric hospital, and continued murdering across Europe before dying in custody in 1988.

## Early Life
Born April 3, 1962, in Mestre, near Venice. On April 9, 1981, at age 19, he murdered both parents. He was committed to a psychiatric hospital in Reggio Emilia. [^1]

## Criminal Career
Succo escaped the hospital in 1986 and fled to France. Over two years, he committed murders, rapes, robberies, and carjackings across southern France. He killed a police officer in Toulon. He maintained relationships while committing crimes. [^2]
