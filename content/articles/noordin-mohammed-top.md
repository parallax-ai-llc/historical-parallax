## Summary

Malaysian terrorist and Jemaah Islamiyah bomb maker responsible for multiple deadly attacks in Indonesia including the 2003 Marriott bombing, 2004 Australian embassy bombing, and 2005 Bali bombings. Killed in police raid.

## Context

Noordin Mohammed Top is commonly described in reporting and reference summaries as a militant figure associated with **Jemaah Islamiyah (JI)**, an extremist network in Southeast Asia that was linked to major terrorist attacks in the region in the early 2000s. His case is often discussed as part of Indonesia’s broader post-2002 counterterrorism campaign.
