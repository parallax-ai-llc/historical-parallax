## Summary

Marvin Minsky was an American cognitive scientist and computer scientist who co-founded the MIT Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. He made numerous contributions to AI, cognitive science, mathematics, and robotics. His books 'The Society of Mind' and 'The Emotion Machine' presented influential theories of how minds work. He received the Turing Award in 1969 for his central role in creating and shaping AI.
