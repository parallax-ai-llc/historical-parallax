## Summary

Markus 'Notch' Persson created Minecraft, one of the best-selling video games of all time with over 300 million copies sold. He developed the game independently and founded Mojang to publish it. Microsoft acquired Mojang for $2.5 billion in 2014, making Persson a billionaire. Minecraft became a cultural and educational phenomenon used in schools worldwide. After selling, he publicly expressed unhappiness with billionaire life.
