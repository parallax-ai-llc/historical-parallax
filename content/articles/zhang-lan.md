## Summary

Zhang Lan is the founder of South Beauty Group, one of China's most prestigious restaurant chains known for high-end Sichuan cuisine. She built the brand from a single restaurant into a luxury dining empire with over 70 locations. She was briefly one of China's richest women. Her attempt to take South Beauty public through a SPAC failed, and she lost control of the company in a leveraged buyout dispute.
