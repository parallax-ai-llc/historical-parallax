## Summary

Michael O'Leary is the CEO of Ryanair, Europe's largest airline by passenger numbers. Under his leadership, Ryanair became the world's largest international airline, carrying over 180 million passengers annually. He is known for his controversial cost-cutting measures and combative personality. His transformation of Ryanair from a small Irish airline into a pan-European carrier is considered one of the greatest business turnarounds in aviation history.
