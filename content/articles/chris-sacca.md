## Summary

Chris Sacca is a venture capitalist who founded Lowercase Capital, making early investments in Twitter, Uber, Instagram, and Kickstarter. His investments in Twitter and Uber alone generated returns of over $5 billion. He appeared as a guest shark on Shark Tank. Before venture capital, he worked at Google on corporate development. His cowboy-shirt-wearing persona made him one of the most recognizable VCs in Silicon Valley.
