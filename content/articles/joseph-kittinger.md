## Summary

Joseph Kittinger set the record for the highest parachute jump, leaping from a helium balloon at 102,800 feet (31,300 m) during Project Excelsior in 1960. During his freefall, he reached a speed of 614 mph before deploying his parachute. His courage and the data gathered from his jump helped develop procedures for high-altitude aircraft escape systems.
