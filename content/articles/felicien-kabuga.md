## Summary
Félicien Kabuga is a Rwandan businessman and alleged financier of the 1994 Rwandan genocide. He funded Radio Télévision Libre des Mille Collines which incited genocide. He was one of the most wanted fugitives for 25 years before being arrested in Paris in 2020.
