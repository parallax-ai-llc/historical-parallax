## Summary

John Clauser is an American theoretical and experimental physicist who shared the 2022 Nobel Prize in Physics for experiments with entangled photons establishing the violation of Bell inequalities. In 1972, he performed the first experimental test of Bell's theorem, showing that quantum mechanics is correct and local hidden variable theories are not.
