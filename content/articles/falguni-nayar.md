## Summary

Falguni Nayar founded Nykaa, India's largest beauty and fashion e-commerce platform. She left a successful career as a managing director at Kotak Mahindra Bank to start Nykaa at age 50. The company went public in 2021 at a $13 billion valuation, briefly making her India's richest self-made woman. Nykaa pioneered beauty e-commerce in India, carrying both international and Indian brands.
