## Summary

Imamichi Tomonobu was a Japanese philosopher who developed the concept of 'eco-ethica,' a systematic ethical framework for understanding technology's relationship with nature and humanity. His work on the ethics of technology influenced Japanese approaches to environmental technology and responsible innovation.
