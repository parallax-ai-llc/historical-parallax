## Summary

John Malone is an American billionaire businessman who built Tele-Communications Inc. (TCI) into the largest cable television company in the United States. He is chairman of Liberty Media and holds interests in Charter Communications, Discovery, and Formula One Group. Known as the 'Cable Cowboy,' he pioneered the cable TV industry's consolidation and is the largest private landowner in the United States with over 2.2 million acres.
