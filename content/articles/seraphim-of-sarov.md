## Summary

Seraphim of Sarov was a Russian Orthodox monk and mystic who is one of the most venerated saints in the Orthodox Church. He lived as a hermit in the forests near the Sarov Monastery for decades, practicing extreme asceticism. He taught that the goal of Christian life is the acquisition of the Holy Spirit. His joyful greeting 'My joy, Christ is Risen!' reflects his profound spiritual experience.
