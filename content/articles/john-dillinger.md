## Summary
John Herbert Dillinger was an American bank robber and gangster during the Great Depression. He was one of the FBI's first 'Public Enemies.' He robbed two dozen banks and four police stations. He was shot and killed by FBI agents outside the Biograph Theater in Chicago in 1934.
