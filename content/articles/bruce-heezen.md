## Summary

Bruce Heezen was an American geologist who, together with Marie Tharp, created the first comprehensive maps of the ocean floor. Their work revealed the global mid-ocean ridge system and rift valleys, providing key evidence for seafloor spreading and plate tectonics. He died of a heart attack in a submarine while surveying the Mid-Atlantic Ridge.
