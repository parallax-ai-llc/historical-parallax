## Summary

Park Tae-joon was a Korean military officer and industrialist who founded POSCO (Pohang Iron and Steel Company) and built it into the world's most efficient steel producer. Despite having no steelmaking experience, he established a world-class integrated steel mill that was crucial for South Korea's industrialization and economic development.
