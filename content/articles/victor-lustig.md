## Summary
Victor Lustig was one of the most notorious con artists of the early 20th century. He is best known for 'selling' the Eiffel Tower twice to scrap metal dealers. He also created a 'money printing machine' scam. He was eventually convicted of counterfeiting and died in Alcatraz.
