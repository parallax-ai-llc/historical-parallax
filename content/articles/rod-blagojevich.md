## Summary

American politician who served as governor of Illinois and was convicted of corruption for attempting to sell Barack Obama's vacated Senate seat. He was recorded saying the seat was 'golden.' Sentence commuted by Trump.

## Legal and political significance (high level)

- The scandal is frequently discussed in connection with the vacancy created when Barack Obama left the U.S. Senate to become president.
