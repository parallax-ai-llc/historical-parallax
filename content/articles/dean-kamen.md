## Summary

Dean Kamen invented the Segway personal transporter, the AutoSyringe insulin pump, the iBOT mobility system, and a water purification device for developing countries. He founded FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) robotics competition, inspiring millions of students worldwide. He holds over 1,000 patents.
