## Summary

Neem Karoli Baba (Maharaj-ji) was an Indian Hindu guru and devotee of Hanuman who became famous in the West through his American and European followers. He is known for his simplicity, miracles, and teachings on love and service. His Western followers include Ram Dass (Richard Alpert), Krishna Das, and Apple co-founder Steve Jobs (who visited his ashram). He emphasized devotion to God and service to humanity over formal teachings.
