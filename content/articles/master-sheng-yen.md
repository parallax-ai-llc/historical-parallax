## Summary

Master Sheng-yen was a Chinese Chan (Zen) Buddhist monk who founded Dharma Drum Mountain, a major Buddhist organization in Taiwan. He held transmission in both the Linji and Caodong lineages. He earned two doctorates and bridged traditional Chinese Buddhism with modern academic scholarship. He founded meditation centers worldwide and wrote prolifically on Chan practice and Buddhist philosophy.
