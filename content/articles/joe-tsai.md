## Summary

Joe Tsai is the co-founder and executive vice chairman of Alibaba Group and the owner of the Brooklyn Nets and New York Liberty. He was one of Jack Ma's earliest partners and played a crucial role in Alibaba's legal and financial structure. He negotiated Alibaba's investments and its record-breaking 2014 IPO. His purchase of the Brooklyn Nets and Barclays Center for $3.5 billion was the largest transaction in U.S. sports history at the time.
