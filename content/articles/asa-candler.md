## Summary

Asa Candler was an American businessman who purchased the Coca-Cola formula from its inventor John Pemberton and built it into one of the world's most recognizable brands. He founded The Coca-Cola Company and transformed it from a local pharmacy drink into a nationally distributed product. His innovative marketing and franchised bottling system were key to Coca-Cola's success. He later served as mayor of Atlanta.
