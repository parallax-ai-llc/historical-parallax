## Summary

Rose of Lima was a Peruvian Dominican tertiary who was the first person born in the Americas to be canonized by the Catholic Church. She practiced severe asceticism and mystical prayer, and devoted her life to helping the sick and poor. She established an infirmary in her home and cared for indigenous and enslaved people. She is the patron saint of Latin America, the Philippines, and Peru.
