## Summary

Al-Battani (Albategnius) was one of the greatest astronomers of the Islamic Golden Age. He improved measurements of the length of the year, discovered the direction of the Sun's apogee changes over time, and introduced trigonometric methods into astronomical calculations. His Zij al-Sabi greatly influenced European astronomy including Copernicus.
