Noam Chomsky is a renowned linguist, philosopher, and political activist. Reports emerged that he had met with Jeffrey Epstein on several occasions, which Chomsky acknowledged but explained as unrelated to any criminal activity.

## Acknowledged Meetings

Chomsky confirmed meeting with Epstein, stating the meetings were:
- Discussions about academic and political matters
- Unrelated to any illegal activities
- Not indicative of friendship or close association

## Chomsky's Explanation

Chomsky stated he met with Epstein because:
- Epstein presented himself as interested in funding scientific research
- The meetings were exploratory regarding potential academic collaboration
- He had no knowledge of Epstein's crimes at the time

## No Criminal Allegations

There are no allegations of criminal conduct or inappropriate behavior by Chomsky related to Epstein.

## Criticism

Chomsky faced criticism for:
- Meeting with Epstein after his 2008 conviction
- Not being more forthcoming about the meetings initially

Chomsky has defended the meetings as ordinary academic networking unrelated to Epstein's crimes.
