## Summary

Constantin Brancusi was a Romanian sculptor who pioneered abstract sculpture with his radically simplified forms. His Bird in Space, Endless Column, and The Kiss reduced sculptural form to its essence. He profoundly influenced modern sculpture and design worldwide.
