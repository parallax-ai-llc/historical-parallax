## Summary

Malian Fulani jihadist who leads the Macina Liberation Front, a militant group that has carried out attacks across central Mali. He has exploited ethnic tensions between Fulani herders and Dogon farmers.

## Context

Koufa is associated with the militant Islamist landscape in Mali and the wider Sahel, where insurgent groups have expanded amid weak state presence, local conflicts, and cross-border security challenges.

## Macina Liberation Front (Katiba Macina)

- Katiba Macina is often described as part of a broader jihadist coalition structure in the region.
- The group has been linked to attacks and intimidation campaigns in central Mali, including targeting security forces and local officials.
- Analysts frequently note how recruitment and messaging can intersect with community grievances and intercommunal violence.

## Links to Regional Coalitions

The Macina Liberation Front has been reported as aligned with Jama'at Nasr al-Islam wal Muslimin (JNIM), an umbrella network active in Mali and neighboring countries.
