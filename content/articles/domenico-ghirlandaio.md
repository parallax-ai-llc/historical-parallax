## Summary

Domenico Ghirlandaio was a leading Florentine Renaissance painter known for elaborate narrative fresco cycles depicting contemporary Florentine life. He ran one of the most successful workshops in Florence, where a young Michelangelo trained. His detailed, naturalistic style made him one of the most sought-after painters of his era.
