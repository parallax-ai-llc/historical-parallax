## Summary

Diebedo Francis Kere is a Burkinabe-German architect who became the first African to receive the Pritzker Prize in 2022. His community-driven designs in Burkina Faso use local materials and traditional techniques in innovative ways. His Gando Primary School launched his career and demonstrated that architecture can empower communities.
