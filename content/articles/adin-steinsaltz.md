## Summary

Adin Steinsaltz (Even-Israel) was an Israeli rabbi, scholar, and educator who completed the first modern translation and commentary on the entire Babylonian Talmud. This monumental work, which took over 45 years, made the Talmud accessible to a new generation of students. He also translated the Talmud into English, French, Russian, and Spanish. He wrote over 60 books on Jewish thought, philosophy, and mysticism.
