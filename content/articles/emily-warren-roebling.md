## Summary

Emily Warren Roebling effectively served as chief engineer of the Brooklyn Bridge construction when her husband Washington became incapacitated by decompression sickness. She studied mathematics, bridge engineering, and materials science to communicate technical decisions between her husband and the site. She was the first person to cross the completed bridge.
