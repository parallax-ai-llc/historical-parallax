## Summary

Mitsuko Uchida is a Japanese-British pianist known for her profound interpretations of Mozart, Schubert, and Beethoven. Her complete cycles of Mozart and Schubert piano sonatas are considered definitive recordings. She is also an acclaimed chamber musician and artistic director of the Marlboro Music Festival.
