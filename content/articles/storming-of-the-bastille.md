## Summary

The Storming of the Bastille on July 14, 1789, was the symbolic beginning of the French Revolution. A Parisian mob attacked the royal fortress-prison seeking weapons and ammunition. The fall of the Bastille symbolized the end of royal tyranny and is celebrated as France's national day.
