## Summary

Katalin Karik√≥ spent decades researching mRNA technology despite lack of funding and academic demotion. Her discovery with Drew Weissman that modified nucleosides make mRNA safe and effective for therapeutic use was the scientific foundation for the Pfizer-BioNTech and Moderna COVID-19 vaccines. She shared the 2023 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine.
