## Summary

Czech Republic's richest man killed in a helicopter crash in Alaska.

Kellner built his fortune primarily through the investment group **PPF** (PPF Group), expanding from post-communist privatization into **consumer finance**, **telecommunications**, and other large-scale holdings in Central and Eastern Europe and beyond.[^1]

Key points (high-level):
- Known for a long-running role as the principal owner of **PPF Group**.
- Associated with the consumer finance business **Home Credit** (part of the PPF ecosystem).
- Often ranked as the **wealthiest person in the Czech Republic** during his lifetime.
- Died on **2021-03-27** in a helicopter crash near Knik Glacier, Alaska.[^1]
