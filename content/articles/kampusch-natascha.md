## Summary

Austrian woman kidnapped at age 10 and held in a cellar for 8 years by Wolfgang Přiklopil. She escaped in 2006 while her captor was distracted and ran to neighbors for help.

## Background

- The case received extensive media coverage in Austria and internationally due to the length of captivity and the victim's age at the time of abduction.
- After her escape, Kampusch gave interviews describing strict control, isolation, and psychological manipulation during captivity.
- The abductor died shortly after her escape, which limited the possibility of a full criminal trial and left some questions about motives and potential accomplices to be addressed largely through investigation and reporting.
