David Copperfield is a world-famous magician and illusionist. His name appeared in Epstein-related court documents in testimony regarding a dinner where he performed magic tricks.

## Context of Mention

According to witness testimony:
- Copperfield attended a dinner at Epstein's residence
- He performed magic tricks for guests
- He allegedly made comments about having "girls" from an agency

## Status

There are no criminal charges or formal allegations against Copperfield related to Epstein. The mention in documents relates to witness recollection of conversations at a social event.
