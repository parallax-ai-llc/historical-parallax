## Summary

Muhammad al-Idrisi created the Tabula Rogeriana in 1154, the most advanced world map of the medieval period, for King Roger II of Sicily. His geography book Nuzhat al-Mushtaq described the entire known world with remarkable detail and accuracy. His cartographic work was the definitive geographical reference for centuries.
