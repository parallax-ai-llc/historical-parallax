## Summary

S. N. Goenka was a Burmese-Indian meditation teacher who became one of the most influential teachers of vipassana meditation in the modern world. He studied under Sayagyi U Ba Khin and brought vipassana to India, where he established a global network of meditation centers. His 10-day vipassana courses, offered free of charge, have been attended by millions worldwide. He emphasized the non-sectarian, universal nature of meditation practice.
