## Summary

Jiddu Krishnamurti was an Indian philosopher and spiritual teacher who was groomed by the Theosophical Society as the coming World Teacher but famously dissolved the organization created for him in 1929. He spent over 60 years traveling the world giving talks on the nature of the mind, meditation, and human freedom. He rejected all organized religion, authority, and tradition. His dialogues with scientists, philosophers, and spiritual seekers attracted worldwide following.
