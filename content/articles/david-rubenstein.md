## Summary

David Rubenstein co-founded The Carlyle Group in 1987, building it into one of the world's largest private equity firms with over $400 billion in assets under management. Before founding Carlyle, he served as a domestic policy advisor in the Carter White House. He is known for his philanthropic efforts in 'patriotic philanthropy,' including funding repairs to the Washington Monument and purchases of historic documents.
