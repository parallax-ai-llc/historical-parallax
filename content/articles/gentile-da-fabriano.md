## Summary

Gentile da Fabriano was one of the leading Italian painters of the International Gothic style. His masterpiece, the Adoration of the Magi (1423), exemplifies the lavish decorative richness of the style with its gold leaf and courtly elegance. He was highly esteemed by contemporaries and influenced the early Renaissance.
