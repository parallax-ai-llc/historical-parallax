## Summary

Ryuichi Sakamoto was a Japanese musician and composer known for his work with Yellow Magic Orchestra and his award-winning film scores. He won an Academy Award for The Last Emperor score and a Grammy for his work on numerous films. His music spans electronic, classical, ambient, and avant-garde genres. He was also a prominent environmental and anti-nuclear activist.
