## Summary

Roddam Narasimha was an Indian aerospace scientist and fluid dynamics expert who made significant contributions to turbulent flow theory, atmospheric sciences, and aerospace engineering. He served as chairman of the Engineering Mechanics unit at IISc and helped design India's Light Combat Aircraft (LCA Tejas). He received the Padma Vibhushan.
