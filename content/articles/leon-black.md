## Summary

Leon Black co-founded Apollo Global Management in 1990, building it into one of the world's largest alternative investment managers. Apollo specialized in distressed debt and leveraged buyout investments. Under his leadership, Apollo managed over $500 billion in assets. He resigned as chairman in 2021 amid revelations of his financial relationship with Jeffrey Epstein, though he denied any wrongdoing.
