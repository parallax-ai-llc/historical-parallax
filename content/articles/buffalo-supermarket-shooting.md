## Summary

Racist mass shooting at Tops supermarket in Buffalo, New York on May 14, 2022 killing 10 Black people. The shooter was motivated by white supremacist replacement theory.

## Additional Context

- **Location:** Tops Friendly Markets supermarket, Buffalo, New York.
- **Date:** May 14, 2022.
- **Victims:** Ten people were killed; the attack was widely described as racially motivated.

## Motivation and Ideology (High-Level)

- **White supremacist framing:** Reporting connected the shooter’s stated beliefs to "replacement" conspiracy narratives.
- **Target selection:** The choice of location was widely discussed in terms of demographic targeting.

## Investigation and Legal Outcome

- **Evidence:** The case involved extensive digital evidence and public documentation.
- **Sentencing:** Public reporting describes a life sentence in state court, with additional federal proceedings discussed in coverage.

## Broader Significance

- **Domestic extremism:** The attack is often cited in analyses of online radicalization and extremist violence.
- **Community impact:** The shooting intensified local and national discussions about racism, public safety, and trauma support.
