## Summary

Carl Benz was a German engineer who invented the first practical automobile powered by an internal combustion engine. His Benz Patent-Motorwagen of 1886 is considered the first true automobile. He founded Benz & Cie., which later merged with Daimler to form Mercedes-Benz. His wife Bertha's famous long-distance drive proved the car's practicality.
