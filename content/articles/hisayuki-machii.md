## Summary
Hisayuki Machii was a Korean-Japanese yakuza boss who founded the Tosei-kai, one of the largest yakuza organizations in postwar Tokyo. He controlled extensive gambling, entertainment, and real estate operations. He was one of the most powerful criminals in Japanese history.
