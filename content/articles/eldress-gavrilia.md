## Summary

Eldress Gavrilia was a Greek Orthodox nun who spent decades as a missionary across four continents, living in India, Africa, and the Middle East. She was known for her radical trust in divine providence, traveling with no money and depending entirely on God's provision. Her biography The Ascetic of Love became a bestseller in Greece. She combined Orthodox contemplative depth with active humanitarian service.
