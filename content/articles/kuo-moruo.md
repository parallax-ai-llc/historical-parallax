## Summary

While primarily a literary and political figure, Guo Moruo made important contributions to Chinese archaeology and paleography. He deciphered oracle bone inscriptions and advanced the study of ancient Chinese bronze inscriptions. His archaeological interpretations, though sometimes politically influenced, opened new understanding of Shang and Zhou dynasty history.
