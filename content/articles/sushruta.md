## Summary

Sushruta was an ancient Indian physician and surgeon who is considered the father of surgery. His text "Sushruta Samhita" describes over 300 surgical procedures, 120 surgical instruments, and is one of the most comprehensive medical texts from antiquity. He pioneered rhinoplasty (nose reconstruction), ophthalmology, and many other surgical techniques that were centuries ahead of their time.
