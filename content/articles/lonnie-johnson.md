## Summary

Lonnie Johnson invented the Super Soaker water gun in 1989, which became one of the best-selling toys in history. A former NASA engineer who worked on stealth bomber technology and the Galileo mission to Jupiter, he accidentally invented the Super Soaker while developing a heat pump. He holds over 120 patents and is developing advanced energy technology.
