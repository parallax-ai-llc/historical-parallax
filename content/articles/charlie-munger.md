## Summary

Charlie Munger was the vice chairman of Berkshire Hathaway and Warren Buffett's long-time business partner. He helped transform Buffett's investment philosophy from purely value-based to embracing quality companies at fair prices. His advocacy of 'mental models' from multiple disciplines became influential in business and investing. He was known for his sharp wit, wisdom, and independent thinking over a career spanning seven decades.
