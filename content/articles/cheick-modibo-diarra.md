## Summary

Cheick Modibo Diarra is a Malian astrophysicist who served as a navigator for NASA's Mars missions including Magellan, Mars Observer, and Mars Pathfinder. He is one of the most prominent African scientists in space exploration. He later served as Prime Minister of Mali and has promoted science education across Africa.
