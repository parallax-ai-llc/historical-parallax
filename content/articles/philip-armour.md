## Summary

Philip Armour built Armour and Company into one of the largest meatpacking firms in the world. He pioneered the use of refrigerated railroad cars and developed the concept of using every part of the animal. His meatpacking innovations helped make Chicago the center of the American meat industry. He also founded the Armour Institute of Technology, which later became part of Illinois Institute of Technology.
