## Summary

Kurozumi Muneharu was a Japanese Shinto leader who was a key successor in the Kurozumikyo movement. He helped organize and expand the religious community founded by his predecessor Kurozumi Munetada. He systematized the teachings and established institutional structures that enabled the movement to survive and grow.
