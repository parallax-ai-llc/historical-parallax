## Summary

Konko Daijin (born Kawate Bunjiro) was a Japanese farmer who founded Konkokyo, one of the oldest and most established Japanese new religions. In 1859, he experienced a divine revelation from Tenchi Kane no Kami, the parent deity of the universe. He devoted the rest of his life to mediating between humans and the divine through toritsugi (spiritual mediation). Konkokyo emphasizes mutual prosperity between humans and the divine.
