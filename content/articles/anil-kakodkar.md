## Summary

Anil Kakodkar is an Indian nuclear scientist who served as chairman of the Atomic Energy Commission. He designed the CIRUS reactor modifications and advanced India's thorium utilization program. He led the development of the Advanced Heavy Water Reactor (AHWR) designed to use India's vast thorium reserves for nuclear power.
