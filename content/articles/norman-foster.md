## Summary

Norman Foster is a British architect and founder of Foster + Partners, one of the most prolific and influential architecture firms in the world. His high-tech designs include the Reichstag dome in Berlin, 30 St Mary Axe (The Gherkin) in London, and the Apple Park campus. He received the Pritzker Prize in 1999.
