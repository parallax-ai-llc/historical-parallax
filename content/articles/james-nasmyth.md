## Summary

James Nasmyth invented the steam hammer in 1839, a powered forging hammer that revolutionized heavy manufacturing by enabling the production of large-scale iron and steel components. His invention was essential for building railways, ships, and heavy machinery. He also made important telescopic observations of the Moon.
