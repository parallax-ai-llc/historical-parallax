## Summary

Lithuanian criminal known as 'Gypsy Antanas' who became a legendary figure in Soviet-era organized crime. He led a gang that carried out daring robberies across the Soviet Union for decades.

## Background and Context

Antanas Gertneris (1923–2002) is most often discussed in the context of Soviet-era organized crime and the folklore that formed around prominent criminal figures.

- In English-language sources he is commonly identified by the nickname **"Gypsy Antanas"**.
- His notoriety is tied to long-running, high-profile robberies attributed to a gang he led across different parts of the Soviet Union.
