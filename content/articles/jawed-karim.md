## Summary

Jawed Karim (born October 28, 1979) is a German-American software engineer and entrepreneur who co-founded YouTube and uploaded its first video, "Me at the zoo." Though less publicly known than his co-founders Chad Hurley and Steve Chen, Karim's technical contributions were crucial to YouTube's creation, and he became a highly successful angel investor after departing before the Google acquisition.

### First Video: "Me at the zoo"

**April 23, 2005**:

- 18-second video at San Diego Zoo
- "Alright, so here we are in front of the elephants..."
