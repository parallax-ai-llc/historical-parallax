## Summary

Rabbi Akiva ben Joseph was one of the greatest and most influential rabbis in Jewish history. He systematized the oral Torah and developed methods of biblical interpretation that profoundly shaped rabbinic Judaism. He supported the Bar Kokhba revolt against Rome, recognizing Simon bar Kokhba as the Messiah. He was martyred by the Romans, who reportedly flayed him alive with iron combs.
