## Summary

Ameenah Gurib-Fakim is a Mauritian biodiversity scientist and organic chemist who served as President of Mauritius (2015-2018). She has extensively researched the medicinal plants of the Mascarene Islands and authored several books on African flora. She was named one of Foreign Policy's 100 Leading Global Thinkers for her work on biodiversity.
