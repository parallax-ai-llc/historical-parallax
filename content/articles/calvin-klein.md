## Summary

Calvin Klein founded Calvin Klein Inc. and built it into one of the most recognizable fashion brands in the world. He revolutionized fashion marketing with provocative advertising campaigns featuring Brooke Shields and later Mark Wahlberg. His minimalist aesthetic defined American fashion in the 1980s and 1990s. He sold his company to PVH Corp in 2003 but his brand continues as a global fashion powerhouse.
