## Summary

Mike Cannon-Brookes co-founded Atlassian, the Australian software company behind Jira, Confluence, and Trello. Atlassian became one of the most valuable software companies in the world, with a market cap exceeding $50 billion. The company went public on NASDAQ in 2015 without raising venture capital. He is also a major advocate for renewable energy and climate action, investing in large-scale solar projects in Australia.
