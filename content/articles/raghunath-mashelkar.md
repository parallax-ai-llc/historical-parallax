## Summary

Raghunath Mashelkar is an Indian chemical engineer who served as Director General of the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) and transformed it into a leading network of research laboratories. He championed Indian innovation policy, fought against bio-piracy, and helped revoke patents on turmeric and neem that exploited traditional Indian knowledge.
