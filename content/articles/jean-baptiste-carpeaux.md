## Summary

Jean-Baptiste Carpeaux was a French sculptor who brought new emotional expressiveness to 19th-century sculpture. His masterwork The Dance on the facade of the Paris Opera caused scandal for its uninhibited vitality. His portraits and group compositions bridge Romanticism and modern sculpture.
