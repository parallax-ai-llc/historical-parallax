## Public Roles

Diezani Alison-Madueke is a Nigerian former senior government official best known internationally for her leadership of the petroleum portfolio in Nigeria, a sector central to national revenue and frequently linked to debates about transparency, subsidy policy, and governance.

She held multiple ministerial appointments in government prior to and alongside her work in the petroleum sector, and her tenure became a focal point for public scrutiny of how licensing, contracting, and revenue flows were administered.
