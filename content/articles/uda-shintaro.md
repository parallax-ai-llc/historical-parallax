## Summary

Uda Shintaro was a Japanese electrical engineer who designed and built the first Yagi-Uda antenna in 1926 while working under Yagi Hidetsugu at Tohoku University. While the antenna bears both names, Uda did much of the practical design work. His antenna became the most common directional antenna worldwide.
