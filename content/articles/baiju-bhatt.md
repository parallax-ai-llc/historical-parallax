## Summary

Baiju Bhatt co-founded Robinhood Markets with Vlad Tenev, creating the commission-free trading platform that disrupted the brokerage industry. He served as co-CEO before transitioning to chief creative officer. A Stanford physics graduate, he helped develop Robinhood's technology platform. He stepped back from day-to-day operations to focus on robotics and other ventures.
