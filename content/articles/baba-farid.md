## Summary

Baba Farid (Fariduddin Ganjshakar) was a Punjabi Sufi saint and poet of the Chishti Order whose poetry profoundly influenced both Islamic Sufism and Sikh spirituality. His verses are included in the Sikh Guru Granth Sahib, making him one of the few Muslim writers whose work appears in the Sikh scripture. He is revered by both Muslims and Sikhs. His shrine in Pakpattan, Pakistan, is a major pilgrimage site.
