## Summary

Philipp Franz von Siebold was a German physician and naturalist who introduced Western medicine to Japan and brought knowledge of Japanese natural history to Europe. During his time in Nagasaki (1823-1829), he trained Japanese physicians and collected specimens. His Flora Japonica and Fauna Japonica were landmark works on Japanese natural history.
