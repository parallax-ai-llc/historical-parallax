## Summary

Ingrid Daubechies is known for her work on wavelets, creating compactly supported continuous wavelets (Daubechies wavelets) that revolutionized signal processing and image compression. Her wavelets are used in JPEG 2000 image compression, fingerprint scanning, and medical imaging. She was the first woman president of the International Mathematical Union.
