## Summary

Roberto Matta was a Chilean artist who became one of the most important figures in Surrealist painting. His vast, cosmic canvases of psychological landscapes influenced Abstract Expressionists including Gorky, Pollock, and Motherwell. His visionary paintings explore the inner landscape of consciousness and the intersection of art and science.
