## Summary
Gary Michael Heidnik was an American serial killer who kidnapped, tortured, and raped six women in his Philadelphia basement between 1986 and 1987, killing two. His case partly inspired the character Buffalo Bill in 'The Silence of the Lambs.' He was executed by lethal injection in 1999.
