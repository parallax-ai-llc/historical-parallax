## Summary

Beulah Louise Henry was a prolific American inventor known as 'Lady Edison' who held 49 patents and created over 110 inventions. Her inventions included a vacuum ice cream freezer, a bobbin-free sewing machine, a continuously attached envelope, and a snap-on hair curler. Despite being one of the most prolific female inventors, she is largely forgotten today.
