## Summary

Cho Jang-hee is a Korean biomedical engineer who has advanced prosthetics and rehabilitation engineering in South Korea. His research on myoelectric prosthetic hands and brain-computer interfaces has improved quality of life for amputees and people with disabilities. He has been a pioneer in Korean rehabilitation technology.
