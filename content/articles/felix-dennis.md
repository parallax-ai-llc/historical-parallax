## Summary

Felix Dennis founded Dennis Publishing, which produced Maxim, The Week, and PC Pro magazines. He also owned Micro Warehouse. After being acquitted in the Oz magazine obscenity trial in 1971, he built a publishing empire worth over $1 billion. He was also a poet and dedicated the last years of his life to planting a forest in Warwickshire. His book 'How to Get Rich' became a bestselling business guide.
