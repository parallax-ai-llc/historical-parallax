## Summary

Ayatollah Ruhollah Khomeini was an Iranian Shia cleric who led the 1979 Iranian Revolution and established the Islamic Republic of Iran. He developed the concept of velayat-e faqih (guardianship of the Islamic jurist), which placed supreme political authority in the hands of a senior cleric. He was the Supreme Leader of Iran from 1979 until his death. His revolution transformed Iran and had a profound impact on global Islam and politics.
