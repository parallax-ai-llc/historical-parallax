## Summary

Alain Wertheimer is the co-owner and chairman of Chanel, one of the world's most valuable private companies. His grandfather Pierre Wertheimer became Coco Chanel's business partner in 1924. Along with his brother Gerard, he controls the entire Chanel empire, which includes fashion, fragrances, and beauty. Chanel generates over $17 billion in annual revenue and the Wertheimer brothers are among the wealthiest people in the world.
