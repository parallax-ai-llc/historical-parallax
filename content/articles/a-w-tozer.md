## Summary

A. W. Tozer was an American pastor and author whose devotional writings have influenced millions of Christians. His books The Pursuit of God and The Knowledge of the Holy are considered classics of evangelical spirituality. He served as pastor of Southside Alliance Church in Chicago for 31 years. Known for his prophetic voice and deep spirituality, he is often called a '20th-century prophet.'
