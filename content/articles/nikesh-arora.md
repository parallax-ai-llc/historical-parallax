## Summary

Nikesh Arora is the CEO of Palo Alto Networks, the world's largest cybersecurity company by market capitalization. He previously served as president of SoftBank Group and was a senior executive at Google where he ran advertising and business operations. At Palo Alto Networks, he led the transformation to a platform-based security approach and grew the company's market cap to over $100 billion.
