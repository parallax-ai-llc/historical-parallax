## Summary

Kunio Busujima founded Sankyo Company, one of Japan's largest pachinko machine manufacturers. Pachinko is a massive industry in Japan generating hundreds of billions in revenue. Sankyo became the dominant pachinko manufacturer under his leadership. The Busujima family is one of the wealthiest in Japan, with a fortune built on Japan's unique gambling entertainment industry.
