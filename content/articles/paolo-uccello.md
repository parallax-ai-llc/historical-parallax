## Summary

Paolo Uccello was a Florentine painter celebrated for his pioneering exploration of perspective. His three-panel Battle of San Romano is a landmark in the application of mathematical perspective to complex narrative scenes. His unique fusion of Gothic decorative style with Renaissance spatial innovations created a distinctive visual language.
