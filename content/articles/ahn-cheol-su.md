## Summary

Ahn Cheol-su is a Korean computer scientist who founded AhnLab in 1995, South Korea's leading cybersecurity company. He created V3, one of the world's first commercial antivirus programs, in 1988. Originally a physician, he transitioned to computer science and became a pioneer of the Korean IT security industry. He later entered politics.
