## Summary

Jamgon Kongtrul Lodro Thaye was a Tibetan Buddhist master and scholar who was one of the founders of the Rime (non-sectarian) movement in 19th-century Tibet. He compiled the Five Great Treasuries, an encyclopedic collection of Tibetan Buddhist teachings spanning over 100 volumes. His work preserved countless teachings that might otherwise have been lost. He is considered one of the greatest polymaths in Tibetan Buddhist history.
