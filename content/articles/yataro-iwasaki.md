## Summary

Yataro Iwasaki founded Mitsubishi, one of Japan's largest and most diversified conglomerates (zaibatsu). Starting with a shipping company, he built an empire spanning banking, mining, shipbuilding, and trade. He competed fiercely with Mitsui and established Mitsubishi as a dominant force in Japanese industry. His business practices and vision laid the foundation for one of the world's most recognizable corporate brands.
