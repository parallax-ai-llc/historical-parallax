## Summary

Sudanese serial killer who murdered at least 7 people in the Minneapolis area between 2004 and 2006.

## Case Overview

- The murders attributed to Omar occurred in the Minneapolis–Saint Paul area across multiple incidents.
- Investigators linked several deaths through forensic evidence and the pattern of the crimes, culminating in federal involvement.

## Legal Outcome

- Omar was prosecuted and ultimately received a sentence of life imprisonment.
- The case is often referenced in discussions of multi-victim homicide investigations and the role of coordinated law-enforcement work across jurisdictions.

## Notability

- The crimes received significant local media coverage due to the number of victims and the period over which the killings occurred.
- The investigation illustrates how serial offenses can be pieced together through a combination of witness accounts, physical evidence, and analytical review of unsolved cases.
