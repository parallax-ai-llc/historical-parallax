## Additional Background

“El Azul” (“The Blue One”) was the widely used alias of Juan José Esparragoza Moreno, an alleged Mexican drug trafficker described in open sources as a senior figure within the Sinaloa Cartel’s broader alliance structure. Public reporting often emphasized his comparatively low media profile relative to other cartel leaders, despite being described as highly influential.[^1]

## Reported Death and Uncertainty

A recurring feature of the “El Azul” story is the lack of definitive public confirmation surrounding his reported death in 2014. Open sources describe reports that he died of a heart attack, while also noting that official confirmation was limited or contested.[^1]
