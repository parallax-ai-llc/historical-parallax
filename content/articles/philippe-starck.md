## Summary

Philippe Starck is a French industrial designer and architect known for his iconic product designs and interiors. From the Juicy Salif lemon squeezer to hotel interiors for Ian Schrager, his playful, provocative designs have made him the most famous designer of his generation. He champions democratic design that is accessible to everyone.
