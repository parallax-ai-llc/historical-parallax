## Summary

Andrej Karpathy is a leading AI researcher who served as director of AI at Tesla, leading the development of Tesla's Autopilot neural network vision system. He pioneered the use of pure vision-based approaches for autonomous driving. He previously co-founded and taught at Stanford's Deep Learning course and worked at OpenAI.
