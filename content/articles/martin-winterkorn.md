## Summary

German businessman who was CEO of Volkswagen during the diesel emissions scandal. VW installed software in 11 million vehicles to cheat emissions tests. He resigned and was charged with fraud in Germany and the US.

## The Diesel Emissions Scandal (High-Level)

- Reports described the use of **"defeat device" software** that could detect testing conditions and alter engine behavior to meet laboratory limits.
- The issue became global because it involved:
  - large-scale production volumes,
  - multiple regulatory regimes, and
  - significant public health and environmental policy concerns.
