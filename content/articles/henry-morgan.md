## Summary
Sir Henry Morgan was a Welsh privateer, plantation owner, and later the Lieutenant Governor of Jamaica. He raided Spanish settlements in the Caribbean, most notably the sack of Panama City in 1671. He was knighted and served as governor despite his piratical activities.
