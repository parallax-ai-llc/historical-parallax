## Summary

Neil deGrasse Tyson is an American astrophysicist and science communicator who has become one of the most recognized scientists in the world. He is the director of the Hayden Planetarium at the Rose Center for Earth and Space. Tyson hosted the 2014 television series 'Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey,' continuing Carl Sagan's legacy. He is known for making astrophysics accessible and engaging to the general public.
