## Summary
Fusako Shigenobu was the founder of the Japanese Red Army, a communist militant group. The group carried out the 1972 Lod Airport massacre in Israel, killing 26 people. She was arrested in Osaka in 2000 after 27 years as a fugitive. She served 20 years and was released in 2022.
