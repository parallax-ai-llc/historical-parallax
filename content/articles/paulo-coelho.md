## Summary

Paulo Coelho is a Brazilian author whose novel The Alchemist has sold over 150 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling books in history. His works blend spiritual seeking, mysticism, and personal transformation themes. He made a pilgrimage along the Camino de Santiago, which inspired his first major work. His books draw on diverse spiritual traditions including Christianity, Islam, Sufism, and indigenous Brazilian spirituality.
