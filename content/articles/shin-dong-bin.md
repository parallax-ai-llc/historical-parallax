## Summary

Lotte Group chairman convicted of bribery in connection with the Park Geun-hye corruption scandal.

Shin Dong-bin (also romanized as **Shin Dong-bin**, Japanese name **Akio Shigemitsu** in some sources) is a South Korean-Japanese business executive who rose within the Lotte conglomerate and became its chairman. Public coverage of his tenure frequently focuses on corporate governance disputes within the founding family and legal proceedings related to political and business corruption scandals in South Korea.
