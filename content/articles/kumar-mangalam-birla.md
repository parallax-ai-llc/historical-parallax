## Summary

Kumar Mangalam Birla is the chairman of the Aditya Birla Group, one of India's largest conglomerates with operations in 36 countries. He took over at age 28 after his father's death and aggressively expanded through acquisitions including Novelis, Columbian Chemicals, and a merger with Vodafone India. Under his leadership, group revenue grew from $2 billion to over $65 billion. UltraTech Cement became India's largest cement company.
