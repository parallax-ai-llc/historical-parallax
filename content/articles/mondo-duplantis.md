## Early Life and Background

Armand "Mondo" Duplantis was born in the United States and later competed internationally for Sweden through his family ties. He emerged as a prodigy in pole vaulting as a teenager, combining elite speed on the runway with refined technical execution.

## Records and Competitive Highlights

Duplantis set multiple world records in the event across both indoor and outdoor seasons. His performances made him the dominant figure in men's pole vaulting in the early 2020s, often winning major championships by clearing heights that separated him from the rest of the field.

## Technique and Style

He is known for a fast approach, efficient takeoff mechanics, and consistent bar clearance. Analysts often note his ability to translate sprint speed into vertical lift while maintaining control at very high grip heights.

## Major Championships

In addition to Olympic success, he has won World Championship titles and European titles, and he has frequently topped the Diamond League standings. His record progression is often discussed as part of a broader trend of improving pole-vault equipment, coaching methods, and event specialization.
