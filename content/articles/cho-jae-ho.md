## Summary

Cho Jae-ho was a Korean nuclear physicist who contributed to the development of South Korea's nuclear power infrastructure. He specialized in reactor physics and safety analysis and helped train the next generation of nuclear engineers. South Korea's nuclear power program now provides approximately 30% of the country's electricity.
