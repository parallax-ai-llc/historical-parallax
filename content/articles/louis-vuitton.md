## Summary

Louis Vuitton founded the luxury fashion house that bears his name, creating one of the world's most valuable and iconic brands. Starting as a trunk-maker in Paris, he revolutionized luggage with flat-topped trunks that could be stacked. His innovative designs and quality craftsmanship attracted aristocratic and royal clients. The LV monogram, introduced by his son Georges, became the most recognized luxury symbol in the world.
