## Summary

Charles McBurney identified the point of maximum tenderness in acute appendicitis, now known as McBurney's point. He also developed the muscle-splitting incision technique for appendectomy that minimized tissue damage. His contributions standardized the diagnosis and surgical treatment of appendicitis.
