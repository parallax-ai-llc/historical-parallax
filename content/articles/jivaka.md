## Summary

Jivaka Komarabhacca was the personal physician of Gautama Buddha and King Bimbisara of Magadha, renowned as one of the greatest doctors in ancient India. According to Buddhist texts, he performed complex surgical procedures including craniotomy and abdominal surgery. He is venerated in traditional Thai medicine as the father of medicine.
