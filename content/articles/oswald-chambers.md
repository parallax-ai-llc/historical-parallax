## Summary

Oswald Chambers was a Scottish evangelist and teacher best known posthumously through My Utmost for His Highest, one of the bestselling devotional books of all time. He served as a YMCA chaplain in Egypt during World War I. His wife compiled his lectures and sermons into over 30 books after his early death from a ruptured appendix at age 43.
