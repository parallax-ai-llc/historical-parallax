Ghislaine Noelle Marion Maxwell is a British former socialite and convicted sex offender who was closely associated with Jeffrey Epstein and played a key role in his sex trafficking enterprise.

## Background

Born in France to media tycoon Robert Maxwell, Ghislaine grew up in privilege and moved in elite social circles. After her father's death in 1991, she relocated to New York City.

## Relationship with Jeffrey Epstein

Maxwell became Jeffrey Epstein's close associate and alleged girlfriend in the early 1990s. She is accused of recruiting and grooming underage girls for Epstein's abuse, acting as a "madam" in his sex trafficking operation.

## Criminal Conviction

In December 2021, Maxwell was convicted on five federal charges:
- Sex trafficking of minors
- Conspiracy to entice minors to travel to engage in illegal sex acts
- Conspiracy to transport minors with intent to engage in criminal sexual activity

She was sentenced to 20 years in federal prison in June 2022.
