## Summary
On April 15, 2013, two homemade pressure cooker bombs detonated near the finish line of the Boston Marathon, killing three and injuring 264. Brothers Tamerlan and Dzhokhar Tsarnaev were identified as the bombers. Tamerlan was killed; Dzhokhar was sentenced to death.
