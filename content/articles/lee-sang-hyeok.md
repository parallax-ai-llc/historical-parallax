## Summary

Already covered as Faker.

Lee Sang-hyeok is best known globally by his in-game name **Faker**, widely regarded as one of the most influential professional players in *League of Legends* history. He is most closely associated with the T1 (formerly SK Telecom T1) organization and is especially known for elite mid-lane play, longevity at the top level, and a career spanning multiple competitive eras.

This article keeps a concise biographical record under his legal name and points to the same primary public sources used for the better-known “Faker” coverage.

## Playstyle and Reputation

- **Role:** Primarily a mid laner; historically known for high mechanical skill and broad champion pool.
- **Competitive identity:** Often described as adaptable across metas and capable of playing both carry and supportive mid styles depending on team needs.
- **Leadership:** Frequently cited as a stabilizing presence for roster changes and as a long-term face of his organization.

## Legacy (High-Level)

- Became an iconic figure for *League of Legends* esports and a major driver of the game’s mainstream visibility.
- Commonly referenced as the benchmark for “greatest of all time” debates in the title.
- Notable for an unusually long period of relevance at the top level compared to typical esports career lengths.
