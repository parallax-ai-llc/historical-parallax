## Summary

Anandamayi Ma was an Indian mystic and saint whom many regarded as a fully realized being from birth. She attracted devotees from all walks of life, including Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, and Paramahansa Yogananda (who featured her in Autobiography of a Yogi). She established ashrams across India and emphasized that the goal of human life is God-realization. She was known for her spontaneous spiritual states and profound simplicity.
