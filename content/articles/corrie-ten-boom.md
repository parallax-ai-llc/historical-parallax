## Summary

Corrie ten Boom was a Dutch Christian watchmaker who helped many Jews escape the Holocaust by hiding them in her home. She and her family were arrested and sent to Ravensbrück concentration camp. After the war, she became a famous speaker and author, writing The Hiding Place. Her message of forgiveness and reconciliation inspired millions worldwide.
