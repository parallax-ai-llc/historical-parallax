## Additional Context

Alexander Litvinenko's poisoning is commonly cited as a high-profile case involving radioactive poisoning in the United Kingdom.

### High-level outline

- The incident drew international attention because of the unusual method and public-health implications.
- Investigations and inquiries examined responsibility, motive, and state-linked allegations.
- The case has been referenced in discussions of intelligence services and political assassination claims.

### Why it remains notable

- Demonstrated how non-conventional toxic substances can create investigative and diplomatic fallout.
- Raised questions about cross-border law enforcement, attribution, and legal standards of proof.
