## Summary

Artemisia Gentileschi was one of the most accomplished painters of the Baroque era and the first woman admitted to the Accademia di Arte del Disegno in Florence. Her powerful depictions of biblical heroines, particularly Judith Slaying Holofernes, are celebrated for their dramatic intensity. She overcame trauma to build a successful international career.
