## Summary

Chairman of the 2010 Commonwealth Games organizing committee who embezzled hundreds of millions from the event. The games became synonymous with Indian corruption.

## Public Role

Kalmadi is best known internationally for his leadership role in the organizing structures around the 2010 Commonwealth Games in Delhi, a major multi-sport event that required large public procurement and time-sensitive contracting.

## Allegations and Procurement Issues

The controversy centered on how contracts were awarded and priced, including claims of inflated costs, irregular tendering, and misuse of organizing-committee authority. Such cases are often discussed as examples of how mega-events can create corruption opportunities through emergency timelines and complex vendor networks.

## Political Impact

The Games scandals contributed to broader public anger over corruption in India during the early 2010s, intersecting with anti-corruption activism and increased scrutiny of public spending.

## Why This Case Is Notable

Analysts frequently cite the episode as a governance lesson: transparent procurement, independent oversight, and clear accountability structures are critical when public funds and reputational stakes are concentrated in a short period.
