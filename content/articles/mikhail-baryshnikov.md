## Summary

Mikhail Baryshnikov is a Latvian-American dancer who is considered one of the greatest ballet dancers of all time. He defected from the Soviet Union in 1974 and joined the American Ballet Theatre, later becoming its artistic director. His extraordinary technique, dramatic ability, and crossover appeal made ballet accessible to a wider audience.
