## Summary

Indonesian terrorist born Riduan Isamuddin who was the operations chief of Jemaah Islamiyah and al-Qaeda's key man in Southeast Asia. He masterminded the 2002 Bali bombings and the 2003 Marriott hotel bombing. Detained at Guantánamo.
