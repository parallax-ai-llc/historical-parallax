## Summary

Ai-Da is the world's first ultra-realistic humanoid robot artist, created by gallery director Aidan Meller. She uses cameras in her eyes and AI algorithms to draw, paint, and create sculptures. Her exhibitions have been shown at the Design Museum, the Venice Biennale, and the United Nations, raising questions about creativity and artificial intelligence.
