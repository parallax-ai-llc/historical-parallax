## Summary

E.I. du Pont founded E.I. du Pont de Nemours and Company in 1802, establishing one of America's most enduring industrial dynasties. Born in Paris, he studied chemistry under Antoine Lavoisier before emigrating to America. His gunpowder factory on the Brandywine River in Delaware grew into the nation's largest supplier. The company later diversified into chemicals, plastics, and advanced materials.
