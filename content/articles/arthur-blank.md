## Summary

Arthur Blank co-founded The Home Depot with Bernard Marcus and served as its CEO and co-chairman. After retiring from Home Depot, he became owner of the Atlanta Falcons NFL team and Atlanta United FC. He built Mercedes-Benz Stadium, noted for its fan-friendly pricing. His philanthropic Blank Family Foundation has donated over $700 million to education, youth development, and community improvement.
