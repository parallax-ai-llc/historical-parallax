## Summary

Park Eun-sik was a Korean historian and independence activist who wrote the Hanguk Tongsa (Painful History of Korea), documenting Korea's scientific and cultural achievements. His systematic historical methodology preserved records of Korean scientific accomplishments during a period when Japanese colonizers attempted to erase Korean cultural identity.
