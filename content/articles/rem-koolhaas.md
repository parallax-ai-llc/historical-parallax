## Summary

Rem Koolhaas is a Dutch architect and urbanist known for his provocative designs and influential theoretical writings. His book Delirious New York and the founding of OMA transformed architectural discourse. His buildings include the CCTV Headquarters in Beijing and the Seattle Central Library. He received the Pritzker Prize in 2000.
