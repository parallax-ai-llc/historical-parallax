## Summary
Antonino Leonardo Accardo, known as 'Joe Batters' and 'Big Tuna,' was the longtime boss of the Chicago Outfit. He held power for nearly 50 years and was one of the most powerful mobsters in American history. Despite being arrested multiple times, he was never convicted.
