## Summary
Mohan Kumar, known as 'Cyanide Mohan,' was an Indian serial killer who murdered at least 20 women in Karnataka between 2005 and 2009. He lured women with promises of marriage, then poisoned them with cyanide capsules disguised as contraceptives and stole their jewelry.
