## Summary

Anita Roddick founded The Body Shop, pioneering ethical and environmentally conscious business practices in the beauty industry. She opened the first Body Shop in Brighton in 1976, selling naturally inspired beauty products. She was an early advocate for fair trade, animal rights, and corporate social responsibility. The Body Shop grew to over 2,000 stores in 55 countries before being acquired by L'Oréal.
