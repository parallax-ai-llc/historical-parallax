## Summary

Jim Elliot was an American evangelical missionary who was killed along with four companions while attempting to make contact with the Waorani people of Ecuador. His journals and letters, published posthumously, revealed a passionate devotion to Christ. His famous quote 'He is no fool who gives what he cannot keep to gain what he cannot lose' inspired generations of missionaries. The story of his martyrdom became one of the most well-known in modern missionary history.
