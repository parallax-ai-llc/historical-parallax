## Summary

Colombian serial killer who raped and murdered over 150 young girls in Colombia and Ecuador in the 1970s and 1980s. He escaped from a Colombian prison and continued killing in Ecuador. He was murdered in prison.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1974 | Began killing young girls in Colombia |
| 1984 | Escaped from prison on Gorgona Island |
| 1986 | Arrested in Ecuador after killing 54 more victims |
| 1994 | Murdered by a fellow inmate's nephew in prison |
