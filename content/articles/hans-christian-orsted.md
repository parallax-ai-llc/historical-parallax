## Summary

Hans Christian Ørsted discovered that electric currents create magnetic fields in 1820 — the first connection found between electricity and magnetism. This discovery launched the science of electromagnetism and led to the development of electric motors and generators. He also isolated aluminum for the first time and discovered piperine.
