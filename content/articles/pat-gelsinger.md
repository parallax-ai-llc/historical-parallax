## Summary

Pat Gelsinger served as CEO of Intel from 2021 to 2024, attempting to revitalize the iconic chipmaker through his IDM 2.0 strategy. He was Intel's first CTO and one of its youngest vice presidents before leaving for EMC and VMware. He returned to Intel with an ambitious plan to regain process technology leadership and build a foundry business. Despite securing $8.5 billion in CHIPS Act funding, Intel's challenges persisted.
