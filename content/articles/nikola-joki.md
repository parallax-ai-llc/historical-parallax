## Summary

Nikola Jokić won three consecutive MVP awards and led Denver to the 2023 NBA championship. He is the most skilled passing big man in history.

## Playing Style

- **Playmaking center**: Jokić is widely known for running offense from the high post and elbows, functioning as a primary passer despite playing center.
- **Scoring efficiency**: He combines touch around the rim with mid-range shooting and strong finishing angles, enabling high-percentage looks.
- **Rebounding and positioning**: Rather than relying only on vertical athleticism, he often wins possessions with timing, leverage, and anticipation.

## NBA Achievements (Selected)

- **NBA Most Valuable Player**: Jokić has won multiple MVP awards (notably in 2021, 2022, and 2024).
- **Denver Nuggets title run**: He led Denver to its first NBA championship in 2023 and earned **Finals MVP**.
- **All-NBA recognition**: Across his prime seasons he has been a frequent All-NBA selection, reflecting sustained elite performance.

## International Career

- **Serbia**: Jokić has represented Serbia in major international competitions, contributing to a national program with a strong post-Yugoslav basketball tradition.

## Legacy and Impact

- **Positional redefinition**: His success helped normalize the idea that an offense can be built around a center as a primary creator.
- **Analytical profile**: Many modern impact metrics rate him among the most valuable players of his era due to efficiency, passing volume, and on-court results.
