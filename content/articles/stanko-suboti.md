## Background

Stanko Subotić (often referred to by the nickname **"Cane"**) is a Serbian businessman who rose to prominence during the political and economic turbulence of the 1990s in the former Yugoslavia. His public profile is closely associated with allegations and prosecutions related to large-scale cigarette trafficking during periods of international sanctions.

## Business Activities and Public Profile

- **Wealth and influence claims:** Media reporting in the Balkans has often portrayed Subotić as one of the wealthiest individuals to emerge from the sanctions period, though estimates and characterizations vary widely by source.
