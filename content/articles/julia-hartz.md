## Summary

Julia Hartz is the co-founder and CEO of Eventbrite, one of the world's largest event technology platforms. She co-founded the company in 2006 and took over as CEO in 2016. Eventbrite processes millions of events annually and went public in 2018. She navigated the company through the devastating impact of COVID-19 on live events and led its recovery. She is one of Silicon Valley's most prominent female tech CEOs.
