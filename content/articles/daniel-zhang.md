## Summary

Daniel Zhang served as CEO and chairman of Alibaba Group from 2015 to 2023. He created the Singles' Day shopping festival (11/11), which became the world's largest online shopping event, generating over $80 billion in sales in a single day. He led Alibaba through its transition after Jack Ma's retirement and the company's restructuring into six business groups. He stepped down to lead Alibaba's cloud computing unit.
