## Summary

The Third Crusade (1189-1192) was launched after Saladin captured Jerusalem. Led by Richard the Lionheart, Philip II, and Frederick Barbarossa, it recaptured the coast but failed to take Jerusalem. Richard and Saladin's legendary rivalry defined the era.
