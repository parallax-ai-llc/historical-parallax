## Additional Context

- **Public profile:** Widely recognized as one of the most technically gifted attacking players of his era, known for close control, creativity, and showmanship.
- **Major honors (high level):** UEFA Champions League, domestic league titles in Spain and Brazil, and individual awards including Ballon d'Or and FIFA World Player of the Year.
- **National team:** Member of Brazil squads during the early-to-mid 2000s, including the 2002 World Cup–winning team.
- **Off-field headlines:** The Paraguay detention centered on travel documentation; it drew significant media coverage but is generally treated as an off-field incident rather than a defining historical event.

## Legacy

- Helped popularize a style of play that blended street-football flair with elite-level efficiency.
- Often cited as an influence on later generations of creative forwards and attacking midfielders.
- Remains a prominent cultural figure in global football, frequently referenced in discussions of “peak skill” and entertainment value.
