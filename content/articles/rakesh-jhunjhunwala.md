## Summary

Rakesh Jhunjhunwala was India's most celebrated stock investor, known as the 'Big Bull' of Indian markets. Starting with just ₹5,000 in 1985, he built a portfolio worth over $5 billion through value investing in companies like Titan, Lupin, and CRISIL. He was a perennial bull on India's economic growth story. He co-founded Akasa Air in 2022, shortly before his death.
