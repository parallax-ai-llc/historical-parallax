## Summary

Lalon Shah was a Bengali mystic, poet, and folk singer who is one of the most important figures in Bengali spiritual and cultural heritage. He composed thousands of songs exploring the nature of the divine, the human soul, and the futility of caste and religious divisions. He refused to identify as either Hindu or Muslim, teaching that the divine exists within every human being. His songs profoundly influenced Rabindranath Tagore and Bengali culture.
