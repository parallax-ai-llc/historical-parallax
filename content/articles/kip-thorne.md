## Summary

Kip Thorne is an American theoretical physicist who shared the 2017 Nobel Prize in Physics for decisive contributions to the LIGO detector and the observation of gravitational waves. He made groundbreaking contributions to gravitational physics and astrophysics, particularly regarding black holes and gravitational waves. Thorne also served as scientific consultant for the film Interstellar. His work helped confirm a major prediction of Einstein's general theory of relativity.
