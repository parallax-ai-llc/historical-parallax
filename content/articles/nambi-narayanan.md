## Summary

Nambi Narayanan was a key scientist at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) who developed the Vikas engine, which powered India's Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle and Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle. He was falsely accused of espionage in 1994, an ordeal later recognized as a grave injustice. The Supreme Court of India exonerated him in 2018.
