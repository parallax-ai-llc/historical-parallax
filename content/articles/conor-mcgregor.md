## Summary

Conor McGregor was the first UFC fighter to hold two titles simultaneously. He boxed Mayweather in a cross-sport mega-fight.

## Additional Context

- **Nationality:** Irish mixed martial artist associated with the Straight Blast Gym (SBG) team early in his career.
- **Historic milestone:** Widely described as the first UFC fighter to hold UFC titles in **two weight classes** at the same time.
- **Fighting style (high-level):** Known for counter-striking, stance switching, and left-hand power, with a heavy emphasis on timing and distance.

## Notable Fights and Career Notes

- Championship wins include the **featherweight** and **lightweight** UFC titles.
- The 2017 bout with Floyd Mayweather Jr. was a major **pay-per-view** event that bridged boxing and MMA audiences.
- Later years included intermittent UFC activity and highly publicized rivalries.

## Business and Media Presence

- Built a major personal brand through press conferences, social media, and promotional tours.
- Associated with commercial ventures (including spirits/whiskey branding) that expanded his profile beyond sport.

## Controversies (high-level)

- Involved in multiple widely reported legal and disciplinary matters over the years; coverage includes both criminal allegations and civil claims depending on the incident.
- The UFC and athletic commissions have at times issued penalties in connection with conduct around events.
