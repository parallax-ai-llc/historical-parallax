## Summary

Othmar Ammann designed several of New York City's most iconic bridges, including the George Washington Bridge, the Bayonne Bridge, the Triborough Bridge, and the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge. The George Washington Bridge was the world's longest suspension bridge when completed in 1931. His designs combined engineering innovation with aesthetic elegance.
