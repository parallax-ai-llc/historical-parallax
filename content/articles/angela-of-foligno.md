## Summary

Angela of Foligno was an Italian mystic and Franciscan tertiary whose spiritual writings, particularly the Memorial and Instructions, describe one of the most intense mystical journeys in Christian literature. After the death of her family, she underwent a radical conversion and experienced dramatic visions and spiritual states. Her descriptions of mystical union with God are among the most vivid and psychologically acute in the Christian tradition.
