## High-level biography (primary subject)

Kevin Systrom (born 1983) is an American entrepreneur best known as the co-founder and former CEO of Instagram.

### Education and early interests

- Studied at Stanford University.
- Built an early interest in photography and product design, which later influenced Instagram’s focus on simple photo-sharing.

### Career highlights

- Worked in product roles prior to Instagram, including at Google.
- Co-founded Instagram with Mike Krieger; the product scaled rapidly after launch.
- Instagram was acquired by Facebook (now Meta) in 2012; Systrom continued to lead Instagram within the company.
- Departed Instagram in 2018.

### Impact

- Helped popularize mobile-first social photo sharing and simplified, feed-based distribution for user-generated media.
- Instagram became one of the most influential consumer social platforms of the 2010s.
