## Summary
Ching Shih (Zheng Yi Sao) was one of the most successful pirates in history. She commanded the Red Flag Fleet of over 1,800 vessels and 80,000 pirates in the South China Sea. She was so powerful that the Chinese, Portuguese, and British navies could not defeat her. She retired undefeated.
