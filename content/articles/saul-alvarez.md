## Notes on naming / duplication

Saúl "Canelo" Álvarez is widely known by his nickname and accent marks in Spanish spelling. Some repositories keep a separate stub under "Saul Alvarez" to catch alternate search queries; when consolidating, the canonical article is typically under **Canelo Álvarez**.

## Overview

Álvarez is a Mexican professional boxer and one of the sport's most commercially successful athletes of his era. He has competed across multiple weight classes and is best known for championship runs at light middleweight, middleweight, and super middleweight.

## Style and reputation

Open sources frequently describe Álvarez as a counterpuncher with strong body work, durable defense, and high ring IQ. Commentary also highlights his long tenure at elite level and willingness to face a wide range of top opponents.
