## Summary

James Dyson invented the bagless cyclone vacuum cleaner after 5,127 prototypes over 15 years of development. His Dual Cyclone vacuum revolutionized the home appliance industry. He later developed the Airblade hand dryer, bladeless fans, and other innovative products. His persistence through thousands of failed prototypes became a symbol of engineering determination.
