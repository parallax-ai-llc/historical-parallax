## Summary

Francisca Nneka Okeke is a Nigerian physicist who became the first female professor of physics at the University of Nigeria. Her research focuses on the dynamic effects of the ionosphere on radio communication and navigation in equatorial Africa. She won the L'Oréal-UNESCO Women in Science Award for Africa in 2013.
