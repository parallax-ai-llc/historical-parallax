## Summary

I.M. Pei was a Chinese-American architect known for his bold geometric designs and masterful use of light and space. His glass pyramid at the Louvre is one of the most iconic modern additions to a historic building. Other major works include the National Gallery of Art East Building and the Museum of Islamic Art in Doha.
