## Summary

The Spanish Inquisition was established in 1478 by Ferdinand and Isabella to maintain Catholic orthodoxy. It persecuted converts suspected of secretly practicing Judaism or Islam. The Inquisition lasted until 1834 and became a byword for religious persecution.
