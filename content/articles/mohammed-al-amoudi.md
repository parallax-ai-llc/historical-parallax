## Summary

Mohammed Hussein Al Amoudi is an Ethiopian-born Saudi billionaire with vast business interests in Saudi Arabia, Ethiopia, Sweden, and Morocco. His companies operate in oil refining, construction, agriculture, and real estate. He is the largest private investor in Ethiopia, controlling major hotels, gold mining operations, and agricultural businesses. He was detained during the 2017 Saudi anti-corruption purge.
