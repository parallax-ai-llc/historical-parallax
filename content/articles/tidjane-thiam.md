## Summary

Tidjane Thiam is an Ivorian-French business executive who served as CEO of Credit Suisse from 2015 to 2020 and previously as CEO of Prudential plc. He was the first Black CEO of a major European bank. At Prudential, he led its expansion into Asian markets. At Credit Suisse, he restructured the bank before being ousted over a spying scandal. He represents the growing influence of African business leaders in global finance.
