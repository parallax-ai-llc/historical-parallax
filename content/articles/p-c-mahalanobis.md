## Summary

Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis was an Indian scientist and statistician who is considered the father of Indian statistics. He developed the Mahalanobis distance, a widely used statistical measure. He founded the Indian Statistical Institute and played a key role in India's economic planning through statistical methods. He also designed large-scale sample surveys.
