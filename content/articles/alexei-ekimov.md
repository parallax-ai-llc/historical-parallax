## Summary

Alexei Ekimov is a Russian-American physicist who was the first to discover quantum dots in a glass matrix in 1981, demonstrating size-dependent quantum effects in semiconductor nanocrystals. His discovery that nanoparticle properties change with size launched the field of quantum dot research. He shared the 2023 Nobel Prize in Chemistry.
