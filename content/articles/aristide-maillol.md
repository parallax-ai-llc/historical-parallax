## Summary

Aristide Maillol was a French sculptor known for his monumental, classically inspired female nudes. His simplified, voluminous forms represented a return to calm classical ideals after the expressive dynamism of Rodin. His Mediterranean and other major bronzes influenced the development of modern figurative sculpture.
