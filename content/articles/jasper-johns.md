## Summary

Jasper Johns revolutionized postwar American art with paintings of flags, targets, maps, and numbers. His work blurs boundaries between abstraction and representation. His debut at Leo Castelli Gallery is considered a watershed moment that paved the way from Abstract Expressionism to Pop Art.
