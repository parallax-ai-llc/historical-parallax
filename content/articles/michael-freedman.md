## Summary

Michael Freedman is an American mathematician who received the Fields Medal in 1986 for his proof of the 4-dimensional Poincaré conjecture. His work established that 4-dimensional topology is fundamentally different from other dimensions. He later joined Microsoft Research to work on quantum computing.
