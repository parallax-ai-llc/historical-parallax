## Summary

Buddhaghosa was an Indian Theravada Buddhist scholar and commentator who is the most important systematizer of Theravada Buddhism. His magnum opus Visuddhimagga (Path of Purification) is the definitive guide to Theravada meditation and philosophy. He traveled to Sri Lanka where he translated and commented on the Pali Canon. His commentaries remain authoritative in Theravada Buddhist countries.
