## Summary

Ruth Benerito developed wrinkle-free cotton fabrics by treating cotton fibers with cross-linking chemicals, saving the American cotton industry from being replaced by synthetic fibers. She held 55 patents and made significant contributions to fat emulsion technology for intravenous feeding of critically ill patients. She was inducted into the National Inventors Hall of Fame.
