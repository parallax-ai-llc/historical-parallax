## Summary

François-Henri Pinault is the chairman and CEO of Kering, the luxury goods group controlling Gucci, Saint Laurent, Balenciaga, and Alexander McQueen. He succeeded his father François Pinault and transformed PPR from a retail conglomerate into a focused luxury group renamed Kering. He is married to actress Salma Hayek. Under his leadership, Kering became the world's second-largest luxury group after LVMH.
