## Summary

Ren Zhengfei founded Huawei Technologies in 1987, building it into the world's largest telecommunications equipment manufacturer and a leading smartphone maker. Huawei's 5G technology became a focal point of US-China trade tensions, leading to sanctions and export bans. Despite sanctions, Huawei developed its own HarmonyOS and Kirin chips. His military background and Huawei's relationship with the Chinese government have been subjects of intense international debate.
