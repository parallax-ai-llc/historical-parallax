## Summary

Jacqueline du Pre was a British cellist whose passionate, intense performances made her one of the greatest cellists in history. Her recording of the Elgar Cello Concerto with John Barbirolli is considered definitive. Her career was tragically cut short by multiple sclerosis at age 28, but her recordings continue to inspire musicians worldwide.
