## Summary

Park Gyeong-ri was Korea's greatest novelist, author of the epic Toji (The Land). While a literary figure, her 16-volume novel spanning from 1897-1945 contains detailed documentation of Korean agricultural technology, medical practices, and social systems during the modernization period, serving as an ethnographic record of Korean technological change.
