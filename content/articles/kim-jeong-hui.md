## Summary

Kim Jeong-hui was a Joseon Dynasty scholar who pioneered epigraphy (the study of inscriptions) in Korea. He used scientific methods to authenticate and date historical inscriptions, applying rigorous textual analysis. His systematic approach to studying historical artifacts anticipated modern archaeological methodology in Korea.
