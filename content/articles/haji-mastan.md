## Summary
Haji Mastan Mirza was one of Mumbai's most famous crime bosses and smugglers in the 1960s-1980s. He was known for his Robin Hood-like image. He was involved in gold and contraband smuggling. He later entered politics. His life inspired the Bollywood film 'Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai.'
