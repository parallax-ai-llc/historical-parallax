## Summary

Ephrem the Syrian was a Syriac Christian deacon, theologian, and hymnographer known as the 'Harp of the Holy Spirit.' He composed hundreds of hymns and poetic homilies that became central to Syriac Christian worship. His theological works addressed Christology, Mariology, and anti-heretical polemics. He was declared a Doctor of the Church in 1920.
