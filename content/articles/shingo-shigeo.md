## Summary

Shingo Shigeo was a Japanese industrial engineer who developed Single-Minute Exchange of Die (SMED) and poka-yoke (mistake-proofing) techniques. His innovations in manufacturing engineering dramatically reduced changeover times and eliminated defects. The Shingo Prize for operational excellence at Utah State University is named after him.
