## Summary
Dato' Sri Mohd Najib bin Tun Haji Abdul Razak was the sixth Prime Minister of Malaysia who was convicted of corruption related to the 1MDB scandal. Approximately $700 million of 1MDB funds ended up in his personal bank accounts. He was convicted and sentenced to 12 years in prison in 2022.
