## Summary

Demis Hassabis is a British artificial intelligence researcher, neuroscientist, and entrepreneur who co-founded DeepMind. Under his leadership, DeepMind created AlphaGo, the first program to defeat a world champion at Go, and AlphaFold, which solved the protein folding problem. He shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for computational protein structure prediction. He was also a chess prodigy and video game designer.
