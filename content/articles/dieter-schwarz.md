## Summary

Dieter Schwarz is the founder and owner of the Schwarz Group, which operates Lidl and Kaufland, making it the world's fourth-largest retailer. Lidl has become Aldi's biggest competitor, operating over 12,000 stores across Europe and the United States. He built the company from his father's wholesale fruit business into a retail empire. He is one of the wealthiest people in the world and one of Germany's most reclusive billionaires.
