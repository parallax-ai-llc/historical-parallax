## Summary

Lee Myung-hee is the eldest daughter of Samsung founder Lee Byung-chul and the chairwoman of Shinsegae Group. She separated Shinsegae from the Samsung Group in 1991 and built it into one of South Korea's largest retail empires. She controls Shinsegae department stores, E-mart, and SSG.COM. She is one of the most powerful businesswomen in South Korea and a member of the Samsung founding family.
