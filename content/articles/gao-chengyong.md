## Summary
Gao Chengyong was a Chinese serial killer who murdered 11 women and girls in Gansu and Inner Mongolia between 1988 and 2002. Known as China's 'Jack the Ripper,' he followed victims home and attacked them. He was caught in 2016 through DNA and executed in 2019.
