## Summary

Theo Albrecht co-founded Aldi with his brother Karl, building the world's most successful discount grocery chain. The brothers split the company into Aldi Nord and Aldi Süd in 1960. Theo ran Aldi Nord and also acquired Trader Joe's in the United States. He was kidnapped and held for ransom for 17 days in 1971. Despite enormous wealth, the Albrecht brothers were famously frugal and reclusive.
