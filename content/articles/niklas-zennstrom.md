## Summary

Niklas Zennström co-founded Skype and Kazaa, transforming internet communications and file sharing. Skype's peer-to-peer voice over IP technology enabled free international calling and was acquired by eBay for $2.6 billion in 2005 and later by Microsoft for $8.5 billion in 2011. He also co-founded Atomico, a leading European venture capital firm that has backed companies like Supercell and Klarna.
