## Summary

Mukesh Ambani is the chairman of Reliance Industries, India's most valuable company, with interests in petrochemicals, refining, telecommunications, and retail. He launched Jio, India's largest mobile network, providing affordable 4G connectivity to hundreds of millions of Indians. His Jamnagar refinery is the world's largest. He is the wealthiest person in Asia and built the Antilia residence, one of the most expensive private homes in the world.
