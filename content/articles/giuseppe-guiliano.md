## Summary

Italian Camorra boss from Naples who controlled drug trafficking and extortion in the Forcella neighborhood. He was one of the most powerful Camorra leaders.

## Background

Giuseppe Guiliano is associated with the Camorra in Naples, particularly in the historic center and port-adjacent neighborhoods where smuggling, extortion, and street-level trafficking have long been sources of illicit income.

## Criminal activities (as described in reporting)

Coverage of Camorra leadership cases in this period often highlights:

- control over local “protection” rackets (extortion imposed on shops and markets)
- drug distribution networks connected to broader regional supply chains
- violence and intimidation used to enforce territorial control

## Law-enforcement pressure

The timeline of arrest and conviction reflects the broader Italian strategy of prosecuting organized crime through:

- long investigations based on surveillance and informants
- financial tracing of proceeds and front businesses
- coordinated arrests targeting multiple associates at once

## Significance

- **Neighborhood-level power**: the case illustrates how Camorra influence can be rooted in specific districts.
- **Fragmented criminal governance**: Naples has often seen shifting alliances and rivalries among groups.
- **Public administration impact**: sustained criminal control can distort local economies and civic life.
