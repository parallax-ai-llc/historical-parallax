## Summary
Robert Christian Hansen was an American serial killer who abducted, raped, and murdered at least 17 women in and around Anchorage, Alaska, between 1971 and 1983. He hunted some victims in the wilderness with a rifle. He was sentenced to 461 years plus life without parole.
