## Summary

Anil Ambani was the sixth-richest person in the world in 2008 but lost nearly his entire fortune in one of the most dramatic wealth destructions in history. After splitting Reliance Industries with brother Mukesh in 2005, he took control of telecom, power, and entertainment businesses. His companies accumulated massive debt and his Reliance Communications went bankrupt. He was found in contempt of court by a UK judge for failing to pay debts.
