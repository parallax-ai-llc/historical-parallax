## Summary
Peter Britton Tobin was a Scottish serial killer convicted of three murders between 1991 and 2006. He is suspected of many more. His first conviction came in 2007 for the murder of Angelika Kluk, whose body was hidden under a church floor. He died in prison in 2022.
