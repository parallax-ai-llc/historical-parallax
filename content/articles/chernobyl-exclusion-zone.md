## Summary

The evacuation of the Chernobyl exclusion zone began April 27, 1986, when 49,000 residents of Pripyat were relocated within hours. Eventually 350,000 people were permanently resettled. The 30-km zone remains largely uninhabited and has become an accidental wildlife sanctuary.
