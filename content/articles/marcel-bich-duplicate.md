## Canonical Subject (Marcel Bich)

Marcel Bich (1914–1994) is best known as the co-founder and long-time leader of **Société Bic**, the company associated with mass-market writing instruments.

High-level, widely cited points about the canonical subject:

- Helped industrialize and popularize the **ballpoint pen** through large-scale manufacturing and low-cost distribution
- Bic’s flagship ballpoint, the **BIC Cristal**, was introduced around **1950** and became one of the most widely sold pens in the world
- The company later expanded into adjacent disposable consumer products (notably **lighters** and **razors**)
