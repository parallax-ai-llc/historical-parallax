## Summary
Imad Fayez Mughniyeh was a senior Hezbollah military commander who was one of the most wanted terrorists in the world. He was linked to the 1983 Beirut barracks bombings, the 1985 TWA Flight 847 hijacking, and the 1992 Argentine embassy bombing. He was killed by a car bomb in Damascus in 2008.
