## Summary

Takahashi Yoshitoki was the official astronomer of the Tokugawa Shogunate who reformed the Japanese calendar using Western astronomical techniques. A student of Asada Goryū, he incorporated Newtonian mechanics into Japanese astronomy. His Kansei Calendar (1798) was the most accurate Japanese calendar of its era.
