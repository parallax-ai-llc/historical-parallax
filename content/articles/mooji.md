## Summary

Mooji (Anthony Paul Moo-Young) is a Jamaican-British spiritual teacher of Advaita Vedanta who is one of the most popular non-dual teachers in the contemporary world. He is a disciple of Papaji (H.W.L. Poonja) and through him the lineage of Ramana Maharshi. His satsangs attract thousands worldwide and his videos have millions of views online. He founded Monte Sahaja, a retreat center in Portugal.
