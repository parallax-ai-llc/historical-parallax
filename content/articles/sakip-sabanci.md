## Summary

Sakip Sabanci was the chairman of Sabanci Holding, one of Turkey's two largest conglomerates. The Sabanci Group operates in banking (Akbank), automotive, cement, energy, and retail. He was Turkey's wealthiest person for many years. He was also a noted art collector and calligraphy enthusiast, and the Sabanci Museum in Istanbul houses one of the country's finest art collections.
