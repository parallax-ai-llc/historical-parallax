## Summary

Arvo Part is an Estonian composer who developed the tintinnabuli compositional style, creating music of profound spiritual simplicity. His works including Fratres, Spiegel im Spiegel, and Tabula Rasa are among the most performed contemporary classical works. He is the most performed living composer in the world.
