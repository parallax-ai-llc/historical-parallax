## Summary

Bambang Hidayat was Indonesia's most prominent astronomer who directed the Bosscha Observatory and established modern astronomical research in Indonesia. He studied stellar atmospheres and variable stars and represented Indonesia in the International Astronomical Union for decades. He helped develop astronomy education throughout Southeast Asia.
