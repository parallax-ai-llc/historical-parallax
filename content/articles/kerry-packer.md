## Summary

Kerry Packer was Australia's richest person and most powerful media mogul. He controlled Channel Nine, the country's top-rated television network, and Australian Consolidated Press. He revolutionized cricket by creating World Series Cricket in 1977, introducing colored clothing, night matches, and massive broadcasting deals. He was also one of the world's most famous high-stakes gamblers, reportedly once winning $33 million in a single blackjack session.
