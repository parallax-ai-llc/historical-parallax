## Summary

Isamu Noguchi was an American-Japanese sculptor and designer whose work bridges East and West, art and design. His sculptures, gardens, furniture, lighting, and public spaces demonstrate a unique fusion of organic forms with modernist clarity. His Akari light sculptures and garden designs are icons of 20th-century design.
