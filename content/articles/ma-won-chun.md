### What “purge” commonly means in DPRK reporting

In North Korea-focused reporting, the term **purge** is frequently used as shorthand for a range of outcomes, such as:

- removal from a formal post or loss of public visibility
- internal investigation and reassignment
- detention, imprisonment, or (in some reports) harsher punishment

Without primary documentation, the safest phrasing is to distinguish between confirmed removal from office and unconfirmed reports about later treatment.
