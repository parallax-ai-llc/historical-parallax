## Summary

Mikio Sasaki served as chairman of Mitsubishi Corporation, one of Japan's largest sogo shosha (general trading companies). Under his leadership, Mitsubishi Corporation expanded its global resource investments and trading operations. He also served as chairman of the Japan Chamber of Commerce and Industry. He represented the traditional Japanese trading house model that helped Japan become an economic powerhouse.
