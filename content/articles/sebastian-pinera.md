## Summary

Sebastián Piñera was a Chilean billionaire businessman and two-time President of Chile. He made his fortune introducing credit cards to Chile and through investments in airlines (LAN Chile), TV stations, and real estate. He served as president from 2010-2014 and 2018-2022. He was one of the few billionaire entrepreneurs in Latin America to serve as a head of state.
