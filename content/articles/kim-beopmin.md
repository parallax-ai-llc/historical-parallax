## Summary

Kim Beopmin was one of the first Koreans to study modern physics. He earned a PhD in physics from Japan and returned to Korea to establish physics education programs. He was instrumental in building the foundation for Korean physics research during a period when Korea had virtually no modern scientific infrastructure.
