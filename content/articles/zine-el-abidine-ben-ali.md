## Summary
Zine El Abidine Ben Ali was the second President of Tunisia from 1987 until his overthrow in 2011 during the Jasmine Revolution, which triggered the Arab Spring. He and his family embezzled billions. He fled to Saudi Arabia, where he died in exile in 2019.
