## Summary

Zhao Jiuzhang was a Chinese meteorologist and geophysicist who is considered the father of China's satellite program. He led the development of China's first artificial satellite, Dong Fang Hong 1. He also made important contributions to meteorology and atmospheric physics. He died during the Cultural Revolution.
