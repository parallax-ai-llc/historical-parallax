## Summary

Kushyar Gilani was a Persian mathematician and astronomer who wrote influential texts on arithmetic and astronomy. His Principles of Hindu Reckoning helped transmit the Hindu-Arabic numeral system throughout the Islamic world and into Europe. He also compiled astronomical tables (zij) and advanced trigonometric calculations.
