## Summary

Lu Yongxiang is a Chinese mechanical engineer who served as president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences from 1997 to 2011. He made significant contributions to fluid power transmission and control, developing new hydraulic systems for Chinese industrial machinery. Under his leadership, CAS expanded China's basic research capabilities significantly.
