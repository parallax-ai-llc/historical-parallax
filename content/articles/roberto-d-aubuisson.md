## Summary

Salvadoran military officer and far-right politician who founded the ARENA party and ordered the assassination of Archbishop Óscar Romero in 1980. He directed death squads that killed thousands during the Salvadoran Civil War. Died of cancer.

- Allegations and documentation of death-squad activity and political violence
- The founding and long-term influence of the ARENA party in postwar politics
