## Summary

Yun Chi-ho was a Korean diplomat and educator who studied at Vanderbilt University and Emory University. He promoted Western science education in Korea and helped establish modern educational institutions. His extensive diaries provide detailed observations of scientific and technological developments he witnessed during the Enlightenment period of Korea.
