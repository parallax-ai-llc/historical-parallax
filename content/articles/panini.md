## Summary

Panini was an ancient Indian linguist who authored the Ashtadhyayi, a comprehensive grammar of Sanskrit in about 4,000 rules. His grammar is considered the most sophisticated linguistic analysis of the ancient world and anticipated concepts in modern formal language theory, computer science, and artificial intelligence. His rule-based system parallels modern programming.
