## Summary

Choe Han-gi was a late Joseon Dynasty scholar who attempted to integrate Western and Eastern scientific thought. His concept of 'gi' (vital force) as the basis of natural phenomena anticipated some ideas of modern physics. He wrote extensively on astronomy, meteorology, and medicine, promoting empirical approaches to knowledge.
