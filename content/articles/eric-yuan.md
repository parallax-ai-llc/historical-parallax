## Summary

Eric Yuan founded Zoom Video Communications in 2011, which became the defining communication tool of the COVID-19 pandemic. Zoom grew from a workplace video conferencing tool to a household name virtually overnight, going from 10 million to 300 million daily meeting participants in just three months. He was previously a VP of engineering at Cisco/WebEx. Zoom's stock surged over 400% in 2020, making Yuan one of the wealthiest people in tech.
