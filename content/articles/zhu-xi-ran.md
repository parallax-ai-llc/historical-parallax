## Summary

Zhu Xi-Ran is a Chinese agricultural scientist who developed high-yield cotton varieties that significantly increased China's cotton production. Her hybrid cotton breeding program combined disease resistance with improved fiber quality, helping China become the world's largest cotton producer.
