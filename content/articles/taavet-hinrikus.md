## Summary

Taavet Hinrikus is the co-founder of Wise (formerly TransferWise), the international money transfer service that disrupted the foreign exchange industry with transparent, low-cost transfers. He was Skype's first employee and used his experience to build Wise into a London-listed fintech with millions of customers. Wise went public through a direct listing in 2021. He has been a leading voice in European fintech and Estonian startup ecosystem.
