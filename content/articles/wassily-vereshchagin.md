## Summary

Vasily Vereshchagin was a Russian battle painter who created powerful anti-war paintings based on his firsthand experiences in military campaigns. His Apotheosis of War, depicting a pyramid of skulls, became a universal symbol of the devastation of war. He traveled extensively across Central Asia and India, documenting cultures and conflicts.
