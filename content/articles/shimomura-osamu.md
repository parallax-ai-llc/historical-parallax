## Summary

Shimomura Osamu was a Japanese organic chemist who discovered green fluorescent protein (GFP) and its companion protein aequorin in the jellyfish Aequorea victoria. He shared the 2008 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for GFP, which revolutionized biological research by enabling scientists to visualize proteins and cellular processes in living organisms.
