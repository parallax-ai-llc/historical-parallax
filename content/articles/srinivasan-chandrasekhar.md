## Summary

Srinivasan Chandrasekhar was an Indian-American engineer who was a pioneer in fiber optics communication. His research at Bell Labs and Standard Telecommunication Laboratories contributed to developing practical optical fiber systems. He made fundamental contributions to understanding signal transmission through optical fibers.
