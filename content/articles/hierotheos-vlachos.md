## Summary

Metropolitan Hierotheos Vlachos is a Greek Orthodox bishop and prolific author whose works have made Orthodox theology accessible to modern readers worldwide. His books, particularly Orthodox Psychotherapy, present the patristic understanding of the human person and spiritual healing. He has written over 100 books that have been translated into many languages. He serves as Metropolitan of Nafpaktos and is one of the most widely read contemporary Orthodox writers.
