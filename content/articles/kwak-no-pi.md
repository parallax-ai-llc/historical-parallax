## Summary

Kwak No-pi was a Joseon Dynasty architect and engineer who designed the Gyeonghoeru Pavilion at Gyeongbokgung Palace, one of Korea's most iconic architectural structures. His engineering feat created a large banquet hall standing on 48 stone columns over an artificial lake, demonstrating advanced architectural and hydraulic engineering in 15th century Korea.
