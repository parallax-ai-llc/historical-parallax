## Summary

The Sengoku (Warring States) period (1467-1615) was an era of civil war and social upheaval in Japan. Powerful daimyo competed for control until Oda Nobunaga, Toyotomi Hideyoshi, and Tokugawa Ieyasu reunified Japan.
