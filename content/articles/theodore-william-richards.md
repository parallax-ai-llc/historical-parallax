## Summary

Theodore William Richards was an American chemist who won the 1914 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his accurate determinations of the atomic weights of a large number of chemical elements. He was the first American to win the Nobel Prize in Chemistry. His precise measurements of atomic weights contributed to the development of the periodic table.
