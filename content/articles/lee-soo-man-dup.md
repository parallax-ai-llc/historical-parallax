## Biography Highlights (Lee Soo-man)

- **Lee Soo-man** (Korean: 이수만; born **18 June 1952**) is a South Korean business executive and record producer.
- He is best known as the **founder of SM Entertainment** (established in **1989**; renamed **SM Entertainment** in **1995**).
- He debuted as a singer in **1971** while a student at **Seoul National University**.
- He later studied in the United States and earned an **M.S.** from **California State University, Northridge**.

## Impact (High-Level)

- Often cited as one of the early architects of modern K-pop’s agency/production model and a contributor to the growth of the Korean Wave (Hallyu).
- Associated with the development of "culture technology" as a concept for systematizing talent development, production, and global distribution.
