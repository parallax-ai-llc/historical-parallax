## Summary

Michael Rubin is the founder and CEO of Fanatics, the world's largest licensed sports merchandise company. He also co-owns the Philadelphia 76ers and New Jersey Devils. Fanatics was valued at $31 billion and controls e-commerce for major sports leagues including NFL, NBA, MLB, and NHL. He founded GSI Commerce (sold to eBay for $2.4 billion) before creating Fanatics. He is one of the most powerful figures in American sports business.
