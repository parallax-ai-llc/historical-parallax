## Summary

Emilio Azcárraga Jean was the chairman of Grupo Televisa, the largest media company in the Spanish-speaking world. Televisa dominates Mexican television and is a major producer of telenovelas distributed globally. He inherited the media empire from his father and oversaw its merger with Univision in 2022, creating TelevisaUnivision, the world's largest Spanish-language media company. The Azcárraga family has controlled Mexican media for three generations.
