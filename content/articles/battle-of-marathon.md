## Summary

The Battle of Marathon in 490 BC was a pivotal Greek victory over Persia. Miltiades led the Athenian army to defeat a larger Persian force, preserving Greek independence and paving the way for the Golden Age of Athens. The legendary run of Pheidippides inspired the modern marathon.
