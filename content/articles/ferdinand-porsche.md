## Summary

Ferdinand Porsche was an automotive engineer who designed the Volkswagen Beetle, one of the most manufactured cars in history, and founded the Porsche sports car company. He designed the Beetle under commission from Adolf Hitler as a 'people's car.' He also created the Mercedes-Benz SS/SSK sports cars and Tiger tanks during WWII. After the war, his son Ferry Porsche built the Porsche 356, launching the iconic sports car brand.
