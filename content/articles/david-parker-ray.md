## Summary
David Parker Ray, the 'Toy Box Killer,' was an American serial killer and torturer who is believed to have kidnapped, tortured, and murdered up to 60 women in Truth or Consequences, New Mexico. He built an elaborate torture chamber in a trailer. He died of a heart attack before serving his 224-year sentence.
