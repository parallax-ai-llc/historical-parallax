## Summary

Gilberto Gil is a Brazilian musician singer-songwriter and politician who co-founded Tropicalia with Caetano Veloso. His music blends traditional Brazilian forms with African rhythms rock and electronic music. He served as Brazil Minister of Culture from 2003 to 2008 and continues to be a major cultural figure.
