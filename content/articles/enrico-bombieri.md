## Summary

Enrico Bombieri is an Italian mathematician who received the Fields Medal in 1974 for his major contributions to number theory, algebraic geometry, and the theory of several complex variables. He proved the large sieve method and made advances on the Riemann hypothesis for algebraic varieties.
