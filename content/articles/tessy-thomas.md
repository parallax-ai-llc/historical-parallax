## Summary

Tessy Thomas, known as India's 'Missile Woman,' was the project director of the Agni-IV and Agni-V missile programs, making her the first woman to head a missile project in India. The Agni-V, an intercontinental ballistic missile, was successfully tested in 2012. She is currently Director General of DRDO's Aeronautical Systems.
