## Summary

Laurene Powell Jobs is the founder of Emerson Collective and one of the world's wealthiest women, inheriting Steve Jobs' stake in Apple and Disney. Through Emerson Collective, she invests in education, immigration reform, social justice, and media. She acquired a majority stake in The Atlantic magazine and invested in media, food, and health companies. She has become one of the most influential philanthropists and investors in Silicon Valley.
