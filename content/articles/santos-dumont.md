## Summary

Alberto Santos-Dumont was a Brazilian aviator and inventor who is a national hero in Brazil for his contributions to aviation. He made the first public flight of a powered heavier-than-air aircraft in Europe with the 14-bis in 1906. He also designed the first practical wristwatch with Louis Cartier and pioneered lighter-than-air flight with his dirigibles. In Brazil, he is considered the 'father of aviation.'
