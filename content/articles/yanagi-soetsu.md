## Summary

Yanagi Soetsu was a Japanese art critic and philosopher who founded the mingei (folk craft) movement, which celebrated the beauty of everyday handmade objects. His writings including The Unknown Craftsman articulated an aesthetic of humble functionality and unconscious beauty. He founded the Japan Folk Crafts Museum in Tokyo.
