## Summary
Kim Dotcom (Kim Schmitz) is a German-Finnish Internet entrepreneur who founded Megaupload, a file-sharing service accused of facilitating massive copyright infringement. The FBI shut down the site in 2012. He has been fighting extradition from New Zealand to the United States.
