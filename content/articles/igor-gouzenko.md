## Summary

Soviet cipher clerk who defected in Ottawa in 1945, exposing a massive Soviet espionage network in Canada and the West. His defection is considered the event that triggered the Cold War.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1943 | Arrived in Ottawa as cipher clerk at Soviet embassy |
| 1945 | Defected with 109 documents proving Soviet espionage |
| 1982 | Died in hiding in Canada under assumed identity |
