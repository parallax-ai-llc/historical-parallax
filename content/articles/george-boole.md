## Summary

George Boole was a British mathematician, philosopher, and logician who is best known as the founder of Boolean algebra, which became the foundation of digital circuit design and modern computer science. His work 'An Investigation of the Laws of Thought' established the algebraic logic that underlies all digital computers. Boolean logic is essential to every computer, smartphone, and digital device.
