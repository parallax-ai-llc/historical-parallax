## Summary

Amy Winehouse was a British singer and songwriter whose soulful voice and deeply personal lyrics made her one of the most talented artists of her generation. Her album Back to Black became one of the best-selling albums in UK history. Her death at 27 from alcohol poisoning cut short one of music most distinctive voices.
