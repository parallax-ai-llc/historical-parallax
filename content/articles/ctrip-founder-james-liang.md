## Summary

James Liang (Liang Jianzhang) co-founded Ctrip (now Trip.com Group), China's largest online travel agency. He is both a successful tech entrepreneur and a prominent economist who has written extensively about China's demographic challenges. Ctrip went public on NASDAQ in 2003 and later rebranded as Trip.com Group. He returned as CEO in 2013 to revitalize the company and later served as chairman.
