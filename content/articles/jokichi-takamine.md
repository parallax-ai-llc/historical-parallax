## Summary

Jokichi Takamine was a Japanese-American chemist who was the first to isolate the hormone adrenaline (epinephrine) in pure crystalline form in 1901. He also developed takadiastase, an enzyme for digestive disorders, which became a commercial success. Takamine donated the cherry trees that bloom along the Tidal Basin in Washington, D.C.
