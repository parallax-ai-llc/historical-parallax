## Summary

Daniel Ek co-founded Spotify in 2006, transforming the music industry with the world's largest audio streaming platform. He launched the service in Sweden in 2008 as an alternative to music piracy. Spotify went public in 2018 through a direct listing and expanded into podcasts and audiobooks. The platform grew to over 600 million users and fundamentally changed how people consume music.
