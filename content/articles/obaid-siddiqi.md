## Summary

Obaid Siddiqi was an Indian molecular biologist who founded the molecular biology program at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR). He pioneered the use of Drosophila genetics to study the nervous system and discovered the first olfactory mutants in fruit flies. He founded the National Centre for Biological Sciences (NCBS) in Bangalore.
