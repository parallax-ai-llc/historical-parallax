## Summary

Ben Enwonwu was Nigeria's most celebrated modern artist, known for his masterful synthesis of Nigerian artistic traditions with European modernism. His painting Tutu, rediscovered in 2018, symbolizes the reconciliation after the Nigerian Civil War. He was the first African artist to gain international recognition in the postwar era.
