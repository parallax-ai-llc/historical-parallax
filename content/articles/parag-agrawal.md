## Summary

Parag Agrawal served as CEO of Twitter from November 2021 until Elon Musk's acquisition in October 2022. He was previously Twitter's CTO, joining the company in 2011 as a software engineer. He led Twitter's technical infrastructure including machine learning and recommendation algorithms. His brief tenure as CEO was dominated by the Musk acquisition saga, which became one of the most dramatic corporate takeover stories in history.
