## Identity and Alternate Spellings

"Nasiruddin Tusi" is a common English rendering of **Nasir al-Din al-Tusi**. Variations reflect different transliteration systems for Arabic and Persian names. Biographical accounts under these spellings generally refer to the same scholar.

## Contributions (High-Level)

Tusi’s work is noted for spanning multiple fields:

- **Astronomy:** Development and critique of planetary models; association with major observational programs.
- **Mathematics:** Important writings in geometry and trigonometry; systematic treatment of trigonometry is often highlighted in histories of mathematics.
- **Philosophy and ethics:** Production of influential ethical and philosophical treatises used in later scholarly curricula.

## Maragheh Scientific Center

Tusi is widely associated with the Maragheh observatory complex, often described as a research institution that:

- coordinated observations and the construction/use of instruments,
- supported teams of scholars working on astronomical tables,
- provided an institutional model for later observatories.

## Influence and Reception

Later scholars referenced and commented on Tusi’s writings across the Islamic world. Modern histories of science often discuss him in connection with the transmission and transformation of mathematical astronomy and the ways pre-modern scholars refined classical models.
