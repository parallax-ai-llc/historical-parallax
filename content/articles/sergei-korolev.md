## Summary

Sergei Korolev was the chief Soviet rocket engineer and spacecraft designer during the Space Race. He led the development of the R-7 rocket, Sputnik (first artificial satellite), and Vostok (first human spaceflight by Yuri Gagarin). His identity was kept secret during his lifetime, and he was known only as the "Chief Designer."
