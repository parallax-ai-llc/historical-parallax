## Summary

Osman Ali Khan, the 7th Nizam of Hyderabad, was once considered the richest man in the world. He ruled the largest and wealthiest princely state in India. His personal wealth included a 185-carat Jacob Diamond used as a paperweight. He invested in banking, railways, and industry, and established Osmania University. After India's independence, Hyderabad was integrated into India through Operation Polo in 1948.
