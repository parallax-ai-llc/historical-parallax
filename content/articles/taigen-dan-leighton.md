## Summary

Taigen Dan Leighton is an American Soto Zen priest, scholar, and author known for his translations of Dogen and his scholarship on bodhisattva archetypes. He teaches at the Ancient Dragon Zen Gate in Chicago. His books, including Faces of Compassion and Visions of Awakening Space and Time, bridge traditional Zen practice with modern Western cultural concerns. He is recognized for making Dogen's profound philosophy accessible to contemporary readers.
