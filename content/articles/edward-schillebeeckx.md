## Summary

Edward Schillebeeckx was a Belgian Dominican theologian who was one of the most prolific Catholic theologians of the 20th century. He served as an advisor at the Second Vatican Council and influenced the Dutch Catechism. His massive Christological trilogy reinterpreted Jesus for the modern world. He was investigated by the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith but never formally condemned.
