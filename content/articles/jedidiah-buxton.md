## Summary

Jedidiah Buxton was an English mental calculator of extraordinary ability who could perform complex calculations in his head but was otherwise illiterate. While primarily known as a mathematical savant, his devout religious life and understanding of spiritual matters were well documented. He is an example of the intersection of natural gifts and religious conviction in the 18th century.
