## Summary

The Battle of Tours in 732 AD saw Frankish leader Charles Martel defeat an invading Umayyad army from Muslim Spain. The victory halted Islamic expansion into Western Europe and established the Franks as the dominant power. Martel's grandson Charlemagne would found a great European empire.
