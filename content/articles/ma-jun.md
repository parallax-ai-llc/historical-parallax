## Summary

Ma Jun was a Three Kingdoms era Chinese mechanical engineer who improved the south-pointing chariot (a non-magnetic compass vehicle using differential gears), built a chain pump for irrigation, and created mechanical puppet theater. He is considered one of the greatest mechanical engineers in Chinese history, known for his innovative problem-solving abilities.
