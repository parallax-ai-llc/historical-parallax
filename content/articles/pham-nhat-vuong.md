## Summary

Pham Nhat Vuong is Vietnam's richest person and founder of Vingroup, the country's largest private conglomerate. He initially made his fortune selling instant noodles in Ukraine before returning to Vietnam to build a real estate empire. He then expanded into electric vehicles (VinFast), smartphones, hospitals, and education. VinFast went public in the U.S. in 2023, briefly making it more valuable than Ford or GM on paper.
