## Additional Context

## Business profile

Bozhkov is widely described as a prominent Bulgarian businessman whose activities have included gambling and other investments. Public profiles often emphasize his influence in sectors where licensing, regulation, and political connections can strongly affect market outcomes.[^1]

## Criminal allegations

Bulgarian prosecutors announced multiple charges against him, including allegations connected to organized crime, extortion, and corruption. Bozhkov has denied wrongdoing, and public discussion frequently centers on the contested nature of the cases and the broader environment of Bulgarian rule-of-law debates.[^1]

## Exile and international actions

After leaving Bulgaria, he was reported to be in the United Arab Emirates. In 2021, the United States imposed sanctions under the Global Magnitsky program, a mechanism used to target foreign persons accused of serious corruption or human-rights abuses.[^1]

## Political and social impact

High-profile cases involving oligarch figures in Bulgaria are often treated as signals of shifting political coalitions and pressure for institutional reform. Bozhkov’s case is commonly referenced in discussions about the relationship between business power, law enforcement, and public trust.[^1]
