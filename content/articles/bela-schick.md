## Summary

Béla Schick developed the Schick test for diphtheria susceptibility in 1913, enabling identification of individuals who needed vaccination. This simple skin test became a crucial public health tool for controlling diphtheria epidemics. He was also a pioneer in pediatric allergy and immunology.
