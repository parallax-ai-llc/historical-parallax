## Summary

W. Edwards Deming was an American statistician who is credited with transforming Japanese manufacturing quality after WWII. His statistical process control methods and 14 Points for Management were adopted widely by Japanese industry, helping Japan achieve world-class manufacturing quality. The Deming Prize in Japan is named in his honor.
