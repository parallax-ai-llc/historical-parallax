## Summary

Zhong Nanshan is a Chinese pulmonologist who gained national fame for his role in identifying and combating the SARS epidemic in 2003. He was among the first to alert the public to the severity of SARS and developed treatment protocols that reduced mortality. In 2020, he again played a leading role in China's COVID-19 response.
