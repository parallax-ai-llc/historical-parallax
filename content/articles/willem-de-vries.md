## Summary

William DeVries implanted the first permanent artificial heart (Jarvik-7) in a human patient, Barney Clark, on December 2, 1982. The procedure at the University of Utah demonstrated that a mechanical device could sustain human life. His surgical achievement was a milestone in the development of artificial organs.
