## Summary
The Brink's-Mat robbery occurred on November 26, 1983, at the Brink's-Mat warehouse at Heathrow Airport. Six armed men stole 3 tons of gold bullion, diamonds, and cash worth £26 million. Only a fraction was recovered. The robbery led to multiple murders as criminals fought over the proceeds.
