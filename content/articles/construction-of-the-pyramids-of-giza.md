## Summary

The Great Pyramid of Giza was built around 2560 BC as a tomb for Pharaoh Khufu. Standing 481 feet tall, it was the world's tallest structure for over 3,800 years. Its precise construction from 2.3 million stone blocks remains an engineering marvel.
