## Summary

Konstantin Tsiolkovsky was a Russian scientist and rocket pioneer who is considered the father of astronautics. He derived the Tsiolkovsky rocket equation, which describes the motion of rocket vehicles. He was the first to theorize about space elevators, multi-stage rockets, and human space colonization. His work inspired subsequent rocket engineers including Korolev and von Braun.
