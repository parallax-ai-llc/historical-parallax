## Summary

Daymond John is the founder and CEO of FUBU, the urban fashion brand that became a cultural phenomenon in the 1990s. He started making tie-top hats in his mother's house in Queens and built FUBU into a $6 billion fashion brand. He later became an investor on Shark Tank. His story of bootstrapping a fashion brand from his neighborhood embodies the American entrepreneurial dream.
