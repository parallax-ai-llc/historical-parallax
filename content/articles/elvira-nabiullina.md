## Summary

Elvira Nabiullina has served as Governor of the Central Bank of Russia since 2013, becoming the first woman to head the central bank of a G8 nation. She stabilized Russia's financial system during the 2014-15 ruble crisis and managed the economy under Western sanctions after 2022. She was previously Russia's Minister of Economic Development. Euromoney named her Central Bank Governor of the Year multiple times.
