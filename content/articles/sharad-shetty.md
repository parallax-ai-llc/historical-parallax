## Background

Sharad Shetty is described in Indian media and law-enforcement reporting as a figure connected to Mumbai’s organized-crime ecosystem, with parts of his network operating through the Gulf region. Public accounts often place him among groups involved in coercive “protection” rackets and underworld financing.

## Alleged activities and network

Open-source reporting commonly attributes to Shetty’s group involvement in:

- Extortion and intimidation of businesses.
- Contract violence tied to gang disputes.
- Use of intermediaries to manage money flows and real-estate dealings.

Such syndicates are often characterized by overlapping alliances and rivalries, where individuals may cooperate on specific operations while competing in others.
