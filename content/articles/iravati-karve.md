## Summary

Iravati Karve was a pioneering Indian anthropologist who conducted physical anthropology studies of the Indian population, using scientific measurements to analyze the diversity of Indian peoples. Her book Kinship Organization in India is a foundational text in Indian sociology and anthropology.
