## Summary

Do Ho Suh is a Korean artist known for his translucent fabric replicas of architectural spaces and his explorations of displacement, memory, and home. His life-size fabric corridors, staircases, and apartments recreate domestic spaces from his life across Seoul, New York, and Berlin. His work poetically addresses the immigrant experience.
