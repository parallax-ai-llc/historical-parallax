## Summary

Johannes van der Waals was a Dutch physicist who won the 1910 Nobel Prize in Physics for his work on the equation of state for gases and liquids. The Van der Waals equation and Van der Waals forces bear his name. His work was fundamental to understanding intermolecular forces and the behavior of real gases.
