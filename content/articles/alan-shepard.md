## Summary

Alan Shepard became the first American in space on May 5, 1961, with a 15-minute suborbital flight aboard Freedom 7. He later commanded Apollo 14 in 1971, famously hitting golf balls on the Moon. He was the only Mercury astronaut to walk on the Moon.
