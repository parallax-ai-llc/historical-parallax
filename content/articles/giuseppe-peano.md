## Summary

Giuseppe Peano was an Italian mathematician and logician who formulated the Peano axioms for the natural numbers, which became a cornerstone of mathematical logic. He developed a formal language for mathematical logic and contributed to the foundations of mathematics. His work influenced Bertrand Russell and the development of axiomatic set theory.
