## Summary

Swami Chinmayananda was an Indian Hindu spiritual leader who founded the Chinmaya Mission and was instrumental in popularizing Vedanta philosophy worldwide. A former journalist, he studied under Swami Sivananda and Swami Tapovanam. He conducted over 500 lecture series (Jnana Yajnas) on the Bhagavad Gita and Upanishads across the world. He helped establish the Vishva Hindu Parishad (VHP) and his mission now operates in over 300 centers worldwide.
