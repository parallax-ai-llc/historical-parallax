Jay-Jay Okocha was one of the most skillful African footballers, famous for his dribbling. He starred at PSG and Bolton Wanderers.

## Overview
Jay-Jay Okocha (Augustine Azuka Okocha) is a Nigerian former professional footballer known for technical skill, creativity, and flair as an attacking midfielder. He played club football in Europe and represented Nigeria internationally.

## Club career
Okocha played for clubs including Eintracht Frankfurt, Fenerbahçe, Paris Saint-Germain, and Bolton Wanderers. He was often praised for dribbling, close control, and the ability to create scoring chances from open play and set pieces.

## International career
He was a key figure for the Nigeria national team across multiple tournaments, including the Africa Cup of Nations and FIFA World Cup appearances. His generation helped establish Nigeria as a consistent force in international football.

## Style and legacy
Okocha is frequently cited as an influential entertainer on the pitch, with highlights that remain widely shared. His reputation extends beyond statistics, reflecting the impact of memorable performances and technical artistry on how fans evaluate attacking players.
