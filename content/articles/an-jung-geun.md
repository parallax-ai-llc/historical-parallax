## Summary

An Jung-geun was a Korean independence activist who assassinated Japanese Resident-General Ito Hirobumi in Harbin, China in 1909. While primarily a political figure, he wrote the Dongyang Pyeonghwa Ron (On Eastern Peace) proposing a systematic framework for East Asian cooperation and peace, demonstrating analytical thinking about international relations.
