## Summary

Heo Nanseolheon was a female poet and scholar of the Joseon Dynasty whose intellectual accomplishments challenged gender barriers. She was educated in astronomy, mathematics, and philosophy in addition to literature. Her poems reflecting on nature show detailed astronomical observations. She died at just 27 but left a remarkable literary legacy.
