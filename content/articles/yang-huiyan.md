## Summary

Yang Huiyan is the chairwoman of Country Garden Holdings, one of China's largest property developers. She became Asia's richest woman after her father Yang Guoqiang transferred his shares to her. Country Garden became the largest property developer in China by contracted sales before the sector's downturn. The Chinese property crisis severely impacted Country Garden, which defaulted on debt in 2023.
