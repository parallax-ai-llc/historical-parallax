## Summary

Luca Signorelli was an Italian Renaissance painter renowned for his powerful depictions of the human body in motion. His fresco cycle of The Last Judgment in Orvieto Cathedral is considered a precursor to Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel ceiling. Trained under Piero della Francesca, he combined spatial clarity with anatomical energy.
