## Summary

Santiago Calatrava is a Spanish-Swiss architect and engineer known for his dramatic, sculptural bridges and buildings. His Turning Torso in Malmo, the World Trade Center Transportation Hub in New York, and the City of Arts and Sciences in Valencia exemplify his fusion of engineering and art.
