## Summary

Quarraisha Abdool Karim is a South African epidemiologist and infectious diseases researcher who has made major contributions to HIV prevention, particularly for women in sub-Saharan Africa. Her CAPRISA 004 tenofovir gel trial was the first to demonstrate that an antiretroviral microbicide could prevent HIV infection. She is associate scientific director of CAPRISA and has received numerous international awards.
