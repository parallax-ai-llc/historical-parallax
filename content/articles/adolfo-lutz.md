## Summary

Adolfo Lutz was a pioneering Brazilian physician who made major contributions to tropical medicine. He identified the mosquito vector of yellow fever independently of American researchers and studied parasitic diseases, leprosy, and mycoses. The Instituto Adolfo Lutz, a major public health laboratory in São Paulo, bears his name.
