## Summary

Leonard Cohen was a Canadian singer-songwriter and poet whose literary songs including Hallelujah, Suzanne, and Famous Blue Raincoat are among the most covered in popular music. His deep baritone voice and philosophical lyrics made him one of music's most revered figures.
