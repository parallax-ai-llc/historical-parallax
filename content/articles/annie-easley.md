## Summary

Annie Easley was one of the first African Americans to work at NASA (then NACA) as a computer scientist. She developed code for the Centaur upper-stage rocket booster, which was used to launch communication satellites and planetary probes including Cassini. She also worked on alternative energy technology including batteries for hybrid vehicles.
