## Summary

John Bunyan was an English Puritan preacher and author of The Pilgrim's Progress, one of the most widely read books in the English language. He spent 12 years in prison for preaching without a license. His allegorical work profoundly influenced Protestant spirituality and English literature. He also wrote Grace Abounding to the Chief of Sinners and The Holy War.
