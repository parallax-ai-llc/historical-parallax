## Summary

Suh Nam Pyo is a Korean-American mechanical engineer at MIT who developed axiomatic design theory, a systematic approach to engineering design. His framework for making design decisions based on functional requirements and design parameters has been adopted in manufacturing and systems engineering worldwide. He also served as president of KAIST.
