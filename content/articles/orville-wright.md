## Summary

Orville Wright piloted the first powered, sustained, and controlled heavier-than-air flight on December 17, 1903, at Kitty Hawk, covering 120 feet in 12 seconds. With his brother Wilbur, he designed and built the Wright Flyer. Their achievement opened the age of aviation and transformed global transportation.
