## Summary

Fei-Fei Li is a Chinese-American computer scientist known for creating ImageNet, a large-scale visual database that catalyzed the deep learning revolution in computer vision. The ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge became a benchmark that demonstrated the superiority of deep neural networks. She co-directs Stanford's Human-Centered AI Institute and served as VP at Google Cloud AI. Li is a leading advocate for human-centered AI.
