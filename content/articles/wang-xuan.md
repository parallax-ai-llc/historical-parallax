## Summary

Wang Xuan was a Chinese computer scientist who invented the Chinese electronic publishing system, revolutionizing Chinese-language printing and publishing. His laser typesetting system solved the enormous technical challenge of digitizing thousands of Chinese characters and was adopted by over 99% of Chinese newspapers. He is considered the 'Gutenberg of China.'
