## Background

Wilson Joseph is identified in reporting as a leader of the 400 Mawozo gang in Haiti. The group has been associated with kidnapping-for-ransom and armed control of territory in and around the Port-au-Prince region.

## 400 Mawozo and the Kidnapping Crisis

In the early 2020s, kidnappings became a prominent security concern in Haiti, affecting residents, humanitarian workers, and international visitors. The 2021 abduction of 17 people connected to a U.S.-based missionary organization brought sustained global coverage to the issue.

## Government and International Response

Public responses have included police operations, calls for stronger institutions, and international attention to the humanitarian and security situation. Discussions around the gang phenomenon often highlight the interaction of political instability, economic hardship, and limited state capacity.
