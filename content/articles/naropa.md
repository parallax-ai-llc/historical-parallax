## Summary

Naropa was an Indian Buddhist scholar and tantric master who is one of the 84 Mahasiddhas (great accomplished ones) of Buddhist tantra. He was a renowned scholar at Nalanda University who abandoned academic life to pursue tantric practice under his teacher Tilopa. His 'Six Yogas of Naropa' became foundational practices in Tibetan Buddhist tantra. He transmitted his teachings to the Tibetan translator Marpa, establishing the Kagyu lineage.
