## Summary

Doris Salcedo is a Colombian sculptor and installation artist whose powerful works address the violence and loss caused by armed conflict. Her installations including Shibboleth at Tate Modern and her memorials using everyday objects like chairs and shoes create haunting testimonies to absent victims. She is considered one of the most important sculptors working today.
