## Summary

Willem Kolff invented the first practical dialysis machine in 1943 during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands, saving patients with kidney failure. He later pioneered the development of artificial hearts and other artificial organs. His inventions in artificial organ technology have saved millions of lives worldwide.
