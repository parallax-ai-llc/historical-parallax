## Summary

Frank Wilczek is an American theoretical physicist who shared the 2004 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of asymptotic freedom in quantum chromodynamics. He made this discovery as a graduate student with David Gross. He also proposed the concept of anyons and time crystals and has written extensively on physics for the general public.
