## Summary

Michael Budi Hartono is an Indonesian billionaire and co-owner of Djarum and BCA with his brother Robert. He is one of Indonesia's richest people through tobacco and banking. BCA has been Indonesia's most valuable and profitable private bank. The Hartono brothers diversified from tobacco into banking, technology, and property.
