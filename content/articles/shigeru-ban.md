## Summary

Shigeru Ban is a Japanese architect who won the 2014 Pritzker Prize for his innovative use of cardboard tubes and other unconventional materials in disaster relief shelters. His paper tube structures can be quickly assembled and provide dignified housing after earthquakes and natural disasters. He has provided emergency shelters in Rwanda, Turkey, India, and Japan.
