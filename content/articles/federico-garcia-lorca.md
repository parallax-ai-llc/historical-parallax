## Summary

Federico Garcia Lorca was a Spanish poet and dramatist who is Spain's most famous 20th-century writer. His poetry, including the collections Gypsy Ballads and Poet in New York, fuses folk traditions with surrealist innovation. His plays Blood Wedding and The House of Bernarda Alba are masterpieces of modern theater. He was assassinated by Nationalist forces at the start of the Spanish Civil War.
