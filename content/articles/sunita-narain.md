## Summary

Sunita Narain is the director general of the Centre for Science and Environment in India and a leading voice on environmental issues including air pollution, water management, and climate change. Her research on vehicular pollution led to the implementation of CNG buses in Delhi. She received the Stockholm Water Prize in 2005.
