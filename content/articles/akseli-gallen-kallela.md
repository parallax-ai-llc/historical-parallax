## Summary

Akseli Gallen-Kallela was Finland's foremost painter and central figure in Finnish national romanticism. His monumental Kalevala paintings became iconic symbols of Finnish identity during the independence struggle. His fusion of mythology with modern expression made him the visual architect of Finnish consciousness.
