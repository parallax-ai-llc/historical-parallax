## Summary

Zhang Lei is the founder of Hillhouse Capital, one of Asia's largest investment firms with over $60 billion in assets under management. He was an early investor in Tencent, JD.com, and many of China's most successful technology companies. Educated at Renmin University and Yale, he bridges Chinese and American investment worlds. His book 'Value' became a bestseller in China and outlines his long-term investing philosophy.
