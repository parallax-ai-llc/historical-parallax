## Summary

Carlos Alberto Sicupira is a Brazilian billionaire and co-founder of 3G Capital alongside Jorge Paulo Lemann and Marcel Telles. He served as CEO of AmBev and was instrumental in the acquisitions that created AB InBev, the world's largest brewer. He also co-owns Americanas (a major Brazilian retailer) and is known for his intense management style and cost-cutting discipline. The 3G Capital partnership transformed global consumer goods.
