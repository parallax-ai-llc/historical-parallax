## Summary

Afghan-American who pleaded guilty to planning suicide bombings on the New York City subway.

## Background

- Zazi was born in Afghanistan and later immigrated to the United States, settling in New York City.
- Public reporting describes him as having worked in the New York area and living in Queens prior to the plot.

## 2009 New York City subway plot (high level)

- The plot focused on coordinated attacks in New York City, with the subway system frequently described as the intended target.[^1]
- Reporting indicates Zazi traveled abroad, received guidance or training linked to al-Qaeda, and then returned to the U.S. to prepare an attack.[^1]
- Investigators alleged the plan involved obtaining chemicals and materials for an improvised explosive device (often described in connection with TATP in contemporaneous coverage).[^1]

## Investigation and arrest

- Federal authorities tracked travel and communications and conducted interviews and searches as the case developed in 2009.[^1]
- Zazi was arrested and charged in federal court; the case became a major post-9/11 counterterrorism prosecution.[^1]

## Guilty plea and cooperation

- In 2010, Zazi pleaded guilty in federal court to charges related to the conspiracy.[^1]
- He agreed to cooperate with investigators, and his cooperation was reported as relevant to cases involving other individuals tied to the same plot.[^1]

## Related people and cases

- The case is often discussed alongside other defendants accused of involvement with the same conspiracy, including Adis Medunjanin and Zarein Ahmedzay.[^1]

## Why the case is notable

- The alleged plan is widely characterized as one of the more serious terrorism threats to New York City in the years after 9/11.[^1]
- It is commonly cited in discussions of U.S. counterterrorism investigations, overseas training networks, and the role of cooperation agreements in terrorism prosecutions.[^1]

## Notes

- Many specifics (exact targets, device design, and timing) are described differently across sources; summaries typically focus on the admitted conspiracy and the disruption of the plan before execution.[^1]
