## Summary
Anthony Edward Sowell was an American serial killer known as the 'Cleveland Strangler.' He murdered at least 11 women in Cleveland, Ohio, between 2007 and 2009, hiding their remains in and around his home. He was convicted and sentenced to death. He died on death row in 2021.
