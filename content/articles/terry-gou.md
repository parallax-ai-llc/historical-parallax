## Summary

Terry Gou (Gou Tai-ming) founded Foxconn (Hon Hai Precision Industry), the world's largest electronics contract manufacturer. Foxconn assembles products for Apple, Sony, Microsoft, and many other major tech companies, employing over 800,000 workers. He built Foxconn from a small plastic parts factory into a $200+ billion revenue company. He ran for president of Taiwan in 2019 but withdrew before the election.
