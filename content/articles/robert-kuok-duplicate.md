## Canonical Subject (Robert Kuok)

The name *Robert Kuok* most commonly refers to **Robert Kuok Hock Nien** (born 1923), a Malaysian business magnate known for building a diversified conglomerate.

### Commonly Cited Areas of Activity

- **Commodities and trading**: early prominence in the **sugar** business, which led to the nickname “Sugar King of Asia” in many secondary sources.
- **Conglomerate building**: development of a multi-sector group spanning commodities, logistics, real estate, and hospitality.
- **Hospitality**: association with major hotel and property brands in Asia through group holdings.

### Companies and Brands Often Linked to the Kuok Group (high-level)

- **Kerry Group** / **Kerry Properties** (real estate and related businesses)
