## Summary

Zhang Zhongjing is considered one of the greatest physicians in Chinese medical history. His treatise Shanghan Zabing Lun (Treatise on Cold Damage and Miscellaneous Diseases), written around 200 CE, systematized the diagnosis and treatment of diseases using herbal formulas. His work remains a foundational text in Traditional Chinese Medicine.
