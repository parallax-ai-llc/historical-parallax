## Summary

Neil Shen (Shen Nanpeng) is the founding managing partner of Sequoia Capital China (now HongShan), one of the most successful venture capital firms in China. He co-founded Ctrip (China's largest travel platform) and Home Inns before entering venture capital. Sequoia China invested in ByteDance, Meituan, Pinduoduo, and many of China's most valuable tech companies. He was named the world's best venture capitalist by Forbes multiple times.
