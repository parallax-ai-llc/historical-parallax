## Summary

Majid Al Futtaim founded the Majid Al Futtaim Group, which operates the Mall of the Emirates (home to Ski Dubai) and over 40 shopping malls across the Middle East, Africa, and Central Asia. He brought the Carrefour hypermarket brand to the region. His company also operates VOX Cinemas, the largest cinema operator in the Middle East. He was one of the architects of Dubai's transformation into a shopping and entertainment destination.
