## Summary
On March 15, 2019, an Australian white supremacist attacked two mosques in Christchurch, New Zealand, killing 51 worshippers and injuring 40. The attacker livestreamed part of the massacre. It was New Zealand's deadliest mass shooting and led to swift gun law reform.
