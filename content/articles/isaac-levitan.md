## Summary

Isaac Levitan was the most celebrated Russian landscape painter, known for his deeply emotional, poetic landscapes that capture the melancholy beauty of the Russian countryside. His paintings like Above Eternal Peace and Golden Autumn are considered the quintessence of the Russian landscape tradition. Chekhov was his close friend and artistic soulmate.
