## Summary

Dennis Sciama was a British physicist who was one of the founders of modern cosmology. He supervised many distinguished physicists including Stephen Hawking, Martin Rees, and Roger Penrose. His textbook "The Unity of the Universe" was influential in establishing cosmology as a serious scientific discipline.
