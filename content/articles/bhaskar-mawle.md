## Summary

Indian serial killer from Maharashtra who murdered at least 10 women and children. He lured victims to remote areas before sexually assaulting and killing them.

## Sentencing Context

Indian criminal law allows for the death penalty in what courts describe as the “rarest of rare” cases. A death sentence at the trial-court stage may also be followed by:

- mandatory confirmation procedures and appeals
- review by higher courts
- petitions for clemency in certain circumstances
