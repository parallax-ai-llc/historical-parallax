## Summary

Dumitru Stăniloae was a Romanian Orthodox theologian who is considered one of the greatest Orthodox systematic theologians of the 20th century. His three-volume Orthodox Dogmatic Theology is one of the most comprehensive works of Orthodox systematic theology. He was imprisoned by the Romanian communist regime for five years. He translated the Philokalia into Romanian with extensive annotations.
