## Summary

Liu Yonghao is the chairman of New Hope Group, China's largest animal feed producer and one of the largest agribusiness companies in Asia. He co-founded the Hope Group with his three brothers in 1982, making it one of China's earliest private enterprises. New Hope expanded into dairy, finance, real estate, and chemicals. He was one of the first Chinese private entrepreneurs to become a billionaire.
