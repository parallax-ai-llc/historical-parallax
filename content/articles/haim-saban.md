## Summary

Haim Saban is an Egyptian-born, Israeli-American media mogul who made his fortune through the Power Rangers franchise and television production. He founded Saban Entertainment and later acquired ProSiebenSat.1 Media, Germany's largest television group. His Power Rangers franchise has generated over $6 billion in toy sales alone. He is also one of the most prominent political donors in the United States.
