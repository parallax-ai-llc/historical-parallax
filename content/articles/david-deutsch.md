## Summary

David Deutsch is considered the father of quantum computing, having proposed the concept of a universal quantum computer in 1985. He developed the Deutsch-Jozsa algorithm, one of the first quantum algorithms to demonstrate quantum advantage. His work laid the theoretical foundation for the entire field of quantum computation.
