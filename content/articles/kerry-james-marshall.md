## Summary

Kerry James Marshall is an American painter known for his large-scale paintings depicting Black American life and asserting Black presence in the canon of Western art. His figures are rendered in the deepest shades of black, a deliberate assertion of Black identity and visibility. His painting Past Times sold for $21.1 million, a record for a living African American artist.
