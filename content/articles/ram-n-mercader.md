## Summary

Spanish-born Soviet agent who assassinated Leon Trotsky in Mexico City on August 20, 1940, by driving an ice axe into his skull. He was operating under orders from Stalin. Served 20 years in Mexican prison and received the Hero of the Soviet Union medal.
