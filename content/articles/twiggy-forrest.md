## Summary

Andrew 'Twiggy' Forrest founded Fortescue Metals Group, which became the world's fourth-largest iron ore producer. He went from bankruptcy to becoming Australia's richest person. He built Fortescue into a major mining company challenging BHP and Rio Tinto's dominance in iron ore. He later launched Fortescue Future Industries, investing billions in green hydrogen. His Minderoo Foundation is one of Australia's largest philanthropic organizations.
