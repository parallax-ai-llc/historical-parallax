## Summary

Alvin Ailey was an American dancer and choreographer who founded the Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater. His masterwork Revelations, set to spirituals and blues, is the most widely seen modern dance work. He created a dance company that celebrated African American cultural heritage and brought modern dance to diverse, international audiences.
