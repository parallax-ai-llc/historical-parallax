## Summary

Timnit Gebru is a leading researcher in AI ethics and algorithmic bias. Her research on racial and gender bias in facial recognition systems revealed significant disparities in accuracy across demographics. Her firing from Google in 2020 over a paper on large language model risks sparked a global conversation about ethics in AI research.
