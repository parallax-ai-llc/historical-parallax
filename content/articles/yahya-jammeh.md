## Summary
Yahya Abdul-Aziz Jemus Junkung Jammeh was the President of the Gambia from 1994 to 2017. He seized power in a military coup and ruled as a dictator. He was accused of torture, forced disappearances, and stealing $1 billion from state coffers. He went into exile in Equatorial Guinea in 2017.
