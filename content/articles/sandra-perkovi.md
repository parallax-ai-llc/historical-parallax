## Profile

### Discipline and style

- Competes in women's discus throw, a power-and-technique event in track and field.
- Known for sustained peak performance across multiple Olympic cycles and the Diamond League era.

### Major achievements (high level)

- Two-time Olympic champion (2012, 2016) in women's discus.
- Multiple-time European champion and Diamond League season winner (across the 2010s).
- Regularly ranked among the top discus throwers globally during her prime competitive years.

### Notable marks

- Personal best: 71.41 m (Croatian national record; reported).
- Achieved her best distances during the mid-to-late 2010s when women's discus saw a deep field of elite throwers.
