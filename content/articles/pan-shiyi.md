## Summary

Pan Shiyi is a Chinese real estate developer who co-founded SOHO China with wife Zhang Xin. Known for his social media presence and philanthropic efforts, he developed some of Beijing's most architecturally distinctive commercial buildings. He donated to Harvard and Yale for scholarships for Chinese students. The couple's shift to selling rather than leasing properties in the early 2020s drew government scrutiny.
