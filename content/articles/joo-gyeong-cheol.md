## Summary

Joo Gyeong-cheol is a Korean nuclear fusion scientist who has contributed to plasma physics research at the Korea Institute of Fusion Energy. His work on plasma confinement and heating systems for the KSTAR tokamak has been important for advancing Korea's fusion energy research capabilities.
