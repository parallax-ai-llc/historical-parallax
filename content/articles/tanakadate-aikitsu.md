## Summary

Tanakadate Aikitsu was a Japanese physicist who pioneered geomagnetic surveys and seismological research in Japan. He conducted the first comprehensive magnetic survey of Japan and advocated for the romanization of Japanese. He was a key figure in establishing modern physics research in Japan and mentored many Japanese physicists.
