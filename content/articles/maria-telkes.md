## Summary

Maria Telkes was a pioneer of solar energy, designing the first solar-heated house (the Dover Sun House) in 1948 and developing solar-powered water desalination systems for the U.S. military. She invented a solar-powered distiller that provided drinking water on life rafts during World War II. She held over 20 patents in solar energy technology.
