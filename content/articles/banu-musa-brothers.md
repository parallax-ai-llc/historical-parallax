## Summary

The Banu Musa brothers — Muhammad, Ahmad, and Hasan — were three 9th-century scholars at the Baghdad House of Wisdom who wrote the Book of Ingenious Devices, describing over 100 mechanical devices including automata, trick vessels, and early programmable machines. They also advanced geometry and astronomy and funded translations of Greek scientific texts.
