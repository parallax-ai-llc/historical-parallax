## Summary

Ashish Thakkar is the founder of the Mara Group, one of Africa's largest diversified conglomerates. He was named Africa's youngest billionaire and founded the Mara Foundation to support African entrepreneurs. Mara Group operates in technology, banking, real estate, and agriculture across 25 African countries. He also launched Mara Phones, Africa's first smartphone manufacturing plant. He represents a new generation of pan-African entrepreneurs.
