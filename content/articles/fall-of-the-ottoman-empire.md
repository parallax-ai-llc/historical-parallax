## Summary

The Ottoman Empire collapsed after WWI defeat. The Armistice of Mudros (1918) and subsequent occupation led to Mustafa Kemal's nationalist resistance. The sultanate was abolished November 1, 1922, ending 623 years of Ottoman rule.
