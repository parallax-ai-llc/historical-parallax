## Summary

Ed White became the first American to walk in space on June 3, 1965, during the Gemini 4 mission. He spent 23 minutes outside the spacecraft and was so exhilarated he had to be ordered back inside. He was tragically killed in the Apollo 1 fire alongside Gus Grissom and Roger Chaffee during a launch pad test.
