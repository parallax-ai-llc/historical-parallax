## Summary

Camille Saint-Saens was a French composer, pianist, and organist of prodigious talent and enormous output. His Symphony No. 3 'Organ Symphony,' the opera Samson and Delilah, and The Carnival of the Animals showcase his masterful craftsmanship and orchestral brilliance. He was a child prodigy who remained active for over seven decades.
