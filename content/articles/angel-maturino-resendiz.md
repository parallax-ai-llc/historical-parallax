## Summary
Ángel Maturino Reséndiz, the 'Railroad Killer,' was a Mexican serial killer who murdered at least 15 people across the United States between 1986 and 1999. He traveled by freight train and attacked people living near railroad tracks. He was executed by lethal injection in Texas in 2006.
