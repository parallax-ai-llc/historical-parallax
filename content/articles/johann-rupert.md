## Summary

Johann Rupert is the chairman of Compagnie Financière Richemont, the Swiss luxury goods group controlling Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels, Montblanc, and IWC. He inherited his father Anton Rupert's luxury interests and built Richemont into the world's second-largest luxury company by market cap. He also controls Remgro, a diversified South African investment company. He is one of the wealthiest people in Africa and a major figure in global luxury.
