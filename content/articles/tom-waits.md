## Summary

Tom Waits is an American singer-songwriter and actor known for his distinctive gravelly voice and eclectic musical style spanning jazz, blues, experimental, and industrial. His albums Rain Dogs, Swordfishtrombones, and Mule Variations are masterworks of American music.
