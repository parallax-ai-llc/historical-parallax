## Summary

Alexander Campbell was an Irish-American religious leader who co-founded the Restoration Movement, which sought to restore Christianity to its New Testament roots. He founded Bethany College in West Virginia and debated prominent skeptics and denominational leaders. His movement gave rise to the Churches of Christ, Christian Churches, and Disciples of Christ. He advocated for baptism by immersion and the unity of all Christians.
