## Summary

Kazuo Shiraga was a noted Japanese ophthalmologist who made significant contributions to the understanding and treatment of retinal diseases. He helped develop laser photocoagulation techniques for diabetic retinopathy. His clinical research improved the standard of care for eye diseases in Japan.
