## Summary

Charles Fefferman is an American mathematician who received the Fields Medal in 1978 for his contributions to mathematical analysis, particularly concerning the disc conjecture in Fourier analysis. He became a full professor at the University of Chicago at age 22, the youngest person in the United States to hold such a position.
