## Summary

Nigerien Tuareg guitarist who has been called the Jimi Hendrix of the Sahara. His electrifying guitar playing fuses Tuareg musical traditions with psychedelic rock. His album Afrique Victime was one of the most acclaimed rock albums of 2021.
