## Summary

Jia Yueting founded LeEco (LeTV), a Chinese tech conglomerate that attempted to become 'China's Apple + Netflix + Tesla.' He built LeTV into China's largest online streaming platform and launched a smartphone, smart TV, and electric car division. His ambitious over-expansion led to a dramatic collapse, and he fled to the U.S. in 2017 amid billions in debt. He founded Faraday Future EV company, which continued to struggle.
