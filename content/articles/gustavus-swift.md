## Summary

Gustavus Swift founded Swift & Company and revolutionized the meatpacking industry by developing the refrigerated railroad car for shipping dressed beef. His innovation eliminated the need to ship live cattle, dramatically reducing costs. He built an integrated meat processing empire that stretched from stockyards to retail. Swift was a key figure in making Chicago the meatpacking capital of America.
