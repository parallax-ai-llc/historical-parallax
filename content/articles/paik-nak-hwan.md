## Summary

Paik Nak-hwan was a Korean agricultural engineer who developed improved irrigation systems for Korean rice paddies. His engineering designs helped modernize Korean agriculture during the rapid development period of the 1960s-1970s, increasing rice yields and contributing to food self-sufficiency in South Korea.
