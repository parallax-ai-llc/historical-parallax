## Summary

The 1973 Oil Crisis began when OPEC proclaimed an oil embargo against nations supporting Israel during the Yom Kippur War. Oil prices quadrupled, causing global recession and long gas lines. It demonstrated the West's dependence on Middle Eastern oil.
