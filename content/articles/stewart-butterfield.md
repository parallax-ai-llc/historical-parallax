## Summary

Stewart Butterfield co-founded Flickr, the pioneering photo-sharing platform, and later created Slack, the workplace messaging tool that transformed business communications. Flickr was sold to Yahoo in 2005. Slack emerged from a failed video game project and became one of the fastest-growing business applications in history. Salesforce acquired Slack for $27.7 billion in 2021.
