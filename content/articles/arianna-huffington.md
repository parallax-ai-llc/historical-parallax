## Summary

Arianna Huffington co-founded The Huffington Post in 2005, which became one of the most widely read news websites in the world. AOL acquired The Huffington Post for $315 million in 2011. She later founded Thrive Global, a behavior change technology company focused on well-being. She has authored 15 books and is a prominent advocate for sleep, wellness, and work-life balance.
