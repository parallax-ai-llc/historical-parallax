## Summary

Salim Abdool Karim is a South African clinical infectious disease epidemiologist who has led groundbreaking HIV prevention and treatment research. He co-founded CAPRISA and served as chair of South Africa's COVID-19 advisory committee. His research on HIV broadly neutralizing antibodies and prevention has shaped global AIDS policy.
