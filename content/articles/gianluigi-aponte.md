## Summary

Gianluigi Aponte founded Mediterranean Shipping Company (MSC), the world's largest container shipping line and second-largest cruise operator. Starting with a single ship in 1970, he built MSC into a global shipping empire overtaking Maersk as the world's largest container carrier. MSC Cruises became a leading European cruise line. The Aponte family is one of the wealthiest in the world through their privately held shipping empire.
