## Background

Allen Weisselberg is an American business executive best known for his long tenure at the Trump Organization, where he served as chief financial officer (CFO). Public reporting describes him as a key financial lieutenant within the company over multiple decades.

## Criminal Cases (High-Level)

The primary criminal matters associated with Weisselberg involve allegations and admissions related to compensation practices and tax reporting.

Key points commonly summarized in public accounts:

- Prosecutors alleged that certain benefits and perks were provided as part of compensation and were not properly reported for tax purposes.
- Weisselberg entered guilty pleas in connection with a set of felony counts related to these practices.
- He received a custodial sentence and served time in jail.

## Perjury Matter

Separate reporting has described a later perjury conviction, tied to statements made in legal proceedings. This has been framed as a distinct case from the earlier tax-related prosecution.

## Significance
