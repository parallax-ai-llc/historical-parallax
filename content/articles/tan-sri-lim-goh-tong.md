## Summary

Tan Sri Lim Goh Tong was the founder of Genting Group, which operates the world's largest integrated resort, Resorts World Genting, in the mountains of Malaysia. He built a road through the jungle to the peak of a mountain to create a hill station resort and casino. Genting expanded globally to Singapore, the UK, the US, and Bahamas. He was one of Malaysia's most successful self-made billionaires.
