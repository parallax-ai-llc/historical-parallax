## Summary

Martyn Lloyd-Jones was a Welsh Protestant minister and preacher who served as minister of Westminster Chapel in London for nearly 30 years. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest preachers of the 20th century. His extensive sermon series on Romans and Ephesians were published as multi-volume commentaries. He championed expository preaching and Reformed theology within evangelical Christianity.
