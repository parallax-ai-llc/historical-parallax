## Summary

Craig Venter is an American biotechnologist and businessman known for leading the private-sector effort to sequence the human genome, competing with the public Human Genome Project. He founded Celera Genomics and used the whole genome shotgun sequencing method. In 2010, his team created the first synthetic bacterial cell, a milestone in synthetic biology. He is one of the most influential and controversial figures in modern biology.
