## Summary

Henry Sy Sr. was the Philippines' richest person and founder of SM Group, the largest conglomerate in the Philippines. He built SM Supermalls, the largest shopping mall operator in Southeast Asia, and SM Prime Holdings. He also controlled BDO Unibank, the Philippines' largest bank. Starting from a shoe store, he built an empire spanning retail, real estate, banking, and tourism.
