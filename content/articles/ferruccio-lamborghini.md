## Summary

Ferruccio Lamborghini founded Automobili Lamborghini to compete with Ferrari, creating one of the world's most iconic supercar brands. He originally made his fortune manufacturing tractors. According to legend, he started making sports cars after a dispute with Enzo Ferrari about the clutch in his Ferrari. The Miura and Countach became defining supercars that established Lamborghini's reputation for dramatic, extreme automotive design.
