## Summary

Arnon Milchan is an Israeli billionaire film producer and businessman who has produced over 130 films including Pretty Woman, Fight Club, 12 Years a Slave, and The Revenant. Through New Regency Productions, he became one of Hollywood's most prolific producers. He was also revealed to have been an Israeli intelligence operative who helped procure nuclear technology. His dual life as spy and movie mogul is one of Hollywood's most extraordinary stories.
