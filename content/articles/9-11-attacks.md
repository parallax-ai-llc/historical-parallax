## Summary

The September 11, 2001, terrorist attacks by al-Qaeda killed 2,977 people when hijacked planes struck the World Trade Center, Pentagon, and crashed in Pennsylvania. The attacks led to the War on Terror, invasion of Afghanistan, and fundamentally changed global security.
