## Summary

David Lynch was an American filmmaker and artist whose surreal, disturbing works created a unique aesthetic known as 'Lynchian.' Eraserhead, Blue Velvet, Mulholland Drive, and the TV series Twin Peaks explore the darkness beneath American suburban life. Mulholland Drive was voted the greatest film of the 21st century in a BBC poll.
