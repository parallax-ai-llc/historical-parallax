## Summary
Patrick Joseph Magee is a former member of the Provisional IRA who planted the bomb at the Grand Hotel in Brighton during the 1984 Conservative Party conference, attempting to assassinate Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher. Five people were killed. He was sentenced to life but released under the Good Friday Agreement.
