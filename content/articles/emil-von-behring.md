## Summary

Emil von Behring was a German physician who won the first Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine in 1901 for his work on diphtheria antitoxin serum therapy. He developed antitoxin treatments for diphtheria and tetanus that saved countless lives, especially children. He is considered the father of serum therapy.
