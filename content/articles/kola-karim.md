## Summary

Kola Karim is the CEO of Shoreline Group, one of Nigeria's largest indigenous energy companies. He built Shoreline from a small startup into a diversified energy, natural resources, and logistics company operating across West Africa. He is a member of the Harvard Business School African Advisory Board and represents the new generation of African energy entrepreneurs building companies that can compete globally.
