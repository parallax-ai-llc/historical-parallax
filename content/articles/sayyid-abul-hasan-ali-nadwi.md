## Summary

Sayyid Abul Hasan Ali Nadwi (known as Ali Miyan) was an Indian Islamic scholar, author, and rector of the Nadwatul Ulama seminary in Lucknow. His book Maza Khasira al-Alam bi-Inhitat al-Muslimin (What the World Lost by the Decline of Muslims) was a landmark work in modern Islamic thought. He was a member of many Islamic institutions worldwide and promoted interfaith dialogue. He was one of the most respected Indian Muslim scholars of the 20th century.
