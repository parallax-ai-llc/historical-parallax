## Summary

Eduardo Chillida was a Basque sculptor known for his monumental steel and iron sculptures exploring space, form, and Basque identity. His Comb of the Wind installation on the San Sebastian coast is one of the most celebrated site-specific works. His work combines industrial materials with organic, hand-forged character.
