## About Rupert Murdoch (Canonical Subject)

Rupert Murdoch (born 1931) is an Australian-born media executive and entrepreneur who built
and controlled a global portfolio of news and entertainment businesses over multiple decades.
He is widely associated with:

- **News Corporation / News Corp** (various incarnations over time)
- **21st Century Fox** (before major asset sales)
- Major newspaper brands in the UK, US, and Australia, and the development of large-scale
  cable and broadcast television holdings

## High-Level Impact
