## Background

Amimour Samy was a French national associated with the Islamic State (ISIS). Public reporting identifies him as one of the attackers involved in the Bataclan theatre assault during the coordinated November 2015 attacks in Paris.

## Role in the November 2015 Paris attacks

- **Target**: Bataclan concert hall, Paris.
- **Attack type**: Armed assault followed by suicide vest detonation.
- **Outcome**: Dozens of hostages and concertgoers were killed; Samy died when he detonated his explosive vest during the police assault.

## Context and aftermath

The Bataclan attack formed part of a wider set of coordinated attacks across Paris on 13 November 2015. Investigations and prosecutions in France subsequently focused on the broader network that planned and supported the attackers.
