## Summary

Lee Jae-hyeon is the chairman of CJ Group, South Korea's leading entertainment, food, and media conglomerate. CJ was originally part of Samsung Group (founded by his grandfather Lee Byung-chul) before separating in 1993. He built CJ into a powerhouse spanning K-pop (Mnet), film (CJ ENM produced Parasite), food (CJ CheilJedang), and logistics (CJ Logistics). CJ played a central role in globalizing Korean culture.
