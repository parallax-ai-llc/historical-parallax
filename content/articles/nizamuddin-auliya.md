## Summary

Nizamuddin Auliya was one of the most famous Sufi saints of the Indian subcontinent and the fourth spiritual successor of the Chishti Order. His dargah (shrine) in Delhi is one of the most visited Sufi shrines in the world. He emphasized love, compassion, and service to humanity regardless of religion or caste. His influence on Indian Islamic culture, particularly through his disciple Amir Khusro's music and poetry, was immense.
