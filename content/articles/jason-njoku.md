## Summary

Jason Njoku is the co-founder and CEO of iROKOtv, often called the 'Netflix of Africa.' He built the largest online distribution platform for Nollywood (Nigerian) movies. IROKOtv has over 6,000 Nollywood titles and serves millions of users across Africa and the diaspora. He was one of the first to digitize and monetize Africa's enormous film industry. His company demonstrated the viability of subscription video services in African markets.
