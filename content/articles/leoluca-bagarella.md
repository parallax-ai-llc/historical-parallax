## Summary
Leoluca Bagarella was a Sicilian Mafia boss and brother-in-law of Salvatore Riina. He took over as head of the Corleonesi after Riina's arrest. He continued the strategy of violence against the Italian state. He was captured in 1995 and sentenced to multiple life terms.
