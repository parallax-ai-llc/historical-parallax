## Event Overview

The Parkland shooting refers to the February 14, 2018 attack at **Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School** in Parkland, Florida, in which **17 people were killed**. The incident became one of the most prominent mass shootings in modern U.S. history and received extensive national media coverage.[^1]

## Public Response and Activism

In the aftermath, student survivors and local organizers helped catalyze a broader political mobilization around gun violence prevention. This included public demonstrations, voter-registration drives, and national advocacy efforts that brought youth-led activism to the center of the U.S. gun policy debate.[^1]
