## Summary

Zhang Xiaogang is a Chinese contemporary painter best known for his Bloodline: Big Family series of portraits resembling Chinese family photographs from the Cultural Revolution era. His haunting, melancholic paintings explore collective memory, identity, and the psychological impact of China's political history.
