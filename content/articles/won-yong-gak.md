## Summary

Won Yong-gak founded Booyoung Group, one of South Korea's largest construction and housing companies. He built hundreds of apartment complexes that housed millions of South Koreans during the country's rapid urbanization. His company was instrumental in creating the distinctive apartment culture that defines South Korean cities. He was one of the first-generation chaebol founders who transformed South Korea's housing landscape.
