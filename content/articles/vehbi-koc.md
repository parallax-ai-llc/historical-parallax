## Summary

Vehbi Koç founded Koç Holding in 1926, building it into Turkey's largest industrial and financial conglomerate. Starting as a small grocery shop in Ankara, he expanded into construction, automotive, finance, and consumer goods. He partnered with major international companies including Ford, Fiat, and Siemens. He was Turkey's first billionaire and his business empire became the backbone of the Turkish private sector.
