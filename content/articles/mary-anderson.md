## Summary

Mary Anderson invented the windshield wiper in 1903 after observing a trolley car driver struggling to see through rain and sleet. She received a patent in 1903, but no manufacturer would buy it at the time. After her patent expired, windshield wipers became standard on all automobiles. Her invention has saved countless lives.
