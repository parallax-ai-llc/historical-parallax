## Summary

Taiichi Ohno was the father of the Toyota Production System (TPS), which revolutionized manufacturing worldwide and became the basis for lean manufacturing. He developed just-in-time production, kanban systems, and continuous improvement (kaizen) methodologies. His innovations eliminated waste and increased efficiency, making Toyota one of the world's most efficient manufacturers. His ideas influenced manufacturing in virtually every industry globally.
