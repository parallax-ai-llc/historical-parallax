## Summary

Ginni Rometty served as CEO of IBM from 2012 to 2020, the first woman to lead the company. She oversaw IBM's pivot toward cloud computing and artificial intelligence, including the development of Watson AI. She also led the $34 billion acquisition of Red Hat, IBM's largest deal. Despite revenue declines during her tenure, she repositioned IBM as a hybrid cloud and AI company.
