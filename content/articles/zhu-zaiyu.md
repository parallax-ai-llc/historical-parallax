## Summary

Zhu Zaiyu was a Ming Dynasty prince and polymath who was the first person to mathematically calculate equal temperament tuning for music in 1584, predating Simon Stevin's work. He used precise mathematical calculations involving the twelfth root of two. This discovery was fundamental to modern Western music and the standardization of musical tuning worldwide.
