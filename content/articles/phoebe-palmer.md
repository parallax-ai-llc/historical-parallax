## Summary

Phoebe Palmer was an American Methodist evangelist and writer who was a leading figure in the Holiness movement. She developed the 'altar theology' of entire sanctification, teaching that believers could experience instant holiness through faith. Her Tuesday Meetings for the Promotion of Holiness influenced thousands. She was one of the most influential women in 19th-century American Christianity.
