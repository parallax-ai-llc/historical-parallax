## Summary

John Sperling founded the University of Phoenix in 1976, creating the first large-scale for-profit university in the United States. He pioneered online education and adult learning programs. The University of Phoenix grew to serve over 400,000 students at its peak. Though controversial, his model demonstrated the demand for flexible, career-focused higher education and anticipated the massive growth of online learning.
