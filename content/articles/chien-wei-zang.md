## Summary

Chien Wei-zang (Qian Weichang) was a leading Chinese physicist and applied mathematician who made significant contributions to elasticity theory and shell mechanics. He developed the perturbation method for solving nonlinear problems in mechanics. He was one of the co-founders of modern mechanics in China alongside Qian Xuesen and Qian Sanqiang.
