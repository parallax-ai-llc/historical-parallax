## Summary

The Fall of the Berlin Wall on November 9, 1989, was a pivotal event in the Cold War's end. East German authorities opened the border checkpoints after weeks of peaceful protests, and jubilant crowds tore down the wall. It symbolized the end of communist rule in Europe.
