## Summary

Yoon Sang-won is a Korean environmental engineer who has developed advanced wastewater treatment technologies. His research on biological nutrient removal and membrane bioreactor systems has been adopted by Korean water treatment plants, contributing to the dramatic improvement in Korean water quality since the 1990s.
