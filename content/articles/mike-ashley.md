## Summary

Mike Ashley founded Sports Direct (now Frasers Group), building it into the UK's largest sports goods retailer. He also owned Newcastle United F.C. from 2007 to 2021. Known for his controversial management style and working conditions in his warehouses, he expanded Frasers Group through acquisitions including House of Fraser, Evans Cycles, and Jack Wills. He sold Newcastle United to a Saudi-led consortium for £305 million in 2021.
