## Summary

Paul Graham co-founded Y Combinator in 2005, creating the most influential startup accelerator in Silicon Valley. YC has funded over 4,000 startups including Airbnb, Dropbox, Stripe, DoorDash, and Coinbase. He previously co-founded Viaweb, one of the first web-based applications, which was sold to Yahoo for $49 million. His essays on startups and programming have influenced a generation of entrepreneurs.
