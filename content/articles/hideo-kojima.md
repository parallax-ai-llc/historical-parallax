## Summary

Hideo Kojima is a Japanese video game designer who created the Metal Gear series and founded Kojima Productions. His cinematic approach to game design and complex narratives earned him the nickname 'the Spielberg of video games.' After a high-profile split with Konami in 2015, he founded Kojima Productions independently and created Death Stranding. He is one of the most influential and celebrated game creators in history.
