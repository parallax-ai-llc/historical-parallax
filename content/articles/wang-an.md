## Summary

Wang An was a Chinese-American computer engineer who invented magnetic core memory, which became the standard computer memory technology for 20 years. He founded Wang Laboratories, which pioneered the word processor and minicomputer industries. At its peak, Wang Labs employed over 30,000 people and was one of the largest computer companies in the world.
