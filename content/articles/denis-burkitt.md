## Summary

Denis Burkitt identified the lymphoma that bears his name (Burkitt's lymphoma) in Ugandan children and proposed its viral and environmental causes. His epidemiological work linking the cancer to Epstein-Barr virus was groundbreaking. He later championed dietary fiber's role in preventing Western diseases, influencing nutrition science globally.
