## Summary

Zao Wou-Ki was a Chinese-French painter who created a unique synthesis of Eastern and Western abstract painting. His luminous, atmospheric canvases combine the spirit of Chinese landscape painting with the energy of Western Abstract Expressionism. He is considered one of the greatest abstract painters of the 20th century.
