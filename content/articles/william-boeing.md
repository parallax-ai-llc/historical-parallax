## Summary

William Boeing founded the Boeing Company, which became the world's largest aerospace company. A timber magnate turned aviation pioneer, he built his first airplane in 1916. He also founded United Air Lines and pioneered airmail delivery. His company would go on to produce iconic aircraft including the B-17, 707, 747, and more, becoming central to both military and commercial aviation.
