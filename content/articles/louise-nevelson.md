## Summary

Louise Nevelson was an American sculptor known for her monumental, wall-like assemblages of found wooden objects painted in uniform black, white, or gold. Her Shadow and Reflection series and environmental installations pioneered installation art. She is considered one of the most important sculptors of the 20th century.
