## Summary
Sirhan Bishara Sirhan is a Palestinian-Jordanian convicted of assassinating Senator Robert F. Kennedy on June 5, 1968, in Los Angeles. He shot Kennedy at the Ambassador Hotel after Kennedy won the California primary. He was sentenced to death, commuted to life. He has been denied parole multiple times.
