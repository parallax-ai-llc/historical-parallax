## Summary

Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 disappeared with 239 people aboard, becoming aviation's greatest mystery. Evidence suggests deliberate diversion; only fragments of the plane have been found.

## What Happened (High-Level)

- **Route**: Scheduled passenger flight from Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia) to Beijing (China).
- **Disappearance**: After routine communications, the aircraft stopped transmitting and deviated from its filed route.
- **Onboard**: 239 people (passengers and crew).

## Investigation & Search (Publicly Reported)

- **Multi-country effort**: The disappearance triggered major search and investigative work involving multiple governments and agencies.
- **Search areas**: Publicly released analysis focused on potential flight paths based on available radar and satellite-derived data.
- **Challenges**: The absence of a confirmed crash site and limited data made conclusions difficult and periodically shifted search priorities.

## Debris Recovery

- **Confirmed fragments**: A small number of parts consistent with the aircraft type were recovered years later, including pieces found on shores in the western Indian Ocean region.
- **Implication**: Debris findings supported the conclusion that the aircraft was lost, but did not by themselves resolve the sequence of events leading to the loss.

## Leading Explanations (Without Speculation)

- **Unresolved cause**: No definitive public finding has established exactly why the aircraft deviated and was lost.
- **Deliberate diversion (possibility)**: Some publicly discussed scenarios consider intentional course changes; this has not been conclusively proven in open sources.
- **Accident/technical failure (possibility)**: Other scenarios consider mechanical, electrical, or onboard emergencies; these too remain unconfirmed publicly.

## Why MH370 Matters

- **Aviation safety**: The case influenced broader discussion about aircraft tracking, communications, and search-and-rescue readiness over oceanic routes.
- **Families & public attention**: The absence of closure for families and the scale of the search contributed to sustained global attention.
- **Modern mystery**: Despite extensive investigation, MH370 remains one of the most prominent unresolved losses in commercial aviation.
