## Summary

Billy Sunday was an American evangelist and former professional baseball player who became the most celebrated revivalist of the early 20th century. His dramatic preaching style and showmanship drew millions to his revivals across America. He was a prominent supporter of Prohibition and conservative morality. He is estimated to have preached to over 100 million people during his career.
