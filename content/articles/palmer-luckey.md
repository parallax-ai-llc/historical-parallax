## Summary

Palmer Luckey founded Oculus VR, reviving commercial virtual reality and launching the modern VR industry. He designed the Oculus Rift headset as a teenager, sparking massive interest through a Kickstarter campaign. Facebook acquired Oculus for $2 billion in 2014. After leaving Facebook, he founded Anduril Industries, a defense technology company that has become one of the most important U.S. defense startups.
