## Summary

Tara Brach is an American psychologist, meditation teacher, and author who is one of the leading voices integrating Western psychology with Buddhist mindfulness practice. She founded the Insight Meditation Community of Washington, D.C. Her books Radical Acceptance and Radical Compassion have helped millions apply Buddhist principles to emotional healing. Her weekly podcast is one of the most popular spiritual podcasts in the world with over 3 million downloads per month.
