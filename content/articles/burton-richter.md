## Summary

Burton Richter was an American physicist who shared the 1976 Nobel Prize in Physics with Samuel Ting for the discovery of the J/psi meson. He discovered the particle independently at SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory. He later served as director of SLAC and advocated for sustainable energy solutions.
