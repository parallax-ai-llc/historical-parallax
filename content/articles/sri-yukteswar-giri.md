## Summary

Sri Yukteswar Giri was an Indian yogi, astrologer, and educator who was the guru of Paramahansa Yogananda. He authored The Holy Science, which sought to demonstrate the unity of Hindu and Christian scripture. He was a disciple of Lahiri Mahasaya and established ashrams in Serampore and Puri. He was known for his rigorous teaching style and his integration of Kriya Yoga with Vedantic philosophy and modern science.
