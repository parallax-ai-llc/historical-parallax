## Summary

Yu Gil-jun was a late Joseon Dynasty intellectual who was the first Korean to study in the West (attending Boston's Governor Dummer Academy). He wrote Seoyu Gyeonmun (Observations from the Western Journey), introducing Western science, technology, and political systems to Korean readers. His work was pivotal for Korean modernization.
