## Summary

Croatian Serb police and political leader convicted of war crimes by the ICTY for ordering the rocket attack on Zagreb in 1995 and persecution of Croatian civilians during the Croatian War.

## Additional Context

- Milan Martić was a political and security figure in the self-proclaimed Republic of Serbian Krajina during the Croatian War of Independence.
- He was prosecuted at the ICTY in connection with crimes committed during the conflicts of the 1990s.
