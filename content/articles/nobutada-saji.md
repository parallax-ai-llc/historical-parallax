## Summary

Nobutada Saji served as president and chairman of Suntory Holdings, one of Japan's oldest and largest beverage companies. Under his leadership, Suntory expanded from Japanese whisky and beer into a global spirits and beverages empire. The acquisition of Beam Inc. for $16 billion in 2014 made Suntory Beam the world's third-largest premium spirits company. The Saji family controls one of Japan's largest private fortunes.
