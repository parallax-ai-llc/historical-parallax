## Summary

Lee Ufan is a Korean artist and philosopher who co-founded the Mono-ha movement in Japan. His minimalist paintings of single brushstrokes on white canvas and stone sculptures explore making and not-making. The Lee Ufan Museum on Naoshima Island was designed by Tadao Ando.
