## Summary

David Rockefeller was a banker, philanthropist, and the patriarch of the Rockefeller family for decades. He served as CEO of Chase Manhattan Bank and was a leading figure in international finance and diplomacy. He co-founded the Trilateral Commission and was chairman of the Council on Foreign Relations. He donated over $900 million to philanthropic causes during his lifetime.
