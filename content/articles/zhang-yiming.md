## Summary

Zhang Yiming founded ByteDance in 2012, the company behind TikTok and its Chinese counterpart Douyin, creating the world's most valuable private startup. TikTok became the most downloaded app globally and transformed social media with its short-form video format and powerful recommendation algorithm. ByteDance grew to over 150,000 employees and a valuation exceeding $200 billion. He stepped down as CEO in 2021 to focus on long-term strategy.
