## Summary

Biz Stone is a co-founder of Twitter who helped define the platform's ethos and user experience during its formative years. He also co-founded Jelly Industries, a Q&A platform, and co-founded Medium with Evan Williams. He briefly returned to Twitter in 2017. His emphasis on Twitter's potential as a force for social good shaped the platform's early culture and mission.
