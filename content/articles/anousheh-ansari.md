## Summary

Anousheh Ansari became the first female private space explorer and the first Iranian in space when she flew to the International Space Station in 2006. She also co-sponsored the Ansari X Prize, which spurred the development of commercial spaceflight. Her company, Prodea Systems, focused on technology innovation and space access.
