## Summary

Irenaeus was an early Church Father and Bishop of Lyon, known for his role in combating Gnosticism. His major work 'Against Heresies' is a foundational text of Christian orthodoxy. He emphasized apostolic tradition and the authority of scripture. He is considered one of the most important early theologians in the development of Christian doctrine.
