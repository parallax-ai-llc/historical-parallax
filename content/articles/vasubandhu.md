## Summary

Vasubandhu was an Indian Buddhist monk and philosopher who made major contributions to both Sarvastivada Abhidharma and Yogacara philosophy. His Abhidharmakosa (Treasury of Abhidharma) is one of the most important philosophical texts in Buddhist history. After converting from Hinayana to Mahayana through his brother Asanga's influence, he wrote foundational Yogacara works including the Vimsatika and Trimsika.
