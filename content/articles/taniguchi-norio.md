## Summary

Taniguchi Norio was a Japanese engineer at Tokyo Science University who coined the term 'nanotechnology' in 1974, defining it as the processing of materials at the nanometer scale. His paper at the International Conference on Production Engineering introduced the concept that became one of the most important technological fields of the 21st century.
