## Summary

John Preskill is an American theoretical physicist who coined the term 'quantum supremacy' and has been a leading figure in quantum information science. He directs Caltech's Institute for Quantum Information and Matter. His work on quantum error correction and quantum computing theory has shaped the field's development.
