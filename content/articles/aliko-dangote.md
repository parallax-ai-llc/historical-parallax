## Summary

Aliko Dangote is Africa's richest person and the founder of Dangote Group, a multi-billion-dollar conglomerate. His Dangote Cement is Africa's largest cement producer, and his newly completed Dangote Refinery is the world's largest single-train petroleum refinery. Starting with commodity trading in the 1970s, he built an industrial empire spanning cement, sugar, flour, and oil refining across Africa. He has transformed Africa's industrial landscape.
