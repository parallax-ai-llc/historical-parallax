## Summary

Raghunath Murmu was an Indian Santal linguist and cultural leader who created the Ol Chiki script for the Santali language in 1925. While primarily known as a linguistic innovator, he was also a significant spiritual figure who drew on Santal traditional religion (Sarna) to create a cultural revival movement. He composed the Darege Dhan, a spiritual text based on Santal cosmology. He is revered as a cultural hero by the Santal people.
