## Summary

Fadi Ghandour is the founder of Aramex, the Middle East's first global logistics and transportation company. Aramex went public on NASDAQ in 1997, the first Arab-based company to do so. He later founded Wamda Capital, a venture fund investing in Middle Eastern startups. He is one of the most respected entrepreneurs in the Arab world and a champion of the MENA startup ecosystem.
