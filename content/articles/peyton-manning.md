## Background

Peyton Manning is an American former professional football quarterback known for his career in the NFL with the Indianapolis Colts and Denver Broncos. He is widely associated with high-level quarterback play, pre-snap adjustments, and passing efficiency in the modern era.

## Career Overview

Manning was a long-time starter, winning multiple league MVP awards and leading teams to Super Bowl championships. After significant neck surgery, he returned to play at a high level with Denver, setting major single-season passing records during the 2013 season in commonly cited summaries.

## Post-Playing Career

After retirement, he became a prominent media figure and analyst/host, with ongoing involvement in football-related programming and philanthropy.
