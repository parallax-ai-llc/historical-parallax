## Summary

Abbas Bahri was a Tunisian mathematician who made fundamental contributions to the calculus of variations, partial differential equations, and geometric analysis. He proved important existence results for nonlinear elliptic equations and the Yamabe problem on manifolds. He was one of the most distinguished mathematicians from the Arab world.
