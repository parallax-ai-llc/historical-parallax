## Summary

Spanish serial killer known as the 'Werewolf of Allariz' who murdered at least 13 people in Galicia. He sold soap and body fat from his victims at markets. He claimed to be a werewolf. His death sentence was commuted; he died in prison.

## 개요

Manuel Blanco Romasanta(마누엘 블랑코 로마산타)는 19세기 스페인 연쇄 살인 사건과 관련해 알려진 인물로, ‘늑대인간’을 자처했다는 서술로 대중 문화에서도 자주 언급됩니다.
