## Summary

Ajahn Sumedho is an American-born Theravada Buddhist monk who is the most senior Western disciple of Ajahn Chah. He founded the Amaravati Buddhist Monastery in England and was the abbot of the Chithurst Buddhist Monastery. He has been instrumental in establishing the Thai Forest Tradition in the West. His teachings emphasize awareness of 'the sound of silence' and the direct investigation of the mind.
