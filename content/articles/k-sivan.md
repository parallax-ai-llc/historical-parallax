## Summary

K. Sivan is an Indian aerospace engineer who served as chairman of ISRO and led the development of the cryogenic rocket engine for India's GSLV Mk III. Known as the 'Rocket Man,' he developed the vikas engine used in ISRO's launch vehicles. Under his leadership, ISRO launched Chandrayaan-2 and the record 104-satellite PSLV mission.
