## Summary

Min Chueh Chang co-developed the oral contraceptive pill by demonstrating that progesterone could inhibit ovulation in rabbits. His work with Gregory Pincus and John Rock led to the development of Enovid, the first birth control pill, approved in 1960. He also achieved the first in vitro fertilization (IVF) in mammals. His contributions transformed reproductive medicine.
