## Summary

Meyer Guggenheim was a Swiss-born American businessman who founded one of the most powerful mining and smelting dynasties in world history. He immigrated to Philadelphia and initially made money in lace and embroidery importing. His investment in Colorado silver mines led to the creation of a global mining empire. His seven sons expanded the family business into a dominant force in metals and mining.
