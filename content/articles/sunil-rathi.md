## Baghpat Jail Incident (2018)

In July 2018, gangster **Munna Bajrangi** (Prem Prakash Singh) was shot and killed inside **Baghpat District Jail** in Uttar Pradesh. The death drew substantial attention due to:

- the high-profile status of Bajrangi in organized-crime coverage,
- the fact the killing occurred inside a secure custodial facility, and
- subsequent questions around prison security, weapons access, and oversight.

Sunil Rathi’s name is commonly mentioned in connection with this incident as an **alleged** perpetrator, with accounts varying by source and by the stage of investigation.
