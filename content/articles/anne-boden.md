## Summary

Anne Boden founded Starling Bank in 2014, the UK's first mobile-only challenger bank. She built Starling into a profitable digital bank with over 3 million accounts. Unlike many fintech startups, Starling achieved profitability relatively quickly. She was named one of the most influential people in fintech and received an MBE for services to financial technology. Her autobiography 'Banking On It' documented her founding journey.
