## Summary

Francis of Assisi founded the Franciscan Order and is one of the most beloved saints in Christian history. He renounced his wealthy upbringing to live in radical poverty and service to the poor. He received the stigmata in 1224 and composed the Canticle of the Sun, considered the first great Italian poem. He is the patron saint of animals and the environment.
