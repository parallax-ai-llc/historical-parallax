## Summary

Toru Iwatani is the creator of Pac-Man, one of the most iconic and commercially successful video games in history. He designed Pac-Man while working at Namco in 1980, inspired by the shape of a pizza with a slice removed. Pac-Man became a cultural phenomenon and has generated over $14 billion in revenue, making it one of the highest-grossing entertainment products ever. His creation transcended gaming to become a symbol of 1980s popular culture.
