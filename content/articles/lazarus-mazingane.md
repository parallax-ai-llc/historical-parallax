## Summary
Lazarus Mazingane, the 'Lover's Lane Killer,' was a South African serial killer convicted of 11 murders and 12 rapes in Mpumalanga between 2006 and 2007. He attacked couples parked in secluded areas. He was sentenced to 11 life terms.
