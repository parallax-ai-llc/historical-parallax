## Summary

Chae Kun-sik was a Korean food scientist who scientifically studied Korean traditional fermented foods including doenjang, gochujang, and jeotgal. His research on fermentation microbiology helped standardize Korean fermented food production while preserving traditional flavors and health benefits.
