## Summary

Daniel Barenboim is an Argentine-Israeli pianist and conductor who has served as music director of the Berlin State Opera and the Chicago Symphony Orchestra. With Edward Said, he co-founded the West-Eastern Divan Orchestra bringing together young Israeli and Arab musicians. His advocacy for dialogue through music has made him a symbol of cultural diplomacy.
