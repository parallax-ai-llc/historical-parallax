## Summary

Lin Dan, 'Super Dan,' won two consecutive Olympic gold medals in badminton singles (2008, 2012) and five World Championships. He is considered the greatest badminton player ever.

## Background

Lin Dan is a Chinese badminton player (retired) often cited as one of the most accomplished men’s singles players in the sport’s modern era.

- **Olympics and World Championships:** His Olympic titles and multiple world titles made him a defining figure of his generation.
- **Rivalries:** Discussions of Lin Dan’s career frequently highlight long-running rivalries—especially in high-stakes tournament finals—as central to badminton’s global popularity.

## Legacy

Lin Dan’s career is commonly credited with elevating the profile of men’s singles badminton and inspiring younger players in China and internationally.
