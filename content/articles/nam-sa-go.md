## Summary

Nam Sa-go was a Joseon Dynasty scholar known for his geomantic (pungsu) predictions and his systematic approach to studying geographical features for site selection. While geomancy is not modern science, his careful observation and classification of landforms represented an early systematic approach to Korean geographical analysis.
