## Summary

Reinhold Würth built the Würth Group from a small screw wholesale business into the world's largest distributor of assembly and fastening materials. He took over the two-employee family business at age 19 after his father's death and grew it to over 80,000 employees. The Würth Group operates in over 80 countries with annual sales exceeding €20 billion. He is also one of Europe's most important art collectors.
