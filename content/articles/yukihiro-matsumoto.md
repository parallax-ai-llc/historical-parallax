## Summary

Yukihiro 'Matz' Matsumoto is a Japanese computer scientist who created the Ruby programming language. Ruby's design philosophy emphasizes programmer happiness and productivity, with clean elegant syntax. Ruby gained widespread popularity through the Ruby on Rails web framework, which revolutionized web development. Matsumoto is known for saying 'Ruby is designed to make programmers happy.'
