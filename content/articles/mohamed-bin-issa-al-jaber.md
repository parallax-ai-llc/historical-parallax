## Summary

Mohamed bin Issa Al Jaber is a Saudi Arabian businessman and philanthropist who founded MBI International, a diversified conglomerate with interests in luxury hotels, real estate, and construction across Europe and the Middle East. He owns several luxury hotels in London and Paris. Through the MBI Al Jaber Foundation, he has donated extensively to education and intercultural dialogue. He represents the internationalization of Saudi private wealth.
