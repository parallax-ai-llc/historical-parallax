## Summary

American singer-songwriter who became a global star as a teenager. Her debut album When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? made her the youngest artist to win all four major Grammy categories in one night. Her dark, whispery vocal style and ASMR-influenced production defined a new sound.
