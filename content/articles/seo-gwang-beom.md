## Summary

Seo Gwang-beom is a Korean corrosion engineer who developed anti-corrosion technologies for Korean ships, bridges, and marine structures. His research on protective coatings and cathodic protection systems has extended the lifespan of Korean maritime and coastal infrastructure.
