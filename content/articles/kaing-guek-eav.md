## Summary
Kaing Guek Eav, known as 'Duch,' was the commandant of the Tuol Sleng (S-21) prison during the Khmer Rouge regime. An estimated 12,000-20,000 people were tortured and executed under his command. He was convicted by the ECCC and sentenced to life. He died in prison in 2020.
