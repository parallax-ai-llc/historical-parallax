## Summary

Osman Hamdi Bey was a pioneering Ottoman painter, museum director, and archaeologist who brought European painting techniques to Ottoman art. His masterpiece The Tortoise Trainer is one of the most celebrated works of 19th-century Ottoman art. He founded the Istanbul Archaeology Museum and the Academy of Fine Arts.
