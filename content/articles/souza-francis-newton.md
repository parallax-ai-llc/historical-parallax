## Summary

F.N. Souza was a founding member of the Progressive Artists' Group in Mumbai and one of the most important Indian modern artists. His raw, expressionist paintings of figures and landscapes combine Western modernism with Indian iconography. His visceral depictions of the human body and religious themes challenged conservative Indian society.
