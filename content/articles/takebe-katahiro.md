## Summary

Takebe Katahiro was a Japanese mathematician and student of Seki Takakazu who made important contributions to the Japanese mathematical tradition (wasan). He calculated pi to 41 digits using a polygon method and developed the theory of infinite series. His work on arc length calculation anticipated integral calculus concepts.
