## Summary

Yi Gwang-su was a Korean writer and intellectual who, though primarily a novelist, was one of the first Korean intellectuals to systematically introduce Western scientific and philosophical ideas through his writings. His emphasis on rational thinking and modern education influenced Korea's intellectual modernization.
