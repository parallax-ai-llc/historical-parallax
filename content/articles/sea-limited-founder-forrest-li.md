## Summary

Forrest Li (Li Xiaodong) founded Sea Limited, Southeast Asia's most valuable tech company. Sea operates Shopee (e-commerce), Garena (gaming, including Free Fire), and SeaMoney (fintech). Sea went public on NYSE in 2017 and its market cap exceeded $200 billion at peak. Shopee became the largest e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia and expanded to Latin America. Free Fire became the most downloaded mobile game globally.
