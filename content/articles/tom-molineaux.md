## Summary

Tom Molineaux was a formerly enslaved African American who became one of the first Black boxing stars, fighting for the heavyweight championship in England in 1810.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1809 | Arrived in England to pursue boxing |
| 1810 | Fought Tom Cribb for heavyweight championship |
| 1811 | Fought Cribb again; lost in 11 rounds |
| 1818 | Died in poverty in Ireland |
