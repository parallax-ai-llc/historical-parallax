## Summary

Peter Scholze is a German mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2018 for transforming arithmetic algebraic geometry over p-adic fields with his theory of perfectoid spaces. He became a full professor at the University of Bonn at age 24 and has been called the "brightest young mathematician alive."
