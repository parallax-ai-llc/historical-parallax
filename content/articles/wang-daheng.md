## Summary

Wang Daheng was a Chinese optical physicist known as the 'Father of Chinese Optics.' He founded China's optical engineering discipline and developed optical instruments for China's military and space programs. He was one of the key figures behind China's high-technology development plan (863 Program) and helped establish China's laser and fiber optics research.
