## Summary

Tebello Nyokong is a South African chemist who is a distinguished professor at Rhodes University and a leading researcher in photodynamic therapy for cancer treatment. She uses phthalocyanine dyes and nanotechnology to develop targeted cancer treatments using light-activated drugs. She has received numerous awards including the L'Oréal-UNESCO Award for Women in Science and is considered one of Africa's leading scientists.
