## Summary

Wallace Carothers invented nylon, the first commercially successful synthetic polymer fiber, while leading research at DuPont. He also invented neoprene synthetic rubber. His pioneering work in polymer chemistry established the field of synthetic fibers. He suffered from severe depression and died by suicide at age 41, before nylon was commercially launched.
