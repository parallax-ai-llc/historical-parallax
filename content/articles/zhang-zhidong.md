## Summary

Zhang Zhidong (Tony Zhang) is a co-founder of Tencent and served as the company's chief technology officer. He was instrumental in developing QQ, China's first dominant instant messaging platform, and in Tencent's technical architecture. Though less publicly known than Pony Ma, his technical leadership was crucial to Tencent's growth into China's most valuable tech company. He retired from daily management in 2014.
