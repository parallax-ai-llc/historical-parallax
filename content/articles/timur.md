## Summary

Turco-Mongol conqueror whose campaigns of conquest killed an estimated 17 million people—about 5% of the world's population. He built pyramids of skulls outside conquered cities and devastated regions from India to Turkey.

## Career Timeline

|------|-------|

| 1398 | Invaded India; sacked Delhi |
| 1401 | Destroyed Baghdad |
| 1405 | Died while planning invasion of China |
