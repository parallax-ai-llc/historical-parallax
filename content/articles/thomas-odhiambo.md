## Summary

Thomas Odhiambo was a Kenyan entomologist and science leader who founded the International Centre of Insect Physiology and Ecology (ICIPE) and the African Academy of Sciences. He championed science and technology as tools for African development and trained generations of African scientists. His research on insect pest management contributed to improving agricultural productivity across Africa.
