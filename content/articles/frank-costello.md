## Summary
Frank Costello, the 'Prime Minister of the Underworld,' was an Italian-American mobster who rose to the top of the Luciano crime family. He was known for his political connections rather than violence. He survived an assassination attempt by Vito Genovese in 1957 and retired from organized crime.
