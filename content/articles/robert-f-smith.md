## Summary

Robert F. Smith is the founder of Vista Equity Partners, one of the most successful private equity firms specializing in enterprise software. He is the wealthiest African American, with a fortune built through acquiring and optimizing software companies. He made headlines in 2019 by paying off the student loan debt for the entire graduating class of Morehouse College ($34 million). Vista Equity Partners has consistently delivered top-tier returns.
