## Summary
Abdelhamid Abaaoud was the suspected mastermind of the November 2015 Paris attacks that killed 130 people. He was killed in a police raid in Saint-Denis five days after the attacks.

## Early Life
- Born and raised in Belgium.
- Came to public attention through criminal activity prior to involvement with ISIS.

## Radicalization and ISIS Role
- Traveled to Syria and became associated with the Islamic State (ISIS).
- Reported by European authorities and media as a key operative involved in plotting or coordinating attacks in Europe.

## 2015 Paris Attacks (Connection)
- Widely described as a central figure in planning/coordination.
- French investigations connected him to the network responsible for the attacks.

## Death
- Killed on **2015-11-18** during a police raid in Saint-Denis, north of Paris.
