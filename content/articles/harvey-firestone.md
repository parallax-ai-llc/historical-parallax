## Summary

Harvey Firestone founded the Firestone Tire and Rubber Company, which became one of the largest tire manufacturers in the world. He was a close friend of Henry Ford and Thomas Edison, and the three frequently went on camping trips together. He pioneered the pneumatic tire for Ford's automobiles. His company also developed large rubber plantations in Liberia to secure raw material supplies.
