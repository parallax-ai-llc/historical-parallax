## Summary

Krishnaswamy Kasturirangan is an Indian space scientist who served as chairman of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Under his leadership, ISRO launched the Chandrayaan-1 Moon mission concept and advanced India's satellite technology. He also contributed to X-ray astronomy research and chaired India's education reform committee.
