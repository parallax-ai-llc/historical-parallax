## Summary

Sir Joshua Reynolds was the most influential British painter of the 18th century and first president of the Royal Academy. His portraits elevated the genre by incorporating the grand manner of Renaissance and Baroque painting. His Discourses on Art established the theoretical framework for British academic art.
