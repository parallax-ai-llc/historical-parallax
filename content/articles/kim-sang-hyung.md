## Summary

Kim Sang-hyung is a Korean marine biotechnologist who has researched bioactive compounds from Korean marine organisms. His work on marine-derived enzymes and pharmaceuticals has contributed to Korea's blue biotechnology sector, discovering novel compounds with antimicrobial and anticancer properties.
