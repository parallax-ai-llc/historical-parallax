## Summary

Kim Yong-wan was a Korean polymer chemist who developed advanced polymer membrane technologies at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). His research on separation membranes was applied in water purification and industrial gas separation, contributing to Korea's environmental technology sector.
