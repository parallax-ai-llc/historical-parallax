## Summary

John Williams is an American composer who created the most iconic film scores in cinema history, including Star Wars, Jaws, Indiana Jones, E.T., Schindler's List, and Harry Potter. His orchestral scores revived the symphonic film score tradition. He has won five Academy Awards and 52 nominations, the most for any living person.
