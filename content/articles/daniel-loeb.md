## Summary

Daniel Loeb is the founder and CEO of Third Point LLC, a prominent activist hedge fund known for writing scathing letters to corporate management. He has led activist campaigns at companies including Yahoo, Sotheby's, Sony, and Disney. His investment style combines deep fundamental analysis with public pressure for corporate change. Third Point has generated strong returns since its founding in 1995.
