## Summary
Ibrahim Awad Ibrahim Ali al-Badri al-Samarrai, known as Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi, was the founder and leader of the Islamic State (ISIS/ISIL). He declared a worldwide caliphate in 2014, controlling territory across Iraq and Syria. He killed himself with a suicide vest during a US military raid in Syria in 2019.
