## Summary

Swedish serial killer known as 'Lasermannen' (The Laser Man) who shot 11 people of immigrant backgrounds in Stockholm in 1991-1992 using a laser-sighted rifle. One victim died. He later also murdered a prostitute in Germany. Serving life.
