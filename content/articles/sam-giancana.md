## Summary
Salvatore 'Sam' Giancana was the boss of the Chicago Outfit from 1957 to 1966. He had connections to the Kennedy family and was involved in CIA plots to assassinate Fidel Castro. He was murdered in his home in 1975, shot seven times while cooking sausages.
