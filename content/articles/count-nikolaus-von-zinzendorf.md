## Summary

Count Nikolaus von Zinzendorf was a German religious leader who established the Moravian Church as a major Protestant missionary movement. He offered refuge to persecuted Moravian Brethren on his estate at Herrnhut. Under his leadership, the Moravians sent missionaries worldwide and pioneered the modern Protestant missionary movement. His emphasis on personal relationship with Christ deeply influenced John Wesley and the Methodist movement.
