## Summary

William Hogarth was a pioneering British painter and social critic who created modern moral subjects - narrative painting series satirizing contemporary society. His A Rake's Progress and Marriage A-la-Mode combined biting social commentary with artistic brilliance. He championed the Copyright Act of 1735.
