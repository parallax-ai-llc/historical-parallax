## Summary
José Efraín Ríos Montt was the military dictator of Guatemala in 1982-83. He was convicted of genocide and crimes against humanity for the massacre of indigenous Ixil Maya people during his rule. His conviction was overturned on a technicality. He died before a retrial concluded.
