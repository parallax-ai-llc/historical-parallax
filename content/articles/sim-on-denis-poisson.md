## Summary

Siméon Denis Poisson was a French mathematician and physicist who made important contributions to probability theory, mathematical physics, and applied mathematics. The Poisson distribution, Poisson's equation, and Poisson's ratio are named after him. His work on electrostatics, magnetism, and mechanics was influential in the development of mathematical physics.
