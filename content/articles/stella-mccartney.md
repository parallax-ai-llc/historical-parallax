## Summary

Stella McCartney built a luxury fashion brand committed to sustainability, refusing to use leather, fur, or feathers. She launched her eponymous label in 2001 in partnership with Kering (later bought out the majority stake). Her brand proved that luxury fashion could be sustainable without compromising design or commercial success. She has been a pioneering voice for ethical fashion in the luxury industry.
