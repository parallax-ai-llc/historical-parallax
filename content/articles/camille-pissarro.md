## Summary

Camille Pissarro was the only artist to exhibit in all eight Impressionist exhibitions. Known as the dean of the Impressionists, he was a generous mentor to Cezanne, Gauguin, and others. His rural landscapes and urban scenes demonstrate steady evolution across movements.
