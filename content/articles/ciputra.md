## Summary

Ciputra (Ir. Ciputra) was one of Indonesia's most prominent real estate developers and a pioneer of modern Indonesian urban planning. He co-founded Jaya Real Property, Metropolitan Land, and Ciputra Development, creating some of Indonesia's largest residential and commercial developments. His projects included entire planned cities. He was a major philanthropist and established Universitas Ciputra for entrepreneurship education.
