## Summary

Park Hyeon-ju is a Korean financial engineer who founded Mirae Asset Financial Group and pioneered quantitative investment strategies in South Korea. His application of mathematical modeling and technology to financial services helped modernize Korea's financial industry and establish Korean fintech capabilities.
