## Summary

Huang Daopo was a Yuan Dynasty textile innovator who revolutionized cotton weaving technology in China. She learned advanced cotton processing techniques from the Li people of Hainan Island and introduced improved spinning wheels and looms to the Yangtze River Delta. Her innovations transformed the Chinese cotton industry and made Shanghai a textile center.
