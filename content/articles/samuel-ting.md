## Summary

Samuel Ting shared the 1976 Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the J/psi particle (which he called the J particle) at Brookhaven National Laboratory, simultaneously with Burton Richter at SLAC. This discovery confirmed the charm quark's existence. He later led the Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer experiment on the ISS searching for dark matter.
