## Additional Context

- **Triad societies**: Triads are organized-crime groups with historical roots in secret societies; modern triads are commonly associated with illicit markets and intimidation.
- **Hong Kong context**: Triad groups have periodically been the focus of policing and anti-organized-crime efforts in Hong Kong, especially around extortion, vice markets, and control of certain local businesses.
