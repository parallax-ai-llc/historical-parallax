## Summary

Masahiro Miki founded Workman, Japan's largest workwear chain, which expanded into affordable outdoor fashion and became one of Japan's fastest-growing retailers. By repositioning functional workwear as affordable everyday fashion, he found a unique market niche between Uniqlo and specialized outdoor brands. Workman's price-performance ratio attracted mainstream consumers, driving rapid store expansion across Japan.
