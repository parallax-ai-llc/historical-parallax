## Summary

Rikitake Tsuneji was a Japanese geophysicist who developed the Rikitake dynamo model for geomagnetic field reversals. He also worked extensively on earthquake prediction research and developed theories about precursory electromagnetic phenomena before earthquakes. He served as president of the International Association of Geomagnetism.
