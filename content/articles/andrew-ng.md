## Summary

Andrew Ng is a British-American computer scientist who is one of the most influential figures in modern artificial intelligence and machine learning education. He co-founded Coursera and Google Brain, and served as chief scientist at Baidu. His online machine learning course at Stanford became one of the most popular MOOCs ever created, teaching millions of students worldwide.
