## Summary

Cai Guo-Qiang is a Chinese artist known for his spectacular explosion events and gunpowder drawings. His works use fireworks and explosives as artistic media to create ephemeral spectacles and permanent artworks. He designed the fireworks for the Beijing Olympics opening ceremony.
