## Summary
The Columbine High School massacre on April 20, 1999, was a school shooting in which two students, Eric Harris and Dylan Klebold, murdered 12 students and one teacher before killing themselves. It was one of the deadliest school shootings in US history and triggered nationwide debates about gun control.
