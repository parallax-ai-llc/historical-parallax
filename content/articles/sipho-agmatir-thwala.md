## Summary
Sipho Agmatir Thwala, the 'Phoenix Strangler,' was a South African serial killer convicted of 19 murders and 18 rapes committed in KwaZulu-Natal between 1996 and 1998. He targeted women in the Phoenix area of Durban. He was sentenced to 506 years.
