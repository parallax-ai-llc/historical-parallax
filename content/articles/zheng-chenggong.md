## Summary
Zheng Chenggong (Koxinga) was a Chinese-Japanese pirate and military leader who used a naval fleet to resist the Qing dynasty. He expelled the Dutch from Taiwan in 1662 and established Chinese rule. He is considered both a pirate and a national hero in China and Taiwan.
