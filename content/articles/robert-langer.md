## Summary

Robert Langer is one of the most prolific inventors in medicine and biotechnology, holding over 1,400 patents. He pioneered controlled drug release systems, tissue engineering, and the biomaterials used in mRNA vaccines. His innovations have led to treatments for cancer, diabetes, and other diseases. He co-founded Moderna and over 40 other biotech companies.
