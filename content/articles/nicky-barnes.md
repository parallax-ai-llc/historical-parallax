## Summary
Leroy 'Nicky' Barnes was an American drug dealer who controlled the heroin trade in Harlem, New York, in the 1970s. Known as 'Mr. Untouchable,' he was profiled on the cover of the New York Times Magazine. He was sentenced to life but became a government informant and entered witness protection.
