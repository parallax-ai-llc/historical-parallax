## Summary

Baba Deep Singh was a Sikh warrior and scholar who is one of the most revered martyrs in Sikh history. He led a force to recapture the Golden Temple from Afghan invaders in 1757. According to tradition, he continued fighting even after being mortally wounded, holding his severed head with one hand while fighting with the other until he reached the precincts of the Golden Temple. He is a symbol of supreme sacrifice in Sikhism.
