## Summary

Sol Price invented the membership warehouse club concept by founding FedMart in 1954 and Price Club in 1976. Price Club pioneered the bulk-buying, membership-based retail format that Costco, Sam's Club, and BJ's later adopted. His ideas influenced an entire generation of retailers including Sam Walton and Jim Sinegal. Price Club eventually merged with Costco in 1993. He is considered one of the most innovative retailers in American history.
