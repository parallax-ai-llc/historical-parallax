## Summary

Brian Eno is a British musician producer and visual artist who pioneered ambient music and has been one of the most influential figures in popular music for five decades. He produced landmark albums for David Bowie Talking Heads and U2. His concept of ambient music and generative art has influenced music architecture and visual art.
