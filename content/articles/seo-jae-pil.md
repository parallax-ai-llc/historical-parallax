## Summary

Seo Jae-pil (Philip Jaisohn) was the first Korean to become a naturalized American citizen and the first Korean to earn an American medical degree. He founded Korea's first modern newspaper (The Independent) and introduced modern scientific concepts to Korean readers. He promoted scientific thinking as essential for Korean independence and modernization.
