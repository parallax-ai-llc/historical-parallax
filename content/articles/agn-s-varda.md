## Summary

Agnes Varda was a pioneering French filmmaker known as the grandmother of the French New Wave. Her film Cleo from 5 to 7 is a masterpiece of the movement, and her later work Faces Places showed her continued vitality. She combined documentary and fiction with radical feminist vision.
