## Summary

Zhang Xin is a Chinese billionaire real estate developer who co-founded SOHO China with her husband Pan Shiyi. She developed iconic commercial buildings in Beijing and Shanghai, including the Galaxy SOHO and Wangjing SOHO. She rose from poverty — working in a garment factory as a teenager — to become one of China's most powerful businesswomen. She has been called China's most influential female business leader.
