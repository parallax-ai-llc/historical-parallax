## Summary

Temple Grandin revolutionized livestock handling in the meat industry by designing systems based on her understanding of animal behavior. Her humane handling designs are used in over half of North American meat processing facilities. Diagnosed with autism, she became one of the most famous autistic people in the world and a powerful advocate for neurodiversity.
