## Summary

Cornelius Vanderbilt Whitney was an American business executive, film producer, and philanthropist. He was a co-founder of Pan American Airways and helped produce several major Hollywood films including Gone with the Wind. He also served as Undersecretary of Commerce under President Truman. He represented the diversification of the Vanderbilt-Whitney family interests into aviation and entertainment.
