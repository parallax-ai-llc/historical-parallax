## Summary

Agnolo Bronzino was the leading Mannerist portrait painter of the Florentine court under Cosimo I de' Medici. His icy, elegant portraits defined the image of the Medici court. His allegorical painting Venus, Cupid, Folly, and Time is one of the most complex works of the 16th century.
