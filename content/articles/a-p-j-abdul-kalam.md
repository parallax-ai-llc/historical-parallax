## Summary

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who led India's missile and nuclear weapons programs. Known as the 'Missile Man of India,' he developed the SLV-III rocket and the Agni and Prithvi missile systems. He later served as the 11th President of India (2002-2007) and was beloved for his vision of technological India.
