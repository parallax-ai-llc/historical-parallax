## Summary

Kazuo Ishiguro is a Japanese-born British novelist who received the Nobel Prize in Literature in 2017. His novels including The Remains of the Day, Never Let Me Go, and An Artist of the Floating World explore memory, self-deception, and the passage of time with quiet emotional devastation. The Remains of the Day won the Booker Prize.
