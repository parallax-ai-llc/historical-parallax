## Summary

Burt Rutan is one of the most innovative aircraft designers in history, creating over 45 aircraft types. His Voyager made the first non-stop, unrefueled flight around the world in 1986. His SpaceShipOne won the $10 million Ansari X Prize in 2004 as the first privately funded spacecraft to reach space twice in two weeks.
