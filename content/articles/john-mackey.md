## Summary

John Mackey co-founded Whole Foods Market in 1980, building it into the largest natural and organic foods retailer in the world. His vision of natural, organic grocery shopping helped transform American food culture. Amazon acquired Whole Foods for $13.7 billion in 2017. He coined the concept of 'conscious capitalism,' arguing that businesses should serve all stakeholders, not just shareholders.
