## Summary
Albert Gonzalez was an American computer hacker who masterminded the theft of over 170 million credit card numbers from major retailers including TJX, Heartland Payment Systems, and 7-Eleven. He was sentenced to 20 years in federal prison in 2010 — the longest sentence for computer crime at the time.
