## Summary

Svante Pääbo is a Swedish geneticist who founded the field of paleogenomics by sequencing the Neanderthal genome. His work revealed that modern humans interbred with Neanderthals and discovered the Denisovans, a previously unknown hominin species. He won the 2022 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his discoveries on human evolution.
