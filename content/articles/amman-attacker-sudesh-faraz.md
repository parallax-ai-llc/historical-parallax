## Summary

See Sudesh Amman. British terrorist who stabbed two people in Streatham, London in February 2020 while wearing a fake suicide vest. He had been released from prison 10 days earlier for terrorism offenses. Shot dead by police.

## Incident Overview

The Streatham attack occurred during daylight hours in London in February 2020. The attacker used a knife and wore a hoax explosive device, prompting an immediate armed police response.

## Criminal Justice and Security Context

- Amman had previously been convicted on terrorism-related charges and was released under licence.
- The incident became part of a broader UK debate over offender risk assessment, post-release supervision, and sentencing rules for terrorism cases.
- Police treated the event as terrorism and responded with lethal force at the scene.
