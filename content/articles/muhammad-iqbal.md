## Summary

Muhammad Iqbal was a Muslim philosopher, poet, and political thinker who is considered the spiritual father of Pakistan. His poetry in Urdu and Persian inspired Muslims to seek self-determination and spiritual renewal. His philosophical work The Reconstruction of Religious Thought in Islam sought to reconcile Islamic tradition with modern philosophy. He was the first to propose a separate Muslim state in South Asia.
