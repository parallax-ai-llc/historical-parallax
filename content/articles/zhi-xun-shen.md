## Summary

Zhi-Xun Shen is a Chinese-American physicist at Stanford University who is an expert in angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES). His work has been crucial for understanding high-temperature superconductors, topological insulators, and other exotic quantum materials. He is one of the most cited physicists in condensed matter.
