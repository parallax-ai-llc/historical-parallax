## Summary
Giovanni Brusca was a Sicilian Mafia boss who detonated the bomb that killed Judge Giovanni Falcone in 1992. He confessed to over 100 murders. He kidnapped and dissolved the body of an 11-year-old boy in acid. Despite his crimes, he became a pentito (informant) and was released in 2021.
