## Summary

Venkatesh Prasad Sarma was an Indian electrical engineer who contributed to the development of India's telecommunications infrastructure. He helped establish the Centre for Development of Telematics (C-DOT) and advanced indigenous telecom technology development, reducing India's dependence on imported telecommunications equipment.
