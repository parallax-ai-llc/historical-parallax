## Summary

Andy Jassy succeeded Jeff Bezos as CEO of Amazon in 2021. He previously founded and led Amazon Web Services (AWS), building it into the world's largest cloud computing platform. AWS generates the majority of Amazon's operating profits. Under his leadership as Amazon CEO, he navigated post-pandemic challenges and invested heavily in AI and automation.
