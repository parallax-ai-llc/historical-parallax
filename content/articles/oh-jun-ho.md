## Summary

Oh Jun-Ho is a Korean mechanical engineer and roboticist at KAIST who led the development of the HUBO humanoid robot series and DRC-HUBO, which won first place at the 2015 DARPA Robotics Challenge. His team's victory made Korea a world leader in humanoid robotics for disaster response.
