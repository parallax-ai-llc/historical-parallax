## Summary

Yoshua Bengio is a Canadian computer scientist known for his pioneering work on deep learning, particularly on recurrent neural networks, word embeddings, and generative adversarial networks. He co-founded the Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms (MILA) and shared the 2018 Turing Award with Geoffrey Hinton and Yann LeCun. He is a prominent voice on the responsible development of AI technology.
