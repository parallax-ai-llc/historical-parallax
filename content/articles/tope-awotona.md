## Summary

Tope Awotona is the founder and CEO of Calendly, a scheduling automation platform used by over 20 million users. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, he immigrated to the U.S. and founded Calendly in 2013 after emptying his savings. The company reached a $3 billion valuation in 2021 without significant venture capital. Calendly exemplifies the bootstrap-to-unicorn path and is one of the most successful companies founded by an African immigrant in tech.
