## Summary

Mary Meeker is one of the most influential technology analysts and investors in Silicon Valley history. As a Morgan Stanley analyst in the 1990s, she was known as the 'Queen of the Internet' for her bullish technology research. She later became a partner at Kleiner Perkins and founded Bond Capital. Her annual Internet Trends report has been one of the most-cited presentations in the technology industry for over two decades.
