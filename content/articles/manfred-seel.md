## Summary

German serial killer suspected of murdering up to 12 women near Frankfurt between 1971 and 1996. Remains were found buried on his property. He was convicted of only one murder due to evidence limitations.

- **Forensic challenges**: When remains are recovered long after death, identification and linkage to specific offenders can be difficult due to degradation, missing documentation, or lack of recoverable evidence.

## Prosecution and Conviction
