## Summary
Lester Joseph Gillis, known as 'Baby Face Nelson,' was an American bank robber and murderer during the Public Enemy era. He killed more FBI agents than any other criminal. He was killed in a shootout that also killed two FBI agents near Barrington, Illinois, in 1934.
