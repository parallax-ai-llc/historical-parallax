## Summary

Brendan the Navigator was an Irish monk and abbot who is famous for his legendary voyage across the Atlantic described in the Navigatio Sancti Brendani, one of the most popular texts of the Middle Ages. His voyage story may contain memories of actual Irish monastic seafaring expeditions. He founded several monasteries, including the famous Clonfert. In 1976, Tim Severin demonstrated that such a voyage was possible using period technology.
