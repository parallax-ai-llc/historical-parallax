## Summary

Al-Hajj Umar Tall was a Toucouleur Islamic scholar, Tijaniyya Sufi leader, and military commander who established the Toucouleur Empire in West Africa. He led a major jihad across West Africa in the mid-19th century, conquering the Bambara kingdoms and establishing Islamic governance. He was a prolific scholar who wrote the Rimah, an important Tijaniyya text. His empire was eventually conquered by the French.
