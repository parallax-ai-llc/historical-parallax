## Summary

Vlad Tenev co-founded Robinhood Markets, the commission-free stock trading app that democratized investing for millions of young Americans. Robinhood's elimination of trading commissions forced the entire brokerage industry to follow suit. The company went public in 2021 amid the GameStop trading frenzy that brought scrutiny to its payment-for-order-flow business model. Robinhood has 23 million funded accounts.
