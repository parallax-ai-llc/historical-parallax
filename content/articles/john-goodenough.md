## Summary

John Goodenough was an American materials scientist who shared the 2019 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development of lithium-ion batteries. He identified the cathode materials that made lithium-ion batteries possible, which power virtually all portable electronics and electric vehicles. At age 97, he was the oldest Nobel laureate at the time of his award. He continued working past 100.
