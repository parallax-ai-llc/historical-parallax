## Summary

Meg Whitman served as CEO of eBay from 1998 to 2008, growing it from 30 employees to 15,000 and revenue from $4 million to $8 billion. She later served as CEO of Hewlett-Packard Enterprise and as U.S. Ambassador to Kenya. At eBay, she transformed a small online auction site into a global e-commerce platform. She also ran for governor of California in 2010.
