## Summary

Mostafa El-Sayed was an Egyptian-American chemist who made fundamental contributions to nanoscience and spectroscopy. He pioneered the use of gold nanoparticles for cancer diagnosis and treatment. El-Sayed's rule in spectroscopy describes the rate of intersystem crossing in molecules. He was awarded the US National Medal of Science in 2007.
