## Summary

Hou Debang was a Chinese chemical engineer who developed the Hou process for producing soda ash, an improvement over the Solvay process. He wrote the first comprehensive textbook on soda ash manufacturing and helped establish China's modern chemical industry. His process was more efficient and produced ammonium chloride fertilizer as a byproduct.
