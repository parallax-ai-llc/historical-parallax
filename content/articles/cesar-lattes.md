## Summary

César Lattes was a Brazilian physicist who played a crucial role in the discovery of the pi meson (pion) in 1947, experimentally confirming Yukawa's theory of nuclear forces. His technique of using photographic emulsions at high altitudes was innovative. He is considered one of Brazil's greatest scientists.
