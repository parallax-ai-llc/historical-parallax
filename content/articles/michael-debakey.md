## Summary

Michael DeBakey was a pioneering cardiovascular surgeon who performed some of the first coronary artery bypass grafts and developed the roller pump, a key component of the heart-lung machine. He developed artificial hearts, performed the first successful carotid endarterectomy, and trained many of the world's leading surgeons. His career spanned over 75 years.
