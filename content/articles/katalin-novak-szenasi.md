## Summary

Katalin Novak-Szenasi is a Hungarian biochemist who contributed to enzyme inhibitor research and pharmaceutical chemistry in Central Europe. Her work on protease inhibitors advanced drug development for inflammatory and cardiovascular diseases. She helped build Hungary's pharmaceutical research infrastructure.
