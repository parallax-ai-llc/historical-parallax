## Summary

Lee Soo-man founded SM Entertainment in 1995, pioneering the modern K-pop industry and the idol training system that produced groups like H.O.T., TVXQ, Girls' Generation, EXO, and aespa. He created the 'cultural technology' framework that systematically produces and exports Korean pop music worldwide. SM Entertainment was a major force behind the Korean Wave (Hallyu). He sold his controlling stake to HYBE in 2023.
