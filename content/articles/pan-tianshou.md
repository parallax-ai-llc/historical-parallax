## Summary

Pan Tianshou was a leading Chinese traditional painter and art educator who championed the independence and vitality of Chinese painting against Western artistic dominance. His powerful paintings of flowers, birds, and landscapes demonstrate bold composition and vigorous brushwork. He served as president of the China Academy of Art before being persecuted during the Cultural Revolution.
