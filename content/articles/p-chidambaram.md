## Roles and Offices (selected)

- **Union Minister of Finance (India)**: served multiple terms, including **2004–2008** and **2012–2014**.
- **Union Minister of Home Affairs (India)**: **2008–2012**.
- **Member of Parliament**: served in the **Lok Sabha** (lower house) and later the **Rajya Sabha** (upper house).

## Education and Profession

- Trained as a **lawyer** and became a **Senior Advocate**.
- Studied at the **University of Madras** (including law), and later pursued further study abroad (commonly reported: **Harvard University**).

## Policy and Portfolio Themes (high level)

- **Public finance and taxation**: budgets, fiscal policy, and financial-sector regulation during periods in the finance portfolio.
- **Internal security**: counter-terrorism and domestic security coordination while leading the home affairs portfolio.
- **Commerce and corporate affairs**: held other Union cabinet portfolios earlier in his career.
