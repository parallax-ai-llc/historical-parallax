## Summary

Sudanese military commander known as 'Hemedti' who leads the Rapid Support Forces (RSF), formerly the Janjaweed militia. He was involved in the Darfur genocide and launched a civil war in 2023 that has killed tens of thousands and displaced millions.

## Background and Aliases

Mohamed Hamdan Dagalo is commonly known by the nickname "Hemedti". He rose from militia leadership in Darfur into a position of national influence through the Rapid Support Forces (RSF), a paramilitary force that grew out of earlier militia structures.

## Sudanese Political Context

Sudan's post-2019 transition featured power struggles among military factions, civilian movements, and armed groups. The RSF became one of the principal armed actors in the country, and tensions between the RSF and the Sudanese Armed Forces culminated in large-scale fighting beginning in 2023.

## Human Rights Allegations

Reporting on the RSF and on Dagalo frequently centers on allegations of war crimes and crimes against humanity, including violence against civilians in Darfur. Claims and casualty estimates vary by source; many summaries present these as allegations documented by international observers and human-rights organizations.
