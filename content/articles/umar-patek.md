## Summary

Indonesian terrorist who helped build the bomb for the 2002 Bali bombings. He was on the FBI's most wanted list with a $1 million bounty. Captured in Abbottabad, Pakistan (near where bin Laden was later found). Sentenced to 20 years; released 2022.
