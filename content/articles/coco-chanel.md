## Summary

Coco Chanel was a French fashion designer who revolutionized women's fashion by liberating women from corsets and introducing a casual, elegant style. Her little black dress, Chanel No. 5 perfume, and the Chanel suit became icons of 20th-century style. She remains one of the most influential figures in fashion history.
