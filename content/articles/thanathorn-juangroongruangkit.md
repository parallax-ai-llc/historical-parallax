## Summary

Thanathorn Juangroongruangkit is a Thai billionaire and politician who is heir to the Thai Summit Group, one of Thailand's largest auto parts manufacturers. He founded the Future Forward Party and became a prominent opposition figure challenging Thailand's military-backed government. His party was dissolved by the court in 2020, but his political movement continued. He represents a new generation of Thai businesspeople entering politics for democratic reform.
