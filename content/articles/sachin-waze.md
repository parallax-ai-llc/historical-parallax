## Background

- Sachin Waze is known in Indian media as a Mumbai Police officer associated with high-profile criminal investigations.
- Public reporting about him increased sharply in early 2021 following the "Antilia" security incident near the residence of Mukesh Ambani.

## 2021 Antilia Incident (High-Level)

- In February 2021, a vehicle containing explosive material was found near Ambani's residence in Mumbai (commonly referred to as the Antilia case).
- Waze was arrested in connection with the investigation and was later in the custody of India's National Investigation Agency (NIA), according to widely cited reporting.
- The case also drew attention because it was linked in public reporting to the death of Mansukh Hiren, who was reported to have had a connection to the vehicle.

## Why the Case Mattered

- The incident occurred at the home of one of India's most prominent business figures, raising national-level security and political scrutiny.
- The allegations against a serving police officer became part of a broader public conversation about corruption, accountability, and oversight within law enforcement.
