## Summary

Omar Yaghi is a Jordanian-American chemist who invented metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) and covalent organic frameworks (COFs), entirely new classes of porous materials. His reticular chemistry has applications in carbon capture, hydrogen storage, water harvesting from air, and drug delivery. He is one of the most cited chemists in the world.
