## Summary
Adam Lanza killed 26 people (20 children aged 6-7 and 6 staff) at Sandy Hook Elementary School on December 14, 2012, after killing his mother. He then killed himself.

## Background
- Lanza lived in Connecticut, United States.
- Reports and later investigations discussed significant social/behavioral difficulties and extensive isolation.

## The Attack (High-Level)
- Date: **2012-12-14**
- Location: Sandy Hook Elementary School, Newtown, Connecticut.
- Sequence (high-level): killed his mother → attacked the school → killed himself.

## Aftermath
- One of the deadliest school shootings in U.S. history.
- Triggered large-scale public debate about firearm policy, school security, and mental health.
- Multiple official investigations examined warning signs, access to weapons, and systemic gaps.
