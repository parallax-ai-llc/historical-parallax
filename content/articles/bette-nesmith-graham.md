## Summary

Bette Nesmith Graham invented Liquid Paper correction fluid in 1956 while working as a secretary. She developed the product in her kitchen using tempera paint and sold it from her home before building it into a multimillion-dollar company. She sold Liquid Paper to Gillette for $47.5 million in 1979. She was also the mother of musician Michael Nesmith of The Monkees.
