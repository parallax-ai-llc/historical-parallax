## Summary

Artur Avila is a Brazilian-French mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2014 for his work on dynamical systems and spectral theory. He solved the Ten Martini Problem and made fundamental contributions to the understanding of chaotic systems. He is the first Latin American to receive the Fields Medal.
