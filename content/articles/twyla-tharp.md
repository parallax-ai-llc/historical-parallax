## Summary

Twyla Tharp is an American dancer and choreographer who pioneered the fusion of ballet, modern dance, and popular music. Her works including Push Comes to Shove, In the Upper Room, and Movin' Out combine rigorous technique with infectious energy. She has choreographed for Broadway, Hollywood, and the world's major ballet companies.
