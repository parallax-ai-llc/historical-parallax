## Summary

Sebastian Siemiatkowski is the co-founder and CEO of Klarna, the Swedish fintech company that pioneered the buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) payment model. Klarna became one of Europe's most valuable startups, reaching a $45.6 billion valuation in 2021, before being revalued at $6.7 billion in 2022. The company serves over 150 million consumers worldwide. He has been a driving force behind the BNPL revolution in e-commerce payments.
