## Summary
Clifford Robert Olson Jr. was a Canadian serial killer who murdered 11 children and young people in British Columbia between November 1980 and July 1981. He controversially received $100,000 from the government in exchange for revealing where victims' bodies were buried. He died of cancer in prison.
