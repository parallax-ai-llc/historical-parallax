## Summary

Gina Rinehart is the chairwoman of Hancock Prospecting, Australia's largest private company, and the wealthiest person in Australia. She inherited iron ore mining interests from her father Lang Hancock and expanded them into one of the world's largest mining operations. Her Roy Hill iron ore mine in Western Australia was the largest single investment in Australian history at $10 billion. She has been a prominent advocate for mining-friendly policies and media investments.
