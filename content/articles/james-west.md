## Summary

James West co-invented the electret microphone with Gerhard Sessler in 1962, which is used in 90% of all microphones manufactured today. Found in telephones, hearing aids, and recording devices worldwide, the electret microphone is one of the most widely produced electroacoustic transducers. He holds over 250 patents.
