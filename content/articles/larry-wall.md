## Summary

Larry Wall is an American computer programmer who created the Perl programming language in 1987. Perl became one of the most popular scripting languages for web development, system administration, and text processing during the early internet era. Known for its motto 'There's more than one way to do it,' Perl was instrumental in the development of the early web. Wall also created the patch utility for Unix.
