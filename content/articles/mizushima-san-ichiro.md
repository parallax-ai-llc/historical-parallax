## Summary

Mizushima San-ichiro was a Japanese physical chemist who pioneered the study of molecular conformations using infrared spectroscopy and Raman spectroscopy. He demonstrated that organic molecules can exist in different rotational conformations, establishing conformational analysis in chemistry. His work predated similar Western discoveries.
