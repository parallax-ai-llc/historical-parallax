## Summary

Lucio Tan was one of the Philippines' wealthiest tycoons, with a business empire spanning tobacco (Fortune Tobacco), airlines (Philippine Airlines), banking (Allied Banking), and real estate. He built Fortune Tobacco into the dominant cigarette manufacturer in the Philippines before selling it to Philip Morris. His acquisition and management of Philippine Airlines was often controversial. He was a major figure in Philippine business for over five decades.
