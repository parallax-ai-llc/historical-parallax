## Summary

Roberta Bondar was the first Canadian woman in space, flying aboard the Space Shuttle Discovery in 1992. She is also a neurologist who has studied the effects of spaceflight on the human nervous system. Her research on astronaut adaptation has contributed to understanding of neurological disorders on Earth.
