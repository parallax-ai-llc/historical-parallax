## Summary

Kim Seong-su was a Korean industrialist and educator who founded Kyungsung Spinning and Weaving (Korea's first modern textile company) and Korea University. His investments in industrial technology and modern education helped build the foundations for Korea's later industrial development. He also founded the Dong-A Ilbo newspaper.
