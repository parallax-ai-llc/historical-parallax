## Summary

Morris Chang founded Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) in 1987, pioneering the pure-play semiconductor foundry business model. TSMC became the world's most valuable semiconductor company and the cornerstone of global chip manufacturing. His innovation of separating chip design from fabrication transformed the entire semiconductor industry.
