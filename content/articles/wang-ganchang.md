## Summary

Wang Ganchang was one of the pioneers of Chinese nuclear science. He proposed the use of a cloud chamber to detect neutrinos in 1942 and co-discovered the anti-sigma-minus hyperon at Dubna in 1959. He played a key role in developing China's first atomic and hydrogen bombs.
