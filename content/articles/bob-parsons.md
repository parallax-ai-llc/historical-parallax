## Summary

Bob Parsons is the founder of GoDaddy, the world's largest domain registrar and web hosting company. A Vietnam War veteran and Purple Heart recipient, he founded GoDaddy in 1997 and built it into a company serving over 20 million customers. GoDaddy went public in 2015. He later founded PXG (Parsons Xtreme Golf) and YAM Worldwide. His provocative advertising during Super Bowl commercials made GoDaddy one of the most recognized internet brands.
