## Summary

Liu Yongxing is a Chinese billionaire and chairman of the East Hope Group, one of China's largest private conglomerates with interests in heavy industry, aluminum, chemicals, and agriculture. He is one of the four Liu brothers who built the Hope Group from a small animal feed business into one of China's earliest private empires. East Hope became a major aluminum producer and one of China's most profitable private companies.
