## Summary
Imelda Romualdez Marcos is a Filipino politician and the widow of President Ferdinand Marcos. She served as Governor of Metro Manila and Minister of Human Settlements. She was convicted of corruption in 2018 but has not served prison time. She became famous for her collection of 3,000 pairs of shoes.
