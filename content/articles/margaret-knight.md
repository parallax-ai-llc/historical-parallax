## Summary

Margaret Knight invented the machine for producing flat-bottomed paper bags, still used today. She received over 20 patents during her career and was called the 'female Edison.' She fought and won a patent infringement case against a man who stole her design. Her paper bag machine was one of the most commercially successful inventions of the 19th century.
