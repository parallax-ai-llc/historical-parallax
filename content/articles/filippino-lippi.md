## Summary

Filippino Lippi was a Florentine Renaissance painter, son of Fra Filippo Lippi and student of Botticelli. He completed the Brancacci Chapel frescoes left unfinished by Masaccio. His increasingly expressive late style anticipated aspects of Mannerism.
