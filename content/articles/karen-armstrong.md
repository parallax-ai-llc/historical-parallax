## Summary

Karen Armstrong is a British author and former nun who is one of the most popular and prolific writers on comparative religion. Her books, including A History of God, The Battle for God, and Muhammad: A Prophet for Our Time, have been translated into 45 languages. She founded the Charter for Compassion, a collaborative effort to restore compassion to the center of religious life. She received the TED Prize in 2008.
