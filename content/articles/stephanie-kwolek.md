## Summary

Stephanie Kwolek was an American chemist who invented Kevlar, the high-strength lightweight fiber used in bulletproof vests, helmets, and many other applications. Her accidental discovery at DuPont in 1965 has saved thousands of lives. Kevlar is five times stronger than steel on an equal-weight basis. She was inducted into the National Inventors Hall of Fame and received the National Medal of Technology.
