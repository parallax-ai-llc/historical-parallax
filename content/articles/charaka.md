## Summary

Charaka was an ancient Indian physician who is considered the father of Indian medicine (Ayurveda). His text "Charaka Samhita" is one of the foundational texts of Ayurveda, the traditional Indian system of medicine. It describes anatomy, physiology, diagnosis, treatment, and philosophy of healing. The text discusses over 600 drugs and their applications.
