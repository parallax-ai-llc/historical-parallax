## Summary

Hafez of Shiraz was a Persian lyric poet whose Divan of collected ghazals is considered the pinnacle of Persian literature. His mystical love poems operate on multiple levels, simultaneously celebrating earthly and divine love. His tomb in Shiraz is a major pilgrimage site, and Iranians traditionally use his poems for divination.
