## Summary

Lee Im-hak was a Korean physicist who made important contributions to nuclear physics and helped establish South Korea's nuclear research infrastructure. He trained at institutions in Europe and returned to build Korea's physics community. His work on nuclear reactions contributed to the country's nuclear energy program.
