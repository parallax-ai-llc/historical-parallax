## Summary

Phuthuma Nhleko served as CEO of MTN Group, Africa's largest mobile network operator, from 2002 to 2011. Under his leadership, MTN expanded from a South African telecom into a pan-African and Middle Eastern mobile giant with over 200 million subscribers. He later became chairman of MTN. His leadership was instrumental in bringing mobile connectivity to hundreds of millions of Africans.
