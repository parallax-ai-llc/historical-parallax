## Summary

Robert Budi Hartono is an Indonesian billionaire and the co-owner (with brother Michael) of Djarum, one of the world's largest tobacco companies. The Hartono brothers also control BCA (Bank Central Asia), Indonesia's largest private bank. Djarum is famous for its kretek (clove) cigarettes and is also a major investor in technology and property. The Hartono brothers are consistently ranked among Asia's wealthiest.
