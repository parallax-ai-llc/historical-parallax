## Summary

Austin Bradford Hill established the methodology for randomized controlled clinical trials with the landmark 1948 streptomycin trial. He, along with Richard Doll, provided the first convincing statistical evidence that smoking caused lung cancer. His 'Bradford Hill criteria' for establishing causation remain fundamental to epidemiology.
