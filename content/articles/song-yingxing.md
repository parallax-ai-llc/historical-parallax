## Summary

Song Yingxing was a Ming Dynasty scientist who authored the Tiangong Kaiwu (The Exploitation of the Works of Nature), an encyclopedia of science and technology. Published in 1637, it documented manufacturing processes including mining, ceramics, papermaking, metallurgy, and agriculture with detailed illustrations. It is considered China's most important pre-modern technology text.
