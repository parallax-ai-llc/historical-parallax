## Summary

Anne Wojcicki co-founded 23andMe, the direct-to-consumer genetic testing company that made personal genomics accessible to millions. She was previously married to Google co-founder Sergey Brin. 23andMe went public via SPAC in 2021 but faced significant challenges, including a major data breach and declining stock price. Despite challenges, 23andMe collected one of the world's largest genetic databases with over 14 million customers.
