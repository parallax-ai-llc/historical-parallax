## Summary

Andrew Murray was a South African writer, teacher, and Christian pastor who authored over 240 books and tracts. His devotional works, including Abide in Christ and With Christ in the School of Prayer, have been translated into many languages. He was a leader in the South African Revival of 1860 and a prominent figure in the Keswick Holiness movement. He also played a key role in founding several educational institutions in South Africa.
