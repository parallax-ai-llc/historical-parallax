## Summary

Lina Bo Bardi was an Italian-Brazilian architect whose visionary buildings combined modernist principles with Brazilian cultural traditions. Her Sao Paulo Museum of Art and SESC Pompeia are icons of Brazilian architecture. She championed popular culture and social engagement in architecture.
