## Summary

Sri Ramakrishna was a Hindu mystic whose spiritual experiences and teachings inspired the Ramakrishna Movement. He practiced disciplines of various religions and proclaimed the unity of all religions based on his direct experience. He attracted a group of devoted disciples who later formed the Ramakrishna Order under Swami Vivekananda. He is regarded as an avatar (incarnation of God) by his followers.
