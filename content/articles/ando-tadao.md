## Summary

Tadao Ando is a self-taught Japanese architect who won the 1995 Pritzker Prize. Without formal architectural education, he developed an innovative style using exposed concrete and natural light. His engineering of light through precise openings and water features demonstrates exceptional understanding of physics and materials. The Church of the Light is his masterpiece.
