## Summary

Susanne Klatten is a German billionaire industrialist and the richest woman in Germany. She owns a 19.2% stake in BMW and is the sole owner of Altana AG, a specialty chemicals company. She is the daughter of Herbert Quandt and sister of Stefan Quandt. Together with her brother, the Quandt family controls nearly 47% of BMW. She also invests in renewable energy through her various holdings.
