## Summary
Wan Kuok-koi, known as 'Broken Tooth,' was the leader of the 14K Triad in Macau. He controlled gambling and organized crime operations. He was convicted of criminal association and sentenced to 15 years in 1999. He was released in 2012. The US sanctioned him in 2020 for his ongoing criminal activities.
