## Summary

Treena Livingston Arinzeh is an American biomedical engineer who has pioneered tissue engineering and stem cell research for bone and cartilage repair. Her work on using mesenchymal stem cells for tissue regeneration has advanced orthopedic medicine. She was the first African American woman to receive the NSF CAREER Award in her field.
