## Summary
Roberto Suárez Gómez was a Bolivian drug lord known as the 'King of Cocaine' before Pablo Escobar's rise. He controlled much of Bolivia's cocaine production and trafficking in the late 1970s and 1980s. He was arrested in 1988 and served 13 years. He died in 2000.
