## Summary

Daniel Bovet discovered antihistamines (used for allergies) and synthetic curare-like compounds used as muscle relaxants in surgery. He received the 1957 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine. His work enabled modern anesthesiology by providing drugs that relax muscles during surgery without affecting consciousness.
