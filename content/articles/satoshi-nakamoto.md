## Summary

Satoshi Nakamoto is the pseudonymous person or group who created Bitcoin, the first decentralized cryptocurrency. In 2008, Nakamoto published a whitepaper describing Bitcoin and its underlying blockchain technology. In 2009, the Bitcoin network was launched. Nakamoto's true identity remains unknown, making them one of the most mysterious figures in technology. Their creation launched the cryptocurrency revolution.
