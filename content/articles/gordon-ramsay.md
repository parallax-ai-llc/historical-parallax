## Summary

Gordon Ramsay built a global restaurant empire and media career from his Michelin-starred restaurants. His Gordon Ramsay Restaurants group operates over 35 restaurants worldwide, including Restaurant Gordon Ramsay (three Michelin stars since 2001). His TV shows Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, and MasterChef made him the world's most famous chef. His business acumen transformed him from a chef into a global media and hospitality brand.
