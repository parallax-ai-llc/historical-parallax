## Summary

Al-Bukhari was a Persian Islamic scholar who compiled Sahih al-Bukhari, regarded by Sunni Muslims as the most authentic collection of hadith. He reportedly examined over 600,000 hadith and selected approximately 7,275 for his collection based on rigorous criteria of authenticity. His work took 16 years to compile and became the most authoritative source of prophetic traditions in Sunni Islam.
