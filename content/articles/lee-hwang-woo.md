## Summary

Lee Hwang-woo was a South Korean nuclear physicist who played a crucial role in establishing physics research in Korea. He contributed to building Korea's scientific infrastructure and trained generations of Korean physicists. He was a leading figure at Seoul National University and contributed to international collaboration in physics.
