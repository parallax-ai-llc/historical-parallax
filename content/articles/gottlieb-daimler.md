## Summary

Gottlieb Daimler was a German engineer and industrialist who pioneered high-speed internal combustion engines and is one of the founders of the automobile industry. He and Wilhelm Maybach developed a compact, high-speed engine and built one of the first motorcycles (1885) and a four-wheeled automobile (1889). His company eventually merged with Karl Benz's to form Mercedes-Benz.
