## Summary

Correggio was an Italian Renaissance painter known for innovative illusionistic dome frescoes and sensuous mythological scenes. His Assumption of the Virgin in Parma Cathedral pioneered sotto in su technique, anticipating the Baroque by over a century. His tender figures and warm palette influenced both Mannerism and Baroque painting.
