## Summary

Mary Kay Ash founded Mary Kay Cosmetics in 1963, creating one of the most successful direct sales companies in history. She started the company with $5,000 and built it into a billion-dollar enterprise. Her pink Cadillac incentive program became iconic in American business culture. She empowered hundreds of thousands of women as independent beauty consultants and was a pioneer of women in business.
