## Summary

Tirunesh Dibaba won three Olympic golds and was the first woman to achieve the 5,000m/10,000m double at one Olympics (2008 Beijing).

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2003 | Won World 5,000m gold at age 17 |
| 2008 | Won 5,000m and 10,000m at Beijing |
| 2012 | Won 10,000m at London |
