## Summary

Adoniram Judson was an American Baptist missionary who spent nearly 40 years in Burma (Myanmar), becoming the first significant American missionary overseas. He translated the entire Bible into Burmese and compiled an English-Burmese dictionary. He endured imprisonment, torture, and personal tragedy. He is regarded as one of the greatest missionaries in American Protestant history.
