## Summary

While Chung Myung-whun is primarily a conductor and pianist, his role as music director of the Seoul Philharmonic and various European orchestras has demonstrated the systematic, analytical approach to musical interpretation that parallels scientific methodology. His work has elevated Korean cultural technology internationally.
