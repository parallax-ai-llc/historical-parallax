## Summary

Zhan Tianyou was the father of Chinese railroad engineering, who designed and built the Beijing-Zhangjiakou Railway in 1909, the first major railroad built without foreign assistance. He solved the problem of crossing steep mountain passes using an ingenious zigzag switchback design. His achievement was a landmark of Chinese engineering independence.
