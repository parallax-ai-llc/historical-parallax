## Summary

Neo Rauch is a German painter who is the most important figure of the New Leipzig School. His enigmatic, surreal paintings combine Socialist Realist figuration with dream logic, creating narratives that feel both familiar and deeply strange. His work addresses the legacy of East Germany.
