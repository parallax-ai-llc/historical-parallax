## Summary

Mutsu Munemitsu was a Japanese diplomat and statesman who as Foreign Minister successfully renegotiated the unequal treaties with Western powers. While primarily a diplomat, his understanding of international law and technology transfer policies was crucial for Japan's scientific modernization and its ability to adopt Western technology on its own terms.
