## Summary

Nigerian drug queen who ran a major heroin trafficking network from Lagos to Southeast Asia. She was one of West Africa's most powerful drug traffickers before arrest by Nigerian authorities.

## Overview

Mama Lau is described in Nigerian anti-narcotics reporting and media coverage as a prominent trafficker associated with heroin smuggling routes linking West Africa with Southeast Asia. Her notoriety is tied to the idea that she operated at a scale unusual for an individual dealer, acting as an organizer and financier rather than a low-level courier.
