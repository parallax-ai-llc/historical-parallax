## Summary
Edward 'Ned' Kelly was an Australian bushranger and outlaw who became a folk hero. He and his gang fought police in a famous suit of homemade armor at their last stand in Glenrowan. He was captured and hanged in Melbourne in 1880. His story embodies Australian anti-authoritarianism.
