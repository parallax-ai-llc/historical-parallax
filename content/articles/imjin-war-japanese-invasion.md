## Summary

The Imjin War (1592-1598) was Japan's invasion of Korea under Toyotomi Hideyoshi. Initial Japanese success was reversed by Admiral Yi Sun-sin's naval victories and Ming Chinese intervention. The war devastated Korea but failed to achieve Japanese objectives.
