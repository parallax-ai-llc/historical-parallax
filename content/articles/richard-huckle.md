## Summary

British pedophile convicted of 71 sex offenses against Malaysian children, murdered in prison.

This article summarizes the publicly reported case at a high level and avoids graphic details.

## Background

- British national who lived and traveled in Southeast Asia, including Malaysia.[^1]
- Reported to have used the internet and social platforms to contact victims and document offending.[^1]

## Investigation and Arrest

- Malaysian authorities arrested him in 2014 after receiving information and evidence tied to his online activity.[^1]
- He was later returned to the United Kingdom to face prosecution.[^1]

## Conviction and Sentence

- In 2016, a UK court convicted him of 71 sexual offenses involving Malaysian children.[^1]
- He received a life sentence with a minimum term (tariff) reported as 22 years before parole eligibility.[^1]

## Death

- He was killed in 2019 while imprisoned in the UK.[^1]
