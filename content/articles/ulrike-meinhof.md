## Summary
Ulrike Marie Meinhof was a German left-wing militant and co-founder of the Red Army Faction (RAF/Baader-Meinhof Gang). She transitioned from respected journalist to urban guerrilla. The RAF conducted bombings, assassinations, and kidnappings. She was found hanged in her prison cell in 1976.
