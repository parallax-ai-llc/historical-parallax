## Summary

Patrick of Ireland was a Romano-British Christian missionary and bishop who is credited with converting Ireland to Christianity. Captured as a teenager by Irish raiders, he spent six years in slavery before escaping. He later returned to Ireland as a missionary bishop. His Confession is one of the earliest and most important documents of the British Isles.
