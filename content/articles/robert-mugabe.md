## Summary
Robert Mugabe was a Zimbabwean revolutionary and politician who served as Prime Minister and then President of Zimbabwe from 1980 to 2017. Initially a hero of the anti-colonial struggle, his rule descended into authoritarianism, economic mismanagement, and hyperinflation.
