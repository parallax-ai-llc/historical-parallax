## Summary

Narada is a divine sage (devarishi) who appears throughout Hindu mythology as a wandering musician and messenger between the gods and humanity. He is credited with authoring the Narada Bhakti Sutra, one of the most important texts on devotional love in Hinduism. He is depicted carrying a veena (stringed instrument) and traveling through the three worlds. He is considered the patron sage of musicians and the inventor of the veena.
