## Summary

Michael Saylor co-founded MicroStrategy and became the most prominent corporate advocate for Bitcoin as a treasury reserve asset. He converted MicroStrategy's corporate treasury into Bitcoin starting in 2020, accumulating over 200,000 BTC worth tens of billions. He previously built MicroStrategy into a leading business intelligence company. His aggressive Bitcoin strategy made him one of the most polarizing figures in finance and technology.
