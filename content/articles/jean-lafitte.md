## Summary
Jean Lafitte was a French pirate and privateer who operated in the Gulf of Mexico. He and his men smuggled goods through Barataria Bay. He helped the United States win the Battle of New Orleans in 1815 under Andrew Jackson. He later returned to piracy and disappeared.
