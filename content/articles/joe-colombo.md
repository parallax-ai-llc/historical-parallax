## Summary
Joseph Anthony Colombo Sr. was an American mobster who became boss of the Colombo crime family. He publicly protested anti-Italian discrimination and founded the Italian-American Civil Rights League. He was shot at a rally in 1971, left paralyzed, and died seven years later.
