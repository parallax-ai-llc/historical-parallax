## Summary

E.C. George Sudarshan was an Indian-American theoretical physicist who made pioneering contributions to quantum optics, quantum field theory, and particle physics. He co-developed the V-A theory of weak interactions and the Sudarshan-Glauber representation in quantum optics. Many believed he deserved the Nobel Prize for his quantum optics work.
