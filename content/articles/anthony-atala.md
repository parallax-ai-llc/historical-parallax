## Summary

Anthony Atala is a pioneer in regenerative medicine who grew the first lab-grown human organ — a bladder — and successfully transplanted it into a patient in 1999. He directs the Wake Forest Institute for Regenerative Medicine, where his team has engineered over 30 different tissues and organs. He developed a 3D bioprinter that can print human tissue.
