## Summary

Antonio Ermirio de Moraes was the patriarch of Grupo Votorantim, one of Brazil's largest industrial conglomerates. Under his leadership, Votorantim expanded into cement, metals, chemicals, pulp and paper, and finance. Votorantim Cimentos became one of the world's largest cement producers. He was known for his frugal lifestyle and his role in building Brazil's industrial base over six decades.
