## Summary

Hugo Boss founded the Hugo Boss fashion company in 1924 in Metzingen, Germany. During the Nazi era, his factory produced uniforms for the Wehrmacht, SS, and Hitler Youth, using forced labor. After his death, the company was transformed by his descendants into a global luxury fashion brand. The brand is known for its men's suits and became one of the world's leading premium fashion houses.
