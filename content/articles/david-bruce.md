## Summary

David Bruce identified the causative organisms of sleeping sickness (African trypanosomiasis) and brucellosis (which was named after him). He discovered that the tsetse fly transmitted the trypanosome parasite causing sleeping sickness. His work in tropical medicine during military service in Africa and Malta was groundbreaking.
