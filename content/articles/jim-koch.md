## Summary

Jim Koch is the co-founder and chairman of Boston Beer Company, which produces Samuel Adams beer. He is widely credited with launching the American craft beer revolution in 1984 when he brewed the first batch of Samuel Adams Boston Lager using his great-great-grandfather's recipe. Boston Beer Company grew into the largest American-owned brewery and went public in 1995. His success inspired thousands of craft breweries across the United States.
