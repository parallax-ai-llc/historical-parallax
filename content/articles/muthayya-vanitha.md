## Summary

Muthayya Vanitha was the project director of India's Chandrayaan-2 lunar mission, making her the first woman to lead an interplanetary mission for ISRO. She oversaw the design and development of the orbiter, lander, and rover. Despite the lander's hard landing, the orbiter continues to function and provide valuable data about the Moon.
