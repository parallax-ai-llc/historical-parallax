## Summary

Dario Amodei is the co-founder and CEO of Anthropic, the AI safety company behind the Claude series of AI assistants. He previously served as VP of Research at OpenAI before leaving with his sister Daniela to found Anthropic in 2021. Anthropic has raised over $7 billion in funding from Google, Amazon, and others. His focus on building AI systems that are safe, interpretable, and steerable represents a distinct approach in the AI industry.
