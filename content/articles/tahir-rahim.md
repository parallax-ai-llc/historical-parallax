## Summary

Dato Sri Tahir is one of Indonesia's richest individuals and the founder of Mayapada Group, which includes Bank Mayapada Internasional, Tahir Foundation, and interests in healthcare and property. He is Indonesia's most generous philanthropist, having donated over $100 million to disaster relief and healthcare. He provided major funding during the COVID-19 pandemic and for the WHO.
