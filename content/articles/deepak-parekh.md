## Summary

Deepak Parekh is the chairman of HDFC (Housing Development Finance Corporation), India's largest housing finance company. He transformed HDFC from a development finance institution into the backbone of India's housing finance market. HDFC Bank, the banking subsidiary, became India's most valuable private sector bank. In 2023, HDFC merged with HDFC Bank in the largest corporate merger in Indian history.
