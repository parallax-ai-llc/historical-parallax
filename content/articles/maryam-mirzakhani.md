## Summary

Maryam Mirzakhani was an Iranian mathematician who became the first woman and the first Iranian to win the Fields Medal in 2014. She was awarded the prize for her outstanding contributions to the dynamics and geometry of Riemann surfaces and their moduli spaces. Her work connected several mathematical disciplines including hyperbolic geometry, complex analysis, topology, and dynamics. She died of breast cancer at age 40.
