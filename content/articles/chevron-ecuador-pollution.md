## Summary

Texaco (now Chevron) dumped 16 billion gallons of toxic waste into the Ecuadorian Amazon between 1964 and 1992, poisoning indigenous communities. An Ecuadorian court ordered Chevron to pay $9.5 billion but the company has refused to pay.
