## 概述

Ali Akbar Khan was an Indian classical musician and sarod virtuoso considered one of the greatest musicians of the 20th century. He founded the Ali Akbar College of Music in California and was instrumental in popularizing Indian classical music in the West. His recordings and concert performances set the standard for Hindustani music.
