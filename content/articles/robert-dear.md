## Notability and Context

- The 2015 Colorado Springs Planned Parenthood shooting is commonly cited in discussions of **ideologically motivated violence** in the United States, particularly violence connected to **anti-abortion extremism**.
- The incident drew national attention because it targeted a high-profile healthcare provider and became part of broader political and legal debates around reproductive health services.

## Event Overview (2015 Colorado Springs)

- **Location:** Colorado Springs, Colorado (United States)
- **Target:** Planned Parenthood clinic
- **Outcome:** 3 people killed; 9 injured (as widely reported)
