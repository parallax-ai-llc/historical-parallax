## Summary

Johanna Döbereiner was a Czech-born Brazilian agronomist who discovered that certain bacteria can fix nitrogen in association with tropical grasses and cereals. Her research on biological nitrogen fixation saved Brazil billions of dollars in fertilizer costs and made Brazilian soybean farming globally competitive without synthetic nitrogen.
