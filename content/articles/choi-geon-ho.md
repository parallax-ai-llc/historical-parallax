## Summary

Choi Geon-ho is a Korean geotechnical engineer who developed foundation design techniques for Korea's challenging geology. His research on soft ground improvement and deep foundation systems was crucial for constructing major Korean infrastructure including the Incheon International Airport on reclaimed land.
