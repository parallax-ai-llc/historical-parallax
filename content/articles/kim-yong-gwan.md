## Summary

Kim Yong-gwan was a pioneering Korean mathematician who made contributions to functional analysis and operator theory. He helped establish modern mathematics education in South Korea after the Korean War and trained many of the country's leading mathematicians. He was instrumental in building Korea's mathematics infrastructure.
