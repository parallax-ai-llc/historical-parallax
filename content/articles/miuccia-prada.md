## Summary

Miuccia Prada transformed her family's luxury leather goods company into one of the most influential fashion houses in the world. She took over the company in 1978 and launched Prada's first women's ready-to-wear collection in 1988. Her intellectual approach to fashion and willingness to challenge conventions made Prada a cultural force. She also launched Miu Miu as a secondary line and co-chairs the Prada Foundation for contemporary art.
