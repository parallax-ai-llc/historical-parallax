## Summary

Noh Tae-won is a Korean physicist at Seoul National University who has made significant contributions to oxide electronics and complex oxide thin films. His research on perovskite materials and their electronic properties has advanced understanding of strongly correlated electron systems and potential applications in next-generation electronics.
