Min Pyeong-deok is a Korean aviation engineer who contributed to the development of Korea's aviation manufacturing industry. His work on aircraft component design and manufacturing helped Korean companies like Korean Aerospace Industries (KAI) develop indigenous aircraft production capabilities.
