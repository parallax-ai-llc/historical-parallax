## Summary

Peng Lei (Lucy Peng) is a co-founder of Alibaba Group and former CEO of Ant Financial (now Ant Group). She was one of the original 18 co-founders of Alibaba and played key roles in human resources, Alipay, and the development of Ant Financial into the world's most valuable fintech company. She later became chairwoman of Lazada, Alibaba's Southeast Asian e-commerce arm.
