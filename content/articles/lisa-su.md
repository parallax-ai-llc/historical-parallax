## Summary

Lisa Su became CEO of AMD in 2014 and transformed it from a struggling chipmaker into a major competitor to Intel and NVIDIA. Under her leadership, AMD's stock price increased by over 30x, one of the greatest turnarounds in semiconductor history. She led the development of the Ryzen and EPYC processors that recaptured market share from Intel. Su also positioned AMD as a key player in AI computing.
