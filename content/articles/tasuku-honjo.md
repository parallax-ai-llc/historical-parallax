## Summary

Tasuku Honjo is a Japanese immunologist who shared the 2018 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine with James Allison for their discovery of cancer therapy by inhibition of negative immune regulation. Honjo discovered the PD-1 protein on immune cells and showed that blocking it could unleash the immune system to fight cancer. This led to revolutionary immunotherapy treatments for various cancers.
