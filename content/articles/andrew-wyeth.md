## Summary

Andrew Wyeth was an American realist painter known for his haunting, detailed depictions of the rural landscapes and people of Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania and Cushing, Maine. His painting Christina's World is one of the most famous images in American art. His meticulous tempera technique and austere compositions evoke an emotional depth beneath apparent simplicity.
