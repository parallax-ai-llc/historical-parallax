## Summary

Alexander Men was a Russian Orthodox priest, theologian, and author who was one of the most influential Christian leaders in late Soviet-era Russia. He wrote extensively on world religions, the Bible, and the relationship between faith and culture. His seven-volume series 'In Search of the Way, the Truth, and the Life' explored the history of world religions. He was murdered with an axe on his way to serve liturgy; the crime remains unsolved.
