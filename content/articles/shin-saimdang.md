## Summary

Shin Saimdang was a Joseon Dynasty artist, calligrapher, and scholar who is revered as a model of Korean womanhood. Her detailed paintings of insects, plants, and natural scenes demonstrate precise scientific observation. She appears on the South Korean 50,000 won banknote and is the mother of the great scholar Yulgok Yi I.
