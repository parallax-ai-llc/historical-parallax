## Summary

Han Sang-jun is a Korean pharmacologist who contributed to the development of new drug discovery programs in South Korea. His research at Korean pharmaceutical research institutes helped build indigenous drug development capabilities, reducing Korea's dependence on imported pharmaceuticals and establishing the Korean pharmaceutical industry.
