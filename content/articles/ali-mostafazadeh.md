## Summary

Ali Mostafazadeh is an Iranian theoretical physicist who made significant contributions to pseudo-Hermitian quantum mechanics and the mathematical foundations of PT-symmetric quantum theory. His work extended quantum mechanics beyond conventional Hermitian Hamiltonians and has applications in optics and photonics.
