## Summary

Leonardo Del Vecchio founded Luxottica, which became the world's largest eyewear company. Raised in an orphanage in Milan, he built a global empire controlling brands like Ray-Ban and Oakley and manufacturing for Chanel, Prada, and Giorgio Armani. In 2018, Luxottica merged with Essilor to form EssilorLuxottica, valued at over $80 billion. He was the wealthiest person in Italy at the time of his death.
