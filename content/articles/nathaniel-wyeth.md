## Summary

Nathaniel Wyeth invented the PET (polyethylene terephthalate) plastic bottle, which revolutionized beverage packaging. His invention solved the problem of making plastic bottles strong enough to hold carbonated beverages. PET bottles are now the most common plastic packaging material in the world, used for water, soft drinks, and food products.
