## Summary
Carmine 'Lilo' Galante was an American mobster and underboss and later acting boss of the Bonanno crime family. He was a major heroin trafficker. He was assassinated while eating lunch at a Brooklyn restaurant in 1979, reportedly for trying to dominate the heroin trade.
