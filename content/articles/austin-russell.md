## Summary

Austin Russell is the founder and CEO of Luminar Technologies, a lidar sensor company for autonomous vehicles. He dropped out of Stanford at 17 to found Luminar and became the world's youngest self-made billionaire at 25 when the company went public in 2020. Luminar's lidar technology is used by major automakers including Volvo, Mercedes-Benz, and Toyota. He holds over 100 patents in photonics and lidar technology.
