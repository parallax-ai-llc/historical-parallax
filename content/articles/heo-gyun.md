## Summary

Heo Gyun was a Joseon Dynasty polymath, brother of the famous poet Heo Nanseolheon. Besides his literary fame, he was deeply interested in astronomy, geography, and technology. He compiled works on military science and geography and advocated for scientific modernization in Joseon society. He was executed for allegedly plotting rebellion.
