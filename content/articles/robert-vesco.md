## Summary
Robert Lee Vesco was an American financier and fugitive who stole $224 million from investors through the mutual fund company Investors Overseas Services. He fled the US in 1972 and spent the rest of his life in Costa Rica, the Bahamas, and Cuba. He died in Cuba in 2007.
