## Summary

Nachmanides (Ramban, Moses ben Nahman) was a leading medieval Jewish scholar, philosopher, and Kabbalist from Catalonia. He is known for his comprehensive Torah commentary that integrated mystical interpretation with rational analysis. He participated in the famous Barcelona Disputation of 1263, defending Judaism against Dominican friar Pablo Christiani. He spent his final years in the Land of Israel, helping rebuild the Jewish community in Jerusalem.
