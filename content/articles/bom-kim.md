## Summary

Bom Kim founded Coupang, South Korea's largest e-commerce company, often called the 'Amazon of South Korea.' He introduced same-day and dawn delivery through Coupang's Rocket Delivery service, which transformed Korean e-commerce. Coupang went public on the NYSE in 2021, raising $4.6 billion in the largest U.S. IPO by an Asian company since Alibaba. He previously founded Vinebox and studied at Harvard Business School.
