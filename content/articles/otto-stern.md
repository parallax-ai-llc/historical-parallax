## Summary

Otto Stern was a German-American physicist who won the 1943 Nobel Prize in Physics for his contribution to the development of the molecular ray method and his discovery of the magnetic moment of the proton. His Stern-Gerlach experiment (1922) demonstrated the quantization of angular momentum and became a cornerstone of quantum mechanics.
