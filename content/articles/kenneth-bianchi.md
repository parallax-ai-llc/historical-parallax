## Summary
Kenneth Alessio Bianchi, one of the 'Hillside Stranglers,' committed 10 murders in Los Angeles with cousin Angelo Buono, plus two murders in Bellingham, Washington, in 1979. He faked multiple personality disorder at trial. He pleaded guilty and was sentenced to life without parole.
