## Summary

Chun Kyung-ja was one of Korea's most popular female painters known for her colorful paintings of flowers, women, and exotic landscapes from her world travels. Her distinctive style combines Eastern ink techniques with Western color and composition. Her work sparked one of Korea's most famous art authentication controversies.
