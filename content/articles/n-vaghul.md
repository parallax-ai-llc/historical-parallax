## Summary

N. Vaghul (Narayanan Vaghul) was the chairman of ICICI who transformed it from a development finance institution into ICICI Bank, India's largest private sector bank. He mentored a generation of Indian bankers including K.V. Kamath and Chanda Kochhar. Under his leadership, ICICI embraced technology and retail banking, challenging the dominance of government-owned banks. He is regarded as one of the architects of modern Indian banking.
