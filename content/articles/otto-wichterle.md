## Summary

Otto Wichterle invented soft contact lenses in 1961, developing hydrogel materials that were comfortable enough for prolonged eye wear. He built the first lens-making apparatus from children's building kit Merkur, a bicycle dynamo, and a bell transformer in his kitchen. His invention transformed vision correction for hundreds of millions of people.
