## Summary

Hans Wilsdorf founded Rolex, the world's most recognized luxury watch brand. He pioneered the waterproof wristwatch (Oyster case) and the automatic self-winding mechanism (Perpetual rotor). He moved the company from London to Geneva, establishing Switzerland as the center of luxury watchmaking. Rolex became synonymous with prestige, precision, and achievement worldwide.
