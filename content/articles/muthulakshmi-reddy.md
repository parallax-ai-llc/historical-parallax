## Summary

Muthulakshmi Reddy was the first Indian woman to be admitted to a men's college, the first woman house surgeon in a government hospital, and the first woman legislator in India. She founded the Adyar Cancer Institute in Madras and campaigned to abolish the devadasi system. She combined medical science with social reform.
