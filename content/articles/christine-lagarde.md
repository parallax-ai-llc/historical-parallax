## Summary

Christine Lagarde is the President of the European Central Bank and formerly the Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund. She was the first woman to lead both institutions. At the IMF, she managed the European debt crisis and reformed the institution's governance. At the ECB, she has navigated post-pandemic inflation and maintained eurozone stability. She previously served as France's Finance Minister.
