## Summary

Johannes Vermeer was a Dutch Golden Age painter who created some of the most exquisite and beloved paintings in Western art. Girl with a Pearl Earring and The Art of Painting demonstrate his unparalleled mastery of light and color. Only about 35 paintings are attributed to him, each a masterwork of intimate domestic poetry.
