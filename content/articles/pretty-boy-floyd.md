## Summary
Charles Arthur 'Pretty Boy' Floyd was an American bank robber who was named 'Public Enemy No. 1' by the FBI. He operated during the Great Depression. He was popular among the public, who saw him as a Robin Hood figure. He was killed by FBI agents in Ohio in 1934.
