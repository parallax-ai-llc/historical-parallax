## Background

Samat Abish (Kazakh: Самат Әбіш) is a Kazakh security official and a member of the extended Nazarbayev family, which has been politically influential in Kazakhstan since independence.

He is widely described in public sources as a nephew of Nursultan Nazarbayev and as having held senior roles within Kazakhstan’s national security apparatus.

## Role in Kazakhstan’s security services

Public reporting commonly associates Abish with the National Security Committee (KNB), the country’s principal domestic security and intelligence body.

In senior security roles, responsibilities typically include internal security coordination, counterintelligence, and the management of sensitive state security operations.

Because many operational details are not public, most open-source accounts focus on his titles, organizational position, and links to the political leadership.

## January 2022 unrest (context)

In early January 2022, Kazakhstan experienced widespread protests and violent unrest, followed by a large-scale security response.
