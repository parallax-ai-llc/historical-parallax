## Summary

Saenchai is one of the greatest Muay Thai fighters ever, winning Lumpinee Stadium titles in four weight classes with over 300 victories.

## Additional Context

### Background

- Saenchai (often billed as **Saenchai Sor. Kingstar**) is widely associated with Thailand’s stadium-era Muay Thai scene.
- He is frequently cited for staying competitive across many years while facing larger opponents, particularly after moving up in weight.

### Style and Reputation

- Commonly described as a **high-IQ, highly technical** fighter.
- Known for creativity and timing, including agile footwork, evasive movement, and unorthodox strikes.
- Highlight reels and instructional breakdowns often point to his ability to set traps and counter immediately after slips or pivots.

### Notable Achievements (High-Level)

- Multiple championship runs at **Lumpinee Stadium** across different weight classes.
- Long international career with bouts promoted across Asia, Europe, and other regions as Muay Thai gained global visibility.

### Legacy

- Frequently referenced by fighters and coaches as a modern benchmark for technical Muay Thai.
- His career is often used as an example of longevity through skill adaptation rather than reliance on size or single-shot power.
