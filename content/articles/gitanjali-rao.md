## Summary

Gitanjali Rao was named TIME's first-ever Kid of the Year in 2020 at age 15 for her invention of Tethys, a device for detecting lead in water, and Kindly, an AI-based tool to detect cyberbullying. She has invented multiple technologies addressing social issues and has inspired young scientists worldwide through mentoring and science advocacy.
