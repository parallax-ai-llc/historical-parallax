## Summary

David Bushnell invented the Turtle, the first submarine used in combat, during the American Revolutionary War in 1775. The one-person, hand-powered vessel attempted to attach a mine to the hull of HMS Eagle in New York Harbor. His pioneering work in submarine warfare and underwater mines influenced naval engineering for centuries.
