## Summary

Josiah Wedgwood founded the Wedgwood pottery company and is considered the father of English ceramics. He revolutionized pottery manufacturing with scientific methods and mass production techniques. He was a pioneer of modern marketing, using celebrity endorsements, catalogs, and showrooms. He was also an abolitionist who created the famous 'Am I Not a Man and a Brother?' cameo. He was Charles Darwin's maternal grandfather.
