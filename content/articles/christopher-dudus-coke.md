## Summary
Christopher Michael Coke, known as 'Dudus,' was a Jamaican drug lord who led the Shower Posse criminal organization from Tivoli Gardens, Kingston. His attempted arrest in 2010 led to a military operation that killed 73 people. He was extradited to the US and sentenced to 23 years.
