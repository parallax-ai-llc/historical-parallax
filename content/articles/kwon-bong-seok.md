## Summary

Kwon Bong-seok is a Korean chemical engineer who served as CEO of LG Energy Solution, leading the company to become one of the world's largest electric vehicle battery manufacturers. His technical expertise in battery chemistry has guided LG's development of advanced lithium-ion and solid-state battery technologies.
