## Summary

Liu Chuanzhi founded Legend Holdings (later Lenovo) in 1984, building it into the world's largest PC manufacturer. Under his leadership, Lenovo acquired IBM's PC division for $1.75 billion in 2005, one of the first major Chinese acquisitions of an iconic American brand. He is considered the godfather of Chinese technology entrepreneurship. Lenovo went on to become the world's largest PC vendor by market share.
