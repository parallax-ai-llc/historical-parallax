## Background

- **Madeleine Beth McCann** (born 12 May 2003) was a British child who disappeared on **3 May 2007** from a holiday apartment in **Praia da Luz**, Algarve, Portugal.
- The disappearance became a major international news story and led to extensive public appeals and fundraising for continued investigative work.

## Investigation (high-level)

- The initial investigation was led by Portuguese authorities. The case later involved UK police, including the **Metropolitan Police Service**.
- In **2011**, the Met began a review and subsequent investigation known as **Operation Grange**.
- Over the years, various individuals were treated as suspects or persons of interest; the case remains unresolved publicly.

## Major milestones

- **2007:** Immediate search efforts and international media coverage following the disappearance.
- **2011:** Launch of **Operation Grange** by the Metropolitan Police to review evidence and pursue investigative leads.
- **2020:** German prosecutors stated they were investigating **Christian Brückner** in connection with Madeleine McCann's disappearance.
