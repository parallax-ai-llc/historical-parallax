## Summary

Yuan Longping, known as the Father of Hybrid Rice, developed the first successful hybrid rice varieties that dramatically increased crop yields. His innovations in rice cultivation have been credited with feeding an additional 100 million people annually. His work earned him the World Food Prize and he is considered one of the most important agricultural scientists in history.
