## Additional Context

### Background (high level)

- Nikolaos G. "Nikos" Michaloliakos is a Greek far-right political figure best known as the founder and long-time leader of Golden Dawn.
- Public biographies commonly describe him as originating from Athens and having been involved in ultra-nationalist circles from a young age.

### Golden Dawn

- Golden Dawn began as a publication and later developed into an organized political movement.
- Michaloliakos is widely identified as the central leader (often styled as general secretary) during the party's rise to national visibility.

### Elected office

- He served as a Member of the Hellenic Parliament during the period when Golden Dawn entered and remained in parliament in the 2010s.

### Criminal conviction (2020)

- In October 2020, an Athens court found Golden Dawn's leadership, including Michaloliakos, guilty in a case that treated the party as a criminal organization.
- This conviction is a key inflection point in both his public biography and the party's modern history.
