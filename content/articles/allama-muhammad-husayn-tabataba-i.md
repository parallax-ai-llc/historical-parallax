## Summary

Allama Tabataba'i was an Iranian Shia scholar, philosopher, and Quran commentator who authored al-Mizan fi Tafsir al-Quran, a 20-volume commentary that is one of the most important works of modern Quranic exegesis. He revived Islamic philosophy in Qom and trained many of Iran's leading intellectuals. His dialogue with the French philosopher Henry Corbin was published as Shi'ite Islam. He is considered one of the greatest Shia scholars of the 20th century.
