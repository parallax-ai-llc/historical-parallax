## Summary

Richard Serra was an American sculptor known for his massive, site-specific steel sculptures that transform viewers' experience of space. His Torqued Ellipses and Tilted Arc challenged and expanded the possibilities of sculpture. His work at the Guggenheim Bilbao and other major institutions represents the pinnacle of postwar sculpture.
