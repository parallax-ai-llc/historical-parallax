## Summary

Kengo Kuma is a Japanese architect known for his use of natural materials, particularly wood, to create buildings that harmonize with their environments. His designs seek to dissolve the boundary between architecture and nature. His Japan National Stadium for the 2020 Tokyo Olympics showcases his philosophy of organic, sustainable architecture.
