## Summary

Mexican drug lord known as El Chapo who led the Sinaloa Cartel and became the world's most powerful drug trafficker. He escaped from maximum security prisons twice before being extradited to the United States.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1989 | Became leader of Sinaloa Cartel |
| 2001 | Escaped from Puente Grande prison |
| 2014 | Recaptured after 13 years |
| 2015 | Escaped through a mile-long tunnel |
| 2019 | Convicted in US and sentenced to life plus 30 years |
