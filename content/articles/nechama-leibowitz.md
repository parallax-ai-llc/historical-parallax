## Summary

Nechama Leibowitz was an Israeli Bible scholar and educator who revolutionized the study of the Torah through her innovative teaching method. Her weekly study sheets (gilyonot), distributed for decades to thousands of subscribers worldwide, taught close reading of biblical commentaries. She received the Israel Prize for Education in 1956. She insisted on being called simply 'Nechama' and refused to accept any honorary titles.
