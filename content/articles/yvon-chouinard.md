## Summary

Yvon Chouinard founded Patagonia, the outdoor clothing company known for its environmental activism. In 2022, he gave away the entire company (valued at $3 billion) to a trust and nonprofit dedicated to fighting climate change. Starting as a rock climber who forged his own pitons, he built Patagonia into a brand that combined business success with environmental responsibility. His decision to donate the company was called the most radical act of corporate philanthropy in history.
