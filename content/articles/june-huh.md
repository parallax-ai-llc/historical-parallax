## Summary

June Huh is a Korean-American mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2022 for bringing the ideas of Hodge theory to combinatorics, proving the Dowling-Wilson conjecture and other long-standing problems. His unconventional path to mathematics — he initially studied journalism — makes his story particularly remarkable.
