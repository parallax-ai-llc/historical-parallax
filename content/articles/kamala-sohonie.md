## Summary

Kamala Sohonie was the first Indian woman to earn a PhD in a scientific discipline. She studied at Cambridge under Nobel laureate Frederick Gowland Hopkins, researching the enzyme cytochrome c. She later worked on the nutritional value of neera (palm sap), helping address malnutrition among poor communities in India.
