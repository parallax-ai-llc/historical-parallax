## Summary

Quek Leng Chan is a Malaysian billionaire and chairman of the Hong Leong Group Malaysia, one of the country's largest conglomerates. He controls Hong Leong Bank, Hong Leong Financial Group, and has interests in property, manufacturing, and hospitality. He is known as an intensely private and shrewd investor who rarely gives interviews. The Malaysian Hong Leong Group is separate from the Singapore-based Hong Leong Group.
