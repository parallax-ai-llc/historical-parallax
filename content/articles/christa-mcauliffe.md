## Summary

Christa McAuliffe was selected as the first Teacher in Space by NASA in 1985. She was killed along with six crewmates when the Space Shuttle Challenger disintegrated 73 seconds after launch on January 28, 1986. Her death shocked the nation and led to major changes in NASA's safety culture. She became a symbol of the risks and aspirations of space exploration.
