## Summary
Chhota Rajan (Rajendra Sadashiv Nikalje) is an Indian crime boss and former associate of Dawood Ibrahim. After splitting from D-Company over the 1993 Mumbai bombings, he became Dawood's bitter rival. He was arrested in Bali, Indonesia in 2015 and extradited to India.
