## Summary
Anatoliy Slivko was a Soviet serial killer who murdered seven boys in Nevinnomyssk between 1964-85. He was a respected youth club leader who hanged and filmed his victims. Executed in 1989.

## Public Persona
- Known locally as a youth club leader and community member.
- Used his position of trust to access children.

## Crimes (High-Level)
- Victims: boys.
- Location: Nevinnomyssk, Soviet Union.
- Timeframe: 1964–1985.
- Method described in investigations: staged hangings; filmed assaults and killings.

## Trial and Execution
- Convicted in the Soviet criminal justice system.
- Executed in 1989.
