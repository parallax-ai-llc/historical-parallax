## Summary
The Mt. Gox hack resulted in the loss of approximately 850,000 bitcoins (worth $450 million at the time) from the world's largest bitcoin exchange. The hack, discovered in February 2014, led to Mt. Gox filing for bankruptcy. It remains one of the largest cryptocurrency thefts.
