## Summary

Zhang Daqian was one of the most renowned Chinese painters of the 20th century, known for his mastery of both traditional Chinese painting and modern abstraction. His splash-ink landscapes of his later career are among the most innovative achievements in Chinese art. A prolific artist, forger, and colorful personality, he was called 'the Picasso of China.'
