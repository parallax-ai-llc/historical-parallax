## Background and Public Profile

Aldrich is widely reported in connection with the 2022 Colorado Springs nightclub shooting at Club Q. Public accounts about the attacker’s personal history are limited and are primarily derived from law enforcement statements, court filings, and reporting summarized in encyclopedic sources.

## Attack Overview (High Level)

The Club Q attack occurred late on November 19, 2022 (into the early hours of November 20). Reporting describes a rapid sequence of gunfire followed by physical intervention by patrons, after which police took the suspect into custody. The incident drew national attention as an example of anti-LGBTQ violence.
