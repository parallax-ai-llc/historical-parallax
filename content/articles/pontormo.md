## Summary

Jacopo Pontormo was a pioneering Italian Mannerist painter known for elongated figures, vivid colors, and emotionally charged compositions. His Deposition from the Cross in the Capponi Chapel is a masterpiece of Mannerism. A student of Andrea del Sarto, he created a distinctive, psychologically intense style.
