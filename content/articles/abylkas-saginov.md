## Summary

Abylkas Saginov was a Kazakh mining engineer and geologist who developed innovative methods for mining Kazakhstan's vast mineral deposits. He founded the Karaganda Polytechnic Institute and trained generations of Kazakh mining engineers. His techniques improved the efficiency and safety of coal and mineral extraction in Central Asia.
