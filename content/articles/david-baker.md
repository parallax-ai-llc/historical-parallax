## Summary

David Baker developed Rosetta, the leading software for computational protein design, and created the first proteins not found in nature. His lab designed de novo proteins with novel structures and functions, opening a new era in bioengineering. He shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for computational protein design.
