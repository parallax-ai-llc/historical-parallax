## Summary

Ernst Mayr was a German-American taxonomist, ornithologist, and evolutionary biologist who was one of the 20th century's leading evolutionary theorists. He defined the biological species concept and contributed to the modern evolutionary synthesis. His books 'Systematics and the Origin of Species' and 'The Growth of Biological Thought' were landmark works. He was called 'the Darwin of the 20th century.'
