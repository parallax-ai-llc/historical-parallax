## Summary

Felix Vallotton was a Swiss-French painter and printmaker associated with the Nabis, known for striking woodcuts and psychologically tense interior scenes. His bold, flat graphic style influenced Art Nouveau and modern illustration. His work anticipates aspects of New Objectivity.
