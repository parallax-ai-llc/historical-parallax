## Summary

Azim Premji transformed Wipro from a vegetable oil company into one of the world's largest IT services firms. He took over the family business at age 21 when his father died and pivoted it into technology services. Wipro became a global leader in IT outsourcing and consulting. He is one of India's most generous philanthropists, having donated over $21 billion through the Azim Premji Foundation, focused on education.
