## Summary

Fred Smith founded Federal Express (FedEx) in 1971, creating the modern express delivery industry. His Yale term paper proposing an overnight delivery service became the foundation for a company that revolutionized logistics. FedEx pioneered the hub-and-spoke distribution model and package tracking technology. The company grew into a $90 billion global logistics empire.
