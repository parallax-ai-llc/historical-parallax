## Summary

Xu Kai (born November 21, 1994) is a Chinese actor who rose to national fame through his performances in period dramas on Chinese streaming platforms. Best known for his leading roles in high-profile costume dramas, he has established himself as one of the prominent young male stars in Chinese entertainment during the late 2010s and early 2020s. His combination of classical looks, versatile acting range, and strong work ethic earned him a dedicated fanbase across China and throughout East and Southeast Asia.

Xu Kai's ascent reflects the broader boom in Chinese web dramas and streaming content, demonstrating how platforms such as iQIYI and Tencent Video have become major launchpads for emerging talent. His success helped reinvigorate interest in historical romance genres among younger audiences, contributing to a renewed appreciation for traditional Chinese aesthetics and storytelling formats.

## Early Life & Background

Xu Kai was born on November 21, 1994, in Guangdong Province, China. He studied at the Beijing Film Academy, one of China's most prestigious performing arts institutions, where he trained formally in acting. His educational background at this competitive institution laid a disciplined foundation for his subsequent career in film and television.

## Rise to Prominence

Xu Kai gained significant attention in 2018 when he was cast in the historical fantasy drama *Story of Yanxi Palace* (延禧攻略), one of the most-watched Chinese dramas of that year. While not the lead, his performance attracted widespread notice and built an early fanbase. He followed this with a starring role in *The Legends* (招摇) in 2019, a wuxia romance drama in which he played the male lead opposite Bai Lu. The series further cemented his reputation as a capable leading man in the costume drama genre.

His profile continued to rise with *Arsenal Military Academy* (烈火军校) in 2019, a Republic-era drama that showcased his ability to handle more grounded, action-oriented material. In 2021, he starred in *Ancient Love Poetry* (千古玦尘), a high-fantasy romance drama that became one of the more popular streaming releases of that year.

## Key Achievements

- Starred in *Story of Yanxi Palace* (2018), which at its peak broke records for daily playback views on iQIYI.
- Led multiple costume dramas that ranked among the top-streamed series on major Chinese platforms between 2019 and 2022.
- Won several fan-voted awards and nominations at Chinese television award ceremonies, including recognition at the Weibo TV Influence Awards.
- Built a substantial social media following, with tens of millions of followers across Weibo and other Chinese platforms.

## Controversies & Criticism

Xu Kai's career has been largely free of major public controversies. Some critics have noted that his roles have been concentrated within a narrow genre — primarily period romance and fantasy dramas — raising questions about the depth of his range. He has faced the general scrutiny that accompanies celebrity status in China's tightly managed entertainment industry, though no significant scandals have been publicly documented as of early 2026.

## Legacy & Influence

Xu Kai represents a generation of Chinese actors whose careers were shaped by the rise of streaming platforms and the global appetite for Chinese historical drama content. His work contributed to expanding the international audience for C-dramas, particularly among diaspora communities and fans in Southeast Asia. As he continues to take on diverse projects, his trajectory will help define how streaming-era Chinese stars transition into broader, more critically recognized careers.
