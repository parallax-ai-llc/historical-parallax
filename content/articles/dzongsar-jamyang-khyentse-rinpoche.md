## Summary

Dzongsar Jamyang Khyentse Rinpoche is a Bhutanese Buddhist lama, filmmaker, and author who is one of the most creative and unconventional Tibetan Buddhist teachers of the modern era. He is the incarnation of Jamyang Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö. He has directed acclaimed films including The Cup and Travellers and Magicians. His books, including What Makes You Not a Buddhist, present Buddhism in provocative contemporary terms.
