## Summary

Roger Tsien was an American biochemist who shared the 2008 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the discovery and development of green fluorescent protein (GFP). He expanded the color palette of fluorescent proteins, creating a rainbow of colors for biological imaging. His work revolutionized cell biology by enabling researchers to watch biological processes in living organisms in real time.
