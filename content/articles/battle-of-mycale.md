## Summary

The Battle of Mycale in 479 BC was a decisive Greek victory over Persia fought on the same day as Plataea. The Greek fleet destroyed the Persian navy on the coast of Ionia, ending Persian naval power in the Aegean and beginning the liberation of Ionian Greek cities.
