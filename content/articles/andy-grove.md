## Summary

Andy Grove was a Hungarian-born American businessman and engineer who was one of the co-founders and CEO of Intel Corporation. Under his leadership, Intel became the world's largest semiconductor company. His management philosophy, particularly as described in his book 'Only the Paranoid Survive,' was highly influential in Silicon Valley. He escaped communist Hungary and the Holocaust, later becoming one of the most admired business leaders in America.
