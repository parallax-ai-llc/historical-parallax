## Summary

Commodore Matthew Perry's 'Black Ships' arrived in Japan on July 8, 1853, demanding trade relations. His return in 1854 led to the Treaty of Kanagawa, opening Japan after over 200 years of isolation and catalyzing the Meiji Restoration.
