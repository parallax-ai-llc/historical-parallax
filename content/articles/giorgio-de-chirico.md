## Summary

Giorgio de Chirico founded Pittura Metafisica (Metaphysical Painting). His haunting cityscapes of deserted piazzas and long shadows profoundly influenced Surrealism. He later repudiated his early work for neo-classicism, creating one of art history's most controversial reversals.
