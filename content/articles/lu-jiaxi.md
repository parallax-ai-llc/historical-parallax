## Summary

Lu Jiaxi was a Chinese structural chemist who made significant contributions to the chemistry of metal clusters and polyoxometalates. He served as president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and was a leader in Chinese scientific development. His work on the structure and bonding of metal cluster compounds was internationally recognized.
