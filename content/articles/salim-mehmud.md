## Summary

Salim Mehmud is a Pakistani nuclear physicist who contributed to the development of Pakistan's nuclear energy program. His work on reactor physics and nuclear fuel cycle technology supported Pakistan's indigenous nuclear power development and contributed to the country's energy security.
