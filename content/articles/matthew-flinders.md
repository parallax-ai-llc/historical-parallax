## Summary

Matthew Flinders was the first person to circumnavigate Australia and is credited with naming the continent. His cat Trim accompanied him on his voyages.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1798 | Circumnavigated Tasmania, proving it an island |
| 1801 | Began circumnavigation of Australia |
| 1803 | Completed circumnavigation; imprisoned on Mauritius |
| 1814 | Published 'A Voyage to Terra Australis'; died same day |
