## Summary

Paul Tudor Jones is a legendary hedge fund manager who founded Tudor Investment Corp. He famously predicted and profited from the 1987 Black Monday crash, reportedly tripling his money. His macro trading style and risk management discipline made Tudor one of the most consistently profitable funds for decades. He also founded the Robin Hood Foundation, which has distributed over $3 billion to fight poverty in New York City.
