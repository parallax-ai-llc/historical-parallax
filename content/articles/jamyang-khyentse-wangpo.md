## Summary

Jamyang Khyentse Wangpo was a Tibetan Buddhist master and one of the founding figures of the Rime (non-sectarian) movement in 19th-century Tibet. Along with Jamgon Kongtrul and Chogyur Dechen Lingpa, he preserved teachings from all schools of Tibetan Buddhism. He was a master of all four schools and received transmissions from over 150 teachers. He is recognized as the incarnation of Jigme Lingpa and Vimalamitra.
