## Summary

Kim Swoo-geun was the most influential Korean architect of the 20th century. His Space Group building in Seoul and the Olympic Gymnastics Arena for the 1988 Seoul Olympics exemplify his distinctive fusion of Korean traditional aesthetics with brutalist concrete forms. He also founded the influential Space magazine.
