## Summary

Susan Wojcicki was the CEO of YouTube from 2014 to 2023 and one of the most important figures in the history of online video. She was Google's 16th employee and the person who rented her garage to Larry Page and Sergey Brin when they founded Google. She led Google's advertising business before taking over YouTube, where she grew it from $4 billion to over $30 billion in annual revenue. She died in 2024 from lung cancer.
