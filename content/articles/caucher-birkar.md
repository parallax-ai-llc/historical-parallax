## Summary

Caucher Birkar is a Kurdish-British mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2018 for his contributions to algebraic geometry, particularly the minimal model program for algebraic varieties. Born in a Kurdish village in Iran, he became a refugee in the UK and rose to become one of the world's leading mathematicians.
