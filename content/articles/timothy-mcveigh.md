## Summary
Timothy James McVeigh was an American domestic terrorist who detonated a truck bomb outside the Alfred P. Murrah Federal Building in Oklahoma City on April 19, 1995, killing 168 people including 19 children. It was the deadliest act of domestic terrorism in US history at the time. He was executed in 2001.
