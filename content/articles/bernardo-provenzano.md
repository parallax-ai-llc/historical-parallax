## Summary
Bernardo Provenzano was the head of the Sicilian Mafia from 1993 until his capture in 2006. He earned the nicknames 'The Tractor' (for mowing people down) and later 'The Accountant' for shifting the Mafia toward less violent, more business-oriented operations. He was a fugitive for 43 years.
