## Summary

Pat Robertson was an American media mogul, televangelist, and political figure who founded the Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN) and hosted The 700 Club for decades. He founded Regent University and ran for the Republican presidential nomination in 1988. He was one of the most influential figures in the American Religious Right. His controversial statements on politics and religion made him a polarizing figure.
