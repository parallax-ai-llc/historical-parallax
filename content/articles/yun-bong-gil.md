## Summary

Yun Bong-gil was a Korean independence activist who threw a bomb at Japanese military commanders during a celebration in Shanghai's Hongkou Park in 1932, killing several Japanese generals. While not a scientist, his action demonstrated the engineering knowledge needed to construct explosive devices and brought international attention to Korea's independence struggle.
