## Summary
Pan Am Flight 103 was destroyed by a bomb over Lockerbie, Scotland, on December 21, 1988, killing all 259 on board and 11 on the ground. Libyan intelligence officer Abdelbaset al-Megrahi was convicted. It was the deadliest terrorist attack in Britain.
