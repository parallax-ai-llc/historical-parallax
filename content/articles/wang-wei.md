## Summary

Wang Wei founded SF Express, China's largest privately owned courier company, often called 'China's FedEx.' He started with a delivery service between Hong Kong and Shenzhen and built it into a logistics empire handling billions of packages annually. SF Express went public in 2017, briefly making Wang China's third-richest person. The company is known for its premium service quality in China's highly competitive delivery market.
