## Summary

Pak Mun-su was a Joseon Dynasty government official famous as an Amhaeng-eosa (secret royal inspector) who traveled the country investigating corruption and helping common people. While not a scientist, his systematic investigative methods for gathering evidence and his detailed documentation of social conditions anticipated modern social science field research.
