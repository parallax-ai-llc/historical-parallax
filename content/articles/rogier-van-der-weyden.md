## Summary

Rogier van der Weyden was one of the most influential Flemish painters of the 15th century, known for emotionally intense religious paintings. His Descent from the Cross is a masterpiece of Northern European art. As city painter of Brussels, he was the most famous painter in Northern Europe during his lifetime.
