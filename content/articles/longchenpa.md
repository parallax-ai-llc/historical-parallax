## Summary

Longchenpa (Longchen Rabjam) was a Tibetan Buddhist philosopher and writer who is considered the most important systematizer of the Nyingma school's Dzogchen (Great Perfection) teachings. His Seven Treasures (mDzod bdun) and other works present the most comprehensive treatment of Dzogchen philosophy. He synthesized the entire range of Buddhist teachings within the Dzogchen framework. He is regarded as one of the greatest thinkers in Tibetan Buddhism.
