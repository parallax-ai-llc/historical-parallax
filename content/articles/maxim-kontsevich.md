## Summary

Maxim Kontsevich is a Russian-French mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 1998 for his contributions to algebraic geometry, topology, and mathematical physics. He proved Witten's conjecture on intersection theory and made important contributions to deformation quantization and mirror symmetry.
