## Summary

Nishi Amane was a Japanese philosopher and scholar who introduced Western philosophy and scientific methodology to Japan. He coined many Japanese terms for Western philosophical and scientific concepts, including the Japanese words for 'philosophy,' 'aesthetics,' and 'psychology.' His terminology enabled Japanese to engage with Western science.
