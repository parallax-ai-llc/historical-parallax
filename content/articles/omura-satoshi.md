## Summary

Omura Satoshi is a Japanese microbiologist and chemist who won the 2015 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for discovering avermectin, which led to the anti-parasitic drug ivermectin. His systematic screening of soil microorganisms for bioactive compounds has saved hundreds of millions of people from river blindness and lymphatic filariasis.
