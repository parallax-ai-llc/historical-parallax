## Summary

Marcel Telles is a Brazilian billionaire and co-founder of 3G Capital alongside Jorge Paulo Lemann. He served as CEO of AmBev and played a key role in the acquisitions of Anheuser-Busch, Burger King, and Kraft Heinz. His management philosophy of meritocracy and extreme cost efficiency transformed global consumer goods companies. He is one of the architects of Brazil's most successful international business ventures.
