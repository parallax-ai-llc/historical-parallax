## Summary

Manny Pangilinan is the chairman of Metro Pacific Investments Corporation and the managing director of First Pacific Company. He controls PLDT (Philippines' largest telecom), Manila Electric Company (Meralco), and infrastructure assets. He also led the acquisition of Indofood in Indonesia through First Pacific. Known as 'MVP,' he has been one of the most powerful business executives in the Philippines for over two decades.
