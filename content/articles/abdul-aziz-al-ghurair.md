## Summary

Abdul Aziz Al Ghurair is one of the wealthiest people in the United Arab Emirates and head of the Al Ghurair Group, a diversified conglomerate spanning banking, food, construction, and real estate. He served as speaker of the UAE Federal National Council. Mashreqbank, controlled by the Al Ghurair family, is one of the UAE's oldest and largest private banks. He also established a $1.1 billion education fund for Arab youth.
