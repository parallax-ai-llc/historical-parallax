## Summary

Swami Ramdas was an Indian saint and devotee of Lord Rama who founded the Anandashram in Kanhangad, Kerala. He experienced a divine transformation through the constant repetition of the Ram mantra and spent years wandering across India as a mendicant. His autobiography In the Vision of God describes his spiritual journey. He taught that the constant remembrance of God's name leads to the realization of God in all beings.
