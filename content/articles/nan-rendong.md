## Summary

Nan Rendong was a Chinese astronomer and engineer who conceived, designed, and oversaw the construction of FAST (Five-hundred-meter Aperture Spherical Telescope), the world's largest single-dish radio telescope. Located in Guizhou Province, FAST surpassed the Arecibo Observatory in sensitivity. Nan dedicated over 20 years to the project before dying shortly after its completion.
