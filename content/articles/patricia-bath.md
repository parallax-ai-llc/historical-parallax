## Summary

Patricia Bath invented the Laserphaco Probe for cataract surgery in 1986, becoming the first African American female doctor to receive a medical patent. Her laser device made cataract surgery more precise and accessible. She co-founded the American Institute for the Prevention of Blindness and traveled internationally to perform sight-restoring surgery.
