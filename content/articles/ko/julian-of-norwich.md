## 개요

노리치의 줄리안 was an English anchoress and mystic who is considered one of the greatest Christian mystics. Her Revelations of Divine Love is the first known book written in English by a woman. She received 16 visions (showings) during a near-death illness in 1373. Her theology of divine love and her famous declaration 'All shall be well' continue to inspire believers worldwide.
