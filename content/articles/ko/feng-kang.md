## 개요

Feng Kang 중국의 수학자 who independently developed the finite element method in 1965, around the same time as Western mathematicians. His method for solving partial differential equations became fundamental to engineering simulation and computational 과학. He also developed symplectic algorithms for Hamiltonian systems. He is considered the father of Chinese computational mathematics.
