## 개요

Dooman Khang 한국의 display 과학자 who 에 기여했다 the development of Samsung's liquid crystal display (LCD) 기술. His research on thin-film transistor LCD panels helped Samsung become 세계's largest flat-panel display manufacturer, dominating the global television market.
