## 요약

The Second Punic War (218-201 BC) featured Hannibal's legendary crossing of the Alps. His string of victories, especially Cannae, nearly destroyed Rome. Scipio's counter-invasion of Africa forced Hannibal home to defeat at Zama.

| 202 BC | Zama: Hannibal defeated |
