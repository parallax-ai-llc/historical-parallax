## 개요

While 고은 is Korea's most celebrated poet (perennially nominated for the 노벨 문학상 until personal controversies), his work extensively documents Korean history, science, and natural world. His epic poem Maninbo catalogs thousands of real Korean people including scientists and inventors, serving as a poetic encyclopedia of Korean life.
