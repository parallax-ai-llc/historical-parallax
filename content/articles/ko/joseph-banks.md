## 개요

조지프 뱅크스 was a British naturalist and botanist who participated in Captain Cook's first voyage of discovery (1768-1771) and collected thousands of plant specimens. He served as president of the Royal Society for 41 years and was instrumental in establishing the botanical collections at Kew Gardens. He funded numerous scientific expeditions.
