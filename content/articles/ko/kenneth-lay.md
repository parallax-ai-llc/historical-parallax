## 개요
Kenneth Lee Lay was the founder, chairman, and CEO of Enron Corporation. He oversaw one of the largest corporate frauds in American history, with Enron's collapse destroying $74 billion in shareholder value. He was convicted of fraud in 2006 but died before sentencing.
