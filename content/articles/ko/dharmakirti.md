## 개요

Dharmakirti 인도의 Buddhist 철학자 and logician whose works on epistemology and logic became foundational in Tibetan Buddhism and Indian 철학. His Pramanavarttika (Commentary on Valid Cognition) is one of the most studied texts in Tibetan monastic 교육. He refined the logical system of his predecessor Dignaga and developed sophisticated arguments for Buddhist metaphysical positions. His work remains central to Tibetan Buddhist intellectual training.
