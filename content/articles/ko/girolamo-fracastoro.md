## 개요

Girolamo Fracastoro proposed an 초기의 form of germ theory in his 1546 work De Contagione, suggesting that epidemic diseases are caused by transferable tiny particles (seminaria). He coined the term 'syphilis' in his 1530 poem Syphilis, sive Morbus Gallicus. His ideas about contagion anticipated 현대의 microbiology by three centuries.
