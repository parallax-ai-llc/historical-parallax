## 개요

Guillermo del Toro is a Mexican filmmaker known for his visually inventive films blending fantasy, horror, and political allegory. Pan's Labyrinth and The Shape of Water both won major awards. His deep love of monsters, gothic imagery, and fairy tales creates a unique cinematic vision.
