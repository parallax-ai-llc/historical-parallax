## 개요

Jack Kornfield is an American author and teacher of Buddhism who is one of the key figures in bringing Theravada Buddhist meditation to the West. He trained as a monk in Thailand under Ajahn Chah and Mahasi Sayadaw. He co-founded the Insight Meditation Society and Spirit Rock Meditation Center. His books, including A Path with Heart and After the Ecstasy, the Laundry, combine traditional Buddhist wisdom with Western psychology.
