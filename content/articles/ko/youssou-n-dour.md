## 요약

Youssou N'Dour is a Senegalese singer and musician who is one of the most famous African musicians in the world. He pioneered mbalax, a popular music style combining traditional Senegalese drumming with pop and jazz. His song 7 Seconds with Neneh Cherry was a worldwide hit. He has also served as Senegal's Minister of Tourism and Culture.
