## 개요

Giovanni Cassini 이탈리아의-French 천문학자 who discovered four moons of Saturn and the gap in Saturn's rings now known as the Cassini Division. He made accurate measurements of the distances of planets from the Sun and was the first 감독 of the Paris Observatory. NASA's Cassini spacecraft, which explored Saturn from 2004 to 2017, was named in his honor.
