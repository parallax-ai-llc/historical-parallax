## 개요

존 점퍼 led the development of AlphaFold2 at DeepMind, an AI system that accurately predicts the 3D structures of proteins from their amino acid sequences. This breakthrough solved a 50-year grand challenge in biology. He shared the 2024 노벨 화학상 for protein structure prediction.
