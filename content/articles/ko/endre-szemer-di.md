## 개요

Endre Szemerédi is a Hungarian-American 수학자 who proved Szemerédi's theorem, which states that any sufficiently dense subset of the natural numbers contains arbitrarily long arithmetic progressions. He has made fundamental contributions to combinatorics and theoretical computer 과학. He received the Abel Prize in 2012.
