## 개요

Yi Sang was a Korean modernist writer and architect who incorporated mathematical and scientific concepts into his experimental literature. Trained as an architectural engineer, his poetry and fiction featured mathematical symbols, geometric forms, and technical diagrams, creating a unique fusion of science and literature.
