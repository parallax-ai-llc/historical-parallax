## 개요

김정주 founded Nexon, the world's first company to create a graphical massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG). He launched The Kingdom of the Winds in 1996, pioneering the free-to-play business model with microtransactions that became the dominant model in gaming. Nexon became one of the world's largest gaming companies by revenue. He donated most of his fortune to education and nature conservation.
