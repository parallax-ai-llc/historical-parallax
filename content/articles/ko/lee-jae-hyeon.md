## 개요

이재현 is the chairman of CJ Group, 대한민국's leading entertainment, food, and media conglomerate. CJ was originally part of Samsung Group (founded by his grandfather 이병철) before separating in 1993. He built CJ into a powerhouse spanning K-pop (Mnet), film (CJ ENM produced Parasite), food (CJ CheilJedang), and logistics (CJ Logistics). CJ played a central role in globalizing Korean culture.
