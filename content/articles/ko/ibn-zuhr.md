## 개요

Ibn Zuhr (Avenzoar) was an Arab physician and surgeon from Al-Andalus who made important contributions to experimental surgery and clinical medicine. He was the first to describe surgeries including tracheotomy and to provide accurate descriptions of mediastinal tumors. His medical textbook "Kitab al-Taisir" was widely influential in both the Islamic world and medieval Europe.
