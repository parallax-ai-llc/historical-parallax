## 개요

Elvira Nabiullina has served as Governor of the Central Bank of Russia ~ 이래로 2013, becoming the first woman to head the central bank of a G8 nation. She stabilized Russia's financial system ~ 동안 2014-15 ruble crisis and managed 경제 under Western sanctions ~ 후에 2022. She was previously Russia's Minister of Economic Development. Euromoney named her Central Bank Governor of the Year multiple times.
