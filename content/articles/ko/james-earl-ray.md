## 개요
James Earl Ray was the American convicted of assassinating civil rights leader Martin Luther King Jr. on April 4, 1968, in Memphis, Tennessee. He pleaded guilty and was sentenced to 99 years. He later recanted his confession. He died in prison in 1998.
