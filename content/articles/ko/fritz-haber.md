## 개요

Fritz Haber 독일의 화학자 who received the 1918 노벨상 in Chemistry for the synthesis of ammonia from nitrogen and hydrogen gases (Haber-Bosch process). This process enabled mass production of fertilizers and explosives, fundamentally changing agriculture and warfare. He is considered both a benefactor of humanity (feeding billions ~을 통해 synthetic fertilizer) and a 전쟁 criminal (developing chemical weapons used in World 전쟁 I). He is sometimes called the 'father of chemical warfare.'
