## 요약

Indian accountant who was convicted of financing and organizing the 1993 Bombay bombings that killed 257 people and injured over 700. He was the only one hanged for the bombings. His brother Tiger Memon remains at large.

| 1994 | Arrested in Delhi |
| 2007 | Sentenced to death |
| 2015 | Executed by hanging |
