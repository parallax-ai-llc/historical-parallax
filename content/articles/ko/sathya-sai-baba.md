## 요약

Sathya Sai Baba was an Indian guru and philanthropist who claimed to be the reincarnation of Shirdi Sai Baba and attracted millions of followers worldwide. He was known for his reported miracles including materializing objects. He established hospitals, schools, and a massive water project in Andhra Pradesh. He was a highly controversial figure, with devotees regarding him as an avatar and critics accusing him of fraud and misconduct.
