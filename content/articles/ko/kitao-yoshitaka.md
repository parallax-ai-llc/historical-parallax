## 개요

Yoshitaka Kitao is the CEO of SBI Holdings, one of 일본's largest financial services groups. He was previously a top executive at SoftBank and helped build its early internet businesses. SBI Holdings operates online securities, banking, insurance, and asset management services. He has been a vocal proponent of 암호화폐 and 블록체인 technology in 일본ese financial services.
