## 개요

Gregg Semenza 미국의 의사-과학자 who shared the 2019 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for discoveries of how cells sense and adapt to oxygen availability. He discovered hypoxia-inducible factor (HIF-1), the master switch that controls cellular response to low oxygen. His work explains how organisms adapt to changes in oxygen levels.
