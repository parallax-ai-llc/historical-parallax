### What She Is Known For

- Hodgkin was a leading figure in **X-ray crystallography**, applying diffraction methods to solve structures of biologically 중요한 molecules.
- Key achievements often summarized in biographies include:
  - Determining the structure of **penicillin**, helping confirm the molecular basis of an essential antibiotic.
  - Solving the complex structure of **vitamin B12**, a landmark in the field.
  - Long-term work culminating in the structure of **insulin**, relevant to diabetes research.
