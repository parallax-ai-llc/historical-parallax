## 개요
다오카 가즈오 was the third kumicho of the Yamaguchi-gumi. Under his leadership from 1946 to 1981, the organization grew from a small Kobe gang to 일본's largest criminal syndicate with 13,000 members. Known as 'The Bear,' he survived an assassination attempt in 1978.
