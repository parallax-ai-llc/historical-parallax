## 요약

Seo Jung-jin co-founded Celltrion and built it into a global biosimilar pharmaceutical powerhouse. He invested heavily in biopharmaceutical manufacturing when few Korean companies were pursuing biosimilars. Celltrion's success in gaining European approval for biosimilar infliximab opened the global biosimilar market. He is one of South Korea's wealthiest individuals through his Celltrion stake.
