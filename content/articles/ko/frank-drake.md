## 개요

Frank Drake conducted the first systematic search for extraterrestrial intelligence (Project Ozma) in 1960 and formulated the Drake Equation, which estimates the number of communicative civilizations in our galaxy. He also designed the Arecibo Message sent into space in 1974. His work founded the scientific field of SETI.
