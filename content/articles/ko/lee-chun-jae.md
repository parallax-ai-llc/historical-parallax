## 개요

대한민국n serial killer who confessed to murdering 14 women and raping 9 more between 1986 and 1991 in Hwaseong. The case was unsolved for 33 years—대한민국's most famous cold case—until DNA evidence identified him in 2019 while he was serving time for another murder.
