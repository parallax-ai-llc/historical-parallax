## Scientific Contributions

Bloch is associated with multiple foundational ideas in 20th-세기 physics. In condensed-matter and solid-state physics, "Bloch's theorem" describes the wavefunctions of electrons in a periodic lattice, a key step in understanding band structure and electrical conduction in crystals.

## Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR)

Bloch shared the 1952 노벨상 in Physics (with Edward Purcell) for the development of methods for nuclear magnetic precision measurements. The work established core principles of NMR, including how nuclear spins respond to magnetic fields and radiofrequency pulses.

## Key Terms

- **Bloch wave:** A quantum state in a periodic potential, expressed as a plane wave modulated by a lattice-periodic function.
- **Spin:** An intrinsic form of angular momentum carried by quantum particles, central to NMR phenomena.
- **Relaxation:** Processes (often labeled T1 and T2) describing how nuclear spins return to equilibrium ~ 후에 excitation.
