## 요약

Werner Arber is a Swiss microbiologist and geneticist who shared the 1978 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for the discovery of restriction enzymes. He discovered that bacteria use restriction enzymes as a defense against bacteriophages. His work provided fundamental tools for recombinant DNA technology and genetic engineering.
