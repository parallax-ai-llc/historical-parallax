## 개요

콜라 카림 is the CEO of Shoreline Group, one of 나이지리아's largest indigenous energy companies. He built Shoreline from a small startup into a diversified energy, natural resources, and logistics company operating across West 아프리카. He is a member of the Harvard Business School 아프리카n Advisory Board and represents the new generation of 아프리카n energy entrepreneurs building companies that can compete globally.
