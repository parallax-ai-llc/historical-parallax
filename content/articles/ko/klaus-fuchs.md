## 개요

German-born British physicist who passed nuclear weapons secrets to the 소련 while working on the Manhattan Project. His espionage enabled the Soviets to build their first atomic bomb years earlier than expected. Served 9 years.

| 1950 | Arrested and confessed |
| 1950 | Sentenced to 14 years; served 9 |
