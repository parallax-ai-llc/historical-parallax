## 개요

존 도어 is a legendary venture capitalist at Kleiner Perkins who made early investments in Google, 아마존, Netscape, and many other transformative companies. He popularized the OKR (Objectives and Key Results) management framework through his book 'Measure What Matters.' His investments have created companies worth over $1 trillion. He is also a major philanthropist focused on climate change and education.
