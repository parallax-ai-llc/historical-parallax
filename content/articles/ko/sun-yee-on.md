Sun Yee On is commonly described as a major triad society associated with Hong Kong, frequently mentioned in discussions of organized crime networks in the region. “Triad” groups are generally portrayed as having a mix of hierarchical structures, local crews, and shifting alliances.

High-level summaries often attribute involvement in activities such as:

- drug trafficking and distribution,
- extortion and loan sharking,
- illegal gambling and protection rackets,
- counterfeiting and fraud.

### Geography and Influence
