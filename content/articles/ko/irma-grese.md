## 개요

German SS concentration camp guard known as the 'Hyena of Auschwitz' who was one of the most sadistic guards at Auschwitz and Bergen-Belsen. She beat, tortured, and killed prisoners. Executed by hanging at age 22—the youngest woman executed under British law.
