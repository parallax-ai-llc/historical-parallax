## 개요

The Eulsa Treaty (Japan-Korea Protectorate Treaty) of November 17, 1905, stripped Korea of its diplomatic 주권, making it a Japanese protectorate. The treaty was signed under military coercion, and five Korean ministers who signed ~로 알려지게 되었다 the 'Eulsa Five Traitors.'
