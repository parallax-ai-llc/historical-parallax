## 개요

Mohamed Alabbar is the founder of Emaar Properties, the developer behind the Burj Khalifa (world's tallest building) and Dubai Mall (world's largest mall). He built Emaar into one of 세계's largest real estate companies. He also founded Noon.com, the Middle East's largest e-commerce platform to compete with Amazon. His projects have defined Dubai's 현대의 skyline and positioning as a global city.
