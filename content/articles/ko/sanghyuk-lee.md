## 요약

Sanghyuk Lee is a Korean bioinformatician who has made significant contributions to genomics and bioinformatics research in South Korea. His work on genome analysis and computational biology has helped establish Korea's capabilities in precision medicine and genomic research, contributing to projects like the Korean reference genome.
