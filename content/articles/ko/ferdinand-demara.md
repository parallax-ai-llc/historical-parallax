## Pattern of impersonation (overview)

Demara became notorious for adopting professional identities he was not formally qualified to hold.

Accounts emphasize a repeatable method:

- Obtaining access to institutions by exploiting gaps in credential verification
- Studying reference materials quickly to imitate expected professional competence
- Moving on when scrutiny increased or exposure became likely

## Korean War incident (high level)

One of the most cited episodes involves Demara serving aboard a naval vessel as a medical officer under an assumed identity.
