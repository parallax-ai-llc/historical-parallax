## 개요

가가와 도요히코 was a 일본ese Christian reformer who dedicated his life to serving the poor and advocating for social justice. He lived in the slums of Kobe, organized cooperatives and labor unions, and was arrested for his peace activism during 제2차 세계대전. He was nominated for the 노벨 평화상 multiple times and was one of the most internationally recognized 일본ese Christians of the 20th century.
