## 요약

Shin Kyuk-ho (Takeo Shigemitsu) was a Korean-born Japanese industrialist who founded the Lotte Group. Starting with a chewing gum company in Japan, he applied food science and chemical engineering to build one of Asia's largest conglomerates spanning confectionery, beverages, chemicals, and construction.
