## 요약
Viktor Fedorovych Yanukovych was the fourth President of Ukraine from 2010 to 2014. He was ousted by the Euromaidan revolution after he rejected an EU association agreement in favor of closer ties with Russia. His lavish estate 'Mezhyhirya' became a symbol of corruption. He fled to Russia.
