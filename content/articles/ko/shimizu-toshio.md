## 요약

Shimizu Toshio was a Japanese architectural engineer who developed base isolation systems for earthquake-resistant buildings. His rubber-bearing base isolation technology allows buildings to survive major earthquakes with minimal damage. Japan's widespread adoption of his technology has saved countless lives and structures.
