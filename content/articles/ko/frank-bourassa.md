## 개요

Canadian counterfeiter who produced $250 million in fake US currency—the largest counterfeiting operation in history. His bills were nearly undetectable. He surrendered most of the counterfeit money in a deal and served only 6 weeks.

## Operation Details (High Level)

- Bourassa’s counterfeiting operation was 주목할 만한 for its scale and the quality of the notes, which were designed to pass routine detection.
- The case became widely cited in discussions of currency security, including how law enforcement balances recovery of counterfeit stock with prosecution.
- His relatively short custodial sentence is often explained in reporting as connected to cooperation and the recovery of a large portion of the counterfeit bills.

## 영향

The incident underscored that sophisticated counterfeiting can be driven by small groups rather than large criminal syndicates, and it highlighted the practical challenges of tracing distribution networks once high-quality notes enter circulation.

## Key Takeaways

- Counterfeiting investigations often focus on production capabilities as well as distribution channels.
- Recovering counterfeit inventory can be a law-enforcement priority ~ 때문에 unrecovered notes continue to circulate.
- High-profile cases can influence how 대중 understands (and sometimes overestimates) the prevalence of counterfeit currency.
