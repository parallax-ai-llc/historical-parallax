## 개요

로티 문 was an American Southern Baptist missionary who spent nearly 40 years in 중국. She is one of the most celebrated missionaries in Baptist history, and the annual 로티 문 Christmas Offering for international missions bears her name. She advocated for women's equality in missions and challenged the Baptist mission board. She died of starvation-related illness during a famine, having given away her food and money.
