## 개요

Elijah McCoy invented the automatic lubricator for steam engines, which allowed trains to be lubricated ~하는 동안 running rather than stopping. His inventions were so reliable that buyers would ask for 'the real McCoy' to distinguish his products from inferior imitations — the likely origin of the 유명한 phrase. He held 57 patents over his career.
