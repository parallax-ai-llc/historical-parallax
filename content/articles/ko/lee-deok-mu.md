## 개요

이덕무 was a 조선 왕조 scholar who compiled Cheongjanggwan Jeonseo, an encyclopedic work covering science, technology, and daily life observations. His methodical documentation of practical knowledge represented the empirical turn in Korean scholarship during the Silhak period.
