## 요약

Seo Taiji is a Korean musician widely regarded as the godfather of K-pop. His group Seo Taiji and Boys revolutionized Korean popular music in 1992 by fusing American hip-hop, rock, and techno with Korean lyrics. He challenged censorship, changed Korean music industry practices, and directly inspired the creation of modern K-pop entertainment companies.
