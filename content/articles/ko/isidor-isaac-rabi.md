## 개요

Isidor Isaac Rabi was an Austrian-born American physicist who won the 1944 Nobel Prize in Physics for his resonance method for recording the magnetic properties of atomic nuclei. His molecular beam magnetic resonance technique laid the foundation for nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) and MRI technology. He was instrumental in the founding of CERN and Brookhaven National Laboratory. Rabi was also an important science advisor to the US government.
