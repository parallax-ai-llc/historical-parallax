## 개요
호세 산타크루스 론도뇨 was a 콜롬비아n drug lord and a leader of the Cali Cartel. Known as 'Chepe,' he oversaw the cartel's operations in 뉴욕 City. He was arrested in 1995, escaped from prison in 1996, and was killed by 콜롬비아n police weeks later.
