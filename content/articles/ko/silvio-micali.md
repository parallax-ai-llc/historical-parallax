## 요약

Silvio Micali is an Italian-American computer scientist who has made fundamental contributions to cryptography and complexity theory. He co-invented zero-knowledge proofs with Shafi Goldwasser and developed verifiable random functions. He also founded Algorand, a blockchain platform. Micali shared the Turing Award in 2012 with Goldwasser for their work on cryptography.
