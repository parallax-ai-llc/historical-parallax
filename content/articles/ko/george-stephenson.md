## 개요

George Stephenson 영국의 civil and mechanical 공학자 known as the 'Father of Railways.' He built the first public railway to use steam locomotives, the Stockton and Darlington Railway, and the Liverpool and Manchester Railway. His locomotive 'Rocket' established the standard for steam locomotives. The 4 feet 8.5 inches standard gauge used by most railways worldwide is based on Stephenson's gauge.
