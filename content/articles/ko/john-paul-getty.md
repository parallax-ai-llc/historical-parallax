## 개요

J. Paul Getty was an American-British oil magnate who founded the Getty Oil Company and was named the richest living American by Fortune magazine in 1957. He built his fortune through strategic oil investments in the 중동 and Oklahoma. Known for his extreme frugality despite enormous wealth, he initially refused to pay ransom when his grandson was kidnapped. He spent his later years at Sutton Place, his English manor, and founded the J. Paul Getty Museum.
