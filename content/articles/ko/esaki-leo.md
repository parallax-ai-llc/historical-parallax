## 개요

Esaki Leo (Esaki Reona) 일본의 물리학자 who discovered quantum tunneling in semiconductor junctions, leading to the invention of the tunnel diode (Esaki diode). He shared the 1973 노벨상 in Physics for this discovery. His work was foundational for 현대의 semiconductor electronics and quantum devices.
