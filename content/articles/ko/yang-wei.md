## 요약

Yang Wei is a Chinese mechanical engineer who has made significant contributions to fracture mechanics and computational solid mechanics. He served as president of Zhejiang University and has been influential in Chinese engineering education reform. His research on damage mechanics has applications in aerospace and infrastructure safety.
