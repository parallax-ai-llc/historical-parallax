## 요약

Tanaka Hisashige was a Japanese inventor and engineer known as the 'Thomas Edison of Japan.' He created remarkable mechanical dolls (karakuri), the perpetual clock (mannen-dokei), and Japan's first domestically produced steam engine. He founded the company that would become Toshiba Corporation. His inventions demonstrated extraordinary mechanical genius.
