## 요약

Tan Sri Vincent Tan is a Malaysian Chinese billionaire and founder of Berjaya Corporation, a diversified conglomerate spanning gaming, real estate, food, and technology. He operates 7-Eleven franchises in Malaysia and owns Cardiff City F.C. He also controls Sports Toto, Malaysia's largest lottery company. His business empire extends across Malaysia, the Philippines, Vietnam, and the United Kingdom.
