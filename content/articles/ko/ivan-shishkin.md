## 개요

Ivan Shishkin was one of the most celebrated Russian landscape painters, known for his meticulous, photorealistic paintings of Russian forests. His iconic Morning in a Pine Forest is one of the most popular paintings in Russian art. His detailed depictions of Russian nature earned him the nickname 'the singer of the forest.'
