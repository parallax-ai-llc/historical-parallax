## 개요

Former governor of Delta State in Nigeria who was convicted in London of stealing $250 million from his state's treasury. He laundered the money through London properties and South African investments.

| 2012 | Convicted in London of money laundering and fraud |
| 2012 | Sentenced to 13 years in UK prison |
