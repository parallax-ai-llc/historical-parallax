## 개요

구인회 founded LG Group (originally Lucky Chemical), building it into one of 대한민국's largest conglomerates. He started with a cosmetics cream called 'Lucky Cream' and expanded into plastics, electronics, and chemicals. He founded GoldStar (later LG Electronics) in 1958, producing 대한민국's first radio. His vision of combining chemicals and electronics under one corporate umbrella created the Lucky-GoldStar (LG) conglomerate.
