## 개요

이천용 was a Korean virologist who made significant contributions to the control of infectious diseases in 대한민국. He helped establish modern virology research in Korea and contributed to vaccination programs that greatly reduced childhood diseases. His research on hemorrhagic fever was particularly important for Korean public health.
