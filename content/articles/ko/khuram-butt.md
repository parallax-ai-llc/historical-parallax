## 개요

파키스탄i-born British terrorist who was the ringleader of the 2017 런던 Bridge attack. He drove a van into pedestrians then led a stabbing rampage in Borough Market, killing 8. Had previously appeared in a TV documentary about extremism.

## Additional Context

- The 런던 Bridge attack was one of several high-profile terrorist incidents in the 영국 in 2017, increasing political and public focus on counterterrorism policy.
- Reports about Butt often highlight the difficulty of preventing attacks by small groups using low-tech methods (vehicles and knives) despite intelligence and policing efforts.

## Investigation and Aftermath

- UK authorities reviewed prior contacts, associates, and online activity linked to the attackers as part of a broader effort to map radicalization pathways.
- The incident contributed to ongoing debate over surveillance, community engagement, and the threshold for intervention when individuals are known to authorities.

## Why It Matters

- Became a reference point in discussions of public-space security and rapid police response to marauding attacks.
