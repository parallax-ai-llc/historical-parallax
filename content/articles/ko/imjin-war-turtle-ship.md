## 개요

The Imjin War (1592-1598) was Japan's invasion of Korea under Toyotomi Hideyoshi. Admiral Yi Sun-sin's turtle ships (geobukseon) and guerrilla warfare turned the tide. Ming Chinese aid helped Korea survive the devastating seven-year war.
