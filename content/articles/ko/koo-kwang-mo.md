## 개요

구광모 became chairman of LG Group in 2018, representing the fourth generation of the founding Koo family. He has led LG's transformation toward electric vehicle batteries, AI, and sustainability. Under his leadership, LG Energy Solution became the world's second-largest EV battery manufacturer, and LG Display pioneered OLED technology. He adopted a younger, more innovative management style compared to his predecessors.
