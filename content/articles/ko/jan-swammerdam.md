## 개요

Jan Swammerdam was a pioneering microscopist who made the first detailed observations of insect anatomy and metamorphosis. He disproved the theory of spontaneous generation for insects and demonstrated that insect development follows consistent patterns. His masterwork Bybel der Natuure was published posthumously and established the study of insect biology.
