## 개요
Jeffrey Keith Skilling was the CEO of Enron Corporation who helped orchestrate the company's massive accounting fraud. Enron's collapse in 2001 was the largest corporate bankruptcy in American history at the time. He was convicted of securities fraud and sentenced to 24 years, later reduced.
