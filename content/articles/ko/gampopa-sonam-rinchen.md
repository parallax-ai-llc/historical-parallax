## 개요

Gampopa was the most 중요한 student of Milarepa and the founder of the Kagyu school as a monastic institution. He combined the scholarly Kadampa tradition with the tantric Mahamudra instructions of Milarepa. His Jewel Ornament of Liberation is one of the most comprehensive guides to the Buddhist path. He established Daklha Gampo monastery, from which all major Kagyu sub-schools originated.
