## 개요

Gampopa was a Tibetan Buddhist 수도승 and 의사 who combined the monastic Kadampa tradition with the tantric Mahamudra teachings of Milarepa. He founded the institutional structure of the Kagyu school. His Jewel Ornament of Liberation is one of the most 중요한 lamrim (stages of the path) texts in Tibetan Buddhism. He established Daklha Gampo Monastery, from which the four major and eight minor Kagyu sub-schools emerged.
