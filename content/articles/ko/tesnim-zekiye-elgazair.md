## 요약

Tesnim Zekiye Elgazair is an Algerian-American nuclear physicist who has contributed to nuclear forensics and nonproliferation research. She has worked at Los Alamos National Laboratory on detecting nuclear materials and understanding nuclear reactions. She represents growing African contributions to advanced nuclear science.
