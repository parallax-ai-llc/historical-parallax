## 개요

Farid ud-Din Attar was a Persian Sufi 시인 and hagiographer whose Conference of the Birds (Mantiq al-Tayr) is one of the masterpieces of Sufi 문학. His Memorial of the Saints (Tadhkirat al-Awliya) compiled biographies of Sufi saints. He wrote over 30,000 couplets of mystical poetry. He reportedly died ~ 동안 Mongol invasion of Nishapur and profoundly influenced Rumi and later Persian poets.
