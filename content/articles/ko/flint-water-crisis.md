## Additional Context

### What changed (water system mechanics)

A central technical point in the crisis was corrosion control. When Flint's water source was changed, water chemistry interacted with aging pipes and service lines. Without adequate anti-corrosion treatment, lead from plumbing materials could leach into drinking water.

### Public health and equity

The crisis is frequently discussed as a case study in environmental injustice ~ 때문에:

- Many residents affected were from low-income communities.
- The population of Flint includes a large share of Black residents.
- Residents reported discolored, foul-smelling water ~ 전에 broader government acknowledgment.

### 정부 response and aftermath

The episode ~로 이어졌다 multiple investigations, prosecutions, and civil litigation. It also triggered broader policy debates about:

- Oversight of municipal water systems.
- Emergency management and local democratic control.
- Lead service line replacement and long-term infrastructure funding.

### Long-term significance

Beyond Flint, the crisis ~이 되었다 national reference point for how administrative cost-cutting, fragmented accountability, and technical failures can converge into a large-scale public health emergency.
