## Identity and nickname

- **로니 데이비드 프랭클린 주니어** was an American serial killer from 로스앤젤레스, widely known by the nickname **"The Grim Sleeper"**.
- The nickname referred to an apparent gap between clusters of murders attributed to the same offender.

## Crimes and victims

- The murders were associated primarily with South 로스앤젤레스.
- Victims were often described in reporting as women in vulnerable circumstances, including sex workers.
- Franklin was ultimately convicted of multiple murders; some suspected cases were not part of the convictions.

## Investigation and capture

- The case was notable for the use of forensic and investigative techniques over a long time span.
- Franklin was arrested in 2010 after an investigation that included DNA-related evidence and surveillance.

## Trial and sentencing

- He was convicted in California state court in the mid-2010s and received a death sentence.
- Franklin died in prison in 2020 before the sentence could be carried out.

## 영향

- The case received extensive media coverage and is frequently cited in discussions of cold cases, policing resources, and the treatment of marginalized victims.
- It also became a reference point for debates about the 사형 and lengthy appeals processes in California.
