## 개요

Dilip Shanghvi founded Sun Pharmaceutical Industries, India's largest pharmaceutical company by market capitalization. He started with a borrowed $1,000 and five psychiatric drugs. Sun Pharma grew into a global generic drug powerhouse ~을 통해 aggressive acquisitions, including the $4 billion takeover of Ranbaxy in 2015. He was briefly India's richest person. Sun Pharma supplies generic medications to patients worldwide.
