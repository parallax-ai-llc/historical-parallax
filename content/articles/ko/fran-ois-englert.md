## 개요

François Englert is a Belgian theoretical 물리학자 who shared the 2013 노벨상 in Physics with Peter Higgs for the theoretical discovery of the mechanism that contributes to our understanding of mass of subatomic particles. He independently proposed the Brout-Englert-Higgs mechanism in 1964 with Robert Brout. His work is fundamental to the Standard Model of particle physics.
