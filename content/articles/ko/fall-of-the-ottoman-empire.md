## 개요

The Ottoman Empire collapsed ~ 후에 WWI defeat. The Armistice of Mudros (1918) and subsequent occupation ~로 이어졌다 Mustafa Kemal's nationalist resistance. The sultanate was abolished November 1, 1922, ending 623 years of Ottoman rule.
