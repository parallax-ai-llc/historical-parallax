## 요약

Ho Ching served as CEO of Temasek Holdings, Singapore's sovereign wealth fund, from 2004 to 2021. Under her leadership, Temasek's portfolio grew from S$90 billion to S$381 billion. She oversaw major investments in technology, healthcare, and financial services globally. She is the wife of Singapore PM Lee Hsien Loong. Her tenure transformed Temasek from a domestic holding company into one of the world's most influential institutional investors.
