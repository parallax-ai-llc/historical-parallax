## 개요

Gertrude Elion 미국의 생화학자 and pharmacologist who shared the 1988 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for developing drugs using 혁신적인 methods of rational drug design. She developed the first effective drugs for leukemia, herpes, malaria, and organ transplant rejection (azathioprine). ~에도 불구하고 never receiving a PhD, she was one of the most productive drug developers in history. Her approach to drug design revolutionized the pharmaceutical industry.
