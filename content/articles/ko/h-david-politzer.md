## 개요

H. David Politzer is an American theoretical physicist who shared the 2004 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of asymptotic freedom in quantum chromodynamics. He independently discovered asymptotic freedom at the same time as Gross and Wilczek, establishing it as a robust prediction of gauge theories.
