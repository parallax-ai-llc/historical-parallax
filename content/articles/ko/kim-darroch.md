## 배경

- Full name: Nigel 킴 대록.
- British career diplomat who served in senior UK foreign policy and national security roles.
- Later became a life peer (House of Lords) as Baron Darroch of Kew.

## Ambassador to the 미국 (2016–2019)

- Served as the UK’s ambassador in 워싱턴 during the Obama and Trump administrations.
- Role focuses on representing UK interests, maintaining bilateral relations, and reporting political analysis back to 런던.

## 2019 Cable Leak and Resignation

- In July 2019, private diplomatic cables attributed to Darroch were leaked to the media.
- The leaked assessments were highly critical of the Trump administration and created a public diplomatic dispute.
- Darroch resigned shortly after the leak, citing the situation as making it difficult to continue effectively.

## Significance

- The episode highlighted how confidential diplomatic reporting can become politically explosive if disclosed publicly.
- It also became a prominent example of the tensions between candid internal analysis and public-facing diplomacy.

## Context: Diplomatic Reporting and Confidentiality

- Ambassadors routinely send candid assessments to their foreign ministries; such reporting is intended for internal decision-making.
- Leaks can undermine trust between governments and reduce the willingness of officials to speak frankly through official channels.

## After the 2019 Resignation (High-Level)

- Darroch’s departure became a major UK political story during a period of leadership transition.
- He later entered the House of Lords as a life peer, continuing public service in a legislative capacity.
