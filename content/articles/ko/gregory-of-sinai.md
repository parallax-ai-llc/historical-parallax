## 개요

Gregory of Sinai was a Byzantine 수도승 who was one of the most 중요한 figures in the Hesychast movement. He traveled to Mount Sinai, Palestine, Crete, and Mount Athos, and finally settled in Bulgaria, where he founded the Paroria monastery. He systematized the practice of Hesychasm — the contemplative prayer tradition of the Jesus Prayer and the seeking of inner stillness.
