## 요약

Thomas Henry Huxley was a British biologist who was the chief advocate for Darwin's theory of evolution and became known as 'Darwin's Bulldog.' He was a skilled comparative anatomist and debated Bishop Wilberforce in the famous 1860 Oxford evolution debate. He coined the term 'agnostic' and reformed British science education.
