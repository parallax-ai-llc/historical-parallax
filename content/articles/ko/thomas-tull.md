## 요약

Thomas Tull founded Legendary Entertainment, the production company behind blockbuster films including The Dark Knight trilogy, Inception, Godzilla, and Jurassic World. He pioneered the co-financing model in Hollywood, partnering with Warner Bros. to fund major productions. He sold Legendary to China's Dalian Wanda Group for $3.5 billion in 2016. He also owns a stake in the Pittsburgh Steelers.
