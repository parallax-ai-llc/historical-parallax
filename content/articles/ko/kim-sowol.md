## 개요

김소월 was one of Korea's most beloved poets, known for his lyrical verses that capture the beauty and sorrow of the Korean soul. His poem Azaleas (Jindalraekot) is the most famous poem in Korean literature. Drawing on traditional Korean folk songs and the rhythms of the Korean language, his poetry is memorized by nearly every Korean.
