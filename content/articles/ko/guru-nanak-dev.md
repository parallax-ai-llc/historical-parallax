## 개요

Guru Nanak Dev was the founder of Sikhism and the first of the ten Sikh Gurus. He proclaimed 'Ik Onkar' (There is One God) and traveled extensively sharing his message of divine unity, social equality, and honest living. His hymns form the core of the Guru Granth Sahib. He established the practices of langar (communal kitchen) and sangat (congregation) that remain central to Sikhism.
