## 개요

Hazama Shigetomi was a Japanese merchant and astronomer from Osaka who was a student of Asada Goryu. He built precise astronomical instruments and conducted detailed observations of eclipses and planetary movements. His observations were used to reform the Japanese calendar (Kansei calendar) and he helped modernize Japanese astronomy.
