## 요약

Zhaozhou Congshen was a Chinese Chan (Zen) master known for his profound yet simple teaching style. His koan 'Does a dog have Buddha-nature?' (Zhaozhou's Dog / Mu) became the most famous koan in Zen Buddhism, used as the first koan in the Mumonkan. He was known for his wit, directness, and ability to turn ordinary situations into teaching moments. He lived to approximately 120 years of age.
