## 개요

George Ivanovich Gurdjieff was an Armenian-Greek 신비주의자, 철학자, and 교사 who founded the Fourth Way tradition of spiritual development. He taught that most humans live in a state of 'waking sleep' and need special practices to develop higher consciousness. He established the Institute for the Harmonious Development of Man near Paris. His ideas, presented ~을 통해 his writings, sacred dances (movements), and 음악, influenced many subsequent spiritual movements.
