## 개요

카를 란트슈타이너 was an 오스트리아n-American biologist and physician who won the 1930 노벨상 in Physiology or Medicine for his discovery of human blood groups (A, B, AB, O). His discovery made safe blood transfusions possible and saved countless lives. He also co-discovered the Rhesus factor (Rh factor) in blood. Landsteiner's work is considered one of the most important in the history of medicine.
