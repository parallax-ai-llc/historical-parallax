## 개요

쿠시아르 길라니 was a Persian mathematician and astronomer who wrote influential texts on arithmetic and astronomy. His Principles of Hindu Reckoning helped transmit the Hindu-Arabic numeral system throughout the 이슬람ic world and into 유럽. He also compiled astronomical tables (zij) and advanced trigonometric calculations.
