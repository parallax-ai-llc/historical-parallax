## 개요

Ernest Lawrence 미국의 nuclear 물리학자 who invented the cyclotron, the first particle accelerator. He won the 1932 노벨상 in Physics for this invention which became essential for nuclear physics research. Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and element 103 (lawrencium) are named in his honor.
