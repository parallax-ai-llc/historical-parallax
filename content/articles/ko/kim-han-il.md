## 개요

김한일 co-founded NHN (now Naver Corporation) and served as its CEO, building Naver into 대한민국's dominant internet search and content platform. Under his leadership, Naver expanded into webtoons, cloud computing, and AI. NHN later split into Naver (search and content) and NHN Entertainment (gaming). He played a key role in establishing 대한민국's digital economy infrastructure.
