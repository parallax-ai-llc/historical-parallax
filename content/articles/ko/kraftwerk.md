## 개요

Ralf Hutter is the co-founder of 크라프트베르크 the German electronic music band that pioneered synth-pop and electronic dance music. Their albums Autobahn Trans-유럽 Express and The Man-Machine influenced virtually every genre of electronic music. 크라프트베르크 are considered the most important electronic music group in history.
