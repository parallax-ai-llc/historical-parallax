## 개요

John Fredriksen is a Norwegian-born Cypriot billionaire and the world's largest shipowner. He controls Frontline, Golden Ocean, and Seadrill, among other maritime companies. He is the largest individual owner of oil tankers and has significant interests in fish farming (Mowi, formerly Marine Harvest). He gave up his Norwegian citizenship for Cypriot citizenship, reportedly for tax reasons. He is one of the wealthiest people in the shipping industry.
