## 개요

솔로몬 왕 was the third king of 이스라엘, son of David, renowned for his wisdom, wealth, and building the First Temple in Jerusalem. He is traditionally credited with authoring Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and the Song of Solomon. His reign represented the golden age of the united 이스라엘ite monarchy. His Temple became the center of Jewish worship and identity for centuries.
