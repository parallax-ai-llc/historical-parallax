## 개요

Ernst Ludwig Kirchner co-founded Die Brucke, one of the seminal groups of 현대의 예술. His jagged, vibrant paintings of Berlin street life capture pre-전쟁 Germany's nervous energy. His bold woodcuts revived the medium as a major 예술 form.
