## 개요
Juan García Ábrego was the leader of the Gulf Cartel and the first drug lord to appear on the FBI's Ten Most Wanted list. He was captured in 1996 and convicted of drug trafficking, money laundering, and murder. He was sentenced to 11 consecutive life sentences.
