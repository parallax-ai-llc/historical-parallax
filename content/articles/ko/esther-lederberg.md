## 개요

Esther Lederberg discovered the lambda bacteriophage and developed replica plating, a technique fundamental to microbiology and genetics. Her method of transferring bacterial colonies enabled the study of antibiotic resistance. ~에도 불구하고 her crucial contributions, she received little public recognition ~ 동안 her lifetime ~하는 동안 her husband Joshua Lederberg won the 노벨상.
