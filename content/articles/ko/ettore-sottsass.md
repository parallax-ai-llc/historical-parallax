## 개요

Ettore Sottsass 이탈리아의 디자이너 and 건축가 who founded the Memphis Group in 1981, revolutionizing postmodern design. His colorful, irreverent furniture and objects challenged the austerity of modernist design. His typewriter design for Olivetti and his Memphis pieces are icons of design history.
