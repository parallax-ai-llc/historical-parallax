## 개요

Edwin Hubble 미국의 천문학자 who 에서 결정적인 역할을 했다 establishing the field of extragalactic astronomy. He proved that many objects classified as 'nebulae' were actually galaxies beyond the Milky Way, fundamentally changing our understanding of the universe. Hubble's law, which describes the expanding universe, provided key observational evidence for the Big Bang theory. The Hubble Space Telescope is named in his honor.
