## 개요

Carlos Lehder, Colombian drug lord and co-founder of the Medellín Cartel who pioneered the use of small aircraft and a private island in the Bahamas to smuggle cocaine. He was the first Colombian drug lord extradited to the US.

## Smuggling Model (High Level)

Lehder is often described as an innovator of an aviation-centered trafficking model. The basic logic was to:

- Use small aircraft to move shipments in stages
- Rely on intermediate “hub” locations to reduce direct exposure
- Scale operations by treating routes, pilots, and logistics as repeatable assets

The Norman’s Cay operation is frequently cited as a key example of this hub-and-spoke approach.
