## 요약

Yamamoto Gempo Roshi was a Japanese Rinzai Zen master who served as abbot of major Rinzai monasteries and was one of the most respected Zen figures of the 20th century. He was known for his powerful personality and his efforts to maintain rigorous Zen training standards. He also helped rehabilitate former convicts through Zen practice. He mentored several important Zen teachers who later taught in the West.
