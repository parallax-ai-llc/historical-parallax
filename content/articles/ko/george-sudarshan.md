## 개요

E.C. George Sudarshan 인도의-American theoretical 물리학자 who made 선구적인 contributions to quantum optics, quantum field theory, and particle physics. He co-developed the V-A theory of weak interactions and the Sudarshan-Glauber representation in quantum optics. Many believed he deserved the 노벨상 for his quantum optics work.
