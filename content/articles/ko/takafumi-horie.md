## 추가 맥락

### Livedoor and Japan’s dot-com era

Horie rose to prominence during Japan’s early-2000s internet boom. Livedoor expanded through aggressive acquisitions and became a high-profile symbol of a new, media-savvy generation of entrepreneurs operating outside traditional corporate norms.

### Securities-fraud case (high level)

The Livedoor case centered on allegations of misleading investors through accounting and disclosure practices tied to acquisitions and corporate structuring. Horie’s conviction was widely covered in Japan and became a reference point in debates about corporate governance, market transparency, and enforcement against high-growth technology firms.

### Public role after incarceration

After serving his sentence, Horie remained a public figure, commenting on business and technology and engaging in new ventures. In Japanese media, he is often discussed as a polarizing figure—admired by some for challenging convention and criticized by others for the scandal that ended Livedoor’s rise.
