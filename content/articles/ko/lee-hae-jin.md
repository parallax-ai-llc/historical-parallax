## 개요

이해진 is a Korean computer engineer who founded Naver Corporation, 대한민국's largest internet company and search engine. His engineering of the Korean-language search engine and internet portal created a platform that dominates Korean internet usage. Naver's Line messaging app also became the dominant messenger in 일본, 대만, and 태국.
