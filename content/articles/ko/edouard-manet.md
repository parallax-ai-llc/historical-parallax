> "It is not enough to know your craft - you have to have feeling. Science is all very well, but for us imagination is worth far more." [^141]

Manet here balances technical mastery with artistic sensibility, arguing that true 예술 requires more than mere technical competence.

> "No one can be a painter unless he cares for painting above all else." [^142]

This statement reflects Manet's total dedication to his 예술, ~에도 불구하고 the professional and personal costs of his unconventional approach.
