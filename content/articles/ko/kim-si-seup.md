## 개요

김시습 was a 조선 왕조 polymath known for his literary works and scientific curiosity. He was one of the Saeng Yuksin (Six Martyred Ministers) loyal to King Danjong. He wrote about natural phenomena, astronomical observations, and practical technologies. His Geumo Sinhwa is considered the first Korean novel.
