## 개요

라히리 마하사야 was an 인도n yogi and teacher who was a key figure in the revival of Kriya Yoga in modern 인도. He was initiated into Kriya Yoga by his guru Babaji in the 히말라야. Unusually for a great yogi, he was a householder — a married man with a career as an accountant. He taught that spiritual practice could be integrated with ordinary family and working life. He was the guru of Sri Yukteswar, who in turn taught Paramahansa Yogananda.
