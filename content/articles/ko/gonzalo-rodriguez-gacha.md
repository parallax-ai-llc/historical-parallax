## 개요
Gonzalo Rodríguez Gacha, known as 'El Mexicano,' was a Colombian drug lord and leading member of the Medellín Cartel. He was one of the wealthiest and most violent drug traffickers in Colombian history, controlling emerald mining and cocaine trafficking routes. He was killed in a police operation in 1989.

## 초기 생애
Born May 14, 1947, in Pacho, Cundinamarca, Colombia. He came from humble origins and was originally involved in the emerald trade ~ 전에 moving into cocaine. He earned his nickname 'El Mexicano' for his love of Mexican culture. [^1]

## 범죄 경력

## 체포 / Downfall
On December 15, 1989, Colombian police tracked Rodríguez Gacha and his son to a ranch in Tolú, on Colombia's Caribbean coast. In the ensuing operation, both were killed by police gunfire. His death was considered a major victory in 전쟁 against the Medellín Cartel. [^3]
