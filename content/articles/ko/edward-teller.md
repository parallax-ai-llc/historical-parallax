## 개요

Edward Teller was a Hungarian-American theoretical 물리학자 known as the 'father of the hydrogen bomb.' He made contributions to nuclear and molecular physics. His advocacy for nuclear weapons and testimony against Oppenheimer made him 논란이 많은.
