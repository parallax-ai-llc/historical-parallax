## 개요

Jerome was a Christian priest, theologian, and historian, best known for translating the Bible into Latin (the Vulgate). His translation became the standard Bible of the Western Church for over a thousand years. He was a prolific writer of letters, commentaries, and polemical works. He spent his later years in a monastery in Bethlehem devoted to scholarship.
