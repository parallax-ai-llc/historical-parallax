## 개요

카지미에시 쿠라토프스키 was a Polish mathematician who made important contributions to topology and set theory. He is known for Kuratowski's theorem on planar graphs, the Kuratowski closure axioms in topology, and the Zorn-Kuratowski lemma. He was a key figure in the Polish school of mathematics and served as president of the Polish Mathematical Society.
