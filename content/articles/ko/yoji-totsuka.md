## 요약

Yoji Totsuka was a Japanese physicist who led the Super-Kamiokande experiment that discovered neutrino oscillations in 1998, proving that neutrinos have mass. His discovery required revision of the Standard Model of particle physics. He served as director of KEK (High Energy Accelerator Research Organization) but died before the 2015 Nobel Prize was awarded for this work.
