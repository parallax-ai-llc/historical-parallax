## 개요

Yayoi Kusama is a 일본ese artist known for her obsessive polka dots, infinity rooms, and pumpkin sculptures. She pioneered aspects of Pop Art and environmental art in 뉴욕 before returning to 일본. Now in her 90s, she is one of the world's most popular living artists.
