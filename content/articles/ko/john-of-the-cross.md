## 개요

십자가의 요한 was a Spanish mystic, Carmelite friar, and poet who co-founded the Discalced Carmelites with Teresa of Ávila. His poetry, including Dark Night of the Soul, is considered among the greatest in the Spanish language. His concept of the 'dark night' — spiritual purification through suffering — became central to Christian mysticism. He was named a Doctor of the Church in 1926.
