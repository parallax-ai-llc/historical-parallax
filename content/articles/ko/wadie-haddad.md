## 요약

Palestinian militant who masterminded numerous PFLP airplane hijackings and the 1976 Entebbe hostage crisis. He organized some of the most spectacular terrorist attacks of the 1970s.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1968 | Began organizing PFLP airline hijackings |
| 1970 | Masterminded Dawson's Field hijackings |
| 1976 | Planned Air France hijacking to Entebbe |
| 1978 | Died of poisoning, reportedly by Mossad |
