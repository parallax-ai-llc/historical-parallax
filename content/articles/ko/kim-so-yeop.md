## 개요

김소엽 is a Korean pharmacognosist who has scientifically studied the bioactive compounds in Korean traditional medicinal plants. His research on ginseng, Angelica, and other Korean herbs has helped validate traditional remedies through modern pharmacological methods, bridging Korean traditional medicine and modern pharmaceutical science.
