## Expanded Notes (High-Level)

### 배경

- Ethan Nordean (born 1990 or 1991) 미국의 far-right political 활동가.
- He has also used the name **"Rufio Panman"**.
- He is known as a recruiter and leader associated with the **Proud Boys**.

### Public Activity (Pre–January 6)

- In mid-2017, he began attending far-right rallies in the Pacific Northwest, including events connected to **Patriot Prayer**.
- Reporting and video evidence referenced in public sources describe his presence at multiple street confrontations at rallies in **Seattle** and **Portland**.

### January 6, 2021 (U.S. Capitol Attack)

- He played a 저명한 role in events surrounding the January 6 attack on the U.S. Capitol.
- He was arrested several weeks ~ 후에 January 6 on federal charges.

### Federal Case (Overview)

- In March 2021, a federal grand jury indictment included conspiracy-related charges connected to January 6.
- In June 2022, prosecutors added **seditious conspiracy** charges against him and other Proud Boys leaders.
- In May 2023, he was found guilty of **seditious conspiracy** and additional felonies.
- On **September 1, 2023**, he was sentenced to **18 years** in prison.
- On **January 20, 2025**, his sentence was **commuted to time served** by 대통령 Donald Trump.

### Why This Matters (Context)

- The Proud Boys leaders' seditious conspiracy convictions were among the highest-profile outcomes of the January 6 prosecutions.
- Nordean's case is frequently cited in discussions about extremist group coordination, online/offline mobilization, and the legal boundaries of political 폭력.
