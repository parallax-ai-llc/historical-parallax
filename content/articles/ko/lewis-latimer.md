## 개요

루이스 래티머 was an 아프리카n American inventor who patented an improved process for manufacturing carbon filaments for light bulbs, making electric lighting affordable and practical. He drafted the patent drawings for Alexander Graham Bell's telephone and worked directly with Thomas Edison. He was the only 아프리카n American member of the Edison Pioneers.
