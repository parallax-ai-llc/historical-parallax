## 개요

Nadiem Makarim co-founded Gojek, Indonesia's first decacorn super-app that started as a motorcycle ride-hailing service and grew into a platform offering food delivery, payments (GoPay), and more. Gojek merged with Tokopedia to form GoTo Group, which went public as Indonesia's largest tech IPO. He was appointed Indonesia's Minister of 교육 in 2019, leaving 회사 at age 35.
