## 요약

Yu Geum was a late Goryeo Dynasty astronomer who worked on calendar reform and astronomical observations. He helped create improved calendrical systems that more accurately tracked solar and lunar cycles. His astronomical work bridged the transition from Goryeo to Joseon Dynasty scientific traditions.
