## 개요

Gerhard Domagk 독일의 pathologist and bacteriologist who discovered sulfonamide, the first commercially available antibiotic. His discovery of Prontosil in 1932, the first drug to successfully treat bacterial infections in humans, saved millions of lives and launched the age of antibacterial chemotherapy. He was awarded the 1939 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 but was forced by the Nazi regime to decline it.
