## 요약

Salim Ali was an Indian ornithologist and naturalist known as the "birdman of India." He conducted systematic bird surveys across India and wrote numerous influential books on Indian birds. His "Book of Indian Birds" was a landmark publication. He influenced conservation policy in India and was awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian honor.
