## 개요

권혁빈 founded Smilegate, one of 대한민국's most profitable gaming companies, known for the CrossFire franchise. CrossFire became one of the most-played online games in the world, particularly in 중국, generating billions in revenue. Smilegate expanded into mobile gaming, entertainment, and AI. Kwon has become one of 대한민국's wealthiest people through gaming.
