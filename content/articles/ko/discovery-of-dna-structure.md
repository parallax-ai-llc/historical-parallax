## 개요

On April 25, 1953, James Watson and Francis Crick published the double helix structure of DNA in Nature magazine. Building on Rosalind Franklin's X-ray crystallography, the discovery revealed the mechanism of genetic inheritance and launched 현대의 molecular biology.
