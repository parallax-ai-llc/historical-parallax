## 개요

Gerty Cori was a Czech-American 생화학자 who shared the 1947 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 with her husband Carl Cori for their discovery of the course of catalytic conversion of glycogen. She was the first American woman to win a 노벨상 in 과학. The Cori cycle, describing how the body converts glycogen to glucose and back, is named ~ 후에 the couple.
