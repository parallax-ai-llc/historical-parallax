## 개요

단게 겐조 was a 일본ese architect who won the Pritzker Prize in 1987 and was a pioneer of modernist architecture in 일본. While primarily an architect, his engineering innovations in structural design, including the dramatic hyperbolic paraboloid roofs of the Yoyogi National Gymnasium for the 1964 도쿄 Olympics, advanced structural engineering technology.
