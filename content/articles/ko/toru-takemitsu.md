## 요약

Toru Takemitsu was Japan's most internationally celebrated composer, known for his synthesis of Japanese and Western musical aesthetics. His orchestral works including November Steps (combining biwa and shakuhachi with orchestra) and A Flock Descends into the Pentagonal Garden create luminous sound worlds influenced by Debussy, Messiaen, and traditional Japanese music. He also composed over 90 film scores.
