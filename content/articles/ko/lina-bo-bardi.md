## 개요

리나 보 바르디 was an Italian-브라질ian architect whose visionary buildings combined modernist principles with 브라질ian cultural traditions. Her Sao Paulo Museum of Art and SESC Pompeia are icons of 브라질ian architecture. She championed popular culture and social engagement in architecture.
