## 개요

Mexican drug lord known as 'El Chapo' who led the Sinaloa Cartel, the world's most powerful drug trafficking organization. He escaped prison twice through tunnels. Extradited to the US and sentenced to life plus 30 years.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1989 | Rose to lead Sinaloa Cartel |
| 2001 | Escaped from Puente Grande prison |
| 2014 | Captured in Mazatlán |
| 2015 | Escaped through tunnel; recaptured 2016 |
| 2019 | Sentenced to life plus 30 years in US |
