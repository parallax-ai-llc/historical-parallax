## 개요

Guru Ram Das was the fourth Sikh Guru who founded the city of Ramdaspur, which later became Amritsar. He began excavation of the sacred pool (Amrit Sarovar) around which the Golden Temple would be built. He composed the Lavan, the Sikh wedding hymn. He was also a poet and many of his compositions are included in the Guru Granth Sahib.
