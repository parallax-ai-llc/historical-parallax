## 개요

Jeffrey Katzenberg served as chairman of Walt Disney Studios, where he oversaw the Disney Renaissance including The Little Mermaid, Beauty and the Beast, Aladdin, and The Lion King. He co-founded DreamWorks Animation, which produced Shrek and Kung Fu Panda. He later founded Quibi, a short-form streaming service that famously failed within six months despite raising $1.75 billion.
