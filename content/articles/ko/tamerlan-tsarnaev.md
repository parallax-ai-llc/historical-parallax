## 요약
Tamerlan Anzorovich Tsarnaev was a Chechen-American who, with his brother Dzhokhar, carried out the Boston Marathon bombing on April 15, 2013, killing 3 and injuring over 260. He was killed during a confrontation with police in Watertown, Massachusetts.
