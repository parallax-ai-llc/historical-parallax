## 개요

후안 트리페 founded Pan American World Airways and built it into the most famous international airline in history. He pioneered transoceanic air travel and commissioned the Boeing 747, the first wide-body commercial aircraft. Pan Am's globe-spanning routes made international air travel accessible to the masses. His vision transformed aviation from a luxury into a global transportation system.
