## 개요

유다 하나시 (Judah the Prince) was a Jewish patriarch and scholar who compiled and edited the Mishnah, the first major written redaction of Jewish oral law. This achievement was revolutionary, preserving the oral tradition in written form and creating the foundation for the Talmud. He was a wealthy and politically connected leader who maintained good relations with the Roman authorities. He is often referred to simply as 'Rabbi.'
