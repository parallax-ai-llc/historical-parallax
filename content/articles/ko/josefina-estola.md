## 개요

호세피나 에스톨라 was a Filipino virologist who discovered the Manila strain of the avian coronavirus, an important contribution to understanding coronavirus biology. She also researched the Newcastle disease virus and other avian pathogens. Her work at the University of the 필리핀 College of Veterinary Medicine advanced Philippine virology.
