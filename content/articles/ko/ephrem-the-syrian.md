## 개요

Ephrem the Syrian was a Syriac Christian deacon, 신학자, and hymnographer known as the 'Harp of the Holy Spirit.' He composed hundreds of hymns and poetic homilies that became central to Syriac Christian worship. His theological works addressed Christology, Mariology, and anti-heretical polemics. He was declared a Doctor of 교회 in 1920.
