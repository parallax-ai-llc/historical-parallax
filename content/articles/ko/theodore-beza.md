## 요약

Theodore Beza was a French-Swiss Reformed theologian who succeeded John Calvin as leader of the Genevan Reformation. He was a prominent biblical scholar who produced important critical editions of the Greek New Testament. He systematized Calvinist theology and defended the doctrine of double predestination. He also played a key political role in the French Wars of Religion.
