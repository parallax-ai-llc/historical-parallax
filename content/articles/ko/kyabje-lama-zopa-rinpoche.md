## 개요

Lama Zopa Rinpoche was a 네팔i Buddhist teacher who was the spiritual director of the Foundation for the Preservation of the Mahayana Tradition (FPMT), a worldwide network of over 160 Buddhist centers, projects, and services. Along with Lama Thubten Yeshe, he was one of the first Tibetan lamas to teach Westerners in 네팔. He was known for his compassion, his extremely long teachings, and his emphasis on the bodhisattva ideal.
