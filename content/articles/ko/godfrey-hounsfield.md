## 개요

Godfrey Hounsfield invented the CT (computed tomography) scanner, enabling three-dimensional X-ray imaging of the body. His first clinical CT scan of a patient's brain in 1971 revolutionized medical diagnosis. He shared the 1979 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 with Allan Cormack for developing computed axial tomography.
