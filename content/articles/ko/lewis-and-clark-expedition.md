## 개요

The 루이스와 클라크 탐험 (1804-1806) explored the newly acquired Louisiana Purchase territory to the 태평양. The Corps of Discovery traveled 8,000 miles, documenting hundreds of plant and animal species, and establishing US claims to the Pacific Northwest.
