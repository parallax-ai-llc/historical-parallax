## 개요

The 1923 Great Kantō Earthquake devastated Tokyo and Yokohama, killing over 105,000. The magnitude 7.9 quake and subsequent fires destroyed most of Tokyo. Tragically, false rumors ~로 이어졌다 mass 폭력 against Korean residents.
