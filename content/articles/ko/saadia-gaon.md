## 요약

Saadia Gaon was a Babylonian rabbi, philosopher, and scholar who is considered the founder of Jewish philosophy. His major work Emunot ve-Deot (The Book of Beliefs and Opinions) was the first systematic attempt to reconcile Jewish theology with Greek philosophy. He produced the first Arabic translation of the Hebrew Bible and wrote extensively on Hebrew grammar and liturgy. He served as Gaon (head) of the academy at Sura.
