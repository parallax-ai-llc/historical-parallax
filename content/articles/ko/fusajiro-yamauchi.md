## 개요

Fusajiro Yamauchi founded Nintendo in 1889 as a handmade playing card company in Kyoto. He began producing hanafuda (flower cards) when Western playing cards were less popular in Japan. His company grew into the largest playing card company in Japan. He laid the foundation for what would become one of 세계's most 중요한 entertainment companies.
