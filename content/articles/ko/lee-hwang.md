## 개요

Yi Hwang (Toegye) was one of Korea's most prominent Neo-Confucian scholars. While primarily a philosopher, his systematic approach to knowledge influenced Korean intellectual and educational traditions. His Seonghakildo (Ten Diagrams on Sage Learning) synthesized and advanced Neo-Confucian thought. He is honored on the 1,000 won Korean banknote.
