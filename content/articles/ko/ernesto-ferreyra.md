### Femicide in Argentine law (high-level)

Argentina recognizes gender-based killing as an aggravated form of homicide in its federal penal code (commonly discussed under the term *femicidio*). In practice, this classification is used when the killing is tied to gender-based 폭력, often within intimate-partner or family relationships.
