## 개요

김정욱 is a Korean physician who served as Director of the Global Influenza Programme at the World Health Organization. He played key roles in international responses to avian influenza and pandemic preparedness. His work on influenza surveillance systems has strengthened global capacity to detect and respond to pandemic threats.
