## 요약
Stephan Balliet was a German far-right terrorist who attempted to enter the synagogue in Halle on Yom Kippur 2019. Failing to breach the door, he killed two people nearby and livestreamed the attack. He was convicted and sentenced to life with subsequent preventive detention.
