## 개요

에사키 레오나 is a 일본ese physicist who shared the 1973 노벨 물리학상 for his experimental discovery of tunneling in semiconductors. He discovered the Esaki diode (tunnel diode), which demonstrated quantum mechanical tunneling in solids. This discovery had important applications in electronics and helped advance the understanding of quantum mechanics in solid-state physics.
