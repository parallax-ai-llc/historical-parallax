## 개요

이동녕 was a Korean organic chemist who developed novel pharmaceutical compounds at the Korea Research Institute of Chemical Technology (KRICT). His work on synthesizing bioactive natural product analogs contributed to Korean pharmaceutical independence and helped establish Korea's drug development capabilities.
