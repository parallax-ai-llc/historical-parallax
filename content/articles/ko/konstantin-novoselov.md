## 개요

콘스탄틴 노보셀로프 is a 러시아n-born British physicist who shared the 2010 노벨 물리학상 with Andre Geim for groundbreaking experiments regarding the two-dimensional material graphene. Using sticky tape, they isolated graphene — a single layer of carbon atoms — from graphite in 2004. This deceptively simple technique opened a new field of materials science.
