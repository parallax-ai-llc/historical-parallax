## 요약

Tan Sri Lee Shin Cheng founded IOI Group, one of the world's largest palm oil companies. He built IOI from a single palm oil plantation into a diversified conglomerate spanning palm oil, property, and oleochemicals. IOI Group became one of the largest palm oil producers globally. He was one of Malaysia's richest people and his story from humble beginnings to palm oil billionaire exemplifies Malaysia's agricultural industrialization.
