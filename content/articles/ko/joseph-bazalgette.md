## 개요

조지프 바잘게트 designed 런던's revolutionary sewer system in response to the Great Stink of 1858, building 82 miles of intercepting sewers and over 1,000 miles of street sewers. His engineering masterpiece transformed public health in 런던 and became a model for cities worldwide. The system he built is still in use today.
