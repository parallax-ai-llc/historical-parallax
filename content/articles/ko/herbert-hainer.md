## 개요

Herbert Hainer served as CEO of Adidas from 2001 to 2016, growing the company into the world's second-largest sportswear brand. Under his leadership, Adidas revenue nearly tripled and the company expanded its market share in North America and China. He later became president of Bayern Munich, one of the world's most successful football clubs. His long tenure at Adidas helped rebuild the brand's competitiveness against Nike.
