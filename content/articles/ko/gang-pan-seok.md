## 개요

Gang Pan-seok 한국의 지리학자 and environmental 과학자 who documented the geographical and ecological changes of the Korean Peninsula. His research on Korean river systems, land use patterns, and environmental degradation provided baseline data for Korea's later environmental protection efforts.
