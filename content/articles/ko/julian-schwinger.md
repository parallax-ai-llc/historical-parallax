## 개요

줄리언 슈윙거 was an American theoretical physicist who shared the 1965 노벨 물리학상 with Richard Feynman and Sin-Itiro Tomonaga for fundamental work in quantum electrodynamics. His operator field theory approach to QED was mathematically rigorous and influential. He mentored numerous doctoral students who went on to distinguished careers. Schwinger was known for his elegant and polished lecture style.
