## 개요

G. N. Ramachandran 인도의 biophysicist who made fundamental contributions to the understanding of protein structure. He developed the Ramachandran plot, an essential tool for validating protein structures that displays the backbone dihedral angles of amino acid residues. He also determined the triple-helical structure of collagen. His work was foundational to structural biology.
