## 요약

Tsukuba Michitaka was a Japanese computer engineer who contributed to the development of Japan's supercomputer industry. His work on high-performance computing systems at NEC helped Japan achieve world-leading computational capabilities in the 1980s and 1990s with the SX series supercomputers.
