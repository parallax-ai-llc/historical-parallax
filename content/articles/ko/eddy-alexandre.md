## 개요

Eddy Alexandre is associated with **EminiFX**, a company described by U.S. law-enforcement authorities as operating a large-scale investment fraud marketed as a cryptocurrency / foreign-exchange trading program.

Public reporting commonly characterizes the alleged operation as a **Ponzi scheme**, meaning earlier investors are paid returns primarily using funds from newer investors rather than from legitimate profit-generating activity.

## Alleged scheme mechanics (typical indicators)

The following features are widely recognized warning signs in investment fraud cases and are often discussed in connection with EminiFX:

- Guaranteed or near-guaranteed returns regardless of market conditions
- Limited transparency on trading strategies, counterparties, and custody of assets
- Difficulty withdrawing funds once deposited
- Reliance on referral-driven growth and new inflows to meet payout expectations
