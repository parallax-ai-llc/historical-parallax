## 개요

40 Chechen militants seized the Dubrovka 극장 in Moscow and demanded Russian withdrawal from Chechnya. ~ 후에 a multi-day standoff, Russian special forces assaulted the building ~ 후에 pumping in an incapacitating chemical agent (widely reported as a fentanyl derivative). Official reporting commonly cites about 130 hostages killed; the number of hostages inside was reported in the hundreds.

## What Happened (High Level)

- **Location:** Dubrovka 극장 (the “극장 Center on Dubrovka”), Moscow, Russia.
- **Setting:** A packed evening performance of the musical *Nord-Ost*.
- **Perpetrators:** Armed militants connected to the Chechen 분쟁.
- **Demand:** An end to (or withdrawal from) Russian military operations in Chechnya.
- **Resolution:** A security-service assault following the release of a chemical agent.

## Why It Matters

- It ~이 되었다 defining mass-hostage crisis in post-Soviet Russia.
- The operation is frequently discussed as a case study in **counterterrorism tradeoffs**, especially around the use of incapacitating agents in enclosed spaces.
- The aftermath raised questions about transparency (including the exact agent used) and emergency medical preparedness for chemical exposure.

## 여파 and Legacy

### Casualties and Medical Response

- Public sources typically distinguish between:
  - **Hostages who died during the assault** (including from the effects of the chemical agent and related complications).
  - **Militants killed** ~ 동안 operation.
- A recurring theme in reporting is that many hostages **did not receive timely antidotes and supportive care**, and that hospitals faced challenges identifying and treating exposure ~ 때문에 the agent was not publicly specified at the time.

### Public and Political Impact

- The siege intensified Russian domestic focus on counterterrorism and internal security.
- It also ~이 되었다 reference point in debates about **state secrecy vs. public health** ~ 동안 crisis response.

### 역사적 맥락

- The incident occurred ~ 동안 broader **Second Chechen War** period and reflected the spillover of that 분쟁 into major Russian cities.
