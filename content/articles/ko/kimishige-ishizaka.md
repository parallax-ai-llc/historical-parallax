## 개요

이시자카 기미시게 was a 일본ese immunologist who, with his wife Teruko, discovered immunoglobulin E (IgE) in 1966 at Johns Hopkins University. Their discovery of IgE as the antibody class responsible for type I hypersensitivity reactions revolutionized understanding of allergies and led to new treatments for allergic diseases.
