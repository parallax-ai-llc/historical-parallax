## 개요

권오현 is a Korean electrical engineer who led Samsung Electronics' semiconductor division to global dominance. As CEO and Vice Chairman, he oversaw the development of advanced memory chips and processors that made Samsung the world's largest semiconductor manufacturer. His technical leadership was critical to Samsung's technological ascendancy.
