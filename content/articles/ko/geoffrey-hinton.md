## 개요

Geoffrey Hinton 영국의-Canadian cognitive 심리학자 and 컴퓨터 과학자 known as one of the 'godfathers of deep learning.' His work on artificial neural networks, particularly backpropagation and deep learning techniques, revolutionized artificial intelligence. He shared the 2024 노벨상 in Physics for foundational discoveries enabling machine learning with artificial neural networks. Hinton left Google in 2023 to speak freely about AI risks.
