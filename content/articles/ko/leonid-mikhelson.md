## 개요

레오니드 미헬손 is the chairman of the board of Novatek, 러시아's largest independent natural gas producer. He built Novatek into a major force in global LNG through the Yamal LNG project in the 북극. He is one of 러시아's wealthiest people and a significant art collector. Despite Western sanctions on 러시아, Novatek has continued its LNG expansion, including the 북극 LNG 2 project.
