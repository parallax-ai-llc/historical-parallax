## 개요

Eunice Newton Foote 미국의 과학자 who was the first person to document the greenhouse effect of carbon dioxide in 1856, three years ~ 전에 John Tyndall's better-known experiments. She demonstrated that CO2 and water vapor absorb heat and proposed that changes in atmospheric CO2 could change climate. Her contribution was overlooked for over 150 years.
