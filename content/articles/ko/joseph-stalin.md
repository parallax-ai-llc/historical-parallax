## 개요

Soviet dictator who ruled through terror for nearly 30 years. He orchestrated the Great Purge, the Holodomor famine in 우크라이나 that killed millions, and sent millions to the Gulag. Estimated 6-20 million died under his rule.

|------|-------|

| 1932 | Holodomor famine killed millions in 우크라이나 |
| 1937 | Great Purge executed hundreds of thousands |
| 1953 | Died of stroke |
