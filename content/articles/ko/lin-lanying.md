## 개요

린란잉 was a pioneering Chinese materials scientist known as the 'Mother of Semiconductors in 중국.' She developed techniques for growing single-crystal silicon and gallium arsenide that were essential to 중국's semiconductor and space programs. Her work on semiconductor materials earned her numerous national science awards.
