## 개요

권성동 is the honorary chairman of Shinsegae Group (formerly part of Samsung Group). He built E-mart into 대한민국's largest discount store chain and Shinsegae into the country's premier department store operator. His retail innovations transformed Korean shopping culture. Shinsegae Group separated from Samsung Group in 1991 under the leadership of 이명희, Samsung's founding family member and Kwon's wife.
