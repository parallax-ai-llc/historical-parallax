## 역사적 평가

### 긍정적 평가

Scholars who view Vargas favorably emphasize his role as a modernizer who transformed Brazil from an oligarchical, agricultural society into a more industrialized, urban nation with expanded rights for workers. ### 부정적 평가

Critics of Vargas emphasize the authoritarian nature of his rule, particularly ~ 동안 Estado Novo period, when he suppressed political opposition, censored media, imprisoned dissidents, and employed 고문 ~을 통해 state security apparatus.[^87] Scholars like R.S. Skeptics question whether Vargas's labor policies represented genuine empowerment of workers or a sophisticated form of social control.
