## 요약
Semion Yudkovich Mogilevich is a Ukrainian-born organized crime figure described by the FBI as the 'most dangerous mobster in the world.' He is involved in arms trafficking, contract murders, drug trafficking, and massive fraud schemes including the $10 billion money laundering scheme through the Bank of New York.
