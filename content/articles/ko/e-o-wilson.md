## 개요

E. O. Wilson 미국의 생물학자, 박물학자, and entomologist known as the 'father of sociobiology' and the 'father of biodiversity.' His work on island biogeography with Robert MacArthur became foundational to conservation biology. His 논란이 많은 book 'Sociobiology' proposed a biological basis for social behavior. Wilson was also one of 세계's leading experts on ants and a passionate advocate for biodiversity conservation.
