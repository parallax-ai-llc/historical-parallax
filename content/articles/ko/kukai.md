## 개요

구카이 (posthumously known as Kobo Daishi) was a 일본ese Buddhist monk who founded the Shingon (True Word) school of esoteric 불교. He studied tantric 불교 in 중국 and brought it to 일본. He was also a poet, calligrapher, engineer, and one of 일본's greatest cultural figures. He is credited with inventing the kana syllabary and establishing the pilgrimage circuit of 88 temples on Shikoku.
