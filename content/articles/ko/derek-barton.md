## 개요

Derek Barton 영국의 화학자 who shared the 1969 노벨상 in Chemistry for his contributions to the development of the concept of conformation and its application in chemistry. His conformational analysis showed how the three-dimensional arrangement of atoms affects molecular behavior and chemical reactivity, revolutionizing organic chemistry.
