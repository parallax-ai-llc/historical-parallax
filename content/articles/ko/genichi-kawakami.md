## 개요

Genichi Kawakami transformed Yamaha from a piano manufacturer into a diversified global corporation producing motorcycles, musical instruments, and electronics. He diversified into motorcycles in 1955, creating Yamaha Motor Company. Under his leadership, Yamaha ~이 되었다 world's largest 프로듀서 of musical instruments and a major motorcycle manufacturer. His vision of synergy between 음악 and 기술 defined Yamaha's unique corporate identity.
