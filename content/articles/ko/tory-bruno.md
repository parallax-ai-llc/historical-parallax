## 요약

Tory Bruno is the president and CEO of United Launch Alliance (ULA), a joint venture between Boeing and Lockheed Martin that is the U.S. government's primary launch services provider. He led the development of the Vulcan Centaur rocket, designed to compete with SpaceX's Falcon 9. ULA has an impeccable record of over 150 consecutive successful launches. He has been instrumental in maintaining ULA's reliability while reducing costs.
