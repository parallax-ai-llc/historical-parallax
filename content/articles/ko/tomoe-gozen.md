## 요약

Legendary female samurai warrior who fought in the Genpei War. Renowned for beheading enemies in battle, she was one of the most famous warriors of medieval Japan.

## Representation in later media

Tomoe Gozen has been reinterpreted in many genres, from traditional performing arts to modern popular culture. These portrayals often emphasize martial prowess and independence, sometimes reframing her story to reflect contemporary ideas about gender and heroism.

### Why she endures
- **Narrative power:** a rare, vivid warrior figure in well-known war chronicles
- **Symbolic flexibility:** can be read as history, legend, or cultural archetype
- **Artistic legacy:** a recurring subject in visual art and storytelling
