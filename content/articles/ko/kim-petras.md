## Controversies & Criticism

The most significant controversy surrounding Petras involves her long-standing professional relationship with Dr. Luke, who was accused of sexual assault and abuse by singer Kesha in a lawsuit filed in 2014. Petras continued working with Dr. Luke's Kemosabe Records label despite widespread calls from fans and fellow artists to distance herself. She largely avoided public comment on the matter for years.

At the 2023 그래미상s, Petras gave her acceptance speech alone after Smith ceded their time to her, but she did not mention Dr. Luke by name despite his role as a producer on "Unholy." The omission drew criticism from advocates supporting Kesha.

## Legacy & Influence

Petras has meaningfully expanded the visibility of transgender artists in mainstream pop. Her Grammy win was received as a landmark moment in industry recognition of transgender performers. She has influenced a generation of LGBTQ+ artists who cite her independent early career as a model for building an audience outside traditional label structures.

Her blend of camp aesthetics, confessional pop songwriting, and dance-floor production has contributed to the broader hyperpop and bubblegum pop revival of the 2020s.
