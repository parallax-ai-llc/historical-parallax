## 개요

Fei-Fei Li 중국의-American 컴퓨터 과학자 known for creating ImageNet, a large-scale visual database that catalyzed the deep learning revolution in computer vision. The ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge ~이 되었다 benchmark that demonstrated the superiority of deep neural networks. She co-directs Stanford's Human-Centered AI Institute and served as VP at Google Cloud AI. Li is a leading advocate for human-centered AI.
