## Additional Context

### Early years and background (high-level)

- According to Wikipedia, he is one of the Ochoa brothers (Juan David and Fabio) and emerged from a family involved in cattle-breeding and restaurant businesses before moving into narcotics trafficking in the mid-1970s.
- Wikipedia describes him as presenting a "businessman" profile and being associated with cattle ranching (including fighting bulls) as well as collecting Harley-Davidson motorcycles.

- Wikipedia lists him as a founding member of the Medellín Cartel (late 1970s) alongside figures such as Pablo Escobar, Carlos Lehder, 호세 곤살로 로드리게스 가차, and Gustavo Gaviria.
- Wikipedia states he coordinated cartel operations in the 미국 and Western 유럽, and claims are attributed to him about shipping large quantities of cocaine per month during the early 1980s.

- Wikipedia reports a Spanish court decision (1986) to extradite him to stand trial in 콜롬비아, and that subsequent U.S. extradition requests were a major political flashpoint.
