## 요약

Teruaki Georges Suami was a pioneering Japanese microsurgeon who advanced lymphatic surgery techniques. His anatomical studies of the lymphatic system using modern imaging have transformed understanding of lymphatic drainage. His work has improved surgical treatment of lymphedema and cancer-related lymphatic disorders.
