## 개요

이응노 was a Korean painter who lived in 파리 from 1958 and developed a unique fusion of 동아시아n ink painting with Western abstraction. His crowd paintings of tiny human figures and his calligraphic abstractions bridge Korean tradition and 유럽an modernism. He founded the 이응노 Museum in Daejeon.
