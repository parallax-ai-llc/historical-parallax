## 개요

조지프 니덤 was a British biochemist and historian of science who is best known for his monumental multi-volume work 'Science and Civilisation in 중국.' This work documented 중국's many scientific and technological innovations and challenged the Western-centric view of science history. He posed the famous 'Needham Question' of why 중국 fell behind the West in science despite its early lead.
