## 초기 생애

Born illegitimate son of notary Piero da Vinci and peasant woman Caterina in Vinci, near Florence. Raised in grandfather's household.

**Apprenticeship:**
- Age 14: Apprenticed to artist Andrea del Verrocchio in Florence
- Learned painting, sculpture, metalworking, mechanical arts
- Collaborated on Verrocchio's *Baptism of Christ*—legend says Verrocchio quit painting after seeing Leonardo's angel

## Scientific Studies

**Anatomy:**
- Dissected ~30 human corpses (illegal but tolerated)
- Drew muscles, organs, bones with unprecedented accuracy
- Discovered aspects of cardiovascular system
- 240+ drawings and 13,000 words of anatomical notes

**Engineering & Inventions:**
- Flying machines (ornithopter, helicopter precursor, parachute)
- Military weapons (tank, giant crossbow)
- Hydraulic engineering
- Submarine
- Most never built but conceptually sound

**Natural Sciences:**
- Geology: Understood fossils proved ancient seas
- Botany: Plant studies
- Optics: How eye works
- Water flow, erosion

---

## Notebooks

Left ~13,000 pages of notes and drawings (estimated 1/4 of total survived). Written in mirror script (right to left), possibly to keep ideas secret or because he was left-handed.

**Content:**
- Scientific observations
- Engineering designs
- Anatomical drawings
- Artistic techniques
- Personal thoughts, to-do lists

**Lost Works:**
Many notebooks lost. Bill Gates purchased Codex Leicester (1994) for $30.8M.
