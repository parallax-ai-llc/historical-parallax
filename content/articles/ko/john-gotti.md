## 개요
John Joseph Gotti Jr. was an American mobster who became boss of the Gambino crime family. Known as the 'Teflon Don' for his ability to avoid conviction, he was finally convicted of racketeering and murder in 1992. He was sentenced to life without parole and died of cancer in prison.
