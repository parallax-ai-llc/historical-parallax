## 요약
The Shan Brothers (Wei Hsueh-kang and Wei Hsueh-long) are ethnic Chinese drug lords from Shan State, Myanmar. They have been major figures in the Southeast Asian methamphetamine trade. Wei Hsueh-kang has a $2 million US bounty. They operate through the United Wa State Army territory.
