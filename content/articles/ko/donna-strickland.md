## 개요

Donna Strickland 캐나다의 optical 물리학자 who shared the 2018 노벨상 in Physics for her development of chirped pulse amplification with Gérard Mourou. She was only the third woman to win the 노벨상 in Physics. Their technique for generating high-intensity ultrashort optical pulses revolutionized laser physics and enabled applications in 의학, industry, and 과학. 특히 she did not have a Wikipedia page ~까지 her 노벨상 was announced.
