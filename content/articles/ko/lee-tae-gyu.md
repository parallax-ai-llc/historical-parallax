## 개요

이태규 was a pioneering Korean physical chemist who made significant contributions to the theory of absolute reaction rates. He worked with Henry Eyring at Princeton on transition state theory, co-authoring influential papers. He later returned to Korea to help build the nation's chemistry research infrastructure and served as president of the Korean Chemical Society.
