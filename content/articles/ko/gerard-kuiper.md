## 개요

Gerard Kuiper 네덜란드의-American 천문학자 who is considered the father of 현대의 planetary 과학. He discovered the moons Miranda (Uranus) and Nereid (Neptune), and predicted the existence of the Kuiper Belt of icy bodies beyond Neptune. He pioneered infrared astronomical techniques and founded the Lunar and Planetary Laboratory at 대학교 of Arizona. The Kuiper Belt is named in his honor.
