## 요약

Rudolf Nureyev was a Russian ballet dancer who defected to the West in 1961, becoming one of the most celebrated dancers of the 20th century. His partnership with Margot Fonteyn at the Royal Ballet was legendary. His raw emotional power and technical brilliance transformed male dancing and brought ballet to international popular attention.
