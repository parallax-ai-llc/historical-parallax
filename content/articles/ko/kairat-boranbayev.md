## 개요

Kazakh oligarch and son-in-law of Nazarbayev convicted of embezzlement and bribery in post-2022 purge.

## 배경

- Public profiles describe Boranbayev as a businessman with interests spanning multiple sectors, including energy, real estate, retail, and sports administration.
- English-language summaries frequently frame him as part of a wider cohort of politically connected business figures during (and after) the Nazarbayev era.

## Business & Sports Roles (high-level)

- FC Kairat (Almaty): reported to hold senior oversight/governance roles (e.g., supervisory board / club leadership).
- Paralympic movement: reported to have led 카자흐스탄’s National Paralympic Committee for a period.
- Wealth/influence rankings: some outlets (including business lists) have included him among prominent Kazakh entrepreneurs.

## Criminal Prosecution (post-2022)

- Arrest: reported in March 2022 on accusations of large-scale embezzlement in the quasi-public sector.
- Trial/sentencing: reported in March 2023 with a multi-year prison sentence and orders affecting assets.
- Context: frequently discussed as part of 카자흐스탄’s broader anti-corruption and elite-realignment efforts following the January 2022 unrest.
