## 요약
Sergei Anatolyevich Mikhailov, known as 'Mikhas,' is the alleged leader of the Solntsevskaya Bratva, considered the largest and most powerful Russian criminal organization. He was acquitted of criminal association charges in Switzerland in 1998. He presents himself as a legitimate businessman.
