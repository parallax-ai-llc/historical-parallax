## 개요

Gustav Krupp von Bohlen und Halbach married into the Krupp dynasty and led the Krupp steel empire during both World Wars. He supported Hitler's rise to power and Krupp factories produced tanks, submarines, and artillery for Nazi Germany. He was indicted at the Nuremberg trials but was found mentally incompetent to stand trial. His story represents the collaboration between German industry and the Nazi regime.
