## 요약

Safra Catz has served as CEO of Oracle Corporation since 2014, making her one of the highest-paid female executives in the world. She joined Oracle in 1999 and was instrumental in the company's aggressive acquisition strategy, overseeing more than 100 acquisitions. Under her leadership, Oracle transitioned to cloud computing. She was born in Israel and briefly served on the Trump transition team in 2016.
