## 개요

조지프 머리 performed the first successful human organ transplant — a kidney transplant between identical twins — on December 23, 1954, at Peter Bent Brigham Hospital in 보스턴. He later pioneered immunosuppressive drug therapy to enable transplants between non-identical individuals. He received the 1990 노벨상 in Physiology or Medicine.
