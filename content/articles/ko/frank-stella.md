## 개요

Frank Stella 미국의 예술가 known for his 선구적인 role in Minimalism and post-painterly abstraction. His Black Paintings of 1959 radically reduced painting to its material essence. His later increasingly baroque reliefs and sculptures demonstrate extraordinary creative range.
