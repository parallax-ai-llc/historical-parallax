## 개요

Canadian 피아니스트 유명한 for his interpretations of Bach, particularly his two recordings of the Goldberg Variations. He retired from live performance at 31 to focus on recording. His idiosyncratic genius and philosophical approach to 음악 made him a cultural icon.
