## 요약

Valentino Rossi won nine MotoGP/World Championship titles across three decades, the most successful motorcycle racer ever. His charismatic personality made him a global star.

| 2001 | Won first MotoGP title |
| 2005 | Won seventh premier-class title |
| 2021 | Retired from MotoGP |
