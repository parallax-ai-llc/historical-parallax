## 개요

고광림 is a Korean food scientist who has researched the microbiology and health benefits of kimchi fermentation. His scientific studies on lactic acid bacteria in kimchi have contributed to the global recognition of kimchi as a health food and supported the Korean fermented food industry's international expansion.
