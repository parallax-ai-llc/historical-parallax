## 개요

이승기 was a Korean chemist who invented Vinalon, one of the first synthetic fibers developed outside the West. He synthesized the fiber from polyvinyl alcohol in 1939 while working at Kyoto Imperial University. After the 한국전쟁, he went to 북한 where Vinalon became the national fiber. His achievement demonstrated Korean capability in advanced chemistry.
