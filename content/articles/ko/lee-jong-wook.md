## 개요

이종욱 served as Director-General of the World Health Organization from 2003 until his death in 2006. He led the WHO's '3 by 5' initiative to provide antiretroviral treatment to 3 million HIV/AIDS patients by 2005. His leadership during the SARS outbreak and his commitment to global health equity made him a respected figure in public health.
