## 개요

이토 기요시 was a 일본ese mathematician who developed Itô calculus, a framework for stochastic integration and stochastic differential equations. His Itô's lemma is fundamental to mathematical finance (the Black-Scholes model depends on it), probability theory, and physics. He received the first Gauss Prize in 2006 for his revolutionary contributions.
