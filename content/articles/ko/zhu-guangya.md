## 요약

Zhu Guangya was a Chinese nuclear physicist who was a key leader in China's nuclear weapons development program. He studied at the University of Michigan and returned to China in 1950. He served as chief scientist and organized the technical teams that successfully developed China's atomic and hydrogen bombs.
