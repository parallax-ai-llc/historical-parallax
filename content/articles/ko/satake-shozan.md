## 요약

Satake Shozan was a Japanese feudal lord (daimyo) of the Akita domain who was unusual among the samurai class for his deep interest in Western science, particularly mineralogy and Western-style painting. He promoted rangaku (Dutch learning) in his domain and encouraged the study of natural sciences and mining technology.
