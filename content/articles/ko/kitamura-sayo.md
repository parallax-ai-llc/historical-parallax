## 개요

기타무라 사요 was a 일본ese farmer's wife who founded Tensho Kotai Jingu-kyo (the Dancing Religion) in 1945. She claimed to be possessed by the absolute God Tensho Kotai Jingu. Her followers practiced ecstatic dancing as a form of worship. She was famous for her bold preaching style and her criticism of established religions and political leaders. The movement spread to Hawaii, the mainland 미국, and other countries.
