## 개요

크론슈타트의 요한 was a 러시아n Orthodox priest who became one of the most beloved and influential spiritual figures in 러시아n history. He served as rector of Saint Andrew's Cathedral in Kronstadt for over 50 years. He was renowned for his healing ministry, charitable work, and deep liturgical spirituality. His spiritual diary My Life in Christ became a classic of Orthodox devotional literature.
