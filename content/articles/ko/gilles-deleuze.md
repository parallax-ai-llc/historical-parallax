Finally, the gap between Deleuze's vitalist 철학 celebrating life, creativity, and becoming, and his death by suicide, troubles critics who see a fundamental contradiction between his thought and his final act.[^157] ~하는 동안 defenders offer sophisticated readings of how his suicide might be understood within his philosophical framework, skeptics see this as elaborate rationalization of an irreconcilable tension between 철학 and life.[^158]
