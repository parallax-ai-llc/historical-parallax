## 개요

Hirokazu Kore-eda is a Japanese filmmaker whose gentle, humanistic films explore family bonds, loss, and the meaning of kinship. Shoplifters won the Palme d'Or at Cannes in 2018. His quiet, observational style has made him the most internationally acclaimed Japanese director of his generation.
