## 요약

Yukichi Fukuzawa was a Japanese educator and writer who founded Keio University and is considered the father of Japanese modernization. He promoted Western science and technology through his books, especially Gakumon no Susume (An Encouragement of Learning). His systematic introduction of Western scientific concepts to Japan was transformative. His face appears on the ¥10,000 note.
