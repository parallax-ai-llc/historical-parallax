## 요약

Walker Evans was an American photographer whose documentary work defined the aesthetic of Depression-era America. His photographs for the FSA and his book Let Us Now Praise Famous Men with James Agee are landmarks of American documentary photography. His influence on photographers from Robert Frank to Stephen Shore is immeasurable.
