## 개요

Gorakhnath (Gorakshanath) was a 중세의 Indian yogi and 성인 who founded the Nath yogic tradition. He is credited with systematizing Hatha Yoga and writing key texts including the Goraksha Samhita. He emphasized physical and spiritual practices including asanas, pranayama, and mudras as tools for spiritual transformation. The Nath tradition he founded spread across India and continues to influence yoga practice worldwide. Many temples and monasteries bear his name.
