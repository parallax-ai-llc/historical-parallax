## 개요

Ingo Potrykus developed Golden Rice, genetically engineered to produce beta-carotene (provitamin A) to combat vitamin A deficiency that causes blindness and death in developing countries. His invention was controversial among anti-GMO activists but has the potential to save millions of lives. TIME magazine put him on its cover in 2000.
