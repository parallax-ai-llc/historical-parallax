## 요약

Zheng Xuan was an Eastern Han Dynasty scholar who is known primarily as a Confucian commentator, but his works also contain important observations on mathematics, astronomy, and natural phenomena. His systematic approach to textual analysis anticipated scientific methodology, and his commentaries preserved much ancient Chinese technical knowledge.
