## 개요

류양 became the first Chinese woman in space when she flew aboard Shenzhou-9 in 2012, docking with the Tiangong-1 space laboratory. She was selected from a pool of military pilots and underwent rigorous training. She flew again on Shenzhou-14 in 2022. Her flights were milestones for women's participation in 중국's space program.
