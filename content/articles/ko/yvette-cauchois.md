## 요약

Yvette Cauchois was a French physicist who developed the bent crystal spectrometer for X-ray spectroscopy, enabling the study of electronic structures of atoms and molecules. Her technique advanced both fundamental physics and materials science. She was a pioneer for women in French physics and directed the Laboratory of Physical Chemistry at Paris.
