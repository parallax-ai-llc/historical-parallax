## 개요

Brazilian drug lord known as Nem da Rocinha who controlled drug trafficking in Rocinha, Rio de Janeiro's largest favela. He ran the favela like a parallel state with armed enforcers.

## 배경

- Elton Brito da Silva became known under the nickname "Nem da Rocinha" in connection with drug trafficking in Rocinha, a large favela in Rio de Janeiro.
- His notoriety grew ~ 동안 a period when Rio authorities pursued "pacification" policies aimed at reducing the influence of armed criminal groups in certain communities.

## Control of Rocinha

- Reports describe a system of armed enforcement and territorial control typical of major Brazilian drug factions operating in urban communities.
- His influence was tied to the local drug trade and the ability to resist or negotiate with rival groups and state interventions.

## Arrest and prosecution

- He was arrested in 2011 ~ 동안 a major police operation connected to efforts to reassert state control in Rocinha.
- Subsequent 법원 proceedings ~로 이어졌다 prison sentences related to drug trafficking and associated crimes.

## Significance

- His case is often cited in discussions of how organized crime can function as a parallel power structure in areas with limited state presence.
- It also illustrates the risks and limits of large-scale security operations as a response to entrenched criminal networks.
