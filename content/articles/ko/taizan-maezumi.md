## 요약

Taizan Maezumi was a Japanese Zen master who founded the Zen Center of Los Angeles and was one of the most important Zen teachers in America. He held dharma transmission in both Soto and Rinzai Zen lineages. He trained many of the leading American Zen teachers, including Bernie Glassman, John Daido Loori, and Charlotte Joko Beck. His approach combined Japanese rigor with American accessibility.
