Captain of the Sewol ferry sentenced to life for abandoning ship while 304 passengers drowned.

## 맥락

이준석 is primarily known publicly in connection with the sinking of the 대한민국n passenger ferry MV *Sewol*, one of the country’s worst peacetime maritime disasters.

## Sewol Disaster (High-Level)

- The ferry sank while carrying passengers (including many students) and crew.
- A total of **304 people** died.
- The captain and some crew survived, while many passengers remained trapped as the vessel capsized.

## Legal and Public Aftermath

- 대한민국n authorities investigated decisions made on board and during the evacuation.
- Court proceedings focused on alleged negligence, failures of duty, and responsibility for passenger safety.
- The case became a focal point in national debates about regulatory oversight, emergency response, and accountability.

## 유산

- The Sewol disaster remains a defining event in modern 대한민국n public life.
- Discussions about maritime safety standards and crisis management frequently reference the incident.
