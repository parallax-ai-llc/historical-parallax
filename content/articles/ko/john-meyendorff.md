## 개요

존 메옌도르프 was a 러시아n-American Orthodox theologian and patristic scholar who was an authority on Byzantine theology, particularly Gregory Palamas. He succeeded Alexander Schmemann as dean of Saint Vladimir's Seminary. His works on Palamas, Byzantine theology, and Orthodox ecclesiology shaped modern Orthodox self-understanding. He was also active in the ecumenical movement.
