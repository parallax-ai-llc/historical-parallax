## 요약
Tommy Lynn Sells was an American serial killer who confessed to killing 70 people across the United States between 1985 and 1999. A drifter, he traveled by freight train. He was convicted of the murder of 13-year-old Kaylene Harris and executed by lethal injection in Texas in 2014.
