## 개요

- The Fritzl case refers to a prolonged captivity and sexual abuse crime in Amstetten, Austria, perpetrated by Josef Fritzl against his daughter.
- It drew global attention due to the duration of confinement, the hidden nature of the underground prison, and the institutional failures that allowed it to persist.
