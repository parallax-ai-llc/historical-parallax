## 개요

다카미네 조키치 was a 일본ese-American chemist who was the first to isolate the hormone adrenaline (epinephrine) in pure crystalline form in 1901. He also developed takadiastase, an enzyme for digestive disorders, which became a commercial success. Takamine donated the cherry trees that bloom along the Tidal Basin in 워싱턴, D.C.
