## 요약

Taguchi Genichi was a Japanese engineer and statistician who developed the Taguchi method for quality engineering. His approach to experimental design and loss function analysis transformed manufacturing quality control worldwide. His methods enabled companies to improve product quality while reducing costs, and were adopted globally by industries from automotive to electronics.
