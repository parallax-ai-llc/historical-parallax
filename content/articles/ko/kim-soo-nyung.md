## 개요

김수녕 won four Olympic gold medals in archery across three Games, the most by any Korean archer.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1988 | Won gold at 서울 Olympics |
| 1992 | Won gold at Barcelona |
| 2000 | Won relay gold at Sydney at age 29 |

- **Olympic longevity:** Winning gold across three Olympics (1988, 1992, 2000) is uncommon in precision sports and reflects sustained elite performance under changing competitive fields.

- **National significance:** Korean Olympic archers are prominent national sports figures, and multi-Games champions often influence training culture and public interest.
