## 개요

궉렝벵 is the executive chairman of Hong Leong Group 싱가포르, one of the largest conglomerates in 동남아시아. He controls CDL (City Developments Limited), one of the largest hotel owners globally, with the Millennium and Copthorne hotel chains. The Hong Leong Group spans real estate, hospitality, finance, and trading. He is one of 싱가포르's most influential business leaders.
