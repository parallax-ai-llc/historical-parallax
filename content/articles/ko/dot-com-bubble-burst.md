## 개요

The dot-com bubble burst in March 2000 when the NASDAQ peaked at 5,048 ~ 전에 crashing 78% by October 2002. Hundreds of internet companies failed, wiping out $5 trillion in market value. It ended the 1990s internet gold rush.
