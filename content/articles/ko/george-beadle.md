## 개요

George Beadle 미국의 유전학자 who shared the 1958 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 with Edward Tatum for discovering that genes regulate biochemical events within cells. Their 'one gene-one enzyme' hypothesis established the link between genes and proteins. This concept was fundamental to the development of molecular biology and our understanding of gene expression.
