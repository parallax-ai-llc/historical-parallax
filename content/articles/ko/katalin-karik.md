## 개요

커털린 커리코 is a Hungarian-American biochemist who shared the 2023 노벨상 in Physiology or Medicine for discoveries concerning nucleoside base modifications that enabled the development of effective mRNA vaccines against COVID-19. Her decades of research on mRNA, conducted despite funding difficulties and career setbacks, proved essential when the COVID-19 pandemic struck. Her work with Drew Weissman led to the Pfizer-BioNTech and Moderna vaccines.
