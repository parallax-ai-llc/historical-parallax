## 개요

김범수 is a Korean software engineer who founded Kakao Corporation, the company behind KakaoTalk, 대한민국's dominant messaging platform used by over 90% of the population. His engineering innovations in mobile messaging, payments, and AI have made Kakao one of Korea's most important technology companies.
