## 개요

Eiichi Shibusawa 일본의 실업가 known as the 'father of Japanese 자본주의.' ~하는 동안 not a 과학자 per se, he founded over 500 enterprises including banks, insurance companies, and industrial firms, and was instrumental in modernizing Japan's economic and industrial infrastructure. He promoted the application of 과학 and 기술 to Japanese industry.
