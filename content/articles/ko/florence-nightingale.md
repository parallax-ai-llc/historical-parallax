## 개요

Florence Nightingale founded 현대의 nursing and was a pioneer in the use of statistics to improve public health. ~ 동안 Crimean 전쟁, she dramatically reduced death rates at the Scutari military hospital ~을 통해 sanitation improvements. Her 혁신적인 polar area diagrams demonstrated the impact of sanitary conditions on mortality.
