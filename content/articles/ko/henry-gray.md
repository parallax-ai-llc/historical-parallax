## 개요

Henry Gray wrote Gray's Anatomy, first published in 1858, which became the most famous and enduring anatomy textbook in the English-speaking world. The book, with its detailed illustrations by Henry Vandyke Carter, has been continuously in print for over 160 years. Gray died tragically young of smallpox at age 34.
