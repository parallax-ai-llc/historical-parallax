## 개요

존 고리 invented the first mechanical refrigeration and ice-making machine in 1851, originally designed to cool hospital rooms for malaria patients. His invention was the forerunner of modern air conditioning and refrigeration. Despite receiving a patent, he could not find financial backing and died in obscurity and debt.
