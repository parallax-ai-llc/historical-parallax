## 개요

Frederick Winslow Taylor is considered the father of scientific management. His time-and-motion studies and systematic approach to improving industrial efficiency transformed manufacturing worldwide. His 1911 book The Principles of Scientific Management was enormously 영향력 있는 in shaping 현대의 management theory and industrial engineering.
