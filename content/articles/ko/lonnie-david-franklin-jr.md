## 개요
로니 데이비드 프랭클린 주니어, the 'Grim Sleeper,' was an American serial killer who murdered at least 10 women in 로스앤젤레스 between 1985 and 2007. His case was notable for a 14-year gap between known killings. He was identified through familial DNA searching and convicted in 2016.

## 초기 생애
Born August 30, 1952, in 로스앤젤레스. He was a former sanitation worker and mechanic. He had a previous conviction for assault. He lived in South 로스앤젤레스. [^1]

## 범죄 경력
Between 1985 and 2007, Franklin murdered at least 10 women in South 로스앤젤레스, primarily sex workers and drug users. He shot and sometimes strangled victims. There was a notable 14-year gap (1988-2002) in known killings, earning him the 'Grim Sleeper' moniker. Police later found photos of over 180 women in his home. [^2]

## 체포 / 몰락
Franklin was identified in 2010 through familial DNA searching — his son's DNA in a criminal database was partially matched to crime scene evidence. Undercover officers collected Franklin's DNA from discarded pizza. He was arrested July 7, 2010, convicted of 10 murders in 2016, and sentenced to death. He died in prison on March 28, 2020. [^3]

## 긍정적 관점
- His identification through familial DNA searching was a landmark in forensic technology [^4]
- His case exposed LAPD's inadequate response to serial crimes in minority communities
- Led to reforms in how LAPD prioritized serial crime investigations
