## 개요

리쓰광 was a pioneering Chinese geologist who developed the theory of geomechanics and used it to predict the location of oil deposits in 중국. When foreign experts claimed 중국 was oil-poor, his geological theories led to the discovery of the Daqing oil field and other major petroleum reserves, transforming 중국's energy landscape.
