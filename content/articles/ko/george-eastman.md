## Key Contributions

- **Roll film commercialization:** Eastman helped move photography away from glass plates toward flexible film, enabling simpler cameras and mass use.
- **Consumer product design:** The Kodak camera line emphasized ease of use and standardized processing.
- **Vertical integration:** Kodak built a large-scale manufacturing and processing ecosystem around film, paper, chemicals, and cameras.

## Business and Impact

Kodak’s approach turned photography into a consumer habit rather than a specialist craft.
That shift also influenced journalism, advertising, and personal documentation by making images cheap and repeatable.
