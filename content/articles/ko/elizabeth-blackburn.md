## 개요

Elizabeth Blackburn is an Australian-American molecular 생물학자 who shared the 2009 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for the discovery of how chromosomes are protected by telomeres and the enzyme telomerase. Her work revealed the mechanisms of cellular aging and has implications for cancer research and understanding age-related diseases.
