## 요약

Yi Cheon was a Korean inventor and military engineer of the Joseon Dynasty who developed advanced firearms and weapons for the Korean military. He improved gunpowder weapons including the hwacha (multiple rocket launcher) and various cannon designs. He also contributed to the development of astronomical instruments and contributed to Korean military technology.
