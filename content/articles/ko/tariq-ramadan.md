## 요약

Tariq Ramadan is a Swiss-Egyptian academic and Islamic scholar who has been one of the most prominent Muslim intellectuals in Europe. He has argued for a European Islam that integrates Muslim identity with Western civic values. He is the grandson of Hasan al-Banna, founder of the Muslim Brotherhood. His career was derailed by sexual assault allegations and a conviction in 2024.
