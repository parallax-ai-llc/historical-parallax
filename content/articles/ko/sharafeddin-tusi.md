## 요약

Sharafeddin Tusi was a Persian mathematician who made significant advances in the study of cubic equations, developing methods for finding their solutions and determining the conditions for the existence of positive roots. His work anticipated concepts in differential calculus and was influential in the development of algebraic geometry.
