## 요약

Yellapragada Subbarow was an Indian-American biochemist who discovered the function of adenosine triphosphate (ATP) as an energy source in cells. He also developed methotrexate, one of the first anticancer drugs, and discovered the role of folic acid in treating anemia. Despite his groundbreaking contributions, he received little recognition during his lifetime. His work has saved millions of lives.
