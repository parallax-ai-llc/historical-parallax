## 개요

Ernest Walton, with John Cockcroft, achieved the first artificial splitting of the atomic nucleus in 1932 at Cambridge. Their Cockcroft-Walton accelerator used high voltage to accelerate protons to split lithium atoms. He shared the 1951 노벨상 in Physics and is the only Irish Nobel laureate in the sciences.
