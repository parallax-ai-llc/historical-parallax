## 개요

이창동 is a Korean filmmaker and novelist whose humanistic films explore ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances. Peppermint Candy, Oasis, Poetry, and Burning are masterworks of contemporary Korean cinema. He also served as 대한민국's Minister of Culture and Tourism.
