## 주요 업적

Diponegoro's most 중요한 achievement was organizing and sustaining a guerrilla 전쟁 that brought the Dutch colonial administration to the brink of collapse. Diponegoro's military strategy showcased sophisticated tactical innovation adapted to Java's terrain and his forces' capabilities. Rather than engaging in conventional set-piece battles where Dutch firepower would prove decisive, he implemented a comprehensive guerrilla warfare campaign.[^21] His forces employed hit-and-run tactics, ambushes, and rapid strikes that exploited their intimate knowledge of local geography. Javanese fighters used the island's rugged landscapes, dense jungles, and mountainous terrain to nullify Dutch technological advantages in specific tactical scenarios.[^22]

The rebels' infrastructure disruption tactics proved particularly effective. Diponegoro ordered villagers to cut down trees to block roads, burn wooden bridges, dig up road surfaces, and plant sharpened bamboo stakes in travel routes to prevent Dutch forces from counterattacking or bringing in reinforcements.[^23] These methods disrupted Dutch communications and supply lines, forcing colonial troops into defensive positions.

For the first three years of the 분쟁, Diponegoro achieved stunning successes. Diponegoro's charismatic leadership proved crucial to sustaining resistance. ### 긍정적 평가

Scholarly and popular assessments in Indonesia overwhelmingly celebrate Diponegoro as a heroic figure of resistance.
