## 개요

Jack Szostak is a Canadian-British-American biologist who shared the 2009 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for the discovery of how chromosomes are protected by telomeres and the enzyme telomerase. He later shifted his research focus to the origin of life, studying how simple chemical systems can develop into living cells.
