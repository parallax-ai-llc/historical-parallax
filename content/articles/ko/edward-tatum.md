## 개요

Edward Tatum 미국의 유전학자 and 생화학자 who shared the 1958 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 with George Beadle for demonstrating that genes regulate chemical processes. Their experiments with the bread mold Neurospora crassa showed that specific genes control specific enzymes. He later 에 기여했다 bacterial genetics and recombination research with Joshua Lederberg.
