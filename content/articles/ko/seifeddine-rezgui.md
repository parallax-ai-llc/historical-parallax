## 요약
Seifeddine Rezgui was a Tunisian student who carried out the 2015 Sousse beach attack, killing 38 tourists (30 of them British) at the Riu Imperial Marhaba hotel in Tunisia. He was affiliated with ISIS. He was shot dead by police.
