## 개요

김린수 is a Korean scholar of technology and innovation policy who analyzed the mechanisms behind 대한민국's technological catch-up. His research at Korea University documented how Korean firms absorbed and improved upon foreign technologies to become global leaders, providing a model for other developing countries' industrialization strategies.
