## 개요

The Great Depression, triggered by the 1929 stock market crash, was the worst economic downturn in 현대의 history. Global GDP fell 15%, unemployment reached 25% in the US, and the crisis 에 기여했다 the rise of 파시즘 and World 전쟁 II.
