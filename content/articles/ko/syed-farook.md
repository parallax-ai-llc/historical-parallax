## 추가 맥락

### 배경

Syed Rizwan Farook was a U.S. resident whose public profile largely entered the historical record through the 2 December 2015 mass shooting in San Bernardino, California. Publicly available accounts typically describe his employment and personal history only insofar as it relates to the investigation and the timeline leading up to the attack.

### San Bernardino attack (overview)

The San Bernardino attack occurred during a gathering associated with a county workplace event. Fourteen people were killed and many others were injured. The incident became one of the major U.S. mass-casualty attacks of the mid-2010s and generated extensive law-enforcement and media coverage.

Following the shooting, Farook and Malik fled the scene. They were later located and killed during a confrontation with law enforcement.

### Investigation and public impact

The case prompted wide public discussion about domestic security, counterterrorism investigations, and the operational challenges of preventing lone-actor or small-cell violence. It also became part of broader debates about workplace safety, emergency response, and community support for victims and survivors.
