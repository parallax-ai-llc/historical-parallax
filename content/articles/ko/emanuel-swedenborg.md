## 개요

Emanuel Swedenborg 스웨덴의 과학자, 철학자, and Christian 신비주의자 who claimed to have experienced visions of the spiritual world. Originally a 과학자 and 발명가, he underwent a spiritual crisis at age 56 and spent the rest of his life writing theological works based on his claimed spiritual experiences. His voluminous writings, including Heaven and Hell, describe the afterlife in elaborate detail. His followers founded the New Church (Swedenborgian Church) ~ 후에 his death.
