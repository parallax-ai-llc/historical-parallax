## 요약

Serge Haroche is a French physicist who shared the 2012 Nobel Prize in Physics with David Wineland for ground-breaking experimental methods that enable measuring and manipulation of individual quantum systems. He developed methods to trap and study individual photons, contributing to the development of quantum computing and quantum information.
