## 요약

Tyler Perry built a $1 billion entertainment empire from Madea stage plays, becoming the most commercially successful African American filmmaker and studio owner. He owns Tyler Perry Studios, a 330-acre studio complex in Atlanta that is the largest in the country. He has produced dozens of films, TV shows, and stage productions. He became a billionaire in 2020. His studio, built on the site of a former Confederate army base, symbolizes Black economic empowerment.
