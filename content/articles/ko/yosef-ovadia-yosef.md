## 요약

Rabbi Ovadia Yosef was one of the greatest Sephardic halakhic authorities of the modern era. His encyclopedic knowledge and courageous legal rulings transformed the religious landscape of Israel. He founded the Shas political party and served as Sephardi Chief Rabbi. His funeral attracted 850,000 mourners, making it one of the largest in Israeli history.
