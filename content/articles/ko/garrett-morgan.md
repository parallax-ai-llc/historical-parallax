## 개요

Garrett Morgan was an African American 발명가 who created the traffic signal and the safety hood (an 초기의 gas mask). His gas mask was used to rescue workers trapped in a tunnel under Lake Erie in 1916. His three-position traffic signal patent was purchased by 장군 Electric. He overcame 중요한 racial barriers to achieve commercial success.
