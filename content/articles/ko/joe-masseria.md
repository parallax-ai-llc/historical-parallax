## 개요
Giuseppe 'Joe the Boss' Masseria was the leader of the Masseria crime family. His war with Salvatore Maranzano — the Castellammarese War — was one of the bloodiest chapters in American Mafia history. He was assassinated while playing cards at a Coney Island restaurant.
