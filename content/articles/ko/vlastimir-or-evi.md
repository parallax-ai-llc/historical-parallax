## 요약

Serbian police general convicted of crimes against humanity for his role in the deportation and persecution of Kosovo Albanians during the 1999 Kosovo War. He oversaw police forces that committed widespread atrocities.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1999 | Directed police operations during Kosovo War |
| 2007 | Arrested in Montenegro |
| 2011 | Convicted by ICTY of crimes against humanity |
| 2014 | Sentence confirmed at 18 years on appeal |
