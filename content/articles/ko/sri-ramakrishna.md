## 요약

Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa was an Indian Hindu mystic and saint who taught that all religions lead to the same ultimate reality. He practiced devotion within multiple religious traditions, including Christianity and Islam, claiming to have experienced God in each. His teachings inspired the Ramakrishna Movement and his chief disciple Swami Vivekananda. He is regarded as one of the greatest modern Hindu saints.
