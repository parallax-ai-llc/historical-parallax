## 개요

The 14th-generation Imaizumi Imaemon is a Japanese ceramicist who carries on the 350-year tradition of Nabeshima ware, the finest porcelain of the Saga domain. His delicate overglaze enameling continues a family tradition designated as Important Intangible Cultural Property.
