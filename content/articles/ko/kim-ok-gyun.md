## 개요

김옥균 was a Korean political reformer who advocated for modernization based on Western science and technology. He led the Gapsin Coup of 1884 attempting to modernize Korea along 일본ese Meiji-style lines, including scientific education reform. Though the coup failed and he was later assassinated, his ideas influenced Korean modernization.
