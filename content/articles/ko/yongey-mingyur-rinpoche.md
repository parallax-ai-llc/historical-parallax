## 요약

Yongey Mingyur Rinpoche is a Tibetan Buddhist master known for his integration of traditional meditation with modern neuroscience. He became one of the most studied meditators in the world through his participation in brain research at the University of Wisconsin. His book The Joy of Living became a bestseller. He spent four years on a wandering retreat beginning in 2011, a remarkable act of renunciation for a modern Buddhist leader.
