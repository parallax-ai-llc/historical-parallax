## 유명한 인용문

> "Knowledge itself is power." (*Meditationes Sacrae*, 1597)[^160]

This is Bacon's most 유명한 aphorism, originally written in Latin as "ipsa scientia potestas est." It expresses his fundamental conviction that understanding nature's laws grants humanity the ability to harness natural forces for practical benefit, fundamentally linking epistemology to human capability.

> "Human knowledge and human power meet in one; for where the cause is not known the effect cannot be produced. Nature to be commanded must be obeyed." (*Novum Organum*, 1620)[^161]

This quote captures Bacon's empirical 철학: true power over nature comes not from imposing theories but from understanding natural laws ~을 통해 observation and experiment. Commanding nature requires first submitting to its regularities.

> "I have taken all knowledge to be my province." (Letter to William Cecil)[^162]

Bacon's declaration of intellectual ambition reveals his comprehensive vision and refusal to limit his investigations to narrow specialization. This statement embodies the 르네상스 ideal of the universal 학자.

> "A little philosophy inclineth man's mind to atheism, but depth in philosophy bringeth men's minds about to religion." (*Essays*, "Of Atheism")[^163]

This observation on the relationship between philosophical inquiry and religious belief suggests that superficial thought leads to skepticism, ~하는 동안 profound investigation reveals deeper truths pointing toward the divine.

> "Some men covet knowledge out of a natural curiosity and inquisitive temper; some to entertain the mind with variety and delight; some for ornament and reputation... But if any man endeavour to establish and extend the power and dominion of the human race itself over the universe, his ambition is without doubt both a more wholesome and a more noble thing than the other two." (*The Great Instauration*)[^164]

Bacon here distinguishes between mere intellectual curiosity or vanity and his own vision of knowledge pursued for humanity's practical benefit—the core of his utilitarian conception of 과학.
