## 개요

Flossie Wong-Staal was the first 과학자 to clone HIV and map its genes, which was essential to developing a blood test for AIDS. Her work at the NIH helped prove that HIV causes AIDS and identified the virus's genome, enabling the development of antiviral treatments. She was the most cited female 과학자 in the 1980s and 1990s.
