## 배경

- The contemporary slave trade in 리비아 is frequently discussed in the context of irregular migration routes across North 아프리카 toward 유럽.
- Smuggling networks, armed groups, and weak law enforcement created conditions where migrants and refugees became vulnerable to trafficking and forced labor.

## Forms of Exploitation Reported

- Forced labor, extortion, and detention in abusive conditions.
- Sale of migrants between groups, including reports of open-air “auctions” in some areas.
- Sexual exploitation and violence, particularly against women and minors, as documented by multiple humanitarian organizations.

## 국제적 대응

- Media coverage in 2017 prompted diplomatic statements and calls for action, including condemnation by international bodies.
- Assistance efforts often focused on evacuation, protection, and voluntary return programs, though effectiveness depends on access, security, and political cooperation.

## Ongoing Challenges

- The phenomenon is not a single centralized “market” but a set of practices embedded in conflict economies and transnational criminal networks.
- Reducing harm requires both immediate protection measures and longer-term stabilization, policing, and legal accountability.
