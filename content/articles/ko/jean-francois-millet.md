## 개요

Jean-Francois Millet was a French painter known for dignified depictions of rural laborers. His iconic paintings The Gleaners and The Angelus became among the most widely reproduced images in art history. A founding member of the Barbizon school, his reverent treatment of working people profoundly influenced Van Gogh.
