## 개요

J. Gresham Machen was an American Presbyterian theologian and New Testament scholar who was a leading opponent of theological liberalism. His book Christianity and Liberalism argued that liberal Christianity was a fundamentally different religion from orthodox Christianity. He founded Westminster Theological Seminary and the Orthodox Presbyterian Church. He was one of the most important conservative Protestant theologians of the 20th century.
