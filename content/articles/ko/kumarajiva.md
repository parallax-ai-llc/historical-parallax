## 개요

Kumarajiva was a Buddhist monk and scholar who was one of the greatest translators of Buddhist texts from Sanskrit into Chinese. His translations of the Lotus Sutra, Heart Sutra, Diamond Sutra, and other key texts became the standard versions used throughout 동아시아. His clear, literary translation style made Buddhist philosophy accessible to Chinese readers. He translated over 300 volumes during his career in Chang'an.
