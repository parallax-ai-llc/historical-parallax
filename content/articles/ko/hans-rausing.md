## 개요

Hans Rausing was a Swedish billionaire who ran Tetra Pak, the world's largest food packaging company, founded by his father Ruben Rausing. He expanded Tetra Pak into a global packaging empire whose carton containers transformed how food and beverages are distributed worldwide. The company's aseptic packaging technology made it possible to store milk and juice without refrigeration, benefiting billions of people in developing countries.
