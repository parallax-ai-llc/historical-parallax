## 요약

Vittorio Colao served as CEO of Vodafone Group from 2008 to 2018, leading one of the world's largest telecommunications companies. Under his leadership, Vodafone sold its 45% stake in Verizon Wireless for $130 billion, the third-largest corporate deal in history. He also led Vodafone's expansion into Africa and mobile money services (M-Pesa). He later served as Italy's Minister of Technological Innovation.
