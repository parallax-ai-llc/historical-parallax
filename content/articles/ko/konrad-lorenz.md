## 개요

콘라트 로렌츠 was an 오스트리아n zoologist and ethologist who shared the 1973 노벨상 in Physiology or Medicine for discoveries about animal behavior patterns. He is considered one of the founders of modern ethology (the study of animal behavior). He discovered the principle of imprinting, in which young animals form attachments during a critical period. His popular books brought ethology to a wide audience.
