## 요약

Walter Brattain co-invented the point-contact transistor with John Bardeen at Bell Labs in December 1947, initiating the semiconductor revolution. His experimental skills complemented Bardeen's theoretical insights in creating the first transistor. He shared the 1956 Nobel Prize in Physics with Bardeen and William Shockley.
