## 요약

Se-Jung Oh is a Korean physicist known for his research on surface physics and X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy. He made significant contributions to understanding the electronic structure of materials and surface phenomena. He trained many leading Korean physicists and helped build South Korea's research infrastructure in materials physics.
