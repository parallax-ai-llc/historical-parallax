## 요약

Thomas Lipton founded the Lipton tea brand, building it into the world's best-selling tea. He revolutionized the tea industry by buying plantations directly in Ceylon (Sri Lanka), cutting out middlemen, and selling affordable tea to the mass market. His marketing innovations included colorful packaging and advertising. He was also a passionate yachtsman who challenged for the America's Cup five times. Lipton tea remains one of the most consumed beverages worldwide.
