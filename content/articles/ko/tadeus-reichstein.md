## 요약

Tadeus Reichstein was a Polish-Swiss chemist who shared the 1950 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for discovering cortisone and other adrenal cortex hormones. He also independently developed a process for synthesizing vitamin C (the Reichstein process), which remains the basis for industrial vitamin C production today.
