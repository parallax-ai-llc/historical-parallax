## 개요

김승연 is the chairman of Hanwha Group, one of 대한민국's ten largest conglomerates with interests in explosives, defense, solar energy, petrochemicals, and finance. He transformed Hanwha from an explosives company into a diversified conglomerate. Hanwha Solutions became one of the world's largest solar panel manufacturers. He was convicted of assault and embezzlement but received presidential pardons.
