## 개요

Huang Wei is a Chinese materials scientist who has made significant contributions to organic optoelectronics at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research on organic light-emitting materials, flexible electronics, and bioelectronics has advanced China's capabilities in next-generation display and wearable technologies.
