## 개요

Glenn Seaborg 미국의 화학자 who shared the 1951 노벨상 in Chemistry for discoveries of transuranium elements. He co-discovered ten elements including plutonium, americium, curium, and seaborgium (named ~ 후에 him ~하는 동안 still alive, a unique honor). He served as chairman of the US Atomic Energy Commission under three presidents. Seaborg's actinide concept reorganized the periodic table.
