## 개요

존 밀너 is an American mathematician who made pioneering discoveries in topology, including exotic spheres — 7-dimensional spheres with nonstandard smooth structures. He received the Fields Medal in 1962 and the Abel Prize in 2011. He is one of only a few mathematicians to have received both awards.
