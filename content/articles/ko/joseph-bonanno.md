## 개요
Joseph Charles Bonanno was the founder and longtime leader of the Bonanno crime family. He was one of the youngest bosses in Mafia history. His attempt to seize control of other families led to the 'Banana War' and his forced retirement. He wrote an autobiography and died of natural causes at 97.
