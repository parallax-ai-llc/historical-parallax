## 개요

Hamza Yusuf is an American Islamic scholar who is one of the most influential Muslim intellectuals in the West. He co-founded Zaytuna College in Berkeley, California, the first accredited Muslim liberal arts college in the United States. He has been a bridge figure between Islamic scholarship and Western academic life. He emphasizes classical Islamic learning and interfaith understanding.
