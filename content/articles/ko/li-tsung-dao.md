## 개요

리정다오 (Tsung-Dao Lee) was a Chinese-American physicist who shared the 1957 노벨 물리학상 with Yang Zhenning for proposing that parity is not conserved in weak nuclear interactions. At age 30, he was one of the youngest Nobel laureates. He also made important contributions to statistical mechanics and field theory.
