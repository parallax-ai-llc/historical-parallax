## 요약
Rosemary West was convicted of 10 murders committed with husband Fred West at 25 Cromwell Street, Gloucester. She participated in rape, torture, and murder of young women, including her stepdaughter. She received a whole life order and will never be released.

## 초기 생애
Born November 29, 1953, in Barnstaple, Devon. Her father was violent and suffered from schizophrenia. She was sexually abused as a child. She met Fred West at 15 and married him in 1972. [^1]

## 범죄 경력
Together with Fred, she abducted, tortured, and murdered young women through the 1970s-80s. She also killed Charmaine West (Fred's stepdaughter) while Fred was in prison, showing independent criminal capacity. She murdered her own daughter Heather in 1987. [^2]

## 체포 / 몰락
After Fred's suicide in January 1995, Rosemary stood trial alone at Winchester Crown Court. Convicted of 10 murders on November 22, 1995. A whole life tariff was imposed in 1997. [^3]

## 긍정적 관점
- Her conviction demonstrated accountability for co-perpetrators [^4]
- Led to strengthened child protection policies
- Prompted victims' rights reforms

## 부정적 관점
- Convicted of 10 murders including stepdaughter and daughter [^5]
- Actively participated in sexual torture and murder
- Sexually abused her own children
- Maintained complete denial showing no remorse
