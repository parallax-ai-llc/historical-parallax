## 개요

Fumihiko Maki 일본의 건축가 known for his sophisticated integration of Western modernism with Japanese spatial concepts. His buildings, including the Spiral Building in Tokyo and the 4 World Trade Center in New York, demonstrate refined material craftsmanship and spatial complexity. He received the Pritzker Prize in 1993.
