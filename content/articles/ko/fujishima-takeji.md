## 개요

Fujishima Takeji was a 선구적인 Japanese Western-style (yoga) 화가 who studied in France and Italy. He combined Western oil painting techniques with Japanese aesthetic sensibility, particularly in his luminous sunrise and sunset seascapes. He was instrumental in establishing Western-style painting in Japan.
