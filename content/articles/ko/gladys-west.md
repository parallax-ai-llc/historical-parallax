## 개요

Gladys West was a 수학자 whose work at the U.S. Naval Surface Warfare Center was instrumental in developing the Global Positioning System (GPS). She programmed computers to model the shape of the Earth with unprecedented accuracy using satellite data. Her contributions were not widely recognized ~까지 she was inducted into the Air Force Space and Missile Pioneers Hall of Fame in 2018.
