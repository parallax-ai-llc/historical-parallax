## 개요

George de Mestral 스위스의 electrical 공학자 who invented Velcro ~ 후에 noticing how burrs stuck to his dog's fur ~ 동안 a walk in the Alps. Examining the burrs under a microscope, he saw tiny hooks that caught on loops of fabric, inspiring the hook-and-loop fastener. It took him over a 10년 to perfect the product. Velcro is now used in countless applications from clothing to space exploration.
