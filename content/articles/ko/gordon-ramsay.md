## 개요

Gordon Ramsay built a global restaurant empire and media career from his Michelin-starred restaurants. His Gordon Ramsay Restaurants group operates over 35 restaurants worldwide, including Restaurant Gordon Ramsay (three Michelin stars ~ 이래로 2001). His TV shows Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, and MasterChef made him 세계's most 유명한 셰프. His business acumen transformed him from a 셰프 into a global media and hospitality brand.
