## 개요

이중근 was the founder of Dongkuk Steel Group, one of 대한민국's major steel producers. He played an important role in the development of 대한민국's steel industry during the country's rapid industrialization. Dongkuk Steel supplied materials for major construction projects including bridges, buildings, and infrastructure across Korea.
