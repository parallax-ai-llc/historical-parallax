## 개요

Gerhard Sessler co-invented the electret microphone with James West in 1962 and also invented the silicon microphone (MEMS microphone) used in 현대의 smartphones. His electret microphone is the most widely used microphone type in 세계. He holds over 200 patents in acoustics and sensor 기술.
