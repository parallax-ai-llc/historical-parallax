## 개요

Gregory Winter 영국의 생화학자 who shared the 2018 노벨상 in Chemistry for the phage display of peptides and antibodies. He used phage display to evolve new antibodies for therapeutic use, leading to the development of adalimumab (Humira), one of the best-selling drugs in history. His work revolutionized the development of antibody-based medicines.
