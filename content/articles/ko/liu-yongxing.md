## 개요

류융싱 is a Chinese billionaire and chairman of the East Hope Group, one of 중국's largest private conglomerates with interests in heavy industry, aluminum, chemicals, and agriculture. He is one of the four Liu brothers who built the Hope Group from a small animal feed business into one of 중국's earliest private empires. East Hope became a major aluminum producer and one of 중국's most profitable private companies.
