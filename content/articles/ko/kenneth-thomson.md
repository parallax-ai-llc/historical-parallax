## 개요

케네스 톰슨, 2nd Baron Thomson of Fleet, expanded his father's media empire and grew the Thomson Corporation into a leading provider of electronic information services. He shifted the company from newspapers to specialized information, anticipating the digital age. He was 캐나다's richest person and an avid art collector whose collection was donated to the Art Gallery of Ontario. He managed one of the most successful media-to-data transitions in business history.
