## 요약

Santideva was an Indian Buddhist monk and scholar at Nalanda University whose Bodhicaryavatara (Guide to the Bodhisattva's Way of Life) is one of the most beloved texts in all of Buddhism. This poetic masterpiece on compassion, patience, and the bodhisattva ideal has been central to Tibetan Buddhism and is frequently taught by the Dalai Lama. His other major work, the Siksasamuccaya, compiled Buddhist scriptural passages on training.
