## 개요

Armed bandits attacked an Abuja-Kaduna train killing 8 and kidnapping over 60 passengers.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 2022 | Train attacked, passengers kidnapped |

## Background and Context

- The Abuja–Kaduna rail line is a key passenger corridor in northern 나이지리아.
- In the years leading up to 2022, parts of northwestern and north-central 나이지리아 faced increased insecurity, including banditry and kidnappings for ransom.

## The Attack (28 March 2022)

- Armed attackers targeted a passenger train traveling between Abuja and Kaduna.
- Reports describe explosives or sabotage used to stop the train, followed by gunmen boarding and firing.
- Passengers were forced off the train and taken into nearby bush areas.

## Casualties and Abductions

- At least 8 people were reported killed in initial reporting.
- Dozens of passengers were abducted; widely cited figures are over 60 kidnapped.
- Some passengers escaped during the chaos or were later rescued/released.

## Response and Aftermath

- 나이지리아n authorities announced security operations to locate the abducted passengers.
- The incident drew national attention to rail security and broader regional violence.
- Train services on the route were affected in the period following the attack.

## Significance

- The attack became one of the most prominent rail-related mass-kidnapping incidents in 나이지리아 in the 2020s.
- It highlighted the vulnerability of civilian transport infrastructure during periods of heightened insecurity.
