## 개요

가와구치 마모루 is a 일본ese structural engineer known for designing innovative large-span structures using tensegrity and space frame technology. He designed the roof structures for the Sant Jordi Sports Palace for the 1992 Barcelona Olympics and numerous other major structures. His engineering pushes the boundaries of what's structurally possible.
