## 요약

Yi Sugwang was a Joseon Dynasty scholar-diplomat who authored Jibong Yuseol, the first Korean encyclopedia covering science, technology, and natural phenomena. Through diplomatic missions to China, he introduced Western scientific concepts including the telescope, European geography, and Catholicism to Korea. His encyclopedia bridged Eastern and Western knowledge.
