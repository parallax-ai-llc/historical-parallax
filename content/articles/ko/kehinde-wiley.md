## 개요

케힌데 와일리 is an American portrait painter known for his vibrant, large-scale paintings of Black subjects in poses from Old Master paintings against ornate floral backgrounds. He gained worldwide fame for painting the official portrait of President Barack Obama in 2018. His work addresses race, power, and representation in art history.
