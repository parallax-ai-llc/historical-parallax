## 개요

Chicago Pile-1, 세계's first nuclear reactor, achieved a self-sustaining nuclear chain reaction on December 2, 1942, under Enrico Fermi's direction. The experiment proved that nuclear energy could be harnessed, paving the way for both nuclear weapons and power.
