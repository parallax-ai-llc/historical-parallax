## 요약

Takemitsu Takizaki is the founder of Keyence Corporation, one of Japan's most valuable companies and the world's leading manufacturer of industrial sensors and automation equipment. Keyence is known for its exceptionally high profit margins (over 50%) and its direct sales model that eliminates distributors. Takizaki was Japan's richest person in 2021. Keyence's market cap exceeds $100 billion despite relatively modest revenue.
