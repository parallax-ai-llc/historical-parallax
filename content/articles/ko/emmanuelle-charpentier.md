## 개요

Emmanuelle Charpentier 프랑스의 미생물학자 and 생화학자 who shared the 2020 노벨상 in Chemistry with Jennifer Doudna for developing the CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing method. She discovered the tracrRNA molecule essential for the CRISPR system ~하는 동안 studying Streptococcus pyogenes. Her collaboration with Doudna transformed CRISPR from a bacterial defense mechanism into a 혁명가 gene editing tool.
