## 개요

이용희 is a Korean nanotechnologist at Sungkyunkwan University who has made significant contributions to graphene and carbon nanotube research. His work on growing and characterizing graphene has advanced Korea's capabilities in nanomaterials, supporting the development of next-generation electronic devices.
