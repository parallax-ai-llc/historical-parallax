## 개요

Drew Weissman 미국의 의사-과학자 who shared the 2023 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 with Katalin Karikó for their discoveries enabling mRNA vaccines. His work on innate immune responses to mRNA ~로 이어졌다 the 비판적인 discovery that modified nucleosides prevent inflammatory reactions, making therapeutic mRNA possible. This discovery enabled the rapid development of COVID-19 mRNA vaccines.
