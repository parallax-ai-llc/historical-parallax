## 요약

Sofya Kovalevskaya was a Russian mathematician who made pioneering contributions to analysis, partial differential equations, and mechanics. She was the first major female mathematician in modern history, the first woman to earn a doctorate in mathematics in Europe, and the first woman appointed to a full professorship in Northern Europe at Stockholm University.
