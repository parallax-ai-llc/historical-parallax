## 개요

Gabe Newell co-founded Valve Corporation, which created the Steam digital distribution platform and iconic games including Half-Life, Portal, and Counter-Strike. Steam ~이 되었다 dominant PC gaming platform with over 120 million active users. He was a former Microsoft employee who left to start Valve. He also launched the Steam Deck handheld gaming device. Valve's unique flat management structure with no formal hierarchy is legendary in the tech industry.
