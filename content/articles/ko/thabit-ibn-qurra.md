## 요약

Thabit ibn Qurra was an Arab mathematician, physician, and astronomer who made important contributions to mathematics and astronomy during the Islamic Golden Age. He generalized the Pythagorean theorem and proved Euclid's parallel postulate equivalent. He translated numerous Greek mathematical works into Arabic, preserving them for posterity, and made advances in number theory.
