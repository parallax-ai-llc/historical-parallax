## 요약

Zaha Hadid was an Iraqi-British architect who was the first woman to receive the Pritzker Architecture Prize. Her futuristic, fluid designs challenged architectural conventions with their dynamic curves and bold geometries. Buildings like the Heydar Aliyev Center in Baku and the MAXXI museum in Rome redefined contemporary architecture.
