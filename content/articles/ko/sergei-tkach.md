## 요약
Sergei Tkach was a Ukrainian serial killer and former police forensic expert convicted of 37 murders committed between 1980 and 2005. He killed young girls and women across central Ukraine. His expertise in forensic science helped him avoid leaving evidence. Multiple innocent men were convicted for his crimes.

## 초기 생애
Born September 15, 1952, in the Ukrainian SSR. He worked as a forensic expert for the police, giving him detailed knowledge of investigation techniques. This expertise allowed him to systematically clean crime scenes. [^1]

## 범죄 경력
Between 1980 and 2005, Tkach murdered at least 37 girls and women aged 8-18 across central Ukraine. He raped and strangled his victims. Using his forensic expertise, he meticulously cleaned crime scenes. At least eight innocent men were convicted and imprisoned for his murders, with one committing suicide in prison. [^2]

## 체포 / 몰락
Tkach was arrested in 2005 after advances in DNA technology overcame his forensic countermeasures. He confessed to 100+ murders, though 37 were confirmed. He was convicted and sentenced to life imprisonment in 2007. [^3]

## 긍정적 관점
- His capture led to the exoneration of several wrongfully convicted men [^4]
- Prompted reforms in Ukrainian forensic investigation oversight
- Highlighted the danger of criminal justice insiders committing crimes
