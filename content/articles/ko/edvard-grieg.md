## 개요

Edvard Grieg was the leading Norwegian 작곡가 who established a distinctive Norwegian musical identity. His Piano Concerto in A minor, Peer Gynt suites, and Lyric Pieces for piano are among the most popular works in the 고전적인 repertoire. His 음악 draws deeply on Norwegian folk melodies and harmonies.
