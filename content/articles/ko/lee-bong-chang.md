## 개요

이봉창 was a Korean independence activist who attempted to assassinate 일본ese Emperor Hirohito in 1932. While not a scientist, his action was part of the broader Korean independence movement that fought for the self-determination necessary for Korea to develop its own scientific and technological capabilities.
