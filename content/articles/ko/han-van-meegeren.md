## 개요

Dutch art forger who became famous for creating fake Vermeer paintings. He sold a fake Vermeer to Nazi Hermann Göring. When accused of collaborating with the enemy, he proved in court that he had actually forged the paintings.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1937 | Created first fake Vermeer |
| 1943 | Sold forged Vermeer to Hermann Göring |
| 1945 | Arrested for collaborating with Nazis |
| 1947 | Convicted of forgery; died of heart attack before serving sentence |
