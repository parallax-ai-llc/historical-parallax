## 요약

Xu Beihong was a pioneering Chinese painter known for combining Western techniques with Chinese ink painting, particularly in his dynamic paintings of galloping horses. He studied in Paris and championed realism in Chinese art education as president of the Central Academy of Fine Arts. His horses remain among the most iconic images in modern Chinese art.
