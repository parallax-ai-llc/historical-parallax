## 개요

Austrian serial killer and writer who murdered women in Austria, Czechoslovakia, and the United States. He became a literary celebrity while in prison for his first murder, was released, then killed again. He hanged himself after conviction.

| 1994 | Convicted and committed suicide in prison |
