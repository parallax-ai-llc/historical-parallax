## 요약

Shoji Hamada was a Japanese potter who was a leading figure in the mingei (folk craft) movement alongside Yanagi Soetsu. He was designated a Living National Treasure of Japan in 1955. His functional stoneware, with its distinctive wax-resist and brushwork decoration, combined Japanese folk traditions with international studio pottery.
