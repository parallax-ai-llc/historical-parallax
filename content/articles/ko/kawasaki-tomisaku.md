## 개요

가와사키 도미사쿠 was a 일본ese pediatrician who first identified Kawasaki disease in 1967, a childhood illness causing inflammation of blood vessels throughout the body. His careful clinical observation and documentation of 50 cases established a new disease entity that affects children worldwide. Untreated, it can cause coronary artery problems.
