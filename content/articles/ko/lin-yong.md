## 맥락

Because much of the underlying activity (e.g., detention, medical testing, transplant logistics, and hospital reporting) is not transparent to outside observers, many claims rely on a mix of witness testimony, statistical analysis, and investigative reporting.

Key terms that often appear in this context:

- **Transplant system**: hospitals, transplant registries, and organ allocation mechanisms.
- **Detention and screening**: accounts of detainees undergoing blood tests and medical exams consistent with organ-compatibility screening.
