## 개요

레오 실라르드 was a Hungarian-American physicist who conceived the nuclear chain reaction in 1933 and patented the idea of a nuclear fission reactor. He co-drafted the Einstein-Szilard letter that led to the Manhattan Project. After the war, he became a vocal advocate for arms control.
