## 개요
Gary Leon Ridgway, the 'Green River Killer,' is convicted of 49 murders and confessed to possibly 71 in the Seattle area between 1982 and 1998. The most prolific convicted serial killer in U.S. history, he targeted sex workers along the Pacific Highway, strangling them and dumping bodies near the Green River.
