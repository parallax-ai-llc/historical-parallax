## 개요

Although primarily a political thinker, 가토 히로유키 was an important figure in introducing Western science and technology to Meiji 일본. As president of 도쿄 Imperial University, he established 일본's modern scientific education system and advocated for Social Darwinism and the adoption of Western scientific methods throughout 일본ese universities.
