## 요약

Uday Kotak is the founder of Kotak Mahindra Bank, India's fourth-largest private sector bank. Starting with a non-banking finance company in 1985, he obtained a banking license in 2003 — the first such license given to a private company in Indian banking history. He grew the bank to over $60 billion in assets. He also served as president of the Confederation of Indian Industry and sits on multiple global advisory boards.
