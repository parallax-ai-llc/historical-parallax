## 개요

German forger who created the fake 'Hitler Diaries' that fooled Stern magazine and historian Hugh Trevor-Roper in 1983. The hoax was one of the biggest media scandals of the 20th century. He later sold paintings as 'genuine Kujau fakes.'

## 경력 연표

| 1985 | Convicted of fraud; sentenced to 4.5 years |
| 2000 | Died of cancer |
