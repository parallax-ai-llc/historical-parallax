## 개요

Jim Simons was a mathematician and hedge fund manager who founded Renaissance Technologies, which operates the Medallion Fund—widely considered the most successful investment fund in history. The Medallion Fund averaged 66% annual returns before fees from 1988 to 2018. He pioneered quantitative trading using mathematical models and algorithms. He was also a major philanthropist, donating billions to mathematics, science, and education.
