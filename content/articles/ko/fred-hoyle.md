## 개요

Fred Hoyle 영국의 천문학자 who made 중요한 contributions to the theory of stellar nucleosynthesis and coined the term 'Big Bang' (originally as a derisive term). He showed how elements heavier than helium are created inside stars. 비록 ~이지만 his steady-state theory of the universe was superseded by the Big Bang theory, his work on nucleosynthesis was 획기적인. He controversially did not share the 노벨상 awarded to his collaborator William Fowler.
