## 요약

Stanislaw Ulam was a Polish-American mathematician who participated in the Manhattan Project and co-designed the thermonuclear weapon (hydrogen bomb) with Edward Teller (Teller-Ulam design). He invented the Monte Carlo method for numerical computation, proposed nuclear pulse propulsion (Project Orion), and made contributions to set theory and ergodic theory.
