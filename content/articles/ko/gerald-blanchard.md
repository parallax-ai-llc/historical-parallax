## 개요

Canadian master thief who stole the priceless Sisi Star from Schönbrunn Palace in Vienna and committed hundreds of bank robberies. He parachuted onto the palace roof to steal the diamond-encrusted star.

| 2000 | Conducted bank robberies across North America |
| 2007 | 체포됨 after lengthy investigation |
| 2009 | Sentenced ~에 8 years, returned Sisi Star |
