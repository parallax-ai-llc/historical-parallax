## 개요

Frederick Banting 캐나다의 medical 과학자 who co-discovered insulin in 1921 with Charles Best, revolutionizing the treatment of diabetes. He received the 1923 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학, becoming the youngest Nobel laureate in that category at the time. Banting shared his prize money with Best, who was excluded from the award. He sold the insulin patent for $1, believing it should be available to all.
