## 요약

Yu Gwan-sun was a Korean independence activist who organized the March 1st Movement demonstrations in 1919 at age 16. While not a scientist, she symbolizes Korean resilience and is one of the most revered figures in Korean history. She was arrested, tortured, and died in prison at age 17. She is often called the Korean Joan of Arc.
