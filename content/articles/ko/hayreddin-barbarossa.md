## 개요
Hayreddin Barbarossa (Khair ad-Din) was an Ottoman corsair and later admiral of the Ottoman Navy. He dominated the Mediterranean, raiding Christian ships and coastal towns. He became the most feared pirate of his era. He was made Kapudan Pasha (Grand Admiral) by Sultan Suleiman.
