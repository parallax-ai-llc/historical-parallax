## 개요

이부진 is the president of Hotel Shilla and daughter of 이건희. She has expanded Hotel Shilla into a luxury hotel chain and duty-free shopping operator. She oversees Samsung C&T's fashion division and hospitality businesses. She is considered one of the most powerful businesswomen in 대한민국 and has been mentioned as a potential successor in Samsung's broader leadership.
