## 개요

Hanabusa Itcho, while primarily known as a painter, also documented various technological innovations of the Edo period including mechanical devices, agricultural tools, and artisan techniques. His detailed illustrations of technology and daily life serve as important records of Edo-period Japanese technology and craftsmanship.
