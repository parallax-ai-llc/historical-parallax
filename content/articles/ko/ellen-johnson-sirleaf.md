## 초기 생애

Born October 29, 1938, in Monrovia, Liberia. Father was indigenous Gola, first native Liberian elected to legislature. Mother was part Kru, part German, educated.

**Education:**

**Historic Moment:**
First elected female head of state in Africa.

### 업적

**Economic Recovery:**

- Economic growth (though from low base)

Awarded for "non-violent struggle for safety of women and for women's rights to full participation in peace-building work." Shared with Leymah Gbowee and Yemen's Tawakkol Karman.

### Ebola Crisis (2014-2015)

West Africa Ebola outbreak devastated Liberia (~5,000 deaths). Sirleaf's government:

- 결국 mobilized, worked with international partners
- Outbreak contained by 2015
