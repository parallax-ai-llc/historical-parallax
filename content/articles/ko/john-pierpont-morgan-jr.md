## 개요

J.P. Morgan Jr., known as 'Jack,' succeeded his father as head of J.P. Morgan & Co. and was one of the most powerful bankers during 제1차 세계대전 and the interwar period. He organized massive loans to Britain and 프랑스 during WWI and was a target of a 1920 bombing on 월스트리트. He navigated the firm through the 1929 crash and the subsequent banking reforms of the New Deal era.
