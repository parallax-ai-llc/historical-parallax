## 개요

김정렴 was a Korean chemical engineer who played a key role in establishing 대한민국's petrochemical industry during the 1960s and 1970s. He helped plan and build major chemical plants that formed the backbone of Korea's industrial economy and contributed to the country's rapid economic development.
