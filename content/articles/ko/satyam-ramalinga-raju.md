## 배경

Ramalinga Raju was the founder of Satyam Computer Services, a major Indian IT services company during the period when India’s outsourcing and software industries were rapidly expanding and attracting global investors.

## The Satyam Scandal

The scandal is generally summarized as a prolonged accounting fraud involving overstated revenues, profits, cash balances, and other key financial statements. The resulting collapse became one of the most prominent corporate governance crises in India’s modern business history.

## Regulatory and Market Impact

The episode is often compared to other global accounting scandals because it raised questions about:

- Board oversight and audit quality.
- The reliability of reported financials in fast-growing public companies.
- Investor protections and enforcement capacity.

## 여파

Following the confession and ensuing crisis, Satyam underwent restructuring and was later acquired and integrated into another IT services group, a transition that was closely watched as a test of India’s ability to stabilize a large listed company after fraud.

## 유산

In business education and compliance discussions, the case is used as a cautionary example of how weak internal controls and concentrated founder influence can enable large-scale misstatement over time.
