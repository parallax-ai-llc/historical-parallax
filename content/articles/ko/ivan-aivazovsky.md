## 개요

Ivan Aivazovsky was a Russian-Armenian marine painter considered one of the greatest seascape artists in history. He created over 6,000 paintings, with The Ninth Wave exemplifying his ability to capture the sea's power and luminosity. He was also a major philanthropist.
