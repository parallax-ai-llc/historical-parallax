## 개요

Emil von Behring 독일의 의사 who won the first 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 in 1901 for his work on diphtheria antitoxin serum therapy. He developed antitoxin treatments for diphtheria and tetanus that saved countless lives, especially children. He is considered the father of serum therapy.
