## 요약

Yuri Milner is a Russian-Israeli billionaire investor who founded DST Global, one of the most successful technology investment firms. His early investments in Facebook, Twitter, Alibaba, Spotify, and Airbnb generated enormous returns. He funded the Breakthrough Prize, the world's largest science award ($3 million per prize), and the Breakthrough Starshot initiative to send spacecraft to Alpha Centauri. His aggressive investing style transformed Silicon Valley venture capital.
