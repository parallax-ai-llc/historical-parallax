## 요약

Nithin Kamath co-founded Zerodha in 2010, building it into India's largest stockbroker by active clients. Zerodha pioneered discount broking in India, offering free equity delivery trades and flat-fee options trading. Without taking any venture capital, Zerodha became one of the most profitable fintech companies in India. The company has over 12 million active clients and processes millions of daily trades.
