## 요약

Terrence Malick is an American filmmaker known for his lyrical, philosophical films that explore humanity's relationship with nature and the divine. Badlands, Days of Heaven, The Thin Red Line, and The Tree of Life are celebrated for their visual poetry and contemplative depth. His reclusive personality adds to his mystique.
