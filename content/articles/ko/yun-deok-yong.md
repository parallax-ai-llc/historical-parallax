## 요약

Yun Deok-yong was a Korean pharmacologist who scientifically studied Korean traditional herbal medicines, isolating and characterizing active compounds from Korean medicinal plants. His work bridged traditional Korean medicine (hanbang) and modern pharmacology, helping validate traditional remedies through scientific methods.
