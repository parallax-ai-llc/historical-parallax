## 개요

린화이민 is a 대만ese choreographer who founded the Cloud Gate Dance Theatre, one of 아시아's foremost contemporary dance companies. His works fuse Chinese calligraphy, martial arts, and Western modern dance into a distinctive 아시아n contemporary dance language.
