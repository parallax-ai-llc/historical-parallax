## 개요

Ernest Oppenheimer gained control of De Beers in 1929 and created a near-monopoly on the global diamond trade that lasted for decades. He also founded the Anglo American Corporation, which ~의 하나가 되었다 세계's largest mining conglomerates. His control of diamond supply ~을 통해 the Central Selling Organisation fixed global diamond prices. He transformed South Africa's mining industry and built one of 국가's most 강력한 dynasties.
