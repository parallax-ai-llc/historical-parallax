## 개요

Hideki Yukawa was a Japanese theoretical physicist who became the first Japanese citizen to win a Nobel Prize, receiving the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1949. He predicted the existence of the meson, the particle that mediates the strong nuclear force between nucleons. His meson theory was a breakthrough in understanding nuclear forces. Yukawa was also a prominent advocate for peace and nuclear disarmament.
