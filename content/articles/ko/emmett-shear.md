## 개요

Emmett Shear co-founded Twitch, the live-streaming platform for gamers that Amazon acquired for $970 million in 2014. He served as CEO of Twitch for over a 10년, growing it into the dominant platform for gaming content with over 31 million daily active users. He briefly served as interim CEO of OpenAI ~ 동안 November 2023 leadership crisis ~ 전에 Sam Altman returned.
