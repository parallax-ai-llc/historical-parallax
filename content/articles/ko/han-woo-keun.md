## 개요

Han Woo-keun was a Korean historian who wrote comprehensive histories of Korea including the authoritative 'The History of Korea.' His systematic historical methodology documented Korean scientific and technological achievements throughout Korean history, helping establish awareness of Korea's scientific heritage.
