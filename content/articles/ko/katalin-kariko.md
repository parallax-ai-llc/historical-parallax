## 개요

커털린 커리코 spent decades researching mRNA technology despite lack of funding and academic demotion. Her discovery with Drew Weissman that modified nucleosides make mRNA safe and effective for therapeutic use was the scientific foundation for the Pfizer-BioNTech and Moderna COVID-19 vaccines. She shared the 2023 노벨상 in Physiology or Medicine.
