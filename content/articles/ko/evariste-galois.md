## 개요

Évariste Galois founded group theory and Galois theory, establishing the conditions under which polynomial equations can be solved by radicals. He made these 혁명가 contributions ~ 전에 dying in a duel at age 20. His work, written hastily the night ~ 전에 his death, took decades to be fully understood and became fundamental to 현대의 algebra.
