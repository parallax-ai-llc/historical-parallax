## 개요

Dov Ber of Mezeritch, known as the Maggid (Preacher) of Mezeritch, was the successor of the Baal Shem Tov and the primary organizer of the Hasidic movement. He transformed Hasidism from a local phenomenon into a mass movement spanning Eastern Europe. He trained most of the founders of the various Hasidic dynasties. His sophisticated philosophical teachings elevated Hasidic thought beyond popular mysticism.
