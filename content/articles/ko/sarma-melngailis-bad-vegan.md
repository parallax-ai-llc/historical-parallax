## 요약

Latvian-American celebrity chef who ran New York's popular Pure Food and Wine restaurant and was convicted of fraud after her con-man husband Anthony Strangis manipulated her into stealing from employees.

## Additional Details

- **Public profile**: became a prominent figure in New York’s raw-vegan fine-dining scene through Pure Food and Wine and related business ventures.[^1]
- **Allegations**: court filings and reporting described misuse of funds and wage-related issues affecting employees and business partners; the case drew significant tabloid and food-industry attention.[^1]
- **Anthony Strangis**: her husband and co-defendant was portrayed in many accounts as a central driver of the fraud scheme; Melngailis later argued she was psychologically coerced, while prosecutors emphasized financial misconduct and contractual deception.[^1]
- **Legal resolution**: pleaded guilty and received a prison sentence; later sought post-conviction relief in court, which was covered in follow-up reporting.[^1]
- **Media adaptation**: the story was popularized internationally via the Netflix docuseries *Bad Vegan: Fame. Fraud. Fugitives.*[^1]
