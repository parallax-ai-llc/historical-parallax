## 요약

Steven Weinberg was an American theoretical physicist who shared the 1979 Nobel Prize in Physics for contributions to the unified theory of the weak and electromagnetic interaction. His electroweak unification was one of the greatest achievements of 20th-century physics. He authored influential textbooks and popular science books including 'The First Three Minutes.' Weinberg was a prominent advocate for reductionism in science.
