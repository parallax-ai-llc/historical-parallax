## 개요

Ibn al-Banna was a Moroccan mathematician and astronomer who wrote Talkhis Amal al-Hisab, a concise treatise on arithmetic that became the standard mathematics textbook in the Maghreb for centuries. He introduced new methods for algebraic computation and combinatorics. He also contributed to astronomy and wrote on the astrolabe.
