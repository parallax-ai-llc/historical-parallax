## 요약

Thailands role as a major transit point for methamphetamine from Myanmar and Laos. The Golden Triangle remains one of the worlds largest drug production areas.

## Regional Context

- Thailand borders Myanmar and Laos, placing it near long-standing production and trafficking corridors associated with the Golden Triangle.
- Drug markets in the region have shifted over time from plant-based narcotics (opium/heroin) toward synthetic stimulants.

## Trafficking Dynamics (High-Level)

- Transit routes adapt to enforcement pressure, often moving across remote border areas and through commercial logistics networks.
- Seizure totals can rise both from increased trafficking volume and from improved enforcement capacity.

## Domestic Impact

- Methamphetamine use can drive public health burdens, incarceration rates, and community safety concerns.
- Policy responses typically combine policing with prevention, treatment, and harm-reduction approaches (implementation varies by period and government).

## 주석

- "Golden Triangle" is a geographic and historical shorthand; production networks and actors change substantially across decades.
