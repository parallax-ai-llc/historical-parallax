## 개요

Ernest Everett Just was an African-American 생물학자 who made 선구적인 contributions to understanding cell biology, fertilization, and embryonic development. His research on marine organisms at Woods Hole demonstrated the importance of the cell surface (ectoplasm) in development. Facing racial 차별 in America, he spent much of his later career working in European laboratories. He published over 70 scientific papers and two books.
