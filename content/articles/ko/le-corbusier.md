## 개요

르 코르뷔지에 was one of the most influential architects of the 20th century, pioneering modern architecture with his five points of architecture and machine for living philosophy. His buildings including the Villa Savoye, Unite d'Habitation, and the chapel of Notre-Dame du Haut at Ronchamp are icons of modernism. His urban planning ideas shaped cities worldwide.
