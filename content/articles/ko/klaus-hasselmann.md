## 개요

클라우스 하셀만 is a German physicist and oceanographer who shared the 2021 노벨 물리학상 for the physical modelling of Earth's climate. He developed methods to identify the fingerprint of human influence on climate change, proving that global warming is caused by human CO2 emissions. His stochastic climate model connected weather variability to climate change.
