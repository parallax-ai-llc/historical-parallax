## 개요

Eileen Collins was the first female pilot and first female commander of a Space Shuttle. She piloted the shuttle ~ 동안 first shuttle-Mir docking mission in 1995 and commanded the return-to-flight mission ~ 후에 the Columbia disaster in 2005. She flew four shuttle missions and ~의 하나로 간주된다 NASA's greatest astronaut-pilots.
