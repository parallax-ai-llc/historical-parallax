## 개요

Hasso Plattner co-founded SAP SE, the world's largest enterprise software company. SAP's enterprise resource planning (ERP) software became the backbone of business operations for most Fortune 500 companies. He served as CEO and later chairman of SAP's supervisory board. He is also a major philanthropist, founding the Hasso Plattner Institute and donating extensively to education and design.
