## 개요

The Emancipation Proclamation, effective January 1, 1863, declared slaves in Confederate states to be free. ~하는 동안 it didn't immediately free all slaves, it transformed the Civil 전쟁 into a fight for human liberty and paved the way for the 13th Amendment.
