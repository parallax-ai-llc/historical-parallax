## 요약
Said Hanai was an Iranian serial killer who murdered 16 sex workers in Mashhad between 2000 and 2001. He claimed to be 'cleansing' the city of moral corruption. His arrest and trial divided Iranian society, with some viewing him as a vigilante hero.
