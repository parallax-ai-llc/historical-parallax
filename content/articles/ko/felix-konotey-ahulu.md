## 개요

Felix Konotey-Ahulu was 세계's leading authority on sickle cell disease, having examined over 8,000 patients. His 1991 book The Sickle Cell Disease Patient remains the definitive clinical text on the condition. He demonstrated that sickle cell patients could live long, productive lives with proper care.
