## 개요

Friedrich Schleiermacher 독일의 신학자 and 철학자 known as the 'Father of 현대의 Liberal Theology.' His work On 종교: Speeches to Its Cultured Despisers redefined 종교 as a feeling of absolute dependence on the infinite. His magnum opus The Christian Faith systematized Protestant theology in a new way. He also pioneered 현대의 hermeneutics as a 장군 theory of interpretation.
