## 개요

Georges Cuvier 프랑스의 박물학자 and 동물학자 who established the fields of comparative anatomy and paleontology. He proposed catastrophism and was the first to demonstrate that animals could go extinct. His classification of the animal kingdom into four embranchements was 영향력 있는.
