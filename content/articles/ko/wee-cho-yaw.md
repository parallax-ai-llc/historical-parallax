## 요약

Wee Cho Yaw is the chairman emeritus of United Overseas Bank (UOB), one of the largest banks in Southeast Asia. He transformed UOB from a small Singaporean bank into a regional banking powerhouse with operations across ASEAN, Greater China, and globally. The Wee family controls one of Singapore's most powerful banking dynasties. He served as chairman for over 40 years.
