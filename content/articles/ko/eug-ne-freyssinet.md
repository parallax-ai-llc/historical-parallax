## 개요

Eugène Freyssinet is considered the father of prestressed concrete, developing the technique of pre-tensioning concrete with steel cables to greatly improve its structural performance. His innovations enabled longer bridge spans, thinner structures, and more efficient construction. Prestressed concrete ~의 하나가 되었다 the most 중요한 materials in 현대의 construction.
