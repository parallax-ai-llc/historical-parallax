## 요약

Tan Jiazhen (C.C. Tan) was the father of modern genetics in China. He made important contributions to Drosophila genetics, discovering the mosaic dominance phenomenon. He trained generations of Chinese geneticists and fought to preserve genetics research during the Lysenko-influenced period when genetics was suppressed in China.
