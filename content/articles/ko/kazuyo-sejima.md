## 개요

세지마 가즈요 is a 일본ese architect who co-founded SANAA with Ryue Nishizawa. Together they received the Pritzker Prize in 2010. Their minimalist, transparent buildings including the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art and the New Museum in 뉴욕 redefine architectural space.
