## 개요

Desh Bandhu Gupta founded Lupin Limited, one of India's largest pharmaceutical companies and a major generic drug manufacturer globally. Starting with just $5,000 in 1968, he built Lupin into a company that supplies medications to millions worldwide. Lupin ~이 되었다 top generic drug company in the 미국 and a major anti-tuberculosis drug manufacturer. His company helped make essential medicines affordable in developing countries.
