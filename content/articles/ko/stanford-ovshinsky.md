## 요약

Stanford Ovshinsky, a self-taught scientist, invented amorphous semiconductor materials (Ovshinsky effect) and developed NiMH rechargeable batteries used in hybrid cars, thin-film solar cells, and rewritable optical discs. He held over 400 patents. His work on phase-change memory was ahead of its time and influenced modern data storage.
