## 개요
José Antonio Rodríguez Vega was a Spanish serial killer who murdered at least 16 elderly women in Santander between 1987-88. Known as 'the old ladies' killer.' Murdered by fellow inmates in prison.

## 배경
- Rodríguez Vega was born in 1957 and lived in 스페인; he is most associated with the city of Santander in Cantabria.
- He became known in Spanish media for targeting elderly women, often described as the “old ladies’ killer.”

## Crimes (1987–1988)
- The murders occurred primarily in Santander during 1987–1988.
- Victims were predominantly elderly women.
- The widely cited minimum victim count is **16**.

## Investigation and Arrest
- Police linked multiple killings through patterns in victim profile and location.
- Rodríguez Vega was identified and arrested during the investigation into the Santander killings.

## Trial, Conviction, and Imprisonment
- He was convicted for multiple murders connected to the 1987–1988 series.
- He was sentenced to prison and remained incarcerated until his death.

## 사망
- Rodríguez Vega died in prison in 2002.
- Accounts commonly report that he was killed by fellow inmates.

## Legacy and Notes
- The case is frequently cited in overviews of Spanish serial crime due to the number of victims and the short time window of the series.
- Reported victim totals can vary by source; summaries typically use the figure of at least 16.
