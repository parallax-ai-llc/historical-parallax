## 개요

Ferdinand von Zeppelin 독일의 장군 and 발명가 who developed the rigid airship that bears his name. His Zeppelin airships ~이 되었다 first commercially 성공적인 aircraft, operating passenger flights across Europe and the Atlantic. The Zeppelin ~이 되었다 symbol of technological progress 초기에 20th 세기, though the Hindenburg disaster in 1937 ended the airship era.
