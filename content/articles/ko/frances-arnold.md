## 개요

Frances Arnold 미국의 chemical 공학자 and 생화학자 who shared the 2018 노벨상 in Chemistry for the directed evolution of enzymes. She pioneered methods for harnessing evolution in the laboratory to create enzymes with novel functions, useful in green chemistry, pharmaceuticals, and biofuels. She was the fifth woman to win the 노벨상 in Chemistry.
