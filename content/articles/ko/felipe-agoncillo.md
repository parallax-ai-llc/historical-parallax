> "The fulfillment of the solemn declaration made by the illustrious William McKinley... that, on going to war, he was not guided by any intention of territorial expansion, but only... the duty of liberating tyrannized people, and the desire to proclaim the unalienable rights of sovereignty of the countries released from the yoke of Spain" - From his protest, holding the 미국 accountable to its stated anti-imperial principles[^114]
