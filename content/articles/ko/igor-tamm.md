## 개요

Igor Tamm shared the 1958 Nobel Prize in Physics for the theoretical explanation of Cherenkov radiation — the blue glow emitted when a charged particle travels through a medium faster than the speed of light in that medium. He also co-developed the tokamak concept for controlled nuclear fusion with Andrei Sakharov.
