## 요약

Walter Payton, 'Sweetness,' held the NFL all-time rushing record for 18 years. He won Super Bowl XX with the Chicago Bears.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1975 | Drafted by Chicago Bears |
| 1977 | Set single-game rushing record |
| 1984 | Became all-time rushing leader |
| 1986 | Won Super Bowl XX |
| 1999 | Died of bile duct cancer |
