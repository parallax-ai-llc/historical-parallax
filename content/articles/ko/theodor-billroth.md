## 요약

Theodor Billroth was the founder of modern abdominal surgery, performing the first successful gastrectomy in 1881. He developed numerous surgical techniques for stomach, esophageal, and intestinal operations that bear his name. His Vienna surgical school trained a generation of Europe's leading surgeons.
