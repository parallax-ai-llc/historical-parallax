## The Assassination and Aftermath

The bombing that killed Bolles is widely cited as a turning point for investigative journalism in the 미국 ~ 때문에 it demonstrated that local political corruption stories could carry lethal risk. In response, journalists from multiple outlets collaborated on the **Arizona Project**, an 초기의 example of coordinated reporting intended to finish a story when a 기자 is attacked.

## 유산

Bolles is frequently referenced in discussions of press 자유 and the idea that threats against journalists can be countered by collaboration rather than silence. His case is also used as an example in journalism ethics and training materials about:

Bolles is remembered not only for the reporting he produced, but for the idea that investigative work can be made more resilient when notes, sources, and leads are structured so that a newsroom can continue the investigation if a 기자 is threatened or harmed.
