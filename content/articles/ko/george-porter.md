## 개요

George Porter 영국의 화학자 who shared the 1967 노벨상 in Chemistry for studies of extremely fast chemical reactions effected by disturbing the equilibrium by means of very short pulses of energy (flash photolysis). His technique of flash photolysis allowed observation of chemical reactions occurring in millionths of a second. He later served as 대통령 of the Royal Society and 감독 of the Royal Institution.
