## 개요

일본ese ukiyo-e artist renowned for his elegant portraits of beautiful women (bijin-ga). His close-up bust portraits with their subtle emotional expressions represent the pinnacle of the bijin-ga genre.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1753 | Born in Edo area |
| 1783 | Begins producing book illustrations |
| 1790 | Creates his famous series of bust portraits |
| 1804 | Arrested for prints deemed offensive to the shogunate |
| 1806 | Dies in Edo |
