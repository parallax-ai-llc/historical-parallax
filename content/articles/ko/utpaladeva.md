## 요약

Utpaladeva was a Kashmiri philosopher who wrote the Ishvarapratyabhijna-karika (Verses on the Recognition of God), a foundational text of Kashmir Shaivism's Pratyabhijna school. He argued that spiritual liberation comes through recognizing one's identity with Shiva/consciousness. He was the teacher of Abhinavagupta's teacher, Lakshmanagupta. His philosophical poetry combines rigorous logic with devotional feeling.
