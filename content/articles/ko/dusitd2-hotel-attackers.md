## 배경

- The DusitD2 hotel complex is part of the larger 14 Riverside development in Nairobi, Kenya.
- The 2019 attack is commonly discussed alongside other Al-Shabaab operations in Kenya and the wider region.

## Attack Overview (2019)

- Date: 15 January 2019 (local time).
- Location: DusitD2 / 14 Riverside complex, Nairobi.
- Reported outcome: 21 people were killed; the attackers were also killed ~ 동안 security response.
- The attack involved a coordinated assault targeting the hotel and surrounding complex.

## Perpetrators and Affiliation

- The attackers are described as Al-Shabaab militants in major summaries of the event.
- Public reporting and reference summaries often focus on the group identity and the attack’s impact rather than detailed personal biographies.

## 여파

- Kenyan security forces conducted a multi-hour operation to end the siege and secure the site.
- The incident received sustained international coverage and prompted renewed discussion about security risks in Nairobi.
