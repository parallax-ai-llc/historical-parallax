## 개요

Im Kwon-taek is Korea's most prolific and celebrated film director, with over 100 films spanning 60 years. While not a scientist, his films including Sopyonje and Chunhyang documented Korean traditional culture and technology, preserving cultural knowledge through cinema. He received numerous international film awards and is considered a national treasure.
