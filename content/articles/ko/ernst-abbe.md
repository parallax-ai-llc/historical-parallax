## 개요

Ernst Abbe developed the mathematical theory of microscope optics, establishing the resolution limits that bear his name (Abbe diffraction limit). His partnership with Carl Zeiss and Otto Schott produced the finest optical instruments of the era. He also established the Carl Zeiss Foundation, introducing progressive labor practices including the eight-hour workday.
