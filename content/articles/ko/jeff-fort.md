## 배경

- Jeff Fort (born 1947) became a prominent figure in Chicago street-gang history.
- He is widely associated with the Black P Stone Nation and later the El Rukn organization.[^1]

## El Rukn and the Libya Conspiracy (high level)

- U.S. authorities alleged that Fort and associates sought support from the Libyan government.
- The case is commonly summarized as a conspiracy to commit acts of terrorism within the United States.[^1]
- Fort was convicted in federal court in connection with this plot (1980s), and the conviction is a central reason he is described as a terrorist in many biographical summaries.[^1]

## Legal Outcome

- The best-known milestone is the 1987 federal conviction referenced in many timelines.[^1]
- Biographical summaries typically focus on the conspiracy charge rather than on day-to-day gang operations.
