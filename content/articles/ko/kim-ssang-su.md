## 개요

김쌍수 (also known as 김성수) was a Korean independence activist and industrialist who founded Kyunghee Textile Company and Dong-A Ilbo newspaper. He used his business wealth to support Korean independence and education, co-founding Korea University (originally Boseong College). He served as the second Vice President of 대한민국. He is remembered for combining entrepreneurship with nationalist activism.
