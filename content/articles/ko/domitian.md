The 암살 conspiracy's breadth—involving Praetorian prefects, freedmen, senators, and provincial governors—indicates widespread desire for regime change rather than a narrow factional plot.[^178] The fact that Nerva was proclaimed 황제 the very day of the 암살 demonstrates that leading Romans had prepared for succession, viewing Domitian's removal as necessary for the empire's welfare.[^179] ~하는 동안 고대의 sources may have exaggerated some aspects of his cruelty, the consistent testimony across multiple authors, the physical evidence of his damnatio memoriae, and the circumstances of his death all suggest that Domitian's authoritarianism genuinely alienated Rome's governing class.[^180]
