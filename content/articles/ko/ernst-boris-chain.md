## 개요

Ernst Boris Chain 독일의-born British 생화학자 who shared the 1945 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for his work on developing penicillin. He isolated and purified penicillin with Howard Florey, making it available as a practical antibiotic. Born to a Jewish family, he fled Nazi Germany in 1933. Chain's biochemical expertise was essential in understanding penicillin's structure and mechanism.
