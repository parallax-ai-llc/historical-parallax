## 개요

로이드 홀 was an 아프리카n American chemist who revolutionized the food preservation industry. He developed methods for curing meats, sterilizing spices, and preserving food using antioxidants. He held over 59 patents and his inventions for food preservation are still used worldwide. His work made processed food safer and more flavorful.
