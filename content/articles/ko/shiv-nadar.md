## 요약

Shiv Nadar founded HCL Technologies, one of India's largest IT services companies. He began by manufacturing calculators and microcomputers in the 1970s before pivoting to IT services. HCL Technologies grew into a $13 billion revenue company competing with Infosys, TCS, and Wipro globally. He is one of India's most generous philanthropists, having donated over $5 billion to education through the Shiv Nadar Foundation.
