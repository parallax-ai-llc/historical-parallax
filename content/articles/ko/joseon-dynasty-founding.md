## 개요

The 조선 dynasty was founded on July 17, 1392, by General Yi Seong-gye (태조) after overthrowing the 고려 dynasty. 조선 ruled Korea for over 500 years, adopting 유교, creating 한글, and shaping Korean culture profoundly.
