## 개요

존 홉필드 is an American physicist who invented the Hopfield network in 1982, an associative memory neural network that could store and retrieve patterns. His work bridging physics and neuroscience laid theoretical foundations for modern neural networks and 머신러닝. He shared the 2024 노벨 물리학상 for foundational work in 머신러닝.
