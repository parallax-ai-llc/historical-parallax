## 개요

Lee Ho-Wang is a Korean physician who discovered the Hantaan virus in 1976, which causes Korean hemorrhagic fever (now called hemorrhagic fever with renal syndrome). This was the first hantavirus identified and the discovery solved the mystery of a disease that had sickened thousands of UN soldiers during the 한국전쟁. He developed a vaccine against the virus.
