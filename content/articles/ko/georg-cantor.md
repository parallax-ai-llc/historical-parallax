## 개요

Georg Cantor 독일의 수학자 who created set theory, which ~이 되었다 fundamental theory in mathematics. He proved that infinite sets can have different sizes (cardinalities) and introduced the concept of transfinite numbers. His work on the nature of infinity was 혁명가 but 논란이 많은 ~ 동안 his lifetime. Cantor suffered from mental health issues partly exacerbated by the opposition his ideas faced.
