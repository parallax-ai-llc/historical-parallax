## 요약
Salvatore 'Totò' Riina was the head of the Sicilian Mafia and boss of the Corleonesi clan. Known as 'The Beast,' he orchestrated a campaign of extreme violence including the assassinations of anti-Mafia judges Giovanni Falcone and Paolo Borsellino in 1992. He was convicted of multiple murders and sentenced to 26 life terms.
