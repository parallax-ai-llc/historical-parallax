## 요약

Vital Brazil pioneered the production of polyvalent antivenoms for treating snakebites and developed specific antivenoms for different snake species. He founded the Butantan Institute in São Paulo, which became one of the world's leading producers of sera and vaccines. His work saved thousands of lives across Latin America.
