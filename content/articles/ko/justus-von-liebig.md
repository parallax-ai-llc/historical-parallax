## 개요

유스투스 폰 리비히 was a German chemist who made major contributions to agricultural chemistry, organic chemistry, and biochemistry. He is considered the father of the fertilizer industry and pioneered methods for analyzing organic compounds. His work on plant nutrition and mineral theory of agriculture revolutionized farming practices worldwide. Liebig also invented the Liebig condenser and developed meat extract (Oxo/Lemco).
