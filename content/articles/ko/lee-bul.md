## 개요

Korean artist known for her futuristic, dystopian sculptures and installations exploring technology, the body, and utopian visions. Her work ranges from wearable soft sculptures to monumental architectural installations.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1964 | Born in Yeongju, 대한민국 |
| 1989 | Begins performance art in 서울 |
| 1997 | Represents Korea at the Venice Biennale |
| 2002 | Creates monumental installation at the Fondation Cartier |
| 2014 | Major retrospective at the Hayward Gallery |
| 2022 | Continues as Korea's most prominent female artist |
