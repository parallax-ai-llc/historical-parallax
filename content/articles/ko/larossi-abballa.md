## 배경

- 라로시 아발라 was a French national associated in reporting with jihadist networks in 프랑스.
- He was previously known to French authorities and had been convicted in the early 2010s in connection with a plot involving travel to 파키스탄 and support for militant activity.
- By 2016, he was described by investigators as a supporter of the 이슬람ic State (ISIS).

## Magnanville attack (June 2016)

- On 13 June 2016 (local time), Abballa attacked police officer **Jean-Baptiste Salvaing** outside the officer’s home in Magnanville (Yvelines, near 파리).
- He then entered the home and killed **Jessica Schneider**, Salvaing’s partner.
- The couple’s young child was present at the scene and survived.
- During the standoff, Abballa posted a video on social media in which he pledged allegiance to ISIS.
- French special police units later stormed the home and killed Abballa.

## Investigation and legal context

- French prosecutors treated the killings as a terrorist act, citing ISIS-linked motivation and Abballa’s pledge.
- Investigations in such cases typically examine:

## Impact and aftermath

- The attack intensified security concerns around targeted violence against police in 프랑스.
- It prompted public and political reactions emphasizing:

  - monitoring of online extremist content
