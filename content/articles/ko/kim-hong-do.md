## 개요

김홍도 (Danwon) was one of the greatest Korean painters of the 조선 dynasty, known for his vivid genre paintings of everyday Korean life. His humorous, lively depictions of farmers, scholars, and craftsmen provide an invaluable record of 18th-century Korean society. He also excelled in portraits, landscapes, and Buddhist art.
