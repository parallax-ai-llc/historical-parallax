## 개요

Dominic de Guzmán founded the Dominican Order (Order of Preachers) dedicated to preaching and combating heresy ~을 통해 교육. He pioneered a new form of religious life combining contemplation with active ministry. The Dominican Order ~의 하나가 되었다 the most 중요한 intellectual forces in 중세의 Christianity. Dominic emphasized poverty, study, and preaching as the cornerstones of religious life.
