## 개요

Devi Shetty 인도의 cardiac 외과의사 and founder of Narayana Health, a chain of hospitals that performs heart surgery at a fraction of Western costs. He pioneered the concept of 'Walmart-style' healthcare, performing over 15,000 heart surgeries annually at his Bangalore facility. He demonstrated that high-quality healthcare could be delivered affordably ~을 통해 economies of scale. Mother Teresa was among his patients.
