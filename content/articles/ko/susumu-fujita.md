## 요약

Susumu Fujita is the president and CEO of CyberAgent, one of Japan's largest internet companies. CyberAgent operates Abema TV (Japan's leading free streaming platform), Ameba (blogging), and a major gaming division producing hits like Uma Musume Pretty Derby. He founded the company at age 24 and built it into a diversified tech conglomerate. He represents the generation of Japanese entrepreneurs who emerged after the dot-com era.
