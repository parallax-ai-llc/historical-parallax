## 개요

조너선 에드워즈 was an American Calvinist theologian and philosopher widely regarded as one of America's greatest intellectuals. His sermon 'Sinners in the Hands of an Angry God' is one of the most famous in American history. He was a key figure in the First Great Awakening. His philosophical works on free will, original sin, and religious affections remain influential.
