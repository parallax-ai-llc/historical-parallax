## 개요

Emily Warren Roebling effectively served as chief 공학자 of the Brooklyn Bridge construction when her husband Washington became incapacitated by decompression sickness. She studied mathematics, bridge engineering, and materials 과학 to communicate technical decisions between her husband and the site. She was the first person to cross the completed bridge.
