## 개요
Sir James Wilson Vincent Savile was a British television presenter and disc jockey who was revealed after his death to have been one of the UK's most prolific sex offenders. He sexually abused hundreds of victims, including children, over six decades. His crimes were exposed by an ITV investigation in 2012.
