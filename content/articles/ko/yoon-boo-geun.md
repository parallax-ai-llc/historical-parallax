## 요약

Yoon Boo-geun is a Korean electronics engineer who served as CEO of Samsung Electronics' consumer electronics division and contributed to the development of Samsung's display and semiconductor technologies. His technical leadership helped Samsung become the world's largest electronics manufacturer.
