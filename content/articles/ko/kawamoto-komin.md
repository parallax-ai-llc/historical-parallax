## 개요

Kawamoto Komin was a 일본ese physician and scholar of Western sciences (rangaku) who wrote the Kikai Kanran Kogi (Outline of Physical Science), one of the first 일본ese texts on Western physics and chemistry. He translated and adapted Western scientific works and experimented with photography, telegraphy, and chemistry.
