## 개요

George Alcorn invented the imaging X-ray spectrometer used in space exploration, which can detect elements in soil and rock samples on other planets. His invention was used on NASA missions. He holds over 25 patents and has made 중요한 contributions to semiconductor 기술 and space 과학.
