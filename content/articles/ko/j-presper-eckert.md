## 개요

J. Presper Eckert was an American electrical engineer who co-invented ENIAC, the first general-purpose electronic digital computer, with John Mauchly. He later co-designed UNIVAC I, the first commercial computer in the United States. Eckert held numerous patents related to computer technology and was a key figure in the transition from mechanical to electronic computing.
