## 요약

William Blake was a visionary British artist and poet who created illuminated books combining poetry with hand-colored relief etchings. His powerful mystical paintings anticipate both Symbolism and Expressionism. Largely unrecognized in his lifetime, he is now considered one of the greatest figures in British art and literature.
