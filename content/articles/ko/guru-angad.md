## 개요

Guru Angad was the second Sikh Guru who developed and standardized the Gurmukhi script used for writing the Punjabi language. He compiled Guru Nanak's hymns and added his own compositions. He established a system of langar (free community kitchen) and physical exercise (mal akhara). His contributions to literacy and community organization laid the foundation for Sikh institutional development.
