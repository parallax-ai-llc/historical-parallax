## 개요

François Jacob 프랑스의 생물학자 who shared the 1965 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for the discovery of genetic regulation of enzyme synthesis. He co-discovered messenger RNA and the operon model of gene regulation with Jacques Monod. His work revealed how genes are switched on and off, a fundamental mechanism in biology. He was also a decorated WWII veteran.
