## 개요

Egas Moniz received the 1949 노벨상 in Physiology or 의학 for developing cerebral angiography and prefrontal leucotomy (lobotomy). Cerebral angiography remains a crucial diagnostic tool, ~하는 동안 his promotion of lobotomy is now considered one of 의학's most 논란이 많은 chapters. He was also a 주목할 만한 정치인 and 외교관.
