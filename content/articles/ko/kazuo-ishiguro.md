## 개요

가즈오 이시구로 is a 일본ese-born British novelist who received the 노벨 문학상 in 2017. His novels including The Remains of the Day, Never Let Me Go, and An Artist of the Floating World explore memory, self-deception, and the passage of time with quiet emotional devastation. The Remains of the Day won the Booker Prize.
