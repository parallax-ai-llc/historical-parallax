## 요약

Sekiya Seikei was the world's first professor of seismology, appointed at Tokyo Imperial University in 1886. He was a pioneer of modern seismological research and created the first three-dimensional models of earthquake ground motion. He advanced seismic instrumentation and founded the Seismological Society of Japan.
