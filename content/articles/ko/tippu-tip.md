## 요약
Hamad bin Muhammad bin Juma bin Rajab el Murjebi, known as Tippu Tip, was the most powerful East African slave and ivory trader of the 19th century. He established a vast commercial empire in Central and East Africa. His raids enslaved thousands. He later worked with European colonists.
