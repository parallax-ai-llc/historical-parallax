## 개요

Dogen Zenji 일본의 Zen Buddhist 수도승 who founded the Soto Zen school in Japan. His masterwork Shobogenzo (Treasury of the True Dharma Eye) is one of the most profound philosophical texts in Japanese 문학. He emphasized shikantaza (just sitting) meditation as itself the expression of enlightenment, not merely a means to it. His 철학 of time, being, and practice continues to influence 철학 worldwide.
