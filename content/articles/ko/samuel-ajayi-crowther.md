## 요약

Samuel Ajayi Crowther was a Nigerian linguist and the first African Anglican bishop. While primarily a religious figure, his systematic linguistic analysis of the Yoruba language produced the first Yoruba grammar and dictionary, and he developed writing systems for multiple West African languages, demonstrating scientific rigor in linguistic documentation.
