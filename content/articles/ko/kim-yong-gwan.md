## 개요

김용관 was a pioneering Korean mathematician who made contributions to functional analysis and operator theory. He helped establish modern mathematics education in 대한민국 after the 한국전쟁 and trained many of the country's leading mathematicians. He was instrumental in building Korea's mathematics infrastructure.
