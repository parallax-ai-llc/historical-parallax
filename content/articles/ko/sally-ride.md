## 요약

Sally Ride became the first American woman in space on June 18, 1983, aboard the Space Shuttle Challenger. She was also the youngest American astronaut to travel to space at age 32. After leaving NASA, she became a physics professor and founded Sally Ride Science, inspiring girls to pursue STEM careers.
