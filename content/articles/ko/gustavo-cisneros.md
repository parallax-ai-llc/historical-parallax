## 개요

Gustavo Cisneros is a Venezuelan billionaire and head of Cisneros Group, one of the largest privately held media and entertainment companies in Latin America. The group operates Venevisión television, Cisneros Media Distribution, and has interests in beer, real estate, and tourism. He partnered with DirecTV for satellite television across Latin America. He is one of the most powerful media executives in the Spanish-speaking world.
