## 개요

K. 배리 샤플리스 is an American chemist who has won the 노벨 화학상 twice: in 2001 for his work on chirally catalyzed oxidation reactions (Sharpless epoxidation and asymmetric dihydroxylation) and in 2022 for the development of click chemistry. He is only the fifth person to win two 노벨상s. He coined the concept of 'click chemistry' in 2001.
