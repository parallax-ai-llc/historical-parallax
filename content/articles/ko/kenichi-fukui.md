## 개요

후쿠이 겐이치 was a 일본ese chemist who shared the 1981 노벨 화학상 with Roald Hoffmann for their theories concerning the course of chemical reactions. He developed frontier molecular orbital theory, showing that chemical reactions are controlled by the highest occupied and lowest unoccupied molecular orbitals (HOMO and LUMO). He was the first 아시아n to receive a 노벨 화학상.
