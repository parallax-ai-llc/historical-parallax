## 개요

김경란 is a Korean virologist who contributed to 대한민국's infectious disease response capability. Her research on respiratory viruses helped Korea respond effectively to the MERS outbreak in 2015 and the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, demonstrating Korea's public health preparedness.
