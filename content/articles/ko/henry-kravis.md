## 개요

Henry Kravis co-founded KKR (Kohlberg Kravis Roberts) in 1976, pioneering the leveraged buyout industry. KKR's $25 billion buyout of RJR Nabisco in 1988 was the largest LBO in history at the time and inspired the book 'Barbarians at the Gate.' Under his leadership, KKR grew into one of the world's largest private equity firms. He has donated hundreds of millions to education, healthcare, and the arts.
