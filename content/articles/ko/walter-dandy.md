## 요약

Walter Dandy was one of the founders of neurosurgery who developed pneumoencephalography and ventriculography — the first techniques for imaging the brain. He performed the first successful removal of a brain tumor and pioneered the surgical treatment of Ménière's disease. He was Harvey Cushing's protégé and rival.
