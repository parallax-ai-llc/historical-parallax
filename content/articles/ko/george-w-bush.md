## 개요

His presidency was defined by the September 11 terrorist attacks, the subsequent 전쟁 on Terror, the invasions of Afghanistan and Iraq, the use of 고문, massive deficits, and the 2008 financial crisis. Once one of the least popular presidents, his reputation has improved slightly in the post-Trump era.
