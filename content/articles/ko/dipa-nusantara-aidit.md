## 유명한 인용문

> "Land to the peasants" - Aidit's central agrarian reform slogan, adopted by the PKI to mobilize millions of rural Indonesians[^146]

> "He believed Indonesia was not developed enough to have a revolution, so he collaborated with the national bourgeoisie and Sukarno." - Describing Aidit's strategic approach to achieving 사회주의 ~을 통해 gradual, peaceful means rather than armed revolution[^147]

> "Agrarian revolution must be the essence of the people's democratic revolution in Indonesia." - From Aidit's 1953 analysis that ~이 되었다 foundation of PKI agrarian policy[^148]

> "The nation's guardian" - The meaning of the name Dipa Nusantara that Ahmad Aidit chose for himself 초기에 1950s when he sensed he would become an 중요한 figure[^149]

> "Aidit was given half an hour before being executed and made use of the time to deliver a speech. The passion with which he spoke made all who heard him very angry." - Account of Aidit's final moments, demonstrating his unwavering commitment to his beliefs even facing execution[^150]
