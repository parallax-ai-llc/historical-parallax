## 개요

Jamie Dimon is the chairman and CEO of JPMorgan Chase, the largest bank in the United States. He built the bank into a financial powerhouse through the 2008 financial crisis, acquiring Bear Stearns and Washington Mutual. Under his leadership, JPMorgan Chase became the most profitable bank in the world. He is widely considered the most influential banker of his generation and frequently mentioned as a potential political candidate.
