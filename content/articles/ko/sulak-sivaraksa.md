## 요약

Sulak Sivaraksa is a Thai Buddhist intellectual, author, and activist who is one of the most prominent voices for engaged Buddhism in Asia. He has been arrested multiple times for his criticism of the Thai military and monarchy. He founded the International Network of Engaged Buddhists and numerous social justice organizations. His work bridges Buddhist ethics with contemporary social, economic, and environmental issues.
