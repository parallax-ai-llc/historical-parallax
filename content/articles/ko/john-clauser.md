## 개요

존 클라우저 is an American theoretical and experimental physicist who shared the 2022 노벨 물리학상 for experiments with entangled photons establishing the violation of Bell inequalities. In 1972, he performed the first experimental test of Bell's theorem, showing that quantum mechanics is correct and local hidden variable theories are not.
