## 개요

Jang Gi-ryang was a Korean entomologist who systematically classified Korean insect species during the colonial period. He documented hundreds of native Korean insect species and developed integrated pest management techniques for Korean rice paddies. His taxonomic work laid the foundation for Korean entomological science.
