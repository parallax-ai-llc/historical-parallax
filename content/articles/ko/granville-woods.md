## 개요

Granville Woods was an African American 발명가 who held over 60 patents, including the multiplex telegraph (allowing communication between moving trains and stations), the third rail for electric trains, and the automatic air brake. He was known as the 'Black Edison' and sold several patents to 장군 Electric, Westinghouse, and Bell Telephone.
