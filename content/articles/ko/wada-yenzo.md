## 요약

Wada Yenzo was a Japanese mathematician and astronomer of the late Edo period who made significant contributions to wasan mathematics. He developed methods for calculating pi to unprecedented accuracy and advanced techniques in infinite series. His mathematical works extended the tradition founded by Seki Takakazu.
