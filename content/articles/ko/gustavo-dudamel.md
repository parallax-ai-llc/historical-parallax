## 개요

Gustavo Dudamel is a Venezuelan conductor who became music director of the Los Angeles Philharmonic and later the New York Philharmonic. A product of Venezuela's El Sistema music education program, his passionate conducting style and advocacy for music education have made him the most famous conductor of his generation.
