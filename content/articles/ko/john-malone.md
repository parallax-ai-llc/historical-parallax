## 개요

존 말론 is an American billionaire businessman who built Tele-Communications Inc. (TCI) into the largest cable television company in the 미국. He is chairman of Liberty Media and holds interests in Charter Communications, Discovery, and Formula One Group. Known as the 'Cable Cowboy,' he pioneered the cable TV industry's consolidation and is the largest private landowner in the 미국 with over 2.2 million acres.
