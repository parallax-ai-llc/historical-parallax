## 개요

Edouard Vuillard 프랑스의 Intimist 화가 associated with the Nabis, known for richly patterned domestic interiors. His 혁신적인 flattening of space and complex textile patterns created a distinctive visual language. His intimate scenes elevate the mundane to poetry.
