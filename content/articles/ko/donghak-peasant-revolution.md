## 개요

The Donghak Peasant Revolution of 1894 was a major Korean uprising against corruption, foreign influence, and social injustice. Led by Jeon Bong-jun, 운동 was suppressed with Japanese military aid, but it catalyzed Korea's modernization reforms.
