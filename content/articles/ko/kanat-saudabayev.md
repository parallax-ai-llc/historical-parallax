## 개요

Former Kazakh foreign minister implicated in corruption tied to the Nazarbayev regime's patronage network.

## Roles and public profile

- **Senior Nazarbayev-era official:** Saudabayev held multiple high-level posts across diplomacy, the executive apparatus, and parliament.
- **Foreign policy portfolio:** His most internationally visible role was as foreign minister (2009–2011), following earlier diplomatic appointments.
- **Long diplomatic track record:** He served as ambassador to 튀르키예, the UK, and the 미국 over more than a decade.

## Background and education (high-level)

Public biographies describe a mixed career spanning **culture administration** and **state diplomacy**, with academic training in the humanities and later advanced degrees.
