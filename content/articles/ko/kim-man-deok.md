## 개요

김만덕 was a legendary female merchant from Jeju Island during the 조선 왕조 who donated her entire fortune to save the people of Jeju from famine. She built her wealth through maritime trade between Jeju and the Korean mainland. When a devastating famine struck Jeju in 1794-1795, she purchased rice with her own money to feed the starving population. She is celebrated as one of Korea's greatest philanthropists and a symbol of female entrepreneurship.
