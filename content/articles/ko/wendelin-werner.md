## 요약

Wendelin Werner is a French-German mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2006 for his contributions to the development of stochastic Loewner evolution, the geometry of two-dimensional Brownian motion, and conformal field theory. His work connects probability theory with theoretical physics.
