## 개요

로런스 호 is the chairman and CEO of Melco Resorts & Entertainment, one of 아시아's largest casino and resort operators. He is the son of Stanley Ho, the former Macau casino monopoly holder. He built Melco independently from his father's STDM, developing City of Dreams and Studio City in Macau. He expanded into the 필리핀, 일본, and Cyprus, establishing himself as a major force in 아시아n gaming separate from his father's empire.
