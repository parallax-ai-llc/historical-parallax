## 개요

The Goryeo dynasty was founded in 918 by Wang Geon, who unified the Later Three Kingdoms of Korea by 936. Goryeo gave Korea its 현대의 English name and ~로 알려져 있었다 celadon pottery, Buddhist culture, and the invention of metal movable type.
