## 개요

Douglas Engelbart 미국의 공학자 and 발명가 who ~로 가장 잘 알려져 있다 inventing the computer mouse and 선구적인 many aspects of 현대의 computing. His 1968 'Mother of All Demos' showcased the mouse, hypertext, networked computing, and video conferencing decades ~ 전에 they became mainstream. He envisioned computers as tools for augmenting human intellect. Engelbart received the Turing Award in 1997.
