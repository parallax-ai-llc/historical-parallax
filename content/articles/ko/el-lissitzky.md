## 개요

El Lissitzky was a key figure in Constructivism and 현대의 graphic design. His Proun series explored the intersection of painting and 건축. His poster Beat the Whites with the Red Wedge became an icon of 혁명가 예술, and his work profoundly influenced the Bauhaus.
