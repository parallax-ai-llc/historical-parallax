## 개요

구로즈미 무네타다 was a 일본ese Shinto priest who founded Kurozumikyo, one of the earliest 일본ese new religious movements. In 1814, he experienced a mystical union with the sun goddess Amaterasu during a period of illness. He taught that becoming one with the divine through sincerity and gratitude could heal sickness and bring happiness. Kurozumikyo influenced later new religions and has approximately 300,000 adherents today.
