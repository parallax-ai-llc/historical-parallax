## 개요

이나모리 가즈오 founded Kyocera and KDDI (일본's second-largest telecom), making him one of the most respected business leaders in 일본ese history. He also rescued 일본 Airlines from bankruptcy in 2010. His 'amoeba management' philosophy, which divides companies into small profit centers, influenced management worldwide. He was ordained as a Buddhist monk and donated his personal fortune to education and culture.
