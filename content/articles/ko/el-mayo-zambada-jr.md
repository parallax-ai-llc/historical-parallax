## Additional Background

“El Mayo Zambada Jr.” is a label used in some summaries to refer to Vicente Zambada Niebla (also known by the nickname “Vicentillo”), a Mexican trafficker described in open sources as the son of Ismael “El Mayo” Zambada, a key Sinaloa Cartel leader. He is widely noted in public reporting for becoming a cooperating witness for U.S. authorities in major cartel prosecutions, including testimony relevant to Joaquín “El Chapo” Guzmán.[^1]
