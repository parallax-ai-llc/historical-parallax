## 개요

George Gabriel Stokes was an Irish-British 수학자 and 물리학자 who made 중요한 contributions to fluid dynamics, optics, and mathematical physics. He formulated the Navier-Stokes equations, discovered fluorescence (Stokes shift), and developed Stokes' theorem. He served as Lucasian 교수 of Mathematics at Cambridge and 대통령 of the Royal Society.
