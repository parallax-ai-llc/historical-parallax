## Notes on Naming

- **리옌훙 (李彦宏)** is the Mandarin pinyin form of the name most widely known in English as **Robin Li**.
- In many English-language sources, "리옌훙" and "Robin Li" refer to the **same person**: the Chinese entrepreneur and technologist associated with Baidu.
