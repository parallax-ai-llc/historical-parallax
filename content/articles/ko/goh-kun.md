## 개요

Goh Kun 한국의 public administrator who served as 총리 and Acting 대통령. As Seoul's mayor, he oversaw the construction of the Seoul subway system and the modernization of Seoul's infrastructure using advanced engineering technologies. His administrative expertise in deploying 기술 for urban development was 주목할 만한.
