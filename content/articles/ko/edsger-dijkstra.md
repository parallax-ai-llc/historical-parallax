## 개요

Edsger Dijkstra 네덜란드의 컴퓨터 과학자 who made fundamental contributions to programming methodology, algorithm design, and the theory of concurrent computing. He invented Dijkstra's algorithm for shortest paths and the semaphore mechanism for concurrent programming. His 유명한 'Go To Statement Considered Harmful' letter helped establish structured programming. He received the Turing Award in 1972.
