## 요약

Sumitomo Masatomo was a Japanese copper merchant and metallurgist who founded what would become the Sumitomo Group. He developed improved copper smelting techniques that increased the purity and quality of Japanese copper. His metallurgical innovations helped Japan become a major copper exporter and established the foundations of Japanese industrial metallurgy.
