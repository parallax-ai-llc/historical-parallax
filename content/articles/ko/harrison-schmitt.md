## 개요

Harrison Schmitt is the only professional scientist to walk on the Moon. As a geologist on Apollo 17 (1972), he discovered orange soil on the lunar surface, evidence of ancient volcanic activity. His geological expertise provided unique scientific insights during the last Moon landing. He later served as a U.S. Senator from New Mexico.
