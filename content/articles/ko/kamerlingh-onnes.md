## 개요

Heike 카메를링 온네스 discovered superconductivity in 1911 when he observed that the electrical resistance of mercury dropped to zero at 4.2 Kelvin. He was the first to liquefy helium in 1908, opening the field of cryogenics. He received the 1913 노벨 물리학상 for his low-temperature research.
