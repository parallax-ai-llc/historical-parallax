## 개요

존 콜린스 워런 was the surgeon who performed the first surgery under ether anesthesia during William Morton's famous Ether Day demonstration at Massachusetts General Hospital on October 16, 1846. He exclaimed 'Gentlemen, this is no humbug!' after the painless operation. He co-founded Massachusetts General Hospital and the New 잉글랜드 Journal of Medicine.
