## 요약

Saharon Shelah is an Israeli mathematician who has made groundbreaking contributions to mathematical logic, set theory, and model theory. He is one of the most prolific mathematicians alive, having authored over 1,000 mathematical papers. His development of proper forcing and pcf theory has had far-reaching consequences in set theory.
