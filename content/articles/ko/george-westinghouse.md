## 개요

George Westinghouse 미국의 기업가 and 공학자 who was a pioneer of the electrical industry. He championed alternating current (AC) power distribution, competing with Thomas Edison's direct current (DC) system in the '전쟁 of Currents.' He founded the Westinghouse Electric Company and acquired many of Nikola Tesla's patents. He also invented the railway air brake, which revolutionized railroad safety.
