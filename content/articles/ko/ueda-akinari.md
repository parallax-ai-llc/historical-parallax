## 요약

Ueda Akinari was a Japanese writer and physician who, beyond his famous literary works, practiced herbal medicine and studied pharmacology. He was trained as a physician and herbalist, applying traditional Chinese and Japanese medical knowledge. His medical practice informed his literary depictions of human nature and suffering.
