## 요약

Chinese smuggling kingpin known as Lai Changxing who ran the Yuanhua Group, which smuggled $10 billion worth of goods through Xiamen port. His ring was the largest smuggling operation in PRC history.

| 1999 | Fled to Canada when investigation began |
| 2011 | Deported from Canada to China |
| 2012 | Sentenced to life imprisonment |
