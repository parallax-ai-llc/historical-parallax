## 개요

이황우 was a 대한민국n nuclear physicist who played a crucial role in establishing physics research in Korea. He contributed to building Korea's scientific infrastructure and trained generations of Korean physicists. He was a leading figure at 서울 National University and contributed to international collaboration in physics.
