## 요약

Yoo Ryong-jun is a Korean nuclear fusion scientist who has contributed to the development of the KSTAR (Korea Superconducting Tokamak Advanced Research) fusion reactor. KSTAR has set multiple world records for sustaining high-temperature plasma, advancing the global quest for fusion energy.
