## 개요

Gene Cernan was the last person to walk on the Moon as commander of Apollo 17 in December 1972. He traced his daughter's initials in the lunar dust and spoke the final words on the Moon's surface. He also performed a spacewalk ~ 동안 Gemini 9 and orbited the Moon ~ 동안 Apollo 10. Over 50 년 후, no human has returned.
