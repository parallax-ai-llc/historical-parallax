## 개요

Massive insurance fraud where executives created 64,000 fake insurance policies worth $2 billion. Used computer fraud to generate fictitious policyholders, one of the first computer-assisted frauds.

## Why It Matters

Equity Funding is remembered less for its legitimate business lines than for demonstrating that:

- **Scale can hide fabrication** when incentives reward growth over verification.
- **Systems and data** can become an attack surface when controls are weak.
- **Audit and compliance** must adapt as recordkeeping becomes more automated.
