## 개요

Hans Selye pioneered the concept of biological stress and developed the General Adaptation Syndrome model. He demonstrated that the body responds to various stressors through predictable physiological stages. His work on the stress response influenced medicine, psychology, and our understanding of psychosomatic illness.
