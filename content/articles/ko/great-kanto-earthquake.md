## 개요

The Great Kantō Earthquake on September 1, 1923, devastated Tokyo and Yokohama with a magnitude 7.9 tremor. Fires destroyed most of Tokyo. Over 105,000 people died. The disaster ~로 이어졌다 major urban planning reforms and tragically sparked 폭력 against Korean residents.
