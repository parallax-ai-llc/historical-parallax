## 개요

The Korean Armistice (1953) ended combat without a peace treaty. The 비무장지대 along the 38th parallel has maintained uneasy peace for 70+ years.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1951 | Negotiations begin |
| 1952 | POW deadlock |
| 1953-07-27 | Armistice signed |
| 1953 | 비무장지대 created |
| 1968 | Pueblo incident |
| 2024 | No peace treaty |
