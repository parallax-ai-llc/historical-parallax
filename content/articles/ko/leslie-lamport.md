## 개요

레슬리 램포트 is an American computer scientist best known for his foundational work in distributed computing. He developed the LaTeX document preparation system, created the Paxos consensus algorithm, and introduced the concept of Byzantine fault tolerance. His work on temporal logic of actions provides a formal framework for specifying concurrent systems. He received the Turing Award in 2013.
