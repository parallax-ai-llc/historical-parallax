## 요약

Sebastiao Salgado is a Brazilian social documentary photographer known for his epic black-and-white photographs documenting workers, migration, and the natural world. His projects Workers, Migrations, and Genesis are landmarks of photographic storytelling. His Instituto Terra has planted millions of trees to restore Brazil's Atlantic Forest.
