## 개요

다카키 가네히로 was a 일본ese naval surgeon who eradicated beriberi from the 일본ese Navy through dietary reform in 1884, decades before the vitamin B1 deficiency cause was understood. He demonstrated that providing a varied diet (rather than polished rice alone) prevented beriberi, saving thousands of sailors' lives. He is considered the father of clinical nutrition in 일본.
