## 개요

Katalin Novak-Szenasi is a Hungarian biochemist who contributed to enzyme inhibitor research and pharmaceutical chemistry in Central 유럽. Her work on protease inhibitors advanced drug development for inflammatory and cardiovascular diseases. She helped build 헝가리's pharmaceutical research infrastructure.
