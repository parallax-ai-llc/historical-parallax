## 개요

Geshe Kelsang Gyatso was a Tibetan Buddhist 수도승 and meditation 교사 who founded the New Kadampa Tradition (NKT), one of the fastest-growing Buddhist movements in the Western world. He wrote 23 books on Buddhist 철학 and meditation. His movement became 논란이 많은 due to the Dorje Shugden controversy, putting him at odds with the Dalai Lama. He established over 1,300 meditation centers worldwide.
