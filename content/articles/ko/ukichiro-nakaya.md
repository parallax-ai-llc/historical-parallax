## 요약

Ukichiro Nakaya was a Japanese physicist who was the first person to create artificial snowflakes in a laboratory. He systematically classified natural snow crystals and showed that their shape depends on temperature and humidity. His work established the science of snow crystal formation and has applications in meteorology, cloud physics, and climate science.
