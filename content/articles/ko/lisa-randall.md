## 개요

리사 랜들 is an American theoretical physicist and a leading expert on particle physics and cosmology. She is best known for the Randall-Sundrum model, which proposes extra dimensions of space that could explain the hierarchy problem in physics. Her research explores questions about the fundamental nature of space and matter. She was the first tenured woman in the Princeton physics department and the first tenured female theoretical physicist at Harvard.
