## 개요

Dmitri Ivanovsky 러시아의 생물학자 who discovered viruses in 1892, demonstrating that the agent causing tobacco mosaic disease could pass ~을 통해 a filter too fine for bacteria. This discovery established virology as a new field of study. 비록 ~이지만 his work predated Martinus Beijerinck's independent discovery, both are credited as co-discoverers of viruses.
