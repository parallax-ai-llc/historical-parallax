## 개요

혼다 고타로 was a pioneer of 일본ese metallurgy who developed KS steel (the strongest permanent magnet steel of its era) and New KS steel. His research on magnetic materials advanced both scientific understanding and industrial applications of steel alloys. He established 일본 as a leader in metallurgical research.
