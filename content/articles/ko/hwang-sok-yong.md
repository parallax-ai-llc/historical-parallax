## 개요

Hwang Sok-yong is one of South Korea's most important living novelists. His works including The Guest, The Old Garden, and At Dusk address Korea's division, the Vietnam War, democratization, and rapid modernization. He served a five-year prison sentence for an unauthorized visit to North Korea. He is frequently mentioned as a Nobel Prize candidate.
