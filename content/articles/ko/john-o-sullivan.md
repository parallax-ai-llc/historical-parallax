## 개요

존 오설리번 led the CSIRO team that developed key technology underlying Wi-Fi wireless networking. While originally researching black hole radiation, his team developed signal processing techniques that solved multipath interference problems in wireless LANs. The patents earned CSIRO over $430 million in royalties.
