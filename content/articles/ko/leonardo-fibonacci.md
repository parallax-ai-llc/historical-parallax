## 개요

레오나르도 피보나치 (Leonardo of Pisa) was an Italian mathematician who introduced the Hindu-Arabic numeral system to 유럽 through his book 'Liber Abaci.' The Fibonacci sequence, in which each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, appears throughout nature in plant growth, branching patterns, and spiral formations. His work transformed 유럽an mathematics by replacing Roman numerals with the more efficient decimal system.
