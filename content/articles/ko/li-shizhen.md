## 개요

이시진 was a Chinese physician, pharmacologist, and naturalist who wrote the 'Bencao Gangmu' (Compendium of Materia Medica), one of the most comprehensive medical texts in history. The work took 27 years to compile and catalogs nearly 1,900 substances with over 11,000 prescriptions. It influenced not only medicine but also biology, mineralogy, and geography. The Compendium remains influential in traditional Chinese medicine.
