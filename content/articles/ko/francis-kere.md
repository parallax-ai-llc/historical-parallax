## Design Philosophy

- **Community participation**: Kéré’s practice is widely associated with participatory building processes—working with local communities to plan, build, and maintain projects.
- **Climate responsiveness**: Many projects emphasize passive cooling, shading, ventilation, and material choices suited to hot climates.
- **Local materials, modern detailing**: A signature approach is combining locally available materials (such as laterite or earth-based construction) with engineering and detailing that improves durability.

## 주요 작품 목록 and Themes

- **Gando projects**: The Gando Primary School ~이 되었다 touchstone for later commissions, demonstrating how low-cost design can deliver comfort and dignity.
- **Civic and cultural buildings**: He has designed or proposed civic, educational, and cultural facilities that use courtyards, deep overhangs, and porous façades to manage heat and light.
- **International commissions**: His later career includes high-profile work outside Burkina Faso, bringing the same emphasis on sustainability and social impact to global stages.

## Recognition and Influence

- **Pritzker Prize (2022)**: The award highlighted his integration of social purpose, environmental performance, and architectural quality.
- **Sustainability discourse**: His work is frequently cited in discussions of climate-adaptive design, especially for regions facing rising temperatures and resource constraints.
- **Educational impact**: Beyond buildings, he is often credited with showing how 건축 can serve as a tool for capacity-building and local empowerment.
- **Material ethics**: Commentators often point to his emphasis on locally sourced materials and labor as a model for lowering embodied cost ~하는 동안 strengthening local economies.
- **Broader influence**: His work is frequently used in 건축 교육 as a case study in combining aesthetics, performance, and social responsibility.
- **Climate adaptation**: He is also referenced in global climate-adaptation conversations as an example of how passive design can reduce reliance on mechanical cooling.
