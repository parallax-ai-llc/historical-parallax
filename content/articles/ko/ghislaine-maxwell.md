## 개요

The daughter of disgraced media 재벌 Robert Maxwell, she became Epstein's girlfriend, confidante, and alleged "madam," recruiting and grooming underage girls for sexual abuse. ~ 후에 Epstein's death in 2019, Maxwell ~이 되었다 primary target of prosecution.
