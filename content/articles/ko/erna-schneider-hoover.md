## 개요

Erna Schneider Hoover invented the first computerized telephone switching system in 1971, which used a computer to monitor incoming calls and automatically adjust call acceptance rates. Her invention prevented overloading ~ 동안 peak periods and ~의 하나로 간주된다 the most 중요한 advances in telecommunications. She was one of the first women to receive a software patent.
