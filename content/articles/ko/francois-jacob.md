## 유산

Jacob’s ideas helped establish molecular biology as a unified discipline linking DNA, RNA, proteins, and regulation. The operon concept ~이 되었다 standard teaching model and influenced later research on regulatory networks in more complex organisms.

## Key Ideas (High Level)

- Gene expression can be regulated by proteins that respond to signals and bind DNA.
- Cells can coordinate multiple genes as a unit to efficiently manage metabolic pathways.
- Regulation is not just “on/off” but can be tuned by conditions and feedback.

These principles generalized beyond bacteria and became part of the conceptual toolkit for 현대의 genetics and systems biology.
