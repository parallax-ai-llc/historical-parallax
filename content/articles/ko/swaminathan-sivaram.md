## 요약

Swaminathan Sivaram is an Indian polymer chemist who served as director of the National Chemical Laboratory and made significant contributions to polymer science and industrial catalysis. His research on polyolefins and coordination catalysis has been important for India's polymer industry development. He received the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize.
