## 개요

Evangelista Torricelli 이탈리아의 물리학자 and 수학자 who invented the barometer and ~로 알려져 있다 Torricelli's theorem in fluid dynamics. As a student of Galileo, he demonstrated that atmospheric pressure exists by creating the first sustained vacuum (Torricellian vacuum). The unit of pressure torr is named in his honor. He also made advances in optics and the study of cycloid curves.
