## 개요

커크 커코리언 was an 아르메니아n-American businessman who built and sold the world's largest hotel three times in Las Vegas. He was a major shareholder in MGM, Chrysler, and other major companies. He flew supplies over the 히말라야 during 제2차 세계대전 and later became a leading figure in Las Vegas development. He was one of the most influential figures in entertainment and hospitality.
