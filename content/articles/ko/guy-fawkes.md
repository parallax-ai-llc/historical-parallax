## 개요
Guy Fawkes was an English Catholic who was a member of the Gunpowder Plot of 1605, a conspiracy to blow up the House of Lords during the State Opening of Parliament. He was caught guarding 36 barrels of gunpowder beneath Parliament. He was tortured and executed. His effigy is burned annually on November 5.
