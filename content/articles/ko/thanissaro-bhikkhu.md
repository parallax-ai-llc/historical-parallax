## 요약

Thanissaro Bhikkhu (Geoffrey DeGraff) is an American Theravada Buddhist monk and scholar who is the abbot of Metta Forest Monastery in California. He is known for his extensive translations of the Pali Canon and the Thai Forest Tradition teachings. His website dhammatalks.org provides one of the largest free repositories of Buddhist texts and teachings in English. He studied under Ajahn Fuang Jotiko in the Thai Forest Tradition.
