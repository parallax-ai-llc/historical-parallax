## 개요

김봉진 founded Woowa Brothers, the company behind Baedal Minjok (Baemin), 대한민국's largest food delivery platform. Baemin transformed Korean food delivery with a mobile-first approach and became a cultural phenomenon with its distinctive branding. The company was acquired by Delivery Hero for $4 billion in 2019. Kim became one of 대한민국's most celebrated young entrepreneurs.
