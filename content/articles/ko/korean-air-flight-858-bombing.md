## 개요

북한n agents bombed 대한항공 858편 in midair killing all 115 aboard. The attack was intended to disrupt the 1988 서울 Olympics.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1987 | 115 killed in midair bombing |

## 배경

- The bombing occurred during heightened tensions on the 한반도 and ahead of the 1988 서울 Olympics.
- The incident is widely described as a state-sponsored terrorist attack intended to intimidate 대한민국 and undermine the upcoming Olympics.

## Attack (high-level)

- 대한항공 858편 was destroyed in flight by an explosive device, killing all **115** passengers and crew.
- Reporting commonly describes the device as a time bomb placed among passenger belongings and detonated midair.

## Investigation and arrests

- Two suspects traveling on forged 일본ese passports were detained after the incident.
- One suspect died by cyanide poisoning during custody; the other, **김현희**, survived and was later extradited to 대한민국.
- 김현희 confessed and said she had been trained by 북한 and acted under orders; 북한 denied responsibility.

## Aftermath and impact

- The attack drew international condemnation and increased focus on aviation security and counterterrorism in the region.
- It became part of the broader political context of the late 냉전 period in 동아시아 and is frequently referenced in accounts of 대한민국’s Olympic-era security environment.

## Ongoing questions

- Families of victims and commentators have raised questions over aspects of the investigation and accountability; some claims remain disputed in public debate.
