## 개요

Gwynne Shotwell is the president and COO of SpaceX, playing a critical role in building it into the world's most successful private space company. She manages SpaceX's day-to-day operations and commercial contracts while Elon Musk focuses on engineering vision. Under her leadership, SpaceX won NASA contracts worth billions and launched the Starlink satellite internet constellation. She is widely regarded as one of the most powerful women in the aerospace industry.
