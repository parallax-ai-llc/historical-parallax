## 개요

다나카 고이치 is a 일본ese engineer who shared the 2002 노벨 화학상 for developing soft desorption ionisation methods for mass spectrometric analyses of biological macromolecules. His technique enabled the analysis of large proteins and other biomolecules by mass spectrometry. Remarkably, he was a salaryman at Shimadzu Corporation with only a bachelor's degree when he won the 노벨상.
