## 개요

Philippine general known as 'The Butcher' convicted of kidnapping university students. Linked to extrajudicial killings during counterinsurgency.

## 개관

Jovito S. Palparan Jr. is a former senior officer in the Armed Forces of the 필리핀 who became a prominent and polarizing figure during campaigns against communist insurgents. Public discussion around him is shaped by two parallel narratives: official claims of effective counterinsurgency and extensive allegations of human-rights abuses tied to anti-insurgency operations.

## Legal Case (High-Level)

Palparan is widely reported to have been prosecuted in connection with the abduction and disappearance of students associated with activist circles. Coverage of the case commonly emphasizes:

- the role of military units operating in counterinsurgency contexts
- questions of command responsibility and the extent to which senior officers can be held liable for crimes carried out by subordinates
- witness testimony and evidentiary disputes that are typical in disappearance cases
