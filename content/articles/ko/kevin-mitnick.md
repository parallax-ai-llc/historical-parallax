## 개요
Kevin David Mitnick was an American computer security consultant and hacker who was once the FBI's most wanted cybercriminal. He gained unauthorized access to dozens of computer networks. After serving five years in prison, he became a respected security consultant. He died of pancreatic cancer in 2023.
