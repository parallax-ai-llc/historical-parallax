## 개요

Jeff Koons is an American artist known for his large-scale reproductions of banal objects and his use of kitsch imagery. His Balloon Dog sculptures, created in highly polished stainless steel, and his Puppy topiary at the Guggenheim Bilbao have made him one of the most famous and controversial living artists.
