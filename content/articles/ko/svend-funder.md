## 요약

Svend Funder is a Danish geologist and glaciologist who has studied the history of Greenland's ice sheet and its response to past climate changes. His research on ancient sediments beneath the Greenland ice sheet has revealed that Greenland was ice-free more recently than previously thought, with implications for understanding future sea level rise.
