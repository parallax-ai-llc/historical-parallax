## 개요
Hector Xavier Monsegur, known as 'Sabu,' was the leader of the hacker group LulzSec, an offshoot of Anonymous. After his arrest, he became an FBI informant and helped take down other hackers. LulzSec conducted high-profile attacks against Sony, the CIA, and the US Senate.
