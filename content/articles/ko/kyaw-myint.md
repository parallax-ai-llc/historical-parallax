## 배경

미얀마’s Shan State is frequently discussed in reporting on synthetic-drug production and trafficking networks operating in and around the Golden Triangle region.

## Operational context (high-level)

- Methamphetamine production can scale quickly with access to precursor chemicals, lab equipment, and protected territory.
- Militia relationships and fragmented authority can reduce the effectiveness of crackdowns.
- Trafficking routes often connect production zones to regional markets via border corridors.

## 결과

- Public health harms (addiction and overdose).
- Corruption risks linked to high-margin illicit trade.
- Spillover violence when armed groups compete over routes and protection.

## Why this matters

Large synthetic-drug flows are a regional security issue because they combine organized crime, armed actors, and cross-border logistics.
