## 요약

Sebastian Thrun led the development of Google's self-driving car project (Waymo) and won the DARPA Grand Challenge with his autonomous vehicle Stanley. He also founded Udacity, a leading online education platform, and developed robotic surgery techniques. His work has been at the forefront of autonomous vehicles and AI applications.
