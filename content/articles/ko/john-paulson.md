## 개요

존 폴슨 is an American hedge fund manager who made the 'greatest trade ever' by betting against subprime mortgages in 2007, earning $15 billion for his fund and $4 billion personally. His firm, Paulson & Co., became one of the world's largest hedge funds. He later made large investments in gold and Puerto Rico real estate. He donated $400 million to Harvard, the largest gift in the university's history.
