## 요약

Seki Takakazu (also known as Seki Kōwa) was a Japanese mathematician who is considered the founder of Japanese mathematics (wasan). He discovered many concepts independently of Western mathematicians, including determinants, Bernoulli numbers, and a version of calculus. He developed a system for solving simultaneous equations and contributed to the theory of algebraic equations. He is often called the 'Japanese Newton.'
