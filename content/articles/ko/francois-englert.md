## 개요

François Englert, ~와 함께 Robert Brout, independently proposed the Brout-Englert-Higgs mechanism in 1964, simultaneously with Peter Higgs. Their theory explained how elementary particles acquire mass ~을 통해 interaction with a scalar field. He shared the 2013 노벨상 in Physics with Peter Higgs ~ 후에 the discovery of the Higgs boson at CERN.
