## 개요

Dumitru Stăniloae was a Romanian Orthodox 신학자 who ~의 하나로 간주된다 the greatest Orthodox systematic theologians of the 20th 세기. His three-volume Orthodox Dogmatic Theology is one of the most comprehensive works of Orthodox systematic theology. He was imprisoned by the Romanian communist regime for five years. He translated the Philokalia into Romanian with extensive annotations.
