## Scientific Contribution (High Level)

Moser’s best-known work centers on how the brain represents space and supports navigation. ~와 함께 collaborators, he helped identify “grid cells,” neurons that fire in a repeating, grid-like pattern as an animal moves ~을 통해 an environment.

## Grid Cells and Spatial Coding

Key high-level points often emphasized in summaries:

- Grid cells are typically observed in the medial entorhinal cortex.
- Their firing fields tile an environment in a regular geometric structure.
- Grid-like representations are thought to provide a coordinate system that complements “place cells” in the hippocampus.

## Why It Mattered

The grid-cell discovery 에 기여했다 a broader framework for understanding how neural circuits encode location, distance, and movement. This line of research is frequently cited in:

- The neuroscience of learning and memory.
- Computational models of navigation and mapping.
- Studies of neurodegenerative disease, where navigation deficits can be an 초기의 symptom.
