## 수사 (High-Level)

In poisoning prosecutions, common evidentiary themes include:

- Victim symptom timelines compared with known toxicological profiles.
- Purchase or possession records for poisons or precursor materials.

- Forensic analysis of remaining substances and containers.

## Legal Proceedings (High-Level)

Key points that often matter in court proceedings for poisoning allegations:

- Causation (linking exposure to outcome) supported by medical and forensic testimony.
- Proof of intent, which may be inferred from patterns, preparation, or concealment.
- Reliability and chain-of-custody for tested samples.

## Public Impact

Cases involving covert poisoning typically draw attention because they:

- Create fear of everyday exposure routes (shared meals, communal events, or household items).
- Highlight the importance of forensic toxicology capabilities.
- Can motivate policy discussions about restricting access to highly toxic compounds.
