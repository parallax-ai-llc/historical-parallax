## 개요

칸드로 린포체 (Tsering Paldrön) is one of the very few female rinpoches (recognized reincarnate teachers) in Tibetan 불교. She is the daughter of the late Mindrolling Trichen and an important lineage holder of the Nyingma tradition. She teaches worldwide and is the spiritual director of the Samten Tse retreat center and the Mindrolling Lotus Garden in Virginia. She is known for her direct, humorous teaching style.
