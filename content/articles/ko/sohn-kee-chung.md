## 요약

Sohn Kee-chung won the gold medal in the marathon at the 1936 Berlin Olympics, forced to compete under the Japanese flag as Korea was under occupation. His training methods incorporated scientific approaches to endurance athletics. His victory remains one of the proudest moments in Korean sports history.
