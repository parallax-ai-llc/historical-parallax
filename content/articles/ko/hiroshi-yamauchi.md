## 개요

Hiroshi Yamauchi led Nintendo for over 50 years, transforming it from a playing card company into the world's most influential video game company. He oversaw the development of the Nintendo Entertainment System, Game Boy, and Super Nintendo. He hired Shigeru Miyamoto, who created Mario and Zelda. His management style was autocratic but his instincts for entertainment products were legendary.
