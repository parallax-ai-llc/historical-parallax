## Archery Discipline and Context

김우진 competes in **recurve** archery, the bow style used in the 올림픽. At elite levels, performance is often evaluated through qualification-round totals, head-to-head match resilience, and consistency under variable outdoor conditions.

대한민국 has been a dominant force in Olympic archery, supported by deep domestic competition and systematic training pipelines. As a result, earning and retaining a national-team slot can be as challenging as winning international medals.

## World Records and Scoring

Modern archery records often reflect:

- high qualification-round scores across **72 arrows**,
- tight grouping consistency rather than occasional perfect ends,
- equipment tuning and repeatable shot processes.

When athletes set world records, it typically indicates sustained technical stability under competition pressure.

## Olympic Team Success (High-Level)

Team medals in Olympic archery are won through cumulative match play where each archer's series contributes to a shared total. Team events reward not only peak scoring but also rhythm management and recovery after individual errors.

## 유산

김우진 is frequently cited among 대한민국's top archers of his era due to repeated Olympic team success and record-setting performances in international competition.
