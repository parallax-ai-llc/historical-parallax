## 요약

Zhang Qian was a Chinese explorer and diplomat who pioneered the Silk Road trade routes connecting China with Central Asia and the West. His expeditions opened overland trade that would last for centuries and transform commerce across Eurasia. He spent 13 years as a captive of the Xiongnu before completing his mission. His diplomatic journeys established the commercial and cultural links that defined East-West exchange.
