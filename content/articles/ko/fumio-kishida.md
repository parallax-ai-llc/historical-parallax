## 유산

Fumio Kishida's legacy will likely be defined primarily by his transformation of Japan's defense and security posture. Historians will likely debate whether Kishida was a pragmatic leader who adapted Japan to new realities or a cautious 정치인 who failed to tackle fundamental challenges.
