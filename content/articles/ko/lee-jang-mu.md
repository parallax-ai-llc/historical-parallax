## 개요

이장무 is a Korean materials scientist who has contributed to the development of lithium-ion battery cathode materials. His research on layered metal oxide cathodes has been important for advancing Korean battery technology, which now leads the global market through companies like LG Energy Solution and Samsung SDI.
