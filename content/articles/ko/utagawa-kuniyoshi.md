## 요약

Japanese ukiyo-e artist known for his dynamic warrior prints, cats, and supernatural subjects. His dramatic compositions and bold colors influenced manga and anime. He was one of the last great masters of ukiyo-e.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1798 | Born in Edo |
| 1827 | Gains fame with the 108 Heroes of the Suikoden series |
| 1840 | Creates satirical prints of cats and animals |
| 1855 | Creates landscape series |
| 1861 | Dies in Edo |
