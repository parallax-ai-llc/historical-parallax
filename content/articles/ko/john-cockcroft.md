## 개요

존 콕크로프트, with Ernest Walton, built the first particle accelerator capable of splitting the atom (1932), using protons accelerated to high energy to split lithium nuclei. This was the first artificial nuclear transmutation using accelerated particles. They shared the 1951 노벨 물리학상.
