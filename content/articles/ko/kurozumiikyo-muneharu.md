## 개요

Kurozumi Muneharu was a 일본ese Shinto leader who was a key successor in the Kurozumikyo movement. He helped organize and expand the religious community founded by his predecessor 구로즈미 무네타다. He systematized the teachings and established institutional structures that enabled the movement to survive and grow.
