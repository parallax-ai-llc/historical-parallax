## 개요
Gilles de Montmorency-Laval, Baron de Rais, 프랑스의 nobleman who fought alongside Joan of Arc. He was convicted and executed in 1440 for the murder of possibly over 100 children. Some historians question whether his confession was coerced to seize his estates.

## 초기 생애
Born September 10, 1404, into one of France's most 뛰어난 families. He inherited vast wealth and ~이 되었다 military hero alongside Joan of Arc at Orléans in 1429. 왕 Charles VII made him 원수 of France. [^1]

## 범죄 경력
~ 후에 Joan of Arc's 처형 in 1431, de Rais descended into profligacy and alleged occultism. He is believed to have kidnapped, raped, tortured, and murdered children — predominantly boys from peasant families. Estimates range from 80 to 200+ victims. [^2]

## 체포 / Downfall
Arrested September 15, 1440, tried by ecclesiastical and secular courts in Nantes. Under threat of 고문, he confessed. He expressed remorse, received last rites, and was hanged and burned on October 26, 1440. [^3]
