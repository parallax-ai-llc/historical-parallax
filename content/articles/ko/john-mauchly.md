## 개요

존 모클리 was an American physicist and computer pioneer who co-invented ENIAC with J. Presper Eckert. He conceived the idea of an electronic computer for weather prediction and collaborated with Eckert to build ENIAC during 제2차 세계대전. He also co-designed UNIVAC I. His vision of general-purpose electronic computing was transformative for the 20th century.
