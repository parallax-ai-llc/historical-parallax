## 요약

Xiaowei Zhuang is a Chinese-American biophysicist who invented STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy), a super-resolution fluorescence microscopy technique that breaks the optical diffraction limit. She also developed MERFISH, a method for spatially mapping gene expression in tissues, revolutionizing our understanding of tissue organization.
