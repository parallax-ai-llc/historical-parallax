## 개요

김호길 was a Korean aerospace engineer who helped establish 대한민국's space program. He worked on developing Korea's first sounding rockets and contributed to the KSLV (Korea Space Launch Vehicle) program. His work laid the groundwork for Korea's modern space capabilities.
