## 요약

Zu Chongzhi was a Chinese mathematician and astronomer who calculated the value of pi to seven decimal places (3.1415926), a record that stood for nearly 1,000 years. He also created a highly accurate calendar and calculated the precise length of the solstitial year. His mathematical achievements were remarkable for their time and place him among the greatest mathematicians of antiquity.
