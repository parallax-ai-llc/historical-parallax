## 요약

Tong Dizhou was a Chinese biologist who was the first person to clone a fish (a carp) in 1963 by nuclear transfer, decades before Dolly the sheep. He overcame extreme poverty to become one of China's greatest biologists and made pioneering contributions to embryology and developmental biology. He served as vice president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
