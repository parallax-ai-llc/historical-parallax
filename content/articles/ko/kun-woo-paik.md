## 개요

Kun-woo Paik is a Korean pianist known for his profound interpretations of the Romantic piano repertoire, particularly Liszt, Rachmaninoff, and Brahms. He studied at Juilliard and has performed with the world's major orchestras. He is widely regarded as one of Korea's greatest classical musicians.
