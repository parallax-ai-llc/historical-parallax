## 개요

이명희 is the eldest daughter of Samsung founder 이병철 and the chairwoman of Shinsegae Group. She separated Shinsegae from the Samsung Group in 1991 and built it into one of 대한민국's largest retail empires. She controls Shinsegae department stores, E-mart, and SSG.COM. She is one of the most powerful businesswomen in 대한민국 and a member of the Samsung founding family.
