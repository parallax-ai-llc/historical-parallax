## 개요

리슈푸 is the founder of Geely Automobile Holdings and the largest shareholder of Mercedes-Benz. He built Geely from a refrigerator parts manufacturer into 중국's largest privately owned automaker. His acquisition of Volvo Cars from Ford for $1.8 billion in 2010 was a landmark deal. He also acquired a 9.7% stake in Daimler (now Mercedes-Benz) and controls Lotus and Polestar.
