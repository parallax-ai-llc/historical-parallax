## Modus Operandi (Common Pattern)

Romance-scam operations generally follow a repeatable playbook:

1. **Identity construction:** Use fabricated personas, stolen photos, or misleading biographical details.
2. **Trust-building phase:** Sustained conversation over email, social media, or dating platforms to create emotional dependency.
3. **Pretext creation:** Claims of emergencies, travel problems, medical needs, business issues, or “fees” that require immediate payment.
4. **Money movement:** Direct bank transfers, wire transfers, gift cards, or intermediary accounts to obscure tracing.
5. **Re-contact and escalation:** Additional payment requests once a victim has already paid (sometimes using threats or guilt).
