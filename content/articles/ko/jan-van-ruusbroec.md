## 개요

Jan van Ruusbroec was a Flemish mystic and priest who is considered the greatest mystic of the Low Countries. His masterwork The Spiritual Espousals describes the mystical journey of the soul toward union with God in three stages. He founded the community of canons regular at Groenendaal, which became a center of mystical spirituality. His works influenced Thomas à Kempis, the Devotio Moderna, and later Christian mysticism.
