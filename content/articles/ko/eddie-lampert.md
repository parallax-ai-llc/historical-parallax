## 개요

Eddie Lampert is the founder of ESL Investments and the former chairman and CEO of Sears Holdings. He was once called the 'next Warren Buffett' for his investment prowess. 그러나 his management of Sears and Kmart ~의 하나가 되었다 the most 논란이 많은 episodes in American retail history, as the companies declined under his leadership and 결국 filed for bankruptcy in 2018. He acquired Sears out of bankruptcy ~을 통해 a 논란이 많은 bid.
