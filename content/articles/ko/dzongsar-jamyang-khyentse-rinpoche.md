## 개요

Dzongsar Jamyang Khyentse Rinpoche is a Bhutanese Buddhist lama, 영화감독, and 저자 who is one of the most creative and unconventional Tibetan Buddhist teachers of the 현대의 era. He is the incarnation of Jamyang Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö. He has directed acclaimed films including The Cup and Travellers and Magicians. His books, including What Makes You Not a Buddhist, present Buddhism in provocative 현대의 terms.
