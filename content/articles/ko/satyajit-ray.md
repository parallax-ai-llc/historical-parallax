## 요약

Satyajit Ray was an Indian filmmaker who is considered one of the greatest directors in world cinema. His Apu Trilogy brought Indian cinema to international attention and demonstrated the power of humanistic storytelling. He directed 36 films, composed music, designed sets, and wrote screenplays. He received an honorary Academy Award shortly before his death.
