## 요약

Wim Wenders is a German filmmaker known for his road movies, contemplative pace, and visual beauty. Paris, Texas and Wings of Desire are landmarks of European art cinema. His later documentaries, including Buena Vista Social Club and Pina, demonstrate his range and his commitment to capturing music and dance on film.
