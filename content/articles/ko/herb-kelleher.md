## 개요

Herb Kelleher co-founded Southwest Airlines and served as its CEO and chairman, revolutionizing the airline industry with low-cost, no-frills air travel. He created a unique corporate culture emphasizing fun, employee empowerment, and customer service. Southwest became the most consistently profitable airline in U.S. history. His business model of point-to-point service with a single aircraft type was widely imitated.
