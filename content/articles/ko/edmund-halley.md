Finally, Halley's failure to produce major original astronomical research ~ 동안 two decades of the "astronomy wars" with Flamsteed and the subsequent period has been noted as hampering English astronomical progress ~ 동안 a crucial period when Continental astronomers were making rapid advances.[^124] ~하는 동안 the 분쟁 was not solely Halley's fault, his role in exacerbating it 에 기여했다 this scientific stagnation.
