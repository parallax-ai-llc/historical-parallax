## 유산

황후 Dowager Lü's legacy is deeply contradictory, reflecting the tension between her effective governance and her violent methods, as well as between her actual achievements and the distorting lens of Confucian historiography. For most of Chinese imperial history, Lü Zhi served as a cautionary tale.
