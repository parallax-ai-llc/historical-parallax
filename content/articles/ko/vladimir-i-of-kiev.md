## 요약

Vladimir I the Great was the Grand Prince of Kiev who converted to Christianity in 988 and baptized the Kievan Rus, establishing Orthodox Christianity as the religion of the East Slavic peoples. He sent envoys to study different faiths before choosing Eastern Orthodox Christianity. His mass baptism of the population of Kiev in the Dnieper River is one of the most significant events in Slavic history. He is venerated as a saint in the Eastern Orthodox Church.
