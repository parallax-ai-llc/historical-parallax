## 개요

Ganapathi Thanikaimoni 인도의 palynologist who made 중요한 contributions to the study of pollen morphology in tropical plants. His work at the Institut Français de Pondichéry documented the pollen of thousands of tropical plant species. He established systematic palynological research in India and trained many researchers.
