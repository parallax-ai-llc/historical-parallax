## 개요

Ei-ichi Negishi 일본의-American 화학자 who shared the 2010 노벨상 in Chemistry for palladium-catalyzed cross couplings in organic synthesis. The Negishi coupling, which he developed, is a 강력한 method for forming carbon-carbon bonds using organozinc compounds. His work enabled the synthesis of complex molecules used in 의학, agriculture, and electronics.
