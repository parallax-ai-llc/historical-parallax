## 배경

카일 데이비스 is primarily known in connection with Three Arrows Capital (3AC), a crypto-focused hedge fund that collapsed during the broader 2022 crypto credit unwind.

## Collapse mechanics (high-level)

- Leverage and concentrated positions can become fatal when market prices gap down and lenders demand collateral.
- Interconnected lending means one fund’s failure can propagate losses across multiple platforms.

## Legal and recovery process

- Insolvency and liquidation proceedings typically focus on asset tracing, creditor coordination, and potential claims about disclosure and risk management.
- Jurisdictional complexity is common when funds, counterparties, and executives are spread across multiple countries.

## Why this matters

3AC is often cited as a catalyst for second-order failures in crypto lending, illustrating how opaque leverage can turn market volatility into a solvency crisis.
