## 개요

Jack Steinberger shared the 1988 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of the muon neutrino. He made important contributions to the study of pions and other particles. He later joined CERN and led the ALEPH experiment at the Large Electron-Positron Collider. He fled Nazi Germany as a child and was raised by foster parents in Illinois.
