## 개요

렉스 프리드먼 is a 러시아n-American computer scientist, 인공지능 researcher, and podcast host whose long-form interview format has made him one of the most listened-to voices in technology and science media. Born in 러시아 and raised partly in the 미국, Fridman built an academic career at MIT before his podcast eclipsed his research work in public influence. He is known for his earnest, philosophical interviewing style and his willingness to engage guests across the full spectrum of human inquiry.

His podcast, the *렉스 프리드먼 Podcast* (originally the *Artificial Intelligence Podcast*), has featured hundreds of guests including Elon Musk, Vladimir Putin, 조 로건, and leading figures in physics, mathematics, biology, and philosophy. Episodes regularly exceed three to five hours, placing Fridman in a niche of extreme long-form media that prizes depth over brevity.

Fridman occupies an unusual position as both a credentialed researcher and a mass-media personality, drawing audiences from academic communities, tech enthusiasts, and general listeners who seek substantive conversation outside mainstream broadcast formats.

## Early Life & Background

렉스 프리드먼 was born on August 15, 1986, in 러시아. His family emigrated, and he spent formative years in the 미국, where he pursued mathematics and computer science. He earned his PhD from Drexel University, focusing on 머신러닝 and human-robot interaction. He subsequently joined MIT as a research scientist affiliated with the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL), where his work examined semi-supervised learning applied to autonomous vehicles and human activity recognition.

## 두각

Fridman launched his podcast in 2018 under the title *Artificial Intelligence Podcast*, interviewing researchers such as Yoshua Bengio, Yann LeCun, and Stuart Russell. As his audience grew, he broadened the scope to include entrepreneurs, athletes, historians, and political figures, renaming the show the *렉스 프리드먼 Podcast* in 2020. By 2022, his YouTube channel had surpassed 2 million subscribers; by 2024 that figure exceeded 4 million. His interview with Elon Musk across multiple appearances became among the most-viewed long-form AI and technology conversations on the platform.

In 2023, Fridman conducted a widely covered sit-down interview with 러시아n President Vladimir Putin, one of the few such interviews granted to a Western media figure amid the ongoing war in 우크라이나. The interview drew both significant viewership and substantial criticism.

## Key Achievements

- Produced over 400 podcast episodes spanning AI, physics, history, philosophy, and geopolitics by 2025.
- Conducted the first major Western podcast interview with Vladimir Putin (February 2024).
- Published peer-reviewed research on semi-supervised learning and autonomous vehicle perception during his MIT tenure.
- Built one of the largest independent long-form interview audiences globally, with cumulative podcast downloads in the hundreds of millions.

## Controversies & Criticism

Fridman's interview with Putin was criticized by journalists and foreign policy analysts who argued the format allowed Putin to deliver propaganda without sufficient challenge. Critics noted Fridman's non-confrontational style, which he applies uniformly across guests, is poorly suited to interviewing heads of state engaged in active conflict. Separately, his perceived credulity toward fringe scientific claims and conspiracy-adjacent guests has drawn criticism from scientists who argue his platform amplifies misinformation. Supporters counter that his open-ended format preserves intellectual honesty and listener autonomy.

## Legacy & Influence

Fridman helped normalize ultra-long-form interview media at a moment when mainstream media was trending shorter. His model — independent, ad-supported, distributed via YouTube and podcast platforms — influenced a generation of researcher-communicators who followed similar paths outside institutional media. Within AI specifically, his early episodes serve as a historical record of the field's leading thinkers during the deep learning revolution of the late 2010s and early 2020s.
