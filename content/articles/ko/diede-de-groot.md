## What “Golden Grand Slam” Means

In wheelchair tennis, the term is used (by analogy with able-bodied tennis) to describe winning:

1. The four major tournaments in the same calendar year, and
2. Paralympic gold in the same year.

De Groot’s 2021 season is frequently cited as a benchmark for sustained excellence across the full annual schedule.
