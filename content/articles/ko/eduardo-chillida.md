## 개요

Eduardo Chillida was a Basque 조각가 known for his monumental steel and iron sculptures exploring space, form, and Basque identity. His Comb of the Wind installation on the San Sebastian coast is one of the most 유명한 site-specific works. His work combines industrial materials with organic, hand-forged character.
