## 개요

Iris Fontbona is the widow of Andrónico Luksic Sr. and the richest person in Chile. She controls the Luksic Group, one of Latin America's largest conglomerates, with interests in mining (Antofagasta PLC), banking (Banco de Chile), beverages (CCU), and shipping. The family's Antofagasta PLC is one of the world's largest copper producers. She is one of the wealthiest women in the world.
