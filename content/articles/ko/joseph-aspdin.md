## 개요

조지프 애스프딘 invented Portland cement in 1824, patenting a process that created a hydraulic cement by heating a mixture of clay and limestone. Portland cement became the basis of modern concrete and the most widely used building material in the world. His invention transformed construction and enabled the creation of the modern built environment.
