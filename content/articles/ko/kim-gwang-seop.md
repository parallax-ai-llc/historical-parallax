## 개요

김광섭 was a Korean forest scientist who led reforestation efforts in 대한민국 after the 한국전쟁. Korea's mountains were nearly completely deforested, and his research on native tree species and reforestation techniques was crucial to Korea's successful reforestation, considered one of the most successful environmental restoration projects in world history.
