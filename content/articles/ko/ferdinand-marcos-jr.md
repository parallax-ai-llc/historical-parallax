## Additional Context

### 배경 and family legacy

Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos Jr. is the son of former Philippine 대통령 Ferdinand Marcos and former first lady Imelda Marcos. His national political career has often been discussed in the context of the Marcos family's return to power ~ 후에 the 1986 People Power Revolution and the subsequent debates over alleged ill-gotten wealth.

### 대통령직 (selected themes)

High-level issues commonly associated with his administration and public discourse include:

- Institutional trust and the historical record of martial law under his father.
- Governance and anti-corruption expectations in light of long-running allegations about the Marcos era.
- Foreign policy balancing among regional partners and major powers.

### Public controversy

Critics have accused Marcos Jr. and allied networks of historical revisionism regarding the martial-law period. Supporters have emphasized economic development and political stability narratives. These competing frames have made memory politics a central part of his public image.
