## 요약

The RMS Titanic sank on April 15, 1912, after striking an iceberg on its maiden voyage. Over 1,500 of the 2,224 passengers and crew died in the freezing North Atlantic. The 'unsinkable' ship's loss led to major improvements in maritime safety regulations.
