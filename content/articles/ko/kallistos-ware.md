## 개요

칼리스토스 웨어 was a British Orthodox bishop, theologian, and author who became one of the most important figures introducing Eastern Orthodoxy to the English-speaking world. His books The Orthodox Church and The Orthodox Way became standard introductions to Orthodox 기독교. He was a titular Metropolitan in the Ecumenical Patriarchate and Spalding Lecturer at Oxford for decades. His gentle wisdom and irenic spirit made him widely loved across Christian traditions.
