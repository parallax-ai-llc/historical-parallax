## 요약

Vitaly Ginzburg was a Soviet-Russian theoretical physicist who shared the 2003 Nobel Prize in Physics for pioneering contributions to the theory of superconductors and superfluids. He co-developed the Ginzburg-Landau theory of superconductivity and contributed to the Soviet hydrogen bomb program. Ginzburg also made important contributions to astrophysics and the theory of cosmic radiation.
