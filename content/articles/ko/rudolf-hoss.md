## 요약
Rudolf Franz Ferdinand Höss was a German SS officer who served as commandant of the Auschwitz concentration camp from 1940 to 1943. He oversaw the murder of over 1.1 million people. He was captured by British forces, tried by a Polish tribunal, and hanged at Auschwitz in 1947.
