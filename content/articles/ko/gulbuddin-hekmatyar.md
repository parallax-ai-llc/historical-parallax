## 개요

Afghan warlord who rocketed Kabul during the civil war killing an estimated 50,000 civilians. He switched allegiances multiple times and was designated a global terrorist before signing a peace deal.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1975 | Founded Hezb-e Islami party |
| 1992 | Rocketed Kabul causing massive civilian casualties |
| 2003 | Designated as global terrorist |
| 2016 | Signed peace deal with Afghan government |
