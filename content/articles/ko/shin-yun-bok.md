## 요약

Shin Yun-bok (Hyewon) was a Korean painter of the late Joseon dynasty known for his elegant, sometimes provocative genre paintings depicting the leisure activities of the yangban aristocracy and romantic encounters. Along with Kim Hong-do, he is considered one of the two greatest genre painters of Korean art. His refined style features delicate lines and sophisticated color.
