## 개요

카리나 (born Yu Ji-min on April 11, 2000) is a 대한민국n singer, dancer, and model best known as the leader of the SM Entertainment girl group aespa. Since her debut in November 2020, she has become one of the most recognizable faces in fourth-generation K-pop, praised for her striking visuals, precise dancing, and commanding stage presence. Her dual identity as both a real-world performer and her group's AI avatar counterpart — a defining concept of aespa's lore — helped distinguish her in an increasingly competitive industry.

Beyond her group activities, 카리나 has carved out a significant solo presence through brand ambassadorships, runway appearances, and modeling campaigns with luxury fashion houses. She represents a new archetype of the K-pop idol: technically rigorous, visually iconic, and deeply embedded in digital and fashion culture simultaneously.

## Early Life & Background

Yu Ji-min was born on April 11, 2000, in Seongnam, Gyeonggi Province, 대한민국. She grew up with a strong interest in performance arts and began training at SM Entertainment after passing their highly competitive audition process. She trained for approximately four years before her debut, during which she refined her vocal, dance, and performance skills under the label's intensive system. Prior to her official debut, she gained attention in 2019 when a pre-debut video of her dancing went viral online, generating early public interest.

## 두각

카리나 debuted as the leader of aespa on November 17, 2020, with the single "Black Mamba." The group's conceptual framework — blending real members with AI avatar counterparts in a fictional metaverse — was unprecedented in K-pop, and 카리나's avatar, æ카리나, became central to the narrative. "Black Mamba" achieved over 100 million views on YouTube within weeks, marking one of the fastest debuts for a K-pop group at the time.

The group's subsequent releases, including "Next Level" (2021) and "Girls" (2022), cemented aespa's position at the top of fourth-generation K-pop. "Next Level" spent multiple weeks atop 대한민국n music charts and became a cultural phenomenon domestically. 카리나's role as center and leader placed her consistently at the forefront of the group's public image.

## Key Achievements

- Co-led aespa to win the Rookie of the Year award at multiple major ceremonies in 2021, including the Melon Music Awards and Mnet 아시아n Music Awards.
- Aespa's mini-album *Savage* (2021) debuted at number 20 on the Billboard 200, a significant milestone for a relatively new act.
- Named a global ambassador for Givenchy in 2022, one of the few fourth-generation idols to secure a major French luxury house partnership so early in their career.
- Consistently ranked among the most influential K-pop idols in brand reputation indices published by the Korean Business Research Institute.

## Controversies & Criticism

In early 2023, 카리나 confirmed a relationship with actor Lee Jae-wook, which prompted a polarized response from some segments of the fanbase. She issued a public apology — a practice common in the K-pop industry but widely criticized internationally as reflective of unrealistic expectations placed on idols regarding their private lives. The episode renewed broader discourse about parasocial relationships and the emotional labor demanded of K-pop performers.

## Legacy & Influence

카리나 has helped define the aesthetic and performative standards of fourth-generation K-pop. Her blend of technical dancing, fashion-forward image, and digital-native branding has influenced how newer groups and idols are packaged and marketed. As aespa continues to expand internationally, 카리나 remains central to the group's identity and to SM Entertainment's global strategy.
