## 개요

Eugene Shoemaker is considered the founder of planetary 과학. He proved that Meteor Crater in Arizona was caused by an asteroid impact and established the field of astrogeology. He co-discovered Comet Shoemaker-Levy 9. He trained Apollo astronauts in geology and was the first person whose ashes were sent to the Moon.
