## 개요

Giorgio Parisi 이탈리아의 theoretical 물리학자 who shared the 2021 노벨상 in Physics for the discovery of the interplay of disorder and fluctuations in physical systems from atomic to planetary scales. His work on spin glasses and complex systems revealed hidden patterns in disordered materials, with applications across physics, mathematics, biology, and machine learning.
