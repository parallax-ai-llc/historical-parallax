## 개요

김현준 is a Korean medical engineer who has contributed to the development of robotic surgery systems in 대한민국. His research on surgical robotics has helped Korea develop indigenous robotic surgery platforms, advancing minimally invasive surgical techniques and reducing Korea's dependence on imported surgical robots.
