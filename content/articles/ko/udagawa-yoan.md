## 요약

Udagawa Yoan was a Japanese scholar of Western studies who introduced modern chemistry and botany to Japan. His Seimi Kaiso (Introduction to Chemistry, 1837) was the first Japanese chemistry textbook. He coined many Japanese scientific terms still in use today, including the Japanese words for oxygen, hydrogen, nitrogen, and carbon.
