> "I want to die a slave to principles, not to men." - Stated in a letter to Pancho Villa, reflecting his commitment to agrarian reform over political alliances

> "It's better to die on your feet than to live on your knees." - Often attributed to Zapata, though possibly apocryphal; ~이 되었다 rallying cry for 혁명가 movements worldwide

> "The land belongs to those who work it with their hands." - Central principle of the Plan de Ayala and Zapatista land reform 철학

> "I am resolved to struggle against everything and everybody." - From a 1918 letter expressing his determination to continue fighting ~에도 불구하고 military setbacks

> "If there is no justice for the people, let there be no peace for the government." - Articulating the Zapatista position that social reform must precede political reconciliation
