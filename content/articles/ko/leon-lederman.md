## 개요

리언 레더먼 was an American experimental physicist who shared the 1988 노벨 물리학상 for the discovery of the muon neutrino. He also co-discovered the bottom quark in 1977 at Fermilab. He served as director of Fermilab and coined the term 'God Particle' for the Higgs boson in his popular book. Lederman was a passionate advocate for science education.
