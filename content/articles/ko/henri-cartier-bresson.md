## 개요

Henri Cartier-Bresson was a French photographer who is considered the father of modern photojournalism and the master of candid photography. His concept of the 'decisive moment' - capturing a fleeting instant that reveals the essence of an event - defined photographic practice. He co-founded the Magnum Photos agency in 1947.
