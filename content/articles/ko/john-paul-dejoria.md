## 개요

존 폴 데조리아 co-founded Paul Mitchell hair products and Patrón Spirits, building two billion-dollar consumer brands from nothing. He was homeless twice before founding Paul Mitchell with $700 in 1980. Paul Mitchell Systems grew into a $1 billion hair care company. He later co-founded Patrón Tequila, which Bacardi acquired for $5.1 billion in 2018. His rags-to-riches story is one of the most remarkable in American entrepreneurship.
