## 개요

Gurdev Singh Khush is an Indian-American plant geneticist who developed over 300 rice varieties at the International Rice Research Institute (IRRI), including IR36 and IR64 — the most widely planted rice varieties in history. His high-yielding, disease-resistant rice varieties helped feed billions of people in Asia and contributed to the Green Revolution.
