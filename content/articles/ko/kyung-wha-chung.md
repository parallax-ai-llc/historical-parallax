## 개요

정경화 is a Korean violinist who was one of the first 아시아n classical musicians to achieve international stardom. Her passionate, technically brilliant playing brought her to prominence after sharing first prize at the Leventritt Competition in 1967. Her recordings of the Beethoven, Brahms, and Sibelius violin concertos are considered definitive.
