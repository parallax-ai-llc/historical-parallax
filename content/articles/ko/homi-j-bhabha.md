## 개요

Homi Jehangir Bhabha was an Indian nuclear physicist who is known as the father of the Indian nuclear program. He established the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research and the Atomic Energy Commission of India. He made important contributions to quantum theory, including the Bhabha scattering cross-section for electron-positron scattering. He died in a plane crash on Mont Blanc.
