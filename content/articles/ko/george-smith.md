## 개요

George Smith 미국의 화학자 who shared the 2018 노벨상 in Chemistry for the phage display of peptides and antibodies. His development of phage display 기술 in 1985 enabled the rapid discovery of new therapeutic antibodies and biomolecules. This technique is used in drug development, diagnostics, and biotechnology.
