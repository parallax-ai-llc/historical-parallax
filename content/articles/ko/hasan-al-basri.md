## 개요

Hasan al-Basri was an early Muslim theologian, preacher, and ascetic who is one of the most important figures in the development of Islamic thought. He was renowned for his piety, eloquence, and moral courage. He is claimed as a spiritual ancestor by both Sufi orders and Mu'tazili rationalist theologians. His teachings on repentance, fear of God, and asceticism profoundly influenced Islamic spirituality.
