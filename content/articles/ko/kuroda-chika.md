## 개요

구로다 지카 was a pioneering 일본ese chemist who was the first 일본ese woman to earn a bachelor's degree in science (1916) and the second to earn a Doctor of Science degree (1929). She studied the chemistry of natural pigments, particularly safflower and shellfish purple dyes, making significant contributions to organic chemistry.
