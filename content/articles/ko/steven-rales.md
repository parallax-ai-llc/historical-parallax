## 요약

Steven Rales co-founded Danaher Corporation with his brother Mitchell, building one of the most admired industrial conglomerates in the world. He has served as chairman of Danaher's board throughout its extraordinary growth. He is also an accomplished film producer through Indian Paintbrush production company, which produced films like The Grand Budapest Hotel and Isle of Dogs. He represents the intersection of industrial excellence and cultural patronage.
