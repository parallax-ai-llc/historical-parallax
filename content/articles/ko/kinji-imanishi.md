## 개요

이마니시 긴지 was a 일본ese ecologist and primatologist who founded 일본ese primatology and developed a unique theory of evolution emphasizing habitat segregation over competition. He pioneered the study of primate social behavior, identifying cultural behavior in 일본ese macaques decades before Western primatologists. His ecological approach influenced conservation biology.
