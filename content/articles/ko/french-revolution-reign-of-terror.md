## 개요

The Reign of Terror (1793-94) was the most radical phase of the French Revolution. Under Robespierre's Committee of Public Safety, an estimated 16,594 people were executed by guillotine, including Marie Antoinette and 결국 Robespierre himself.

| 1794-07-28 | Robespierre executed |
