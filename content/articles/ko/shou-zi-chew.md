## 요약

Shou Zi Chew is the CEO of TikTok and CFO of ByteDance. He became a global figure when he testified before the U.S. Congress in March 2023 defending TikTok against allegations of Chinese government influence and data security concerns. A former Goldman Sachs banker and CFO of Xiaomi, he was appointed TikTok CEO in 2021. He has navigated the company through one of the most complex geopolitical technology disputes in history.
