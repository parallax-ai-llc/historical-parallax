## 요약

Shimonoseki (1895) ended the First Sino-Japanese War. China ceded Taiwan to Japan, recognized Korean independence, and was further humiliated.

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1894 | War begins |
| 1895-04-17 | Treaty signed |
| 1895 | Taiwan ceded |
| 1895 | Triple Intervention |
| 1895 | Japan colonial power |
| 1945 | Taiwan returned |
