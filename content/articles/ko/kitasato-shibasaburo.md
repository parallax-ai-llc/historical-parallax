## 개요

기타사토 시바사부로 was a 일본ese physician and bacteriologist who co-discovered the infectious agent of bubonic plague. He also developed a treatment for diphtheria using antitoxin serum with Emil von Behring and isolated the tetanus bacillus. He founded the Kitasato Institute and is considered one of the founders of 일본ese modern medicine.
