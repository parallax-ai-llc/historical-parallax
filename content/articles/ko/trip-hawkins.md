## 요약

Trip Hawkins founded Electronic Arts (EA) in 1982, building it into the world's largest video game publisher. He pioneered the concept of treating game developers as creative artists (hence 'Electronic Arts'). EA became dominant through franchises like Madden NFL, FIFA, The Sims, and Battlefield. He also founded 3DO Company for console gaming. His vision of games as an art form transformed the entertainment industry.
