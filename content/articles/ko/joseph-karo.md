## 개요

요제프 카로 was a rabbi and jurist who authored the Shulchan Aruch (Set Table), the most authoritative code of Jewish law. Originally from 스페인, he settled in Safed in the Land of 이스라엘 after the expulsion. The Shulchan Aruch became the standard reference for Jewish legal practice across the Sephardic and later the Ashkenazi world. He was also a mystic who recorded his experiences with a heavenly mentor (maggid).
