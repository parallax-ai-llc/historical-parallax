## 요약

Seok Ju-myeong was a prominent Korean botanist who dedicated his career to cataloging and studying Korea's native plant species. He established the Korean names for hundreds of plant species and worked to preserve botanical knowledge during the Japanese colonial period. His botanical surveys laid the foundation for Korean plant taxonomy.
