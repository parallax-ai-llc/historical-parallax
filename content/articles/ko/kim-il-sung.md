## 개요

Founder and 'Eternal President' of 북한 who established one of the world's most repressive totalitarian states. He started the 한국전쟁, created an extreme personality cult, and built a dynasty that continues today.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1948 | Became leader of 북한 |
| 1950 | Launched 한국전쟁 |
| 1972 | Became president under new constitution |
| 1994 | Died of heart attack; succeeded by son 김정일 |
