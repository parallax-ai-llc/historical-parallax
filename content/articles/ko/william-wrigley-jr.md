## 요약

William Wrigley Jr. founded the Wm. Wrigley Jr. Company and built it into the world's largest chewing gum manufacturer. He started as a soap salesman who gave away gum as a premium, then switched to selling gum when it proved more popular. Wrigley's Juicy Fruit and Spearmint became iconic American brands. He also owned the Chicago Cubs and Catalina Island off the coast of California.
