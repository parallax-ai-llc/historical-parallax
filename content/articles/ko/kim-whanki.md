## 개요

김환기 was the most important Korean abstract painter of the 20th century. His early work combined Korean motifs with 유럽an modernism, while his later dot paintings created in 뉴욕 are celebrated for their cosmic, meditative beauty. His painting Universe sold for a record price in Korean art.
