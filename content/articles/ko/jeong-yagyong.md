## 개요

Jeong Yagyong (pen name Dasan) was a Korean philosopher and scholar of the late Joseon Dynasty who is considered the greatest figure in the Silhak (Practical Learning) movement. He wrote over 500 volumes covering philosophy, law, government, engineering, and medicine. He spent 18 years in exile during which he produced his most important works. He advocated for practical reforms including land redistribution and modern governance.
