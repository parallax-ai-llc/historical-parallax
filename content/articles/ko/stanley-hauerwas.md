## 요약

Stanley Hauerwas is an American theologian and ethicist who has been one of the most influential voices in Christian ethics. His emphasis on virtue, narrative, and the church as a distinctive community challenged both liberal and conservative assumptions. Time magazine named him 'America's Best Theologian' in 2001. His works, including A Community of Character and The Peaceable Kingdom, advocate for a pacifist, community-centered Christianity.
