## 개요

존 아치볼드 휠러 was an American theoretical physicist who coined the terms 'black hole,' 'wormhole,' and 'quantum foam.' He made important contributions to nuclear physics through the Wheeler-Feynman absorber theory and the Bohr-Wheeler theory of nuclear fission. Wheeler mentored many prominent physicists including Richard Feynman and 킵 손. He was one of the last major physicists to have worked with both Einstein and Bohr.
