## 개요

가와이 하야오 was a 일본ese clinical psychologist and cultural figure who introduced Jungian psychology to 일본. While primarily known as a psychologist, his work deeply explored the intersection of 일본ese spirituality, 불교, and psychology. He was the first 일본ese Jungian analyst and served as Commissioner of the Agency for Cultural Affairs. His books on 일본ese mythology and the 일본ese psyche bridged psychology and spirituality.
