## 개요

레나르트 칼레손 is a Swedish mathematician who proved Carleson's theorem (the pointwise convergence of Fourier series for L² functions), solving a major problem that had been open since 1913. He also proved the existence of strange attractors in dynamical systems. He received the Abel Prize in 2006.
