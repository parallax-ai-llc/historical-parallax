## 개요

Irène Joliot-Curie was a French scientist who shared the 1935 Nobel Prize in Chemistry with her husband Frédéric Joliot-Curie for their discovery of artificial radioactivity. She was the daughter of Marie and Pierre Curie and continued the family legacy in nuclear physics. She also contributed to the discovery of nuclear fission and served as undersecretary of state for scientific research in France.
