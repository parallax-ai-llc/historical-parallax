## 개요

George Church is a pioneer of genomics who helped initiate the Human Genome Project and developed multiple DNA sequencing technologies. He invented foundational methods in synthetic biology, including CRISPR gene editing in human cells, genome writing, and xenotransplantation (pig-to-human organ transplants). He is working on de-extinction of the woolly mammoth.
