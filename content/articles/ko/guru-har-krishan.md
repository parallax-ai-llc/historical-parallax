## 개요

Guru Har Krishan was the eighth Sikh Guru who became Guru at the age of five, the youngest of all Sikh Gurus. He served as Guru for only two years and eight months before dying of smallpox at age seven. Despite his young age, he is celebrated for his compassion, having healed many during a smallpox epidemic in Delhi. The Gurdwara Bangla Sahib in New Delhi marks the site where he stayed and healed the sick.
