## 요약

Xu Rongmao is the founder of Shimao Group, one of China's largest property developers. He built Shimao from a small property company in Fujian into a major developer of residential, commercial, and hotel properties. Shimao Group also operates hotels and tourism businesses. The company was affected by the Chinese property downturn but has been restructuring its debt. He is one of the wealthiest real estate developers in China.
