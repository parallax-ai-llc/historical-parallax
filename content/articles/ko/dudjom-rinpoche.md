## 개요

Dudjom Rinpoche was a Tibetan Buddhist master who served as the first Supreme Head of the Nyingma school in exile. He was one of the greatest scholars and meditation masters of the 20th 세기. His two-volume Nyingma School of Tibetan Buddhism is the definitive history and doctrinal presentation of the Nyingma tradition. He was also a 저명한 treasure revealer (terton) who discovered hidden spiritual texts.
