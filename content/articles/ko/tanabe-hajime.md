## 요약

Tanabe Hajime was a Japanese mathematician and philosopher who contributed to the development of mathematical logic and philosophy of science in Japan. He studied under Husserl in Germany and established the Kyoto School's mathematical-philosophical tradition. His work on dialectical logic influenced Japanese scientific thought.
