## 개요

South African prime minister known as the 'Architect of Apartheid' who designed and implemented the apartheid system of racial segregation. He created the Bantustan system and restricted black South Africans' rights. Assassinated in parliament.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1950 | Became minister of native affairs; designed apartheid |
| 1958 | Became prime minister |
| 1960 | Sharpeville massacre under his watch |
| 1966 | Assassinated by Dimitri Tsafendas in parliament |
