## 개요

추이랍치 led the team that identified the cystic fibrosis gene (CFTR) in 1989, one of the first major achievements of positional cloning. His discovery of the gene responsible for the most common genetic disease in Caucasians opened the door to genetic testing and potential gene therapies. He later served as Vice-Chancellor of the University of 홍콩.
