## 요약

Tadao Ando is a self-taught Japanese architect known for his masterful use of reinforced concrete and natural light. His meditative buildings, including the Church of the Light and the Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth, embody a spiritual minimalism that synthesizes Western modernism with Japanese spatial tradition. He received the Pritzker Prize in 1995.
