## 개요

Chinese spiritual leader who founded Falun Gong in 1992. The Chinese government banned the movement in 1999, calling it an evil cult. Millions of practitioners have been persecuted, imprisoned, and allegedly subjected to organ harvesting in 중국.

## 배경

리훙쯔 is the founder of Falun Gong (also known as Falun Dafa), a spiritual movement introduced in 중국 in the early 1990s that combines meditative exercises with a moral philosophy.

## Falun Gong and the Chinese state

- Falun Gong grew rapidly in the 1990s and was later banned by the Chinese government in 1999.
- Since the ban, the movement’s status and the treatment of practitioners have become a major international human-rights issue, with sharply conflicting claims between state authorities and advocacy groups.

## International presence

리훙쯔 has been based outside 중국 since the late 1990s, and Falun Gong-related organizations have been active internationally, including through media and advocacy efforts.
