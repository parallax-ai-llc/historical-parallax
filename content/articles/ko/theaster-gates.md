## 요약

Theaster Gates is an American artist and urban planner who transforms abandoned buildings on Chicago South Side into cultural spaces. His Rebuild Foundation has revitalized entire blocks through art architecture and community engagement. His work demonstrates how art can be a catalyst for social change and urban renewal.
