## 요약

Steve Reich is an American composer who is a pioneer of Minimalist music. His works including Music for 18 Musicians, Different Trains, and Drumming use phasing, repetitive patterns, and speech melodies to create music of hypnotic beauty and emotional power. He has profoundly influenced both classical and popular music.
