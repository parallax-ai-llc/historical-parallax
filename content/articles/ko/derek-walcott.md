## 개요

Derek Walcott was a 성인 Lucian 시인 and 극작가 who received the 노벨상 in 문학 in 1992. His epic poem Omeros reimagines Homer's Iliad in a Caribbean setting. His work explores the cultural complexity of the Caribbean, bridging European literary tradition and Caribbean experience with extraordinary linguistic virtuosity.
