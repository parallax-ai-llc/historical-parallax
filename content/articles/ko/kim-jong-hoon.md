## 개요

김종훈 is a Korean shipbuilding engineer who contributed to the development of advanced ship designs at Hyundai Heavy Industries. His engineering work on LNG carriers and ultra-large container ships helped Korea become the world's largest shipbuilding nation, dominating the global market for large commercial vessels.
