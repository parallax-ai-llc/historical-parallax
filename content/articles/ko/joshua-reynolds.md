## 개요

Sir 조슈아 레이놀즈 was the most influential British painter of the 18th century and first president of the Royal Academy. His portraits elevated the genre by incorporating the grand manner of 르네상스 and Baroque painting. His Discourses on Art established the theoretical framework for British academic art.
