## 개요

하라 겐야 is a 일본ese graphic designer who has served as art director of MUJI since 2002. His design philosophy emphasizes emptiness (ku) and whiteness as positive aesthetic values. His books Designing Design and White explore the relationship between 일본ese aesthetics and modern design.
