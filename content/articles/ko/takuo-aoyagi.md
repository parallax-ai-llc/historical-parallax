## 요약

Takuo Aoyagi invented pulse oximetry in 1974, a non-invasive method to measure blood oxygen saturation. The pulse oximeter became one of the most important vital signs monitors in medicine, essential in operating rooms, ICUs, and during the COVID-19 pandemic. His invention has saved millions of lives by enabling early detection of dangerous drops in oxygen.
