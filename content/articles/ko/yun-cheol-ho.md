## 요약

Yun Cheol-ho was a Korean ceramic engineer who advanced Korea's ceramics industry from traditional pottery to advanced technical ceramics. His research on electronic ceramics and piezoelectric materials contributed to Korea's electronics industry, supplying key components for semiconductor and display manufacturing.
