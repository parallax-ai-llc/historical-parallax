## 개요

Hugo Duminil-Copin is a French mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2022 for solving longstanding problems in the probabilistic theory of phase transitions in statistical physics. His work on percolation theory and the Ising model has established rigorous mathematical foundations for understanding phase transitions.
