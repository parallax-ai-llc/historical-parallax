## 개요

이임학 was a Korean physicist who made important contributions to nuclear physics and helped establish 대한민국's nuclear research infrastructure. He trained at institutions in 유럽 and returned to build Korea's physics community. His work on nuclear reactions contributed to the country's nuclear energy program.
