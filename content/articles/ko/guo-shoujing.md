## 개요

Guo Shoujing was a Yuan Dynasty astronomer and engineer who created the Shoushi calendar, the most accurate calendar in the world at that time. He calculated the length of the year to within 26 seconds of the modern value. He also designed water management systems and built 27 astronomical instruments including an advanced gnomon for measuring solar altitude.
