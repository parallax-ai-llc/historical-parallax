## 개요

Jarmila Kratochvílová set the 800m world record of 1:53.28 in 1983, which has stood for over 40 years — the longest-standing world record in running.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1983 | Set 800m world record of 1:53.28 |
| 1983 | Won World Championship gold in 400m and 800m |

## Athletic Profile

- **Events**: Best known for middle-distance racing, especially 800m, while also competing at elite level in the 400m.
- **Style**: Often described in summaries as an exceptionally strong, endurance-oriented runner with the ability to sustain high pace through the final stages of an 800m.

## Major Achievements

- 1983 World Championships: won gold medals in both the **400m** and **800m**, a rare double at the top level.
- 800m world record: 1:53.28 (set in 1983), one of the most durable marks in track and field.

## Record Longevity and Debate

Because the 800m record has stood through multiple eras of training and anti-doping enforcement, it is frequently discussed in historical reviews of athletics:

- Some commentary links record-era performances to broader concerns about state-sponsored doping in Cold War sport.
- Public sources also note that definitive proof for specific allegations can be difficult to establish decades later; the record itself remains officially recognized.

## 유산

Kratochvílová is remembered for combining sprint speed with middle-distance strength and for setting a benchmark that has shaped how later generations evaluate women's 800m performance.
