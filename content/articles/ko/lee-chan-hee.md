## 개요

이찬희 was a Korean marine biologist who pioneered seaweed aquaculture research in 대한민국. His work on laver (gim) and kelp cultivation techniques helped establish Korea as one of the world's leading seaweed producers. He developed improved cultivation methods that increased yields and quality of edible seaweed.
