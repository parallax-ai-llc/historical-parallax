## 개요

Edgar F. Codd 영국의-American 컴퓨터 과학자 who invented the relational model for database management, which ~이 되었다 basis for relational databases used worldwide. His 1970 paper 'A Relational Model of Data for Large Shared Data Banks' transformed how data is stored and accessed. SQL databases, which implement his model, are the backbone of most enterprise data management. He received the Turing Award in 1981.
