## 개요

키즈메키아 코벳 was a lead researcher at the NIH's Vaccine Research Center who played a pivotal role in developing the Moderna COVID-19 vaccine. Her expertise on coronaviruses, developed over six years of research, enabled the rapid design of the mRNA-1273 vaccine. She is one of the most prominent young scientists to emerge from the pandemic response.
