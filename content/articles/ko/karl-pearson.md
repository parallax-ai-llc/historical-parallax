## 개요

카를 피어슨 founded modern mathematical statistics, establishing the first university statistics department and developing numerous statistical tools still in daily use. He created the Pearson correlation coefficient, the chi-squared test, the concept of standard deviation, and principal component analysis. He was a towering figure in making statistics a rigorous scientific discipline.
