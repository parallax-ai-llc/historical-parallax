## 개요

George Papanicolaou developed the Pap smear test (Papanicolaou test) for 초기의 detection of cervical cancer, one of the most 성공적인 cancer screening methods in history. His technique of examining exfoliated cells reduced cervical cancer mortality by over 70%. He is considered the father of cytopathology.
