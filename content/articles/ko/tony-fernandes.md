## 요약

Tony Fernandes transformed AirAsia from a failing government-owned airline into Asia's largest low-cost carrier. He purchased AirAsia for 1 Malaysian ringgit (about $0.26) in 2001 and built it into a regional aviation powerhouse with the motto 'Now Everyone Can Fly.' AirAsia expanded to multiple countries across Southeast Asia and pioneered budget air travel in the region. He also owns QPR football club and has interests in Formula One.
