## 요약

Sim Wong Hoo founded Creative Technology, a Singaporean company that created the Sound Blaster sound card, which became the de facto standard for PC audio in the 1990s. Creative Technology was at the forefront of PC multimedia and later pioneered portable digital audio players with the Nomad Jukebox (before the iPod). He also developed the ZEN media player. His innovation helped establish Singapore as a technology hub.
