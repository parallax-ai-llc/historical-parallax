## 개요

Ge Hong was an Eastern Jin Dynasty alchemist and 의사 whose Baopuzi documented 초기의 chemical experiments, medicinal treatments, and alchemical processes. He described the use of artemisinin-containing herbs for malaria treatment, which inspired Tu Youyou's 노벨상-winning discovery 1,700 년 후. He also documented 초기의 smallpox treatment methods.
