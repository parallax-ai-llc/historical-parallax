## 개요
John Reginald Halliday Christie murdered at least eight people at 10 Rillington Place in 런던 between 1943 and 1953. His case is infamous because innocent Timothy Evans was wrongfully executed for one of Christie's murders, contributing to the abolition of the 사형 in the UK.

## 초기 생애
Born April 8, 1899, in Halifax, Yorkshire. He served in WWI and was gassed. He had a criminal record for theft and assault. He served as a special constable during WWII. [^1]

## 범죄 경력
Christie murdered at least six women and two babies at 10 Rillington Place. He gassed victims with coal gas and strangled them. He murdered his upstairs neighbors Beryl and baby Geraldine Evans in 1949, for which innocent Timothy Evans was hanged. He killed his wife Ethel in 1952. [^2]

## 체포 / 몰락
A new tenant discovered three bodies in a papered-over alcove in 1953. More remains were found under floorboards and in the garden. Christie was arrested, confessed, and hanged at Pentonville Prison on July 15, 1953. Evans received a posthumous pardon in 1966. [^3]
