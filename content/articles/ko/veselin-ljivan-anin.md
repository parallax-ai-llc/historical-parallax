## 요약

Serbian military officer convicted of aiding and abetting torture for his role in the Vukovar hospital massacre of 1991. He blocked Red Cross access to prisoners who were then executed at Ovčara farm.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1991 | Participated in siege of Vukovar |
| 1991 | Blocked Red Cross from accessing prisoners at Vukovar hospital |
| 2003 | Surrendered to ICTY |
| 2007 | Convicted and sentenced to 17 years, reduced to 10 on appeal |
