## 개요

시모무라 고타로 was a 일본ese seismologist who contributed to the development of 일본's earthquake monitoring network in the early 20th century. His work on seismic instrumentation and earthquake data collection helped establish the infrastructure that would eventually make 일본's earthquake early warning system the most advanced in the world.
