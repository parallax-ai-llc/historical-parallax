## 요약

Yinka Shonibare CBE is a Nigerian-British artist known for his exploration of cultural identity globalization and post-colonialism. His signature use of Dutch wax print fabrics in his headless mannequin sculptures examines the tangled history of trade and empire. His Nelson Ship in a Bottle was displayed on the Fourth Plinth in Trafalgar Square.
