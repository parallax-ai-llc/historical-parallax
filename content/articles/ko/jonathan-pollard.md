## 개요

American naval intelligence analyst who spied for 이스라엘, passing enormous quantities of classified documents. He is the only American to receive a life sentence for spying for an ally. Paroled in 2015; moved to 이스라엘 in 2020.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1984 | Began passing classified documents to 이스라엘 |
| 1985 | Arrested outside 이스라엘i embassy |
| 1987 | Sentenced to life in prison |
| 2015 | Paroled |
| 2020 | Moved to 이스라엘 |
