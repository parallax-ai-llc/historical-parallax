## 요약

Samantha Cristoforetti is an Italian astronaut who holds the record for the longest uninterrupted spaceflight by a European astronaut (199 days). She became the first Italian woman in space in 2014 and served as commander of the ISS in 2022. She is also famous for making the first espresso in space using a specially designed ISS capsule machine.
