## 개요

조지프 브라마 was a prolific inventor who developed the hydraulic press, an improved flush toilet, the beer engine (beer pump), and a famously unpickable lock. His hydraulic press, based on Pascal's principle, was fundamental to industrial manufacturing. He was one of the most important inventors of the 산업 혁명.
