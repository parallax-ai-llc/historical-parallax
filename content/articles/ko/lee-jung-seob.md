## 개요

이중섭 was a Korean painter known for his passionate paintings of bulls, children, and family scenes, as well as his unique silver foil drawings. Separated from his 일본ese wife and children by the 한국전쟁, his art expresses intense longing and emotional power. He died in poverty at 40 but is now one of Korea's most beloved artists.
