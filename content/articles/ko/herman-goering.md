## 개요
Hermann Wilhelm Göring was a German political and military leader who served as commander of the Luftwaffe and Hitler's designated successor. He was convicted of war crimes and crimes against humanity at the Nuremberg Trials. He committed suicide by cyanide the night before his scheduled execution.
