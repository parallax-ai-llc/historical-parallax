## 요약

Xavier Niel is a French billionaire who founded Iliad SA and its subsidiary Free, which disrupted the French telecommunications market with dramatically lower prices. Free's mobile service launched in 2012 at a fraction of competitors' prices, forcing the entire French telecom industry to lower costs. He co-founded Station F, the world's largest startup campus in Paris. He also co-owns Le Monde newspaper.
