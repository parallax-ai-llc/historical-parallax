## Disambiguation / Data Note

Some datasets include an entry titled "이건희". In practice, the biographical details (1942–2020) correspond to **이건희 (이건희)**, the long-time chairman of Samsung, and many summaries treat this as a duplicate or mis-specified record rather than a distinct historical figure.

## High-level Context (이건희)

- Business leader associated with the rise of Samsung from a value-oriented electronics maker into a premium global brand.
- Known for emphasizing quality, design, and international competitiveness during Samsung's global expansion.
- His tenure is frequently discussed in relation to 대한민국's 재벌-led industrial development and the globalization of Korean manufacturing.

## Themes Commonly Cited in Accounts

- **"Quality-first" management:** repeated internal messaging that product quality and customer perception were strategic priorities.
- **Global talent and localization:** recruitment of international expertise and pushing Samsung's presence in major overseas markets.
- **Brand repositioning:** moving from OEM-style manufacturing toward higher-margin branded consumer electronics.
- **Organizational change:** periodic restructuring and investment in R&D to support long-term competitiveness.
