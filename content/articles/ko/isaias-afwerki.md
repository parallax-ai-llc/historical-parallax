## 개요

Eritrean president since 1993 who has created one of the world's most repressive states. No elections, no free press, and indefinite military conscription have driven hundreds of thousands to flee. Known as 'Africa's North Korea.'

| 1998 | Launched border war with Ethiopia |
| 2001 | Imprisoned journalists and dissidents; no elections since |
| 2018 | Signed peace deal with Ethiopia |
