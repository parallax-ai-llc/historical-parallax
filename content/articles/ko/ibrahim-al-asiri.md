## 개요

Saudi bomb-maker for AQAP who designed innovative explosive devices including underwear bombs and printer cartridge bombs. He is considered one of the most skilled terrorist bomb-makers in history.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 2009 | Designed underwear bomb used in Christmas Day plot |
| 2010 | Created printer cartridge bombs shipped on cargo planes |
| 2017 | Reported killed in US operation, unconfirmed |
