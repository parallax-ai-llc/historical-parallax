## 개요

대한민국 experienced massive 암호화폐 fraud including the Terra/Luna collapse ($40B losses), Bithumb embezzlement, and numerous Ponzi schemes targeting retail investors.

## 배경

- 대한민국 has had high retail participation in 암호화폐 markets relative to many countries.
- The combination of rapid price cycles, online promotion, and complex products increased exposure to scams and mismanagement.
- Fraud cases ranged from traditional Ponzi structures to exchange-related misconduct and market manipulation allegations.

## Common fraud patterns (high level)

- Ponzi and pyramid schemes promising high, stable returns.
- Misuse of customer funds, insider trading allegations, or opaque token issuance practices.
- Social engineering tactics, including influencer marketing, chat-room hype, and fabricated partnerships.

## Regulatory and enforcement response

- Investigations involved financial regulators and law enforcement, sometimes with cross-border dimensions.
- Policy debates focused on consumer protection, exchange licensing, and transparency requirements.
- High-profile cases increased pressure to clarify the legal status of tokens and strengthen oversight.

## Societal impact

- Retail losses contributed to public anger and calls for accountability.
- The scandals influenced perceptions of the broader fintech and 블록체인 sector in Korea.
- They also spurred interest in risk disclosure, custody practices, and auditing standards.
