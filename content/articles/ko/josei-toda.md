## 개요

도다 조세이 was the second president of Soka Gakkai who transformed it from a small educational organization into a major religious movement in post-war 일본. He was imprisoned during 제2차 세계대전 for refusing to accept Shinto as a state religion. During imprisonment, he experienced an awakening through chanting and studying the Lotus Sutra. After the war, he rebuilt Soka Gakkai into a mass movement. He made a declaration calling for the abolition of nuclear weapons.
