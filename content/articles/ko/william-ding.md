## 요약

William Ding (Ding Lei) founded NetEase in 1997, building it into one of China's largest internet and gaming companies. NetEase operates major online games, music streaming, and e-commerce platforms. Its gaming division rivals Tencent in China and has expanded globally through partnerships with Blizzard Entertainment. He has been one of China's wealthiest individuals through his NetEase stake.
