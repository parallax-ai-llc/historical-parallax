## 요약

Wang Zhenyi was a Chinese astronomer, mathematician, and poet of the Qing Dynasty who made important contributions to astronomy and mathematics despite living only 29 years. She wrote about lunar and solar eclipses, created models to demonstrate eclipse mechanics, and wrote on spherical trigonometry. She also advocated for women's education.
