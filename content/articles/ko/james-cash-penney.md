## 개요

James Cash Penney founded the J.C. Penney department store chain, which grew into one of America's largest retailers with over 1,600 stores. He started with a single store called The Golden Rule in Kemmerer, Wyoming in 1902. His philosophy of fair dealing and profit sharing with employees was innovative for its time. Despite losing his fortune in the 1929 crash, he rebuilt and continued expanding the chain.
