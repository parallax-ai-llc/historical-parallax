## 요약
Thug Behram, also known as Buhram, was an Indian serial killer associated with the Thuggee cult in central India. He is often cited as the world's most prolific serial killer, with claims of 931 murders by strangulation with a ceremonial cloth (rumal). Modern historians dispute the numbers, but he was convicted and hanged around 1840.
