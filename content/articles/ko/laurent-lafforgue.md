## 개요

로랑 라포르그 is a French mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2002 for his proof of the Langlands conjecture for function fields. His work on the Langlands program, which connects number theory with representation theory and geometry, is considered one of the grand unifying programs in mathematics.
