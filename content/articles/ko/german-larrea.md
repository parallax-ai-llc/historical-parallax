## 개요

Germán Larrea Mota-Velasco is a Mexican mining 억만장자 and CEO of Grupo México, 세계's third-largest copper 프로듀서. He inherited the mining business from his father and expanded it into one of the most profitable mining operations globally. Grupo México also operates the largest railroad in Mexico and has interests in infrastructure. He is one of the wealthiest people in Latin America.
