## 개요

Giulio Natta 이탈리아의 화학자 who shared the 1963 노벨상 in Chemistry with Karl Ziegler for their discoveries in polymer chemistry. He developed stereoregular polymers (isotactic polypropylene), enabling precise control of polymer structure. His work was fundamental to the 현대의 plastics and synthetic materials industry.
