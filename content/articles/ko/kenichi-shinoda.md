시노다 겐이치, also known as Shinobu Tsukasa, is the sixth and current kumicho (supreme godfather) of the Yamaguchi-gumi, 일본's largest yakuza organization with approximately 8,200 members. He was released from prison in 2011 after serving six years for firearms possession.
