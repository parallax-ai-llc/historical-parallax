## 요약
Salvador 'Sal' Magluta was a Cuban-American drug lord who, with partner Willy Falcon, ran one of the largest cocaine trafficking operations in 1980s Miami. After initially being acquitted (the jury was bribed), he was convicted of money laundering and witness tampering and sentenced to 195 years.
