## 개요

고이즈미 군지 was a 일본ese-British judo master who founded the Budokwai in 런던 (1918), the first judo club in 유럽. Beyond judo teaching, he systematically analyzed the biomechanics and physics of throwing techniques, publishing scientific analyses of judo that contributed to sports science.
