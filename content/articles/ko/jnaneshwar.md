## 개요

Jnaneshwar (Dnyaneshwar) was a Marathi saint and philosopher who wrote the Jnaneshwari, a commentary on the Bhagavad Gita in Marathi, at the age of 15. This work is considered one of the greatest literary and philosophical works in Marathi literature. He made the teachings of the Gita accessible to common people who did not know Sanskrit. He entered samadhi (deep meditation leading to death) at the age of 21.
