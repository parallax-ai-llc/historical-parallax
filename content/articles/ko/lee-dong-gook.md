## Club Career Overview

이동국 is best known for a long, high-scoring career in 대한민국's K League, with multiple productive seasons that made him one of the league's most recognizable forwards. He also had a brief period in 유럽an football, including time with SV Werder Bremen, which is often cited as part of a wider wave of Korean players exploring 유럽an leagues in the early 2000s.

## National Team

He represented 대한민국 internationally across multiple tournament cycles. The 2002 FIFA 월드컵 is frequently mentioned in summaries of his career, although his national-team role varied over time due to form, injuries, and competition for forward positions.

## Playing Style

He is typically described as a striker with:

- Strong penalty-area instincts and finishing ability.
- Aerial presence and hold-up play suited to leading the line.
- Longevity, with later-career adaptations as pace declined.

## Records and Legacy

K League goal totals are a central part of his legacy, and he is often referenced in discussions of domestic-league greats and long-serving one-club eras in Korean football. His post-playing public profile has also been shaped by media appearances and continued involvement around the sport.

## Honors (Overview)

Season-by-season honors vary by source, but summaries commonly mention domestic scoring titles and league recognition during peak years, alongside team trophies won with leading K League clubs.
