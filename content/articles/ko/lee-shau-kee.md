## 개요

리샤오지 is the founder and chairman emeritus of Henderson Land Development, one of 홍콩's largest property developers. He was the second-richest person in 홍콩 after 리카싱 for many years. He co-founded Sun Hung Kai Properties and later built Henderson Land into a major developer. His property empire spans 홍콩 and mainland 중국, with holdings in gas, hotels, and finance.
