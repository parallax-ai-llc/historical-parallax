## 개요

Jean-Baptiste Joseph Delambre was one of the astronomers who measured the meridian arc from Dunkirk to Barcelona, which was used to define the meter — the fundamental unit of the metric system. He also compiled comprehensive astronomical tables and wrote a monumental six-volume history of astronomy. He was one of the creators of the metric system.
