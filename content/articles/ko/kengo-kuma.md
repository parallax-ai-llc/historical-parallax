## 개요

구마 겐고 is a 일본ese architect known for his use of natural materials, particularly wood, to create buildings that harmonize with their environments. His designs seek to dissolve the boundary between architecture and nature. His 일본 National Stadium for the 2020 도쿄 Olympics showcases his philosophy of organic, sustainable architecture.
