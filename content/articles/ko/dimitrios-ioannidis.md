## 배경 and Background

- **Dimitrios Ioannidis (1923–2010)** was a senior Greek Army officer associated with the **Greek military dictatorship (1967–1974)**.
- He is widely described as the **de facto leader** of Greece ~ 동안 final phase of the dictatorship, ~ 후에 removing the earlier junta leadership.

## Role in the Greek Junta

## Cyprus and the 1974 Crisis

- In **July 1974**, the Athens-backed coup against **President Makarios** in Cyprus helped trigger the subsequent **Turkish military intervention** on the island.
- The crisis contributed directly to the **collapse of the dictatorship** and Greece’s transition back to civilian government.
