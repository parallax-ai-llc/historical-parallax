## 요약

Rudolf Steiner was an Austrian philosopher, social reformer, and esotericist who founded Anthroposophy, a spiritual movement integrating science and mysticism. He established Waldorf education, biodynamic agriculture, and anthroposophical medicine. He was the head of the German section of the Theosophical Society before breaking away to found the Anthroposophical Society. His ideas have influenced education, agriculture, medicine, and the arts worldwide.
