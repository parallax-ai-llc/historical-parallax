## 개요

Fazang 중국의 Buddhist 수도승 who is considered the third and most 중요한 patriarch of the Huayan (Flower Garland) school. He developed the doctrine of the mutual interpenetration of all phenomena — that every element of reality contains and reflects all others. He famously used a hall of mirrors and a golden lion to illustrate his 철학 for 황후 Wu Zetian. His system represents one of the most sophisticated philosophical achievements in Buddhist history.
