## 개요

Imamura Akitsune was a Japanese seismologist who predicted the Great Kanto Earthquake of 1923 years in advance. He warned that Tokyo was overdue for a major earthquake and advocated for earthquake preparedness measures. Though initially ridiculed, his warning proved tragically prescient. He advanced earthquake prediction research in Japan.
