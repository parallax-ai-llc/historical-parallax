## 개요

조제프 모니에 patented reinforced concrete in 1867, embedding iron mesh within concrete to create a material of extraordinary strength and versatility. Although not the sole inventor, his systematic patenting and promotion made reinforced concrete commercially viable. This material became the foundation of modern construction worldwide.
