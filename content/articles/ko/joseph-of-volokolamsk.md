## 개요

볼로콜람스크의 요시프 was a 러시아n Orthodox monk and abbot who led the Possessor movement, defending the right of monasteries to own land and property. He argued that wealthy monasteries could better serve the poor, educate clergy, and support the church. His conflict with Nil Sorsky's Non-Possessors shaped 러시아n church history. He also wrote against the Judaizers heresy and supported the alliance between the 러시아n Church and the Muscovite state.
