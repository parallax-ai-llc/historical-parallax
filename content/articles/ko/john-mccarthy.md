## 개요

존 매카시 was an American computer scientist who coined the term '인공지능' and was a pioneer of AI research. He invented the Lisp programming language, one of the earliest high-level languages still in use. He also developed time-sharing computing and made fundamental contributions to the theory of computation. McCarthy received the Turing Award in 1971.
