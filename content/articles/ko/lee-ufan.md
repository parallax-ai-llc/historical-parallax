## 개요

이우환 is a Korean-일본ese artist and philosopher who is a leading figure of the Mono-ha (School of Things) movement. His art systematically explores the relationship between materials, space, and human perception, applying philosophical analysis that parallels scientific inquiry into the nature of matter and consciousness.
