## 개요

Henry J. Heinz founded the H.J. Heinz Company, which became one of the world's leading food processing companies. He popularized ketchup and introduced the '57 Varieties' slogan that became iconic in American marketing. Heinz was a pioneer in food safety, advocating for the Pure Food and Drug Act of 1906. He was also noted for progressive labor practices and philanthropy in Pittsburgh.
