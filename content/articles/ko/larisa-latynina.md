## 개요

라리사 라티니나 held the Olympic medal record (18) for 48 years until Phelps surpassed her in 2012.

## Early Life and Training

Latynina was born in Kherson (then in the Ukrainian SSR) and developed as a gymnast in the Soviet sports system.
Her rise coincided with the USSR’s increasing emphasis on Olympic success as a marker of national prestige.

## Olympic Achievements (High-Level)

She competed at three Summer Olympics (1956, 1960, 1964) and won medals across multiple artistic gymnastics events.
Her total of 18 Olympic medals (including 9 gold) stood as the all-time record for decades.

## Style and Competition Era

Latynina competed in an era when women’s artistic gymnastics balanced ballet-like presentation with emerging athletic difficulty.
She was known in historical accounts for consistency across team, all-around, and apparatus competitions.

## Later Career and Legacy

After retiring from competition, she remained involved in gymnastics, including work as a coach and within Soviet sports organizations.
Her medal record is frequently cited in comparisons across eras, especially in discussions of longevity and dominance at the Olympic level.
