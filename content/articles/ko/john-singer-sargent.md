## 개요

존 싱어 사전트 was the most sought-after portrait painter of his generation, celebrated for virtuosic brushwork. His controversial Portrait of Madame X is a masterpiece of modern portraiture. His later watercolors and 보스턴 Public Library murals reveal broader artistic vision.
