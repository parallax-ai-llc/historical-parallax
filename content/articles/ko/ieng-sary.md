## 개요

Cambodian politician and Khmer Rouge leader who served as foreign minister during the Cambodian genocide. He was charged with genocide and crimes against humanity by the ECCC but died during trial.

## 경력 연표

| 연도 | 사건 |
|------|-------|
| 1975 | Became foreign minister of Democratic Kampuchea |
| 1979 | Fled after Vietnamese invasion |
| 2007 | Arrested and charged with genocide |
| 2013 | Died during trial |
