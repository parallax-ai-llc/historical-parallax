## 요약

Watsuji Tetsuro was a Japanese philosopher who developed a cultural climatology linking geography and climate to cultural development. His 1935 work Fudo (Climate and Culture) systematically analyzed how different climate types shape human societies and their technological development, bridging natural and social sciences.
