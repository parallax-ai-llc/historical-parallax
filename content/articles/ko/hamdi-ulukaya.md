## 개요

Hamdi Ulukaya is the founder and CEO of Chobani, which became the best-selling Greek yogurt brand in the United States. A Turkish immigrant, he purchased a shuttered Kraft yogurt plant in 2005 and launched Chobani in 2007. Within five years, Chobani held over 20% of the U.S. yogurt market. He is known for his progressive employee policies, including giving workers equity stakes, and his advocacy for refugee employment.
