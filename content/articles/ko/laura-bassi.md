## 개요

라우라 바시 was an Italian physicist who became the first woman to earn a professorship in physics at a 유럽an university (University of Bologna, 1732). She was a leading Newtonian physicist in 이탈리아 and conducted experiments on electricity. She published 28 papers on physics and held a chair in experimental physics.
