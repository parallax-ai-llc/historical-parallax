## 개요

Francis de Sales was a 주교 of Geneva and a Doctor of 교회 who is one of the most beloved Catholic spiritual writers. His Introduction to the Devout Life and Treatise on the Love of God made Christian spirituality accessible to laypeople. He pioneered the idea that holiness was possible in 일상생활, not just in monasteries. He co-founded the Order of the Visitation of Holy Mary with Jane Frances de Chantal.
