## Summary

Manny Villar is a Filipino billionaire and the richest person in the Philippines. He built Vista Land & Lifescapes, the largest homebuilder in the Philippines, and expanded into malls, hotels, and retail. He also served as Senate President and ran for president in 2010. His AllDay Supermarket and Camella housing brands are among the most recognized consumer brands in the Philippines.
