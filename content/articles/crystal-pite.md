## Summary

Crystal Pite is a Canadian choreographer who is one of the most important dance-makers of the 21st century. Her works with Kidd Pivot and major ballet companies combine theatrical storytelling with innovative movement. Her piece Betroffenheit, created with Jonathon Young, was hailed as a masterwork.
