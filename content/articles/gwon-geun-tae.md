## Summary

Gwon Geun-tae was a Korean democracy activist who was imprisoned and tortured during military dictatorships. While primarily a political figure, his systematic documentation of state violence and his advocacy for human rights used evidence-based approaches to exposing injustice, demonstrating the application of analytical methods to social justice.
