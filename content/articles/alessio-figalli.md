## Summary

Alessio Figalli is an Italian mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2018 for his contributions to the theory of optimal transport. His work has connected optimal transport with partial differential equations, calculus of variations, and geometric analysis. He is a professor at ETH Zurich.
