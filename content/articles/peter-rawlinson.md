## Summary

Peter Rawlinson is the CEO of Lucid Motors, the luxury electric vehicle maker. He previously served as chief engineer of the Tesla Model S, designing one of the most acclaimed electric vehicles ever made. At Lucid, he developed the Lucid Air, which set the record as the longest-range electric vehicle (520 miles). Lucid went public via SPAC in 2021, backed by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund.
