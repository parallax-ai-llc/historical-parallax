## Summary

Alfonso Cuaron is a Mexican filmmaker whose visually stunning films span genres from fantasy to science fiction to memoir. His Roma, a deeply personal black-and-white film about 1970s Mexico City, won the Academy Award for Best Director. Gravity and Children of Men demonstrate his technical virtuosity.
