## Summary

Edvard Grieg was the leading Norwegian composer who established a distinctive Norwegian musical identity. His Piano Concerto in A minor, Peer Gynt suites, and Lyric Pieces for piano are among the most popular works in the classical repertoire. His music draws deeply on Norwegian folk melodies and harmonies.
