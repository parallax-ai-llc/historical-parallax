## Summary

Abu Kamil Shuja ibn Aslam was an Egyptian mathematician who advanced algebra beyond al-Khwarizmi's work, developing algebraic solutions to more complex problems including systems of equations and irrational numbers. His Kitab fi al-jabr wa al-muqabala influenced Leonardo of Pisa (Fibonacci) and was foundational for European algebra.
