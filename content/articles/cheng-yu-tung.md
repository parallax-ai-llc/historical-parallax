## Summary

Cheng Yu-tung built Chow Tai Fook Jewellery into one of the world's largest jewellery retailers and controlled New World Development, a major Hong Kong conglomerate. Starting as an apprentice in a Macau jewellery shop, he married the owner's daughter and expanded the business into a global empire. He was one of Hong Kong's most respected tycoons and his family remains among the wealthiest in Asia.
