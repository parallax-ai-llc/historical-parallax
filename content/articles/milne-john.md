## Summary

John Milne was a British geologist and mining engineer who is considered the father of modern seismology. Working in Japan from 1876 to 1895, he invented the horizontal pendulum seismograph and established the first worldwide seismograph network. His work in Japan created the scientific foundation for earthquake study and monitoring.
