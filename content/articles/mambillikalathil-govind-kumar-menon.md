## Summary

M. G. K. Menon was a leading Indian physicist who made important contributions to cosmic ray and high-energy physics. He led India's scientific infrastructure development as director of the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research and served as scientific advisor to the Prime Minister. He was instrumental in developing India's space, nuclear, and defense research programs.
