## Summary

Stelios Haji-Ioannou is the founder of easyJet, the British low-cost airline that revolutionized European air travel. He launched easyJet in 1995 with two leased Boeing 737s flying from London Luton. The 'easy' brand expanded into car rental, hotels, and other services. EasyJet became Europe's largest low-cost carrier by passengers. His family's wealth originally came from Stelmar Shipping, founded by his father.
