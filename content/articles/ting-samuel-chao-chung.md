## Summary

Samuel C.C. Ting is a Chinese-American physicist who shared the 1976 Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the J/psi particle (J particle), which provided evidence for the charm quark. His discovery at Brookhaven National Laboratory confirmed the existence of a new quark flavor, extending the Standard Model. He leads the Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer experiment on the ISS.
