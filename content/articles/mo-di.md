## Summary

Mo Di (Mozi) was an ancient Chinese philosopher, engineer, and scientist who founded the Mohist school. His followers developed formal logic, optics, mechanics, and geometry. The Mohist Canon contains descriptions of the camera obscura, principles of mechanics, and logical reasoning that paralleled Greek developments. He also designed defensive military siege equipment.
