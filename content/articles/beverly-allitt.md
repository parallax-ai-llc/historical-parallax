## Summary
Beverly Gail Allitt is a British serial killer who attacked 13 children at Grantham Hospital in 1991, killing four. She suffered from Munchausen syndrome by proxy. She was convicted of four murders, three attempted murders, and six counts of grievous bodily harm.
