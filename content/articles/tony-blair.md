## Summary

Anthony Charles Lynton Blair (born May 6, 1953) served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom (1997-2007) and Leader of the Labour Party (1994-2007). Architect of "New Labour," Blair modernized the left-wing party and won three consecutive elections. However, his support for the Iraq War destroyed his reputation and left a deeply controversial legacy.

**The Decision**:
- Supported Bush's Iraq invasion
- Claimed WMD threat
- Dodgy dossier (45-minute claim)
- "I believe Saddam Hussein's regime is a threat"

**Public Opposition**:
- Massive protests (1 million+ in London)

- 179 British soldiers killed
