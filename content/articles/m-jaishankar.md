## Summary

Indian serial killer and rapist who murdered at least 30 women across multiple Indian states. He was a habitual offender who repeatedly escaped from prison. Shot dead in an encounter with police in Tirupati.

A recurring theme in coverage is that he escaped or absconded from custody more than once. In criminal-justice discussions, repeated escapes typically raise questions about:
