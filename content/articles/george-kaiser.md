## Summary

George Kaiser is an American billionaire businessman and philanthropist who built his fortune through BOK Financial Corporation and oil and gas investments. He is the chairman of BOK Financial, one of the largest banking companies in the Great Plains region. He signed the Giving Pledge and has donated extensively to early childhood education through the George Kaiser Family Foundation. He funded Tulsa's Gathering Place, one of the most acclaimed public parks in America.
