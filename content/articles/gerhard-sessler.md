## Summary

Gerhard Sessler co-invented the electret microphone with James West in 1962 and also invented the silicon microphone (MEMS microphone) used in modern smartphones. His electret microphone is the most widely used microphone type in the world. He holds over 200 patents in acoustics and sensor technology.
