## Summary

Benjamin Britten was the most important British composer of the 20th century. His operas Peter Grimes, Billy Budd, and The Turn of the Screw revitalized English opera. The War Requiem, premiered at the rebuilt Coventry Cathedral, is one of the great anti-war works. He founded the Aldeburgh Festival.
