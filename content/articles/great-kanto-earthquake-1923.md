## Summary

The 1923 Great Kant≈ç Earthquake devastated Tokyo and Yokohama, killing over 105,000. The magnitude 7.9 quake and subsequent fires destroyed most of Tokyo. Tragically, false rumors led to mass violence against Korean residents.
