## Summary

Darshan Ranganathan was an Indian organic chemist known for her work on supramolecular chemistry and the chemical simulation of key biological processes. She developed novel methods for cyclophane and functionalized amino acid synthesis. She received the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize, India's highest science award.
