## Summary

Mikaela Shiffrin holds the all-time World Cup wins record. She won Olympic slalom gold at 18.

- Born in the United States and raised in a family involved in skiing; she began skiing at a young age.

### Competitive Strengths

- Known primarily for elite technical skiing, especially in slalom and giant slalom.
- Also competed across disciplines in World Cup events, including speed events in some seasons.

### Major Achievements (High-Level)

- Multiple-time overall World Cup champion (the overall title reflects season-long performance).
- Won World Championship medals in addition to Olympic medals.
