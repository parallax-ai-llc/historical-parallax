## Summary

Ku Cha-kyung was the honorary chairman of LG Group and the second-generation leader of the conglomerate. He led LG's diversification from chemicals into electronics, building GoldStar (later LG Electronics) into a major global brand. Under his leadership, LG entered telecommunications, home appliances, and displays. He guided LG's transformation from a domestic Korean company into a global technology conglomerate.
