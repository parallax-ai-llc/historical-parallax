## Summary

Ha Bu-yeong was a pioneering Korean mathematician who helped establish modern mathematics education in South Korea. He contributed to algebra and number theory research while building the institutional foundations for Korean mathematical scholarship. He mentored many of Korea's first generation of professional mathematicians.
