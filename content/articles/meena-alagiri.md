## Summary

Meena Alagiri is an Indian geneticist who has contributed to cancer genetics research in India. Her work on hereditary cancer syndromes and genetic counseling has helped establish genetic testing services in Indian hospitals and improved early detection of familial cancers across the subcontinent.
