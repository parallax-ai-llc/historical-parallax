## Summary

The 5th Dalai Lama, Ngawang Lobsang Gyatso, known as the 'Great Fifth,' unified Tibet under his rule and established the Dalai Lama as both spiritual and temporal leader of Tibet. He built the Potala Palace in Lhasa and oversaw a golden age of Tibetan culture and learning. He was the first Dalai Lama to exercise effective political power over all of Tibet. His death was concealed for 15 years by his regent.
