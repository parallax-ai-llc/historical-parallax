## Summary

Surdas was a blind Hindu devotional poet and musician who composed the Sursagar, a collection of over 100,000 devotional songs celebrating the life and love of Lord Krishna. His descriptions of Krishna's childhood and his love for Radha are considered among the most beautiful devotional poetry in Hindi literature. He was a disciple of Vallabhacharya and is one of the most important poets of the Krishna bhakti tradition.
