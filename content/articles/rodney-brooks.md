## Summary

Rodney Brooks revolutionized robotics with his behavior-based approach, arguing that robots should interact with the real world rather than build internal models. He co-founded iRobot (maker of the Roomba vacuum) and Rethink Robotics. As director of MIT's Computer Science and AI Lab, he influenced a generation of robotics researchers.
