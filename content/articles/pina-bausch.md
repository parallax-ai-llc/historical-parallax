## Summary

Pina Bausch was a German dancer and choreographer who created Tanztheater (dance theater), revolutionizing contemporary dance. Her works with the Tanztheater Wuppertal combine dance, theater, and spoken word to explore human emotions and relationships. Cafe Muller and The Rite of Spring are among her most celebrated works.
