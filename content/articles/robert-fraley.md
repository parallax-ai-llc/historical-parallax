## Summary

Robert Fraley is an American biochemist who led the development of the first genetically modified crops at Monsanto. He developed the techniques for inserting genes into plant cells, enabling the creation of Roundup Ready soybeans and Bt corn. He shared the 2013 World Food Prize for his contributions to agricultural biotechnology.
