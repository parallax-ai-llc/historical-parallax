## Summary

Huh Chang-soo served as chairman of GS Group and chairman of the Federation of Korean Industries. GS Group was separated from LG Group in 2005 and operates in energy, retail, construction, and logistics. GS Caltex is South Korea's second-largest oil refiner. He also played a significant role in Korean business diplomacy and industry leadership.
