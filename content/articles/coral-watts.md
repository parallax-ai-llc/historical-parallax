## Summary
Carl Eugene Watts, known as 'Coral,' was an American serial killer estimated to have murdered between 14 and 100 women across Michigan and Texas between 1974 and 1982. He was called 'The Sunday Morning Slasher.' He received immunity for many murders in a plea deal that was later overturned.
