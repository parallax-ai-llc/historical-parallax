## Summary

Nicolas Hayek saved the Swiss watch industry from extinction by creating the Swatch Group, the world's largest watchmaking conglomerate. In the 1980s, Japanese quartz watches threatened to destroy Swiss watchmaking. Hayek led the merger of ASUAG and SSIH and launched the Swatch brand, revitalizing the industry. Under his leadership, the Swatch Group encompassed brands including Omega, Longines, Tissot, and Breguet.
