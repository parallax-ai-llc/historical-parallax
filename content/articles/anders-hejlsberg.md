## Summary

Anders Hejlsberg is a Danish software engineer known for creating Turbo Pascal, leading the design of Delphi, and being the lead architect of C# at Microsoft. He later created TypeScript, which has become one of the most popular programming languages for large-scale web applications. His contributions span decades of programming language design and development.
