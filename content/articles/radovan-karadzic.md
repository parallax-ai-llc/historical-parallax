## Summary
Radovan Karadžić was the first President of Republika Srpska during the Bosnian War. He was convicted of genocide, crimes against humanity, and war crimes by the ICTY. He was sentenced to life imprisonment in 2019. He was a fugitive for 13 years, hiding as an alternative medicine practitioner.
