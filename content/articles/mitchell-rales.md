## Summary

Mitchell Rales co-founded Danaher Corporation with his brother Steven in 1984, building it into one of the world's most successful diversified industrial conglomerates. Danaher's business system, inspired by Toyota's lean manufacturing, consistently improved the operations of acquired companies. The company grew from a small real estate investment trust to a $200+ billion market cap corporation. Rales is also the founder of the Glenstone Museum near Washington, D.C.
