## Summary

Richard Leakey made major fossil discoveries in East Africa that reshaped understanding of human evolution, including Homo habilis and Homo erectus specimens at Lake Turkana. He was also a passionate wildlife conservationist who led Kenya's campaign against elephant poaching. He served as head of the Kenya Wildlife Service and fought corruption in government.
