## Summary

Kim Jeong-ho created the Daedongyeojido (Great Map of the Eastern Land), the most detailed and accurate map of the Korean peninsula produced during the Joseon Dynasty. Completed in 1861, this remarkable woodblock-printed map consisted of 22 volumes covering the entire peninsula with unprecedented accuracy. His cartographic achievement rivaled contemporary Western mapping.
