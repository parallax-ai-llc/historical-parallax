## Summary

Tertullian was a prolific early Christian author from Carthage and the first major Christian writer in Latin. He coined many theological terms still used today, including 'Trinity'. His apologetic and polemical writings shaped Western Christian theology profoundly. Later in life he joined the Montanist movement.
