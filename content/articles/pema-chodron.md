## Summary

Pema Chödrön is an American Tibetan Buddhist nun, author, and teacher who is one of the most popular Buddhist teachers in the Western world. She was the first American woman to be ordained as a Buddhist nun in the Tibetan tradition. Her books, including When Things Fall Apart and The Places That Scare You, have sold millions of copies. She served as principal teacher at Gampo Abbey in Nova Scotia for decades.
