## Summary

James Hansen is one of the earliest and most prominent scientists to warn about human-caused global warming. His 1988 testimony before the U.S. Congress brought climate change to worldwide public attention. As director of NASA's Goddard Institute for Space Studies, he developed models predicting the consequences of increasing greenhouse gas emissions.
