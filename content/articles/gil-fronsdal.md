## Summary

Gil Fronsdal is an American Buddhist teacher and scholar who is the primary teacher at the Insight Meditation Center in Redwood City, California. He has a PhD in Buddhist Studies from Stanford and has translated the Dhammapada from Pali. He studied in the Zen and Theravada traditions, including years as a monk in Myanmar and Japan. His accessible and scholarly approach to Buddhist practice has made him one of the most respected meditation teachers in America.
