## Summary

Sachin Bansal co-founded Flipkart in 2007, India's largest e-commerce company. He and co-founder Binny Bansal (no relation) built Flipkart from a small online bookstore into a $21 billion company. After Walmart's $16 billion acquisition, Sachin exited and founded Navi Technologies, a fintech company focused on lending, insurance, and mutual funds. He was instrumental in proving that large-scale e-commerce could work in India.
