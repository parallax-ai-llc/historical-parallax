## Summary

Gregory of Nazianzus was a Cappadocian Father and Archbishop of Constantinople. He is known as 'The Theologian' in the Eastern Church for his contributions to Trinitarian doctrine. He presided over the First Council of Constantinople in 381. His theological orations are considered masterpieces of Christian rhetoric and poetry.
