## Summary

Cho Young-gul was a Korean textile engineer who helped modernize South Korea's textile industry during its rapid industrialization period. His technical improvements to synthetic fiber manufacturing contributed to Korea becoming one of the world's largest textile exporters in the 1970s and 1980s.
