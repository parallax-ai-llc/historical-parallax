## Summary

Abu Rayhan Biruni was a Persian polymath and scholar who made important contributions to mathematics, astronomy, physics, and geography during the Islamic Golden Age. He calculated the circumference of the Earth with remarkable accuracy using trigonometry. He studied Indian culture and science, writing the comprehensive 'Indica.' Biruni is considered one of the greatest scholars of the medieval Islamic world.
