## Summary
Abimael Guzmán Reynoso was the founder and leader of the Shining Path (Sendero Luminoso), a Maoist guerrilla organization in Peru. The Shining Path's insurgency from 1980 to 2000 resulted in approximately 70,000 deaths. Guzmán was captured in 1992 and sentenced to life. He died in prison in 2021.
