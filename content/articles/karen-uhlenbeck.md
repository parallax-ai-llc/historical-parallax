## Summary

Karen Uhlenbeck is an American mathematician who was the first woman to receive the Abel Prize (2019) for her pioneering achievements in geometric partial differential equations, gauge theory, and integrable systems. Her work has been foundational in the development of modern geometric analysis and mathematical physics.
