## Summary

Alejandro Santo Domingo is a Colombian billionaire and heir to the Santo Domingo Group fortune. His family sold Bavaria, Colombia's largest brewery, to SABMiller for $7.8 billion in 2005. He manages the family's investments through Quadrant Capital Advisors, with major stakes in AB InBev, Jet Smart airlines, and technology companies. He is one of the wealthiest people in Latin America.
