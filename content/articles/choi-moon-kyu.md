## Summary

Choi Moon-kyu is a Korean aerospace engineer who contributed to the development of liquid rocket engines for the KSLV (Korea Space Launch Vehicle). His propulsion research helped Korea develop indigenous rocket technology, culminating in the successful launch of the Nuri rocket (KSLV-II).
