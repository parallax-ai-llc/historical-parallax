## Summary

Ye Duzheng was a Chinese meteorologist who discovered the role of the Tibetan Plateau as a heat source driving the Asian monsoon. His work on atmospheric circulation over the Tibetan Plateau was fundamental to understanding Asian weather patterns. He co-authored pioneering studies on jet streams and atmospheric energy cycles.
