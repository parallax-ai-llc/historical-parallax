## Summary

Michael Houghton is a British virologist who shared the 2020 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for the discovery of the Hepatitis C virus. Working at Chiron Corporation, he and his team isolated the genetic sequence of the Hepatitis C virus in 1989, enabling the development of blood screening tests and antiviral treatments.
