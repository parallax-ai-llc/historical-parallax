## Summary

Garrett Camp co-founded Uber in 2009 and StumbleUpon, an early web discovery platform. He came up with the original concept for a ride-hailing app. StumbleUpon was acquired by eBay in 2007 and later shut down. He also co-founded Expa, a startup studio that has launched multiple companies. Camp is one of Canada's most successful tech entrepreneurs.
