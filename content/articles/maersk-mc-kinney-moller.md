## Summary

Mærsk Mc-Kinney Møller led A.P. Moller-Maersk for over 50 years, building it into the world's largest container shipping company. He expanded Maersk from a Danish shipping line into a global logistics and energy conglomerate. Maersk's distinctive blue containers are visible in every port in the world. He was Denmark's wealthiest person and one of the most respected business leaders in Scandinavian history.
