## Background

- Reported to have been active primarily in the Indian state of **Uttar Pradesh**.
- Frequently described in sources as an **organized-crime figure** involved in violent criminal networks.

## Notability and Allegations (High-level)

The public record around Bajrangi centers on repeated law-enforcement actions and long-running court cases.
According to Wikipedia and secondary reporting referenced there, he was:

- **Accused/implicated in multiple murder cases**, with proceedings extending over many years.
- Associated with broader **gang rivalries** that affected politics and local business in parts of Uttar Pradesh.

## Death

- Died on **2018-07-09** while incarcerated in **Baghpat jail**.
- Widely reported as a **targeted shooting inside the jail**, attributed to a rival.
- His death drew attention to **custodial security** and the persistence of gang conflicts even within prisons.
