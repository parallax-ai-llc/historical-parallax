## Summary

Nima Arkani-Hamed is a Canadian-American theoretical physicist of Iranian descent who has made significant contributions to particle physics and cosmology. He proposed the ADD model of large extra dimensions with Dimopoulos and Dvali. He is known for the amplituhedron concept and is a professor at the Institute for Advanced Study.
