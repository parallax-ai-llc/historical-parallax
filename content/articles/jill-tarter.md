## Summary

Jill Tarter is a pioneering astronomer who led the search for extraterrestrial intelligence (SETI) for over 35 years. She served as director of the Center for SETI Research at the SETI Institute and was the inspiration for Jodie Foster's character in the film Contact. She developed signal detection algorithms for identifying potential alien signals.
