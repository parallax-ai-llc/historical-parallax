## Summary

The Revolutions of 1848 were a wave of political upheavals across Europe, the 'Springtime of Nations.' Revolts in France, Germany, Austria, Hungary, and Italy demanded liberal reforms and national independence. Most were suppressed but planted seeds of future change.
