## Summary

Athanasius Kircher was a German Jesuit scholar and polymath often called the 'Master of a Hundred Arts.' He published over 40 works on subjects ranging from Egyptology to medicine, optics, and music theory. He was one of the first scholars to study Egyptian hieroglyphs. His Mundus Subterraneus explored geology and his museum in Rome was one of the finest in Europe.
