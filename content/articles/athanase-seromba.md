## Summary

Rwandan Catholic priest who ordered the bulldozing of his own church in Nyange with 2,000 Tutsi refugees sheltering inside during the 1994 genocide. He was convicted of genocide by the ICTR.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1994 | Ordered destruction of Nyange parish church killing 1,500 Tutsi |
| 2002 | Arrested in Italy where he served as a parish priest |
| 2006 | Convicted of genocide, sentence increased to life on appeal |
