## Summary

Mani Lal Bhaumik was a key contributor to the development of the excimer laser, which is widely used in LASIK eye surgery. Born in an impoverished village in Bengal, he studied under S.N. Bose and later joined UCLA where he worked on high-energy laser development. Excimer laser LASIK has corrected vision for millions of people worldwide.
