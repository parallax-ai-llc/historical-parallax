## Summary

Yuri Senkevich was a Russian explorer and TV host who participated in Thor Heyerdahl's Ra and Ra II expeditions. He hosted the travel show 'Travelers' Club' for 30 years.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|

| 1970 | Sailed on Ra II from Morocco to Barbados |

| 2003 | Died in Moscow |
