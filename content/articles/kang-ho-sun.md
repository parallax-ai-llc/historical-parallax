## Summary
Kang Ho-sun was a South Korean serial killer who murdered seven women in Gyeonggi Province between 2006 and 2008. He lured victims to karaoke bars and restaurants, then strangled them. He buried their bodies in rural areas. He was sentenced to death in 2009.
