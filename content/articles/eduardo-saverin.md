## Summary

Eduardo Saverin is a Brazilian-born co-founder of Facebook who provided the initial funding for the company. His contribution and subsequent legal battle with Mark Zuckerberg was depicted in the film The Social Network. He renounced his U.S. citizenship in 2011 before Facebook's IPO and moved to Singapore. He co-founded B Capital Group, a venture capital firm focused on growth-stage technology companies.
