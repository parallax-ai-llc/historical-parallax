## Summary

Nanamoli Bhikkhu was a British-born Theravada Buddhist monk who produced some of the most important English translations of Pali Buddhist texts. His translation of Buddhaghosa's Visuddhimagga (Path of Purification) is considered definitive. He also translated the Majjhima Nikaya (completed posthumously by Bhikkhu Bodhi). He lived in Sri Lanka and was known for his meticulous scholarship and deep practice.
