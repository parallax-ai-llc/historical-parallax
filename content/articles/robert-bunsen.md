## Summary

Robert Bunsen was a German chemist who developed the Bunsen burner and pioneered emission spectroscopy with Gustav Kirchhoff. Together they discovered the elements cesium and rubidium. His work on gas analysis and spectroscopic methods was foundational. The Bunsen burner remains one of the most widely used pieces of laboratory equipment.
