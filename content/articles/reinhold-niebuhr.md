## Summary

Reinhold Niebuhr was an American theologian and public intellectual who applied Christian realism to social and political ethics. His works Moral Man and Immoral Society and The Nature and Destiny of Man profoundly influenced American politics and foreign policy. He is widely credited with authoring the Serenity Prayer. He combined orthodox Christian theology with progressive social activism.
