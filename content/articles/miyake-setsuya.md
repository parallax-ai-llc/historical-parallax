## Summary

Miyake Setsuya was a Japanese geochemist who pioneered marine geochemistry research in Japan. He studied the chemical composition of seawater, marine sediments, and atmospheric-ocean interactions. His research on radioactive fallout in the Pacific Ocean after nuclear tests provided crucial data for understanding oceanic circulation.
