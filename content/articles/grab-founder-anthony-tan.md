## Summary

Anthony Tan co-founded Grab, Southeast Asia's largest ride-hailing and super-app platform. Starting as a taxi-booking app in Malaysia, Grab expanded across eight Southeast Asian countries, defeating Uber in the region and acquiring Uber's Southeast Asian operations in 2018. Grab went public via SPAC in 2021 at a $40 billion valuation. The app provides ride-hailing, food delivery, payments, and financial services to millions of users.
