## Summary
Bongani Mfeka, the 'Donnybrook Serial Killer,' was a South African serial killer convicted of 13 murders in KwaZulu-Natal between 1995 and 1998. He attacked young people with a hammer. He was sentenced to 13 life terms plus additional years for rape.
