## Summary

Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw founded Biocon in 1978, which became India's largest listed biopharmaceutical company. She pioneered affordable biologic drugs including biosimilar insulin for diabetes patients in developing countries. She is one of India's wealthiest self-made women and has championed affordable healthcare through biotechnology.
