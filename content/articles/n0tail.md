## Career Overview

- Johan "N0tail" Sundstein is closely associated with the organization OG, where he played a central role as a captain and in-game leader.
- He is commonly described as one of the most successful Dota 2 players by tournament earnings and by major championship results.

- Team success is often attributed not only to individual skill but to drafting decisions, coordination, and leadership under pressure.
