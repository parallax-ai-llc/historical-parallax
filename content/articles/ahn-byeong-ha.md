## Summary

Ahn Byeong-ha is a Korean metallurgist who developed high-strength steel alloys for Korean automotive and construction industries. His research on advanced high-strength steels at POSCO contributed to the development of lighter, stronger structural materials used in Korean-built vehicles and buildings.
