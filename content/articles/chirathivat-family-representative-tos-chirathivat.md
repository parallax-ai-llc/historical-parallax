## Summary

Tos Chirathivat is the CEO of Central Group, Thailand's largest retail and property conglomerate. Central Group operates Central department stores, Robinson, and luxury malls across Southeast Asia. The company acquired Selfridges Group (including Selfridges, de Bijenkorf, and Brown Thomas) in 2022 for over $6 billion. The Chirathivat family built Thailand's most prominent retail dynasty over seven decades.
