## Summary

Rupert Spira is a British spiritual teacher and author who teaches from the non-dual tradition, drawing on Advaita Vedanta and the teachings of his guru Francis Lucille (through the lineage of Jean Klein and Atmananda Krishna Menon). He is also an accomplished ceramic artist. His books, including The Transparency of Things and Being Aware of Being Aware, present a contemporary approach to the recognition of awareness as the fundamental nature of experience.
