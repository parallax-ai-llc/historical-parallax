## Summary

Clement of Rome was the third Bishop of Rome and one of the earliest Church Fathers. His letter to the Corinthians (1 Clement) is one of the oldest Christian documents outside the New Testament. He emphasized ecclesiastical order and apostolic succession. His writings provide crucial insight into early Christian community organization.
