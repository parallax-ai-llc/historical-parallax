## Summary

Jeong Min-gi is a Korean wireless communications engineer who contributed to the development of mobile communication standards in South Korea. His research on CDMA and LTE technologies helped Korea become a global leader in mobile telecommunications, with Korean carriers often being first to deploy new network generations.
