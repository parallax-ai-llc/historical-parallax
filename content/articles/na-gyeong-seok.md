## Summary

Na Gyeong-seok was one of Korea's first modern biologists who conducted systematic zoological surveys of the Korean Peninsula. He documented numerous species of Korean fauna and established biological research programs at Korean universities. His surveys created baseline biodiversity data that remains valuable for Korean ecological research.
