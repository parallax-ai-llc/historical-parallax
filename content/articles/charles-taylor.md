## Summary
Charles McArthur Ghankay Taylor was the 22nd President of Liberia. He was convicted of aiding and abetting war crimes and crimes against humanity during the Sierra Leone Civil War, including the use of child soldiers. He was sentenced to 50 years by the Special Court for Sierra Leone.
