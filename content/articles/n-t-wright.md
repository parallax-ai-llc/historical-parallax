## Summary

N. T. Wright is a British New Testament scholar and retired Anglican bishop who has profoundly influenced contemporary Christian theology. His multi-volume series Christian Origins and the Question of God is one of the most ambitious New Testament scholarship projects of the modern era. He has argued for a 'new perspective on Paul' and a bodily resurrection of Jesus. He served as Bishop of Durham from 2003 to 2010.
