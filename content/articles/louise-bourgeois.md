## Summary

Louise Bourgeois was a French-American artist known for her monumental spider sculptures and deeply personal works exploring trauma, sexuality, and the body. Her Maman spider sculptures stand in major cities worldwide. She became one of the most important artists of the 20th century, gaining recognition especially after a 1982 MoMA retrospective.
