## Summary

Ryue Nishizawa is a Japanese architect who co-founded SANAA with Kazuyo Sejima. Their ethereal, light-filled buildings demonstrate a unique sensitivity to transparency and spatial fluidity. He also practices independently, creating the Teshima Art Museum.
