## Summary
Low Taek Jho, known as Jho Low, was the mastermind behind the 1MDB scandal, one of the largest financial frauds in history. He orchestrated the theft of $4.5 billion from Malaysia's sovereign wealth fund. He remains a fugitive, believed to be in China or Macau.
