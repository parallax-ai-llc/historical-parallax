## Summary

Asghar Farhadi is an Iranian filmmaker who has won two Academy Awards for Best International Feature Film. A Separation and The Salesman explore moral dilemmas within Iranian society with extraordinary psychological complexity. He is the most internationally acclaimed Iranian filmmaker of his generation.
