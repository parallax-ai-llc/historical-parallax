## Summary

Rajaa Cherkaoui El Moursli is a Moroccan nuclear physicist who contributed to the discovery of the Higgs boson at CERN. She was part of the ATLAS experiment team that detected the Higgs particle in 2012. She received the L'Oréal-UNESCO Women in Science Award for Africa and is one of the most prominent Arab women in physics.
