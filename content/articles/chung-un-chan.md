## Summary

Chung Un-chan is a Korean physicist who made contributions to condensed matter physics and served as Prime Minister of South Korea (2009-2010). He was president of Seoul National University and has been influential in shaping Korea's science and education policy. His combination of scientific and political leadership is notable.
