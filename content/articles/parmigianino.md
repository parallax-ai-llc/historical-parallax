## Summary

Parmigianino was an Italian Mannerist painter known for elegant, elongated figures. His Madonna with the Long Neck epitomizes the Mannerist aesthetic. His Self-Portrait in a Convex Mirror, painted at age 21, is one of the most virtuosic demonstrations of artistic skill in Renaissance art.
