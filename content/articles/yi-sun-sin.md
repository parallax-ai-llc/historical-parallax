## Summary

Yi Sun-sin was a Korean naval commander who is widely regarded as one of the greatest admirals in world history. He designed the geobukseon (turtle ship), an armored warship with a covered deck that was one of the world's first ironclad vessels. His 23 consecutive naval victories during the Imjin War saved Korea from Japanese invasion.
