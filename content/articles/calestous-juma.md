## Summary

Calestous Juma was a Kenyan environmental scientist and innovation scholar who served as professor at Harvard Kennedy School. He was a leading authority on the application of science and technology for sustainable development in Africa. His book 'Innovation and Its Enemies' analyzed why societies resist new technologies. Juma advised governments worldwide on science, technology, and sustainable development policy.
