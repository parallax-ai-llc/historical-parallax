## Summary

Adrien-Marie Legendre was a French mathematician who made important contributions to number theory, statistics, and mathematical analysis. He developed the method of least squares, Legendre polynomials, and the Legendre transformation. His "Éléments de géométrie" was a standard geometry textbook for over a century. His work on elliptic integrals was foundational.
