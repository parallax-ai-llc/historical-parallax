## Summary

Evan Williams co-founded Twitter, Blogger, and Medium, making him one of the most influential figures in the history of online publishing and social media. He created Blogger in 1999, one of the first platforms to popularize blogging, and sold it to Google in 2003. He served as CEO of Twitter from 2008 to 2010. He then founded Medium, a publishing platform focused on long-form content and thoughtful writing.
