## Summary

Sumio Iijima discovered multi-walled carbon nanotubes in 1991 and single-walled carbon nanotubes in 1993. These cylindrical carbon molecules, with remarkable strength and electrical properties, opened the field of nanotechnology and nanomaterials. Carbon nanotubes have found applications in electronics, materials science, and biomedical devices.
