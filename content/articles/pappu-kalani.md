## Background

- **Full name**: Suresh (alias Pappu Budharmal) Kalani.
- **Base**: Ulhasnagar, Maharashtra, India.
- **Family/business context (reported)**: Born into a wealthy family involved in the liquor business; family members were active in local Congress politics.

## Politics and Public Office (reported)

Kalani is widely described as a *criminal-politician* who moved between organized crime influence and electoral politics.

- **1986**: Elected president of the Ulhasnagar Municipal Council.
- **1990**: Elected to the Maharashtra Vidhan Sabha from Ulhasnagar (Indian National Congress candidate).
- **1995, 1999**: Won the same assembly seat as an Independent.
- **2004**: Elected again from Ulhasnagar as a member of the Republican Party of India (Athawale).

## Alleged/Reported Criminal Network (high-level)

Sources describe Ulhasnagar in the 1970s–1980s as a place where:

- protection/extortion became a profitable business,
- criminal groups operated with political patronage,
- violence escalated during factional conflicts.

Kalani is reported to have emerged as a syndicate leader during the late 1980s, amid retaliatory cycles of killings in the area.

## Legal Cases and Convictions

- **Multiple cases (reported)**: Described as being on bail across numerous cases, including several murder cases.
- **2013**: Received a life sentence (reported) related to the 1990 murder of Ghanashyam Bhatija.
