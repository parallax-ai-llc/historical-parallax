## Summary
Ilich Ramírez Sánchez, known as 'Carlos the Jackal,' was a Venezuelan-born terrorist who was one of the most wanted men in the world during the 1970s and 1980s. He carried out a series of bombings, assassinations, and kidnappings. He was captured in Sudan in 1994 and is serving life in France.
