## Summary

Yi So-yeon became the first Korean in space in 2008, flying to the International Space Station aboard a Russian Soyuz spacecraft. She conducted 18 scientific experiments during her 11-day mission. A bioengineering researcher, she represented South Korea's growing space ambitions and inspired Korean youth in science and technology.
