## Summary

Former Russian GRU officer who co-founded the Wagner Group and served as its military commander. Named after his SS-themed radio call sign, he was killed alongside Prigozhin in a plane crash.

## Background

Utkin was reported to have served as an officer in Russian military intelligence (GRU) prior to his association with private military structures. He became one of the key early figures linked to the formation and leadership of the Wagner Group.

## Role in Wagner Group

Open sources commonly describe Utkin as a military commander within Wagner, a private military company that deployed personnel in multiple conflicts. Wagners activities have been closely scrutinized due to allegations of serious human-rights abuses in several theaters.

## Public Profile and Symbolism

Utkins call sign and reported interest in Nazi-era symbolism were frequently referenced in media coverage as part of broader discussion around Wagners internal culture and branding.

## Death

Utkin died in 2023 in a plane crash that also killed Yevgeny Prigozhin and others associated with Wagner, an event that drew significant international attention.
