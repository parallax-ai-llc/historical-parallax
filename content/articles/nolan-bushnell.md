## Summary

Nolan Bushnell founded Atari in 1972, launching the commercial video game industry. Atari's Pong became the first commercially successful video game, and the Atari 2600 console brought gaming into millions of homes. He also founded Chuck E. Cheese's restaurant chain. Steve Jobs was one of his early employees at Atari. He is widely regarded as the 'father of the video game industry.'
