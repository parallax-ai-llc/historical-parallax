## Summary

Rakesh Jhunjhunwala was known as India's Warren Buffett and was one of the most successful stock market investors in Indian history. Starting with just $100 in 1985, he built a portfolio worth over $5 billion. He was known for his bullish outlook on India and his ability to identify multibagger stocks. He co-founded Akasa Air, a new ultra-low-cost airline, just months before his death in 2022.
