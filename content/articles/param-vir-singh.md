## Summary

Param Vir Singh is an Indian nuclear physicist who contributed to the design of India's Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWRs). His work on reactor physics and nuclear fuel management helped India develop indigenous nuclear power technology. He served at the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre and contributed to India's energy security.
