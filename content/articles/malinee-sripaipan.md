## Summary

Malinee Sripaipan is a Thai epidemiologist who has made significant contributions to public health research in Southeast Asia, focusing on HIV prevention, tuberculosis, and tropical disease control. Her research on preventive health interventions has influenced Thai and regional public health policy.
