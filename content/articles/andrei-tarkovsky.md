## Summary

Andrei Tarkovsky was a Russian filmmaker whose seven feature films are considered among the greatest achievements in cinema. Ivan's Childhood, Andrei Rublev, Solaris, Mirror, and Stalker explore memory, spirituality, and the nature of art with unprecedented visual poetry. Bergman called him 'the greatest filmmaker of all time.'
