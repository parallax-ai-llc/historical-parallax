## Summary

Yo-Yo Ma is a French-born American cellist of Chinese descent who is the most famous classical musician alive. His repertoire spans Bach's Cello Suites to contemporary commissions, and his Silk Road Ensemble promotes cross-cultural musical exchange. He has won 19 Grammy Awards and been a cultural ambassador for music worldwide.
