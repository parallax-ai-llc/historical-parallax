## Summary

Monir Farmanfarmaian was an Iranian artist known for her mirror-mosaic sculptures inspired by traditional Persian geometric patterns and architecture. Her abstract geometric works combine Islamic artistic traditions with modern abstract art. She was one of the most important Iranian artists of the 20th century.
