## Profile

- **Known for**: pace, direct running, and finishing as a striker/forward.[^1]
- **Era**: rose to prominence in the early 2000s and remained active across multiple leagues through the 2010s.[^1]
- **Geographic footprint**: career included top-flight football in Italy, England, Germany, Russia, Spain, the United States, and China.[^1]

## High-level Career Notes

- Martins is commonly cited as part of a wave of Nigerian forwards who built prominent European club careers, particularly in Italy and England.[^1]
- He is associated with Inter Milan during a period when the club was competing for domestic honors and European places.[^1]
- In Major League Soccer, he is remembered for his productive seasons with Seattle Sounders FC and his role in the club’s attacking identity in that era.[^1]

## International Career (Nigeria)

- Represented **Nigeria** at the senior international level.[^1]
- Participated in major international competitions for Nigeria (see Wikipedia for tournament-by-tournament details).[^1]

## Style and Reputation

- Often described as a forward who threatened defensive lines with **speed** and **quick finishing**.[^1]
- Career arc reflects a striker who adapted to different tactical contexts across leagues with varying styles and physical demands.[^1]

## Legacy (high-level)

- Example of an African forward sustaining a long, multi-league professional career, including impactful stints outside Europe (MLS and China).[^1]
- Frequently referenced by fans and media for highlight goals and a fast, direct attacking profile.[^1]
