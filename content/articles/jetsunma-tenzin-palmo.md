## Summary

Jetsunma Tenzin Palmo is a British-born Buddhist nun of the Drukpa Kagyu lineage who spent 12 years in solitary meditation in a remote Himalayan cave. Her extraordinary story, told in Cave in the Snow, has inspired millions. She has been a powerful advocate for the full ordination and equal treatment of Buddhist nuns. She founded the Dongyu Gatsal Ling Nunnery to provide education for women from the Himalayan region.
