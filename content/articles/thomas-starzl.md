## Summary

Thomas Starzl is considered the father of modern organ transplantation. He performed the first successful liver transplant in 1967 and pioneered immunosuppressive drug protocols that made organ transplantation viable. His development of anti-rejection medications transformed transplant medicine from experimental to routine.
