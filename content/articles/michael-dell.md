## Summary

Michael Dell founded Dell Technologies, pioneering the direct-to-consumer model for personal computers. He started the company from his University of Texas dorm room in 1984. Dell's build-to-order model eliminated middlemen and revolutionized PC manufacturing and distribution. He took the company private in 2013 and later returned it to public markets after acquiring EMC Corporation.
