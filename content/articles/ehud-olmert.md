## Summary
Ehud Olmert was the 12th Prime Minister of Israel. He was convicted of bribery and corruption in 2014 related to real estate deals during his time as mayor of Jerusalem. He served 16 months in prison, becoming the first former Israeli PM to be imprisoned.
