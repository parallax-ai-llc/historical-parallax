## Summary

Alex Krizhevsky designed AlexNet, the deep convolutional neural network that won the 2012 ImageNet challenge by a huge margin, sparking the modern deep learning revolution. AlexNet demonstrated that deep neural networks trained on GPUs could dramatically outperform traditional computer vision methods. This single result transformed artificial intelligence.
