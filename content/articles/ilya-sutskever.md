## Summary

Ilya Sutskever co-founded OpenAI and served as its chief scientist. He co-authored the AlexNet paper that launched the deep learning revolution and was instrumental in developing GPT language models. A student of Geoffrey Hinton, he made key contributions to sequence-to-sequence learning and the scaling of neural networks that enabled modern AI systems.
