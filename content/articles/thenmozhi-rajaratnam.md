## Summary

Sri Lankan LTTE suicide bomber known as 'Dhanu' who assassinated Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi on May 21, 1991, at an election rally in Sriperumbudur, Tamil Nadu. She detonated a belt bomb while greeting him.
