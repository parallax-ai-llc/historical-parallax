## Summary

Takano Choei was a Japanese physician and rangaku scholar who studied Western medicine and sciences. He wrote about Western vaccination methods and warned about the dangers of isolationism in his essay Yume Monogatari (Tale of a Dream). He was imprisoned for his progressive views but escaped and continued clandestine scientific work until his death.
