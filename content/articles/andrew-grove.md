## Summary

Andrew Grove was a co-founder of Intel and its CEO from 1987 to 1998, leading the company during its most transformative era. He turned Intel into the dominant PC microprocessor company through the 'Intel Inside' marketing campaign and aggressive manufacturing investments. His management book 'Only the Paranoid Survive' became a Silicon Valley classic. Time magazine named him Person of the Year in 1997 for driving the digital revolution.
