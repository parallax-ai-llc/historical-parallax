## Summary

Cecily Brown is a British painter known for her large-scale, sensuous paintings that combine gestural abstraction with figurative elements. Her work draws on art-historical references from Rubens to de Kooning, exploring themes of eroticism, nature, and mortality. She is considered one of the most important painters of her generation.
