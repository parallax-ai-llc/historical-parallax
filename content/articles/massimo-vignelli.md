## Summary

Massimo Vignelli was an Italian-American designer whose work spans graphic design, product design, interior design, and architecture. His New York City subway map, American Airlines logo, and Bloomingdale's shopping bags are iconic examples of modernist design. His rigorous, disciplined approach influenced a generation of designers.
