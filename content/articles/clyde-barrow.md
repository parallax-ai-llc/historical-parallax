## Summary
Clyde Chestnut Barrow was an American outlaw who, with Bonnie Parker, formed the notorious Bonnie and Clyde gang. They committed robberies and murders across the American heartland from 1932 to 1934. They were ambushed and killed by law enforcement near Bienville Parish, Louisiana.
