## Summary

Ralf Hutter is the co-founder of Kraftwerk the German electronic music band that pioneered synth-pop and electronic dance music. Their albums Autobahn Trans-Europe Express and The Man-Machine influenced virtually every genre of electronic music. Kraftwerk are considered the most important electronic music group in history.
