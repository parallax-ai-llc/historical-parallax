## Summary
Ariel Castro kidnapped three women in Cleveland, Ohio between 2002-2004 and held them captive for approximately a decade. Amanda Berry, Gina DeJesus, and Michelle Knight escaped in 2013. Castro hanged himself in prison.

## Kidnappings (High-Level)
- Location: Cleveland, Ohio.
- Timeframe: 2002–2004 abductions; captivity lasted until 2013.
- Victims: **Amanda Berry**, **Gina DeJesus**, **Michelle Knight**.

## Discovery and Escape
- In 2013, the captives escaped and police intervened.
- The case became internationally known due to the long duration of captivity.

## Legal Outcome
- Castro was charged and convicted (plea/conviction details vary by summary and should be verified via court reporting).
- He died by suicide in prison in 2013.
