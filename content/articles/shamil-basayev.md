## Summary
Shamil Salmanovich Basayev was a Chechen militant leader and the most wanted man in Russia. He masterminded the 2004 Beslan school siege that killed 334 people including 186 children, and the 2002 Moscow theater hostage crisis. He was killed by Russian forces in 2006.
