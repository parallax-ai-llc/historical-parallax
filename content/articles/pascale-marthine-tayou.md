## Summary

Pascale Marthine Tayou is a Cameroonian artist known for his large-scale, colorful installations made from everyday materials. His work addresses globalization, migration, and cultural exchange through accumulations of plastic bags, crystals, and found objects. He is one of the most visible African artists on the international scene.
