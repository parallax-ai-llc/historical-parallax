## Summary

Justin Popović was a Serbian Orthodox monk and theologian who is one of the most important Serbian theological voices of the 20th century. His three-volume Orthodox Faith and Life in Christ is a major work of dogmatic theology. He was persecuted by the Yugoslav communist regime and confined to a monastery for decades. He was canonized by the Serbian Orthodox Church in 2010.
