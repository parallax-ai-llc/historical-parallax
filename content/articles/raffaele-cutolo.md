## Summary
Raffaele Cutolo was the founder and leader of the Nuova Camorra Organizzata (NCO) in Naples, Italy. From prison, he built one of the most powerful criminal organizations in Campania. He was a charismatic leader who maintained power for decades from behind bars. He died in prison in 2021.
