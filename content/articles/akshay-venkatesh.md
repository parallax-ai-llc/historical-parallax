## Summary

Akshay Venkatesh is an Australian mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2018 for his synthesis of analytic number theory, homogeneous dynamics, topology, and representation theory. Born to Indian immigrant parents in Australia, he was a child prodigy who entered university at age 13. He is a professor at the Institute for Advanced Study.
