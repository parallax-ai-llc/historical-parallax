## Summary

Florence Nightingale founded modern nursing and was a pioneer in the use of statistics to improve public health. During the Crimean War, she dramatically reduced death rates at the Scutari military hospital through sanitation improvements. Her innovative polar area diagrams demonstrated the impact of sanitary conditions on mortality.
