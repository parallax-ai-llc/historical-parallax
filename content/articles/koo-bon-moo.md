## Summary

Koo Bon-moo served as chairman of LG Group from 1995 until his death in 2018. Under his leadership, LG Electronics became a global brand in home appliances and displays. He oversaw LG's development of OLED display technology, which became the industry standard for premium televisions. He also led LG's expansion into chemical batteries, which positioned LG Energy Solution as a major EV battery supplier.
