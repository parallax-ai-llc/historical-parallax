## Summary
Michele Greco, known as 'The Pope,' was the head of the Sicilian Mafia Commission from the late 1970s until his arrest in 1986. He oversaw the Mafia's bloody internal wars. He was sentenced to life imprisonment in the Maxi Trial of 1986-1987 and died in prison.
