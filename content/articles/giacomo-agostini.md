## Summary

Giacomo Agostini won 15 Grand Prix motorcycle racing World Championships, a record that stood for decades. He won 122 Grand Prix races.

- Agostini is known for elite performance across multiple classes in Grand Prix motorcycle racing, including during an era when circuit safety standards differed markedly from modern practice.
