## Summary
Frank William Abagnale Jr. is a former American con artist who, before age 21, posed as a Pan Am pilot, doctor, and lawyer while forging checks worth millions. His story was depicted in the film 'Catch Me If You Can.' He later became a consultant for the FBI and legitimate security companies.
