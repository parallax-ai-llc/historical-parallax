## Summary

Syrian arms dealer known as the 'Prince of Marbella' who sold weapons to conflicts worldwide for decades. He was linked to the Iran-Contra affair and the Achille Lauro hijacking. Convicted in US for conspiring to sell arms to Colombian FARC.
