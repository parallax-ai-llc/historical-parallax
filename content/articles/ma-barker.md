## Summary
Arizona Donnie Clark 'Ma' Barker was the mother of several criminals who were part of the Barker-Karpis Gang during the Public Enemy era. The FBI alleged she was the mastermind behind the gang, though this characterization has been disputed. She was killed in a four-hour FBI gunfight.
