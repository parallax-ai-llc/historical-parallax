## Summary

Bonaventure was an Italian medieval scholastic theologian and Doctor of the Church known as the 'Seraphic Doctor.' He served as Minister General of the Franciscan Order and was created cardinal. His Journey of the Mind to God is a masterpiece of mystical theology. He balanced intellectual rigor with Franciscan spirituality in a distinctive theological synthesis.
