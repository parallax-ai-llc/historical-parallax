## Summary

Dada Lekhraj (later Prajapita Brahma Baba) was a wealthy Indian jeweler who founded the Brahma Kumaris World Spiritual University, one of the largest new religious movements to originate from India. He began experiencing visions in 1936 and established a community that eventually grew into a worldwide movement with centers in over 100 countries. The Brahma Kumaris practice Raja Yoga meditation and promote spiritual empowerment, particularly for women.
