## Summary

PV Sindhu won the 2019 World Championship gold and Olympic silver at 2016 Rio and bronze at 2020 Tokyo. She is the most successful Indian Olympic badminton player.

## Career Highlights (High-Level)

- Sindhu is one of the most prominent figures in Indian badminton, with major medals at the Olympics and World Championships.
- Her 2019 World Championship title is often cited as a landmark achievement for Indian singles badminton.

## Playing Style and Profile

- Known for athleticism, aggressive rallies, and strong rear-court attack, with a game built around power and endurance.
- Competed across multiple Olympic cycles, sustaining elite performance amid a highly competitive women’s singles field.

## Recognition

- Widely recognized in India as a leading international athlete and a key contributor to badminton’s popularity and visibility.
