## Summary

Nathan Blecharczyk is a co-founder and chief strategy officer of Airbnb. As the technical co-founder, he built Airbnb's original platform and led its engineering team during the company's rapid growth phase. He helped scale Airbnb from a simple website into a global platform with millions of listings. He also serves as chairman of Airbnb.org, the company's philanthropic arm that provides temporary housing during crises.
