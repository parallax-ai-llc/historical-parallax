## Summary

Isaac Singer developed and commercialized the sewing machine, founding the Singer Manufacturing Company which became the first multinational corporation. While he did not invent the sewing machine, his improvements made it practical for home use. His company pioneered the installment payment plan, making sewing machines affordable for ordinary families. Singer machines became ubiquitous worldwide and transformed the garment industry.
