## Summary

Syed Mokhtar Al-Bukhary is one of Malaysia's richest people and one of the country's most prominent Bumiputera (ethnic Malay) tycoons. He controls MMC Corporation, which operates ports, power plants, and engineering businesses. He also has significant interests in DRB-HICOM (which owns Proton cars), rice distribution, and media. He is known for his philanthropic activities through the Al-Bukhary Foundation.
