## Summary

Kawai Kanjiro was a Japanese potter and key member of the mingei movement alongside Yanagi Soetsu and Hamada Shoji. His bold, colorful ceramics combined folk art energy with sophisticated technique. He famously declined the designation of Living National Treasure.
