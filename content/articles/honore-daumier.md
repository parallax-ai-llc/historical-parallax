## Summary

Honore Daumier was a French artist and one of the greatest caricaturists of the 19th century. His thousands of lithographs skewered politicians and bourgeois society with devastating wit. His bold, gestural style anticipated Expressionism and his political courage made him a hero of artistic freedom.
