## Summary

Lygia Clark was a Brazilian artist who moved from Neo-Concretism to radical participatory art. Her Bichos (Creatures) are hinged metal sculptures viewers can manipulate, and her later Relational Objects invite physical engagement. She is considered one of the most important Latin American artists of the 20th century.
