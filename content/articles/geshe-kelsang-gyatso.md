## Summary

Geshe Kelsang Gyatso was a Tibetan Buddhist monk and meditation teacher who founded the New Kadampa Tradition (NKT), one of the fastest-growing Buddhist movements in the Western world. He wrote 23 books on Buddhist philosophy and meditation. His movement became controversial due to the Dorje Shugden controversy, putting him at odds with the Dalai Lama. He established over 1,300 meditation centers worldwide.
