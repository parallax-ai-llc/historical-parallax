## Summary

Song Mi-ryeong is a Korean astrophysicist at Yonsei University who has made contributions to observational cosmology and galaxy evolution. Her research on distant galaxies and large-scale cosmic structure has been part of international astronomical surveys and has advanced Korea's standing in astronomical research.
