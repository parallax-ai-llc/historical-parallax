## Summary

Shunpei Yamazaki is a Japanese engineer who holds the Guinness World Record for the most patents held by a single individual, with over 11,000 patents worldwide. His inventions span semiconductor devices, thin-film transistors for LCD and OLED displays, solar cells, and battery technology. He founded Semiconductor Energy Laboratory.
