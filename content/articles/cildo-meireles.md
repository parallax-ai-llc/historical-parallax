## Summary

Cildo Meireles is a Brazilian conceptual artist known for immersive installations that engage all senses. His Insertions into Ideological Circuits stamped subversive messages on currency, and his Through uses barriers to explore restriction and freedom. He is one of the most important Latin American artists alive.
