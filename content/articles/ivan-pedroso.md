## Summary

Ivan Pedroso won Olympic long jump gold at the 2000 Sydney Games and four World Championship titles.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1995 | Won first World Championship gold |
| 2000 | Won Olympic gold at Sydney |
| 2001 | Won fourth World Championship gold |
