## Summary

Jeff Skoll was eBay's first president and first full-time employee, helping build it from a startup into a global marketplace. After leaving eBay, he founded Participant Media (now Participant), a film production company focused on social impact films including An Inconvenient Truth, Spotlight, and Roma. Through the Skoll Foundation, he supports social entrepreneurs worldwide. He is one of Canada's wealthiest people.
