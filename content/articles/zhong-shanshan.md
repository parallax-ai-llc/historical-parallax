## Summary

Zhong Shanshan is the founder of Nongfu Spring, China's largest bottled water company, and a major shareholder in Beijing Wantai Biological Pharmacy. He became Asia's richest person in 2020 after both companies went public. Known as the 'Lone Wolf' for his reclusive nature and independence from business associations, he built his fortune through beverages and biotechnology. His rise was one of the most dramatic wealth stories in Chinese business.
