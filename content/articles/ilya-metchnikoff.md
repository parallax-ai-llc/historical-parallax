## Summary

Ilya Metchnikoff (Élie Metchnikoff) discovered phagocytosis — the process by which immune cells engulf and destroy pathogens — founding cellular immunology. He shared the 1908 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine with Paul Ehrlich. He later researched the role of gut bacteria in health, promoting yogurt and probiotics decades before the microbiome revolution.
