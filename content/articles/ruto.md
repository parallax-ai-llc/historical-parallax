## Summary

See William Ruto. Kenyan president who was charged by the ICC for organizing post-election violence in 2007-2008 that killed over 1,000 people. Charges were dropped due to witness tampering. He became president in 2022.

## Career Timeline

## ICC Case (High-Level)

- Allegation: organizing/instigating aspects of violence following Kenya’s 2007 election.
- Outcome: charges were dropped/vacated after the prosecution cited evidentiary issues, including claims of witness interference and non-cooperation that complicated testimony.
