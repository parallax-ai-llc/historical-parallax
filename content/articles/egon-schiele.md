## Summary

Egon Schiele was an Austrian Expressionist known for raw, psychologically intense figurative work. His twisted nudes and unflinching self-portraits explore sexuality and mortality with radical honesty. A protege of Klimt, he died in the 1918 influenza pandemic at age 28.
