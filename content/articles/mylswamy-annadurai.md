## Summary

Mylswamy Annadurai is an Indian aerospace engineer known as 'Moon Man of India' for his role as project director of Chandrayaan-1, India's first Moon mission. He also directed the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan). His low-cost space mission designs demonstrated that developing countries can conduct planetary exploration affordably.
