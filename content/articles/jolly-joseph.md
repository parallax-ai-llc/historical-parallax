## Summary

Indian serial killer from Kerala who poisoned six family members with cyanide between 2002 and 2016. She killed for property and to marry her lover. Known as the 'Koodathayi serial killer.'

## Additional Context

- The case is widely reported in India as the "Koodathayi" murders, involving allegations of repeated poisoning within an extended family over many years.
- Public reporting highlights **cyanide** as the alleged method and the role of renewed investigation when multiple suspicious deaths were reconsidered together.
