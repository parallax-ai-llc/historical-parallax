## Summary

Gangaji (born Toni Roberson) is an American spiritual teacher who met her guru Papaji (H.W.L. Poonja) in India in 1990, receiving his transmission of the Advaita teaching. She teaches that the truth of who you are is already present and needs only to be recognized. Her books, including The Diamond in Your Pocket, have reached a wide audience. She founded the Gangaji Foundation and conducts satsangs worldwide.
