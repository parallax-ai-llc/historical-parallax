## Summary

Byung-Gil Ahn is a Korean telecommunications engineer who contributed to the development of CDMA technology in South Korea. Working with Qualcomm's technology, Korean engineers like Ahn helped Korea become the first country to commercialize CDMA wireless communications, launching Korea's mobile telecommunications revolution.
