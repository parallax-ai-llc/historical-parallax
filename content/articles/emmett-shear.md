## Summary

Emmett Shear co-founded Twitch, the live-streaming platform for gamers that Amazon acquired for $970 million in 2014. He served as CEO of Twitch for over a decade, growing it into the dominant platform for gaming content with over 31 million daily active users. He briefly served as interim CEO of OpenAI during the November 2023 leadership crisis before Sam Altman returned.
