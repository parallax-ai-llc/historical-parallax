## Summary

Christo Wiese is a South African billionaire who built Shoprite Holdings into Africa's largest supermarket chain. He also held a major stake in Steinhoff International, whose accounting scandal in 2017 destroyed over $10 billion in market value. Before Steinhoff's collapse, he was one of the richest people in Africa. Shoprite operates over 3,000 stores across 14 African countries and is a pillar of retail across the continent.
