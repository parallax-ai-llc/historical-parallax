## Summary

Viktor Vekselberg is a Russian billionaire who founded the Renova Group, with interests in aluminum, oil, technology, and utilities. He co-owns the world's largest aluminum company, RUSAL, and has significant stakes in Swiss and Russian companies. He purchased one of the world's largest Fabergé egg collections for over $100 million. He was sanctioned by the U.S. government in 2018 over Russia's activities in Ukraine.
