## Summary

Former Rivers State governor accused of embezzling billions in oil revenues. He obtained a perpetual court injunction preventing his prosecution.

## Background

- **Public role:** Peter Odili served as Governor of Rivers State, one of Nigeria’s major oil-producing states, where political power is closely intertwined with oil revenue and public contracts.
- **Political environment:** Rivers State politics has frequently been characterized by intense competition and high-stakes control over resources.

## Allegations and Legal Disputes (High Level)

- **Corruption allegations:** He has been accused in public reporting and by anti-corruption actors of large-scale diversion of public funds.
- **Injunctions and procedural barriers:** A key feature of his public profile is the use of court orders (injunctions) that reportedly limited or delayed prosecution efforts by Nigeria’s anti-corruption agency, the EFCC.
