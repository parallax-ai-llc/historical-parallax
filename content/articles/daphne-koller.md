## Summary

Daphne Koller is a pioneer in probabilistic graphical models and machine learning in biology. She co-authored the definitive textbook on probabilistic graphical models and co-founded Coursera, bringing free university education online. She later founded Insitro, applying machine learning to drug discovery. She received the MacArthur Fellowship.
