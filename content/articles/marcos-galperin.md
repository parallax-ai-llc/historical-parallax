## Summary

Marcos Galperin is the founder and CEO of MercadoLibre, Latin America's largest e-commerce and fintech company. Often called 'the Amazon of Latin America,' MercadoLibre operates across 18 countries. Its Mercado Pago fintech division became one of the region's largest payment platforms. He founded the company while at Stanford Business School. MercadoLibre's market cap has exceeded $80 billion.
