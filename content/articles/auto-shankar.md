## Summary
Gowri Shankar, known as Auto Shankar, was an Indian serial killer who murdered at least six young women in Chennai (Madras) between 1988 and 1989. He was a auto-rickshaw driver and contract killer who claimed to murder on behalf of local politicians. He was hanged in 1995.
