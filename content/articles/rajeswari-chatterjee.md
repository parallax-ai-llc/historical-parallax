## Summary

Rajeswari Chatterjee was an Indian electrical engineer who was one of the first women to earn a PhD in electrical engineering from the University of Michigan. She pioneered microwave engineering research at the Indian Institute of Science and developed antennas and microwave components used in Indian telecommunications.
