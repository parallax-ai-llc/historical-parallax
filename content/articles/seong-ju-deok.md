## Summary

Seong Ju-deok was a Joseon Dynasty astronomer and mathematician who improved calendrical calculations and astronomical instruments. He served in the Gwansanggam (Bureau of Astronomy) and contributed to the accuracy of the Joseon court's astronomical observations and calendar systems.
