## Summary

Nishina Yoshio is considered the father of modern physics in Japan. He derived the Klein-Nishina formula for Compton scattering with Oskar Klein and built Japan's first cyclotron. He trained most of Japan's leading physicists including Nobel laureates Tomonaga, Yukawa, and others. His institute became the nucleus of Japanese nuclear physics research.
