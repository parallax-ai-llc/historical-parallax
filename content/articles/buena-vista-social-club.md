## Summary

Compay Segundo was a Cuban guitarist and singer who became internationally famous through the Buena Vista Social Club project. The 1997 album and Wim Wenders documentary introduced Cuban son music to millions worldwide. He performed until his death at age 95 embodying the enduring vitality of Cuban musical traditions.
