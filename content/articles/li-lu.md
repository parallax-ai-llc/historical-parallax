## Summary

Li Lu is the founder of Himalaya Capital Management and one of the most successful value investors of his generation. Charlie Munger called him 'the Chinese Warren Buffett.' A former Tiananmen Square protest leader, he fled China and built a successful investment career in the U.S. Munger entrusted him with managing part of his personal fortune. He was instrumental in Berkshire Hathaway's investment in BYD.
