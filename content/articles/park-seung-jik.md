## Summary

Park Seung-jik is often called the first modern entrepreneur of Korea. He co-founded Doosan Group's predecessor, Park Seung-jik Store, in 1896, making Doosan one of the oldest companies in Korea. His store in Baeogae (Jongno, Seoul) became a pioneering modern retail establishment. He helped introduce modern business practices to Korea during the turbulent period of Japanese colonization.
