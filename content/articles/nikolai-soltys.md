## Summary
Nikolai Soltys was a Ukrainian immigrant to the US who murdered six members of his own family in Sacramento, California, in August 2001, including his pregnant wife, his 3-year-old son, and his aunt and uncle. He was captured after a nationwide manhunt and killed himself in jail.
