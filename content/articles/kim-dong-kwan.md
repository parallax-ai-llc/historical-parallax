## Summary

Kim Dong-kwan is the vice chairman of Hanwha Solutions and a member of the Hanwha founding family. He leads Hanwha's solar energy and green technology businesses. Under his leadership, Hanwha Q Cells became one of the world's largest solar cell manufacturers. He represents the younger generation of chaebol heirs focusing on sustainable energy and technology transformation.
