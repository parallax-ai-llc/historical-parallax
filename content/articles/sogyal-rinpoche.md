## Summary

Sogyal Rinpoche was a Tibetan Buddhist teacher and author of The Tibetan Book of Living and Dying, one of the best-selling books on Tibetan Buddhism, with over 3 million copies sold in 34 languages. He founded Rigpa, an international network of Buddhist centers. His career ended in controversy when multiple students accused him of physical, sexual, and emotional abuse. He resigned from Rigpa in 2017 and died in 2019.
