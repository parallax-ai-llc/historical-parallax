## Summary

Maria Magdalena de Pazzi was an Italian Carmelite nun and mystic who experienced frequent ecstasies and visions. Her mystical utterances, dictated during her ecstatic states and recorded by her fellow nuns, fill five volumes. She experienced years of spiritual aridity and desolation that anticipated the 'dark night' experiences described by John of the Cross. She was canonized in 1669 and is a Doctor of mystical prayer.
