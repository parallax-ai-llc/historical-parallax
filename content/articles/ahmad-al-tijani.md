## Summary

Ahmad al-Tijani was an Algerian Sufi master who founded the Tijaniyya Sufi order, one of the largest and most influential in Africa. He claimed to have received direct spiritual authority from the Prophet Muhammad in a waking vision. The Tijaniyya spread rapidly across North and West Africa and now has tens of millions of adherents. The order emphasizes spiritual devotion and social engagement.
