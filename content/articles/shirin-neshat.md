## Summary

Shirin Neshat is an Iranian-American visual artist known for her work exploring the complex realities of Muslim women identity. Her photographs, films and video installations address themes of exile gender and the relationship between East and West. Women of Allah photo series and her film Women Without Men are her most celebrated works.
