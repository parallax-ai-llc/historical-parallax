## Summary
Meyer Lansky was a Belarusian-born American mobster and financier who, alongside Lucky Luciano, was instrumental in the development of the National Crime Syndicate. Known as the 'Mob's Accountant,' he developed the organization's financial operations and established gambling operations in Cuba and Las Vegas.
