## Summary

Yasir Qadhi is an American Muslim scholar and academic who is one of the most prominent Islamic voices in North America. He holds a PhD in religious studies from Yale and has studied at the Islamic University of Medina. He was previously associated with the Salafi movement but has moved toward a more inclusive traditional approach. He is the dean of academic affairs at the Al-Maghrib Institute and a professor at Rhodes College.
