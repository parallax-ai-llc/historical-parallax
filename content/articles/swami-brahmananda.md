## Summary

Swami Brahmananda (Rakhal Chandra Ghosh) was a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna and the first president of the Ramakrishna Order and Mission. Ramakrishna considered him his 'spiritual son' and he was known as the 'Mind-born Son of Sri Ramakrishna.' He was a master of meditation who combined deep contemplative life with administrative leadership. Under his presidency, the Ramakrishna Mission expanded its educational and humanitarian work across India.
