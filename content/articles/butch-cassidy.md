## Summary
Robert LeRoy Parker, known as Butch Cassidy, was an American outlaw who led the Wild Bunch gang. He committed robberies of banks, trains, and mine payrolls across the West. He and the Sundance Kid fled to South America and were reportedly killed in Bolivia in 1908.
