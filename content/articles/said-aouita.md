## Athletic Profile

Said Aouita is a Moroccan track athlete best known for elite performances across multiple middle- and long-distance events. He is frequently cited as one of the rare runners of his era to compete at a world-class level from 1,500 meters through 5,000 meters, helping define a period in which record progression and tactical racing were both prominent.

## Major Achievements

He won Olympic gold in the 5,000 meters at the 1984 Los Angeles Games and later set world records in several distances (as summarized in mainstream reference sources). ## Legacy
