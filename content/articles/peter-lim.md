## Summary

Peter Lim is a Singaporean billionaire investor known as the 'Remisier King.' He made his fortune through stock trading and palm oil investments. He owns Valencia CF in Spain and has investments in salted egg company The Golden Duck and esports. He is one of Singapore's richest people and represents the city-state's investor class that has grown wealthy through strategic Asian investments.
