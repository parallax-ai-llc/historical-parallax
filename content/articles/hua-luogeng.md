## Summary

Hua Luogeng (Hua Loo-Keng) was one of the leading mathematicians of the 20th century, making fundamental contributions to number theory, algebra, and applied mathematics. Self-taught after dropping out due to poverty, he became China's most influential mathematician and established the Chinese Academy of Sciences mathematics institute. His work on the Waring problem and analytic number theory is widely cited.
