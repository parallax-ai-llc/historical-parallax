## Summary

Jamsetji Tata was an Indian industrialist who founded the Tata Group, India's largest conglomerate. He established India's first modern steel plant (Tata Steel), the Indian Institute of Science, and the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel. His vision for India's industrialization through science and education laid the foundations for modern Indian industry and research.
