## Summary

Tan Sri Jeffrey Cheah is the founder and chairman of Sunway Group, one of Malaysia's largest conglomerates spanning property, construction, education, healthcare, and hospitality. He built Sunway City, a fully integrated township with theme parks, malls, and a university. He established the Jeffrey Cheah Foundation, Malaysia's largest education-focused social enterprise. He was named among Fortune's Greatest Leaders in 2019.
