## Summary

Maryna Viazovska is a Ukrainian mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2022 for proving the optimal sphere packing in 8 and 24 dimensions. She became the second woman to receive the Fields Medal. Her elegant proof resolved a long-standing mathematical problem using modular forms.
