## Background

Adil Ahmad Dar (also spelled Adil Ahmad Dhar in some reporting) was from the Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir.

## Attack (Pulwama, 2019)

- The attack occurred on 14 February 2019 near Lethpora in Pulwama, when a vehicle carrying explosives rammed a convoy of India’s Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).
- The bombing killed 40 CRPF personnel and was among the deadliest attacks on Indian security forces in Kashmir.
- The Pakistan-based militant group Jaish-e-Mohammed (JeM) publicly claimed responsibility.

## Immediate Aftermath

- India blamed JeM and intensified counterterrorism operations in the region.
- The incident triggered a major India–Pakistan crisis, including airstrikes and aerial engagements later in February 2019.

## Why He Is Notable

Dar is primarily noted for his role as the suicide attacker in the 2019 Pulwama bombing, an event that had significant security and geopolitical consequences in South Asia.
