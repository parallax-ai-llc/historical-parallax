## Summary

Kwon Hyuk-bin founded Smilegate, one of South Korea's most profitable gaming companies, known for the CrossFire franchise. CrossFire became one of the most-played online games in the world, particularly in China, generating billions in revenue. Smilegate expanded into mobile gaming, entertainment, and AI. Kwon has become one of South Korea's wealthiest people through gaming.
