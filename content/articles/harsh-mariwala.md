## Summary

Harsh Mariwala is the founder and chairman of Marico Limited, one of India's leading consumer goods companies. He founded Marico by acquiring Parachute coconut oil from the family business and building it into a market leader. Marico products like Parachute and Saffola are household names across India. He also founded ASCENT, a peer-to-peer platform for entrepreneurs. His transformation of a commodity product into a branded FMCG success story is a classic of Indian business.
