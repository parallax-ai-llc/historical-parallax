## Summary

Patrice Motsepe is a South African mining magnate and the first Black billionaire in South Africa. He founded African Rainbow Minerals, one of the country's largest diversified mining companies. He also owns Mamelodi Sundowns F.C. and serves as president of the Confederation of African Football (CAF). He signed the Giving Pledge, committing half his fortune to charity.
