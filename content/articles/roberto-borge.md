## Summary

Mexican former governor of Quintana Roo who was convicted of embezzlement and fraud for selling state property at below-market prices. He fled to Panama before being captured and extradited. Part of a wave of corrupt Mexican governors.

## Background

- Roberto Borge Angulo is a Mexican politician associated with the Institutional Revolutionary Party (**PRI**).
- He served as governor of the state of **Quintana Roo**, a major tourism-centered state that includes destinations such as Cancún and the Riviera Maya.

## Allegations and Investigations (high level)

- Reporting and official statements described allegations including misuse of public funds and irregular asset transactions.
- Cases involving former state governors became a broader political issue in Mexico during the 2010s, with multiple high-profile investigations across different states.

## Arrest, Extradition, and Court Proceedings

- Borge was arrested abroad and extradited to Mexico in connection with the investigations.
