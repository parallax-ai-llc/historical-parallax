## Political profile (high-level)

Abdoulaye Wade is widely documented as a long-running opposition figure who ultimately became **President of Senegal** in 2000 and served until 2012. Senegal’s modern political history is often cited for its relative institutional continuity in the region, including competitive elections and changes of power through the ballot box.
