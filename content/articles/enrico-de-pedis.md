## Summary
Enrico 'Renatino' De Pedis was an Italian gangster and the leader of the Banda della Magliana, a powerful criminal organization in Rome active from the late 1970s. The gang had connections to the Italian secret services, the P2 Masonic lodge, and the Vatican. He was murdered in 1990.
