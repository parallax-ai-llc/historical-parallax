## Summary

Alexander Prokhorov shared the 1964 Nobel Prize in Physics for fundamental work in quantum electronics leading to masers and lasers. With Nikolay Basov, he independently developed the principles of the maser. He also conducted important research on ruby lasers and non-linear optics.
