## Summary

Sariputta was the foremost disciple of the Buddha in wisdom and one of the two chief disciples alongside Moggallana. He was known for his systematic understanding and teaching of the Dharma. He played a crucial role in developing the Abhidharma (systematic philosophy). He predeceased the Buddha by a few months.
