## Summary

Low Tuck Kwong is an Indonesian coal mining billionaire and the founder of Bayan Resources, one of Indonesia's largest coal producers. He built his fortune through coal mining and energy production during the commodity supercycle. Bayan Resources became one of the most profitable mining companies in Southeast Asia. He ranks among Indonesia's wealthiest individuals.
