## Summary
Pablo Acosta Villarreal was a Mexican drug lord who controlled the cocaine and marijuana trade across the Ojinaga plaza on the US-Mexico border. He was one of the most powerful traffickers of the 1980s. He was killed in a joint US-Mexican raid in 1987.
