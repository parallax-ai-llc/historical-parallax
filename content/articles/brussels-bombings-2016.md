## Summary
On March 22, 2016, three coordinated suicide bombings hit Brussels — two at Zaventem airport and one at Maalbeek metro station — killing 32 people and injuring 340. The attacks were linked to the same ISIS cell responsible for the November 2015 Paris attacks.
