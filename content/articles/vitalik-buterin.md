## Summary

Vitalik Buterin is the co-founder of Ethereum, the world's second-largest cryptocurrency platform. He proposed Ethereum in 2013 at age 19 and launched it in 2015, introducing smart contracts that enabled decentralized applications. Ethereum's blockchain became the foundation for DeFi, NFTs, and thousands of decentralized applications. He is one of the most influential figures in cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.
