## Summary

Dutch-Moroccan Islamist who murdered filmmaker Theo van Gogh on November 2, 2004, by shooting and nearly decapitating him on an Amsterdam street. He pinned a note threatening Ayaan Hirsi Ali to the body. Sentenced to life without parole.
