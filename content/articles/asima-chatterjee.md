## Summary

Asima Chatterjee was a pioneering Indian chemist who developed anti-epileptic and anti-malarial drugs from indigenous plants. She was the first woman to receive a Doctor of Science degree from an Indian university. Her work on alkaloids and medicinal plants bridged traditional Indian medicine with modern pharmacology.
