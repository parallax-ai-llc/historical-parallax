## Summary

George Papanicolaou developed the Pap smear test (Papanicolaou test) for early detection of cervical cancer, one of the most successful cancer screening methods in history. His technique of examining exfoliated cells reduced cervical cancer mortality by over 70%. He is considered the father of cytopathology.
