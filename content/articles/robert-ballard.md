## Summary

Robert Ballard discovered the wreck of RMS Titanic in 1985 using remotely operated vehicles, one of the most famous underwater discoveries. He also found hydrothermal vents on the ocean floor and discovered the wrecks of the Bismarck, PT-109, and ancient ships. His innovations in deep-sea exploration technology revolutionized underwater archaeology.
