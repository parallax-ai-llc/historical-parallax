## Summary

Stephen Paddock fired into a music festival crowd from the 32nd floor of the Mandalay Bay hotel, killing 60 people. Deadliest mass shooting in modern US history.

## Context

- The shooting occurred during the Route 91 Harvest music festival on the Las Vegas Strip.
- The attacker used an elevated position in the Mandalay Bay hotel to fire on an outdoor crowd.

## Key Facts (High Level)

- **Date**: 2017-10-01 (local time).
- **Location**: Las Vegas, Nevada, United States.
- **Fatalities**: 60 people were killed (including the perpetrator).
- **Injuries**: Hundreds of people were wounded.
- **Method**: Gunfire from a hotel room into a large public event.

## Immediate Aftermath

- Victims were treated on-scene and transported to multiple hospitals in the Las Vegas area.
- The Mandalay Bay and surrounding locations were secured while law enforcement searched for additional threats.

## Investigation (Overview)

- Authorities identified the shooter as Stephen Paddock.
- The perpetrator died at the scene; public reporting has described the death as self-inflicted.
- Investigators documented extensive weaponry and ammunition connected to the incident.

## Public Impact

- The event is widely cited as the deadliest mass shooting in modern U.S. history by victim count.
- It intensified national debate over firearm access, regulation, and mass-casualty preparedness.
