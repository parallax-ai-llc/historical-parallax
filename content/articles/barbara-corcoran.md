## Summary

Barbara Corcoran is an American businesswoman and investor who founded The Corcoran Group, a leading New York City real estate brokerage. She started with a $1,000 loan and built it into a company she sold for $66 million in 2001. She is known as a shark on ABC's Shark Tank, where she has invested in dozens of companies. Her story of building a real estate empire from nothing became an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs.
