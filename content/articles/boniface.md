## Summary

Boniface was an Anglo-Saxon missionary who became the 'Apostle of the Germans' for his efforts to Christianize the Germanic peoples. He reformed the Frankish church and established the ecclesiastical structure of Germany. He is famous for felling the sacred Thor's Oak at Geismar. He was martyred by pagan Frisians near Dokkum.
