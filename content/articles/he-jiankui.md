## Summary

He Jiankui created the world's first gene-edited babies using CRISPR in 2018, sparking global outrage and ethical debate. He modified the CCR5 gene in twin embryos to make them resistant to HIV. His experiment was condemned by the scientific community for its premature application and ethical violations. He was imprisoned in China for three years.
