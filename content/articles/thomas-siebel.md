## Summary

Thomas Siebel founded Siebel Systems in 1993, which became the dominant CRM software company before being acquired by Oracle for $5.8 billion in 2006. He then founded C3.ai, an enterprise AI company that went public in 2020. His first company defined the CRM industry before Salesforce, and his second aims to do the same for enterprise artificial intelligence. He has donated over $100 million to education and research.
