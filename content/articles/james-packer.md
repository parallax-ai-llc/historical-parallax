## Summary

James Packer is an Australian billionaire and the son of Kerry Packer, one of Australia's most powerful media moguls. He inherited control of Publishing and Broadcasting Limited and later pivoted to casino development through Crown Resorts. Crown Resorts became one of Australia's largest casino operators before being embroiled in money laundering scandals. Blackstone acquired Crown Resorts in 2022 for $6.3 billion.
