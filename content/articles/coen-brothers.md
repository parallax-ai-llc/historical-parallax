## Summary

Joel Coen is an American filmmaker who, with his brother Ethan, created some of the most distinctive and acclaimed films in American cinema. Their darkly comic works including Fargo, No Country for Old Men, The Big Lebowski, and Barton Fink combine genre mastery with philosophical depth and regional American authenticity.
