## Summary

Bak Je-ga was a Joseon Dynasty Silhak scholar who wrote Bukhakui (Discourse on Northern Learning), advocating for Korea to adopt Chinese and Western technologies for economic development. He promoted international technology transfer, improved agricultural techniques, and commercial development as paths to national prosperity.
