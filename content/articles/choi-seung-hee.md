## Summary

Choi Seung-hee was a Korean microbiologist who scientifically studied Korean fermented foods, particularly kimchi and doenjang (fermented soybean paste). Her research documented the beneficial microorganisms in traditional Korean fermentation processes and helped establish the scientific basis for Korean food science.
