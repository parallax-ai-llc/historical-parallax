## Additional Context

### What Zeromax Was

Zeromax is described in public summaries as a dominant private conglomerate in Uzbekistan during the 2000s, active across multiple sectors. Its scale and apparent proximity to state-linked business networks made it a focal point in later reporting about corruption and patronage.
