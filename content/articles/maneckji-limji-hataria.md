## Summary

Maneckji Limji Hataria was an Indian Parsi social activist who spent over 25 years in Iran working to improve the conditions of the Zoroastrian community there. He successfully lobbied for the abolition of the jizya (poll tax) on Zoroastrians in Iran in 1882. He established schools, hospitals, and fire temples for the oppressed Iranian Zoroastrian community. He is considered a hero by both Indian and Iranian Zoroastrians.
