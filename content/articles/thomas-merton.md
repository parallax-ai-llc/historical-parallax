## Summary

Thomas Merton was an American Trappist monk and one of the most influential spiritual writers of the 20th century. His autobiography The Seven Storey Mountain became a bestseller and inspired many to consider religious life. He wrote extensively on contemplative prayer, social justice, and interfaith dialogue. He became increasingly engaged with Eastern religions, especially Zen Buddhism, and died during a trip to Asia.
