## Summary
Harvey Weinstein is a former American film producer whose sexual abuse of women over decades was exposed in 2017, triggering the #MeToo movement. He was convicted of rape and sexual assault in New York in 2020 and again in Los Angeles in 2022. He has been sentenced to 39 years total.
