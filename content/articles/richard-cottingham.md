## Summary
Richard Francis Cottingham, the 'Times Square Torso Ripper,' was an American serial killer who murdered at least 11 women in New York and New Jersey between 1967 and 1980. He dismembered some victims and set their bodies on fire. He has continued confessing to additional murders from prison.
