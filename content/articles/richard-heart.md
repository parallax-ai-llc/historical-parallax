## Background

- Richard Heart is the public persona of a crypto promoter associated with the HEX ecosystem.
- His public profile is primarily tied to marketing, community building, and fundraising narratives around crypto projects.

## Projects and Associations

- **HEX**: A token marketed as a high-yield crypto product on Ethereum.
- **PulseChain**: A proposed/marketed Ethereum-compatible network positioned as a lower-fee alternative.
- **PulseX**: A decentralized exchange concept promoted alongside PulseChain.

## Legal and Regulatory Issues (high level)

- The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) filed a civil complaint alleging unregistered securities offerings and fraud related to fundraising and token promotion.
- The allegations center on how funds were solicited and used, and on representations made to participants.
