## Summary

Yoo Sang-chul was a key player in South Korea's 2002 World Cup semi-final run, scoring against Poland. He died of pancreatic cancer in 2024.

At the 2002 FIFA World Cup, co-hosted by South Korea and Japan, Yoo was part of the national team that reached the semi-finals. His goal against Poland is frequently highlighted in Korean football retrospectives, and his performances during the tournament contributed to the team's historic run.
