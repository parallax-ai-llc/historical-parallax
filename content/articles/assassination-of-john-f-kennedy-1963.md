## Overview
On November 22, 1963, President John F. Kennedy was assassinated while riding in a presidential motorcade through Dealey Plaza in Dallas, Texas.

## The Event
Shots rang out as the motorcade passed the Texas School Book Depository. Kennedy was struck in the neck and head. He was rushed to Parkland Memorial Hospital but was pronounced dead shortly after. Lee Harvey Oswald was arrested for the crime but was himself killed by Jack Ruby two days later.

## Impact
The assassination shocked the world and led to the Warren Commission, which concluded Oswald acted alone. However, debates and conspiracy theories persist to this day.
