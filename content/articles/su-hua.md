## Summary

Su Hua is the co-founder and CEO of Kuaishou Technology, China's second-largest short video platform after Douyin (TikTok). Kuaishou has over 600 million monthly active users, primarily in China's lower-tier cities and rural areas. The company went public in Hong Kong in 2021, raising $6.2 billion in the largest tech IPO of the year. Kuaishou's focus on grassroots content creators differentiated it from ByteDance's more polished approach.
