## Summary

Nandan Nilekani is a co-founder of Infosys and the architect of Aadhaar, India's biometric identity system covering over 1.3 billion people. He served as CEO of Infosys from 2002 to 2007. As chairman of the Unique Identification Authority of India, he built the world's largest biometric identification system. Aadhaar became the foundation for India's digital public infrastructure revolution.
