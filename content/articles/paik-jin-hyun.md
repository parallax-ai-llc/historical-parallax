## Summary

Paik Jin-hyun is a Korean international law scholar who served as a judge at the International Tribunal for the Law of the Sea (ITLOS). His expertise in maritime law and ocean science has influenced international policies on marine resource management, deep-sea mining, and ocean conservation.
