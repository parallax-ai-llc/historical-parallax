## Summary

Ilya Repin was the most celebrated Russian realist painter of the 19th century. His masterworks include Barge Haulers on the Volga and Ivan the Terrible and His Son. His extraordinary ability to combine social commentary with psychological depth defined the Peredvizhniki movement.
