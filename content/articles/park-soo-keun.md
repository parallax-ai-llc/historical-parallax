## Summary

Park Soo-keun was a beloved Korean painter known for his distinctive granite-textured paintings of ordinary Korean people and village scenes. His thick, stone-like paint surfaces and simplified forms captured the warmth and resilience of Korean folk life. He is considered one of Korea's most treasured modern artists despite his life of poverty.
