## Summary

Hans Vestberg is the CEO of Verizon Communications, one of the world's largest telecommunications companies. He previously served as CEO of Ericsson, the Swedish telecom equipment giant. At Verizon, he led the company's 5G network rollout across the United States. His background in global telecom equipment gave him unique insight into network infrastructure that he brought to Verizon's consumer and enterprise businesses.
