## Summary

Carl F. H. Henry was an American evangelical theologian and the first editor of Christianity Today magazine. His six-volume God, Revelation and Authority is one of the most comprehensive evangelical systematic theologies. He was a founding faculty member of Fuller Theological Seminary and a key architect of the neo-evangelical movement. He helped establish evangelical Christianity as an intellectual force in American public life.
