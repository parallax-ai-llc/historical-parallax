## Summary
Sabri Khalil al-Banna, known as Abu Nidal, was a Palestinian militant who founded the Abu Nidal Organization. He was described as the most dangerous terrorist in the world. His organization carried out attacks in 20 countries, killing over 900 people. He was found dead in Baghdad in 2002.
