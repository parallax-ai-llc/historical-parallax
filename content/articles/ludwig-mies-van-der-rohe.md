## Summary

Ludwig Mies van der Rohe was one of the pioneers of modern architecture, known for his principle 'less is more.' His Barcelona Pavilion and Farnsworth House are icons of minimalist design. As the last director of the Bauhaus, he emigrated to America where his steel-and-glass skyscrapers transformed the urban landscape.
