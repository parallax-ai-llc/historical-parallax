## New Bilibid Prison scandal (high-level)

Colanggo became internationally known through investigations and raids that alleged large-scale narcotics trafficking continued **inside New Bilibid Prison (NBP)**, the Philippines’ main national penitentiary.

Key themes that are frequently mentioned in reporting include:

- **Contraband and corruption:** the ability of some high-profile inmates to obtain prohibited items and preferential treatment.
- **Command-and-control from custody:** allegations that drug distribution networks were directed using smuggled phones and intermediaries.
- **Symbolic “luxury cell” evidence:** the discovery of cells outfitted with amenities (sometimes described as including hot tubs or jacuzzis) became a public shorthand for systemic failure.

## Institutional and political fallout

The NBP revelations fed broader debates about:

- accountability within the Bureau of Corrections
- oversight of maximum-security facilities
- links between prison governance and outside criminal networks

## Caution on details

Specific claims in this episode (who authorized improvements, which officials were involved, and the full scope of profits) have been contested across investigations and political testimony; summaries typically focus on the documented pattern of illicit activity and the evident breakdown of prison controls.
