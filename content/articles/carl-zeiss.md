## Summary

Carl Zeiss founded the Carl Zeiss optical works in Jena, producing microscopes that revolutionized scientific research. His collaboration with physicist Ernst Abbe and glassmaker Otto Schott led to the development of superior optical instruments based on scientific principles rather than trial and error. Zeiss microscopes became the gold standard for scientific observation.
