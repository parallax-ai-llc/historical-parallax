## Summary

William Lever, 1st Viscount Leverhulme, founded Lever Brothers, which became one of the world's largest soap and consumer goods companies. He introduced Sunlight Soap and built Port Sunlight, a model village for his workers. Through mergers, Lever Brothers eventually became Unilever, one of the world's largest consumer goods corporations. He was a pioneer of corporate welfare and branding in consumer goods.
