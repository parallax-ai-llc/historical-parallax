## Summary

Oskar Kokoschka was a leading Austrian Expressionist known for psychologically penetrating portraits and turbulent landscapes. His early portraits were described as 'psychological X-rays.' His tempestuous affair with Alma Mahler inspired The Bride of the Wind.
