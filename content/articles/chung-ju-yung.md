## Summary

Chung Ju-yung was a Korean industrialist who founded Hyundai Group and transformed it into a global engineering and industrial conglomerate. He built Korea's first supertanker, established Hyundai Motor Company, and constructed major infrastructure projects worldwide. His engineering achievements helped drive South Korea's rapid industrialization.
