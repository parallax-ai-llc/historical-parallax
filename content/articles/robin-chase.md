## Summary

Robin Chase is an American entrepreneur who co-founded Zipcar in 2000, pioneering the car-sharing industry. She created the concept of shared-use vehicles, which laid the groundwork for the broader sharing economy. Zipcar grew into the world's largest car-sharing company before being acquired by Avis for $500 million in 2013. She also co-founded Veniam, a networking company for vehicles and cities.
