## Role in the 2002 Bali Bombings

Amrozi was one of several perpetrators involved in the planning and execution of the October 2002 bombings in Bali, Indonesia, which killed more than 200 people and drew global condemnation.

## Arrest and Legal Proceedings

- He was arrested by Indonesian authorities and prosecuted in relation to the attack.
- The case included multiple defendants connected to Jemaah Islamiyah, a militant Islamist network in Southeast Asia.
- Amrozi was sentenced to death and later executed.

## Public Profile

The nickname "Smiling Bomber" reflects photographs and courtroom appearances in which he appeared smiling, which received extensive media attention.
