## Summary

American prisoner who spent 36 years in solitary confinement, the longest stretch in US federal prison history. He killed a prison guard in 1983 and was placed in a specially designed underground cell.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1977 | Entered federal prison for armed robbery |
| 1983 | Murdered correction officer Merle Clutts |
| 1983 | Placed in permanent solitary confinement |
| 2019 | Died in prison after 36 years in solitary |
