## Summary

Jaime Zobel de Ayala is the patriarch of the Ayala family, one of the oldest and most powerful business families in the Philippines. He served as chairman of Ayala Corporation, the country's oldest conglomerate, spanning banking (BPI), real estate (Ayala Land), telecommunications (Globe Telecom), and water. The Ayala family has shaped Philippine business for over 180 years since its founding in 1834.
