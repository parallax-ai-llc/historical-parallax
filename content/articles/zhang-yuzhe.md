## Summary

Zhang Yuzhe was a Chinese astronomer who discovered and named several asteroids, including 1125 China and 1802 Zhang. He was the first Chinese astronomer to discover an asteroid and the first to compute a comet's orbit. He served as director of the Purple Mountain Observatory and helped establish modern Chinese astronomy.
