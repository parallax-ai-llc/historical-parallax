## Summary

Placido Domingo is a Spanish operatic tenor who, alongside Pavarotti and Carreras, formed the Three Tenors. With over 150 roles, he has the largest repertoire of any tenor in history. He has also pursued a successful second career as a conductor and opera administrator, serving as general director of the Los Angeles Opera and Washington National Opera.
