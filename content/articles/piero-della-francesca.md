## Summary

Piero della Francesca was an Italian painter celebrated for his serene, geometrically precise compositions and masterful use of light. His fresco cycle The Legend of the True Cross in Arezzo is a masterpiece of Renaissance painting. Also a gifted mathematician, he wrote influential treatises on perspective.
