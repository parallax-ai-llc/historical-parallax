## Background and Setting

- **Susukino** is an entertainment district in **Sapporo**, Hokkaido, Japan.
- The incident is commonly referred to in media as the “Susukino head case” because reporting centered on the neighborhood where the victim was last seen and where key events were believed to have unfolded.

## What the Label Refers To

The phrase “Susukino head case” is used as a shorthand for a **2023 homicide and dismemberment case** involving:

- a male victim,
- a young woman alleged to have carried out the decapitation,
- and her parents, who were alleged to have assisted with concealment and related acts.

Because the case was investigated and prosecuted under Japanese law, public descriptions often focus on the **alleged roles** of each family member and the sequence of concealment.
