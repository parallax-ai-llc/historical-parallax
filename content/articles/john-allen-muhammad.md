## Summary
John Allen Muhammad was an American serial killer who, with Lee Boyd Malvo, carried out the Beltway sniper attacks in October 2002, killing 17 people and wounding 10 in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. He used a modified rifle fired from the trunk of a car. He was executed by lethal injection in 2009.
