## Summary

Peter Lombard was an Italian bishop and theologian whose Four Books of Sentences became the standard theological textbook in medieval universities for centuries. His work organized and synthesized the writings of the Church Fathers on key theological topics. Nearly every major medieval theologian wrote a commentary on the Sentences. He served as Bishop of Paris in his final years.
