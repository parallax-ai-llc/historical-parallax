## Summary

Mian Muhammad Mansha is Pakistan's wealthiest person and chairman of the Nishat Group, one of the country's largest conglomerates. Nishat Group spans textiles, cement, banking (MCB Bank), power generation, insurance, and real estate. He built the group from his family's textile business into a diversified empire. MCB Bank is one of Pakistan's most profitable private banks.
