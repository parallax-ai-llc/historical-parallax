## Summary

David Vélez is the founder and CEO of Nubank, Latin America's largest digital bank and one of the most valuable fintech companies in the world. He founded Nubank in Brazil in 2013 to challenge the country's expensive, oligopolistic banking sector. Nubank went public in 2021 at a $41 billion valuation and now serves over 90 million customers. Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway invested $500 million in Nubank before its IPO.
