## Summary

Bae Bien-u is a Korean photographer known for his meditative, large-format photographs of Korean pine forests. His Sonamu (Pine Tree) series, photographed over decades, transforms Korean landscapes into abstract, contemplative images. His work bridges Eastern aesthetics with contemporary photographic practice and has been exhibited worldwide.
