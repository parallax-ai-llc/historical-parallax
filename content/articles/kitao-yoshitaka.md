## Summary

Yoshitaka Kitao is the CEO of SBI Holdings, one of Japan's largest financial services groups. He was previously a top executive at SoftBank and helped build its early internet businesses. SBI Holdings operates online securities, banking, insurance, and asset management services. He has been a vocal proponent of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology in Japanese financial services.
