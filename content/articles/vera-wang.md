## Summary

Vera Wang built a global bridal and fashion empire after entering the industry at age 40. She worked as a Vogue editor for 17 years and at Ralph Lauren before designing her own wedding dress and launching her bridal line. Her designs have been worn by celebrities, Olympic skaters, and Hollywood stars. She expanded into ready-to-wear, fragrances, and home goods, becoming one of the most recognized names in bridal fashion.
