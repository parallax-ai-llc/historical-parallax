## Summary
On April 2, 2015, al-Shabaab militants stormed Garissa University College in northeastern Kenya, killing 148 people, mostly students. They separated Christian students from Muslims and executed them. It was the deadliest attack in Kenya since the 1998 US embassy bombing.
