## Summary
On May 22, 2017, a suicide bomber detonated a shrapnel-laden device as concertgoers were leaving an Ariana Grande concert at Manchester Arena. Twenty-two people were killed and over 1,000 injured. The bomber, Salman Abedi, was linked to ISIS.
