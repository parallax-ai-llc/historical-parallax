## Summary

Cathie Wood is the founder and CEO of ARK Invest, an investment management firm focused on disruptive innovation. Her flagship ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK) gained fame for its huge returns in 2020, driven by investments in Tesla, genomics, fintech, and AI. She became one of the most followed investors on social media. Her high-conviction, concentrated investment strategy has produced both spectacular gains and significant losses.
