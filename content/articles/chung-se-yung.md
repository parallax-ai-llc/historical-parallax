## Summary

Chung Se-yung served as chairman and CEO of Hyundai Motor Company, leading it during a critical growth period from 1992 to 1999. He was the younger brother of Hyundai founder Chung Ju-yung. Under his leadership, Hyundai Motor expanded into international markets and launched the first independently designed Korean car. He played a crucial role in establishing Hyundai as a competitive global automaker.
