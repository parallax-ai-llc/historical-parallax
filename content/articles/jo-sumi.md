## Summary

Jo Sumi (Sumi Jo) is a Korean operatic soprano known for her coloratura virtuosity. She debuted at La Scala and has performed at the world's leading opera houses. Her collaboration with Herbert von Karajan launched her international career, and she has become one of the most celebrated sopranos of her generation.
