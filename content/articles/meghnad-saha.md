## Summary

Meghnad Saha was an Indian astrophysicist best known for the Saha ionization equation, which describes the physical and chemical condition of matter in stars. This equation became a fundamental tool in astrophysics for understanding stellar spectra and atmospheres. He also contributed to nuclear physics and was a member of India's Parliament, advocating for science and technology development.
