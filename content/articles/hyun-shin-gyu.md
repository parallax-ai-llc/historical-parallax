## Summary

Hyun Shin-gyu was a Korean agricultural scientist who developed improved rice varieties that increased grain yields in South Korea. His plant breeding research during the 1960s and 1970s was part of Korea's agricultural modernization, helping the country achieve food self-sufficiency. He trained many Korean agricultural researchers.
