## Summary

Dwight L. Moody was an American evangelist who became one of the most influential Christian figures of the 19th century. He founded the Moody Bible Institute and the Moody publishing house in Chicago. His revivals in America and Britain drew millions. He pioneered modern mass evangelism techniques that influenced Billy Sunday and Billy Graham.
