## Summary

Yves Saint Laurent was a French fashion designer who is regarded as the most influential designer of the 20th century after Chanel. He introduced the tuxedo suit for women, the Mondrian dress, and safari jacket, bringing art, gender fluidity, and multiculturalism into fashion. His ready-to-wear line democratized high fashion.
