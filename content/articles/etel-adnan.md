## Summary

Etel Adnan was a Lebanese-American artist and writer whose vibrant, small-scale landscape paintings and tapestries gained global recognition late in her career. Her colorful abstractions of Mount Tamalpais and Mediterranean landscapes are celebrated for their joyful simplicity. She was also an acclaimed poet and novelist.
