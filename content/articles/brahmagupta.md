## Summary

Brahmagupta was an Indian mathematician and astronomer who made important contributions to mathematics including rules for zero and negative numbers. He was the first to formulate rules for arithmetic operations with zero and negative numbers. His Brahmagupta's formula gives the area of a cyclic quadrilateral. He also made advances in astronomy, calculating the Earth's circumference with considerable accuracy.
