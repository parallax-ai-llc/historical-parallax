Choi Min-jeong won gold at both 2018 PyeongChang and 2022 Beijing in short track.

## Additional Context

Choi Min-jeong is one of South Korea’s most prominent short track speed skaters of her generation, noted for consistency across Olympic, World Championship, and World Cup seasons.

### Competitive profile

- Typically competes in 500 m, 1000 m, and 1500 m, as well as relay events.
- Known for tactical racing: conserving position early and accelerating in later laps.

### International significance

South Korea treats short track as a flagship winter sport, and Olympic outcomes often carry major public attention.

- Her medals contributed to South Korea’s continued dominance in women’s short track.
- Her career overlaps an era of intense international parity, with strong challengers from China, Canada, the Netherlands, and others.

### Recognition

In South Korean sports media, she is frequently discussed alongside other top national-team skaters as part of a long-running pipeline from junior competition to Olympic results.
