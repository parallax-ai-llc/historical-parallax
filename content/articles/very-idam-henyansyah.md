## Summary

Indonesian serial killer known as 'Ryan' who murdered 11 people and buried their bodies in his backyard in Jombang, East Java. He was Indonesia's most notorious serial killer. Executed by firing squad with the Bali bombers.

## Case Discovery (High-Level)
