## Summary

Verner Panton was a Danish designer known for his futuristic, colorful furniture and interior designs. His Panton Chair was the first single-form injection-molded plastic chair. His psychedelic Varna restaurant interior and the Phantasy Landscape demonstrate his radical vision of living environments.
