Michael Jackson, the "King of Pop," had his name mentioned in Epstein-related court documents. However, witness testimony explicitly stated he declined inappropriate activities.

## Context of Mention

Jackson's name appeared in testimony from Johanna Sjoberg, who stated:
- Jackson visited Epstein's Palm Beach residence
- He was offered a massage
- **He declined and did not participate**

## Important Clarification

The testimony specifically exonerates Jackson:
- He refused the massage offer
- There is no allegation of misconduct
- The mention serves to show he was not involved

## No Allegations

There are no allegations or evidence of any inappropriate conduct by Michael Jackson related to Epstein.
