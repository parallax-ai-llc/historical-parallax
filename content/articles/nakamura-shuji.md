## Summary

Nakamura Shuji is a Japanese-American engineer who independently developed practical high-brightness blue LEDs and blue laser diodes using InGaN technology while working at Nichia Corporation. He shared the 2014 Nobel Prize in Physics. His work enabled white LED lighting and Blu-ray disc technology, and he later won a landmark lawsuit over inventor compensation.
