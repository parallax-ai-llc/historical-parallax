## Overview
Facing impossible odds, Admiral Yi Sun-sin utilized the strong tidal currents of the Myeongnyang Strait to trap and destroy the Japanese fleet. This victory prevented the Japanese from entering the Yellow Sea and cutting off supplies to the Joseon capital.

## Legacy
It is considered one of the greatest naval victories in world history and cemented Yi Sun-sins status as a military genius and national hero.
