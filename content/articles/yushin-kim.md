## Summary

Yushin Kim founded Krafton, the Korean gaming company behind PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG), one of the most successful video games in history. PUBG popularized the battle royale genre and generated billions in revenue. Krafton went public in 2021 in one of South Korea's largest IPOs. The company expanded into multiple gaming titles and interactive entertainment.
