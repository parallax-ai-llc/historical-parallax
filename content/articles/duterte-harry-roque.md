## Background

- Harry Roque (Harold Joseph Modesto Roque) is a Filipino lawyer and academic who became a prominent public figure in national politics.
- Before joining the Duterte administration, he was known for legal work and teaching, and he engaged in public-interest litigation and policy debate.

## Role in the Duterte administration

- Roque served as a presidential spokesperson during the Rodrigo Duterte presidency and was frequently tasked with communicating government policy to domestic and international audiences.
- In public briefings, he defended a range of administration positions, including those related to security policy and the government’s anti-drug campaign.

## Public controversy

- His transition from earlier advocacy to an official communications role made him a polarizing figure, with critics questioning his alignment with administration rhetoric.
- Roque’s statements were often scrutinized in relation to international human-rights concerns and Philippines–foreign relations.
