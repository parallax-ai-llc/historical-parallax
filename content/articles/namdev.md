## Summary

Namdev was a Marathi poet-saint whose devotional verses (abhangas) to Lord Vitthala made him one of the most important figures in the Bhakti movement. He traveled across India spreading devotional worship and his poetry is included in the Sikh Guru Granth Sahib. He is venerated by both Hindus and Sikhs. He preached the equality of all people before God and composed over 2,500 abhangas.
