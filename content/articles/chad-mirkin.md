## Summary

Chad Mirkin is an American chemist who invented spherical nucleic acids (SNAs) and dip-pen nanolithography, two foundational technologies in nanotechnology. SNAs have transformed diagnostics and therapeutics, while dip-pen nanolithography enables writing at the nanoscale. He holds over 1,100 patents and is one of the most cited chemists in the world.
