## Summary

Carl-Henric Svanberg served as CEO of Ericsson and later as chairman of BP (now bp) and Volvo Group. At Ericsson, he led the company's transformation during the telecom downturn. As BP chairman, he oversaw the company's response to the Deepwater Horizon oil spill disaster. He became the face of BP during one of the worst environmental disasters in history, famously saying BP cared about 'the small people.'
