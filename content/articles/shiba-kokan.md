## Summary

Shiba Kokan was a Japanese painter and scientist who was one of the first Japanese to study and apply Western scientific methods including Copernican astronomy, perspective drawing, and copperplate engraving. He published works on astronomy showing the heliocentric model and created oil paintings using Western techniques, bridging Japanese and European scientific traditions.
