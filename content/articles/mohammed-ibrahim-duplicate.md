## Canonical person (Mo Ibrahim) — brief context

Mo Ibrahim (born **3 May 1946**) is a **Sudanese-British** businessman and engineer who:

- Worked in telecommunications companies before founding **Celtel** (a mobile operator in multiple African countries).
- Sold Celtel in **2005** (widely reported sale price: **US$3.4B**).
- Founded the **Mo Ibrahim Foundation** (post-sale), which focuses on **governance and leadership** in Africa.
- Initiated the **Mo Ibrahim Prize for Achievement in African Leadership** (announced/initiated in **2007**).
