## Summary

Park Dong-in was a pioneering Korean oceanographer who helped establish marine science research in South Korea. He led early oceanographic surveys of the seas around the Korean Peninsula and helped found the Korea Institute of Ocean Science and Technology. His research was fundamental to Korea's marine resource development.
