## Summary

David Attenborough is a British natural historian and broadcaster who is widely considered a national treasure in Britain. His career spanning seven decades with the BBC has brought the natural world to billions of viewers through landmark series including 'Life on Earth,' 'The Blue Planet,' and 'Planet Earth.' He has visited every continent and is one of the most-traveled people in history. Attenborough is a leading voice on climate change and conservation.
