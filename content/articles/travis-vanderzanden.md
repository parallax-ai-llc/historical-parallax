## Summary

Travis VanderZanden founded Bird, one of the first electric scooter sharing companies, pioneering the micromobility industry. Before Bird, he was an executive at Lyft and Uber. Bird deployed thousands of electric scooters in cities worldwide, disrupting urban transportation. The company went public via SPAC in 2021, though it later faced financial challenges and filed for bankruptcy in 2023.
