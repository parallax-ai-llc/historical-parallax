## Summary

Sunil Bharti Mittal is the founder and chairman of Bharti Enterprises, which operates Airtel, India's largest telecommunications company. He built Airtel from a small telecom operator into a pan-African and Asian telecommunications giant with over 500 million subscribers. He pioneered the managed services model in telecom, outsourcing network operations to reduce costs. His expansion into Africa made Airtel one of the continent's largest mobile operators.
