## Summary

Chanda Prescod-Weinstein is a theoretical physicist working on dark matter, cosmic inflation, and the intersection of physics with social justice. She became one of the first Black American women to earn a PhD in cosmology and particle physics. Her research focuses on ultralight dark matter and early universe dynamics.
