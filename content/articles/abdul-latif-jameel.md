## Summary

Abdul Latif Jameel Group is one of Saudi Arabia's largest private conglomerates, established by the late Abdul Latif Jameel. The company is the exclusive Toyota distributor in Saudi Arabia and several other Middle Eastern countries, making it one of Toyota's largest distributors worldwide. The group has diversified into real estate, finance, media, and renewable energy. The Abdul Latif Jameel Poverty Action Lab (J-PAL) funds development economics research at MIT.
