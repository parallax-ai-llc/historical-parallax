## Summary

Jean-Claude Biver is a legendary Swiss watch industry executive who revived multiple watch brands including Blancpain, Omega, and Hublot. He bought Blancpain from its bankrupt owner and rebuilt it into a prestigious brand before selling to the Swatch Group. He then transformed Hublot into a luxury powerhouse through celebrity marketing and innovative materials. He is considered the most influential marketing genius in the modern luxury watch industry.
