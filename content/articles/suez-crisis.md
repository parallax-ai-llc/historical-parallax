## Summary

The Suez Crisis of 1956 occurred when Britain, France, and Israel invaded Egypt after President Nasser nationalized the Suez Canal. US and Soviet pressure forced withdrawal, humiliating the old colonial powers and establishing the US as the Middle East's dominant Western power.
