## Summary

George Smith is an American chemist who shared the 2018 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the phage display of peptides and antibodies. His development of phage display technology in 1985 enabled the rapid discovery of new therapeutic antibodies and biomolecules. This technique is used in drug development, diagnostics, and biotechnology.
