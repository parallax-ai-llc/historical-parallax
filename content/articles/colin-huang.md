## Summary

Colin Huang (Huang Zheng) founded Pinduoduo (PDD Holdings) in 2015, creating an innovative social e-commerce platform that became China's second-largest online marketplace. Pinduoduo's group-buying model allowed users to get lower prices by shopping together. Its international expansion through Temu disrupted global e-commerce. Huang briefly became Asia's richest person before stepping back from the company.
