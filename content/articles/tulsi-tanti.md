## Summary

Tulsi Tanti founded Suzlon Energy, which became India's largest and the world's fifth-largest wind turbine manufacturer. He entered the wind energy business to lower power costs for his textile company and eventually pivoted entirely to wind. Suzlon expanded globally, acquiring REpower Systems in Germany. Despite financial difficulties and debt restructuring, Suzlon pioneered India's renewable energy industry.
