## Summary

S.H. Raza was an Indian painter who lived in France for six decades, creating abstract paintings inspired by Indian philosophy and nature. His iconic Bindu (point) paintings explore the cosmic symbolism of the dot in Indian thought. He was a founding member of the Progressive Artists' Group and received France's Legion of Honour.
