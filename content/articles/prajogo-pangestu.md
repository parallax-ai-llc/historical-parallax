## Summary

Prajogo Pangestu is an Indonesian billionaire and the wealthiest person in Indonesia. He founded Barito Pacific Group, spanning timber, petrochemicals, and mining. His company Chandra Asri is Indonesia's largest petrochemical company. He holds significant stakes in multiple publicly listed companies and has been a major player in Indonesian industry for decades. His wealth surged with the commodities boom.
