## Summary

John Logie Baird was a Scottish inventor who demonstrated the first working television system in 1926. He gave the world's first public demonstration of television and pioneered the first transatlantic television transmission and the first color television demonstration. Although his mechanical television was eventually superseded by electronic systems, he is recognized as a key pioneer of television technology.
