## Summary

Kano Jigoro founded judo in 1882 by applying scientific principles of leverage, balance, and biomechanics to traditional jujutsu. He systematically analyzed the physics of throwing, grappling, and falling techniques. His scientific approach to martial arts training and physical education influenced sports science worldwide.
