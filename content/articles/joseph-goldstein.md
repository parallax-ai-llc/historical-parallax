## Summary

Joseph Goldstein is an American Buddhist teacher and one of the first to introduce Theravada Buddhist meditation to mainstream America. He co-founded the Insight Meditation Society in Barre, Massachusetts, with Jack Kornfield and Sharon Salzberg. He studied with Anagarika Munindra, S.N. Goenka, and other teachers in India. His books, including The Experience of Insight and Mindfulness, have been widely influential.
