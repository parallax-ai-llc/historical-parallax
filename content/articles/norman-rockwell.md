## Summary

Norman Rockwell was an American painter and illustrator whose Saturday Evening Post covers defined American popular culture for five decades. His paintings of everyday American life, humor, and values, including the Four Freedoms series, became beloved national icons. His work bridges illustration and fine art with extraordinary narrative skill.
