## Summary
Jacob Leon Rubenstein, known as Jack Ruby, was the Dallas nightclub owner who fatally shot Lee Harvey Oswald on November 24, 1963, two days after Oswald allegedly assassinated President Kennedy. He was convicted of murder but died of a pulmonary embolism before a new trial.
