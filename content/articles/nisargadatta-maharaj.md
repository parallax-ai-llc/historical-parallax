## Summary

Nisargadatta Maharaj was a simple shopkeeper in Mumbai who became one of the most important Advaita Vedanta teachers of the 20th century. His dialogues with visitors were compiled in the book I Am That, which has become a spiritual classic. He taught that the sense of 'I Am' is the doorway to the absolute reality. His direct, uncompromising teaching style attracted seekers from around the world.
