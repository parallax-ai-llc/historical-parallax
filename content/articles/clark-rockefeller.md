## Summary
Christian Karl Gerhartsreiter, known as 'Clark Rockefeller,' was a German impostor who assumed multiple identities in the US, ultimately claiming to be a member of the Rockefeller family. He was convicted of kidnapping his daughter and the murder of his landlord's son.
