## Summary

Samuel C.C. Ting is an American physicist who shared the 1976 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of the J/psi meson, a subatomic particle that confirmed the existence of the charm quark. His discovery, made simultaneously with Burton Richter, was called the 'November Revolution' in particle physics and supported the quark model of particle physics.
