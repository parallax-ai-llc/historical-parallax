## Summary

Umm Kulthum was an Egyptian singer regarded as the greatest vocalist in Arabic music history. Her monthly radio concerts drew audiences of millions across the Arab world, and her performances could last over four hours. She became a symbol of Arab cultural identity and Egyptian national pride.
