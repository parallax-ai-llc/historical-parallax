## Summary

Lee Soo-sung is a Korean marine engineer who contributed to the development of offshore engineering technology at major Korean shipbuilders. His research on floating production storage and offloading (FPSO) vessels and offshore platforms helped Korea become a world leader in offshore engineering and construction.
