## Summary

Miyoshi Manabu was a Japanese botanist and plant physiologist who studied the mechanisms of plant defense against fungi and made important contributions to mycology. He established the botanical garden at the University of Tokyo and documented Japanese cherry blossom varieties, preserving knowledge of Japan's iconic flowering trees.
