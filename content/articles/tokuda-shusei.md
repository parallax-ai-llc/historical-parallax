## Summary

Tokuda Shusei was a Japanese seismologist who advanced earthquake prediction research and developed improved methods for seismic analysis. His work on understanding the mechanisms of deep earthquakes and plate tectonics contributed to Japan's world-leading earthquake preparedness systems.
