## Summary

Chen Tianqiao founded Shanda Interactive Entertainment, which became one of China's first major online gaming companies. He became China's youngest billionaire at age 31. Shanda pioneered the free-to-play gaming model with microtransactions in China. He later pivoted to neuroscience philanthropy, founding the Tianqiao and Chrissy Chen Institute at Caltech. His transition from tech mogul to neuroscience philanthropist reflects China's evolving entrepreneurial landscape.
