## Summary

Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan was a Pakistani vocalist widely regarded as the greatest qawwali singer of all time. His powerful voice and innovative fusion of traditional Sufi devotional music with Western forms brought qawwali to global audiences. His collaborations with Peter Gabriel and Eddie Vedder introduced him to millions worldwide.
