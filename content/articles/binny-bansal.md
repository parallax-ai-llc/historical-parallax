## Summary

Binny Bansal co-founded Flipkart, India's largest e-commerce company, with Sachin Bansal (no relation). Flipkart revolutionized online shopping in India by building logistics infrastructure and the cash-on-delivery payment model. Walmart acquired Flipkart for $16 billion in 2018, the largest e-commerce acquisition in history. Binny served as CEO before departing after the Walmart acquisition.
