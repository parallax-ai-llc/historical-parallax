## Summary

Lee Seo-hyun is the president of Samsung Welfare Foundation and a member of the Samsung founding family. She oversees the family's philanthropic activities and fashion businesses including Samsung C&T's fashion division. As the daughter of Lee Kun-hee, she represents the Samsung family's involvement in culture, fashion, and social responsibility. She sits on the board of Samsung Life Insurance.
