## Summary
Ayman Mohammed Rabie al-Zawahiri was an Egyptian physician who became the leader of al-Qaeda after Osama bin Laden's death. He was a co-founder of Egyptian Islamic Jihad. He was killed in a US drone strike in Kabul, Afghanistan on July 31, 2022.
