## Summary
Harry Alonzo Longabaugh, known as the Sundance Kid, was an American outlaw who was a member of Butch Cassidy's Wild Bunch gang. He and Cassidy fled to South America after the Pinkerton Agency tracked them. They were reportedly killed in a shootout in Bolivia.
