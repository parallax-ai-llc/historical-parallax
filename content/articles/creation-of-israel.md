## Summary

The State of Israel was declared on May 14, 1948, fulfilling the Zionist vision of a Jewish homeland. The declaration triggered immediate invasion by Arab neighbors. Israel's creation and the resulting Palestinian displacement remain among the most contentious issues in world politics.
