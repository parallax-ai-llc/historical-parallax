## Summary

Sergey Galitskiy founded Magnit, Russia's largest food retailer with over 27,000 stores. He built the company from a single store in Krasnodar into one of the most successful retail operations in Russia. He sold his controlling stake to VTB Bank in 2018 and redirected his attention to FC Krasnodar football club and its world-class training academy. He is one of the few Russian billionaires who built wealth through retail rather than natural resources.
