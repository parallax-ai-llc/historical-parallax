## Summary

The Lewis and Clark Expedition (1804-1806) explored the newly acquired Louisiana Purchase territory to the Pacific Ocean. The Corps of Discovery traveled 8,000 miles, documenting hundreds of plant and animal species, and establishing US claims to the Pacific Northwest.
