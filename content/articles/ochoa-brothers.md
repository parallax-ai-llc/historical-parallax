## Summary
The Ochoa brothers — Jorge Luis, Juan David, and Fabio — were Colombian drug lords and founding members of the Medellín Cartel alongside Pablo Escobar. They surrendered in the early 1990s and received reduced sentences. They later claimed to have gone legitimate.
