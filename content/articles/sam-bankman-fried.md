## Summary
Samuel Benjamin Bankman-Fried was the founder of FTX, a cryptocurrency exchange that collapsed in November 2022 in one of the largest financial frauds in history. He was convicted of seven counts of fraud and conspiracy for misusing $8 billion in customer funds. He was sentenced to 25 years in 2024.
