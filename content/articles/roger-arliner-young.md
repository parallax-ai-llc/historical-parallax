## Summary

Roger Arliner Young was the first African American woman to receive a PhD in zoology. She studied the effects of radiation on sea urchin eggs and the structures controlling salt concentration in paramecia. Despite facing severe discrimination and personal hardships, she contributed important research to marine biology and zoology.
