## Summary

Tetsuya Theodore Fujita was a Japanese-American meteorologist who created the Fujita Scale (F-Scale) for rating tornado intensity in 1971. Known as 'Mr. Tornado,' he also discovered microbursts, dangerous wind shear events that had caused multiple aircraft crashes. His microburst discovery led to improved aviation safety, saving thousands of lives.
