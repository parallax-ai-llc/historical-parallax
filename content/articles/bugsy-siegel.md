## Summary
Benjamin 'Bugsy' Siegel was an American mobster who was one of the founders of Murder, Inc. He is credited with developing Las Vegas as a gambling destination through the construction of the Flamingo Hotel. He was assassinated in Beverly Hills in 1947, allegedly for cost overruns on the Flamingo.
