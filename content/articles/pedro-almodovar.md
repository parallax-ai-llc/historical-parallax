## Summary

Pedro Almodovar is a Spanish filmmaker whose vibrant, emotionally intense films explore desire, identity, and family with a distinctive visual flair. All About My Mother, Talk to Her, and Volver are masterworks of modern European cinema. He is the most internationally celebrated Spanish filmmaker since Bunuel.
