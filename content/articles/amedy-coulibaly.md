## Summary
Amedy Coulibaly was a French-born terrorist who carried out the Hypercacher kosher supermarket siege in Paris on January 9, 2015, killing 4 hostages. His attack was coordinated with the Charlie Hebdo shooting by the Kouachi brothers. He was killed by police.
