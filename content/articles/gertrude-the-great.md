## Summary

Gertrude the Great was a German Benedictine nun and mystic at the convent of Helfta who is one of the great mystical writers of the Church. Her Legatus Memorialis Abundantiae Divinae Pietatis (The Herald of Divine Love) records her visions and mystical experiences with extraordinary literary skill. She was one of the first to promote devotion to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. She is the patron saint of the West Indies.
