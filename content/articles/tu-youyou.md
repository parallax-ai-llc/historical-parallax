## Summary

Tu Youyou is a Chinese pharmaceutical chemist who won the 2015 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for her discovery of artemisinin, a drug therapy for malaria that has saved millions of lives. She found the antimalarial compound in sweet wormwood (Artemisia annua) using clues from traditional Chinese medicine. She was the first Chinese Nobel laureate in physiology or medicine and the first Chinese woman to win any Nobel Prize.
