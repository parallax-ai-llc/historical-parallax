## Summary

Baudhayana was an ancient Indian mathematician who authored the Baudhayana Sulba Sutra around 800 BC, which contains the earliest known statement of the Pythagorean theorem, predating Pythagoras by several centuries. He also calculated the square root of 2 accurately to five decimal places and described geometric constructions for altars.
