## Summary

Kim Seung-youn is the chairman of Hanwha Group, one of South Korea's ten largest conglomerates with interests in explosives, defense, solar energy, petrochemicals, and finance. He transformed Hanwha from an explosives company into a diversified conglomerate. Hanwha Solutions became one of the world's largest solar panel manufacturers. He was convicted of assault and embezzlement but received presidential pardons.
