## Summary

RJ Scaringe is the founder and CEO of Rivian Automotive, the electric vehicle startup backed by Amazon and Ford. He founded the company in 2009, initially working in stealth mode for nearly a decade. Rivian launched the R1T, the first mass-produced electric pickup truck, in 2021. The company went public in 2021 in the sixth-largest U.S. IPO ever, briefly reaching a market cap above $100 billion.
