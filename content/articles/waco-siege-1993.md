## Summary
The Waco siege was a 51-day standoff between the Branch Davidian religious sect led by David Koresh and US federal agents from February 28 to April 19, 1993. The siege ended in a fire that killed 76 Branch Davidians including 25 children. It became a rallying point for anti-government movements.
