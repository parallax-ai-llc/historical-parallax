## Overview

On Friday the 13th of November, 2015, a cell of **Islamic State (ISIS)** terrorists launched coordinated attacks across Paris.
**Stade de France:** Suicide bombers detonated outside the national stadium during a France-Germany soccer match (President Hollande was attending).
**Caf√©s:** Gunmen sprayed bullets at people drinking on terraces in the 10th and 11th arrondissements.
**The Bataclan:** The deadliest event. Three gunmen stormed the Bataclan concert hall during an Eagles of Death Metal concert, taking hostages and executing them.

---

## The Bataclan Massacre

1,500 people were at the concert.
The terrorists entered firing AK-47s.
People lay on the floor pretending to be dead. The attackers shot into the piles of bodies.
Siege lasted over 2 hours. Police (BRI) stormed the building, killing the terrorists. **90 people died** in the theater alone.

---

## Aftermath

**"Fluctuat nec mergitur":**
The motto of Paris ("She is tossed by the waves but does not sink") became a viral symbol of defiance.
**State of Emergency:**
France declared a state of emergency that lasted for two years.
**War:**
France intensified airstrikes on ISIS in Raqqa, Syria.
**Belgian Connection:**
The attacks were planned in the Molenbeek district of Brussels, highlighting intelligence failures in sharing data across EU borders.
