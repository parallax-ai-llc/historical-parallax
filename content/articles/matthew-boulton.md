## Summary

Matthew Boulton partnered with James Watt to manufacture and market the improved steam engine, making steam power commercially viable across Britain and beyond. His Soho Manufactory in Birmingham was one of the most advanced factories of the 18th century. He also introduced modern coinage technology and was a leader of the Lunar Society.
