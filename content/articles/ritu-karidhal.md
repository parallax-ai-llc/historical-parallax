## Summary

Ritu Karidhal was the mission director of India's Chandrayaan-2 lunar mission and deputy operations director of the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan). Known as the 'Rocket Woman of India,' she played a crucial role in India's successful Mars mission in 2013, making India the first Asian country to reach Mars orbit.
