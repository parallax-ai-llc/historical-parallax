## Summary

Jürgen Moltmann was a German Reformed theologian best known for his Theology of Hope, which reimagined Christian eschatology in terms of promise and transformation. His work The Crucified God explored God's solidarity with human suffering. He was one of the most important Protestant theologians of the late 20th century. His political theology influenced liberation and ecological theology worldwide.
