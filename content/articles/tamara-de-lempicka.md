## Summary

Tamara de Lempicka was a Polish Art Deco painter known for sleek, glamorous portraits. Her style combines Cubist geometry with sensuous finish, creating iconic Jazz Age imagery. Rediscovered in the 1970s, she became a symbol of Art Deco style and female empowerment.
