## Summary

Uğur Şahin co-founded BioNTech with his wife Özlem Türeci and developed the BNT162b2 mRNA COVID-19 vaccine in partnership with Pfizer. Their vaccine was the first mRNA vaccine authorized for emergency use and has been administered billions of times worldwide. Their rapid vaccine development demonstrated the potential of mRNA technology.
