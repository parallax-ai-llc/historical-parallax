## Overview
Beginning as a student demonstration, the uprising quickly spread across Hungary. The new government declared its intention to withdraw from the Warsaw Pact and re-establish free elections.

## Suppression
Soviet tanks rolled into Budapest to crush the revolution. Thousands were killed, and over 200,000 Hungarians fled as refugees. The Wests lack of intervention highlighted the reality of Cold War spheres of influence.
