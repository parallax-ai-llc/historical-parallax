## Summary

George Gamow was a Soviet-American physicist and cosmologist who made major contributions to nuclear physics, cosmology, and molecular biology. He predicted the cosmic microwave background radiation and developed the Big Bang nucleosynthesis theory. He also proposed how the genetic code works through the RNA Tie Club. Gamow was an acclaimed science popularizer through his 'Mr. Tompkins' book series.
