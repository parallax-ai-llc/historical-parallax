## Summary
The Lufthansa heist was a robbery at the Lufthansa cargo terminal at John F. Kennedy International Airport on December 11, 1978. The thieves stole $5 million in cash and $875,000 in jewelry. It was the largest cash robbery in American history at the time. The robbery was connected to the Lucchese crime family.
