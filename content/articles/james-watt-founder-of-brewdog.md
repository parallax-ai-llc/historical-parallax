## Summary

James Watt co-founded BrewDog in 2007, building it into one of the world's most successful craft beer brands through provocative marketing and equity crowdfunding. BrewDog pioneered the 'Equity for Punks' crowdfunding model, raising over £90 million from over 200,000 investors. The company's anti-establishment branding disrupted the British beer industry. He faced criticism after allegations of a toxic workplace culture emerged in 2021.
