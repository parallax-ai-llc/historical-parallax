## Summary

Nassef Sawiris is an Egyptian billionaire and CEO of OCI N.V., one of the world's largest nitrogen fertilizer producers. He is the son of Onsi Sawiris, who built Orascom, and brother of Naguib Sawiris. He holds significant stakes in Adidas and other global companies. He is the wealthiest person in Egypt and one of the richest in Africa.
