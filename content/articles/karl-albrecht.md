## Summary

Karl Albrecht co-founded Aldi with his brother Theo, building the pioneering discount grocery chain that operates over 10,000 stores worldwide. He ran Aldi Süd, which expanded aggressively in Germany, the UK, Australia, and the United States. The brothers' radical approach to low-cost retailing — minimal product range, no frills, and extreme efficiency — revolutionized grocery shopping worldwide. He was the richest person in Germany at the time of his death.
