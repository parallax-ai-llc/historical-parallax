## Summary

Lucie Rie was an Austrian-born British studio potter known for her refined, elegant vessels with their distinctive thin forms and subtle glazes. Her work combined Viennese modernism with Japanese ceramics influence. She is considered one of the most important studio potters of the 20th century.
