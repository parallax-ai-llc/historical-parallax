## Summary

Wei Baoming is a Chinese marine scientist who made significant contributions to fish genetics and breeding at the Chinese Academy of Fishery Sciences. His work on selective breeding of commercially important fish species has improved aquaculture productivity in China, the world's largest aquaculture producer.
