## Summary

George Whitefield was an English-American evangelist and one of the founders of Methodism and the evangelical movement. He was the most famous preacher of the 18th century, drawing enormous crowds on both sides of the Atlantic. He was a key figure in the First Great Awakening in America. Benjamin Franklin estimated he could be heard by 30,000 people at once.
