## Summary

While Park Won-soon was primarily a lawyer and politician who served as Mayor of Seoul, he promoted Seoul as a smart city and advanced urban technology innovation. Under his leadership, Seoul became a global model for urban technology implementation including open data initiatives and smart city infrastructure.
