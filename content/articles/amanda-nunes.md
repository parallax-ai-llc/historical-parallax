## Summary

Amanda Nunes is the greatest female MMA fighter ever, becoming the first woman to hold two UFC titles simultaneously.

## Background

Nunes rose through Brazilian and international MMA circuits before joining the UFC, where she became known for power striking, clinch strength, and an aggressive finishing style.

## Championships and Notable Bouts

- UFC Women’s Bantamweight Champion and UFC Women’s Featherweight Champion (simultaneous champion status).
- Defeated multiple former champions and top contenders across two weight classes.
- Regained the bantamweight title after a brief loss, highlighting career-long resilience and tactical adjustments.

## Retirement and Impact

Nunes is widely cited for elevating the visibility of women’s MMA and for setting a high benchmark for multi-division championship performance.
