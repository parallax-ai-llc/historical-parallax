## Summary

Tracey Emin is a British artist known for her raw confessional works exploring personal trauma and female experience. Her installation My Bed and the tent Everyone I Have Ever Slept With 1963-1995 made her one of the most famous and controversial Young British Artists. She was appointed Professor of Drawing at the Royal Academy.
