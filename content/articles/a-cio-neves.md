## Background

Aécio Neves is a Brazilian politician associated with the Brazilian Social Democracy Party (PSDB). He became a nationally prominent figure through executive and legislative roles and later ran for president, making him a central actor in Brazil’s polarized political environment during the 2010s.

At a high level, Neves’ public profile has been shaped by:

- Executive leadership at the state level (often discussed in relation to Minas Gerais).

- National electoral politics, including a highly competitive presidential race.
