## Summary

Mustafa Suleyman is the co-founder of DeepMind and the CEO of Microsoft AI. He co-founded DeepMind with Demis Hassabis and Shane Legg in 2010. After Google acquired DeepMind, he led its applied AI division. He later founded Inflection AI and launched Pi, a personal AI assistant, before joining Microsoft as CEO of its consumer AI division. His book 'The Coming Wave' explored the implications of AI and biotechnology.
