## Summary

Judea Pearl is an Israeli-American computer scientist and philosopher known for his development of Bayesian networks and the structural theory of causation. His work on causal inference and probabilistic reasoning transformed artificial intelligence and statistics. His book 'The Book of Why' popularized causal reasoning. Pearl received the Turing Award in 2011.
