## Summary

Ruth Handler created the Barbie doll in 1959, revolutionizing the toy industry by creating the first adult-figured fashion doll for children. She co-founded Mattel and made Barbie the best-selling doll in history. She later invented a more realistic breast prosthesis for mastectomy patients after her own breast cancer surgery.
