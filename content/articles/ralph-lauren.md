## Summary

Ralph Lauren built a global fashion empire around his brand of American luxury lifestyle. Starting with neckties in 1967, he expanded into a full range of clothing, accessories, fragrances, and home furnishings. The Polo Ralph Lauren brand became synonymous with American aspirational style. He is one of the wealthiest people in the fashion industry and his company generates over $6 billion in annual revenue.
