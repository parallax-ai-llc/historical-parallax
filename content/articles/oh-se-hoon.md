## Summary

While Oh Se-hoon is primarily a politician serving as Mayor of Seoul, his policies have promoted Seoul as a global smart city. Under his leadership, Seoul has implemented advanced urban technologies including smart transportation, digital governance, and IoT-based city management systems.
