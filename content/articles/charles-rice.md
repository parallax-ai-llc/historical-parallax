## Summary

Charles Rice is an American virologist who shared the 2020 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for the discovery of the Hepatitis C virus. He provided the final proof that Hepatitis C virus alone could cause hepatitis by demonstrating that a modified RNA clone of the virus could replicate and cause disease. His work was crucial for developing antiviral treatments.
