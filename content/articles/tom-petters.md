## Summary
Thomas Joseph Petters was an American businessman who ran a $3.65 billion Ponzi scheme through his company, Petters Group Worldwide. He defrauded investors by claiming to buy consumer electronics for resale. He was convicted and sentenced to 50 years in federal prison in 2010.
