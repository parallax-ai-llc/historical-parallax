## Summary

Georg Philipp Telemann was the most prolific composer in history and the most famous German composer during his lifetime, surpassing even Bach and Handel in contemporary reputation. His enormous output of over 3,000 works spans every genre of the era. His music combines German, French, Italian, and Polish influences.
