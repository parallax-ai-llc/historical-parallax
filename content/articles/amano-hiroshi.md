## Summary

Amano Hiroshi is a Japanese physicist and engineer who contributed to the development of the efficient blue LED. Working with Isamu Akasaki, he grew high-quality gallium nitride crystals using a novel buffer layer technique and achieved p-type GaN doping. He shared the 2014 Nobel Prize in Physics.
