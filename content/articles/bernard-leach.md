## Summary

Bernard Leach was a British studio potter who is considered the father of British studio pottery. His A Potter's Book became the most influential text in ceramics, and his Leach Pottery in St Ives established the model for studio pottery worldwide. His work bridging Japanese and British ceramic traditions influenced generations of potters.
