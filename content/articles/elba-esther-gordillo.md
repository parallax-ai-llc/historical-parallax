## Summary

Mexican former leader of the teachers' union (SNTE) who was arrested for embezzling $200 million in union funds. She spent lavishly on luxury goods including Neiman Marcus shopping sprees. One of Mexico's most powerful and corrupt union bosses.

## Power and Political Role (High Level)

- **Union influence:** As a long-time leader within Mexico's teachers' union (SNTE), Gordillo wielded major influence over labor politics, education policy debates, and electoral coalitions.
- **Political brokerage:** Large unions in Mexico have historically acted as power centers; her career is often discussed in terms of patronage networks and negotiated political support.

## Corruption Allegations and Legal Case

- **Embezzlement accusations:** Authorities alleged diversion of union funds for personal luxury spending and private benefit.
- **High-profile arrest:** Her detention was widely publicized and interpreted by many observers as both an anti-corruption signal and a reflection of shifting political alliances.
- **Procedural end:** The legal process ultimately concluded with charges being dismissed on procedural grounds, leaving a contested public narrative about guilt and accountability.
