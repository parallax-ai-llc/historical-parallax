## Summary

Said Nursi was a Kurdish-Turkish Islamic scholar who wrote the Risale-i Nur, a comprehensive Quran commentary that sought to demonstrate the compatibility of Islam with modern science and reason. He was exiled and imprisoned by the Turkish Republic for decades. His Nur movement, emphasizing education and non-political spiritual renewal, attracted millions of followers. He is one of the most influential Islamic thinkers of the 20th century in Turkey.
