## Summary

Ray Dalio founded Bridgewater Associates in 1975, building it into the world's largest hedge fund with over $150 billion in assets under management. He developed the concept of 'radical transparency' in corporate management and the risk parity investment strategy. His book 'Principles' became a bestselling guide to life and management. He stepped down from day-to-day management in 2022.
