## Summary

Kosovar Albanian guerrilla commander who was twice acquitted of war crimes by the ICTY. He later became prime minister of Kosovo despite Serbia's continued allegations against him.

## Roles and Public Profile

Haradinaj is a Kosovo Albanian politician and former Kosovo Liberation Army (KLA) commander who served two non-consecutive terms as Prime Minister of Kosovo (2004–2005 and 2017–2020). In party politics he has been closely associated with the Alliance for the Future of Kosovo (AAK), which he led for many years.

## ICTY Indictment and Trials (High-Level)

- After entering politics, he resigned from government after being indicted by the ICTY in connection with alleged war crimes and crimes against humanity during the Kosovo conflict.
- He was acquitted of the charges in 2008.
- Following an appeal, a partial retrial was ordered and concluded with a second acquittal in 2012.

## Notes on Labeling

Although he has been repeatedly accused by opponents and prosecutors, the ICTY proceedings referenced above ended in acquittals. This distinction matters when summarizing his career and legacy.
