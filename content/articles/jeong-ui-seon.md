## Summary

Jeong Ui-seon (Euisun Chung) is a Korean automotive engineer who serves as Executive Chairman of Hyundai Motor Group. Under his technical leadership, Hyundai has invested heavily in electric vehicles, hydrogen fuel cell technology, and autonomous driving, positioning Korea as a leader in future mobility technology.
