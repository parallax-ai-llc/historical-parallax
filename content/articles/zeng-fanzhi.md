## Summary

Zeng Fanzhi is one of China most important contemporary painters. His Mask series of figures in Western suits wearing white masks critiques Chinese society rapid transformation. His Last Supper sold for over 23 million dollars making it the most expensive contemporary Asian artwork at the time.
