## Summary

Wang Xing is the co-founder and CEO of Meituan, China's largest food delivery and local services platform. He is known as 'China's serial entrepreneur' for founding multiple companies before Meituan, including Fanfou (China's first Twitter clone) and Xiaonei (China's Facebook clone). Meituan went public in 2018 and expanded from group buying into delivery, travel, and ride-hailing. The company serves hundreds of millions of users daily.
