## Summary

Lee Tai-young was the first female lawyer and judge in South Korea. While not a scientist, she pioneered legal reform for women's rights and family law in Korea. She founded the Korea Legal Aid Center for Family Relations and received the Ramon Magsaysay Award for her work.
