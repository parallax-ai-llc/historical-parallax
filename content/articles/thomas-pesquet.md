## Summary

Thomas Pesquet is a French astronaut who has spent over 396 days in space across two missions. He was the first French commander of the ISS in 2021 and has become one of the most prominent astronauts on social media with millions of followers. He flew on both Soyuz and SpaceX Crew Dragon spacecraft.
