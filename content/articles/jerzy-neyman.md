## Summary

Jerzy Neyman, with Egon Pearson, developed the Neyman-Pearson lemma and the framework for hypothesis testing that became the standard method in scientific research. He also developed confidence intervals and contributed to the theory of sampling surveys. His work provided the mathematical foundation for how scientists draw conclusions from data.
