## Summary

Anandi Gopal Joshi was one of the first Indian women to earn a medical degree, graduating from Woman's Medical College of Pennsylvania in 1886. She traveled to America at age 18 to study medicine, overcoming immense societal opposition. Tragically, she died of tuberculosis at age 21, shortly after returning to India, but she inspired generations of Indian women doctors.
