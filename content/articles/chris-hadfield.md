## Summary

Chris Hadfield became the first Canadian to walk in space (2001) and the first Canadian commander of the ISS (2013). He gained worldwide fame by performing David Bowie's Space Oddity aboard the ISS, a video viewed over 50 million times. His social media presence from space inspired millions about space exploration.
