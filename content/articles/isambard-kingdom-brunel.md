## Summary

Isambard Kingdom Brunel was a British civil and mechanical engineer who is considered one of the greatest engineers in history. He designed the Great Western Railway, several famous steamships including the SS Great Britain and SS Great Eastern, and numerous bridges and tunnels. His innovative designs pushed the boundaries of engineering in the Victorian era. In a 2002 BBC poll, he was voted the second greatest Briton of all time.
