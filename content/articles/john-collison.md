## Summary

John Collison co-founded Stripe with his brother Patrick in 2010 and serves as president of the company. He became the world's youngest self-made billionaire at age 26. Stripe grew into a critical payment infrastructure company processing hundreds of billions in transactions. Born in Ireland, he dropped out of Harvard to build Stripe into one of the most successful fintech companies in the world.
