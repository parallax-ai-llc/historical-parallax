## Summary

Yasujiro Ozu was a Japanese filmmaker whose serene, contemplative films about family life are considered among cinema's greatest achievements. His masterpiece Tokyo Story is regularly voted the best film ever made in directors' polls. His distinctive low camera angle, static shots, and pillow shots create a uniquely meditative visual style.
