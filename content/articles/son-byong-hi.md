## Summary

Son Byong-hi was the third leader of Cheondogyo (formerly Donghak) and a key figure in the Korean independence movement. He reorganized Donghak as Cheondogyo (Religion of the Heavenly Way) in 1905. He was one of the 33 signatories and a main organizer of the March 1st Movement for Korean independence in 1919. He was arrested and imprisoned, dying shortly after his release.
