## Summary

Naguib Sawiris is an Egyptian billionaire who built Orascom Telecom into one of the largest mobile operators in the Middle East and Africa. He sold Orascom Telecom's assets for billions and invested in media, mining, and real estate. He also founded La Mancha Resources, a gold mining company. He is known for his outspoken political views and once offered to buy an island to house refugees.
