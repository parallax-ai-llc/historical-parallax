## Summary

Amy Carmichael was an Irish missionary who spent 55 years in India without a furlough. She founded the Dohnavur Fellowship in Tamil Nadu to rescue children from temple prostitution. She wrote 35 books, including Things as They Are, which exposed the realities of Hindu temple practices. She was one of the most influential Christian missionaries of the 20th century.
