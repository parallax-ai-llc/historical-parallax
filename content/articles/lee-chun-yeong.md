## Summary

Lee Chun-yeong was a Korean virologist who made significant contributions to the control of infectious diseases in South Korea. He helped establish modern virology research in Korea and contributed to vaccination programs that greatly reduced childhood diseases. His research on hemorrhagic fever was particularly important for Korean public health.
