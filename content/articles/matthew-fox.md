## Summary

Matthew Fox is an American priest and theologian who founded Creation Spirituality, a movement that seeks to integrate mysticism, social justice, and environmental awareness. He was expelled from the Dominican Order by Cardinal Ratzinger (later Pope Benedict XVI) and later became an Episcopal priest. His books, including Original Blessing and The Coming of the Cosmic Christ, challenged the doctrine of original sin and promoted a creation-centered theology.
