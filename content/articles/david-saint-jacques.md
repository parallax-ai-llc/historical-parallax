## Summary

David Saint-Jacques is a Canadian astronaut with a unique combination of qualifications: MD, PhD in astrophysics, and engineering degree. He flew to the ISS in 2018-2019, conducted two spacewalks, and performed the first Canadian use of the Canadarm2 for astronaut support during a spacewalk. He also practiced medicine in Inuit communities in northern Canada.
