## Summary

Cai Lun is credited with inventing modern papermaking in 105 CE, creating a process using tree bark, hemp, rags, and fishing nets. While paper existed before him, he standardized and improved the manufacturing process, making paper widely available. Paper is considered one of the Four Great Inventions of China and one of the most important inventions in human history.
