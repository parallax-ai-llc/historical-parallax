## Summary

Ajay Bhatt led the team at Intel that created the Universal Serial Bus (USB) standard, one of the most widely adopted interface technologies in computing history. USB simplified the connection of peripherals to computers, replacing a confusing array of different ports. He also contributed to the development of PCI Express and other Intel platform technologies.
