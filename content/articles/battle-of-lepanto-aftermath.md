## Summary

The aftermath of Lepanto saw 12,000 Christian galley slaves freed and Ottoman naval expansion checked. However, the Ottomans rebuilt their fleet within a year, and Venice eventually ceded Cyprus. The battle's cultural impact exceeded its strategic importance.
