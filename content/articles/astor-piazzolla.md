## Summary

Astor Piazzolla was an Argentine tango composer and bandoneon player who revolutionized traditional tango by incorporating elements of jazz and classical music into what he called nuevo tango. His compositions including Libertango, Adios Nonino, and the Five Tango Sensations transformed tango from dance music into a sophisticated concert genre.
