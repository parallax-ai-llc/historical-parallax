## Summary

Jagannatha Samrat was an Indian astronomer who worked at the court of Maharaja Jai Singh II and translated Euclid's Elements and Ptolemy's Almagest from Arabic into Sanskrit. His translations helped preserve and transmit mathematical knowledge in India. He also contributed to astronomical observations at the Jantar Mantar observatories.
