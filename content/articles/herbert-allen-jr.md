## Summary

Herbert Allen Jr. is the president of Allen & Company, an investment bank known for its annual Sun Valley Conference, the most exclusive gathering of media and technology moguls in the world. The conference has been the site of major media deals including Disney's acquisition of ABC and the AOL-Time Warner merger. Despite its small size, Allen & Company has been one of the most influential dealmakers in media.
