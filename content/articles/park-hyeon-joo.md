## Summary

Park Hyeon-joo founded Mirae Asset Financial Group, South Korea's largest independent financial services company. He pioneered the mutual fund industry in South Korea and expanded into global asset management. Mirae Asset became a major global investor with assets under management exceeding $500 billion. He transformed South Korea's investment landscape from bank-dominated savings to active asset management.
