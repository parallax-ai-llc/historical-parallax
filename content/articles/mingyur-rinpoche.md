## Summary

Yongey Mingyur Rinpoche is a Tibetan Buddhist teacher and author known for integrating traditional Buddhist practice with modern neuroscience. He has participated in scientific studies of meditation at the University of Wisconsin, becoming one of the most studied meditators in history. His books, including The Joy of Living, make Tibetan Buddhist meditation accessible to Western audiences. In 2011, he undertook a wandering retreat for four years.
