## Summary

Jöns Jacob Berzelius was a Swedish chemist who is considered one of the founders of modern chemistry. He developed the modern system of chemical notation using element symbols and determined the atomic weights of many elements. He discovered the elements cerium, selenium, silicon, and thorium. Berzelius also coined many important chemistry terms including catalysis, polymer, isomer, and allotrope.
