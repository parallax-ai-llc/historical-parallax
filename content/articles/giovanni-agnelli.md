## Summary

Giovanni Agnelli co-founded Fiat (Fabbrica Italiana Automobili Torino) in 1899, building it into Italy's largest industrial company. Under his leadership, Fiat became one of the largest automobile manufacturers in the world. He also expanded into aviation, publishing, and banking. The Agnelli family became Italy's most powerful industrial dynasty, controlling a vast business empire for over a century.
