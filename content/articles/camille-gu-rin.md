## Summary

Camille Guérin co-developed the BCG vaccine against tuberculosis with Albert Calmette through 13 years of meticulous bacterial subculturing. His veterinary expertise was crucial in attenuating the bovine tuberculosis bacillus. The BCG vaccine has been administered to billions of people worldwide and remains in use today.
