## Case Overview

Jack Douglas Teixeira is an American former airman in the Massachusetts Air National Guard who became known for the 2022–2023 leak of U.S. classified defense and intelligence documents. In April 2023, U.S. federal authorities arrested him after an investigation into the removal and online disclosure of hundreds of classified Pentagon documents.

## Key Facts (High Level)

- **Affiliation:** Massachusetts Air National Guard, 102nd Intelligence Wing.
- **Role (reported):** Cyber Transport Systems journeyman.
- **Clearance (reported):** Held a Top Secret security clearance as part of his duties.
- **Leak channel:** Shared material within private Discord communities; the incident drew broad media attention after images of documents spread beyond the original server(s).
- **Legal outcome (public reporting):** He pleaded guilty to multiple counts related to willful retention and transmission of national defense information, and was later sentenced in U.S. federal court.

## Significance

The Teixeira leak is frequently cited in discussions about:

- **Information security controls:** How broad access to sensitive networks can create risk even for relatively junior personnel.
- **Insider-threat detection:** The importance of monitoring, auditing, and behavioral signals in classified environments.
- **Online diffusion dynamics:** How small-group sharing can quickly escalate into global dissemination once images are reposted.
