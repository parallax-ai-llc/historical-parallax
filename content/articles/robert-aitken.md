## Summary

Robert Aitken was an American Zen teacher who co-founded the Diamond Sangha in Hawaii and was a pioneering figure in American Zen Buddhism. He studied under Yasutani Hakuun and Nakagawa Soen. He was also a prominent peace activist, helping found the Buddhist Peace Fellowship. His books, including Taking the Path of Zen, are widely used introductions to Zen practice. He ordained teachers who now lead communities across the Pacific region.
