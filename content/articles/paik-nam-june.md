## Summary

Paik Nam-June was a Korean-American artist widely considered the father of video art. He was the first artist to use television as an artistic medium, creating interactive video sculptures and satellite art broadcasts. His work anticipated YouTube, video streaming, and the internet age by decades. He coined the term 'electronic superhighway' in 1974.
