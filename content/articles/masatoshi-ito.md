## Summary

Masatoshi Ito founded Ito-Yokado and built the Seven & i Holdings retail empire, which includes 7-Eleven, the world's largest convenience store chain. He brought the 7-Eleven franchise from the U.S. to Japan in 1973 and transformed it into a far more sophisticated operation. His retail innovations made Japan's convenience store culture one of the most advanced in the world. Seven & i Holdings became one of the world's largest retail groups.
