## Summary

Kim Taek-jin is a Korean software engineer who founded NCSOFT, one of the world's largest online game companies. His engineering of massively multiplayer online games (MMORPGs) like Lineage pioneered the online gaming business model. He has invested heavily in AI research through NCSOFT's AI Center.
