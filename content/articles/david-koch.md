## Summary

David Koch was an American businessman and philanthropist who served as executive vice president of Koch Industries. Along with his brother Charles, he was one of the most influential political donors in American history, funding libertarian and conservative causes. He was also a major philanthropist, donating over $1.3 billion to cancer research, medical centers, education, and cultural institutions. He ran as the Libertarian Party's vice presidential candidate in 1980.
