## Summary

Hwang Kyu-baek is a Korean abstract painter known for works that fuse Korean calligraphic tradition with Western abstract expressionism. His powerful brushwork and muted palette reflect the Korean aesthetic of stillness and restraint while engaging with international abstract movements.
