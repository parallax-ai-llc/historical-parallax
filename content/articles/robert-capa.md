## Summary

Robert Capa was a Hungarian-American photojournalist who co-founded the Magnum Photos agency and is considered the greatest war photographer in history. His images of the D-Day landings and the Spanish Civil War defined the genre. His motto 'If your pictures aren't good enough, you're not close enough' embodied his daring approach.
