## Summary
José Luis Calva Zepeda was a Mexican serial killer and cannibal known as 'El Caníbal de la Guerrero.' He murdered at least three women in Mexico City, cooking and eating their flesh. Human remains were found in his refrigerator and a novel about cannibalism on his desk. He hanged himself in prison in 2007.
