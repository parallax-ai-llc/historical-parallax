## Summary

Bang Si-hyuk (known as 'Hitman' Bang) founded HYBE Corporation (formerly Big Hit Entertainment), the company behind BTS, the most commercially successful K-pop group in history. He revolutionized the K-pop industry by building a global entertainment platform that transcended traditional music industry boundaries. HYBE went public in 2020, and the BTS phenomenon demonstrated the global potential of Korean pop culture.
