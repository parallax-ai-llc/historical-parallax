## Summary

Ohtake Masaru was a Japanese fisheries scientist who pioneered the cultivation of yellowtail (hamachi) and other commercial fish species. His aquaculture techniques transformed Japanese fish farming from small-scale operations to a major industry, making Japan a global leader in aquaculture technology.
