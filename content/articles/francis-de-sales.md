## Summary

Francis de Sales was a Bishop of Geneva and a Doctor of the Church who is one of the most beloved Catholic spiritual writers. His Introduction to the Devout Life and Treatise on the Love of God made Christian spirituality accessible to laypeople. He pioneered the idea that holiness was possible in everyday life, not just in monasteries. He co-founded the Order of the Visitation of Holy Mary with Jane Frances de Chantal.
