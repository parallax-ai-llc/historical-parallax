## Summary

Shin Eun-chul is a Korean chemical engineer who has developed advanced desalination and water treatment technologies. His research on reverse osmosis membranes has contributed to Korea's water technology exports, with Korean-made desalination plants operating in the Middle East and other water-scarce regions.
