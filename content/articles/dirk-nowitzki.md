## Background

Nowitzki was born in Wrzburg, Germany, and developed as a prospect in the German league before entering the NBA. He was selected in the 1998 NBA draft and spent his entire NBA career with the Dallas Mavericks.

## Playing Style

He became known for his scoring versatility as a seven-footer, especially his one-legged fadeaway jump shot and ability to stretch defenses with perimeter shooting. His style helped normalize the modern "stretch four" archetype in NBA roster construction.

## Major Achievements

- NBA Most Valuable Player (2007)
- NBA champion and Finals MVP (2011)
- Multiple-time NBA All-Star and All-NBA selections
- One of the NBAs all-time leading scorers

## Legacy

Nowitzki is widely cited as one of the most influential European players in NBA history. His long tenure with a single franchise is often highlighted as a rare modern example of sustained team loyalty at a superstar level.
