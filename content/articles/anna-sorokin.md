## Summary
Anna Sorokin, also known as Anna Delvey, was a Russian-born German woman who posed as a wealthy German heiress to defraud banks, hotels, and individuals in New York City. She was convicted of theft of services and grand larceny in 2019. Her story inspired the Netflix series 'Inventing Anna.'
