## Summary

Chip Wilson founded Lululemon Athletica in 1998, creating the athleisure category and building one of the most valuable apparel brands in the world. His yoga-inspired athletic wear became a cultural phenomenon and Lululemon grew to a market cap exceeding $50 billion. He stepped down from the board amid controversial comments but remains the largest shareholder. Lululemon's success spawned the entire athleisure industry.
