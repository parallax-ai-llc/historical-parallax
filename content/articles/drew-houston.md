## Summary

Drew Houston co-founded Dropbox, a cloud storage and file synchronization service that pioneered cloud computing for consumers and businesses. He started the company after repeatedly forgetting his USB flash drive. Dropbox grew to over 700 million registered users and went public in 2018. The company became a pioneer of the remote-work movement and expanded into collaboration tools.
