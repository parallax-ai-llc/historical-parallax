## Summary

Benjamin Banneker was a self-taught African American astronomer, mathematician, and almanac author. He accurately predicted a solar eclipse in 1789, published almanacs containing astronomical calculations, and helped survey the boundaries of Washington, D.C. His intellectual achievements challenged prevailing racial prejudices and he corresponded with Thomas Jefferson about racial equality.
