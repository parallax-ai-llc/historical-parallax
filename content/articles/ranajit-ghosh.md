## Summary

Ranajit Ghosh was an Indian-British chemist who co-discovered the organophosphate insecticide temephos (Abate) and developed nerve agent antidotes. His most significant contribution was discovering VX nerve agent while working at ICI, though this was later classified for military use. He made important contributions to pesticide chemistry.
