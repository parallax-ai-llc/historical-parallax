## Summary

Charles Haddon Spurgeon was an English Baptist preacher known as the 'Prince of Preachers.' He preached to huge congregations at the Metropolitan Tabernacle in London for nearly 40 years. His sermons were published weekly and have been translated into many languages. He also founded an orphanage, a pastors' college, and numerous charitable institutions.
