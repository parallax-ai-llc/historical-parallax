## Summary

Mark Cuban is an American billionaire who made his fortune by selling Broadcast.com to Yahoo for $5.7 billion in 1999. He owns the Dallas Mavericks NBA team, stars on the TV show Shark Tank, and founded Cost Plus Drugs, which sells affordable generic medications. He is known for his outspoken personality and diverse business interests. He briefly ran for president in 2024 before withdrawing.
