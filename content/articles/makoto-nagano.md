## Identity Note

This entry’s frontmatter appears to describe a different person (a physicist), but the linked Wikipedia page is about **Makoto Nagano (長野 誠)**, a Japanese commercial fisherman and TV competitor best known for *Sasuke* ("Ninja Warrior"). The expanded content below follows the cited Wikipedia article.

## Overview

- **Full name (JP):** 長野 誠 (Nagano Makoto)
- **Known for:** *Sasuke* (TBS), one of the "SASUKE All-Stars"
- **Nickname:** "World's Strongest Fisherman" (as commonly described in media)
- **Primary work:** Commercial fisherman / vessel captain

## Notability

Nagano is widely recognized for:

- Repeated deep runs on *Sasuke* over many tournaments
- Achieving **Total Victory** in the **17th tournament (2006)**, making him one of the small number of champions in the show’s history
- A training lifestyle closely tied to his fishing work (frequently described as training on his ship for much of the year)
