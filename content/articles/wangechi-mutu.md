## Summary

Wangechi Mutu is a Kenyan-American artist known for her collages and sculptures exploring femininity, ecology, and postcolonialism. Her bronze sculptures The NewOnes, will free Us were the first artworks commissioned for the facade niches of the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
