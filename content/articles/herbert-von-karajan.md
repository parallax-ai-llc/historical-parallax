## Summary

Herbert von Karajan was an Austrian conductor who was the most commercially successful classical music conductor of the 20th century. As principal conductor of the Berlin Philharmonic for 35 years, he recorded extensively and shaped the modern image of the conductor as cultural icon. His recordings dominate the classical music catalog.
