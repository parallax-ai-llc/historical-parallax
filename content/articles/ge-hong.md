## Summary

Ge Hong was an Eastern Jin Dynasty alchemist and physician whose Baopuzi documented early chemical experiments, medicinal treatments, and alchemical processes. He described the use of artemisinin-containing herbs for malaria treatment, which inspired Tu Youyou's Nobel Prize-winning discovery 1,700 years later. He also documented early smallpox treatment methods.
