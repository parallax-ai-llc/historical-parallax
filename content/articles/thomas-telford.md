## Summary

Thomas Telford was a pioneering civil engineer who built roads, bridges, canals, and harbors across Britain. His Menai Suspension Bridge connecting Wales to Anglesey was the world's first major suspension bridge. He also designed the Ellesmere Canal and was the first president of the Institution of Civil Engineers.
