## Summary

Matsyendranath was a legendary Indian yogi who is considered the founder of the Nath tradition and the guru of Gorakhnath. He is credited with discovering or receiving the teachings of Hatha Yoga from Lord Shiva. He is one of the 84 Mahasiddhas (great accomplished ones) in both Hindu and Buddhist tantric traditions. He is also venerated in Nepal as the patron deity of the Kathmandu Valley. His Kaula Jnana Nirnaya is an important tantric text.
