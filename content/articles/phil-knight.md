## Summary

Phil Knight co-founded Nike, Inc., building it into the world's largest athletic footwear and apparel company. He started by importing Japanese running shoes under the name Blue Ribbon Sports in 1964. The Nike brand, named after the Greek goddess of victory, became synonymous with athletic culture worldwide. Knight's marketing innovations, including celebrity endorsements and the 'Just Do It' campaign, revolutionized sports marketing.
