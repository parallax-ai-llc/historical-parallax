## Summary

Mohed Altrad is a Syrian-born French billionaire who founded the Altrad Group, one of the world's largest scaffolding and construction equipment companies. He was named World Entrepreneur of the Year by EY in 2015. Born into a Bedouin family in the Syrian desert, he overcame extreme poverty to build a €4 billion revenue industrial group. He also owns Montpellier Hérault Rugby and is one of France's most inspiring self-made billionaires.
