## Summary

Last commander of the FARC guerrilla group known as 'Timochenko' who signed the 2016 peace agreement ending 52 years of conflict. He ran for president in 2018.

## Additional Context

- **Alias and role**: Commonly known by the nom de guerre **Timoleón Jiménez** ("Timochenko"), he led the FARC-EP during the final phase of the armed conflict and the transition to legal politics.
- **Peace process**: He was a key figure in keeping the organization engaged in negotiations that culminated in the 2016 agreement and the subsequent demobilization and disarmament process.
- **Health and public life**: After the peace agreement, he appeared in public political roles connected to the movement’s successor party, alongside periods of reported health issues.

### Why he is notable

- Central leadership position in one of Latin America’s longest-running insurgencies.
- Symbolic figure in the shift from armed struggle to electoral participation.
- Frequently referenced in debates about reintegration, transitional justice, and the durability of the Colombian peace process.
