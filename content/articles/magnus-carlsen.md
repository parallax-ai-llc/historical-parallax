## Notability

- Long-time world No. 1 and one of the most dominant players of the modern era.
- Known for endgame technique, practical decision-making, and converting small advantages.
- Competed across time controls: classical, rapid, and blitz.

## Key Achievements (high level)

- World Chess Champion (classical) from 2013 to 2023.
- Multiple-time World Rapid and World Blitz champion (titles across several years).
- Highest peak rating recorded by FIDE (2882).

## Approach & Style

- Emphasizes posing persistent, practical problems rather than relying only on opening novelty.
- Frequently chooses solid openings and aims for long games where incremental advantages matter.
- Strong performance in simplified positions and technical endgames.
