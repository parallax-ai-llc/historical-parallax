## Summary

Jabir ibn Aflah (Geber) was an influential Andalusian mathematician and astronomer who criticized Ptolemy's astronomical system and made significant contributions to spherical trigonometry. His Islah al-Majisti (Correction of the Almagest) influenced European astronomers including Copernicus. He developed trigonometric identities that advanced mathematical astronomy.
