## Summary

Shoghi Effendi was the Guardian of the Baha'i Faith from 1921 to 1957, the grandson of Abdul-Baha. He oversaw the expansion of the Baha'i Faith to virtually every country and territory in the world. He translated major Baha'i texts into English and developed the administrative order of the faith. He also supervised the construction of the Shrine of the Bab on Mount Carmel in Haifa.
