## Summary

Sara Blakely founded Spanx in 2000, creating a shapewear empire with just $5,000 in savings. She became the world's youngest self-made female billionaire when Spanx was valued at over $1 billion. Starting by selling fax machines door-to-door, she invented footless pantyhose and built a global brand. In 2021, Blackstone acquired a majority stake valuing Spanx at $1.2 billion, and she gave every employee $10,000 and first-class plane tickets.
