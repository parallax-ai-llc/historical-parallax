## Summary

Luis Miramontes was a Mexican chemist who at age 26 co-invented norethindrone, the chemical compound that became the basis for the first oral contraceptive pill. His synthesis of the progestin in 1951 was one of the most socially impactful chemical achievements of the 20th century, fundamentally changing human reproductive control.
