## Summary

Lee Chang-dong is a Korean filmmaker and novelist whose humanistic films explore ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances. Peppermint Candy, Oasis, Poetry, and Burning are masterworks of contemporary Korean cinema. He also served as South Korea's Minister of Culture and Tourism.
