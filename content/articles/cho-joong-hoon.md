## Summary

Cho Joong-hoon founded Hanjin Group and built Korean Air into South Korea's flag carrier airline. He also played a major role in developing South Korea's transportation infrastructure through Hanjin Transportation and Korean Air. He expanded into shipping, creating one of the world's largest logistics networks. The Cho family dynasty continued to control Hanjin Group through his sons.
