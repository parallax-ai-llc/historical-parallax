## Summary

While Lee Won-bok is primarily a cartoonist, his manhwa series 'Far Countries, Close Countries' (Meon Nara Iut Nara) used systematic analysis to explain the science, technology, and culture of different nations to Korean readers. This educational manhwa helped Koreans understand global scientific and technological developments.
