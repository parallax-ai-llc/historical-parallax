## Summary

American who shot and killed 18 people at a bowling alley and restaurant in Lewiston, Maine in the deadliest mass shooting in state history. He was found dead two days later.

## Incident overview

Robert Card is associated with the **Lewiston, Maine shootings** of October 2023, in which multiple locations were attacked in a single evening, resulting in 18 deaths and many injuries.[^1]

## Sequence of events (high level)

Public accounts generally describe:

- An initial attack at a public venue, followed by a second attack at another location.
- A large-scale law-enforcement response, including a multi-day search.
- Card being found dead from a self-inflicted gunshot wound after the manhunt.[^1]

## Community and policy impact

The event became a major moment for Maine, including:

- Mass casualty response and victim support efforts
- Renewed public debate about firearm policy and crisis intervention
- Reviews of how warning signs are reported, documented, and acted upon

## Investigations and reviews

After incidents of this type, authorities often conduct overlapping reviews that may include:

- Operational timeline of the response and communications
- Firearms acquisition history and compliance with applicable laws
