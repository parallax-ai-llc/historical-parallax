## Summary

Lee Hae-jin is a Korean computer engineer who founded Naver Corporation, South Korea's largest internet company and search engine. His engineering of the Korean-language search engine and internet portal created a platform that dominates Korean internet usage. Naver's Line messaging app also became the dominant messenger in Japan, Taiwan, and Thailand.
