## Summary

Hatoyama Hideo was a pioneering Japanese civil engineer who designed many of Japan's first modern bridges and infrastructure during the Meiji period. He introduced Western engineering techniques to Japan and helped establish civil engineering as a professional discipline. His bridges remain landmarks of Japan's modernization.
