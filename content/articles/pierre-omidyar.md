## Summary

Pierre Omidyar founded eBay in 1995, creating the world's first major online auction platform and one of the earliest successful e-commerce companies. What began as a personal website became a global marketplace connecting millions of buyers and sellers. He stepped back from day-to-day operations early and focused on philanthropy through Omidyar Network. His foundation has invested billions in journalism, financial inclusion, and civic technology.
