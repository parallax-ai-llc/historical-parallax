## Summary

Wolfhart Pannenberg was a German Lutheran theologian known for his emphasis on the historical resurrection of Jesus as the foundation of Christian faith. His Systematic Theology is one of the most comprehensive theological works of the 20th century. He argued that theology must be a public discipline subject to rational inquiry. He also contributed significantly to the dialogue between theology and science.
