## Summary

Theodore Gericault was a pioneering French Romantic painter whose brief career produced some of the most powerful paintings of the 19th century. His monumental Raft of the Medusa became a manifesto of Romantic art. His portraits of psychiatric patients are remarkable for their empathetic humanity.
