## Summary

Teruaki Georges Suemitsu is a Japanese-Brazilian physicist who contributed to the understanding of semiconductor physics and Brazilian scientific development. He played a role in developing physics education and research in Brazil, bridging Japanese and Brazilian scientific communities.
