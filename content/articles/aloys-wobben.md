## Summary

Aloys Wobben founded Enercon, one of the world's largest wind turbine manufacturers. He pioneered the gearless wind turbine design, which became the most reliable technology in the industry. Enercon became the market leader in Germany and one of the top manufacturers globally. He was extremely private, rarely giving interviews, and Enercon remains privately held. His innovation was crucial to Germany's Energiewende (energy transition).
