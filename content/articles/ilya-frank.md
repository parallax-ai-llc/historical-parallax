## Summary

Ilya Frank, together with Igor Tamm, provided the theoretical explanation for Cherenkov radiation in 1937. Their theory showed that the radiation is emitted when a charged particle moves faster than light in a medium. He shared the 1958 Nobel Prize in Physics for this work, which has applications in particle physics detectors.
