## Summary
The Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum robbery on March 18, 1990, was the largest art theft in history. Two men disguised as police officers stole 13 works of art valued at $500 million, including works by Vermeer, Rembrandt, and Degas. The crime remains unsolved, with a $10 million reward offered.
