## Summary

Giorgio Armani founded the Armani fashion empire, building one of the most influential Italian luxury brands. He revolutionized men's fashion by deconstructing the suit jacket, creating a relaxed, modern silhouette. His designs have been worn by Hollywood celebrities, and his brand encompasses haute couture, ready-to-wear, accessories, and hotels. He remains one of the wealthiest people in fashion, with sole ownership of his privately held company.
