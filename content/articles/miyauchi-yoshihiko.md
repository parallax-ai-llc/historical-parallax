## Summary

Yoshihiko Miyauchi built ORIX Corporation from a small leasing company into one of Japan's largest financial services conglomerates. Under his leadership as CEO and chairman for over 30 years, ORIX expanded into banking, insurance, real estate, and infrastructure globally. He pioneered the leasing industry in Japan and advocated for deregulation and corporate governance reform. He also owned the Orix Buffaloes baseball team.
