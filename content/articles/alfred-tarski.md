## Summary

Alfred Tarski was a Polish-American mathematician and logician who made foundational contributions to model theory, metamathematics, and algebraic logic. His semantic theory of truth is one of the most important results in mathematical logic. He also proved the Banach-Tarski paradox and made fundamental contributions to the theory of formal languages.
