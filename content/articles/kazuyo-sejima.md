## Summary

Kazuyo Sejima is a Japanese architect who co-founded SANAA with Ryue Nishizawa. Together they received the Pritzker Prize in 2010. Their minimalist, transparent buildings including the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art and the New Museum in New York redefine architectural space.
