## Summary

Amadeo Giannini founded the Bank of Italy in San Francisco, which became Bank of America, the largest bank in the world. He pioneered branch banking and lending to small businesses and immigrants. After the 1906 San Francisco earthquake, he set up a temporary bank on the waterfront and made loans to help rebuild the city. He believed in banking for ordinary people, not just the wealthy elite.
