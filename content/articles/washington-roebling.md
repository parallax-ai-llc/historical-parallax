## Summary

Washington Roebling completed the construction of the Brooklyn Bridge after his father John Roebling's death. He supervised construction from his home with a telescope after developing decompression sickness (the bends) from working in underwater caissons. His wife Emily Warren Roebling served as his on-site representative.
