## Summary

Priscilla Chan is a physician, philanthropist, and co-founder of the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI) with her husband Mark Zuckerberg. They pledged 99% of their Facebook shares (worth over $45 billion) to CZI for advancing human potential and promoting equality. CZI invests in education, science, and criminal justice reform. She also founded The Primary School, a private school in East Palo Alto combining health and education.
