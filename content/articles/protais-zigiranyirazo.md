## Summary

Rwandan politician and brother-in-law of President Habyarimana known as Mr. Z who was a key organizer of the 1994 genocide. He controlled death squads and organized massacres in the Gisenyi area.

## ICTR Proceedings (High-Level)

- He was tried at the International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda (ICTR), convicted at trial, and later acquitted on appeal.
- The appellate outcome is often cited in discussions of evidentiary standards, witness credibility, and the challenges of prosecuting mass-atrocity cases years after events.
