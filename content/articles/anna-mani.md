## Summary

Anna Mani was a pioneering Indian physicist and meteorologist who specialized in meteorological instrumentation. She standardized over 100 weather instruments used across India and conducted pioneering research on solar radiation and wind energy. Her work was crucial for India's weather monitoring infrastructure and renewable energy development.
