## Summary

William Colgate was an English-American businessman who founded Colgate & Company in 1806, which became one of the world's largest consumer products companies. He started as a candlemaker and soapmaker in New York City. His company introduced Colgate Toothpaste in 1873, which became a global brand. He was also a devout Baptist and major philanthropist who helped found several educational institutions.
