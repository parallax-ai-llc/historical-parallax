## Summary

Yuki Kaji (梶裕貴) is one of Japan's most prominent voice actors, born on September 3, 1985, in Tokyo. Known for his versatile range spanning intense action heroes to gentle supporting characters, Kaji has become one of the most recognizable voices in modern anime. His prolific output across hundreds of titles has made him a defining figure of his generation in the industry.

Rising to international recognition through landmark roles in globally successful anime franchises, Kaji represents the contemporary voice acting ideal — technically precise, emotionally committed, and capable of sustaining fan bases across cultural boundaries. His work has contributed substantially to the global spread of anime fandom throughout the 2010s and beyond.

## Early Life & Background

Yuki Kaji was born and raised in Tokyo, Japan. He developed an early interest in performance and enrolled at the Nippon Narration Acting Institute, where he trained formally in voice and acting techniques. He made his professional debut in 2004, entering an industry that demands both vocal precision and sustained emotional stamina. His early years were spent in supporting and minor roles, building the technical foundation that would later support leading performances in high-profile productions.

## Rise to Prominence

Kaji's ascent accelerated significantly in the early 2010s as he landed increasingly prominent roles. His casting as Issei Hyodo in *High School DxD* (2012) and Shido Itsuka in *Date A Live* (2013) established him as a reliable lead voice. The pivotal breakthrough came with his portrayal of Eren Yeager in *Attack on Titan* (*Shingeki no Kyojin*), which premiered in April 2013. The series became a global phenomenon, and Kaji's raw, anguished delivery of Eren's transformation from determined youth to morally complex figure was widely credited as central to the character's impact. The role earned him sustained international recognition and industry awards attention.

## Key Achievements

Kaji has voiced lead characters in numerous major franchises. Among his most celebrated roles are Eren Yeager in *Attack on Titan* (2013–2023), Meliodas in *The Seven Deadly Sins* (2014), Yukine in *Noragami* (2014), and Alibaba Saluja in *Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic*. He won the Best Lead Actor award at the 8th Seiyu Awards in 2014, a recognition voted on by industry peers. He has also appeared in numerous video game titles, including *Tales of Zestiria* as Sorey and several entries in the *Fire Emblem* series. By the mid-2010s, Kaji was regularly cited as one of the busiest voice actors in the industry, reportedly recording for multiple series simultaneously.

## Controversies & Criticism

In 2021, Kaji announced his marriage to fellow voice actress Ayana Taketatsu, confirming a relationship that fans had long speculated about. While the reception was largely positive, it prompted discussion about the pressures placed on voice actors to maintain a certain public persona for commercial reasons. More broadly, Kaji has occasionally been noted in industry conversations around workload sustainability, as his schedule during peak years exemplified a demanding pattern common among top-tier voice actors in Japan.

## Legacy & Influence

Kaji's career has helped define what mainstream anime voice acting looks like in the post-2010 landscape — emotionally expressive, internationally visible, and franchise-sustaining. His portrayal of Eren Yeager in particular stands as one of the most discussed vocal performances in modern anime history, spanning a decade of increasingly complex characterization. He has influenced younger voice actors entering the industry and remains an active presence across new productions as of 2026.
