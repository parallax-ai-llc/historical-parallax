## Summary

Evan Spiegel co-founded Snapchat while a student at Stanford University, creating one of the most popular social media platforms among young people. He became the youngest billionaire in the world at age 25. Snap Inc. went public in 2017 and pioneered innovations in augmented reality and ephemeral messaging. Despite competition from Instagram and TikTok, Snapchat maintained a loyal user base of over 750 million.
