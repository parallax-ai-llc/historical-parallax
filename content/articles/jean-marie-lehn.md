## Summary

Jean-Marie Lehn is a French chemist who shared the 1987 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development and use of molecules with structure-specific interactions of high selectivity (supramolecular chemistry). He is considered the founder of supramolecular chemistry, which studies chemical systems made up of multiple molecular components. His work has applications in drug design, materials science, and nanotechnology.
