## Summary

Stephen Schwarzman co-founded Blackstone Group in 1985, building it into the world's largest alternative investment firm with over $1 trillion in assets under management. The company went public in 2007, and Schwarzman is one of the wealthiest people in America. He has donated extensively to education, including a $400 million gift to MIT for an AI computing college. He served as chairman of President Trump's Strategic and Policy Forum.
