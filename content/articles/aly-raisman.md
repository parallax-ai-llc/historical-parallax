## Summary

Aly Raisman won six Olympic medals, captaining the US 'Fierce Five' and 'Final Five' teams. She became a vocal advocate against sexual abuse in gymnastics.

## Background

Raisman trained in the United States junior and senior elite systems and became known for consistency on floor exercise and balance beam, as well as for her leadership presence on team competitions.

## Major Achievements

- Two-time U.S. Olympic team member (2012, 2016) with medals in team and individual events.
- 2012 Olympic floor exercise champion; also medaled on balance beam.
- Recognized as team captain for both the 2012 and 2016 U.S. Olympic women’s gymnastics teams.

## Advocacy

After retiring from competition, Raisman became widely known for public testimony and advocacy related to abuse in gymnastics and institutional accountability.
