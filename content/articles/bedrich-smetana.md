## Summary

Bedrich Smetana was the founder of Czech national music. His cycle of symphonic poems Ma vlast (My Homeland), including the famous Vltava, and his opera The Bartered Bride established Czech music as a distinct national voice. He composed despite becoming completely deaf in 1874.
