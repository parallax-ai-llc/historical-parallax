## Summary
Andrei Chikatilo was a Soviet serial killer responsible for dozens of murders, primarily targeting women and children in the Russian SFSR (notably the Rostov region) between the late 1970s and 1990. He was arrested in 1990, convicted in 1992, and executed in 1994.
