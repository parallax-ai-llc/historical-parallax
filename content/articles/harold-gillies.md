## Summary

Harold Gillies is considered the father of modern plastic surgery. During World War I, he developed many fundamental techniques for facial reconstruction of soldiers with devastating injuries. He pioneered the tubed pedicle skin flap and established the first dedicated plastic surgery unit. His work laid the foundation for all modern reconstructive and cosmetic surgery.
