## Summary

Adam Riess is an American astrophysicist who shared the 2011 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of the accelerating expansion of the universe through observations of distant supernovae. His analysis of Type Ia supernovae data was crucial to demonstrating that dark energy is driving the universe's acceleration. He continues to refine measurements of the Hubble constant.
