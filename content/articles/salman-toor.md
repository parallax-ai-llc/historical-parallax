## Summary

Pakistani-American painter known for intimate paintings of young brown men in private moments. His work explores queer identity, immigration, and the digital age with a painterly technique recalling old masters.

## Themes and Technique

- **Subject matter:** Many discussions of Toor's work focus on everyday interiors, nightlife, and private social spaces as a way to depict intimacy, vulnerability, and community.
- **Art-historical dialogue:** Critics often note how contemporary scenes are rendered with compositional echoes of European painting traditions while keeping modern details (phones, apartments, clothing) visible.
