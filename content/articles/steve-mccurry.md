## Summary

Steve McCurry is an American photojournalist known for his iconic Afghan Girl portrait, one of the most recognized photographs in the world. His vivid, saturated images from conflict zones and across Asia demonstrate extraordinary empathy and compositional skill. He has covered conflicts in Afghanistan, Iraq, and throughout the developing world.
