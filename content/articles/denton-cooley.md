## Summary

Denton Cooley performed more than 100,000 open-heart operations and was the first surgeon to implant a total artificial heart in a human in 1969. He founded the Texas Heart Institute and pioneered numerous cardiac surgical techniques. His speed and skill in the operating room were legendary among cardiac surgeons.
