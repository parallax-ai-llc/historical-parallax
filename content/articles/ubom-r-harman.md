## Summary

Slovak man who committed Slovakia's worst mass shooting, killing 7 people in Devínska Nová Ves.

## Incident Overview (2010)

- The attack is commonly referred to as the **Devínska Nová Ves shooting** or the **2010 Bratislava shooting**.
- It occurred in the **Devínska Nová Ves** district of **Bratislava, Slovakia**.
- Public reporting describes the event as a **spree shooting / mass shooting** that ended in a **murder–suicide**.
- Counts frequently distinguish between **victims killed** and **the perpetrator**, who also died at the scene.

## Reported Casualties

- **Victims killed:** 7
- **Perpetrator:** died by suicide (bringing the commonly cited total deaths to 8 including the perpetrator)
- **Injured:** 17

## Context and Aftermath (High-Level)

- The incident prompted scrutiny of **emergency response and police procedures** for rare mass-casualty shootings in Slovakia.
- Coverage notes continued public interest in:
  - how authorities communicated during and after the event
  - what lessons were drawn for future crisis response
