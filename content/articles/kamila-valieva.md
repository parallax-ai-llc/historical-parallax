## Summary

Kamila Valieva's doping controversy at the 2022 Beijing Olympics became one of the biggest scandals in Olympic history. She was 15 years old.

## Competitive Profile

Valieva emerged as a leading athlete in women’s figure skating in the early 2020s, noted for high technical difficulty and strong scoring in domestic and international events prior to and around the Beijing Olympic cycle.

## Beijing 2022 Context

At the 2022 Winter Olympics, she was at the center of a high-profile eligibility dispute after a reported positive test for a prohibited substance. The situation drew attention because of her age, the timing of the test report, and the implications for medals in the team and individual events.

## Adjudication and Sanction (High Level)

Public reporting describes proceedings involving anti-doping authorities and sports arbitration mechanisms. The final sanction referenced in many summaries is a multi-year period of ineligibility issued in 2024, with related consequences for results and standings depending on the governing bodies’ rulings.

## Broader Impact

The case became part of broader debates about anti-doping enforcement, athlete safeguarding, and the responsibilities of adult support personnel and institutions in elite sport.
