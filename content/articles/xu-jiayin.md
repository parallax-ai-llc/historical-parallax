## Summary

Xu Jiayin (Hui Ka Yan) founded China Evergrande Group, which became China's largest real estate developer before its spectacular collapse in 2021. At its peak, Evergrande had $300 billion in liabilities, making its debt crisis the largest in global real estate history. Xu was briefly Asia's richest person in 2017. The Evergrande crisis shook China's entire property sector and became a symbol of China's real estate bubble.
