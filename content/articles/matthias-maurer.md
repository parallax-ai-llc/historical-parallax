## Summary

Matthias Maurer is a German astronaut and materials scientist who flew to the ISS aboard SpaceX Crew Dragon in 2021. He conducted over 35 European experiments during his 177-day mission, focusing on materials science, bioprinting, and Earth observation. His research included printing human tissue cells in microgravity.
