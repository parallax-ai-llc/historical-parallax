## Summary
The USS Cole bombing occurred on October 12, 2000, when al-Qaeda suicide bombers attacked the USS Cole with an explosive-laden small boat while it was harbored in Aden, Yemen. Seventeen American sailors were killed and 37 injured. The attack demonstrated al-Qaeda's growing capability.
