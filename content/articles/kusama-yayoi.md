## Summary

Yayoi Kusama is a Japanese artist known for her obsessive polka dots, infinity rooms, and pumpkin sculptures. She pioneered aspects of Pop Art and environmental art in New York before returning to Japan. Now in her 90s, she is one of the world's most popular living artists.
