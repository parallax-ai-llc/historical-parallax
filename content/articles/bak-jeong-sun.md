## Summary

Bak Jeong-sun is a Korean soil scientist who developed improved soil management techniques for Korean agriculture. Her research on soil fertility, crop rotation, and sustainable farming practices has helped Korean farmers maintain productivity while protecting soil health. She has been an advocate for sustainable agricultural practices.
