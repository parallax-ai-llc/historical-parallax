## Summary

Simon Edward Minter, known online as Miniminter, is a British YouTuber and content creator born on September 7, 1992, in Barnsley, South Yorkshire, England. He rose to prominence through FIFA gaming content and daily lifestyle vlogs, amassing over 10 million subscribers on his primary YouTube channel. As a founding member of the Sidemen, one of the United Kingdom's most influential YouTube collectives, Miniminter helped establish a template for long-form collaborative content that shaped British YouTube culture throughout the 2010s.

Beyond gaming, Miniminter expanded into lifestyle vlogging, sports challenges, and entrepreneurial ventures. His involvement with the Sidemen brand — spanning merchandise, a dedicated Sidemen YouTube channel exceeding 20 million subscribers, and charity football matches watched by millions — cemented his status as a major figure in digital entertainment. His longevity and adaptability in a fast-evolving platform landscape distinguish him from many of his contemporaries.

## Early Life & Background

Simon Minter was born on September 7, 1992, in Barnsley, South Yorkshire. He grew up in England and attended school in Yorkshire before pursuing higher education. He studied at the University of Kent, where he began creating YouTube content seriously. His early videos focused on FIFA, the popular football simulation video game series, which was a dominant genre on YouTube throughout the early 2010s. His approachable personality and consistent upload schedule helped him build an initial fanbase during his student years.

## Rise to Prominence

Miniminter's channel gained significant traction around 2012–2013 as FIFA content exploded in popularity. He became a central member of the Sidemen — a group of seven British YouTubers including KSI, Vikkstar123, W2S, Behzinga, TBJZL, and Zerkaa — which officially formed in 2013. The group's collaborative challenge videos, FIFA content, and later lifestyle vlogs drew enormous audiences. The Sidemen YouTube channel, launched to host group content, became one of the largest collaborative channels in the UK.

His solo channel grew steadily alongside the group, reaching 1 million subscribers by 2014 and continuing to expand through diversified content including vlogs, challenges, and commentary videos. Miniminter's willingness to document his personal life, including his relationship with fellow creator Talia Mar, added a dimension that resonated with viewers beyond gaming.

## Key Achievements

- Surpassed 10 million subscribers on his personal YouTube channel
- Co-founded the Sidemen, whose group channel exceeds 20 million subscribers
- Participated in multiple Sidemen Charity Football Matches, including events at Stadium MK and London Stadium that sold out and raised hundreds of thousands of pounds for charity
- Co-founded Sides, the Sidemen's fast-food restaurant venture launched in 2021, which expanded to multiple locations across the UK
- Featured in the 2021 Sidemen documentary, broadening the group's mainstream media presence

## Controversies & Criticism

Miniminter and the Sidemen have faced criticism over the years regarding the tone of certain videos, including a 2017 controversy involving a diss track and public dispute between members of the group that temporarily fractured the collective. The feud, which involved a public fallout with KSI and other members, was documented across social media and drew significant attention. The Sidemen subsequently reconciled and addressed the situation publicly. Some critics have also pointed to earlier content containing language or humor now considered inappropriate by modern standards.

## Legacy & Influence

Miniminter's career reflects the broader evolution of British YouTube from gaming-focused channels to diversified entertainment brands. His role in the Sidemen helped prove that collaborative creator groups could build sustainable, multi-platform businesses. The Sidemen's charity matches demonstrated the real-world mobilizing power of creator communities, with events drawing tens of thousands of live attendees. As a content creator who has sustained relevance for over a decade, Miniminter represents the generation that professionalized YouTubing as a career path in the UK.
