## Summary

Vice President of Equatorial Guinea and son of dictator Obiang. He is known for extreme extravagance funded by oil wealth, owning a $30 million Malibu mansion, a Gulfstream jet, and Michael Jackson memorabilia while most citizens live in poverty.
