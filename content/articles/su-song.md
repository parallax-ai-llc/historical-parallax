## Summary

Su Song built the world's first known astronomically driven mechanical clock tower in 1088 in Kaifeng, China. His water-driven astronomical clock tower featured an escapement mechanism, a rotating celestial globe, and mechanized figurines announcing the hours. He also compiled a comprehensive pharmaceutical treatise identifying hundreds of medicinal substances.
