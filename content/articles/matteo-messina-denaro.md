## Summary
Matteo Messina Denaro was Italy's most wanted man and the last of the old-guard Sicilian Mafia bosses. He was a fugitive for 30 years. He was involved in the 1992 bombings that killed judges Falcone and Borsellino. He was captured in Palermo in January 2023 and died of cancer in September 2023.
