## Summary

Kim Linsu is a Korean scholar of technology and innovation policy who analyzed the mechanisms behind South Korea's technological catch-up. His research at Korea University documented how Korean firms absorbed and improved upon foreign technologies to become global leaders, providing a model for other developing countries' industrialization strategies.
