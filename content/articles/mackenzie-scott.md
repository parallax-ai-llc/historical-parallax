## Summary

MacKenzie Scott is a novelist and philanthropist who became one of the world's wealthiest women after her divorce from Jeff Bezos in 2019, receiving a 4% stake in Amazon worth over $35 billion. She has since donated over $16 billion to nonprofits, making her one of the most generous philanthropists in history.
