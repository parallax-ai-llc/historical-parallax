## Summary

Timothy Keller was an American Presbyterian pastor, theologian, and author who founded Redeemer Presbyterian Church in Manhattan. His intellectual approach to Christianity attracted urban professionals and influenced a generation of church planters. His bestselling books, including The Reason for God and The Prodigal God, engaged skeptics and believers alike. He was known for engaging thoughtfully with secular culture and philosophy.
