## Summary

Christo and Jeanne-Claude were artists known for their large-scale environmental installations wrapping buildings, coastlines, and landscapes. Their projects, including Wrapped Reichstag and The Gates in Central Park, transformed familiar places into temporary artworks. They self-financed their projects, maintaining complete artistic independence.
