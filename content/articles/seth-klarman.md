## Summary

Seth Klarman is the CEO of the Baupost Group, one of the largest hedge funds in the world. His book 'Margin of Safety' (1991) became a cult classic among value investors, with original copies selling for over $1,000. He is considered one of the greatest value investors of his generation, alongside Warren Buffett. Baupost has generated strong returns over three decades with a focus on distressed debt and special situations.
