## Summary

Kim Han-il co-founded NHN (now Naver Corporation) and served as its CEO, building Naver into South Korea's dominant internet search and content platform. Under his leadership, Naver expanded into webtoons, cloud computing, and AI. NHN later split into Naver (search and content) and NHN Entertainment (gaming). He played a key role in establishing South Korea's digital economy infrastructure.
