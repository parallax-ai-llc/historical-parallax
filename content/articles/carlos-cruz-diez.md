## Summary

Carlos Cruz-Diez was a Venezuelan-French artist who was a pioneer of kinetic and Op Art. His Physichromies and Chromosaturations create immersive color experiences that change as viewers move. His work demonstrates that color exists as an autonomous reality, independent of form.
