## Summary

Issey Miyake was a Japanese fashion designer known for his technology-driven designs and his synthesis of Eastern and Western aesthetics. His Pleats Please line and A-POC (A Piece of Cloth) concept revolutionized how clothing is designed and manufactured. He also created the iconic black turtlenecks worn by Steve Jobs.
