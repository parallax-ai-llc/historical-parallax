## Summary

Issad Rebrab is Algeria's richest person and the founder of Cevital Group, the largest private conglomerate in Algeria. Cevital operates in food processing (including the world's largest sugar refinery), steel, electronics, and media. He built his empire during Algeria's economic liberalization. He was briefly arrested in 2019 during Algeria's political upheaval but was later released. He represents North Africa's entrepreneurial potential.
