## Summary

Robert Morrison was a Scottish missionary who was the first Protestant missionary to China. He translated the Bible into Chinese and compiled the first Chinese-English dictionary. He worked for the British East India Company as a translator while pursuing his missionary work. His linguistic achievements laid the foundation for all subsequent Protestant missionary work in China.
