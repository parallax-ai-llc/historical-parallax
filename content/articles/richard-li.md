## Summary

Richard Li is a Hong Kong billionaire and the younger son of Li Ka-shing. He founded Pacific Century Group and PCCW Limited, Hong Kong's largest telecommunications provider. He also controls HKT and FWD Group, a major Asian insurance company. Despite operating in the shadow of his father, he built his own significant business empire across technology, media, and financial services.
