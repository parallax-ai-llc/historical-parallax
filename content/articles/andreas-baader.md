## Summary
Andreas Bernd Baader was a German left-wing militant and co-founder of the Red Army Faction. He led the group's bombing and robbery campaigns. He was arrested in 1972 and died in his cell at Stammheim Prison in 1977 during the 'German Autumn,' officially by suicide.
