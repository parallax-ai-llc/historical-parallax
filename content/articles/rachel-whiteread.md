## Summary

Rachel Whiteread is a British sculptor known for her casts of negative spaces, including the interior of an entire house. She was the first woman to win the Turner Prize in 1993. Her Holocaust Memorial in Vienna and various public commissions explore memory, absence, and domestic space.
