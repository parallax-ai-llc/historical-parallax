## Summary

Gedaliah Kenett was a British-Israeli Orthodox rabbi and educator who served the Jewish community of Manchester and later Israel. He was known for his dedicated teaching and community leadership. He represented the tradition of British Orthodox rabbis who combined Torah scholarship with pastoral care and communal engagement.
