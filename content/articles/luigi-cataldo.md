## Context: Sacra Corona Unita (SCU)

Sacra Corona Unita (often abbreviated **SCU**) is an Italian organized-crime group historically associated with the **Apulia (Puglia)** region in southern Italy.
In reporting and academic writing, SCU is commonly discussed alongside other major Italian criminal organizations, but it is generally characterized as having a more regionally concentrated base of operations.

High-level activities attributed to SCU in open sources typically include:

- Drug trafficking networks (importation and distribution)
- Extortion and protection rackets
- Smuggling and facilitation of illicit logistics
- Violence and intimidation used to enforce control
