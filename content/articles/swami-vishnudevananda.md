## Summary

Swami Vishnudevananda was an Indian yoga guru and peace activist who founded the International Sivananda Yoga Vedanta Centres, one of the largest yoga organizations in the world. He was a disciple of Swami Sivananda who brought classical yoga to the West. He founded yoga teacher training courses and ashrams worldwide. He was also a peace activist, flying over conflict zones in a small plane painted with 'peace' messages.
