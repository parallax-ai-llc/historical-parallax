## Summary

Otake Masao was a Japanese epidemiologist who studied the long-term health effects of radiation exposure on Hiroshima and Nagasaki atomic bomb survivors. His decades of research at the Radiation Effects Research Foundation documented the dose-response relationship between radiation exposure and cancer risk, providing fundamental data for radiation protection standards worldwide.
