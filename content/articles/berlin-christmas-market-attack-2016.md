## Summary
On December 19, 2016, Anis Amri drove a truck into a Christmas market at Breitscheidplatz in Berlin, killing 12 and injuring 56. He was an ISIS-affiliated Tunisian asylum seeker. He was killed in a shootout with Italian police in Milan days later.
