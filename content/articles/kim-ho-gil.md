## Summary

Kim Ho-gil was a Korean aerospace engineer who helped establish South Korea's space program. He worked on developing Korea's first sounding rockets and contributed to the KSLV (Korea Space Launch Vehicle) program. His work laid the groundwork for Korea's modern space capabilities.
