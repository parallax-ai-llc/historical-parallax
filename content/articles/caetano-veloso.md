## Summary

Caetano Veloso is a Brazilian musician and political activist who co-founded the Tropicalia movement in the late 1960s. His music fuses Brazilian traditions with global influences including rock psychedelia and electronic music. He is considered one of the greatest and most influential Brazilian musicians of all time.
