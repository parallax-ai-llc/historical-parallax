## Summary

Patrick Collison co-founded Stripe with his brother John in 2010, building it into the world's most valuable private fintech company. Stripe simplifies online payment processing for businesses and has become critical infrastructure for internet commerce. Born in Ireland, he moved to the U.S. at 16 and dropped out of MIT to start Stripe. The company has processed hundreds of billions of dollars in payments annually.
