## Summary

Choi Hyung-sup was a pioneering Korean nuclear physicist who played a key role in establishing South Korea's nuclear energy program. He trained at institutions in Japan and the United States and returned to Korea to develop nuclear science education and research. His work laid the groundwork for South Korea's nuclear power industry.
