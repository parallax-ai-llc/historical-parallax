## Summary
The Jonestown massacre on November 18, 1978, was a mass murder-suicide at the Peoples Temple agricultural commune in Guyana. Jim Jones ordered his followers to drink cyanide-laced punch. 918 people died, including 304 children. It was the largest deliberate loss of American civilian life before 9/11.
