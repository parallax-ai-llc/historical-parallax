## Summary

Njideka Akunyili Crosby is a Nigerian-American painter known for her large-scale, densely layered paintings combining painting, collage, and photo transfer to explore themes of cultural hybridity and the immigrant experience. Her domestic scenes layer Nigerian and American imagery to capture the complexity of diasporic identity. She has rapidly become one of the most sought-after painters of her generation.
