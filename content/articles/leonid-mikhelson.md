## Summary

Leonid Mikhelson is the chairman of the board of Novatek, Russia's largest independent natural gas producer. He built Novatek into a major force in global LNG through the Yamal LNG project in the Arctic. He is one of Russia's wealthiest people and a significant art collector. Despite Western sanctions on Russia, Novatek has continued its LNG expansion, including the Arctic LNG 2 project.
