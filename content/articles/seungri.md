- **Stage name:** Seungri (Lee Seung-hyun) is a South Korean entertainer best known as a member of the K-pop group **BIGBANG**.
- **Industry role:** Beyond performing, he was involved in entertainment business activities and operated venues associated with nightlife and hospitality.

## Legal Controversies and Investigation

- **Military court proceedings:** Because he entered mandatory military service, parts of the legal process were handled within South Korea’s military justice system.
- **Charges and outcomes:** Courts addressed multiple charges; the case was widely covered due to its intersection with celebrity culture, policing, and allegations of corruption.

## Broader Significance

- **Public debate:** The events intensified debate in South Korea about accountability in the entertainment industry, the power of celebrity networks, and oversight of nightlife businesses.
