## Summary
Bernard Lawrence Madoff orchestrated the largest Ponzi scheme in history, defrauding thousands of investors of an estimated $65 billion over decades. His scheme collapsed during the 2008 financial crisis. He was sentenced to 150 years in federal prison and died there in 2021.
