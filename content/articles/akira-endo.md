## Summary

Akira Endo discovered the first statin (compactin/mevastatin) in 1973, which led to the development of statin drugs that lower cholesterol. Statins have become the most prescribed class of drugs in the world and have prevented millions of heart attacks and strokes. Despite their enormous impact, he never received the Nobel Prize.
