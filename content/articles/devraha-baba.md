## Summary

Devraha Baba was a legendary Hindu saint who lived on a wooden platform (machan) above the banks of the Yamuna River near Vrindavan. He was venerated by millions across India, including several Indian Prime Ministers and Presidents. His exact age was unknown, but devotees believed he was several centuries old. He rarely spoke but his presence was considered a powerful spiritual blessing. He practiced extreme austerities and was devoted to Lord Krishna.
