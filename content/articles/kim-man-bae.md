Owner of Hwacheon Daeyu who reaped $340 million from the Daejang-dong land development scandal. Arrested for bribery and fraud in one of Korea's biggest corruption cases.

## Daejang-dong Development Scandal (High-Level)

The Daejang-dong case centers on a land development project in Seongnam, South Korea, that became a major political and legal controversy. The core public issue has been how a project involving a public entity and private partners produced unusually large profits for a small group of private participants.

## Business Role

Kim Man-bae is commonly described in coverage as a key figure behind **Hwacheon Daeyu** (화천대유), a private entity that received significant returns tied to the project structure. Reporting emphasized the gap between the firm's capital/scale and the size of the eventual gains.

## Allegations and Charges

Publicly reported allegations have included:

- bribery or improper payments,
- breach of trust and fraud-related claims,
- influence-peddling connected to public officials or intermediaries.

Because the case involves multiple defendants and politically sensitive claims, public understanding has often evolved as investigations and court proceedings progressed.

## Broader Significance

The scandal became a flashpoint in South Korean politics and media because it combined:

- public-private partnership design questions,
- real-estate windfalls and perceived unfairness,
- allegations that political connections shaped outcomes.
