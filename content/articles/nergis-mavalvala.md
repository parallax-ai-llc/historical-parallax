## Summary

Nergis Mavalvala is a Pakistani-American astrophysicist who was a key member of the LIGO team that detected gravitational waves in 2015. She developed quantum measurement techniques that improved LIGO's sensitivity. She became the first woman and openly LGBTQ person to serve as dean of the MIT School of Science.
