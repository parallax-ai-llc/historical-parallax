## Summary

Peter Doig is a British painter known for his lush, dreamlike landscape paintings that have made him one of the most acclaimed painters of his generation. His atmospheric canvases draw on photographs, film stills, and memory to create scenes of uncanny beauty. White Canoe sold for a record price.
