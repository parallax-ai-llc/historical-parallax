## Summary

Andre Geim is a Russian-Dutch-British physicist who shared the 2010 Nobel Prize in Physics for experiments with graphene. He is the only person to have received both a Nobel Prize and an Ig Nobel Prize (for levitating a frog using magnets). His isolation of graphene with Novoselov opened revolutionary applications in electronics, composites, and energy.
