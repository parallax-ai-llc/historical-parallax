## Summary

Rei Kawakubo is a Japanese fashion designer and founder of Comme des Garcons. Her radical, avant-garde designs challenge Western conventions of beauty and form. Her 1981 Paris debut with its dark, deconstructed aesthetic shocked the fashion world. She became only the second living designer to receive a Met Costume Institute exhibition.
