## Summary

Martinus Beijerinck was a Dutch microbiologist and botanist who is considered one of the founders of virology and environmental microbiology. He independently discovered viruses and coined the term 'virus' to describe the filterable infectious agent of tobacco mosaic disease. He also discovered nitrogen fixation by bacteria and developed enrichment culture techniques essential to environmental microbiology.
