## Summary

Wan Hu is a legendary Chinese official from the Ming Dynasty who allegedly attempted to fly to space around 1500 CE by strapping 47 gunpowder rockets to a chair. According to legend, when the rockets were ignited, he disappeared in a massive explosion. While likely apocryphal, his story represents humanity's earliest recorded aspiration for rocketry and spaceflight. A lunar crater is named after him.
