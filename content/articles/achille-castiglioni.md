## Summary

Achille Castiglioni was an Italian industrial designer known for his witty, inventive approach to everyday objects. His Arco lamp, Mezzadro stool (from a tractor seat), and Parentesi lamp are masterpieces of Italian design. He received fourteen Compasso d'Oro awards, more than any other designer.
