## Summary

Drew Weissman is an American physician-scientist who shared the 2023 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine with Katalin Karikó for their discoveries enabling mRNA vaccines. His work on innate immune responses to mRNA led to the critical discovery that modified nucleosides prevent inflammatory reactions, making therapeutic mRNA possible. This discovery enabled the rapid development of COVID-19 mRNA vaccines.
