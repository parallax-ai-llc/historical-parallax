## Summary
Abu Musab al-Zarqawi was a Jordanian jihadist who led al-Qaeda in Iraq, the precursor to ISIS. He was responsible for numerous bombings, beheadings, and attacks during the Iraq War. He was killed in a US airstrike in June 2006.
