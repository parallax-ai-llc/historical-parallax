## Summary

Imam Shamil was the third Imam of the Caucasian Imamate who led the resistance of the Caucasian peoples against Russian imperial expansion for over 25 years. He was a Naqshbandi Sufi who combined Islamic revivalism with guerrilla warfare. His resistance became legendary and he was treated with honor after his capture. He eventually went on hajj and died in Medina.
