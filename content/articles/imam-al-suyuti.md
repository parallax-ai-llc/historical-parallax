## Summary

Imam al-Suyuti was an Egyptian Islamic scholar who was one of the most prolific authors in Islamic history, writing over 500 works. His expertise spanned virtually every Islamic discipline, including Quran commentary, hadith, jurisprudence, history, and linguistics. His Tafsir al-Jalalayn (co-authored with al-Mahalli) remains one of the most widely used Quran commentaries. He also wrote al-Itqan fi Ulum al-Quran, a comprehensive work on Quranic sciences.
