## Summary

Wang Yinglai was a Chinese biochemist who led the team that achieved the first total synthesis of crystalline bovine insulin in 1965, making China the first country to synthesize a protein. This landmark achievement in biochemistry demonstrated that proteins could be artificially created. He was a founder of modern Chinese biochemistry.
