## Summary

Kwon Sung-dong is the honorary chairman of Shinsegae Group (formerly part of Samsung Group). He built E-mart into South Korea's largest discount store chain and Shinsegae into the country's premier department store operator. His retail innovations transformed Korean shopping culture. Shinsegae Group separated from Samsung Group in 1991 under the leadership of Lee Myung-hee, Samsung's founding family member and Kwon's wife.
