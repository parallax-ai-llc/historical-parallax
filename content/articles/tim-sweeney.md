## Summary

Tim Sweeney is the founder and CEO of Epic Games, the company behind Fortnite and the Unreal Engine. Fortnite became a cultural phenomenon with over 350 million registered players. Unreal Engine is the most widely used game engine in the industry, powering films, architecture, and virtual production. His legal battle with Apple over App Store fees became a landmark antitrust case. Epic Games was valued at over $30 billion.
