## Summary

Bediuzzaman Said Nursi was a Kurdish-Turkish Islamic scholar who wrote the Risale-i Nur, a 6,000-page Quran commentary addressing modern challenges to faith. He argued for the harmony of Islam and science, countering materialism and atheism. He spent decades in exile and prison under both Ottoman and Republican Turkish governments. His Nur movement has millions of followers worldwide.
