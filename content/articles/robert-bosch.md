## Summary

Robert Bosch founded the Robert Bosch GmbH, which became one of the world's largest suppliers of automotive components and industrial technology. He developed the magneto ignition system for automobiles, which was critical to the early auto industry. His company pioneered fuel injection, anti-lock braking systems, and electronic stability control. He was also known for his progressive labor policies and philanthropy.
