## Summary

Stéphane Bancel served as CEO of Moderna during the development of the mRNA-1273 COVID-19 vaccine, one of the first vaccines authorized against the pandemic. Under his leadership, Moderna transformed from a small biotech startup to a major pharmaceutical company. The mRNA vaccine platform he championed is being developed for other diseases including cancer.
