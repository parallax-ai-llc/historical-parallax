## Summary

Tim Peake was the first British ESA astronaut to visit the ISS, spending 186 days in space in 2015-2016. He performed a spacewalk and ran the London Marathon on a treadmill aboard the ISS. His mission generated huge public interest in space science across the United Kingdom and inspired a new generation of British scientists.
