## Summary

Bernard Marcus co-founded The Home Depot, the world's largest home improvement retailer. After being fired from Handy Dan, he and Arthur Blank launched the first Home Depot stores in Atlanta in 1979. The concept of large warehouse stores with low prices and expert advice revolutionized the home improvement industry. Home Depot grew to over 2,300 stores with $150 billion in annual revenue.
