## Summary

Elder Sophrony (Sakharov) was a Russian-British Orthodox monk and author who was the disciple and biographer of Saint Silouan the Athonite. His book Saint Silouan the Athonite became one of the most important spiritual texts of the 20th century. He founded the Stavropegic Monastery of Saint John the Baptist in Essex, England, which became a major center of Orthodox spirituality in the West.
