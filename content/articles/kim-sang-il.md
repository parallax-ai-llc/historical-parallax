## Summary

Kim Sang-il is a Korean telecommunications engineer who was instrumental in developing South Korea's CDMA mobile network infrastructure. His engineering work in the 1990s helped Korea become the first country to commercialize CDMA technology, launching Korea's mobile revolution.
