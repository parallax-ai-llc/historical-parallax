## Summary

Kim Gwang-seop was a Korean forest scientist who led reforestation efforts in South Korea after the Korean War. Korea's mountains were nearly completely deforested, and his research on native tree species and reforestation techniques was crucial to Korea's successful reforestation, considered one of the most successful environmental restoration projects in world history.
