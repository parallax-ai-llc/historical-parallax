## Summary

Marc Lore is a serial entrepreneur who founded Diapers.com (Quidsi, sold to Amazon for $545 million), Jet.com (sold to Walmart for $3.3 billion), and the city-building project Telosa. At Walmart, he led the company's e-commerce division, growing it to compete with Amazon. He is now building Telosa, a planned city in the American desert designed around equitable principles. He also co-owns the Minnesota Timberwolves.
