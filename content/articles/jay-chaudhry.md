## Summary

Jay Chaudhry is the founder and CEO of Zscaler, a cloud security company that pioneered the zero-trust network architecture. He has founded multiple cybersecurity companies including AirDefense, CipherTrust, CoreHarbor, and SecureIT. Zscaler went public in 2018 and grew to a market cap exceeding $30 billion. His zero-trust approach to cybersecurity became the industry standard for enterprise network security.
