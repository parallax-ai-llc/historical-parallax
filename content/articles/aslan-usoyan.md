## Summary
Aslan Rashidovich Usoyan, known as 'Ded Khasan' (Grandpa Khasan), was a Georgian-born Russian crime boss and one of the most powerful 'thieves-in-law' (vor v zakone) in the post-Soviet criminal world. He was assassinated by a sniper in Moscow in 2013.
