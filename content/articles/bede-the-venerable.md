## Summary

Bede the Venerable was an Anglo-Saxon monk at the monastery of Jarrow and the greatest scholar of early medieval England. His Ecclesiastical History of the English People is the primary source for early English history. He popularized the dating system of AD (Anno Domini). He was the first person to be named a Doctor of the Church from England.
