## Summary

Otis Boykin invented an improved electrical resistor used in computers, radios, televisions, and military hardware. He also invented a control unit for the cardiac pacemaker that helped regulate the heartbeat, saving countless lives. He held 26 patents and his resistors were used in missiles and military electronics.
