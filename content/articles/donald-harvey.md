## Summary
Donald Harvey, the 'Angel of Death,' was an American serial killer who worked as a hospital orderly. He confessed to killing 37 people in hospitals in Kentucky and Ohio between 1970 and 1987. He used cyanide, arsenic, and other poisons. He was murdered in prison in 2017.
