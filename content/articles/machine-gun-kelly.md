## Summary
George Celino Barnes Jr., known as 'Machine Gun Kelly,' was an American bootlegger and gangster during the Prohibition era. He is best known for kidnapping oil tycoon Charles Urschel. He reportedly coined the term 'G-Men' for FBI agents. He died of a heart attack in prison.
