## Summary

Vinod Khosla co-founded Sun Microsystems, which pioneered network computing and created the Java programming language. He later became a partner at Kleiner Perkins and founded Khosla Ventures, one of Silicon Valley's most influential venture capital firms. He has been an early investor in technologies ranging from clean energy to AI. Sun Microsystems' motto 'The network is the computer' anticipated the cloud computing era by two decades.
