## Summary
Vito Genovese was an Italian-American mob boss who led the Genovese crime family. Known for his ambition and treachery, he orchestrated the 1957 assassination attempt on Frank Costello and took control of the family. He was convicted of narcotics trafficking in 1959 and died in federal prison.
