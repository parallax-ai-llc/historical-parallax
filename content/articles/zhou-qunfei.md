## Summary

Zhou Qunfei is the founder of Lens Technology, the world's largest manufacturer of glass screens for smartphones and electronics. She supplies cover glass for Apple, Samsung, and other major tech companies. Born into poverty and working in a factory at 16, she became the world's richest self-made woman. Her rags-to-riches story exemplifies China's manufacturing revolution and female entrepreneurship.
