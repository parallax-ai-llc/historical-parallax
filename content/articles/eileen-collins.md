## Summary

Eileen Collins was the first female pilot and first female commander of a Space Shuttle. She piloted the shuttle during the first shuttle-Mir docking mission in 1995 and commanded the return-to-flight mission after the Columbia disaster in 2005. She flew four shuttle missions and is considered one of NASA's greatest astronaut-pilots.
