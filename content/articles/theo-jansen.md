## Summary

Theo Jansen is a Dutch artist and engineer known for his Strandbeesten (beach animals), large kinetic sculptures made from PVC pipes that walk on wind power along Dutch beaches. His creations blur the boundaries between art, engineering, and biology. He describes his work as creating new forms of life.
