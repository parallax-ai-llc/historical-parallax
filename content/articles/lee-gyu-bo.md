## Summary

Yi Gyu-bo was a Goryeo Dynasty scholar and writer who documented in his Dongguk Isanggukjip the earliest known reference to metal movable type printing in Korea. His writings provide historical evidence that Korea had metal movable type by the 13th century, centuries before Gutenberg's press in Europe.
