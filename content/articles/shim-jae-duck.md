## Summary

Shim Jae-duck was a Korean environmental scientist who spent decades cleaning and restoring Korean rivers, particularly through the Nakdong River restoration project. His citizen science approach to river monitoring and his environmental advocacy helped raise awareness of water pollution in South Korea.
