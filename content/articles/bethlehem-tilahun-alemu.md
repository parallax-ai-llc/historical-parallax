## Summary

Bethlehem Tilahun Alemu is an Ethiopian entrepreneur who founded soleRebels, the world's first fair trade footwear brand. Starting from her grandmother's tire-soled shoe-making tradition, she built an internationally recognized ethical fashion brand exported to over 50 countries. She was named one of Forbes' Most Powerful Women in Africa. Her company demonstrates how African traditional craftsmanship can compete in global markets.
