## Summary

Qian Sanqiang was a Chinese nuclear physicist who discovered the triple fission of uranium while working with Frédéric and Irène Joliot-Curie in Paris. He returned to China and became a key leader of China's nuclear weapons and atomic energy programs. He is considered one of the fathers of China's nuclear capability.
