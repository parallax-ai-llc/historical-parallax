## Summary

K. R. Rao was a leading Indian physicist known for his extensive spectroscopic research. He analyzed the spectra of numerous elements and molecules, contributing to atomic physics. He founded the Spectroscopy Laboratory at Andhra University, which became a leading center for spectroscopic research in India.
