## Summary

Janet Yellen is the first woman to serve as U.S. Secretary of the Treasury, the first woman to chair the Federal Reserve, and the first woman to lead the Council of Economic Advisers. She navigated the post-pandemic economic recovery and managed the debt ceiling crisis. At the Fed, she maintained accommodative monetary policy during the recovery from the 2008 financial crisis. She is the most accomplished woman in American economic policy history.
