## Summary

Germán Larrea Mota-Velasco is a Mexican mining billionaire and CEO of Grupo México, the world's third-largest copper producer. He inherited the mining business from his father and expanded it into one of the most profitable mining operations globally. Grupo México also operates the largest railroad in Mexico and has interests in infrastructure. He is one of the wealthiest people in Latin America.
