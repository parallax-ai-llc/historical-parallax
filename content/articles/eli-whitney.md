## Summary

Eli Whitney was an American inventor best known for inventing the cotton gin in 1793, which revolutionized the cotton industry but also entrenched the institution of slavery in the American South. He also pioneered the concept of interchangeable parts in manufacturing, which became a cornerstone of mass production. His manufacturing methods helped launch the American system of manufacturing.
