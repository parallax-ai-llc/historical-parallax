## Summary

Data breach at Capital One in 2019 that exposed personal information of 106 million customers. Former Amazon employee Paige Thompson was convicted.

## Additional Context

- **What happened (high level):** The incident involved unauthorized access to data associated with Capital One, affecting tens of millions of individuals in the United States and Canada.
- **Type of data exposed:** Public summaries commonly mention items such as names, addresses, credit scores, credit limits, and application-related information, with varying exposure depending on the individual record.
- **Cloud and configuration focus:** Coverage of the breach frequently emphasized the relationship between cloud infrastructure and security controls, including the importance of access management, monitoring, and configuration review.

## Response and Aftermath

- **Notification and remediation:** Large-scale breaches typically lead to customer notification, credit-monitoring offers, and internal security remediation, alongside regulatory and legal scrutiny.
- **Legal proceedings:** The case became associated with the arrest and subsequent prosecution of Paige Thompson, with later court outcomes discussed as part of the broader timeline.
- **Industry impact:** The breach is often cited in discussions about third-party risk, responsible disclosure, and the operational realities of securing complex, cloud-based environments.

## Why It Matters

- The event remains a reference point for how quickly a single control weakness can scale into a nationwide exposure when systems aggregate large amounts of personal data.
- It is frequently used as an example in cybersecurity education to stress defense-in-depth: identity controls, segmentation, logging, and incident response planning.

## Commonly Discussed Lessons

- Strong identity and access management, including least privilege, is critical in cloud environments.
- Continuous monitoring and alerting can reduce dwell time between intrusion and detection.
- Post-incident transparency and structured remediation plans help rebuild stakeholder trust.
