## Summary

Lee Boo-jin is the president of Hotel Shilla and daughter of Lee Kun-hee. She has expanded Hotel Shilla into a luxury hotel chain and duty-free shopping operator. She oversees Samsung C&T's fashion division and hospitality businesses. She is considered one of the most powerful businesswomen in South Korea and has been mentioned as a potential successor in Samsung's broader leadership.
