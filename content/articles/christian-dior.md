## Summary

Christian Dior founded the House of Dior and revolutionized post-war fashion with his 'New Look' in 1947, which featured cinched waists and full skirts. His designs restored Paris as the center of world fashion after World War II. In just ten years, he built one of the most influential fashion houses in history. The Dior brand continues as a pillar of the LVMH luxury empire.
