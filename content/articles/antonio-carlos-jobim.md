## Summary

Antonio Carlos Jobim was a Brazilian composer and musician who co-created bossa nova and became one of the most influential songwriters of the 20th century. His compositions including The Girl from Ipanema, Corcovado, and Waters of March are jazz and popular music standards. He brought Brazilian music to the world stage.
