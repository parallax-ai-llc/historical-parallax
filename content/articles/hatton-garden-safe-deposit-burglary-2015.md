## Summary
The Hatton Garden safe deposit burglary occurred over the Easter weekend of 2015. A group of aging criminals drilled through a vault wall in London's diamond district, stealing an estimated £14 million in valuables. The criminals, dubbed the 'Diamond Wheezers' due to their age, were caught and convicted.
