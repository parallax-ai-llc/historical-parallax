## Summary

Congolese businessman and politician who served as Katanga governor before falling out with President Kabila. He was sentenced in absentia for property fraud.

## Background

Katumbi is known internationally as a Congolese businessman-turned-politician whose rise was closely tied to the politics of the mineral-rich Katanga region.

## Political role (high level)

As a provincial governor, he was associated with efforts to attract investment and develop local infrastructure, while also operating within a national system marked by patronage and intense competition.

## Controversies and legal disputes

Coverage of Katumbi often emphasizes:

- disputes with the central government during the later Kabila period
- legal cases that intersected with political conflict
- questions about the use of state power and courts in elite rivalries

## Why he matters

Katumbi is frequently cited in discussions of:

- opposition politics in the Democratic Republic of the Congo
- the political economy of mining provinces
- how provincial power bases can challenge (or be constrained by) the presidency
