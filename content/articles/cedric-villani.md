## Summary

Cédric Villani received the Fields Medal in 2010 for his work on optimal transport and Landau damping. His mathematical proofs on the Boltzmann equation advanced understanding of entropy and convergence to equilibrium. He later entered politics, winning a seat in the French National Assembly. He is known for his distinctive style including spider brooches.
