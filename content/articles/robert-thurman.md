## Summary

Robert Thurman is an American Buddhist scholar who was the first Westerner to be ordained as a Tibetan Buddhist monk. He later returned to lay life and became the Jey Tsong Khapa Professor of Buddhist Studies at Columbia University. He is a close friend of the 14th Dalai Lama and has been instrumental in popularizing Tibetan Buddhism in America. He is the author of numerous books including Inner Revolution and a translation of the Vimalakirti Sutra.
