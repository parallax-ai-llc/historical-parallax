## Summary

Lin Fengmian was a pioneering Chinese modernist painter who blended Western and Eastern techniques to create a distinctive lyrical style. He founded the National Academy of Art in Hangzhou and is considered one of the pioneers of modern Chinese painting. His atmospheric landscapes, opera figures, and female portraits combine French Post-Impressionism with Chinese ink tradition.
