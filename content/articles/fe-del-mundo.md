## Summary

Fe del Mundo was a pioneering Filipino pediatrician who was the first Asian woman to be admitted to Harvard Medical School. She founded the first pediatric hospital in the Philippines and invented an improved incubator and a jaundice-relieving device. She devoted her career and personal fortune to children's health in the Philippines.
