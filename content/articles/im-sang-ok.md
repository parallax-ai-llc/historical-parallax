## Summary

Im Sang-ok (Lim Sang-ok) was one of the most famous merchants in Korean history during the late Joseon Dynasty. Known as a 'great merchant' (geosang), he built a vast trading network connecting Korea with China and Japan. He is celebrated in Korean popular culture as a symbol of Korean entrepreneurial spirit. His story has been depicted in multiple Korean TV dramas and films about Joseon-era commerce.
