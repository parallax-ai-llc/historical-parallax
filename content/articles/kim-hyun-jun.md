## Summary

Kim Hyun-jun is a Korean medical engineer who has contributed to the development of robotic surgery systems in South Korea. His research on surgical robotics has helped Korea develop indigenous robotic surgery platforms, advancing minimally invasive surgical techniques and reducing Korea's dependence on imported surgical robots.
