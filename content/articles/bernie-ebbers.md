## Summary
Bernard John Ebbers was the CEO of WorldCom, which committed one of the largest accounting frauds in history, inflating assets by $11 billion. WorldCom's 2002 bankruptcy was the largest in US history until Lehman Brothers. He was convicted and sentenced to 25 years. He died after early release.
