## Summary

Amancio Ortega founded Inditex, the world's largest fashion retailer, and its flagship brand Zara. Starting as a delivery boy for a shirt maker, he built a fast-fashion empire that revolutionized the clothing industry. Zara's model of quickly copying runway trends and delivering them to stores in weeks disrupted traditional fashion cycles. He has been the wealthiest person in Europe and periodically the richest in the world.
