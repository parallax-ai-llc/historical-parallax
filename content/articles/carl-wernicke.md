## Summary

Carl Wernicke identified the brain region responsible for language comprehension, now known as Wernicke's area, complementing Broca's earlier discovery of the speech production area. He described Wernicke's aphasia and Wernicke's encephalopathy. His work was foundational to the neuroscience of language.
