## Summary

Kim Gyeong-ran is a Korean virologist who contributed to South Korea's infectious disease response capability. Her research on respiratory viruses helped Korea respond effectively to the MERS outbreak in 2015 and the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, demonstrating Korea's public health preparedness.
