## Summary

Vikram Sarabhai was an Indian physicist and industrialist who is considered the father of the Indian space program. He founded the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Physical Research Laboratory. He believed that space technology could solve development challenges in education, agriculture, and communication. Several Indian space facilities are named in his honor.
