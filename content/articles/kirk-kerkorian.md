## Summary

Kirk Kerkorian was an Armenian-American businessman who built and sold the world's largest hotel three times in Las Vegas. He was a major shareholder in MGM, Chrysler, and other major companies. He flew supplies over the Himalayas during World War II and later became a leading figure in Las Vegas development. He was one of the most influential figures in entertainment and hospitality.
