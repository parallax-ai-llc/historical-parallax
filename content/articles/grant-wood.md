## Summary

Grant Wood was an American painter known for American Gothic, one of the most iconic and parodied images in American art. His paintings of rural Iowa life and the American heartland established him as a leader of the Regionalist art movement. His meticulous, stylized realism draws on Northern European painting traditions.
