## Summary

Kim Minji (김민지), known mononymously as Minji, is a South Korean singer, dancer, and model who serves as the leader and oldest member of the K-pop girl group NewJeans, formed under ADOR (a subsidiary of HYBE). Debuting in July 2022, she quickly became one of the most recognizable faces of the fourth generation of K-pop, noted for her natural beauty, versatile performance style, and poised stage presence.

NewJeans' meteoric rise propelled Minji to international fame within months of debut, a trajectory rarely seen even in an industry known for rapid ascents. Her dual role as both a vocalist and visual anchor for the group established her as one of K-pop's most sought-after talents, landing her high-profile modeling contracts and magazine covers across Asia and beyond.

As the group's de facto leader, Minji has been widely credited with anchoring NewJeans' youthful, retro-influenced aesthetic — a refreshing contrast to the polished, high-concept image dominant in contemporary K-pop. Her influence extends beyond music into fashion and youth culture, making her a symbol of a new direction in the industry.

## Early Life & Background

Kim Minji was born on May 7, 2004, in South Korea. Before her entertainment debut, she was scouted by agencies at a young age due to her striking appearance and natural charisma. She trained under ADOR prior to NewJeans' formation, honing her vocal and dance abilities. Details about her early education remain largely private, consistent with the group's management approach of protecting members' personal backgrounds.

## Rise to Prominence

NewJeans debuted on July 22, 2022, with their self-titled EP, and Minji was immediately spotlighted as the group's leader and visual. The debut tracks "Attention" and "Hype Boy" became viral hits, accumulating hundreds of millions of streams across platforms within weeks. The group's distinctive sound — blending Y2K aesthetics, Jersey club, and R&B — set them apart, and Minji's composed, confident performances drew particular attention from fans and industry observers alike.

By late 2022, she had appeared on the covers of major fashion publications including *Vogue Korea* and *W Korea*, and secured modeling partnerships with global luxury brands. In 2023, NewJeans continued their momentum with the hit "OMG" and the single "Super Shy," both of which charted internationally, further cementing Minji's profile as a global K-pop star.

## Key Achievements

- Co-debuted on NewJeans' debut EP (July 2022), which broke multiple Gaon Chart records for a rookie act
- Featured in campaigns for luxury brands including Chanel and Burberry
- NewJeans won "Artist of the Year" and "Album of the Year" at the 2022 Melon Music Awards and 2023 Mnet Asian Music Awards
- "Super Shy" (2023) reached the top 10 of Billboard's Global 200
- Named one of *Time* magazine's Next Generation Leaders in 2023

## Controversies & Criticism

In late 2024, the members of NewJeans — including Minji — publicly disputed their management situation at ADOR following the dismissal of CEO Min Hee-jin, who had been central to the group's creative identity. The members expressed their intent to terminate their contracts with ADOR/HYBE, citing a breakdown of trust. The legal and corporate conflict attracted significant media coverage and divided fan communities, raising broader questions about artist autonomy and corporate control in the K-pop industry.

## Legacy & Influence

Minji's role in NewJeans helped redefine the template for fourth-generation K-pop girl groups, prioritizing authenticity, minimalism, and a nostalgic sonic palette over the hyper-produced maximalism of predecessors. Her influence on youth fashion trends — particularly the early-2000s revival — has been documented by stylists and cultural commentators across South Korea and East Asia. At just 21, she stands as one of the most commercially and culturally impactful young artists of her generation.
