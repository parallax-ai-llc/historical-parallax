## Summary

Lee In-won was the founder of Daesang Group, South Korea's leading food and bio company. Daesang produces Chung Jung One, Korea's most popular seasoning brand, and is the world's largest producer of MSG and amino acids. He built the company from a small fermentation business into a global food and biotechnology conglomerate.
