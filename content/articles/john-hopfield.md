## Summary

John Hopfield is an American physicist who invented the Hopfield network in 1982, an associative memory neural network that could store and retrieve patterns. His work bridging physics and neuroscience laid theoretical foundations for modern neural networks and machine learning. He shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Physics for foundational work in machine learning.
