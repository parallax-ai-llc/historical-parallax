## Summary

Sophus Lie was a Norwegian mathematician who created the theory of continuous transformation groups, now called Lie groups, which became fundamental to modern mathematics and theoretical physics. Lie groups describe symmetries of differential equations and are essential in quantum mechanics, particle physics, and general relativity.
