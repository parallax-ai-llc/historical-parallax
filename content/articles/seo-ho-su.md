## Summary

Seo Ho-su was a late Joseon Dynasty scholar who advanced mathematical and astronomical knowledge in Korea through his study of Western scientific works transmitted via China. He translated and adapted European mathematical texts and astronomical methods, contributing to the modernization of Korean scientific thought during the Silhak (practical learning) movement.
