## Summary
Alimzhan Tokhtakhunov, known as 'Taiwanchik,' is an Uzbek-born organized crime figure with extensive connections in Russia. He was indicted in the US for his role in fixing the ice skating competition at the 2002 Winter Olympics. He remains in Russia, which has refused extradition.
