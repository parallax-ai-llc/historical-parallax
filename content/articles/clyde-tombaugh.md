## Summary

Clyde Tombaugh was an American astronomer who discovered the dwarf planet Pluto in 1930 at the Lowell Observatory. A self-taught astronomer from a Kansas farm, he was hired to search for the predicted Planet X. His discovery made him famous, and he went on to discover hundreds of asteroids and star clusters. Some of his ashes were placed aboard the New Horizons spacecraft that flew past Pluto in 2015.
