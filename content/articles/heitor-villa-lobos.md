## Summary

Heitor Villa-Lobos was Brazil's greatest composer and one of the most important Latin American composers of the 20th century. His Bachianas Brasileiras fuse Bach's style with Brazilian folk music, while his Choros series draws on the rich diversity of Brazilian musical traditions. He composed over 1,000 works.
