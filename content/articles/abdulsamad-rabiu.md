## Summary

Abdulsamad Rabiu is the founder of BUA Group, one of Nigeria's largest conglomerates with operations in cement, sugar, real estate, and infrastructure. BUA Cement is the third-largest cement producer in Africa. He also founded BUA Foods, one of Nigeria's largest food processing companies. He is one of the richest people in Africa and his industrial investments have contributed significantly to Nigeria's manufacturing sector.
