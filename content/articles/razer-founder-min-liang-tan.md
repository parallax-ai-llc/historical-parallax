## Summary

Min-Liang Tan co-founded Razer Inc., the world's leading gaming hardware company known for gaming mice, keyboards, laptops, and peripherals. He grew Razer from a niche gaming peripheral maker into a $10+ billion global gaming brand. He also launched Razer Fintech and Razer Gold digital payment services. Razer went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2017.
