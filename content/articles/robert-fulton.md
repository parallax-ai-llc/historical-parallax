## Summary

Robert Fulton built the first commercially successful steamboat, the North River Steamboat (Clermont), which began regular passenger service on the Hudson River in 1807. He also designed early submarines and naval torpedoes. His steamboat revolutionized river transportation in America and opened up westward expansion.
