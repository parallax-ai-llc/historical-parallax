## Summary

Dara Khosrowshahi is the CEO of Uber Technologies, taking over in 2017 after Travis Kalanick's ouster. Previously, he served as CEO of Expedia Group for 12 years, growing it from $2.1 billion to $10 billion in revenue. Born in Iran, he fled with his family during the Iranian Revolution. Under his leadership, Uber achieved profitability and expanded into delivery and freight services.
