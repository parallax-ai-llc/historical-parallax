## Summary

Last leader of the RUF rebel movement convicted of 16 counts of war crimes and crimes against humanity by the Special Court for Sierra Leone. Sentenced to 52 years.

## Context: Sierra Leone Civil War

- The Revolutionary United Front (RUF) is documented as one of the principal armed groups in Sierra Leone's civil war.
- The Special Court for Sierra Leone was established to prosecute those who bore the greatest responsibility for serious crimes committed during the conflict.
