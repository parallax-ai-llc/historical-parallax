## Summary
Juana Barraza Samperio, the 'Old Lady Killer,' was a Mexican serial killer and professional wrestler who murdered at least 11 elderly women in Mexico City between 1998 and 2006. She gained victims' trust by posing as a nurse or social worker before strangling them. She was sentenced to 759 years in prison.
