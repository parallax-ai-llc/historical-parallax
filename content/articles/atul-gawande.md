## Summary

Atul Gawande is a surgeon and public health researcher whose Surgical Safety Checklist, adopted by the WHO, has reduced surgical complications by over 30% worldwide. He has written influential books on medical practice and healthcare systems. His work has improved surgical outcomes across the developing world.
