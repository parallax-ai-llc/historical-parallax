## Summary
John Gilligan is an Irish drug lord who led a major cannabis importation gang in Dublin. He was suspected of ordering the 1996 murder of journalist Veronica Guerin. He was acquitted of her murder but convicted of drug trafficking and sentenced to 28 years.
