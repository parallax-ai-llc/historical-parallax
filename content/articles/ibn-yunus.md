## Summary

Ibn Yunus was one of the greatest Islamic astronomers, known for his precise astronomical observations and mathematical innovations. His astronomical tables (Hakimi Zij) were the most accurate of their time. He introduced prosthaphaeresis formulas for trigonometric calculations that were not rediscovered in Europe for centuries.
