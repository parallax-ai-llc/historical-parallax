## Summary

Yuan T. Lee is a Taiwanese-American chemist who shared the 1986 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his contributions to the dynamics of chemical elementary processes. He developed the crossed molecular beam technique to study chemical reactions at the molecular level. He was the first Taiwanese Nobel laureate and later served as president of Academia Sinica in Taiwan.
