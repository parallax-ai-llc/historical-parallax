## Summary

Ajahn Chah was a Thai Buddhist monk who was one of the most influential meditation teachers of the 20th century. He revitalized the Thai Forest Tradition and attracted a large following of both Thai and Western disciples. He founded Wat Nong Pah Pong and later the International Forest Monastery (Wat Pah Nanachat) for Western students. His students established over 300 monasteries worldwide.
