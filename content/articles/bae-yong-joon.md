## Summary

Although Bae Yong-joon is famous as a Korean actor, he founded KeyEast entertainment and invested in technology startups. His entrepreneurial activities in the Korean Wave (Hallyu) demonstrated how cultural technology exports could be systematically developed, inspiring Korea's approach to cultural technology as a scientific discipline.
