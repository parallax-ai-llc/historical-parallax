## Summary

Ennio Morricone was an Italian composer who created some of the most iconic film scores in cinema history. His scores for Sergio Leone's Spaghetti Westerns, including The Good, the Bad and the Ugly, revolutionized film music. He composed over 500 film and TV scores across a career spanning six decades.
