## Summary

Hwang Ho-ik is a Korean electrical power engineer who contributed to the development and expansion of South Korea's electrical power grid. His work on high-voltage transmission systems and grid stability helped electrify Korea during its rapid industrialization period, supporting the country's economic miracle.
