## Summary

While Kim Jong-un is primarily a political leader (Supreme Leader of North Korea), under his direction North Korea has developed nuclear weapons and ballistic missile technology, representing significant (though controversial) engineering achievements. North Korea's ICBM and nuclear programs demonstrate advanced rocketry and nuclear physics capabilities.
