## Summary

Frank Stronach founded Magna International, one of the world's largest automotive parts suppliers with over $36 billion in revenue. He built it from a small tool-and-die shop in Toronto into a global company supplying parts to every major automaker. Magna can manufacture virtually every component of a car. He also built a horse racing empire before legal disputes with his family emerged in 2024.
