## Summary

French dark web drug dealer known as OxyMonster who was a top vendor on Dream Market selling opioids and other drugs. He was arrested at Atlanta airport on his way to a beard competition.

## Modus Operandi (High-Level)

Vallerius was associated with the online persona “OxyMonster,” described in reporting as a high-volume seller on the Dream Market darknet marketplace. These markets generally rely on anonymizing networks, cryptocurrency payments, and pseudonymous vendor accounts, with reputation systems and escrow mechanisms used to facilitate transactions.

In many investigations of darknet drug distribution, operational security failures are a recurring theme—such as reuse of usernames, weak compartmentalization between “real life” and online identities, or identifiable shipping patterns.

## Arrest and Prosecution

He was arrested in the United States after traveling internationally, which removed some of the jurisdictional friction that can complicate cybercrime cases. U.S. prosecutions of darknet vendors often emphasize controlled purchases, tracing of shipments, account attribution, and financial evidence to connect a vendor identity to a person.

## Broader Context

- **Darknet marketplaces:** Dream Market was one of several large platforms that facilitated illicit sales, following earlier sites like Silk Road and preceding later marketplace cycles.
- **Public health angle:** The sale of opioids online is frequently discussed alongside the broader opioid crisis and the risks of counterfeit or misrepresented substances.
- **Law-enforcement focus:** High-profile vendor cases are often used as deterrence messaging, even as marketplaces reconstitute under new names and infrastructure.

## Sentencing and Aftermath

Court reporting summarizes his outcome as a lengthy federal sentence. In the broader literature on cyber-enabled drug trafficking, such sentences are framed as an attempt to raise the perceived cost of operating as a marketplace “top vendor,” while acknowledging that demand and platform churn can keep the ecosystem resilient.

A commonly repeated detail in summaries of the case is that his arrest occurred while he was traveling to participate in a beard and moustache competition, which helped make the story widely shareable in mainstream media.
