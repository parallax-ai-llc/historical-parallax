## Summary

Peter Zumthor is a Swiss architect known for his contemplative, sensory-rich buildings that emphasize materials, light, and atmosphere. His Therme Vals spa, Kolumba Museum, and Bruder Klaus Chapel are considered masterpieces of contemporary architecture. He received the Pritzker Prize in 2009.
