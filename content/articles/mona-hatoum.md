## Summary

Mona Hatoum is a Palestinian-British artist known for her large-scale installation art that transforms familiar domestic objects into threats. Her works address themes of displacement, danger, and the body politic. Her piece Hot Spot, a glowing neon globe, is an icon of contemporary art.
