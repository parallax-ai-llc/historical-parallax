## Summary

Apichatpong Weerasethakul is a Thai filmmaker whose dreamlike, contemplative films explore memory, nature, and the spiritual world. Uncle Boonmee Who Can Recall His Past Lives won the Palme d'Or at Cannes in 2010. His meditative style has redefined the possibilities of art cinema.
