## Summary

Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev is an Indian yogi, mystic, and author who founded the Isha Foundation. He has spoken at the World Economic Forum, United Nations, and numerous other global forums. His 'Inner Engineering' program has been attended by millions worldwide. He has been involved in environmental campaigns, including Rally for Rivers. He is a polarizing figure, praised for making yoga accessible and criticized for some of his political and scientific claims.
