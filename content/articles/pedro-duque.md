## Summary

Pedro Duque was the first Spanish astronaut, flying on the Space Shuttle Discovery in 1998 and to the ISS on a Soyuz in 2003. He conducted materials science experiments in microgravity. He later served as Spain's Minister of Science, Innovation and Universities. He is Spain's only person to have flown in space.
