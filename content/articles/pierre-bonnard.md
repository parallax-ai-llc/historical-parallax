## Summary

Pierre Bonnard was a French Post-Impressionist painter and Nabis member known for intensely colorful domestic interiors and landscapes. His luminous canvases at Le Cannet capture the sensory richness of everyday life. His late bathing nudes are among the finest 20th-century paintings.
