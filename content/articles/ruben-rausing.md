## Summary

Ruben Rausing co-founded Tetra Pak, inventing the tetrahedron-shaped cardboard container that revolutionized food and beverage packaging worldwide. His innovation of filling cartons from a continuous tube of paper reduced waste and improved hygiene. Tetra Pak's packaging technology enabled the global distribution of milk and juice and is used in virtually every country in the world. He is one of Sweden's most important inventors and industrialists.
