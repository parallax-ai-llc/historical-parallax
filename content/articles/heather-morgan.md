## Additional Context

- Morgan became publicly known under the online persona "Razzlekhan," a self-styled rapper and social-media figure.
- U.S. authorities alleged she and her husband, Ilya Lichtenstein, conspired to launder cryptocurrency tied to the 2016 Bitfinex exchange hack.

## Alleged Laundering Pattern (High-Level)

- Public summaries of the case describe repeated transfers across wallets, use of mixing/obfuscation techniques, and conversion between cryptoassets and fiat-linked instruments.
- The laundering allegations emphasize the difficulty of moving very large sums without creating an investigatory trail, even on pseudonymous blockchains.

## Legal Proceedings (High-Level)

- In 2022, the pair were arrested and charged in connection with money laundering and related offenses tied to the hack proceeds.
- The case is frequently cited in discussions of crypto forensic tracing and large-scale asset seizure.
