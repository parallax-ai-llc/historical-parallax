## Summary

Swami Satchidananda was an Indian yoga guru who brought Integral Yoga to the West and became famous for giving the opening speech at the Woodstock Festival in 1969. He was a disciple of Swami Sivananda and founded numerous Integral Yoga Institutes and the Light of Truth Universal Shrine (LOTUS). He was a pioneer of interfaith dialogue and dedicated to the unity of all religions.
