## Summary

Kawasaki Tomisaku was a Japanese pediatrician who first identified Kawasaki disease in 1967, a childhood illness causing inflammation of blood vessels throughout the body. His careful clinical observation and documentation of 50 cases established a new disease entity that affects children worldwide. Untreated, it can cause coronary artery problems.
