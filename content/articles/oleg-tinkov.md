## Summary

Oleg Tinkov is a Russian entrepreneur who founded Tinkoff Bank, Russia's first fully online bank and one of the most innovative digital banks in the world. He previously founded multiple businesses including a brewery, electronics chain, and restaurant. Tinkoff Bank grew to over 20 million customers without a single physical branch. He sold his stake to Potanin's Interros after being sanctioned and facing tax issues in the United States.
