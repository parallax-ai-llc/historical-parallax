## Summary

Nobu Matsuhisa founded the Nobu restaurant chain, pioneering Japanese-Peruvian fusion cuisine and building one of the world's most recognized luxury restaurant brands. Partnering with Robert De Niro, he expanded Nobu to over 50 locations worldwide and added Nobu Hotels. His innovative fusion of Japanese techniques with South American ingredients created a new culinary genre. The Nobu brand became synonymous with celebrity dining culture.
