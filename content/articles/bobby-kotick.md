## Summary

Bobby Kotick served as CEO of Activision Blizzard from 1991 to 2023, transforming it from a near-bankrupt company into the world's most valuable video game publisher. Under his leadership, Activision produced the Call of Duty franchise (the best-selling FPS series), World of Warcraft, and Candy Crush. Microsoft acquired Activision Blizzard for $68.7 billion in 2023, the largest gaming acquisition in history.
