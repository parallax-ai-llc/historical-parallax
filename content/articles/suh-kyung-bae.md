## Summary

Suh Kyung-bae transformed Amorepacific Group from a domestic Korean cosmetics company into Asia's largest beauty conglomerate. He inherited the company from his mother and father (Suh Sung-hwan) and invested in premium brands like Sulwhasoo, Laneige, and Innisfree. He pioneered the 'K-beauty' movement that made Korean skincare products a global phenomenon. Amorepacific operates in over 50 countries worldwide.
