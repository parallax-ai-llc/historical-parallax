## Summary

Louis Farrakhan is an American religious leader who has served as the head of the Nation of Islam since 1978. He rebuilt the organization after Warith Deen Mohammed dissolved it in 1975. He organized the Million Man March in Washington, D.C., in 1995, one of the largest gatherings in American history. He is a highly controversial figure, praised for his community organizing and self-help message but condemned for anti-Semitic and anti-LGBT statements.
