## Summary

Marian Croak is a pioneering engineer who holds over 200 patents in Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) and other communication technologies. Her work at AT&T Bell Labs and later Google transformed how voice calls are transmitted over the internet. She was inducted into the National Inventors Hall of Fame in 2022 for her contributions to VoIP.
