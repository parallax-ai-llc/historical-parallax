## Summary

Lin Lanying was a pioneering Chinese materials scientist known as the 'Mother of Semiconductors in China.' She developed techniques for growing single-crystal silicon and gallium arsenide that were essential to China's semiconductor and space programs. Her work on semiconductor materials earned her numerous national science awards.
