## Summary

Charlotte Joko Beck was an American Zen teacher who founded the Ordinary Mind Zen School. She studied under Taizan Maezumi and Hakuyu Taizan Maezumi at the Zen Center of Los Angeles. Her books Everyday Zen and Nothing Special presented Zen practice as grounded in everyday life rather than exotic spiritual experiences. She was one of the most influential American Zen teachers, emphasizing direct engagement with daily life as spiritual practice.
