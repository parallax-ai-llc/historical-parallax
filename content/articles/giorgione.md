## Summary

Giorgione was a Venetian painter whose poetic, atmospheric style revolutionized painting despite his short career. His enigmatic masterpieces The Tempest and Sleeping Venus pioneered the genre of poetic landscape painting and the reclining nude. Only a handful of paintings are securely attributed to him.
