## Summary

Iranian serial killer who murdered at least 6 women in Kermanshah province. He was a former wrestler who strangled victims.

## Additional Context

Reporting on the case described a series of murders of women in Iran’s Kermanshah province. The attacks were characterized by strangulation and a repeated victim profile, factors that helped investigators and media frame the suspect as a serial offender.
