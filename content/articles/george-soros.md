## Summary

George Soros is a Hungarian-American billionaire investor and philanthropist who founded Soros Fund Management. He became famous as 'The Man Who Broke the Bank of England' by short-selling $10 billion worth of British pounds in 1992, earning over $1 billion in profit. Through the Open Society Foundations, he has donated over $32 billion to causes promoting democracy, human rights, and education. He is one of the most influential and controversial investors in history.
