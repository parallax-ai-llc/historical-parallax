## Summary

Jerry Falwell was an American Baptist pastor and conservative political activist who founded the Moral Majority in 1979. He served as pastor of Thomas Road Baptist Church in Lynchburg, Virginia for over 50 years. He founded Liberty University, which became one of the largest Christian universities in the world. He was a central figure in the rise of the Religious Right in American politics.
