## Summary

Tony Tan Caktiong founded Jollibee Foods Corporation, the largest fast food chain in the Philippines and one of the fastest-growing Asian food companies globally. Jollibee famously outcompeted McDonald's in the Philippine market. He expanded through acquisitions including Tim Ho Wan, The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf, and Smashburger. Jollibee now operates over 6,000 stores worldwide across 34 countries.
