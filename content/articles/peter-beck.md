## Summary

Peter Beck is the founder and CEO of Rocket Lab, which has become the world's second most frequently launched commercial rocket operator after SpaceX. He built the Electron rocket, optimized for launching small satellites, and the larger Neutron rocket for medium payloads. Rocket Lab went public in 2021. He represents the new wave of space entrepreneurs outside the United States and made New Zealand a spacefaring nation.
