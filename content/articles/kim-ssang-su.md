## Summary

Kim Ssang-su (also known as Kim Seong-su) was a Korean independence activist and industrialist who founded Kyunghee Textile Company and Dong-A Ilbo newspaper. He used his business wealth to support Korean independence and education, co-founding Korea University (originally Boseong College). He served as the second Vice President of South Korea. He is remembered for combining entrepreneurship with nationalist activism.
