## Summary

Ngô Bảo Châu is a Vietnamese-French mathematician who won the Fields Medal in 2010 for his proof of the fundamental lemma in the theory of automorphic forms, a conjecture that had been open for more than 30 years. He is the first Vietnamese person to win the Fields Medal. His work has applications in number theory and representation theory.
