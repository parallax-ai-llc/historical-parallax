## Summary

Lori Greiner is an American entrepreneur, inventor, and investor known as the 'Queen of QVC.' She holds over 120 patents and has created over 700 products. She is a shark on ABC's Shark Tank, where she has invested in over 300 companies. Her first invention, a jewelry organizer, sold millions of units. She helped launch Scrub Daddy, Squatty Potty, and other products that became household names.
