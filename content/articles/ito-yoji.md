## Summary

Ito Yoji was a Japanese marine biologist who made significant contributions to fisheries science and aquaculture technology. His research on fish breeding, nutrition, and disease control advanced Japan's aquaculture industry, making it one of the most productive in the world. He developed improved techniques for farming yellowtail and sea bream.
