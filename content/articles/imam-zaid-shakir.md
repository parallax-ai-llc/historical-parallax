## Summary

Imam Zaid Shakir is an American Muslim scholar, author, and co-founder of Zaytuna College, the first accredited Muslim liberal arts college in the United States. He is one of the most influential Muslim leaders in America, known for his scholarship, social activism, and interfaith work. He has been involved in promoting racial justice, environmental awareness, and Muslim-Christian dialogue. He is a convert to Islam who served in the US Air Force before his studies.
