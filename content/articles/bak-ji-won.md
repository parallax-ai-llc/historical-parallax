## Summary

Bak Ji-won was a Joseon Dynasty Silhak scholar and satirical writer who visited Qing China in 1780 and documented Chinese technology and modernization in Yeolha Ilgi (Jehol Diary). His detailed observations of Chinese agriculture, commerce, and technology advocated for Korean adoption of practical scientific knowledge.
