## Summary

Kevin Systrom co-founded Instagram, the photo-sharing social media platform that became one of the most popular apps in the world. He launched Instagram in 2010, and it gained one million users within two months. Facebook acquired Instagram for $1 billion in 2012. Under his leadership, Instagram introduced Stories, IGTV, and shopping features, growing to over one billion users before his departure in 2018.
