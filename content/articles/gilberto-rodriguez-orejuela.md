## Summary
Gilberto José Rodríguez Orejuela was the co-founder and leader of the Cali Cartel, the main rival to the Medellín Cartel. Known as the 'Chess Player,' he ran a more sophisticated operation than Escobar. He was extradited to the US in 2004 and sentenced to 30 years.
