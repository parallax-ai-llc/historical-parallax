## Summary

Belgian-Moroccan terrorist known as the 'Man in the Hat' who was involved in both the 2015 Paris attacks and the 2016 Brussels bombings. He was sentenced to 30 years.

Abrini is generally discussed in connection with the Islamic State-linked attack networks that operated in Belgium and France in the mid-2010s.
