## Summary

Jiro Yoshihara was a Japanese painter who founded the Gutai Art Association in 1954, one of the most influential postwar avant-garde groups. Gutai pioneered performance art, installation, and interactive art, anticipating Happenings and Fluxus. Yoshihara's circle paintings became his signature works.
