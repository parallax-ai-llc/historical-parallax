## Summary

Chung Yong-jin is the vice chairman of Shinsegae Group, South Korea's largest retail conglomerate operating department stores, discount stores (E-mart), and convenience stores. He led E-mart's transformation into a major retail force and SSG.COM's growth in e-commerce. Shinsegae Group was separated from Samsung Group in 1991. He is known for his bold management style and social media presence.
