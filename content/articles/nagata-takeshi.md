## Summary

Nagata Takeshi was a Japanese geophysicist who made pioneering contributions to the study of rock magnetism and paleomagnetism. His discovery of self-reversal of thermoremanent magnetization was crucial for understanding geomagnetic field reversals. He also led Japanese Antarctic research expeditions.
