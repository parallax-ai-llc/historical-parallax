## Summary

Italian-Moroccan terrorist who was one of three attackers in the June 2017 London Bridge attack. He and two others drove into pedestrians and then stabbed people at Borough Market. Shot dead by police.

## Incident Overview (High-Level)

The June 2017 London Bridge and Borough Market attack involved a vehicle-ramming phase followed by a knife attack in crowded public areas. It was treated by UK authorities as a terrorist incident and prompted a major emergency response in central London.

Key features commonly noted in public reporting include:

- The use of easily obtainable weapons and a fast-moving sequence of events.
- Rapid armed police response, ending with the attackers being shot.
- Significant public-safety and counterterrorism investigation activity after the incident.
