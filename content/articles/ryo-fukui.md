## Summary

Ryo Fukui was a Japanese jazz pianist from Sapporo whose album Scenery gained a cult following decades after its release. His virtuosic bebop piano style was rediscovered through the internet becoming one of the most streamed jazz albums online. His warm lyrical playing style enchanted a new generation of jazz listeners.
