## Summary

Rainer Maria Rilke was one of the most significant poets of the modern era, writing in German with extraordinary lyric intensity. His Duino Elegies and Sonnets to Orpheus are among the greatest achievements of 20th-century poetry. His Letters to a Young Poet remains one of the most influential guides to creative life.
