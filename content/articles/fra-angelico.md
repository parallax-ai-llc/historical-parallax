## Summary

Fra Angelico was an Early Renaissance Italian painter known for his deeply spiritual and luminous frescoes and altarpieces. A Dominican friar, he combined devout religious conviction with mastery of perspective and color. He was beatified by Pope John Paul II in 1982 and declared patron of Catholic artists.
