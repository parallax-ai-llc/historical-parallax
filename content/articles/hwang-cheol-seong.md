## Summary

Hwang Cheol-seong is a Korean chemist at Seoul National University who has made significant contributions to atomic layer deposition (ALD) technology for semiconductor manufacturing. His research on high-k dielectric thin films has been adopted by global semiconductor manufacturers for advanced chip production.
