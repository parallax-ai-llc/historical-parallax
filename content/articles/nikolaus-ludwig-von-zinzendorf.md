## Summary

Count Zinzendorf was a German nobleman who founded the renewed Moravian Church, one of the earliest Protestant missionary movements. He offered his estate at Herrnhut as a refuge for persecuted Moravians, creating a model Christian community. Under his leadership, the Moravians launched missions across the globe. His emphasis on personal devotion to Christ influenced John Wesley.
