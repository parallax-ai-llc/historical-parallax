## Summary

The First Transcontinental Railroad was completed on May 10, 1869, when the Central Pacific and Union Pacific met at Promontory Summit, Utah. The 'golden spike' ceremony symbolized the unification of a continent. The railroad transformed American commerce and westward expansion.
