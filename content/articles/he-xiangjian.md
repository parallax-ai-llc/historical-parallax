## Summary

He Xiangjian founded Midea Group, one of the world's largest home appliance manufacturers. Starting with a small workshop making bottle caps in 1968, he built Midea into a global appliance and robotics giant. Midea acquired KUKA, the German industrial robot manufacturer, for $5 billion in 2016. He is one of China's wealthiest individuals and a symbol of Guangdong's manufacturing prowess.
