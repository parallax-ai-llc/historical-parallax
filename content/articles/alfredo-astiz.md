## Summary

Argentine naval officer known as the 'Angel of Death' who infiltrated the Mothers of the Plaza de Mayo during the Dirty War. He was responsible for the kidnapping of two French nuns and Swedish-Argentine teenager Dagmar Hagelin. Sentenced to life.
