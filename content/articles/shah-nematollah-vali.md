## Summary

Shah Nematollah Vali was a Persian Sufi master who founded the Nimatullahi Sufi order, one of the most important Sufi orders in Iran. He was known for his prophecies, poetry, and emphasis on the unity of existence (wahdat al-wujud). He traveled extensively before settling in Mahan, Kerman, where his shrine remains a major pilgrimage site. The Nimatullahi order continues to be active in Iran and the West.
