## Summary

Watanabe Makoto Oscar is a Japanese architect and environmental designer known for his algorithmic and nature-inspired architectural designs. His Induction Design method uses computational algorithms derived from natural systems to create organic-looking structures. His subway station designs in Tokyo demonstrate the integration of technology and natural forms.
