## Summary

Shi Zhengrong founded Suntech Power, which became the world's largest solar panel manufacturer. He was called the 'Sun King' and became China's first solar billionaire. His company demonstrated that China could dominate the global solar industry. Suntech went bankrupt in 2013 after aggressive expansion and falling panel prices, but it pioneered the Chinese solar industry that now controls 80% of global solar manufacturing.
