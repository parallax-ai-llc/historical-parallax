## Summary

Mikhail Prokhorov is a Russian billionaire who made his fortune in nickel and precious metals through Norilsk Nickel, and later through various investments. He owned the Brooklyn Nets NBA team from 2010 to 2019. He ran for president of Russia in 2012, receiving 8% of the vote. He founded the Onexim Group investment company and has interests across energy, media, and technology.
