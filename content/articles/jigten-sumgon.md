## Summary

Jigten Sumgon was a Tibetan Buddhist master who founded the Drikung Kagyu school, one of the major sub-schools of the Kagyu lineage. He was a student of Phagmodrupa and is credited with writing the One Thought commentary, which presents the entire Buddhist path in a single framework. At its peak, his community at Drikung Thil had over 100,000 monastics and laypeople.
