## Summary

Dietrich Mateschitz co-founded Red Bull GmbH and created the energy drink category, building one of the most successful beverage brands in history. He discovered a Thai energy drink (Krating Daeng) and adapted it for Western markets. Red Bull's unconventional marketing through extreme sports sponsorships and media created a cultural phenomenon. He also built a sports empire including Formula One teams and football clubs.
