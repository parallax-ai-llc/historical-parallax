## Summary

Esaki Leo (Esaki Reona) is a Japanese physicist who discovered quantum tunneling in semiconductor junctions, leading to the invention of the tunnel diode (Esaki diode). He shared the 1973 Nobel Prize in Physics for this discovery. His work was foundational for modern semiconductor electronics and quantum devices.
