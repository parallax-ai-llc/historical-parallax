## Summary

Tukaram was a Marathi poet-saint and the most important figure in the Bhakti movement in Maharashtra. He composed thousands of devotional poems (abhangas) dedicated to Lord Vitthala (Vishnu) that are still widely sung today. He challenged caste discrimination and religious hypocrisy. His poetry is characterized by its emotional intensity, everyday language, and profound spiritual insight. He is considered one of the greatest saints in Hindu tradition.
