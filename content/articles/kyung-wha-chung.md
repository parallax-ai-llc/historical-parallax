## Summary

Kyung-wha Chung is a Korean violinist who was one of the first Asian classical musicians to achieve international stardom. Her passionate, technically brilliant playing brought her to prominence after sharing first prize at the Leventritt Competition in 1967. Her recordings of the Beethoven, Brahms, and Sibelius violin concertos are considered definitive.
