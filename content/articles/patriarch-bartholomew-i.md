## Summary

Patriarch Bartholomew I has served as the Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople since 1991, recognized as the spiritual leader of Orthodox Christians worldwide. He has been called the 'Green Patriarch' for his pioneering work on environmental issues from a religious perspective. He has worked tirelessly for Christian unity and interfaith dialogue. He co-signed the joint declaration on climate change with Pope Francis in 2017.
