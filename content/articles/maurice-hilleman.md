## Summary

Maurice Hilleman developed more than 40 vaccines during his career, including vaccines for measles, mumps, rubella, hepatitis A, hepatitis B, chickenpox, meningitis, and pneumonia. He is estimated to have saved more lives than any other scientist in the 20th century. He developed the MMR vaccine when his daughter caught mumps.
