## Summary

Luciano Pavarotti was an Italian operatic tenor who became the most commercially successful classical vocalist of all time. His brilliant, powerful voice and charismatic stage presence, showcased in the Three Tenors concerts with Domingo and Carreras, brought opera to a global mass audience. His rendition of Nessun dorma became a worldwide anthem.
