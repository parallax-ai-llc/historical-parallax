## Summary

Nick Molnar co-founded Afterpay, the buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) platform that revolutionized consumer payments. Starting in Australia, Afterpay expanded globally and popularized the BNPL model. Block (formerly Square) acquired Afterpay for $29 billion in 2022, one of the largest fintech acquisitions ever. Molnar was the youngest self-made billionaire in Australia at age 30.
