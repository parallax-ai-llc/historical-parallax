## Summary

Aileen Lee is the founder of Cowboy Ventures and the person who coined the term 'unicorn' to describe startups valued at $1 billion or more. Her 2013 article 'Welcome to the Unicorn Club' created one of the most commonly used terms in startup culture. She was previously a partner at Kleiner Perkins and led investments in several successful companies. Her conceptual framework for analyzing startup valuations influenced how the entire venture capital industry thinks.
