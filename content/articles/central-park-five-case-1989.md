## Summary
The Central Park Five case involved the wrongful conviction of five teenagers — Antron McCray, Kevin Richardson, Yusef Salaam, Raymond Santana, and Korey Wise — for the 1989 assault and rape of a jogger in Central Park. They were exonerated in 2002 when the actual perpetrator confessed.
