## Summary

Michael Bloomberg founded Bloomberg L.P., a global financial data and media company that transformed how financial professionals access information. The Bloomberg Terminal became indispensable on Wall Street. He served three terms as Mayor of New York City from 2002 to 2013. His philanthropy through Bloomberg Philanthropies has donated over $17 billion to education, environment, public health, and the arts.
