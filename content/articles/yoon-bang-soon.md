## Summary

Yoon Bang-soon was a Korean veterinary scientist who developed disease control methods for livestock diseases in South Korea. His research on foot-and-mouth disease and avian influenza helped protect Korea's livestock industry and contributed to developing the country's veterinary public health infrastructure.
