## Summary

Michael Mann is an American climatologist who co-created the 'hockey stick' graph showing that recent global temperatures are unprecedented in the last 1,000 years. His multiproxy temperature reconstruction became an iconic symbol of climate change science and was featured in the IPCC Third Assessment Report. He has been a vocal advocate for climate action.
