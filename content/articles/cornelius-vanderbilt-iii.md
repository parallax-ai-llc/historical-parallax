## Summary

Cornelius Vanderbilt III was an American military officer and socialite from the Vanderbilt family. He served as a brigadier general in the United States Army and was active in various civic organizations. He married Grace Graham Wilson, a prominent socialite, and they were fixtures of New York high society. He represented the transition of the Vanderbilt family from industry to society.
