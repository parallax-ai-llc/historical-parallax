## Summary

Mikhail Fridman co-founded Alfa Group, one of Russia's largest privately owned financial-industrial conglomerates. Alfa-Bank is Russia's largest private bank. He also co-founded TNK-BP, a major Russian oil company, which was sold to Rosneft for $28 billion in 2013. He relocated to London but was sanctioned by the EU following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, severely impacting his business operations.
