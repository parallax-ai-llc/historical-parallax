## Summary

Sumner Redstone built National Amusements into a media empire controlling Viacom and CBS Corporation. He coined the phrase 'content is king' and believed in the enduring value of media content over distribution. He survived a major hotel fire in 1979, an experience that shaped his tenacious approach to business. His conglomerate controlled Paramount Pictures, MTV, Nickelodeon, and BET.
