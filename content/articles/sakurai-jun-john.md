## Summary

Jun John Sakurai was a Japanese-American theoretical physicist who made important contributions to particle physics and wrote one of the most widely used graduate textbooks in quantum mechanics, 'Modern Quantum Mechanics.' His textbook, completed posthumously by San Fu Tuan, remains the standard reference for teaching quantum mechanics at the graduate level.
