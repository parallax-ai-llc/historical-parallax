## Summary

Robert Bellarmine was an Italian Jesuit cardinal and one of the most important figures of the Counter-Reformation. His Disputationes was the most comprehensive defense of Catholic doctrine against Protestantism. He was involved in the trials of Giordano Bruno and Galileo. He was named a Doctor of the Church in 1931.
