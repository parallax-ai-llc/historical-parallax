## Summary

John Jumper led the development of AlphaFold2 at DeepMind, an AI system that accurately predicts the 3D structures of proteins from their amino acid sequences. This breakthrough solved a 50-year grand challenge in biology. He shared the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for protein structure prediction.
