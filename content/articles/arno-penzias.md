## Summary

Arno Penzias is a German-born American physicist who shared the 1978 Nobel Prize in Physics with Robert Wilson for the discovery of cosmic microwave background radiation. Born to a Jewish family, he fled Nazi Germany as a child on the Kindertransport. His accidental discovery at Bell Labs of the remnant radiation from the Big Bang confirmed predictions of cosmic expansion theory.
