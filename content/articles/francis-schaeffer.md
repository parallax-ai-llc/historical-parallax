## Summary

Francis Schaeffer was an American evangelical theologian and philosopher who founded the L'Abri Fellowship in Switzerland. His books and films, including How Should We Then Live? and Whatever Happened to the Human Race?, influenced the rise of the Religious Right in America. He argued that Christianity provided the only coherent worldview and critiqued modern relativism. He was one of the most influential evangelical intellectuals of the 20th century.
