## Summary

Niccol√≤ Tartaglia was an Italian mathematician and engineer who discovered the general solution to the cubic equation. His solution was published by Cardano in 'Ars Magna,' leading to a famous mathematical dispute. He also made important contributions to ballistics, publishing the first science of ballistics, and translated Euclid's 'Elements' into Italian.
