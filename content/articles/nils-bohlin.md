## Summary

Nils Bohlin invented the three-point seatbelt in 1959 while working at Volvo. His design, which secures both the chest and hips with a single belt, has saved over a million lives worldwide. Volvo made the patent freely available to all car manufacturers, prioritizing safety over profit. The three-point seatbelt is considered one of the most important safety inventions.
