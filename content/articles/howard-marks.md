## Summary
Howard Marks, known as 'Mr Nice,' was a Welsh drug smuggler who was one of the world's most wanted drug traffickers in the 1980s. He smuggled marijuana using 43 aliases and 89 phone lines. He served seven years in US prison and became a best-selling author and campaigner for drug legalization.
