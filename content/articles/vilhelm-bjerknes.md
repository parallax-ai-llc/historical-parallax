## Summary

Vilhelm Bjerknes is considered the father of modern weather forecasting. He developed the theory that weather prediction could be based on the laws of physics and pioneered the concept of air masses and weather fronts. His Bergen School of Meteorology trained generations of meteorologists and revolutionized weather prediction methods.
