## Summary

Geshe Lhundub Sopa was a Tibetan Buddhist scholar and monk who was one of the most important Tibetan Buddhist teachers in America. He was a professor at the University of Wisconsin-Madison for over 30 years and founded the Deer Park Buddhist Center. He was instrumental in inviting the Dalai Lama to conduct the Kalachakra initiation in the United States. His commentaries on Tsongkhapa's Lamrim Chenmo are widely respected.
