## Summary

Former mayor of Nanjing convicted of accepting 11.3 million yuan in bribes. He was known for his massive infrastructure projects that were later linked to kickback schemes.

In the 2000s and early 2010s, many Chinese cities pursued rapid urban expansion, often relying on land sales, local-government financing vehicles, and extensive contracting networks.
