## Summary
Leonard Thomas Lake was an American serial killer who, with Charles Ng, kidnapped, raped, tortured, and murdered between 11 and 25 people at his ranch in Calaveras County, California. He built a bunker where victims were held. He killed himself by swallowing cyanide after arrest in 1985.
