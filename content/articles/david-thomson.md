## Summary

David Thomson is the chairman of Thomson Reuters and the richest person in Canada. His family's fortune began with his grandfather Roy Thomson, who built a newspaper empire. Thomson Reuters is the world's largest provider of financial data and legal research. The Thomson family also controls The Globe and Mail, Canada's national newspaper, and the Thomson Collection of art. His net worth exceeds $50 billion.
