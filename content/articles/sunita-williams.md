## Summary

Sunita Williams holds records for most spacewalk time by a female astronaut and ran the first marathon in space (Boston Marathon on a treadmill aboard the ISS). She flew on two space shuttle missions and served as ISS commander. Her Indian-Slovenian heritage made her a role model for diversity in space exploration.
