## Summary

Richard Liu (Liu Qiangdong) founded JD.com, one of China's two largest e-commerce companies alongside Alibaba. JD.com differentiated itself through its direct sales model and massive logistics network, delivering to 99% of China's population. He built one of the most extensive delivery networks in China with 250,000 logistics employees. He stepped back from daily management after personal controversies.
