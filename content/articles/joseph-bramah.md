## Summary

Joseph Bramah was a prolific inventor who developed the hydraulic press, an improved flush toilet, the beer engine (beer pump), and a famously unpickable lock. His hydraulic press, based on Pascal's principle, was fundamental to industrial manufacturing. He was one of the most important inventors of the Industrial Revolution.
