## Summary

C. Walton Lillehei, known as the father of open-heart surgery, pioneered cross-circulation technique where a parent's circulatory system sustained a child during heart surgery. He trained more cardiac surgeons than any other person and developed numerous innovations in cardiac surgery including early pacemakers and heart valves.
