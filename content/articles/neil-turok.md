## Summary

Neil Turok is a South African theoretical physicist and cosmologist who has made important contributions to cosmology, including the cyclic model of the universe with Paul Steinhardt. He served as director of the Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics and founded the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), which has trained thousands of African scientists across multiple countries.
