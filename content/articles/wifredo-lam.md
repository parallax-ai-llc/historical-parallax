## Summary

Wifredo Lam was a Cuban painter who fused Surrealism and Cubism with Afro-Caribbean imagery and symbolism. His masterpiece The Jungle combines modernist formal innovation with the spiritual traditions of his mixed Chinese, African, and European heritage. He was close to Picasso and Breton and remains the most internationally celebrated Cuban artist.
