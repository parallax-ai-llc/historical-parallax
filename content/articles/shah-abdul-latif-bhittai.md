## Summary

Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai was a Sindhi Sufi poet and musician who is regarded as the greatest poet of Sindh. His Shah Jo Risalo (collection of poetry) is the masterpiece of Sindhi literature, exploring divine love through the folk tales and landscapes of Sindh. He also created a musical instrument called the tamburo. His poetry addresses themes of mystical longing, social justice, and the unity of existence.
