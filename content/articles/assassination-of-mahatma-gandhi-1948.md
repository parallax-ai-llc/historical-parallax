## Overview
On January 30, 1948, Mahatma Gandhi was assassinated in the garden of Birla House (now Gandhi Smriti) in New Delhi.

## The Event
Gandhi was on his way to a prayer meeting when Nathuram Godse, a Hindu nationalist, approached him and fired three shots into his chest. Gandhis last words were reportedly "He Ram" (Oh God).

## Impact
Gandhis death was mourned globally. He is revered as the Father of the Nation in India, and his philosophy of non-violence (Ahimsa) continues to inspire movements worldwide.
