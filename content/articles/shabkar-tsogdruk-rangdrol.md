## Summary

Shabkar Tsogdruk Rangdrol was a Tibetan Buddhist yogi, poet, and vegetarian advocate who is one of the most beloved spiritual figures in Tibetan literature. His autobiography, The Life of Shabkar, is one of the greatest spiritual autobiographies in any tradition, describing his years of meditation in remote caves and his pilgrimages across Tibet. He was a strong advocate for vegetarianism and animal welfare, unusual for his time.
