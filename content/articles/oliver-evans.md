## Summary

Oliver Evans built the first high-pressure steam engine in America and created the first automated flour mill, using conveyor belts and bucket elevators. He also built one of the first amphibious vehicles, the Oruktor Amphibolos. His innovations in automation and steam power were ahead of their time and influenced American industrialization.
