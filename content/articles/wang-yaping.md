## Summary

Wang Yaping became the second Chinese woman in space in 2013 aboard Shenzhou-10 and conducted China's first live broadcast space lecture to 60 million students. In 2021, she flew on Shenzhou-13, becoming the first Chinese woman to perform a spacewalk. She is a role model for women in China's space program.
