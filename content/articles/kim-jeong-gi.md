## Summary

Kim Jeong-gi was a Korean artist famous for his extraordinary ability to draw incredibly detailed and accurate illustrations from memory without any preliminary sketches. His remarkable spatial memory and anatomical knowledge demonstrated exceptional neurological capabilities that fascinated both art and science communities worldwide.
