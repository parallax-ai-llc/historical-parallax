## Summary

Chogyur Dechen Lingpa was one of the most important Tibetan Buddhist treasure revealers (tertons) of the 19th century. He was a key figure in the Rime movement alongside Jamyang Khyentse Wangpo and Jamgon Kongtrul. He discovered numerous terma (hidden treasure teachings) attributed to Padmasambhava. His terma cycle, the Seven Profound Cycles, is widely practiced in the Nyingma and Kagyu schools. His lineage continues through his descendants.
