## Summary

Karl Rahner was a German Jesuit priest and one of the most influential Catholic theologians of the 20th century. His concept of the 'anonymous Christian' and his transcendental method reshaped Catholic theology. He served as an expert advisor at the Second Vatican Council. His multi-volume Theological Investigations covered virtually every area of Catholic theology.
