## Summary

Shin Hee-sup was a Korean microbiologist who contributed to the development of vaccines in South Korea. His research on viral vaccines helped establish Korea's domestic vaccine manufacturing capabilities, reducing dependence on imported vaccines and improving public health infrastructure throughout the country.
