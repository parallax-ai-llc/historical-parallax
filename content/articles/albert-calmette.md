## Summary

Albert Calmette, together with Camille Guérin, developed the BCG (Bacillus Calmette-Guérin) vaccine against tuberculosis, one of the most widely used vaccines in history. He also developed antivenoms for snake bites while working in Saigon. His contributions to public health in tropical medicine were foundational.
