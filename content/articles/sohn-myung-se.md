## Summary

Sohn Myung-se is a Korean archaeologist who discovered Paleolithic hand axes at Jeongok-ri in 1978, overturning the belief that East Asia had no Acheulean hand axe culture. This discovery by an American soldier-turned-archaeologist fundamentally changed our understanding of prehistoric human migration and technology development in East Asia.
