## Summary

Nambu Yoichiro was a Japanese-American physicist who won the 2008 Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the mechanism of spontaneous broken symmetry in particle physics. His work explained how particles acquire mass and was foundational for the Standard Model. The Nambu-Goldstone theorem and Nambu-Jona-Lasinio model are named after him.
