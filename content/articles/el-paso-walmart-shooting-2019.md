## Summary
On August 3, 2019, a gunman opened fire at a Walmart in El Paso, Texas, killing 23 people. The shooter targeted Hispanic shoppers and posted a white supremacist manifesto online before the attack. He pleaded guilty to federal hate crimes and was sentenced to 90 consecutive life sentences.
