## Summary

Nicholas Hlobo is a South African artist whose sculptures and performances use rubber, ribbon, and leather to explore Xhosa cultural identity and queer sexuality. His biomorphic forms reference traditional Xhosa initiation ceremonies while addressing contemporary South African social dynamics.
