## Additional Background

“El Güero Palma” is the alias of Héctor Luis Palma Salazar, a Mexican drug trafficker described in open sources as a senior figure in the early formation and evolution of the Sinaloa Cartel network. He is frequently mentioned in connection with factional conflict among major trafficking organizations in Mexico during the 1990s and subsequent decades.[^1]
