## Summary

Bjarke Ingels is a Danish architect who founded BIG (Bjarke Ingels Group). Known for his concept of 'hedonistic sustainability,' his designs combine social responsibility with playful form. Projects like the 8 House, CopenHill (a ski slope on a waste-to-energy plant), and the Lego House exemplify his innovative approach.
