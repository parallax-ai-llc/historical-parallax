## Summary

Aditya Birla expanded the Aditya Birla Group from an Indian conglomerate into a global industrial powerhouse spanning aluminum, cement, carbon black, and textiles. He was among the first Indian industrialists to invest abroad, establishing companies in Southeast Asia, Egypt, and North America. Under his leadership, the group became India's third-largest business house. He died young at 51, and his son Kumar Mangalam Birla continued the expansion.
