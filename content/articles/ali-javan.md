## Summary

Ali Javan was an Iranian-American physicist who co-invented the first gas laser (helium-neon laser) in 1960 at Bell Labs. The gas laser became one of the most widely used types of lasers in science, industry, and telecommunications. His invention was a milestone in the development of laser technology and optical communications.
