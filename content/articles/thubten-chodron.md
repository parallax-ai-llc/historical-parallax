## Summary

Thubten Chodron is an American Tibetan Buddhist nun, author, and founder of Sravasti Abbey in Newport, Washington. She has written over 20 books on Buddhism and has been instrumental in establishing full ordination for Buddhist nuns. She is a student of the 14th Dalai Lama and one of the foremost Western authorities on Tibetan Buddhist monastic training. Her book Buddhism for Beginners has been translated into many languages.
