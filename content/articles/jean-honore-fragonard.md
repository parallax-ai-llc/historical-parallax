## Summary

Jean-Honore Fragonard was one of the leading French Rococo painters, known for exuberant paintings of love and pleasure. His iconic The Swing captures the playful spirit of the pre-Revolutionary French aristocracy. His loose, energetic brushwork anticipated aspects of Impressionism by a century.
