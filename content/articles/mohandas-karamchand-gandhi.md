## Summary

Mahatma Gandhi was an Indian political and spiritual leader who led India's independence movement through non-violent civil disobedience. He drew deeply on Hindu philosophy, Jain non-violence (ahimsa), and the teachings of Jesus and Tolstoy. His concept of satyagraha (truth-force) combined spiritual practice with political activism. He is regarded as one of the greatest spiritual and political leaders of the 20th century.
