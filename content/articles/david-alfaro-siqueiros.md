## Summary

David Alfaro Siqueiros was a Mexican muralist known for his dynamic, politically charged murals and his innovations in industrial painting techniques. Along with Rivera and Orozco, he formed the triumvirate of Mexican Muralism. His revolutionary activism, including his role in an assassination attempt on Trotsky, made him one of the most controversial artists of the 20th century.
