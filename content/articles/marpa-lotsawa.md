## Summary

Marpa Lotsawa (Marpa the Translator) was a Tibetan Buddhist teacher who founded the Kagyu lineage by bringing tantric teachings from India to Tibet. He made three perilous journeys to India to study under the great masters Naropa and Maitripa. Despite being a married farmer and householder, he became one of Tibet's most important spiritual masters. His most famous student was Milarepa.
