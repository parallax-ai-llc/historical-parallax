## Summary
Edward Teach, known as Blackbeard, was the most notorious pirate of the Golden Age of Piracy. He terrorized the West Indies and the eastern coast of the American colonies from 1716 to 1718. He was killed in battle with Lieutenant Robert Maynard of the Royal Navy off North Carolina.
