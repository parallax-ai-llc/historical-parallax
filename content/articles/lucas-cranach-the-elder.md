## Summary

Lucas Cranach the Elder was a major German Renaissance painter who served as court painter to the Electors of Saxony. He was a close friend of Martin Luther and created iconic portraits of the reformer. His distinctive elongated nudes combined Northern European precision with a unique sensual elegance.
