The 'Ndrangheta is the Calabrian Mafia and is now considered the most powerful organized crime group in Italy and one of the most powerful in Europe. Unlike the Sicilian Mafia, it is organized into family-based 'ndrine (cells). It controls much of Europe's cocaine trade.
