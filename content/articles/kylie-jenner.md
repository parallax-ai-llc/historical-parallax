## Summary

Kylie Jenner is an American media personality and entrepreneur who founded Kylie Cosmetics, leveraging her massive social media following into a beauty brand that generated $420 million in retail sales in its first 18 months. She sold a 51% stake to Coty Inc. for $600 million in 2020. Forbes controversially named her the world's youngest self-made billionaire, though this was later revised. She exemplifies the influencer-to-entrepreneur pipeline.
