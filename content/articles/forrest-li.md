## Summary

Forrest Li (Li Xiaodong) founded Sea Limited in Singapore, building it into Southeast Asia's most valuable technology company. Sea operates Shopee (e-commerce), Garena (gaming, including Free Fire), and SeaMoney (fintech). Free Fire became the most downloaded mobile game globally in 2019-2021. Sea's stock rose from its 2017 IPO to make Li one of the wealthiest people in Southeast Asia before market corrections.
