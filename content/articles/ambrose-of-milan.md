## Summary

Ambrose was the Bishop of Milan and one of the four original Doctors of the Church. He was a powerful preacher who influenced Augustine's conversion to Christianity. He famously stood up to Emperor Theodosius I, forcing him to do public penance. He also made significant contributions to church music and hymnology.
