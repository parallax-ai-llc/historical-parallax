## Summary

Eddie Lampert is the founder of ESL Investments and the former chairman and CEO of Sears Holdings. He was once called the 'next Warren Buffett' for his investment prowess. However, his management of Sears and Kmart became one of the most controversial episodes in American retail history, as the companies declined under his leadership and eventually filed for bankruptcy in 2018. He acquired Sears out of bankruptcy through a controversial bid.
