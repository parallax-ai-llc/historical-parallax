## Summary

Abdullah Al Subeaei is a prominent Saudi Arabian banker and businessman who served as chairman of SAMBA Financial Group. He played a key role in modernizing Saudi Arabia's banking sector. Under his leadership, SAMBA became one of the most profitable banks in the Middle East. He represents the professionalization of Saudi financial institutions.
