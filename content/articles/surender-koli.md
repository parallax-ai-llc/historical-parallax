## Summary
Surinder Koli was an Indian serial killer convicted of raping and murdering at least 19 children near Noida, Uttar Pradesh, between 2005 and 2006. His employer, Moninder Singh Pandher, was also charged. Human remains were found in drains around their home in Nithari. The case is known as the Nithari killings.
