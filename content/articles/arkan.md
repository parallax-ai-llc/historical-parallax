## Summary
Željko Ražnatović, known as Arkan, was a Serbian paramilitary commander, crime boss, and indicted war criminal. He led the Serbian Volunteer Guard (Arkan's Tigers) during the Yugoslav Wars. He was indicted for crimes against humanity by the ICTY. He was assassinated in Belgrade in 2000.
