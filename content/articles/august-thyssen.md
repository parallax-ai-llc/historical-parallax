## Summary

August Thyssen founded the Thyssen steel and industrial empire, which became one of the largest in Germany. He built an integrated industrial conglomerate spanning steel, coal, machinery, and shipping. His company later merged with Krupp to form ThyssenKrupp, one of Germany's largest industrial companies. He was known for his autocratic management style and relentless expansion.
