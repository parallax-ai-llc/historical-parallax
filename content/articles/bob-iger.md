## Summary

Bob Iger served as CEO of The Walt Disney Company from 2005 to 2020 and returned in 2022, overseeing a transformative era of acquisitions and expansion. He acquired Pixar, Marvel, Lucasfilm, and 21st Century Fox, making Disney the dominant force in entertainment. He launched Disney+ streaming service in 2019, which quickly attracted over 150 million subscribers. Under his leadership, Disney's market value grew from $46 billion to over $250 billion.
