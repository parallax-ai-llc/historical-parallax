## Summary

Jang Seung-eop (Owon) was one of the last great traditional Korean painters of the Joseon dynasty. Self-taught and of humble origins, he became one of the most celebrated artists of his era with his bold, expressive brushwork in paintings of animals, flowers, and landscapes. He is one of the Three Wons of Korean painting alongside Danwon and Hyewon.
