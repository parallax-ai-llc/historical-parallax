## Summary

Kwek Leng Beng is the executive chairman of Hong Leong Group Singapore, one of the largest conglomerates in Southeast Asia. He controls CDL (City Developments Limited), one of the largest hotel owners globally, with the Millennium and Copthorne hotel chains. The Hong Leong Group spans real estate, hospitality, finance, and trading. He is one of Singapore's most influential business leaders.
