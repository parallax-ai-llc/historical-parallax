## Summary

Park Jin-young (J.Y. Park) founded JYP Entertainment in 1997, one of K-pop's 'Big Three' agencies. He produced globally successful acts including Wonder Girls, 2PM, GOT7, TWICE, and Stray Kids. As both a performer and producer, he shaped the sound and style of K-pop for three decades. JYP Entertainment became one of the most valuable entertainment companies in Asia.
