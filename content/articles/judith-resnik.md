## Summary

Judith Resnik was the second American woman in space and the first Jewish American astronaut. She flew on the maiden voyage of the Space Shuttle Discovery in 1984 and was killed in the Challenger disaster in 1986. She was also a biomedical engineer who developed a special software system for NASA's telemetry.
