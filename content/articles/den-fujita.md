## Summary

Den Fujita brought McDonald's to Japan in 1971, founding McDonald's Japan and making it the company's most successful international franchise. He opened the first Japanese McDonald's in Tokyo's Ginza district. Under his leadership, McDonald's Japan grew to over 3,000 locations. His unconventional marketing and localization strategies became a model for international franchise operations.
