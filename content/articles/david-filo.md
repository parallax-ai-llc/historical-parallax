## Summary

David Filo co-founded Yahoo! with Jerry Yang in 1995, building one of the first and most important internet companies. He served as Yahoo!'s chief architect and was the technical mind behind the company's early growth. Unlike many tech founders, Filo shunned the limelight and focused on engineering. He remained at Yahoo! through its acquisition by Verizon in 2017.
