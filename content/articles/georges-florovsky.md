## Summary

Georges Florovsky was a Russian Orthodox theologian and historian who was one of the founders of the neo-patristic synthesis in Orthodox theology. He called for a 'return to the Fathers' as the basis for theological renewal. He was a founding member of the World Council of Churches and taught at several American universities, including Harvard and Princeton. His work profoundly shaped modern Orthodox theology.
