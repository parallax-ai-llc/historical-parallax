## Summary

Dorothea Lange was an American documentary photographer whose images of the Great Depression profoundly influenced the development of documentary photography. Her iconic Migrant Mother is one of the most famous photographs in history. Her work for the Farm Security Administration documented the human cost of poverty and displacement.
