## Quick Facts

- **Known for:** Longtime attorney and fixer for Donald J. Trump; later became a cooperating witness in multiple investigations.
- **Public profile:** Moved from behind-the-scenes legal work into a highly public legal and media presence after 2018.
- **Core legal outcomes:** Pleaded guilty to federal crimes and served a prison sentence.

## Background

- Practiced law in the United States and worked in the private sector prior to becoming closely associated with Trump and the Trump Organization.
- Became widely covered in U.S. politics and legal news during the investigations and prosecutions connected to the 2016 U.S. election period.

## Legal Issues (High-Level)

- **Campaign finance-related conduct:** Admitted to conduct tied to payments intended to influence the 2016 election.
- **False statements to Congress:** Admitted to lying to Congress in connection with testimony about business matters.
- **Sentencing and custody:** Served time in federal custody and later completed supervised release.

## Why This Matters

- Cohen’s case is often cited as an example of how personal lawyers can face criminal exposure when business, political activity, and legal strategy blur.
- His cooperation and public testimony contributed to broader public understanding of legal and political controversies surrounding the Trump era.
