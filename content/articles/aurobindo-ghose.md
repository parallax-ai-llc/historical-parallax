## Summary

Sri Aurobindo was an Indian philosopher, yogi, and revolutionary leader who developed Integral Yoga and a comprehensive philosophy of spiritual evolution. Initially a leader in India's independence movement, he withdrew to Pondicherry to pursue his spiritual practice. His magnum opus The Life Divine presents a vision of the evolution of consciousness toward a divine life on earth. He and his spiritual collaborator the Mother founded the Sri Aurobindo Ashram.
