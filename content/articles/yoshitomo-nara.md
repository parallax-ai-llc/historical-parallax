## Summary

Yoshitomo Nara is a Japanese artist known for his seemingly cute but emotionally complex paintings and sculptures of children and animals. His work combines influences from manga, punk music, and lonely childhood memories. He is one of the most important figures in Japanese Neo-Pop art.
