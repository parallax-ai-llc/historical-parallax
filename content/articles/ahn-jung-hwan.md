## Club Career Overview

Ahn Jung-hwan played for clubs in South Korea and abroad, including spells in Italy (Perugia) and Japan, and was known for his aerial ability and clutch scoring.

## 2002 World Cup Significance

- The 2002 FIFA World Cup, co-hosted by South Korea and Japan, became a defining moment for South Korean football.
- Ahn’s extra-time golden goal against Italy in the Round of 16 is often remembered as a turning point in public perception of Korean football on the global stage.
