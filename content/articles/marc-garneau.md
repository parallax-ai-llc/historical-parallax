## Summary

Marc Garneau was the first Canadian in space, flying aboard the Space Shuttle Challenger in 1984. He flew three space shuttle missions in total, making him Canada's most experienced astronaut. He later served as president of the Canadian Space Agency and entered politics, serving as a Member of Parliament and government minister.
