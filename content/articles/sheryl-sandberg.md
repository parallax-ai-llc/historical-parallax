## Summary

Sheryl Sandberg served as COO of Meta (Facebook) from 2008 to 2022, playing a crucial role in building the company's advertising business into a $100+ billion revenue machine. Before Facebook, she was a key executive at Google. Her book 'Lean In' sparked a global conversation about women in leadership. She helped transform Facebook from a startup into one of the world's most profitable companies.
