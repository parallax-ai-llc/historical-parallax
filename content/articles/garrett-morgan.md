## Summary

Garrett Morgan was an African American inventor who created the traffic signal and the safety hood (an early gas mask). His gas mask was used to rescue workers trapped in a tunnel under Lake Erie in 1916. His three-position traffic signal patent was purchased by General Electric. He overcame significant racial barriers to achieve commercial success.
