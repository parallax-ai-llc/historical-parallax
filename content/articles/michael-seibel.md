## Summary

Michael Seibel is a managing director of Y Combinator and co-founder of Justin.tv (which became Twitch). He was CEO of Justin.tv when it pivoted to Twitch, the gaming live-streaming platform that Amazon acquired for $970 million in 2014. He was one of the first Black partners at YC and has helped fund numerous diverse founders. He became an influential figure in startup mentoring and diversity in tech.
