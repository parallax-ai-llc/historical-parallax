## Summary
Philip Onyancha (Ondi Magambo) was a Kenyan serial killer who confessed to killing 17 women in Nairobi and other Kenyan cities between 2002 and 2010. He claimed to be driven by a childhood pact with the devil. He drank some victims' blood.
