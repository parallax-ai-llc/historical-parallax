## Summary

Brook Taylor developed the Taylor series expansion, one of the most important tools in mathematical analysis and applied mathematics. The Taylor theorem allows any smooth function to be approximated by a polynomial, fundamental to physics, engineering, and computer science. He also made contributions to the theory of perspective in art.
