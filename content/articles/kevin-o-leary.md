## Summary

Kevin O'Leary, known as 'Mr. Wonderful,' is a Canadian businessman and investor who co-founded The Learning Company, which Mattel acquired for $3.7 billion in 1999 (though the deal later proved disastrous for Mattel). He is known for his blunt style as an investor on both Dragons' Den and Shark Tank. He founded O'Leary Financial Group and is a vocal advocate for financial literacy and entrepreneurship.
