## Summary

Jacob Rothschild, 4th Baron Rothschild, was a British investment banker and member of the famous Rothschild banking family. He founded J Rothschild Assurance Group (later St James's Place Wealth Management) and RIT Capital Partners, one of the UK's largest investment trusts. He was a major philanthropist and patron of the arts, supporting cultural institutions including the National Gallery and Waddesdon Manor.
