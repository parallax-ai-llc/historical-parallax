## Summary

Zong Qinghou founded Hangzhou Wahaha Group, China's largest beverage company, and was China's richest person in 2010 and 2012. He built Wahaha from a school-run grocery store into a beverage empire selling products across China. His company's distribution network reached the remotest parts of China. He was known for his frugal lifestyle despite his vast wealth.
