## Summary

Ali Shariati was an Iranian sociologist and philosopher who was one of the most influential intellectual architects of the Iranian Revolution. He reinterpreted Shia Islam as a revolutionary ideology compatible with modern social justice. His lectures at the Husayniyya Ershad in Tehran drew massive audiences. He died under mysterious circumstances in England shortly before the revolution he helped inspire.
