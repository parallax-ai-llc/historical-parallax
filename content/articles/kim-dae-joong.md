## Summary

Kim Dae-joong is a Korean geotechnical engineer who contributed to the development of tunnel and underground construction technology in South Korea. His engineering innovations in shield tunneling and ground reinforcement were crucial for building Seoul's extensive subway system and other underground infrastructure projects.
