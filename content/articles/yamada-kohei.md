## Summary

Yamada Kohei was a Japanese fermentation scientist who developed industrial fermentation processes for producing amino acids, vitamins, and other bioactive compounds. His work at Kyoto University advanced Japan's biotechnology industry, particularly in the production of glutamic acid and other food ingredients.
