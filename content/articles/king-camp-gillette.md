## Summary

King Camp Gillette invented the disposable safety razor blade and founded the Gillette Safety Razor Company. His innovation of cheap, disposable blades paired with a durable razor handle created the 'razor and blades' business model used across many industries. The company became a global consumer products giant. He also wrote utopian social philosophy books advocating for a single world corporation.
