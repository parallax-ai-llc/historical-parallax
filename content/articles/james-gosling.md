## Summary

James Gosling is a Canadian computer scientist best known as the creator of the Java programming language. Developed at Sun Microsystems in the mid-1990s, Java became one of the most popular and widely-used programming languages with its 'write once, run anywhere' philosophy. Java's platform independence and object-oriented design made it the foundation for enterprise software, Android apps, and web services.
