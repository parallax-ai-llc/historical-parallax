## Summary

Mike Adenuga is a Nigerian billionaire and one of Africa's wealthiest people. He founded Globacom, Nigeria's second-largest mobile network, and Conoil, a major oil exploration company. He built his initial fortune in the oil industry before expanding into telecommunications, real estate, and banking. Globacom (Glo) became the first company to lay a submarine fiber optic cable from the UK to Nigeria.
