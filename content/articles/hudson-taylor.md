## Summary

Hudson Taylor was an English missionary to China and the founder of the China Inland Mission (now OMF International). He spent 51 years in China and was instrumental in bringing over 800 missionaries to the country. He adopted Chinese dress and customs, pioneering a new approach to cross-cultural mission. His faith-based funding model influenced many later missionary organizations.
