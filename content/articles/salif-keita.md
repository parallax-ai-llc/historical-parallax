## Summary

Salif Keita is a Malian singer known as the Golden Voice of Africa. Born an albino into a noble Mandinka family his singing was considered taboo but he persevered to become one of Africa most celebrated musicians. His fusion of traditional Mandinka music with Western pop rock and electronic elements created Afro-pop.
