## Summary

Ahmed Yasawi was a Turkic Sufi poet and mystic who founded the Yasawi Sufi order, one of the earliest Turkic Sufi brotherhoods. His collection of mystical poems, Divan-i Hikmet (Collection of Wisdom), is written in Turkic and became central to Central Asian Sufi spirituality. He is considered the most important spiritual figure in Turkic Islamic history. Timur later built a grand mausoleum over his tomb in Turkestan.
