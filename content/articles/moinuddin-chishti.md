## Summary

Moinuddin Chishti was a Persian Sufi saint who brought the Chishti Order to India and established it in Ajmer, Rajasthan. His dargah in Ajmer is one of the most important Sufi pilgrimage sites in the world, visited by millions of people of all faiths. He emphasized love, tolerance, and service to humanity. He is known as Gharib Nawaz (Benefactor of the Poor) and is credited with the peaceful spread of Islam in the Indian subcontinent.
