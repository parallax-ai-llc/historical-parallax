## Summary

Abhinav Bindra won India's first individual Olympic gold medal in the 10m air rifle at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.

## Early Career

Bindra began competing internationally at a young age and became one of India’s most prominent sport shooters. His rise coincided with increased investment in training, sports science, and international competition exposure for elite Indian athletes.

## Major Results and Recognition

In addition to the 2008 Olympic title, Bindra is noted for success at world-level events, including world championship and World Cup competitions. His Olympic gold is frequently cited as a milestone for India in individual Olympic sport, alongside later breakthroughs by other athletes.

## Post-Competition Work

After stepping back from peak competition, Bindra has been involved in sports development and athlete support initiatives, and he is often referenced in discussions about professionalizing elite sport pathways in India.
