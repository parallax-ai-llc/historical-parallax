## Summary

Matsuoka Hisato was a Japanese agricultural scientist who developed improved silkworm breeding methods that increased Japan's silk production quality. His systematic selective breeding of silkworms applied Mendelian genetics to sericulture, helping Japan dominate the world silk market in the early 20th century.
