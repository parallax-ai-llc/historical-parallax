## Summary
Derrick Todd Lee, the 'Baton Rouge Serial Killer,' was an American serial killer who murdered at least seven women in Louisiana between 1992 and 2003. He was identified through DNA evidence. He was convicted of two murders and sentenced to death. He died of heart disease on death row in 2016.
