## Summary
Adrian Lamo was an American hacker known as the 'Homeless Hacker' for his use of coffee shops and libraries for hacking. He gained notoriety for hacking Microsoft, Yahoo!, and The New York Times. He later became controversial for reporting Chelsea Manning to the FBI. He died at age 37.
