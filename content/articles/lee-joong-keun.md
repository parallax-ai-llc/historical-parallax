## Summary

Lee Joong-keun was the founder of Dongkuk Steel Group, one of South Korea's major steel producers. He played an important role in the development of South Korea's steel industry during the country's rapid industrialization. Dongkuk Steel supplied materials for major construction projects including bridges, buildings, and infrastructure across Korea.
