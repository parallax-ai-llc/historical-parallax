## Summary

The Siege of Acre (1189-1191) was a key engagement of the Third Crusade. Crusaders besieged the Muslim-held city for nearly two years before Richard the Lionheart's arrival turned the tide. Saladin's relief attempts failed, and Acre became the Crusader capital.
