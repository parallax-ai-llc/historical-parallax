## Summary

Georges de La Tour was a French Baroque painter known for dramatic nocturnal scenes illuminated by candlelight. His serene compositions show Caravaggio's influence filtered through a French sensibility. Despite being named Painter to the King, he was almost completely forgotten until the 20th century.
