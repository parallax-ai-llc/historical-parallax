## Summary

Rakesh Sharma became the first Indian in space when he flew aboard the Soviet Soyuz T-11 mission to the Salyut 7 space station in 1984. He conducted Earth observation experiments and yoga exercises in microgravity. When asked by Prime Minister Indira Gandhi how India looked from space, he famously replied 'Saare jahan se accha' (the best in the world).
