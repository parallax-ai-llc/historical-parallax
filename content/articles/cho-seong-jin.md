## Summary

Cho Seong-jin is a Korean pianist who won the International Chopin Piano Competition in 2015, the first Korean to win this prestigious award. His elegant, poetic playing has earned him a major recording contract with Deutsche Grammophon and performances with the world's leading orchestras. He is one of the most acclaimed pianists of his generation.
