## Summary

Birbal Sahni was an Indian paleobotanist who made pioneering contributions to the study of plant fossils from the Indian subcontinent. He established Indian paleobotany as a distinct scientific discipline and founded the Birbal Sahni Institute of Palaeosciences. His work on Gondwana fossils was internationally recognized and helped reconstruct ancient continental distributions.
