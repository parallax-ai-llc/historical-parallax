## Summary

Joseph Safra was a Brazilian-Lebanese banker who built the Safra banking empire into one of the world's most respected private banking groups. Safra National Bank of New York and Banco Safra in Brazil served ultra-high-net-worth clients worldwide. He was one of the wealthiest bankers in the world. His family's banking tradition spans three generations and multiple continents, from Aleppo to São Paulo to Geneva.
