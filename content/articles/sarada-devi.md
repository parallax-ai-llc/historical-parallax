## Summary

Sarada Devi, known as the Holy Mother, was the wife and spiritual companion of Sri Ramakrishna and one of the most revered figures in the Ramakrishna movement. After Ramakrishna's death, she became the spiritual guide of his disciples and the wider movement. She was known for her extraordinary compassion, treating all who came to her as her own children. She said 'I am the mother of the wicked, as I am the mother of the virtuous.' She is considered an incarnation of the Divine Mother.
