## Summary

Lu Ban was an ancient Chinese carpenter, engineer, and inventor who is revered as the patron saint of Chinese builders. He is credited with inventing the saw, the carpenter's plane, the drill, the shovel, and the grappling hook. He also created a wooden bird that could fly for three days. He was considered the greatest artisan in Chinese history.
