## Summary

Ulmas Mirsaidov was a Tajik physicist who contributed to materials science and semiconductor research in Central Asia. He helped establish physics research infrastructure in Tajikistan and trained a generation of Central Asian physicists. His work on radiation effects on materials was significant for the Soviet space program.
