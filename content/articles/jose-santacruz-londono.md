## Summary
José Santacruz Londoño was a Colombian drug lord and a leader of the Cali Cartel. Known as 'Chepe,' he oversaw the cartel's operations in New York City. He was arrested in 1995, escaped from prison in 1996, and was killed by Colombian police weeks later.
