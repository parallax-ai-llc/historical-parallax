## Summary

Chang Ucchin was a Korean painter known for his childlike, whimsical oil paintings of birds, trees, and Korean village life. His deceptively simple style with its flat colors and naive charm belies sophisticated compositional skill. He is one of the most beloved modern Korean painters.
