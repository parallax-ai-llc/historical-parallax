## Summary

Toumani Diabate is a Malian kora player who is considered the greatest living master of the West African harp. His solo album Kaira was the first kora solo recording. His collaborations with Bjork Damon Albarn and the Symmetric Orchestra have brought the kora to global audiences while maintaining its traditional depth.
