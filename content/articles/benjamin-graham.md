## Summary

Benjamin Graham is known as the 'father of value investing' and the 'Dean of Wall Street.' His books 'Security Analysis' (1934) and 'The Intelligent Investor' (1949) established the intellectual framework for modern investing. He was Warren Buffett's professor and mentor at Columbia Business School. His concepts of 'margin of safety' and 'Mr. Market' remain fundamental to investment analysis.
