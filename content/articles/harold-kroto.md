## Summary

Harold Kroto was a British chemist who shared the 1996 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the discovery of fullerenes. He named buckminsterfullerene after architect Buckminster Fuller's geodesic domes. Kroto was also a graphic designer, science educator, and vocal advocate for science communication. He founded the Vega Science Trust to promote science education.
