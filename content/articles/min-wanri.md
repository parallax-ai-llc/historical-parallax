## Summary

Min Wanri founded Kyobo Life Insurance, one of South Korea's largest life insurers. He also co-founded the Kyobo Book Centre, Korea's largest bookstore chain. His commitment to education and reading led him to build the Kyobo Book Centre in Gwanghwamun, which became a cultural landmark in Seoul. The Kyobo Group represents the intersection of finance and culture in Korean business.
