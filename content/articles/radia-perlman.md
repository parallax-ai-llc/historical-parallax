## Summary

Radia Perlman invented the Spanning Tree Protocol (STP), which is fundamental to the operation of network bridges and ethernet switches. Her invention enabled the creation of large, robust computer networks by preventing loops in network topology. She is often called the 'Mother of the Internet' for her foundational networking contributions.
