## Summary

Guido Reni was a leading Italian Baroque painter known for idealized, classically beautiful compositions. His Aurora ceiling fresco is one of the great achievements of Baroque painting. His elegant fusion of Bolognese classicism with Baroque dynamism created an enduring ideal of beauty.
