## Summary

Mo Yan is a Chinese novelist who received the Nobel Prize in Literature in 2012 for his work that 'with hallucinatory realism merges folk tales, history and the contemporary.' His novels including Red Sorghum and Life and Death Are Wearing Me Out draw on Chinese folk traditions with exuberant storytelling. The film adaptation of Red Sorghum won the Golden Bear at Berlin.
