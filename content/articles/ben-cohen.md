## Summary

Ben Cohen co-founded Ben & Jerry's Homemade Ice Cream, building it into one of the most successful socially conscious businesses in history. He and Jerry Greenfield started with a $5 correspondence course in ice cream making. The company pioneered linking business with social causes, donating 7.5% of pre-tax profits. Unilever acquired Ben & Jerry's for $326 million in 2000, but the brand maintained its social mission.
