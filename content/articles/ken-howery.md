## Summary

Kenneth D. Howery is an American entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and diplomat who co-founded PayPal and Founders Fund. He served as U.S. Ambassador to Sweden (2019-2021) and has been a key figure in Silicon Valley's most successful venture investments.
