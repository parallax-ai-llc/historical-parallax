## Summary

Noel Tata is the half-brother of Ratan Tata and chairman of Tata Trusts, which collectively hold approximately 66% of the equity of Tata Sons. He served as managing director of Tata International and chairman of Trent Ltd., which operates Westside stores and Zudio. He became chairman of Tata Trusts after Ratan Tata's death in 2024, cementing his role in the Tata dynasty.
