## Summary

Park Kyong-ni was one of the most important Korean novelists, best known for her monumental 16-volume novel Land (Toji). Written over 25 years, Land chronicles the lives of Korean families from the late 19th century through the Japanese colonial period. It is considered one of the greatest achievements in Korean literature and has been adapted into TV dramas, films, and opera.
