## Summary
Herbert William Mullin was an American serial killer who killed 13 people in Santa Cruz, California, between October 1972 and February 1973. He suffered from paranoid schizophrenia and believed human sacrifices were necessary to prevent earthquakes. He was convicted of 10 murders.
