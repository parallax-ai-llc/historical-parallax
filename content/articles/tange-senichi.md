## Summary

Tange Senichi was a Japanese psychiatrist and neuroscientist who studied the neural basis of consciousness and psychiatric disorders. His research on brain imaging and neuropharmacology contributed to advances in Japanese psychiatric medicine and understanding of mental illness.
