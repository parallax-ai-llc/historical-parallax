## Summary

Jean (Hans) Arp was a French-German artist who was a founding member of Dada in Zurich and later associated with Surrealism. His biomorphic abstract sculptures and reliefs, which he called 'concretions,' celebrate organic natural forms. His influence spans sculpture, painting, collage, and poetry.
