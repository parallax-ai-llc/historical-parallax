## Summary

Ole Kirk Christiansen founded the LEGO Group in 1932 and developed the LEGO brick in 1949. The interlocking plastic brick system became the world's most popular toy and an educational tool that has inspired creativity and engineering thinking in billions of children. LEGO has become the world's largest toy company.
