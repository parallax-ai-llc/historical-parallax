## Summary
James Joseph 'Whitey' Bulger Jr. was an American organized crime leader who headed the Winter Hill Gang in South Boston. He was a secret FBI informant whose handler protected him. He fled in 1995 and spent 16 years as a fugitive. Captured in 2011, convicted in 2013, and murdered in prison in 2018.
