## Summary

Richard Doll, with Austin Bradford Hill, produced the first major epidemiological evidence that smoking causes lung cancer, through their landmark 1950 British Doctors Study. Their research fundamentally changed public health policy worldwide and saved millions of lives. He continued to study cancer epidemiology for over 50 years.
