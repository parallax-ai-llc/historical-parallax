## Summary

Kim Yeon was a Goryeo Dynasty military inventor who created the Hwacha, a multiple rocket launcher system. The Hwacha could fire up to 200 rocket-propelled arrows simultaneously, making it one of the most devastating weapons of its era. It was used extensively against Japanese invasions and became an iconic Korean military invention.
