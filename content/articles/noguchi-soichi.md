## Background

- **Agency**: Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) astronaut (selected in the 1990s).
- **Training**: Completed astronaut training in Japan and the United States, including ISS operations and EVA (spacewalk) preparation.
- **Pre-astronaut work**: Engineering career prior to astronaut selection (commonly described as aerospace/engineering roles).

## Spaceflight Highlights

- Noguchi is known for flying **multiple missions spanning the Space Shuttle, Soyuz, and SpaceX Crew Dragon eras**.
- He has performed **extravehicular activities (EVAs)** on the International Space Station.

### Major Missions (high level)

- **STS-114 (Space Shuttle Discovery, 2005)**: Returned the Space Shuttle to flight after the Columbia accident; Noguchi participated as a mission specialist and performed spacewalks.
- **Soyuz TMA-17 / ISS Expedition 22/23 (2009810)**: Long-duration stay aboard the ISS.
- **SpaceX Crew-1 / ISS Expedition 64 (202021)**: One of the first operational Crew Dragon missions, marking his return to space in the commercial crew program era.
