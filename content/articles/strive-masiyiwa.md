## Summary

Strive Masiyiwa is a Zimbabwean billionaire who founded Econet Wireless, one of Africa's largest telecommunications companies. He fought a five-year legal battle against the Zimbabwean government to win a mobile phone license. Econet now operates mobile networks across Africa, and Masiyiwa expanded into fintech, media, and renewable energy. He serves on the boards of several major international organizations and is one of Africa's most respected business leaders.
