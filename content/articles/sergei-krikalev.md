## Summary

Sergei Krikalev holds the record for the most time in space by any person, with 803 days over six missions. He was aboard Mir when the Soviet Union dissolved in 1991 and could not return home for months, earning him the nickname 'the last Soviet citizen.' He was the first Russian to fly on the Space Shuttle and was ISS commander.
