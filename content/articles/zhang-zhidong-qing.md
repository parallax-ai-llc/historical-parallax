## Summary

Zhang Zhidong was a Qing Dynasty official and one of the leaders of the Self-Strengthening Movement to modernize China. He founded the Hanyang Iron and Steel Works, China's first modern steel plant, and the Hubei Arsenal. He also established several modern schools and promoted the slogan 'Chinese learning for essential principles, Western learning for practical use.' His industrial projects helped lay the groundwork for Wuhan's development as an industrial center.
