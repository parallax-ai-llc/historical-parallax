## Summary

David Einhorn is the founder and president of Greenlight Capital, a value-oriented hedge fund known for its short-selling expertise. He famously shorted Lehman Brothers before its collapse in 2008, warning publicly about its accounting problems. He also wrote 'Fooling Some of the People All of the Time' about his battle with Allied Capital. He is a professional poker player and has finished third in the World Series of Poker main event.
