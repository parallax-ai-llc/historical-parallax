## Summary

Abhinavagupta was a Kashmiri philosopher, mystic, and aesthetician who was the greatest exponent of Kashmir Shaivism. His magnum opus Tantraloka is the most comprehensive treatise on tantric philosophy. He also wrote the Abhinavabharati, a landmark commentary on Bharata's Natyashastra that revolutionized Indian aesthetics with his theory of rasa (aesthetic experience). He is considered one of India's greatest philosophical minds.
