## Overview (high-level)

- Pravin Gordhan is a South African politician and former finance minister, widely known for his work on public finance and tax administration.
- He is frequently described in mainstream reporting as an anti-corruption and “clean governance” figure within the ANC-era state, though his tenure has also been the subject of political disputes and investigations.
