## Summary

Charles Koch is chairman and CEO of Koch Industries, the second-largest privately held company in the United States. He and his late brother David transformed their father's oil refining company into a diversified conglomerate with interests in chemicals, energy, textiles, and consumer products. He is also known for his libertarian political activism and funding of political causes. Koch Industries generates over $115 billion in annual revenue.
