## Summary

Choi Gyu-ha was a pioneering Korean computer scientist who helped introduce computer technology to South Korea in the 1960s. He led early computing research at KAIST and helped establish Korea's first computer science programs. His work laid foundations for Korea's later emergence as a global technology powerhouse.
