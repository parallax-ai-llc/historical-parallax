## Summary

Abdal Hakim Murad (born Tim Winter) is a British Muslim scholar and theologian who is Dean of the Cambridge Muslim College and the Aziz Foundation Professor at the Cambridge Divinity Faculty. He is considered one of the most influential Muslims in the Western world. He has translated several classical Islamic texts and is known for his integration of traditional Islamic scholarship with Western academic rigor. His book Travelling Home explores Islam and modernity.
