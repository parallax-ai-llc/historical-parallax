## Summary

Carlo Rubbia is an Italian particle physicist who shared the 1984 Nobel Prize in Physics for the discovery of the W and Z bosons, the carriers of the weak nuclear force. He proposed converting CERN's Super Proton Synchrotron into a proton-antiproton collider to find these particles. He later served as director-general of CERN.
