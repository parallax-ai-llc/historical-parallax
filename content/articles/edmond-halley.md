## Summary

Edmond Halley was an English astronomer, geophysicist, and mathematician who is best known for computing the orbit of Halley's Comet. He demonstrated that certain comets are periodic, predicting the return of the comet that now bears his name. He also funded the publication of Newton's Principia and made important contributions to actuarial science, geophysics, and navigation.
