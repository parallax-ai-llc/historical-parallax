## Summary

Robert Herjavec is a Croatian-Canadian businessman, founder of Herjavec Group, and star of Shark Tank. He immigrated to Canada as a child and built Herjavec Group into one of the world's largest cybersecurity firms. He is known for his investments on Shark Tank (Canada and U.S. versions) and his rags-to-riches immigrant story. His company protects 65% of the Fortune 100 companies.
