## Summary

Shigeru Miyamoto is the creator of Mario, Zelda, Donkey Kong, and numerous other Nintendo franchises that defined the video game industry. As representative director and fellow at Nintendo, he has shaped more beloved entertainment properties than perhaps any other individual in history. His design philosophy of 'fun above everything' revolutionized game design. He is widely considered the most important video game designer of all time.
