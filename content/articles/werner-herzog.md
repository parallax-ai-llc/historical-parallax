## Summary

Werner Herzog is a German filmmaker whose visionary, often extreme films explore the boundaries of human ambition and nature's indifference. Aguirre the Wrath of God, Fitzcarraldo, and Grizzly Man demonstrate his willingness to push filmmaking into dangerous territory. His prolific output of fiction and documentary films has no equal.
