## Summary

Ritesh Agarwal founded OYO Rooms at age 19, building it into the world's third-largest hotel chain by room count. Starting by aggregating budget hotels in India, he expanded OYO to 80+ countries with over 157,000 hotels. He dropped out of school and became one of the youngest billionaire entrepreneurs in Asia. Despite setbacks during COVID-19, OYO filed for IPO and continued its global expansion.
