## Summary

Margery Kempe was an English mystic whose Book of Margery Kempe is considered the first autobiography in English. She experienced dramatic visions, uncontrollable weeping, and spiritual encounters throughout her life. She made pilgrimages to Jerusalem, Rome, and Santiago de Compostela. Her emotional expressiveness scandalized many of her contemporaries but she was supported by Julian of Norwich. Her text provides invaluable insight into medieval female spirituality.
