## Summary

Adi Godrej is the chairman of the Godrej Group, one of India's oldest and most diversified conglomerates. Founded in 1897, Godrej spans consumer goods, real estate, agriculture, and furniture. Adi transformed the group from a manufacturing company into a consumer-focused conglomerate. Godrej Consumer Products became one of India's largest FMCG companies. The Godrej family has managed the company for over 125 years.
