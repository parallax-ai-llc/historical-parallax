## Summary
Bonnie Elizabeth Parker was one half of the famous criminal duo Bonnie and Clyde. With Clyde Barrow, she traveled across the American South and Midwest robbing banks, gas stations, and small stores during the Great Depression. They were killed in a police ambush in Louisiana in 1934.
