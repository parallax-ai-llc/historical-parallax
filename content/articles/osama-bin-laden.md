## Summary
Osama bin Mohammed bin Awad bin Laden was the founder of al-Qaeda, the militant Islamist organization responsible for the September 11, 2001 attacks on the United States that killed nearly 3,000 people. He was the FBI's most wanted person for over a decade before being killed in a US Navy SEAL raid in Abbottabad, Pakistan.
