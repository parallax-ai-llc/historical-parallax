## Summary

Alan Kay is an American computer scientist known for his pioneering work on object-oriented programming and windowing graphical user interfaces. He conceived the Dynabook concept, a personal portable computer for children, decades before laptops and tablets existed. He led the development of Smalltalk at Xerox PARC, which influenced many modern programming languages. His famous quote 'The best way to predict the future is to invent it' encapsulates his philosophy.
