## Summary

Kim Jeong-seop (Whanki) was one of Korea's most celebrated modern painters. His later abstract works featuring dots and lines on dark backgrounds were inspired by starlight and natural phenomena, representing an artistic engagement with the physical world that paralleled scientific observation of cosmic patterns.
