## Summary

Alain Ducasse is a Monegasque-French chef and restaurateur who built the world's largest fine dining restaurant empire. He was the first chef to own restaurants simultaneously holding three Michelin stars in three cities. His Ducasse group operates over 30 restaurants worldwide. He transformed haute cuisine into a global business while maintaining the highest quality standards. He is considered one of the most commercially successful chefs in history.
