## Summary

Reinhold Würth transformed his father's small screw wholesaling business into Würth Group, the world's largest distributor of assembly and fastening materials with over €19 billion in revenue. He built the company from 2 employees to over 85,000 globally. He is also one of the world's foremost art collectors, with the Würth Collection comprising over 18,000 works.
