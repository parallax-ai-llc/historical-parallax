## Background and Training

Park Seung-hi is a South Korean short track speed skater who competed across multiple Olympic cycles, spanning the Vancouver 2010 and Sochi 2014 Winter Games.[^1]

## Olympic and International Highlights

- **Vancouver 2010**: Earned an Olympic silver medal as part of South Korea's short track program.[^1]
- **Sochi 2014**: Captured two individual gold medals and contributed to a relay gold, becoming one of the most decorated short track athletes of those Games.[^1]

## Significance

Her results are frequently cited as part of South Korea's sustained dominance in short track speed skating, a discipline where the country has historically produced multiple Olympic champions.[^1]
