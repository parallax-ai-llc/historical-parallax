## Summary

Nadiem Makarim co-founded Gojek, Indonesia's first decacorn super-app that started as a motorcycle ride-hailing service and grew into a platform offering food delivery, payments (GoPay), and more. Gojek merged with Tokopedia to form GoTo Group, which went public as Indonesia's largest tech IPO. He was appointed Indonesia's Minister of Education in 2019, leaving the company at age 35.
