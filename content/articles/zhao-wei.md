## Summary

Chinese criminal boss who operated the Kings Romans Casino in Laos's Golden Triangle Special Economic Zone as a front for drug trafficking, wildlife trafficking, and human trafficking. He was sanctioned by the US Treasury.

## Additional Context

- Zhao Wei is most commonly discussed in connection with the Golden Triangle region, where cross-border criminal markets have long been a concern for governments.
- The Kings Romans Casino and surrounding zone have been described in reporting as blending legitimate tourism development with criminal revenue streams.

## Sanctions and Allegations

- U.S. Treasury sanctions are a financial tool intended to restrict access to the dollar system and discourage international partners from doing business with designated entities.
- Allegations associated with his network commonly include drug trafficking, wildlife trafficking, and human trafficking, as reflected in public sanction statements.

## Ongoing Relevance
