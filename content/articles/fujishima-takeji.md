## Summary

Fujishima Takeji was a pioneering Japanese Western-style (yoga) painter who studied in France and Italy. He combined Western oil painting techniques with Japanese aesthetic sensibility, particularly in his luminous sunrise and sunset seascapes. He was instrumental in establishing Western-style painting in Japan.
