## Summary

Roelof F. Botha (born September 20, 1973) is a South African-American venture capitalist and partner at Sequoia Capital, one of Silicon Valley's most prestigious VC firms. A former PayPal CFO and member of the PayPal Mafia, Botha has become one of the most successful technology investors, with early investments in YouTube, Instagram, Square, and dozens of other unicorns. He became Sequoia Capital's senior steward (managing partner) in 2022, leading one of tech's most influential firms.
