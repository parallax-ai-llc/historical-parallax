## Summary

Émilie du Châtelet was a French mathematician, physicist, and author who made major contributions to physics and philosophy. She translated Newton's 'Principia Mathematica' into French, adding her own commentary that clarified and extended Newton's work. She also proposed the concept of kinetic energy (vis viva) as proportional to the square of velocity.
