## Summary

Minoru Shirota was a Japanese microbiologist who isolated the Lactobacillus casei strain Shirota in 1930 and developed Yakult, the world's first probiotic drink, in 1935. His concept of 'preventive medicine' through beneficial bacteria consumption pioneered the global probiotics industry. Yakult is now sold in 40 countries worldwide.
