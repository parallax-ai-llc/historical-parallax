## Summary

Brazilian crime boss whose real name is Marcos Willians Herbas Camacho. He is the leader of the Primeiro Comando da Capital (PCC), Brazil's largest criminal organization controlling drug trafficking from prison. He ordered the 2006 São Paulo attacks.
