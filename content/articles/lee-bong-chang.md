## Summary

Lee Bong-chang was a Korean independence activist who attempted to assassinate Japanese Emperor Hirohito in 1932. While not a scientist, his action was part of the broader Korean independence movement that fought for the self-determination necessary for Korea to develop its own scientific and technological capabilities.
