## Summary

Al-Jazari was the most outstanding mechanical engineer of the medieval Islamic world. His Book of Knowledge of Ingenious Mechanical Devices (1206) described over 50 mechanical devices including programmable automata, water clocks, and the crankshaft mechanism. His inventions anticipated many concepts in modern robotics and mechanical engineering.
