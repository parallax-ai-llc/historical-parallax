## Background

- Indian Police Service (IPS), 1988 batch (Maharashtra cadre).
- Born in Chandigarh; studied sociology (M.A., Panjab University).

## Roles and Postings (selected)

- Police Commissioner of Mumbai (appointed Feb 2020).
- Director General (DG), Maharashtra Home Guard.
- Posts reported in public sources include:
  - DG, Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB)
  - Police Commissioner of Thane
  - Additional Commissioner roles in Mumbai (including Anti-Terrorism Squad)
  - Additional Director General of Police (Law and Order)

## Publicly Reported Episodes

- 2008: Associated with counter-terror operations during the 26/11 Mumbai attacks (as part of the police response).
- 2020–2021: Oversaw or was linked in reporting to multiple high-profile Mumbai cases while commissioner.
