## Summary

Wang Zhen invented wooden movable type printing in 1298 and a revolving typecase that greatly increased printing speed. He also wrote the Nong Shu (Book of Agriculture), one of the most comprehensive agricultural treatises in Chinese history, describing farming techniques, tools, and irrigation methods.
