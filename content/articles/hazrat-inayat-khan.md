## Summary

Hazrat Inayat Khan was an Indian Sufi master and musician who founded the Sufi Order International and brought Sufism to the West. He was a classical musician from a distinguished family of musicians before becoming a Sufi teacher. He traveled to the United States and Europe in 1910, establishing Sufi centers across the Western world. His teachings emphasized the unity of all religions and the development of the heart.
