## Summary

Ashneer Grover co-founded BharatPe, a fintech company that provided merchant payment solutions. He gained fame as a judge on Shark Tank India. He was forced out of BharatPe in 2022 amid allegations of financial irregularities. His book 'Doglapan' became one of the bestselling business books in India. His controversial exit and public disputes made him one of India's most talked-about startup founders.
