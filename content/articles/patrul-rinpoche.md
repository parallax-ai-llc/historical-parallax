## Summary

Patrul Rinpoche was a Tibetan Buddhist master and writer whose Words of My Perfect Teacher is one of the most widely studied introductory texts in Tibetan Buddhism. He was known for his eccentric lifestyle, wandering as a simple nomad despite being one of the greatest teachers of his era. He was a key figure in the Rime (non-sectarian) movement and a master of Dzogchen (Great Perfection) meditation.
