## Summary

Ravi Shankar was an Indian sitar virtuoso and composer who became the most internationally famous Indian musician of the 20th century. His collaborations with George Harrison and performances at Monterey and Woodstock festivals introduced Indian classical music to Western audiences. His Concert for Bangladesh was a pioneering charity concert.
