## Summary

Marion Donovan invented the waterproof diaper cover (the 'Boater') using a shower curtain and later created the first disposable paper diaper. She held over 20 patents and sold her company for $1 million in 1951. Her innovations in diaper technology laid the groundwork for the modern disposable diaper industry.
