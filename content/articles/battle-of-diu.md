## Summary

The Battle of Diu on February 3, 1509, was a Portuguese naval victory over a combined Ottoman-Mamluk-Gujarat fleet in the Indian Ocean. The victory established Portuguese naval dominance in the Indian Ocean and secured their spice trade monopoly.
