## Additional Context

The Christchurch mosque shootings were carried out on 15 March 2019 at Al Noor Mosque and the Linwood Islamic Centre during Friday prayers.

### Perpetrator and ideology

- The attacker livestreamed part of the assault and published a manifesto associated with white supremacist and extremist themes.
- The event became a case study in how online radicalization and platform amplification can intersect with real-world violence.

### National response

New Zealand’s response was notable for a combination of public mourning and rapid policy action.

- The government moved quickly to tighten firearms regulation.
- Community solidarity and support for Muslim New Zealanders were widely emphasized in public messaging.

### Platform and content moderation impact

- Tech platforms faced intense pressure over the spread of the livestreamed footage.
- International discussions about live video, moderation, and terrorist content policies intensified after the attack.

### Notes

- New Zealand’s policy response is frequently cited in comparative discussions of firearm regulation and extremist violence prevention.
