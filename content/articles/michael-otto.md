## Summary

Michael Otto is a German billionaire and the head of the Otto Group, one of the world's largest mail-order and e-commerce companies. The Otto Group encompasses over 30 companies including Otto.de, Crate and Barrel, and Hermes logistics. He transformed the company from a catalog business into a digital commerce platform. He is also a major environmentalist and philanthropist, founding the Michael Otto Foundation for environmental protection.
