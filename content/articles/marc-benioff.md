## Summary

Marc Benioff founded Salesforce in 1999, pioneering the software-as-a-service (SaaS) business model that transformed enterprise computing. He introduced the concept of delivering business applications via the internet, eliminating the need for expensive on-premises software. Salesforce became the world's largest CRM platform. He is also known for his philanthropy and advocacy for corporate social responsibility.
