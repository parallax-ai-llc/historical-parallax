## Summary

Carolyn Bertozzi is an American chemist who shared the 2022 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development of click chemistry and bioorthogonal chemistry. Her bioorthogonal reactions can occur inside living organisms without interfering with normal biological processes, enabling the study and modification of biomolecules in living cells. This has applications in drug development and understanding disease.
