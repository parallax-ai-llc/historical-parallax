## Summary

Park In-kook is a Korean nuclear engineer who contributed to the design of South Korea's APR-1400 nuclear reactor, which became Korea's first nuclear reactor design exported internationally. The APR-1400 was sold to the United Arab Emirates in 2009, establishing Korea as a nuclear technology exporter.
