## Summary

Antonio Canova was the greatest Neoclassical sculptor, whose elegant marble works revived the ideals of ancient Greek sculpture. His Psyche Revived by Cupid's Kiss and Napoleon as Mars the Peacemaker demonstrate extraordinary technical virtuosity. He was the most celebrated sculptor in Europe during his lifetime.
