## Summary

The Joseon dynasty was founded on July 17, 1392, by General Yi Seong-gye (King Taejo) after overthrowing the Goryeo dynasty. Joseon ruled Korea for over 500 years, adopting Confucianism, creating Hangul, and shaping Korean culture profoundly.
