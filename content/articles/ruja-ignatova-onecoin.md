## Summary

Bulgarian businesswoman known as the Cryptoqueen who ran OneCoin, a $4 billion cryptocurrency Ponzi scheme that defrauded millions of investors worldwide. She disappeared in 2017 and is on the FBI's Ten Most Wanted.
