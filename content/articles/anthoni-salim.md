## Summary

Anthoni Salim is the head of the Salim Group, Indonesia's largest and most diversified conglomerate. He took over from his father Liem Sioe Liong, who built the group during the Suharto era. The Salim Group controls Indofood (Indonesia's largest food company), Indomaret (largest convenience store chain), and has interests in banking, property, and telecommunications. He rebuilt the group after the 1997 Asian financial crisis devastated its assets.
