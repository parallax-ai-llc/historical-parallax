## Summary

Lee Won-chul was a pioneering Korean physicist who earned his doctorate in cosmic ray physics from Japan's Osaka Imperial University. He became one of the founders of modern physics education in South Korea and helped establish the Korean Physical Society. He mentored many of Korea's early generation of physicists.
