## Context (high-level)

### Infanticide in Polish criminal law

- Poland’s Penal Code includes a specific offense for a mother killing a child “during childbirth” while under the influence of the birth process (commonly referred to as infanticide).
- This is typically treated differently from ordinary homicide/murder, reflecting a narrower legal definition and different sentencing range.
- The exact article numbering and penalties can change with amendments; readers should consult the current consolidated text of the Penal Code for the authoritative wording.
