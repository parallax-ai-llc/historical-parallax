## Summary

Abigail Johnson is the chairman and CEO of Fidelity Investments, one of the world's largest financial services companies with over $12 trillion in assets under administration. She succeeded her father Edward Johnson III and represents the third generation of Johnson family leadership. Under her direction, Fidelity embraced cryptocurrency, launched Bitcoin ETFs, and expanded digital services. She is one of the wealthiest women in the world.
