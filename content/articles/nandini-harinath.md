## Summary

Nandini Harinath is a rocket scientist at ISRO who served as deputy operations director for the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan). She has worked on 14 missions including satellite launches and the Mars mission. She is an advocate for women in STEM and has been recognized as one of India's pioneering women in space science.
