## Summary

Toyoda Sakichi was a Japanese inventor and industrialist known as the 'King of Japanese Inventors.' He developed numerous improvements to the power loom, including the automatic loom with non-stop shuttle change. His Toyoda Automatic Loom Works later became Toyota Motor Corporation. His invention philosophy inspired the Toyota Production System.
