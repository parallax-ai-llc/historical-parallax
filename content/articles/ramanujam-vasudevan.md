## Summary

Ramanujam Vasudevan was an Indian chemist known as the 'Plastic Man' who developed a process for using waste plastic in road construction. His shredded plastic coating technique produces more durable roads while recycling plastic waste. Over 100,000 km of roads in India have been built using his technology, providing an innovative solution to plastic pollution.
