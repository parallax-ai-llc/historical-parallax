## Summary

Yoshiko Shinohara founded Temp Holdings (now Persol Holdings), Japan's largest staffing company. She built the company from a small temporary staffing agency into a major human resources group. She was Japan's first self-made female billionaire. Starting her company at age 38 after returning from Australia, she demonstrated that entrepreneurship had no age limit.
