## Summary

Moungi Bawendi is a French-Tunisian-American chemist who won the 2023 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development of quantum dots. He invented the method for producing nearly perfect nanocrystal quantum dots with precisely controlled sizes and optical properties. Quantum dots are used in QLED displays, medical imaging, and solar cells.
