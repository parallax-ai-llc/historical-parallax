## Summary

Song I-yeong was a Joseon Dynasty astronomer and horologist who constructed Korea's first known astronomical clock using Western mechanical techniques. He learned clock-making from Jesuit missionaries' texts transmitted through China. His astronomical instruments combined traditional Korean astronomy with European mechanical principles.
