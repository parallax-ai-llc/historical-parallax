## Summary

The Trinity test on July 16, 1945, was the first detonation of a nuclear weapon, in the New Mexico desert. J. Robert Oppenheimer quoted the Bhagavad Gita: 'Now I am become Death, the destroyer of worlds.' Three weeks later, atomic bombs destroyed Hiroshima and Nagasaki.
