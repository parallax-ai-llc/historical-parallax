## Background

Ian Khama is a prominent Botswana political figure and the son of Seretse Khama, Botswana’s first president. Before the presidency, he held senior roles in the country’s defense establishment and later served as vice president, positioning him as a central actor in Botswana’s post-independence governing elite.
