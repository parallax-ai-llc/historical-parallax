## Summary

Wadati Kiyoo was a Japanese seismologist who discovered deep-focus earthquakes and the relationship between earthquake depth and distribution (the Wadati-Benioff zone). His observations of earthquakes at depths greater than 300 km challenged existing theories and contributed to the development of plate tectonics theory.
