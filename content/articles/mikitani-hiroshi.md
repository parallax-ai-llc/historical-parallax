## Summary

Hiroshi Mikitani founded Rakuten in 1997, building it into Japan's largest e-commerce company and one of the world's largest internet services companies. He expanded Rakuten into financial services, telecommunications, and sports, including ownership of FC Barcelona sponsorship. He also purchased the Tohoku Rakuten Golden Eagles baseball team. His 'Englishnization' policy requiring English as Rakuten's official language was groundbreaking for a Japanese company.
