## Identity and Disambiguation

This entry exists because some sources render **Masayoshi Son** (SoftBank Group founder) in Chinese characters as **孫正義**, which is romanized in pinyin as **Sun Zhengyi**. In most English-language business coverage he is known as **Masayoshi Son**, and the Wikipedia page for “Sun Zhengyi” is effectively a redirect/disambiguation to that primary biography.

- **Masayoshi Son (孫正義)**: Japanese entrepreneur and investor; founder and CEO of SoftBank Group.
- **“Sun Zhengyi”**: a romanization of the same Chinese characters, not a separate public figure with a distinct career record.

## Naming Notes (High-level)

- In Japanese, the family name **Son (孫)** and given name **Masayoshi (正義)** correspond to the characters used on the disambiguation page.
- In Mandarin pinyin, the same characters are typically read as **Sun (孫) Zhengyi (正義)**.
- Because romanization is language-dependent, biographies and databases can accidentally create duplicate entries when they ingest names from mixed-language sources.
