## Summary

Phạm Tuân became the first Vietnamese and first Southeast Asian in space when he flew to the Salyut 6 space station aboard Soyuz 37 in 1980. He conducted experiments in crystal growth and Earth observation during his 8-day mission. He was already a national hero in Vietnam for shooting down an American B-52 bomber during the Vietnam War.
