## Summary
On September 21, 2013, al-Shabaab militants attacked the Westgate shopping mall in Nairobi, Kenya, killing 67 people in a four-day siege. Attackers separated Muslim and non-Muslim shoppers. The attack highlighted al-Shabaab's threat beyond Somalia.
