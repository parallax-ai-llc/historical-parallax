## Summary

Lang Lang is a Chinese pianist who is the most commercially successful classical musician of his generation. His dynamic, passionate performances have attracted millions of new listeners to classical music. He performed at the Beijing Olympics opening ceremony and has been named one of Time's 100 Most Influential People.
