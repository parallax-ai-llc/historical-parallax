## Summary

Joseph Whitworth established the first uniform system of screw threads (British Standard Whitworth), enabling standardized interchangeable parts across the engineering industry. His precision measurement techniques, accurate to one millionth of an inch, set new standards for manufacturing. He also developed the Whitworth rifle with hexagonal bore rifling.
