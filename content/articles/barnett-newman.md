## Summary

Barnett Newman was an Abstract Expressionist known for his large-scale color field paintings divided by vertical lines called 'zips.' His monumental paintings like Vir Heroicus Sublimis challenged viewers to confront the sublime through pure color and scale. His writings on art and aesthetics were highly influential.
