## Summary

Indian self-styled godman who ran an armed compound in Haryana with 15,000 followers and resisted police with women and children as human shields. Skeletal remains were found in his ashram.

## Background and Organization

Rampal (born Rampal Singh Jatain) is described as a spiritual leader associated with Kabir Panth. Accounts describe him as a disciple and successor of Swami Ramdevanand, and as the leader of the Satlok Ashram organization in Haryana.

## Legal Cases (High-Level)

- In reporting summarized on Wikipedia, a 2006 confrontation involving followers of Arya Samaj and Rampal's supporters led to criminal proceedings.
- In 2014, authorities sought his arrest after repeated failures to appear in court; police actions at the Satlok Ashram led to a prolonged standoff and multiple fatalities.
- Subsequent proceedings included convictions and appeals in different cases; public reporting has treated the legal record as complex and evolving across years.
