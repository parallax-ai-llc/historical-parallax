## Summary

Shunryu Suzuki was a Japanese Soto Zen monk who came to the United States in 1959 and became one of the most influential Zen teachers in America. He founded the San Francisco Zen Center and Tassajara Zen Mountain Center, the first Zen monastery in the West. His book Zen Mind, Beginner's Mind is one of the most widely read books on Zen.
