## Summary

German nurse who is one of the most prolific serial killers in history, convicted of murdering 85 patients at hospitals in Delmenhorst and Oldenburg between 2000 and 2005. He injected patients with cardiac drugs to induce emergencies.

## Modus Operandi (High Level)

- Inducing medical crises in patients and then participating in emergency responses.
- Abuse of access to medications and clinical trust.

## Systemic Lessons (General)
