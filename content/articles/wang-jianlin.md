## Summary

Wang Jianlin founded Dalian Wanda Group, building it into China's largest real estate and entertainment conglomerate. He acquired AMC Theatres, Legendary Entertainment, and significant European and American assets. He was briefly Asia's richest person before Chinese government pressure and debt forced massive asset sales. His rise and fall reflected the dynamics of Chinese private enterprise under state capitalism.
