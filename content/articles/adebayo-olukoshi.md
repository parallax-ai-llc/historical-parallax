## Summary

Adebayo Olukoshi is a Nigerian political economist who has served as director of the United Nations African Institute for Economic Development and Planning. His research on African development, structural adjustment, and democratic governance has shaped policy across the continent. He has been one of Africa's most influential social scientists.
