## Summary

Hyujeong (also known as Seosan Daesa or Western Mountain Master) was a Korean Buddhist monk, scholar, and military leader during the Joseon Dynasty. When Japan invaded Korea in 1592, he organized a monk army of thousands to resist the invasion. He wrote major works on meditation and the harmonization of Buddhism, Confucianism, and Taoism. He is one of the most revered figures in Korean Buddhist history.
