## Summary

Rihanna (Robyn Rihanna Fenty) became a billionaire entrepreneur through Fenty Beauty and Savage X Fenty, in addition to her music career. Fenty Beauty, launched in partnership with LVMH in 2017, revolutionized the beauty industry with its inclusive shade range (40 foundation shades). The brand generated $550 million in revenue in its first year. She became the wealthiest female musician and one of the most successful celebrity entrepreneurs.
