## Summary

Ismail al-Faruqi was a Palestinian-American scholar and philosopher who was one of the most influential Islamic intellectuals in North America. He founded the International Institute of Islamic Thought (IIIT) and developed the concept of 'Islamization of Knowledge.' He sought to integrate Islamic values with modern academic disciplines. He and his wife were murdered in their home in 1986.
