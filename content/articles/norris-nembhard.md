## Context: The Shower Posse

The Shower Posse is a Jamaica-based criminal organization associated with West Kingston (especially Tivoli Gardens) that has been linked in public reporting and law-enforcement accounts to drug and firearms trafficking and related violence. It has also been described as operating through diaspora networks in North America, including parts of the eastern United States.

This context matters because many accounts that mention Norris Nembhard do so primarily in connection with broader Shower Posse operations rather than as an extensively documented public figure with a long standalone biography.

## Reported Role and Allegations (high-level)

Open sources generally describe Nembhard as:
