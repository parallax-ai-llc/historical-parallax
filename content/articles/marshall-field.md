## Summary

Marshall Field founded Marshall Field and Company, which became the largest wholesale and retail dry goods enterprise in the world. He pioneered the modern department store concept and coined the phrase 'the customer is always right.' His Chicago flagship store became a landmark institution. Field was also a major philanthropist, with his donations helping establish the Field Museum of Natural History and the University of Chicago.
