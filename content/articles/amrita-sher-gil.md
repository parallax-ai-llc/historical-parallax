## Summary

Amrita Sher-Gil was one of the most important modern Indian painters, known for her vibrant paintings that synthesized Western modernism with Indian artistic traditions. Born to a Sikh father and Hungarian mother, she studied at the Ecole des Beaux-Arts in Paris before returning to India. Her paintings of Indian village life and women are icons of Indian modern art.
