## Names and Aliases

- Commonly identified as **Juan Carlos Ramírez Abadía**, a Colombian drug trafficker.
- Wikipedia lists the alias **"Chupeta"** among his best-known nicknames.

## Role and Historical Context

- Frequently described as a leader associated with the **Norte del Valle Cartel** (North Valley Cartel).
- Public sources characterize him as involved in large-scale **cocaine trafficking**, and also mention **money laundering** activities.

## Arrest, Extradition, and Later Proceedings (High-level)

- He was **arrested in São Paulo, Brazil** in 2007.
- He was later **extradited to the United States** in 2008.
- Public reporting (as summarized by Wikipedia) describes later **U.S. federal proceedings**, including a **guilty plea** and cooperation/testimony in subsequent cases.
