## Summary

Michio Kaku is an American theoretical physicist and science communicator who is one of the co-founders of string field theory. He is a professor at City College of New York and a well-known science popularizer through his books, television appearances, and radio show. His books include 'Hyperspace,' 'The Future of the Mind,' and 'The God Equation.' Kaku has made complex physics concepts accessible to millions.
