## Summary

Scott Kelly spent 340 consecutive days aboard the ISS (2015-2016), the longest single spaceflight by an American at the time. His mission was part of NASA's Twins Study, comparing his biology with his twin brother Mark Kelly (also an astronaut) on Earth to study the effects of long-duration spaceflight on the human body.
