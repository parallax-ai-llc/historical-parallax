## Summary

Madam C.J. Walker (Sarah Breedlove) developed a line of hair care products for Black women and became America's first self-made female millionaire. She invented the Walker System of hair care and built a manufacturing empire. She was also a major philanthropist supporting African American education and anti-lynching campaigns.
