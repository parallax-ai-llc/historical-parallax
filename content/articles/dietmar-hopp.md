## Summary

Dietmar Hopp co-founded SAP SE, the world's largest enterprise software company, and later became one of Germany's most prominent philanthropists and sports investors. He served as chairman of SAP before focusing on his investment company and philanthropic foundation. He is the majority owner of TSG 1899 Hoffenheim football club, whose rise from amateur football to the Bundesliga under his backing was controversial.
