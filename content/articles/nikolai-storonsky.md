## Summary

Nikolai Storonsky is the co-founder and CEO of Revolut, one of the world's most valuable fintech companies. He founded Revolut in 2015 to offer borderless banking and foreign exchange services. The company grew to over 40 million customers and was valued at $45 billion in 2024, making it the most valuable fintech startup in Europe. He previously worked at Credit Suisse and Lehman Brothers.
