## Summary

Nicolas Berggruen is a German-American billionaire investor and founder of the Berggruen Institute, a think tank focused on governance and philosophy. He was known as the 'homeless billionaire' for living in hotels rather than owning property. He made his fortune through Berggruen Holdings, investing in companies across Europe. He has become one of the most prominent philanthropists focused on ideas and governance rather than traditional charity.
