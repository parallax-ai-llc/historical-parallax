## Summary

Bartolome Esteban Murillo was one of the most popular Spanish Baroque painters, celebrated for tender religious paintings and vivid genre scenes of street children. His Immaculate Conception paintings established the canonical visual representation of the doctrine. He co-founded the Seville Academy of Art.
