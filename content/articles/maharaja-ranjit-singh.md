## Summary

Maharaja Ranjit Singh was the founder and ruler of the Sikh Empire, the most powerful Indian kingdom of the early 19th century. Known as the 'Lion of Punjab,' he united the Sikh confederacies (misls) into a unified kingdom. He built one of the most effective armies in Asia by modernizing it with European help. He was known for his secular governance, employing Hindus, Muslims, and Europeans in his administration.
