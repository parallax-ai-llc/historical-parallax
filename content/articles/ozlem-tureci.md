## Summary

Özlem Türeci co-founded BioNTech with her husband Uğur Şahin and served as chief medical officer during the development of the COVID-19 mRNA vaccine. Her expertise in cancer immunotherapy helped develop the mRNA platform that was pivoted to create the vaccine. She previously co-founded Ganymed Pharmaceuticals for cancer antibody therapies.
