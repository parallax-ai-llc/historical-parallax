## Summary

Yoshiro Nakamatsu (Dr. NakaMats) is a prolific Japanese inventor who claims over 3,500 patents, including contributions to the floppy disk, CD and DVD technology. While some of his claims are disputed, he received the Ig Nobel Prize for Nutrition in 2005. He is considered one of Japan's most eccentric and productive inventors.
