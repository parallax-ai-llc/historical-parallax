## Summary

Cindy Sherman is an American artist known for her conceptual self-portraits in which she photographs herself in various costumes and settings. Her Untitled Film Stills series deconstructed female stereotypes in media. She is one of the most important and influential artists of the late 20th century, exploring identity, gender, and representation.
