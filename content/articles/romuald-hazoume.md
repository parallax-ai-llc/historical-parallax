## Summary

Romuald Hazoume is a Beninese artist known for his mask-like sculptures made from recycled gasoline cans. His large-scale installation Dream about the trafficking of gasoline across West African borders. His work combines Yoruba artistic traditions with contemporary commentary on globalization.
