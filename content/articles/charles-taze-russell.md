## Summary

Charles Taze Russell was an American Christian restorationist and the founder of the Bible Student movement, which later became Jehovah's Witnesses. He published Zion's Watch Tower magazine and the multi-volume Studies in the Scriptures. He predicted the second coming of Christ and the end of the age. His movement grew into a worldwide organization after his death.
