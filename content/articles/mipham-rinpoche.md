## Summary

Jamgon Ju Mipham was a Tibetan Buddhist philosopher and polymath who is considered the greatest Nyingma scholar of the 19th-20th centuries. He wrote over 30 volumes covering Buddhist philosophy, logic, medicine, astronomy, poetry, and Sanskrit grammar. His philosophical works on Madhyamaka and Yogacara revitalized Nyingma intellectual tradition. He was a key figure in the Rime (non-sectarian) movement alongside Jamgon Kongtrul and Khyentse Wangpo.
