## Summary

Patrick Drahi is a French-Israeli billionaire who built Altice, a multinational telecoms and media empire spanning Europe, the U.S., and Israel. He acquired SFR (France's second-largest telecom), Cablevision, and Optimum in the U.S., and Sotheby's auction house. His aggressive debt-fueled acquisition strategy created one of the largest telecom empires in the world. He faced challenges from high debt levels and competition.
