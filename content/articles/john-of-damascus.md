## Summary

John of Damascus was the last of the Church Fathers and a major theologian of the Eastern Orthodox Church. His Fount of Knowledge is one of the first systematic theological works in Christianity. He was a key defender of the veneration of icons during the Iconoclast controversy. He also composed important hymns still used in Orthodox liturgy.
