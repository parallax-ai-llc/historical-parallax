## Summary
Velupillai Prabhakaran was the founder and leader of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE), known as the Tamil Tigers. He led a 26-year civil war for an independent Tamil homeland in Sri Lanka. The LTTE pioneered the use of suicide bombing. He was killed by Sri Lankan forces in 2009.
