## Summary

Malik ibn Anas was an Islamic jurist and hadith scholar who founded the Maliki school of jurisprudence. His work al-Muwatta is one of the earliest and most important compilations of hadith and Islamic law. He emphasized the practice of the people of Medina as a source of law alongside the Quran and hadith. The Maliki school is predominant in North and West Africa.
