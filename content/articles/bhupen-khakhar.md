## Summary

Bhupen Khakhar was an Indian painter who brought narrative figuration and openly gay themes to Indian contemporary art. His colorful, story-telling paintings of ordinary people and his frank depictions of homosexuality broke new ground. He was a central figure of Indian art from the 1960s onward.
