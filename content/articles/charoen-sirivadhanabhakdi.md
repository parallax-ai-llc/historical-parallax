## Summary

Charoen Sirivadhanabhakdi is a Thai billionaire and the founder of Thai Beverage (ThaiBev), the largest beverage company in Southeast Asia. He also controls Fraser and Neave (F&N), TCC Group, and vast real estate holdings. His acquisition of F&N from a competing bid by Heineken in 2012 was one of the largest corporate battles in Southeast Asian history. He is one of Thailand's richest people with interests spanning beverages, real estate, and agriculture.
