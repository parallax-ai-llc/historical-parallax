## Summary

Kalpana Chawla was the first Indian-born woman in space, flying aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia in 1997. She was killed along with six crewmates during the Columbia's re-entry on February 1, 2003. She was a mission specialist and aerospace engineer who inspired millions, especially in India, with her achievements.
