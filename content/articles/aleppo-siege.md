## Summary

Four-year siege of Aleppo, Syrias largest city, from 2012-2016 during the Syrian Civil War. The Assad regimes barrel bombing and Russian airstrikes killed thousands of civilians.

## Additional Context

The siege is discussed as part of the wider Syrian Civil War and the prolonged contest for control of Aleppo, a major urban, industrial, and cultural center.

### Humanitarian impact (high level)

- Protracted urban fighting contributed to large-scale displacement.
- Civilian infrastructure (housing, hospitals, utilities) was heavily affected over time.
- Access constraints and shifting front lines complicated aid delivery.

### How it is analyzed

- Urban sieges often combine military pressure with economic and humanitarian effects.
- Airpower, artillery, and street-level control can shift the balance in dense city terrain.
- Reporting frequently distinguishes between phases of escalation and negotiated pauses.
