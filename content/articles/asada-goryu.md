## Summary

Asada GoryÅ« was a self-taught Japanese astronomer who independently discovered Kepler's third law of planetary motion and developed methods for predicting eclipses. He founded a school of astronomy in Osaka that trained leading Japanese astronomers. He was one of the most original scientific thinkers of the Edo period.
