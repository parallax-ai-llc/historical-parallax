## Summary

Cher Wang co-founded HTC Corporation, which became one of the world's leading smartphone manufacturers. She also co-founded VIA Technologies, a major chipmaker. HTC produced the first Android smartphone and the first commercially available 4G phone. At its peak, HTC controlled over 10% of the global smartphone market. She later pivoted HTC toward virtual reality with the Vive headset, partnering with Valve.
