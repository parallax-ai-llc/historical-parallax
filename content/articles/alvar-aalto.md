## Summary

Alvar Aalto was a Finnish architect and designer who pioneered a humanistic modernism using natural materials, especially wood. His buildings including the Paimio Sanatorium and Finlandia Hall combine functionalism with organic warmth. His furniture designs, including the Aalto stool, remain icons of Scandinavian design.
