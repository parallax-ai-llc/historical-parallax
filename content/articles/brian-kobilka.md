## Summary

Brian Kobilka is an American physician and molecular biologist who shared the 2012 Nobel Prize in Chemistry with Robert Lefkowitz for studies of G-protein-coupled receptors. He captured the first image of a GPCR in action, showing how it transmits signals from outside to inside a cell. This work was crucial for understanding drug-receptor interactions.
