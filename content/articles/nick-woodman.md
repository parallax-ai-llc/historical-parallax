## Summary

Nick Woodman is the founder and CEO of GoPro, the company that popularized action cameras. He created the first GoPro camera to capture surfing footage and built it into a billion-dollar consumer electronics brand. GoPro went public in 2014, briefly making Woodman a billionaire. Though the company faced challenges from smartphone cameras, GoPro remained the leader in action and sports photography.
