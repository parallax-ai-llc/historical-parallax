## Summary

Shi Changxu was a Chinese materials scientist known as the 'Father of Chinese Superalloys.' He developed high-temperature alloys essential for jet engines and gas turbines, enabling China's aerospace and power generation industries. His superalloy research reduced China's dependence on imported materials for critical applications.
