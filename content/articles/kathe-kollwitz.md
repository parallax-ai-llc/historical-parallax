## Summary

Kathe Kollwitz was a German artist whose powerful prints and sculptures depict the suffering of the poor, war, and maternal love. Her print cycles A Weavers' Revolt and The Peasants' War are among the most moving works of social art. She was the first woman elected to the Prussian Academy.
