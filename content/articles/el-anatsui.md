## Summary

El Anatsui is a Ghanaian sculptor known for his monumental, shimmering tapestry-like installations made from recycled aluminum bottle caps and copper wire. His sculptures address themes of consumption, waste, and the legacy of colonialism and the transatlantic trade. He is one of the most acclaimed African artists of the 21st century.
