## Summary

Steve Cohen is the founder of Point72 Asset Management and one of Wall Street's most successful hedge fund managers. His earlier fund, SAC Capital, was one of the most profitable in history but pleaded guilty to insider trading in 2013. He returned to managing outside capital with Point72. He purchased the New York Mets in 2020 for $2.4 billion, the highest price ever paid for a sports franchise at the time.
