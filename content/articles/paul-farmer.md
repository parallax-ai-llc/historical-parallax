## Summary

Paul Farmer co-founded Partners in Health, a global health organization that brought high-quality medical care to some of the world's poorest communities. He demonstrated that diseases like tuberculosis and HIV could be effectively treated in resource-poor settings, challenging the notion that poor countries couldn't provide quality healthcare. His work transformed global health policy.
