## Summary

Former LRA commander who was the first person to face trial for atrocities committed during the northern Uganda conflict. His trial has been plagued by legal challenges and delays.

## Career Timeline

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1987 | Abducted by LRA as a child |
| 2009 | Captured by Ugandan army |
| 2011 | Trial began at International Crimes Division |
| 2024 | Trial still ongoing |
