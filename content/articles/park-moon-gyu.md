## Summary

Park Moon-gyu is a Korean materials scientist at Seoul National University who has made significant contributions to nanomaterials and energy storage research. His work on nanostructured electrodes for lithium-ion batteries has been influential in advancing Korean battery technology, supporting the global leadership of Korean companies in battery manufacturing.
