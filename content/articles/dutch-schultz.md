## Summary
Arthur Simon Flegenheimer, known as Dutch Schultz, was a New York City gangster and bootlegger during the 1920s and 1930s. He controlled the numbers racket in Harlem. He was murdered by Murder, Inc. after planning to assassinate prosecutor Thomas Dewey against the Commission's wishes.
