## Summary

Koizumi Gunji was a Japanese-British judo master who founded the Budokwai in London (1918), the first judo club in Europe. Beyond judo teaching, he systematically analyzed the biomechanics and physics of throwing techniques, publishing scientific analyses of judo that contributed to sports science.
