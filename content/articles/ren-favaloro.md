## Summary

René Favaloro performed the first planned coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery using the saphenous vein in 1967 at the Cleveland Clinic. This technique became one of the most common operations in the world, saving millions of lives from coronary artery disease. He returned to Argentina to establish a foundation for cardiac surgery.
