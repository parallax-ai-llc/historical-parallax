## Summary

Deadly crowd crush in the Itaewon nightlife district of Seoul on October 29, 2022 during Halloween celebrations. 159 people were killed, mostly young people, in a narrow alley.

## Context: Crowd Safety

- Crowd crush disasters are commonly analyzed in terms of density, constrained egress routes, and the absence (or failure) of active crowd management.
- High-attendance public festivities without ticketing can be difficult to manage because arrival patterns are diffuse and can surge quickly.

## Aftermath and Accountability (High-Level)

- Public discussion after major disasters often covers emergency response coordination, communication to the public, and investigations into planning and negligence.

## Lessons Commonly Cited in Safety Literature

- Monitoring crowd density and establishing controlled one-way flows in narrow corridors.
- Clear incident command structures and rapid escalation protocols.
- Public messaging designed to reduce surges and encourage dispersal before densities become critical.
