## Summary

Lee Krasner was a pioneering Abstract Expressionist painter whose powerful, rhythmic paintings demonstrate extraordinary range from intimate collages to monumental canvases. Long overshadowed by her husband Jackson Pollock, she has been recognized as a major artist in her own right. Her work combines Cubist structure with gestural intensity.
