## Summary
Nathuram Vinayak Godse was a Hindu nationalist who assassinated Mahatma Gandhi on January 30, 1948, in New Delhi. He believed Gandhi was too accommodating to Muslims and responsible for India's partition. He was tried, convicted, and hanged on November 15, 1949.
