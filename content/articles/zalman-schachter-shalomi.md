## Summary

Zalman Schachter-Shalomi (Reb Zalman) was a Polish-American rabbi who founded the Jewish Renewal movement, which sought to revitalize Judaism with Hasidic mysticism, meditation, and feminism. He integrated insights from other spiritual traditions, including Sufism and Buddhism, into Jewish practice. He was a pioneer of Jewish-Buddhist dialogue and ecumenical spirituality. His approach to Judaism emphasized personal spiritual experience over rigid adherence to law.
