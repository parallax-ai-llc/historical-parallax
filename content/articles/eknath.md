## Summary

Eknath was a Marathi saint, scholar, and poet who made significant contributions to both literature and social reform in Maharashtra. He wrote a Marathi version of the Ramayana (Eknathi Bhagavat) and commentaries on important Hindu texts. He challenged caste discrimination by eating with and serving lower-caste people. He is considered a key figure linking the earlier and later phases of the Bhakti movement in Maharashtra.
