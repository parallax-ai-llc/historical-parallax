## Summary
Akku Yadav was an Indian serial rapist and murderer who terrorized the Kasturba Nagar slum in Nagpur for over a decade. He raped and murdered numerous women while police refused to act. In 2004, a mob of 200 women lynched him in open court, stabbing him over 70 times. No one was convicted of his death.
