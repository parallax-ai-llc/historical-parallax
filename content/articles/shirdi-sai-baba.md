## Summary

Shirdi Sai Baba was an Indian spiritual master who lived in the town of Shirdi in Maharashtra. His origins are unknown â€” he appeared in Shirdi as a young man and lived there until his death. He drew followers from both Hindu and Muslim communities, teaching 'Sabka Malik Ek' (One God governs all). He lived in a dilapidated mosque, maintained a sacred fire (dhuni), and was known for miraculous healings. His shrine in Shirdi is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India.
