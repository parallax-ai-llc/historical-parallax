## Summary

Mochtar Riady founded the Lippo Group, one of Indonesia's largest conglomerates spanning banking, real estate, retail, and healthcare. He is known as a pioneer of modern banking in Indonesia and developed Lippo Karawaci, one of the largest integrated real estate developments in Southeast Asia. The Lippo Group also operates Siloam Hospitals, Indonesia's largest private hospital chain. He has been involved in international political controversies including U.S. campaign finance issues.
