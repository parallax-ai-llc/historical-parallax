## Summary

Chinese avant-garde novelist whose hallucinatory fiction pushes the boundaries of narrative. Her experimental works including The Last Lover and Love in the New Millennium have been compared to Kafka and Borges. She is frequently mentioned as a Nobel Prize candidate.
