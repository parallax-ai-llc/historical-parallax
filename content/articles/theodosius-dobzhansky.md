## Summary

Theodosius Dobzhansky was a Ukrainian-American geneticist and evolutionary biologist who helped establish the modern evolutionary synthesis, combining Mendelian genetics with Darwinian natural selection. His book 'Genetics and the Origin of Species' (1937) was foundational. His famous essay 'Nothing in Biology Makes Sense Except in the Light of Evolution' encapsulated his views on the centrality of evolution to biology.
