## Research Focus

His work is associated with theoretical particle physics in the era when the **Standard Model** was being established. High-level topics linked to his contributions include:

- **Gauge theory and renormalization**: the mathematical consistency of quantum field theories describing fundamental forces.
- **Electroweak theory**: unification of electromagnetic and weak interactions.
- **Weak interactions**: processes such as beta decay that are mediated by W and Z bosons.

Some accounts also connect him to work that helped shape expectations for new particles (including quark-sector developments) and to influential results used by later researchers.
