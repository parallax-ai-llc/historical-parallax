## Summary

Lawrence Ho is the chairman and CEO of Melco Resorts & Entertainment, one of Asia's largest casino and resort operators. He is the son of Stanley Ho, the former Macau casino monopoly holder. He built Melco independently from his father's STDM, developing City of Dreams and Studio City in Macau. He expanded into the Philippines, Japan, and Cyprus, establishing himself as a major force in Asian gaming separate from his father's empire.
