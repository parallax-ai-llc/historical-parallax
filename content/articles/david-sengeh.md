## Summary

David Sengeh is a Sierra Leonean biomedical engineer who developed comfortable prosthetic sockets using 3D printing and MRI data at MIT Media Lab. He became Sierra Leone's Chief Innovation Officer and Minister of Education, applying technology to solve development challenges in one of the world's poorest countries.
