## Summary

Kim Jeong-ryeom was a Korean chemical engineer who played a key role in establishing South Korea's petrochemical industry during the 1960s and 1970s. He helped plan and build major chemical plants that formed the backbone of Korea's industrial economy and contributed to the country's rapid economic development.
