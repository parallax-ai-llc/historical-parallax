## Summary

Rabia Bhuiyan is a pioneering Bangladeshi plant pathologist who has made significant contributions to understanding plant diseases affecting rice and other crops in South Asia. Her research on rice blast disease has helped develop resistant varieties crucial for food security in Bangladesh.
