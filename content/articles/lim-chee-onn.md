## Summary

Lim Chee-Onn is a Singaporean engineer who played a key role in developing Singapore's infrastructure and technology sector. His engineering and policy work contributed to Singapore's transformation from a developing nation into a global technology hub. He served in multiple government and corporate leadership positions.
