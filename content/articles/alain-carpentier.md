## Summary

Alain Carpentier pioneered heart valve repair surgery and developed the Carpentier-Edwards bioprosthetic heart valve, the most widely used prosthetic valve in the world. He also led the development of the CARMAT total artificial heart. His contributions to cardiac surgery have saved hundreds of thousands of lives.
